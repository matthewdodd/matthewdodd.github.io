





617. 
               
                  SION 
                  FREEBODY
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th day of May
 last, one dead lamb, value 13 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Elland
                  




               
            .
The prisoner sold the lamb to 
               
                  Margaret
                   Smith
               



             for 13 s. which was identified, and the sets that were with it were sworn to by the prosecutor and his servant.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
