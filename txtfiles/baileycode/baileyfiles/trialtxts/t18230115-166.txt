





336. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     HARDING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of December
, a tea-kettel, value 12 s., and a phosphorus box, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Dyer 
                  Lennan
               



            .
SECOND COUNT, stating it to belong to 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Weston
                  



               
            .
DYER LENNAN I live in Holborn


. On the 26th of December, between five and six o'clock, I missed these things, they were safe at four o'clock, on the back of the shop counter; next morning they were produced by the officer.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  WILLIAM 
                  COLTON
               



            . On the evening of the 26th of December, between six and seven o'clock; I saw the prisoner running down Holborn, with the kettle in his hand, I followed him, he was secured without my loosing sight of him, but he threw it away.


                  JOHN 
                  BOSTON
               



            . I saw the kettle thrown into the road. I picked it up and gave it to Colton, the phosphorus box was inside it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
