





792. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BARNES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 7th ofAugust
 last, part of an iron bar, value 10 d. one iron spear with a wooden handle, value 3 d. a steel screw-driver, value 3 s. two locks, value 6 d. four staples, value 2 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Coles
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Gould
                  



               
            .
Part of the property was found on the prisoner; and 
               
                  William
                   Box
               



            , a constable, found the things mentioned in the indictment in the prisoner's lodging.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
