







                     Robert 
                     Bowden 
                  



               
            , of Stoke Newington


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 14 Guineas
            , the Money of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Hills 
               




             , the 14th of December
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prosecutor and Prisoner were Chamber-Fellows, and that the Prisoner took the opportunity to take the Money out of a Box in the Chamber, in the Morning, after the Prosecutor was gone out to his Work; that being pursued, he was taken, and the Money was found under a Bench where the Prisoner sat, he being carried into an Alehouse, when he was apprehended, and the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
