





505. 
               
                  
                     JANE 
                     BAXTER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of February
, 2 silver forks, value 1l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Abbott
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  BURFORD
               



            . I am in the service of 
               
                  William 
                  Abbott
               



             - he is a gentleman
, and lives in Wynyat-place


 - the prisoner used to come to visit the cook - she was there on the 8th of February - we missed some property; I suspected her - we inquired, and some property was found at the pawnbroker's - she has been in the habit of coming there for three months.


                  JOHN 
                  MATTHEWS
               



            . I am a pawnbroker - I have two silver forks, pawned by the prisoner on the 10th of February - I have no doubt of her person.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I know nothing about them further than washing them up and leaving them in the kitchen.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 32. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
