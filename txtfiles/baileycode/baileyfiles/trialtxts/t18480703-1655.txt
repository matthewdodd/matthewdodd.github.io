




1655. 




HENRY JOHNSON
 and 




JOHN BROWN



, stealing 1 clock, value 5l.; the goods of 



Richard Smith, in his dwelling-house; having both been before convicted; to which


JOHNSON pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 27.—


Transported for Seven Years
.

BROWN pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 19.—



Confined One Year
.
