, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Piece of Persian Silk, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Nathan

 , the 16th of April
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner came to his Shop under pretence of buying Calamanco, and took an opportunity to steal the Goods ; which he perceiving, followed her out of the Shop, and found the Goods under her Ridinghood. The Fact being plainly proved, the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 4s. 10d.
