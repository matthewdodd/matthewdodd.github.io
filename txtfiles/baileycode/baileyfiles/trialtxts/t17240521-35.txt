








                  William 
                  Perkins 
               



            , of the Parish of Christchurch


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Ribbon, and divers other Goods
            , the Property of 
               

                        John 
                        Sewel 
                     




                  , and Company







            , the first of this Instant May
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and took the Goods, and was apprehended offering them to Sale The Fact was plainly prov'd, and the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
