







                     George 
                     Watson 
                  



               
            , of St. James's Clerkenwell


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Shirt, value 4 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Andrew 
                     Field 
                  




                
            , on the 7th of March
last. 
               
                  Elizabeth 
                  Thorndel 
               



             depos'd, that she saw the prisoner get over Ditton's Wall, jump back again and cram something white into his Pocket; she raised the neighbourhood, who took him near a Brewhouse, and in a Stable belonging to that Brew house they found the Shirt. The Constable deposed, that when he was searched, they found a large Wallet about him, with a Tinder Box, Flint, Steel, Matches, a Gimblet, Knife and Pick-lock-key: all which were produced in Court. The prisoner in his defence said, he carried the Wallet to put Carrots in, which he was then sent to buy, but being pursued by the Bailiffs, he jumpt over the Wall to hide himself. 

               Guilty of Felony
            .
