





NEW COURT. Wednesday, August 14, 1833.
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1171. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     SHAW
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously forging three several receipts for the sums of 13l. 7s. 6d.; 30l. 1s. 3d.; and 14l. 6s. 6d., with intent to defraud the Right Hon. 
                     
                        Viscount Goderich
                        



                     
                  , now Earl
 of Ripon
            . - Other counts, varying the manner of stating the charge. - To which indictments he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .(See Sixth Sess. p. 568.)
