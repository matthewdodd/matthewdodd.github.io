



61. 




JOHN SEARBY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
1st of November, 1 snuff-box, value 2s.; 7 shawls, value 3s.; 4 scrafs, value 10s.; 1 gown, value 3s.; and 2 pair of trowsers, value 5s.; the goods of 



Thomas Green.






THOMAS GREEN
. I live in Shoe-lane, and am a broker. The prisoner lodged in my house—there was a trunk in one of my rooms, which contained this property—it was locked—the prisoner slept in that room—I missed this property about the 1st of November, and had the prisoner taken on the 8th of November.





JOSEPH JOHN YOUNG
. I am an officer. I took the prisoner—I found on him some duplicates, which led to the pawnbrokers—I produce a key that I found on him, which unlocks the box where the property was.





JAMES SAYER
. I am a pawnbroker, and live in Drury-lane. I have a shawl and a half shawl—I do not know who pawned them—I gave this duplicate for them.





JACOB RUSSELL
. I am a pawnbroker, and live in Holborn. I have a shawl—I do not know who pawned it—I gave the person this duplicate.





WILLIAM ROBERTS
. I am a pawnbroker in Drury-lane. I have two scrafs pawned by the prisoner.





CHARLES SIMON HYAMS
. I am a pawnbroker. I have two shawls and a scraf—I do not know who pawned them. I gave this duplicate.





WILLIAM BARRY
. I am shopman to a pawnbroker. I have two gowns and a shawl—I do not know who pawned them. I gave this duplicate.





EDWARD HENRY CANE
. I am a pawnbroker. I have a scarf pawned for 1s.—this is the duplicate.





ISAAC HUGHES PUGH
. I have a gold watch. I do not know who pair pawned it—this is the duplicate.





WILLIAM CLARK
. I am a pawnbroker. I have a of trowsers—I do not know who pawned them—these are the duplicates.





AMELIA GREEN
. These are my husband's property—this watch is mine, but is not in the indictment.

Prisoner. I never was charged with any offence before.




GUILTY
. Aged 24.—


Transported for Seven Years.
