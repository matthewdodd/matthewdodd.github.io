





30. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     RITCHEY
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 8th of July
, without lawful excuse had in his custody and possession, three forged 1 l. bank notes, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Graham
               



            .
