





193. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     HARGRAVE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 2nd of December
, 1 pair of shoes, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Dawson
                  




               
            .


                  FRANCES 
                  DAWSON
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  John 
                  Dawson
               



             - we live in Monmouth-street


. On the 2nd of December I saw the prisoner, and watched him for some time; he was then alone; I then saw him with another bigger boy
 - he passed our door, but I did not see him take the shoes; he passed again, and I saw he had them under his arm; I ran, and cried Stop thief! he threw down the shoes, and the officer took them up; they are my husband's property.


                  WILLIAM 
                  ARNEY
               



            . I saw the prisoner running, and the prosecutor following him; I took the shoes up.
Prisoner's Defence. The other boy gave them to me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 9. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
