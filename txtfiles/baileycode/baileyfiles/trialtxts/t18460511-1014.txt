




1014. 




SAMUEL STANDEN
 and 




WILLIAM JONES
 were indicted for

 stealing 250lbs. weight of tea, value 40l.; 8 chests, 10s.; and 3 hats, 10s.; the goods of 



James Rattroy: and 1 pair of boots, value 5s.; the goods of 



Peter Patenon, on board a vessel on the navigable fiver 
Thames; to which


STANDEN pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 36.

JONES pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 23.





Transported for Seven Years.
