




1641. 




WILLIAM WARD
 was indicted for

 stealing 1 mare. price 12l.; the property of 



Thomas Fairclough
; and 




CHARLES HEWITT



 for feloniously receiving the same, well knowing it to have been stolen; against the Statute, &c.; to which


WARD pleaded 


GUILTY.* Aged 13.

HEWITT pleaded 


GUILTY. Aged 17.





Transported for Seven Years.
