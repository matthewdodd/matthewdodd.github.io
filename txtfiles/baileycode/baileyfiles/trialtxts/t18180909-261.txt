





1321. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  WILSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of July
, one copper boiler, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Allen
               




            .
WILLIAM ALLEN. I keep the Portland Arms, public-house, in Upper Berkley-street




. On the 30th of July, about a quarter before ten o'clock at night, I missed the copper boiler out of my kitchen.


                  WILLIAM 
                  RUSSELL
               



            . I am a watchman. I was at the corner of Upper Berkley-street, about a quarter before ten o'clock at night, the prisoner passed me with a basket. I heard the alarm, and ran after him; he threw the basket down. I picked it up, and found the boiler in it.


                  BENJAMIN 
                  RUSSEL
               



            . I am a watchman. I saw the prisoner come out of the prosecutor's house with the basket in his hand; I ran after him, he threw it down. I secured him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I know nothing of it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
