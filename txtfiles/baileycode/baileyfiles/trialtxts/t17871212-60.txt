





65. 
               
                  ANN 
                  BROOKS
               



             was indicted for 


               burglariously and feloniously breaking and entering the dwelling of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Gould
                        




                     
                  , about the hour of nine in the night, on the 15th of November
 last, and burglariously stealing therein, two linen sheets, value 5 s. his property
            .
The prosecutor took the prisoner with the property on her, just coming out of the house.




               GUILTY Of the stealing, not of the burglary
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   PERRYN
               



            .
