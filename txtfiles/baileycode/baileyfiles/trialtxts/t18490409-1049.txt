




1049. 




JAMES M'LOCHLIN
 and 



MATTHEW KEOGH



, stealing 1 copper boiler, 25lbs. weight of copper pipe, and 100lbs. weight of lead pipe; the goods of 



Theodore Ferrina Pints and another.


M'LOCHLIN pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.


KEOGH pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 19.—



Confined Nine Months.
