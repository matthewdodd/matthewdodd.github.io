





475. 
               
                  
                     AMELIA 
                     HATFIELD
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               she, on the 4th of May
, feloniously had in her custody and possession, a certain forged bank note for the payment of 1 l
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
