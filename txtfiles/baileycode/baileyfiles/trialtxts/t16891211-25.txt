







                     Elizabeth 
                     Sherwood
                  



               
             was Indicted as principal, and 2 others as Accessaries to Sherwood, for 


               Robbing 
                     
                        Richard 
                        Comer
                     




                  , on the 22d of Novemb.
 of 10 Gold Rings value 7 l. a pair of Silver Clasps for a Cloak value 7 s. and Five l. in Money
            . The Prosecutor declared, that he lost his Money and the Rings out of a Trunk, but he could not say, that she took away his Goods, but the Constable found some of the Rings about her, and she confest that she had the Money: So upon the whole, the Woman was found 

               Guilty
            , and the two latter were Acquitted.
