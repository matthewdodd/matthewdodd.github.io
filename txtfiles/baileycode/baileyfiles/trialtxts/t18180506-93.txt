





831. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     WELLINGTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of April
, eleven napkins, value 20s., and one table-cloth, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Mary Sarah 
                     Nasmyth
                  




               
            , widow
.


                  ELIZA 
                  DAVISON
               



            . I am housekeeper to Mrs. 
               
                  Mary 
                  Sarah Nasmyth
               



            , who lives in Lower Harley-street


; the things were on the window-cill of my room - I left the room at one o'clock, and went into the kitchen; a man came and asked if I had lost any thing? I went and found my window lifted up and my things gone. He must have gone down the area steps to take them.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BODY
               



            . I am a coachman. I saw the prisoner run up the mews with the things under his arm. I secured him, and took the things back.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. A man gave them to me at the top of the steps.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 27.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
