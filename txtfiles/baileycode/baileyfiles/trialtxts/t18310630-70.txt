





First London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
1259. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     MILLER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of May
, 1 ream of paper, value 12s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Bonsor
                  




               
            ; and that he had before been convicted of felony; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 24. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
