





779. 
               
                  
                     DAVID JOHN 
                     HUGHES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of February
, 1 cap, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Benjamin 
                           King
                        



                     
                  , from the person of 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           King
                        



.


                  GEORGE 
                  KING
               



            . I am ten years old. I know the prisoner - I was in Bell-street


, near the Key public-house, and saw the prisoner - he came behind me, took my cap off my head, and ran away; I live in Bury-place, with my father - we sell milk; the prisoner put my cap under his coat, and ran off with it - I gave an alarm, and he was pursued.


                  THOMAS 
                  OLDACRE
               



            . I am butcher. I heard the alarm of Stop thief! I went into Chapple-street - I saw several persons running; the prisoner was stopped at the corner of Market-street, and I took the cap from under his coat -I am a constable of the parish; I saw the lad crying, and he said the prisoner had taken his cap - the prisoner asked me to let him go, and said he did not take it.
Prisoner's Defence. The cap was taken off his head, and thrown to me - I took it up, and ran off with it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
