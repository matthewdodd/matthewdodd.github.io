



1156. 




JOHN WILSON
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
3rd of April, 1 pair of boots, value 25s., the goods of 



Richard Robinson Alderson.






RICHARD ROBINSON ALDERSON
. I lodge at Mrs. Goldsworthy's. I never gave any body directions to go there to get any boots; but I missed a pair on the 3rd of April.





CATHARINE DAWSON
. I am servant to Mrs. Goldsworthy—the prosecutor lodged there. On the 3rd of April the prisoner came there and asked

me for a pair of new boots of Mr. Alderson's, which were to be stretched, and he would bring them back that night, but he did not.




GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.
