



265. 





DANIEL LLOYD



, stealing, whilst engaged in the Post-office, a letter, containing 2 sovereigns and 6 postage stamps; the property of 

Her Majesty's Postmaster-General.—There were 3 other indictments against the prisoner for like offences: to all of which he





PLEADED GUILTY
. Aged 33.—


Transported for Twenty Years.

(MR. BODKIN, for the prosecution, stated that, on the prisoners lodging being searched, upwards of forty letters were found opened which had contained money).
