





317. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     COLLINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing' on the 19th of December
, 1 pair of shoes, value 5s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Hayhow
                  




               
            .
GEORGE EAST. I am in the employ of William Hayhow, a shoemaker
, in the New-road


. I was sitting in the kitchen on the 19th of December, and heard a person sing out that a pair of shoes were taken - I ran up stairs, and saw the prisoner turning the corner of Shovel-alley; I went and took him with these shoes, which had been brought in about three hours before, and I had hung them up at the door - I missed two pairs, but one pair had fallen down.
Prisoner's Defence. I found them on the pavement, more than two feet from the house.
GEORGE EAST. I took them from under his jacket, and he said that was just what he wanted; he wanted to go to prison.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 37. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
