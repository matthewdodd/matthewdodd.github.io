





206. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     BIRD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of December
, 1 gown, value 5s, the goods of 
                     
                        Elizabeth 
                        Smith
                     




                  , from her person
            .


                  ELIZABETH 
                  SMITH
               



            . I am single
. On the 3d of December I was going from town to Kensington


, and had a gown in a parcel in my hand - I was on the other side the turnpike at a quarter before ten o'clock at night; the prisoner came and snatched it from me with violence and ran off - I never lost sight of him; I am sure he is the person.


                  JAMES 
                  LARTER
               



            . I saw this lady crying Stop thief! the prisoner was coming down the foot-path - be broke into the road, threw down a parcel, and ran towardstown; I sprung my rattle - he was stopped by three of the Royal Horse Guards; I took him from them.


                  GEORGE 
                  LIDDELL
               



            . I am a constable. I bring this parcel, which was brought to the watch-house.

CALEB-HALL
            . I was returning from town, I heard a female voice cry Stop thief! I turned and pursued the prisoner - the two Horse Guards had got him; I took and delivered him to the watchman - I did not see him throw any thing down.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
