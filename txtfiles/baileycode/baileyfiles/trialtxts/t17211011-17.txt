







                  John 
                  Mitchell , alias 
Mitcheller




            , of St. James at Clerkenwell


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2 Feather  Bolsters value 2 s. and one pair of Sheets value 6 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Kendrick 
                  




                
            , on the 2d of this Instant October
. It appeared that the Prisoner lodged at the Prosecutor's, took the Watchman(Atlee) about 4 in the Morning, to whom he at last confest that he had stole them from the Prosecutor; who being sent for came and owned them. The Prisoner on his Trial said, a Man gave him 6 d. to carry them into Southwark for him. The Jury considering the matter, found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
