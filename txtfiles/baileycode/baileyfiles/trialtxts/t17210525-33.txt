







                     Cuthbert 
                     Wharton 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary in White Chapel


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Linnen Handkerchief value 4 d. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Bell 
                        



                      
                  , from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Martha 
                           Bell 
                        



, on the 1st of this Instant May
, 
               
                  Martha 
                  Bell 
               



             deposed, that she lost her Handkerchief about 7 in the Evening; but did not know it till a Woman call'd to her. 
               
                  Elizabeth 
                  Price 
               



             deposed, that she saw the Prisoner take the Handkerchief out of the Prosecutor's Pocket, and call'd to her before he had got it quite out (he being some time about it) that the Prosecutor's Petticoat was over her Head (it Raining) and the Prisoner lifted up-her Pocket with one Hand, and pickt it with the other. The Jury found him 

               Guilty
