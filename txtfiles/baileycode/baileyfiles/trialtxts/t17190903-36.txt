







                     Martha 
                     Gray 
                  



               
             of St. Anns Westminster


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Quarter-Guinea, a Silver Bodkin, a Necklace, a Shift, a Napkin,&c.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Pretty 
                  




                
             on the 1st of August
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's Servant
, and had liv'd there a Fortnight when she committed the Fact, which was fully prov'd upon her. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
