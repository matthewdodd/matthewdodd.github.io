





628. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     GREGORY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 27th of September
 last, two live pigs, value 3 l.
             the property of 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Hodges
               




            .
The prisoner was taken with the pigs.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
