





115. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     DAVEY
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he on the 18th of December
, without lawful excuse had in his custody and possession a forged bank note for the payment of two pounds
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded.




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
