





388. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     WILLIAMSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a leaden pipe with a brass cock, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Margaret 
                     Chapman
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was found with the property upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
