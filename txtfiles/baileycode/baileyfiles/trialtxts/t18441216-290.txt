




290. 




WILLIAM DOWNES
 and 




CHARLES HEATH
 were indicted for

stealing, on the 
13th of Dec., 2 lamps, value 2l. 10s., the goods of 



George Clark; and that Heath had been before convicted of felony; to which


DOWNES pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 21.—



Confined Four Months.


HEATH pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 31.—


Transported for Seven Years
.
