





359. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     ANDERSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th day of April
, one man's linen frock, called a smock frock, value 3 s. a woollen jacket, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Edwards
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN
                   EDWARDS
               



             sworn.
On the 17th of this month, between six and seven in the evening, I was informed my door was robbed; I live in Barbican


; the things were hung at the outside of the door; I am a salesman
.


                  GEORGE
                   EDWARDS
               



             sworn.
On Saturday last I was in the shop, and I thought I saw the things at the door move; I came to the door directly, and a woman informed me we were robbed.


                  MARY
                   HARGEST
               



             sworn.
I saw a man snatch the things, and go off with them.
(
               
                  John
                   Negus
               



             the Constable produced the things.)
Prisoner. I am innocent.
The prisoner called one witness to his character.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
