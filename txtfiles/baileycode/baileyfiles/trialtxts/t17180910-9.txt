                      Hawkins  alias 
                     
Dyton

               Feloniously stealing several pieces of small Linnen value 6 s.

                  Elizabeth 
                  Humphreys 
               



             the 16th of July
 last. The Prosecutor, deposed she employ'd the Prisoner to help her to wash, and she stole the Linnen which was found upon her, the Prisoner having nothing to say in her defence the Jury, found her
