





Second London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1561. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PIKE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of June
, 31 yards of printed cotton, value 28s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Francis 
                           Edwards
                        




                     
                  ; and that he had been before convicted of felony
            . - To which he pleaded.




               GUILTY
            . Aged 44. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
