





244. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     TAWELL
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that he, on the 28th of January
, feloniously, and without lawfulexcuse had in his custody and possession a forged bank note, for the payment of 10 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
