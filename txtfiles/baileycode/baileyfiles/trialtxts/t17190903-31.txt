







                     Mary 
                     Dowlas 
                  



               
            , of St. Paul's Shadwell


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Callicoe Gown value 20 s in the House of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Bampton 
                        




 , on the 22d of July
 last. 
               
                  Elizabeth 
                  Bampton 
               



             deposed that the Prisoner got privately into her Chamber, and took the Gown out of her Chest; that she meeting her coming down Stairs with it, had her before a Justice, where she confess'd the Fact. The Gown was found under her Petticoats. The Jury found her 

               Guilty
             of Felony.
