





51. 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Dunstan 
                  



               
             , was indicted for 


               stealing 2 Guineas, the Money of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Knowles 
                        




                      
                  , in his Dwelling-house
            , Oct 17
 .
Mr Knowles. The Prisoner was my Servant
. On the 17th of Oct. she was sent up Stairs by my Wife, and when she came down again I missed my Money; she went out of Doors, and I took her in Chick Lane, with a Guinea and half in her Pocket, which she confessed was part of my Money.




               Guilty, 4 s. 10 d.
