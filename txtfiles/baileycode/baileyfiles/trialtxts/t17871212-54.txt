





59. 
               
                  
                     SUSANNAH 
                     PICKET
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of November
 last, a black silk cloak, value 15 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Eleanor 
                     Farey
                  




               
            .
The prisoner came to the prosecutor's shop, and as soon as she was gone he missed the cloak, which the prisoner afterwardsoffered for sale at a Mrs. Davidson's, a pawnbroker's.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
