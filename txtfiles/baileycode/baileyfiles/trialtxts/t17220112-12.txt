







                     James 
                     Gardiner
                  



               
             of St. Mildred Poultry


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 6 Guineas
            , on the 30th of December
 last. It appear'd that the Prosecutor lodg'd in the House where the prisoner was an Apprentice
, and when he was asleep, the prisoner took the Breeches from under his Head, and pickt his Pocket; and afterwards confest it, and return'd 4 of the Guineas. The prisoner said, that he went into Bird Cage Alley, in the Mint, with 1 s. and won 5 l. but no body proving it, the Jury found him 

               guilty of the Felony
            .
