





240. 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Thompson 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing twelve shillings
            , the property of 
               
                  John 
                  Burt 
               




            , March 8
.
The Prisoner is between eleven and twelve years of age, and had lived about three weeks with Mr. Burt. who keeps a publick house
. He owned that he lifted up the sash of a back window of a lower room about four in the afternoon, got a knife, and broke open a lock of a chest of drawers, and took out twelve shillings, and had laid part of it out in shoes and stockings.



               Guilty
            .
