





306. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     SMITH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of April
, two linen shirts, value 3 s. two linen neck handkerchiefs, value 6 d. three pair of cotton stockings, value 1 s. one linen waistcoat, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Landame
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the things upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   HOTHAM
               



            .
