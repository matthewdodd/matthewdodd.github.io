





669. 
               
                  
                     MARIA 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of March
, four yards of linen, value 8 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Riley
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  SCOTCHFORD VINER
               



            . I am apprentice to 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Riley
               



            , who is a linen-draper
, and lives in Oxford-street


. On the 23d of March the prisoner came and looked at some cotton stockings, she did not like them; and as she was going out I saw the linen under her arm. I called to her, she ran out, I pursued, secured, and took it from her.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 25.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
