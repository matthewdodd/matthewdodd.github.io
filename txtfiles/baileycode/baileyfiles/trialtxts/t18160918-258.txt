





1078.
               
                  THOMAS 
                  HOLMES
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of September
, one hundred pounds weight of lead, value 1l.
             the property of our Lord 
               the King




            .


                  JOHN 
                  JAMES
               



            . I am a carpenter, employed about the building of Sommerset House


. This lead was lost on the night of the 7th of September. In consequence of information, I found a quantity of it to the amount of sixty-nine pounds weight in the prisoner's boat, and I found chips of lead also. We apprehended the prisoner about two o'clock on Wednesday morning going into his own house in Eaglecourt.



               GUILTY
            , aged 66.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
