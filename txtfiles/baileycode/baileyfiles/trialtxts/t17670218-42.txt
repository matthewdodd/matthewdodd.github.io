





176. (L.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Owen 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a hand saw, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Bowlder 
                  




                
            , Feb. 16
. +


                  William 
                  Bowlder 
               



            . I am a carpenter
, and live in Black-friars; I lost a hand saw last Monday; being informed of it, I went out, and overtook the prisoner on Ludgate-hill. with it upon him; (produced and deposed to) he said before Sir 
               
                  Matthew 
                  Brakiston 
               



            , that he found it at my door.
Prisoner's defence.
I found it at a distance from his door. I am an harness-maker
 by trade.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
