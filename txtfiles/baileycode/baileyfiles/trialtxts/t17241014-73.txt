








                     John 
                     Lowen 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Mary le Bow in the Ward of Cheap


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief, value 2 s. 6. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Nicholas 
                           Bland 
                        





 the 19th of October

 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that as the Prosecutor was going along Cheapside



 he lost his Handkerchief, and the Prisoner being Suspected was apprehended, having that Handkerchief in his Hand, and 6 more in his Pocket. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
He was also try'd a second Time, for 


               stealing 4 Handkerchiefs from Persons unknown
            : But of this he was 

               acquitted
            .
