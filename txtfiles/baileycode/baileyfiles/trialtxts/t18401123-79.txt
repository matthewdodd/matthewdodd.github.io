



79. 




WILLIAM RUTTER
. jun. was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
4th of November, 1 tea-caddy, value 1l.; 1 watch-guard, value 5s.; 3 thimbles, value 3s.; 2 pairs of ear-rings, value 10s.; 1 sovereign, 2 half-sovereigns, 1 crown, 14d., 13d., 12d., and 1d.; the property of 



William Rutter the elder; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 13.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Parkhurst.
