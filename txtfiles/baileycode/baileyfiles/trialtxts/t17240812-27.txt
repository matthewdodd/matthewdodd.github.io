







                     Robert 
                     Cheeseman 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. John Baptist


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Pocket, &c. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Dorothy 
                           Horn 
                        




, the 11th of July
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, That going along Dowgate Hill


, she had her Pocket pull'd of, she cry'd out, and the Prisoner was pursu'd and taken. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
