







                     Sarah 
                     Osborn 
                  



               
            , of St. Ethelberg


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing out of the House of 
                     
                        John 
                        Hall 
                     




                   , 4 l. 9 s. and 6 d
            . The prisoner was a Servant
 in the House, and Money being often mist, she was tax'd with it, and confest, that at Green Goose Fair she met 
               
                  Hannah 
                  Brown 
               



            , an old Playfellow of hers, who lives in Long Alley, Moorfields: This Brown gave her a Picklock Key, and persuaded her to rob her Mistress, which she did at several times, and gave it all to Brown, who promised to keep her when she was out of Place, and in Necessity. 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s
