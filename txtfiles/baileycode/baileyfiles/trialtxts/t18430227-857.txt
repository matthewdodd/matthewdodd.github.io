



857. 




JOHN LEWIS
 was indicted

 for burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling house of 



John Andrews, after the hour of nine in the night of the 
4th of Feb., at 
St. John the Evangelist, Westminster, with intent to steal, and stealing therein 1 boot, value 6d., his property; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. * Aged 15.—


Transported for Ten Years.—Convict Ship.
