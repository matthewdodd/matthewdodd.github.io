



1741. 




HENRY SERGEANT
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
11th of February, at 
St. Bartholomew-the-Great, 1 bag, value 1d.; 100 sovereigna, 25 half-sovereigns, 1 £40 Bank-note, and 1 order for the payment of 14l. 19s., the goods and monies of 



James Houghton, his master, in his dwelling-house; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported Life.
