





1357. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     LEWIS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of July
, 1 waistcoat, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Atkinson
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  ATKINSON
               



            . I live at No. 32, Barbican


; I am a hosier and general dealer
. On the 30th of July, I heard a sudden jerk; I looked at my door, and missed a waistcoat which had been inside - I saw the prisoner cross the street; I sent a boy after him, and saw them scuffling; I went and took the prisoner, and found this waistcoat about two yards from him, it is mine - my boy is gone away.
Prisoner. I saw the waistcoat by a pump, I took it up, and his boy came and took me - he asked what I had got; I thought he was a strange boy and I hit him; he was going to hit me, and the waistcoat fell from my jacket.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
