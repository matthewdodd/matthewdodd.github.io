





360. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PALMER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of April
, thirty-one pounds weight of lead, value 10 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Reynolds
                        




                     
                  , affixed to a building of his against the statute
            .


                  William
                   Camell
               



             caught the prisoner twisting off a lead pipe which was in the kitchen; he had one piece twisted off, which lay by him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
