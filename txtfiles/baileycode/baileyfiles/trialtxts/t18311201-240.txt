





240. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     YOUNG
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of November
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s. the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Charles 
                           Fentum
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  FENTUM
               



            . On the 9th of November I was on Westminster-bridge


 - I did not feel any thing taken, but the officer spoke to me; I missed my handkerchief, which the officer had.


                  JAMES 
                  WILD
               



            . I am a Police-officer. I had watched the prisoner for nearly half an hour - there were one or two more with him; they were trying several gentlemen's pockets - I saw the prisoner take this handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket; I seized him, and took it from him, the other got away.
Prisoner. Q. Did you see me take it from the prosecutor's pocket? A. Yes, and I took it from your waistcoat.(Property produced and Sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
