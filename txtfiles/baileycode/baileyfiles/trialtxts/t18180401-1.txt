





529. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     ROBERTS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of March
, two coats, value 16s., and one pair of pantaloons, value 8s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Francois 
                     Marquis de Borbon Conty





               
            .
To which indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
