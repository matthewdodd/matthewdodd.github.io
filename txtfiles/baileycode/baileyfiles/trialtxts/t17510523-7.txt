





357. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Beard 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing one cloth jacket, one pair of canvass trowsers, one linen shirt, one canvass bag, the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Richardson 
                        



                      
                  ; five shirts, three pair of stockings, one hat, one wig, one silk handkerchief, two pillowbears
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Farley 
                  



                
            , April 28
 ++.


                  Henry 
                  Richardson 
               



             and 
               
                  David 
                  Farley 
               



             were two sailors

 belonging to the ship call'd the Friends Glory, a New-Castle collier, lying near Execution-dock


; they missed the things mentioned taken out of their chests; they were told the prisoner had been on board; he had formerly sailed on board the same ship; they went and found him, and be confess'd he had taken the things, and directed them to them in an empty house where they found them. Some of them were produced in court and deposed to.



               Guilty
            .
