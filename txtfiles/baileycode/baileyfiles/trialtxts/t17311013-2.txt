






C- K-

            , of St. Mary le Bow


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 19 Yards of Linen, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Allen 
                           Brent
                        




, the 11th of September
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, That the piece of Linen hung out of the Prosecutor's Shop-window, but being taken away, notice was given, and she being follow'd by 
               
                  Joshua 
                  Snowden
               



            , was taken near Bow-Church, having the Linen under her Apron.
The Prisoner pleaded, That she found it lying on the Ground; the Jury not giving Credit to this Plea of hers, and the Fact being fully prov'd upon the Prisoner, the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
