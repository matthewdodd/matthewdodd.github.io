





177. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     STANWAY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of November
, 8ozs. of segars, value 12s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Burton
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  BURTON
               



            . I keep a segar-shop
, in Great Ormond-street


. On the 22nd of November I was told these cigars were taken, by a person in the next shop - I followed, and took the prisoner with the box in his hand, about one hundred yards off - I believe he had been in the shop one night before, and we lost a box then.


                  JOHN 
                  MILLS
               



             (Police-constable F 86.) I took the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence. A gentleman gave them to me, and said he would give me 2d.



               GUILTY
            *. Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
