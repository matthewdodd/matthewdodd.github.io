





939. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  LITTLEWOOD
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of March
, 4 doors, value 20s., the goods of 
                     
                        James 
                        Stedman
                     




                  , and fixed to a certain building of his, against the Statute
            .
JAMES STEDMAN. I have a house in St. Luke's


 - it was empty on the 21st of March; these doors were fixed in it; I have compared them - they fit exactly.


                  ROBERT 
                  LOCK
               



            . I am an officer. On the morning of the 21st of March I saw the prisoner in a stone passage in Chequer-alley, offering these four doors for sale; that was two or three hundred yards from the house they were taken from; he said a man gave them to him - he said he should not know the man, but that he had a fustian coat on, and white stockings.
Prisoner's Defence. A gentleman brought them to me, and said, "Will you just look after these till I go and get a customer," and he said he would give me a shilling.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
