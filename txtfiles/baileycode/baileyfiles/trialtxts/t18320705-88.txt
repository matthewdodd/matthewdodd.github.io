





1511. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     TUDDELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of June
, 10 yards of oil-cloth, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Hutton
                  




               
            .
RICHARD HUTTON. I live in Crawford-street


, and sell oil-cloth
. On the 9th of June I lost a piece, and, from information, I went about a mile, or a mile and a quarter, and found the prisoner carrying it on his shoulder - I took him with it - this is it - there were two others with him - he said one of them gave it to him to carry - they went away.
Prisoner's Defence. I pointed out the man who employed me to carry it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 28. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
