





1444. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     FAIR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of October
, two pieces of dimity, containing 60 yards, value 2l. 15s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Helps



            , 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Ray
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Helps
                  



               
            .
TIMOTHY ROE. I am clerk to Thomas Helps, 
               
                  James 
                  Helps
               



             and 
               
                  Richard 
                  Ray
               



            , who are warehousemen


, and live in Cheapside


. On the 17th of October, I was at the desk; I saw the prisoner come into the warehouse, take up two pieces of dimity, put them on his shoulder, and go out; I followed, and caught hold of him with the dimity on his shoulder. He could not see me.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. They laid on the ground - He took them up and put them on my shoulder. I never saw them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
