




293. 




HENRY MAYNARD
 and 




JOHN MAYNARD
 were indicted for

 stealing 4 handkerchiefs, value 2s., the goods of 



Harriet Finch; and that 



Henry Maynard had been previously convicted of felony.






HARRIET FINCH
. I am single, and am a milliner, and live in King-street, Hammersmith. On the 3rd of Dec, between three and four o'clock, I had some handkerchiefs safe—I missed them between seven and nine o'clock in the

evening—these produced are them—there were marks on them, but they have been taken off—the red one is damaged, which enables me to swear to it.





CHARLES HAMILTON
 (policeman.) On the 3rd of Dec, a little after six o'clock, I took the prisoners in charge for something else—I found three handkerchiefs on John, and this red one on Henry.





JAMES YAPP
. produce a certificate of the prisoner Henry's former conviction—(read—Convicted Oct., 845, and confined six months)—the prisoner Henry is the person.

H. MAYNARD—


GUILTY
.** Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.


J. MAYNARD—


GUILTY
. Aged 12.—



Confined Seven Days.
