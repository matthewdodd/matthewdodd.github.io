







                     Solomon 
                     Hargrave 
                  



               
             , was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief, value 18 d. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Tho.
                            Wardent
                        




, the 7th of January
. The Prosecutor depos'd, That walking near the Playhouse, he perceiv'd his Handkerchief to be taken out of his Pocket by the Prisoner; that thereupon he apprehended him, and took the Handkerchief upon him. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
