





765. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     LEVY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Williams
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
JOHN WILLIAMS. I live at Crayford. On Saturday night, the 19th of April, I was in London - I did not see the prisoner till the officer came to me with this handkerchief, which is mine - I had had it safe a few minutes before.


                  GEORGE 
                  KEMP
               



             (police-constable N 82.) I was on duty in Old-street-road


 - I saw the prisoner with another - I followed them about forty yards, and saw the prisoner take this handkerchief from the prosecutor, and thrust it into his pocket - I took him, and he threw it down.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
