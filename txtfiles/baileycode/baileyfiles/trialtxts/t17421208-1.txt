                     John 
                     Creighton 
                  



               
             , of St Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               stealing one Pair of Leather Pumps, Value 6 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Crouch 
                  




                
            , November 6th
.


                  John 
                  Crouch 
               



             . These are my Pumps; they hung at my Door, the Corner of Turnstile-Alley


 in Lincoln's-Inn-Fields : I was told, somebody had stole a Pair of Shoes; the Prisoner had got them under his Coat; I took them out of his Right Hand, and he fell down on his Knees and begged Pardon. 

               Guilty
            .
