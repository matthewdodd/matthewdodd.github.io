




2595. 




ROBERT BRANCH
 and 




WILLIAM REYNOLDS
 were indicted for

stealing, on the 
17th of October, 391bs. weight of lead, value 5s., the goods of 



Thomas Cook, and fixed to a certain building; to which


BRANCH* pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.

REYNOLDS pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 21.—



Confined Twelve Months.
