





366. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Andrew 
                     Duffey 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing three pair of leather shoes, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Davis 
                  




                
            , June 10
. *


                  Thomas 
                  Davis 
               



            . I was not at home the time the three pair of shoes were lost; coming home I was told I had been robbed, and the thief was then before Justice Welch; I went there and saw the shoes, and knew them to be my property.


                  John 
                  Fowler 
               



            . I am apprentice to the prosecutor; I was filling the water-tub, a young man came and old me a man had taken three pair of shoes; he and I run and found the prisoner in the back alley, I took the shoes from him, they were my master's property, taken from out of our window.
Prisoner's defence.
I was very much in liquor.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
