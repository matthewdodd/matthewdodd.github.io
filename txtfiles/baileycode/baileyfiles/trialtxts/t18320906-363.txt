





OLD COURT. FRIDAY, JULY 14.
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
2094. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     NEWHAM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of August
, 1 coat, value 2l.; 1 cap, value 3s.; 1 waistcoat, value 5s.; 4 sheets, value 5s.; 3 razors, value 2s.; 1 handkerchief, value 1s., and 2 pairs of stockings, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Roberts
                  




               
            . - To which he pleaded.




               GUILTY
            . Aged 30. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
