





419. 
               
                  
                     ANTHONY 
                     PERRIGALL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of March
, one handkerchief, value 2 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Pryer
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  PRYER
               



            . I live in Fenchurch-street. On the 8th of March I was in Guildhall-yard


, returning from the hall at the election time. I was hustled by a number of persons - the prisoner was close behind me, and on looking round I saw my handkerchief in his breast. I secured him, with assistance, and he let it fall.


                  JOHN 
                  MARKS
               



            . I am a shoemaker. I was in Guildhall-yard, and saw the prisoner hustling to get away from the gentleman. I caught hold of him, and saw the handkerchief drop from him. Mr. Pryer picked it up.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I picked it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Life
            .
London Jury, before Mr Common Sergeant.
