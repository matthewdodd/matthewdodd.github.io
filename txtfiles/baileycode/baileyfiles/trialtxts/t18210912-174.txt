





1223. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     JOHNSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 4th of August
, one copper, value 18 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Shelly
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  SMITH
               



            . I am foreman to Mr. Shelly. This copper stood at his door - I know it to be his.


                  JOHN 
                  BARNARD
               



            . I was left in care of the shop, and saw the prisoner two doors off with it on his back, I stopped him; he said a man gave him sixpence to carry it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was employed by another person to take it to Great Queen-street, and he would give me sixpence. I did not consider what I was doing.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 50.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
