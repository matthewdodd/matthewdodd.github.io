





25. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     WILCOX
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of October
, two chaise cushions, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     George
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  GEORGE
               



            . I am a coach-builder
, and live in London-wall


. On the 29th of October, between one and two o'clock the cushions were on the premises in a chaise.


                  JOHN 
                  GRIFFIN
               



            . I am servant to Mr. George. I was at work in the gateway, and went into a loft for something, I heard a noise, ran to the loft window, and saw the prisoner and another man going out of the gateway - the prisoner had a bag under his arm, I ran down and caught him about two hundred yards off, I asked what he had in the bag, he said, "What is that to you?" I shook the bag and one of the cushions fell out, the other was in the bag - they were in the chaise before.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was hired to carry them to Hounsditch, by a young lad for 1 s., I was to stop at the corner of Hounsditch for him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
