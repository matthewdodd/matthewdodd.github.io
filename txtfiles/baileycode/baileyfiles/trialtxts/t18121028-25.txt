





767. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     SMITH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 14th of September
, a wrapper, value 6 d. thirty-six pair of stockings, value 2 l. 17 s. 6 d. and thirty-six pair of stockings, value 24 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Sykes
                  




               
            , widow
.
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
