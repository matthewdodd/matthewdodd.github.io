






NEW COURT. Wednesday, September 10, 1834.
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1461. 
               
                  RICHARD 
                  HARTLEY
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of September

, 3 shillings; the monies of 
                     
                        George 
                        Walker
                     



                   and another, his masters
            ; also, for 


               stealing, on the 28th of August

, 1 snuff-box, value 2s. 6d.; and 1 silver medal, value 5s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Walker, jun.



            ; to which indictments he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
