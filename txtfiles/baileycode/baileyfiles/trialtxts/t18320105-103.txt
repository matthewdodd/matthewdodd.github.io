





397. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of December
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Marston
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
THOMAS MARSTON. I am a journeyman bookbinder
. I was on Holborn-hill


 on Sunday evening, the 11th of December, between eight and nine o'clock; I felt a tug at my pocket, and my handkerchief go; I turned and caught the prisoner's hand, with my handkerchief in it - he appeared to be alone; I took him to the watch-house; this is my handkerchief - my initials are on it.
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
