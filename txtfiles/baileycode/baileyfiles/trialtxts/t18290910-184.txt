





1714. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     DAVIS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of July
, 2 check braces, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles Lynes 
                     Stephens
                  




               
            , Esq
.

               pleaded GUILTY
