





753. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     BROWN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of March
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Jenkin Edward 
                           Tappenden
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
JENKIN EDWARD TAPPENDEN. I live in Milbank-street, Westminster. On the 29th of March, between three and four o'clock, I was in the New-road


; I missed my handkerchief, which I had safe a few minutes before - I had seen the prisoner repeatedly at my heels, and suspected him; I followed him, and gave him into custody; my handkerchief was found on him at the station.
DENNIS KEAYS (Police-constable D 113). I took the prisoner, and found this handkerchief on him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
