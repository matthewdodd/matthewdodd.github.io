





217. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     LAMBERT
                  




               
             (aged 14) was indicted for 


               burglariously and feloniously breaking and entering the dwelling house of 
                     
                        
                           Samuel 
                           Swain
                        




                     
                  , about the hour of six in the night, on the 20th day of December
 last, and burglariously stealing therein, two geese, value 7 s. his property
            .
The prosecutor heard his shop window lift up, and missed two geese, which lay there just before; but the window was down as he left it, and 
               
                  Daniel
                   Stone
               



             took the prisoner with the geese.




               GUILTY Of stealing, but not of the burglary
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
