





1322. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     HOLLAND
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of May
, 1 pewter pot, value 1s. 3d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Payne
                  




               
            .

THOMAS PAYNE. I am a publican
, and live in Shoreditch. The prisoner was brought to my house on the 23d of May; I found my quart pot concealed in his basket.
HENRY STEBBING. I saw the prisoner take this pot from a butcher's steps; he put a cloth over it, and put it into his basket - I took him with it.
GEORGE SMITH. I am an officer, and took the prisoner - he has been one of the paupers of our parish.
Prisoner's Defence. I took it through distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 51.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
