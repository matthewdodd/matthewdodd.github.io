





599. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SOPER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of March
, 39 yards of sail-cloth, value 30s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Taylor
                  




               
            .



                  GEORGE 
                  TAYLOR
               



            . I am a sailcloth-factor
, and live in Fenchurch-street


. On the 13th of March, about eleven o'clock in the morning, I lost the cloth.


                  JOHN 
                  COOPER
               



            . I am Mr. Taylor's porter. On the 13th of March, I saw the prisoner come into the warehouse, lift the cloth on his shoulder, and walk out with it. I followed him about thirty yards, and secured him with it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. A gentleman came out, and gave it to me to carry.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            ,
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
