





1672. 
               
                  THOMAS JAMES 
                  DENKIN
               




             was indicted for 


               embezzling the sums of 90l. 4s. 2d. - 3l. 3s. 9d. - 9l. 8s. 6d., and 120l.
            , which he had received on account of 
               
                  
                     Nicholas 
                     Denny
                  



               
            , Esq.
, his master.
SEVERAL OTHER COUNTS, describing it to have been received on account of the 
               
West of England Fire and Life Insurance Company



            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 34. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
