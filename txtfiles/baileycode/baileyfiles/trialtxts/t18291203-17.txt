





17. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE ALBERT 
                     BENNETT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of November
, 17 yards of merino, value 26s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Leigh
               




            .
FREDERICK GRAY. On the 7th of November, about five o'clock in the afternoon, I saw the prisoner take a piece of merino from a pile inside the door of Mr. Leigh, a linen-draper
, of Bishopsgate-street


 - I am his shopman; he ran away; I followed, and took him about a hundred yards off with it.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
