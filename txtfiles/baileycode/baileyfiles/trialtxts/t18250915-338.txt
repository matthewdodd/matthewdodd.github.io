





1563. 
               
                  ELIZABETH 
                  BLACKLOCK
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of July
, three pairs of sheets, value 34 s.; four handkerchiefs, value 14 s.; three shifts, value 10 s.; three shirts, value 14 s; two towels, value 1 s. 6 d.; a napkin, value 1 s.; a night-gown, value 1 s.; five tablecloths value 18 s., and a gown, value 8 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Frederick 
                  Boulton
               




            .


                  HARRIET 
                  BOULTON
               



            . I am the wife of Frederick Boulton, a publican
, who lives at Whitefriars' New-wharf


. The prisoner lived with me as a servant
. I missed some articles on the 17th of July, and sent for an officer.


                  SAMUEL 
                  BEAVAN
               



            . I am a constable of Whitefriars. I was sent for, and found several duplicates upon the prisoner.


                  JOHN 
                  FLOWERS
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. Cotterel, pawnbroker, of Shoe-lane. I have several articles of table linen, some sheets, gowns, and shirts, pawned by the prisoner, at different times.


                  JOSHUA 
                  MORTIMER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Fleet-market. I have eleven separate articles, partly pawned by the prisoner.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HOLDING
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Fleet-market. I have a handkerchief, pawned for 3 s., by a person in the name of 
               
                  Ann 
                  Smith
               



            . I am not certain whether it was by the prisoner.


                  THOMAS 
                  LINSCOT
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a handkerchief, pawned on the 11th of July, in the name of 
               
                  Ann 
                  Hawkins
               



            .
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 42.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
