





145. 
               
                  PHILIP 
                  CHAMBERS
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of March
, a table, value 20 s. the goods of 
                     
                        Paul 
                        Mullett
                     




                  , his master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .
