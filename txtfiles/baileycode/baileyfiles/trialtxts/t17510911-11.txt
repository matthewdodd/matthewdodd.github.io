                     Richard 
                     Dempsey 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing 2 cloth coats, value 2 l. 2 s. the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Cook 
                        




                      
                  , in the dwelling house of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Faulkner 
                     



, August 10
.* 
Richard Cook. I live at Chelsea


, at the house of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Faulkner 
               



            ; I miss'd these 2 coats the 11 of August, from out of a 2 pair of stairs room. I advertis'd them; the man that bought them came to me, and told me of them. The prisoner was taken up on the 14. he had lain with me 3 weeks before; he confess'd the fact and fell down on his knees.


                  Simon 
                  Cherry 
               



            . I am a salesman, I bought the coats of the prisoner at the bar, on the 13 of August: he produced them; the prosecutor depos'd to them as his property.




               Guilty 39 s.
