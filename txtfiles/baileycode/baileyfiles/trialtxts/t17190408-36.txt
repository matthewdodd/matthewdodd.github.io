







                     Grace 
                     Rodgers 
                  



               
             of St. James Clerkenwell


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Gown and two Petticoates
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Timothy 
                     Gregory 
                  




                
             the 5th of January
 last. Mrs. Gregory deposed that she kept Hungerford Market


, and that while she was at Market she lost her Goods; that the Prisoner, who was her Servant
, was taken that very Morning and had the two Petticoats on. The Prisoner in her Defence said, that her Mistress gave her leave to put on the Clothes at any time. 
               
                  Richard 
                  Ashton 
               



             deposed he knew her a Servant at the Bear Tavern in the Butcher-Row near Temple-Bar, where she was trusted with Plate and Goods of Value, and behaved her self very honestly. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
