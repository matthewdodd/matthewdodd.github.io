







                     Susannah 
                     Creamer 
                  



               
             of Edminton


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Flaxen Pillowbear, 4 Aprons, 1 Neckcloth, and a Silk Handkerchief
            , on the 5th of March
 last. 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Taylor 
                  




                
             deposed that the Prisoner was his Servant
 and went away; that he miss his things and found them upon her; that she confest the Fact before the Justice; which Confession was read in Court. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
