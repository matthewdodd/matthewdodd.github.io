





First London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
1744. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23rd of July
, 1 umbrella, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Benjamin 
                     Tomkins
                  




               
            . - To which he pleaded



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30. -
