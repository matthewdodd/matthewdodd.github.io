





714. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     MOORE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of April
, 1 pair of trousers, value 6s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Goften
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  PICKETT
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  William 
                  Gofton
               



            , of Gilbert-street


. On the 6th of 'April the prisoner came to his shop and asked the price of a gown; as she went away from the door I saw her take a pair of trousers and put them under her arm - I went and took her with them; I had seen her the day before.


                  MICHAEL 
                  HOLDEN
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner - she had no money about her.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was looking at a gown at the door; a woman said, "Hold up your lap" - she put these trousers into it; I hardly knew what I was doing; when young man took me I had some money.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
