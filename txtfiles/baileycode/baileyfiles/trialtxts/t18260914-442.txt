





1754. 
               
                  HANNAH 
                  MURPHY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of August
, 4 silk handkerchiefs, value 12s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Palmer
               




            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  WRIGHT
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. Wm. Palmer, hosier
, Ludgate-hill


. At three o'clock in the afternoon of the 24th of August, I saw the prisoner lurking about the shop; I watched her for an hour and a half, saw her unpin the handkerchiefs, and take them away; I followed, brought her back, and took them from under her apron, between her and a child, which was in her arms.


                  JOHN 
                  BROWN
               



            . I am an officer. I took her in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
