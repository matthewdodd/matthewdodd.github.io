





444. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 15th of April
, a jacket, value 5 s. and a pair of trowsers, value 6 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Cutler
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  SIDEY
               



            . Q. You searched the woman what did you find upon her - A. I searched the prisoner, I found in her apron before her this jacket and trowsers, they were claimed by Mr. Cutler. I also found in her apron an old bed gown and a pelliese.
MRS. CUTLER. On the 15th of April, about six o'clock in the evening, I missed my little boy, he was stripped. I live upon Saffron-hill, I am sure the jacket and trowsers are my property.



               GUILTY
             aged 45.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
