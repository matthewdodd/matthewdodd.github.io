





592. 
               
                  
                     LAWRENCE 
                     WILSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th day of September
, a pair of leather shoes, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Murehead
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the shoes immediately.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
