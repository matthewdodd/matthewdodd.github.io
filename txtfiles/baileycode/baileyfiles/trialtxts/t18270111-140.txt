





THIRD DAY. SATURDAY, JANUARY 13, 1827.
Third Middlesex Jury - Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
417. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     MARTIN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of January
, 1 rummer glass, value 10d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Ralph 
                           Horner
                        



                     
                  , and 1 other rummer-glass, value 10d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Greyatt
                  



               
            .




               The prisoner pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 59.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
