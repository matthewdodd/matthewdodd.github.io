





288. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     YORKE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of May
, four pair of plated buckles, and one pair of black studded buckles, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Davies
                  




               
            .
The prisoner pawned the buckles, which he had from the prosecutor to polish.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. COMMON SERJEANT.
