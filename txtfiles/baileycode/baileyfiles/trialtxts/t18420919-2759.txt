



2759. 




ELIZABETH NORRIS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
12th of August, 20 yards of flannel, value 20s. the goods of 



Henry Batchelor and another.






HENRY BATCHELOR
. I am a linen-draper, and live in Wellington-street, Southwark, I have one partner. On the 12th of August I saw this flannel in the lobby of my shop, about nine o'clock in the morning—when I retorned home at two I missed it—this now proeuced is it.





CHARLES CHAMBERS
 (City police-constable, No. 523.) I saw the prisoner about a quarter before two on the 12th of August, going up Water-lane, Lower Thames-street, with something bulky under her shawl—I

found it was this flannel—she said she had found it near Shoreditch church.

Prisoner's Defence. A woman asked me to carry it for her, and said she would give me 6d.; I had it about a quarter of an hour when the policeman came.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.
