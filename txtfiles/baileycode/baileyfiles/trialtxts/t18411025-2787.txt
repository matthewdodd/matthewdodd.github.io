



2787. 




THOMAS LEWIS
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
21st of September, 1 sheet, value 6s.; 2 towels, value 1s.; and 1 blanket, value 2s.; the goods of 



Henry Jaggs.






HARRIETT LONG
. I live with my brother, Henry Jaggs, at Lambeth. On the 20th of September we had a sheet, and these other articles, safe in the wash-house, and the next morning they were missing—this now produced is the sheet—it is my brother's.





WILLIAM MURRAY
. On the 21st of October, at half-past five o'clock in the morning, I traced the prisoner across the walls, and saw him drop this sheet—my sergeant picked it up.




GUILTY
.* Aged 19—


Transported for Seven Years.
