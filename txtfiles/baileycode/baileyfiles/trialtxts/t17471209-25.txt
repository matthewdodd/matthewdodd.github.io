





30. 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Fitzsimons 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing three pounds weight of sugar, value 18 d. the goods of persons unknown
            , Dec. 3
.


                  Philip 
                  Breading 
               



            . The Prisoner was a labourer
 on board of my ship that was laden from Jamaica with rum and sugar, I helpt to take the sugar out of the lining of his breeches.
Q. What did the Prisoner say?
Breading. He did not think any harm in it; the same day I cut his pocket off with a pound of sugar.


                  Thomas 
                  Wesbrook 
               



            . The 4th of December I detected him in the morning with stuffing a lump of sugar in his pocket, after that the Captain called them all down to the hold, to work upon logwood, being too wet that day to work the sugar, and I found three pounds and a half upon him at four o'clock.




               Guilty 10 d.
