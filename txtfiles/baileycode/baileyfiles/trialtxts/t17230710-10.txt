







                     Elizabeth 
                     Beby 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               stealing an Apron, a Coral Necklace, and other Goods, value 10 s.
             the 2d of July
. The Prisoner own'd the taking of the Things, but said she was in Drink, and wanted Mony, and therefore took them. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
