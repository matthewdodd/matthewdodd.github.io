





Before Mr. Recorder.
1324. 
               
                  GEORGE 
                  SMITH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of May
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Thompson
                     




                  , from his person
            .
WILLIAM THOMPSON. I live in Gloucester-buildings, Walworth. On the 25th of May, at a quarter-past four o'clock, I was in Watling-street


, and thought I felt something at my pocket - I turned round, and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand, concealing it under his jacket - I took it from him, and gave him in charge - I saw nobody with him.


                  JOHN 
                  JACKSON
               



            . I am a constable, and received him in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. A lad gave it into my hand.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
