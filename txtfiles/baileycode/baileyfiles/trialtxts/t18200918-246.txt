





1145. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     CAMPBELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of August
, 14 lbs. of bacon, value 7 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Gammage
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  GAMMAGE
               



            . I am a cheesmonger
, and live in Great Earl-street, Seven Dials


. On the 2d of August, about eight o'clock in the evening, Sweeney alarmed me; I ran out, and secured the prisoner with the bacon, which she had taken from the window.


                  DENNIS 
                  SWEENY
               



            . I live next door to Mr. Gammage. I saw the prisoner take the bacon, and told him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 48.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
