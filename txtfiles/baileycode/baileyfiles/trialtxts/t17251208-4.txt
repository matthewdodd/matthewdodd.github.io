            ; were indicted for 


               stealing a Canvas Bag, with Money in it
            , on the 22d of November
 last. It appeared that the Prosecutor fell in Company with the two Prisoners, and lost his Money, that he made enquiry after it before he went away, and call'd a Constable who search'd the Prisoners, and found nothing upon them, only the Bag was found empty under Dunster's Chair. The Prisoners confest that they were in the Prosecutors Company, but deny'd that they took his Money. 


               Guilty. 39 Shillings
            .
