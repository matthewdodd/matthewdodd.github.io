





719. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     OSBORNE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of March
, a handkerchief, value 3 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John Hartwell Bournel 
                  Williams
               




            .
JOHN HARTWELL BOURNELL WILLIAMS. On the evening of the 7th of March, I was in Aylesbury-street


, and felt somebody fumbling at my pocket, and on turning round, I saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand, it was safe a few minutes before - he dropped it; I picked it up and secured him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  JOHN 
                  HAYFORD
               



            . I took the prisoner in charge.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
