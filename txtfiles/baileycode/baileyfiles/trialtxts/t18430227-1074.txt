



1074. 



WILLIAM WRIGHT
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
28th of June, at 
Plumstead, 1 watch, value 3l.; 1 bag, 1d.; 16 half-crowns, 100 shillings, 60 sixpences, and 30 groats, the property of 



Jonathan Brazefield, in his dwelling-house:—also on the 
27th of June, 1 watch, 4l.; 1 seal, 7s.; 1 watch-key, 2s. 6d.; and 1 watch-ribbon, 1d.; the goods of 



Jonathan Brazefield; to both which he pleaded





GUILTY
.—


Transported for Ten Years.
