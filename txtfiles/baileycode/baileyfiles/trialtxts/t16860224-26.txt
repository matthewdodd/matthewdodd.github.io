








                     Thomas 
                     Smith
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     Morrice 
                     Archer
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Bennedict



, indicted for 


               stealing one silver Tankard, value 7 l.
             on the 17th. of January
, from one 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Owen
                  




               
            ; it was proved on the Tryal, that the Prisoner Smith confessed that Morrice Archer stole the Tankard from the Bar; and on the contrary Archer impeach'd Smith, saying, he took it from the Bar; but the Prisoner Smith excused himself at the Bar and Archer acknowledging it, they were found 

               Guilty
            .
