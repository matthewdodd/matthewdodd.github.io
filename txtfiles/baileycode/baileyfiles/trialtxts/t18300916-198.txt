





1662. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     HARBROE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of April
, 1 pair of boots, value 18s.; 1 pair of shoes, value 9s.; 1 pair of trousers, value 6s.; 1 waistcoat, value 4s., and 2 coats, value 1l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Kemp
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
