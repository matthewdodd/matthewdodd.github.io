





459. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BARGIN
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 21st of February
, had in his custody and possession, a forged bank note, for the payment of two pounds, he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
