








                     Mary 
                     Palmer 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Daniel 
                     Watson 
                  



                
            . of St. Martin's Whitechapel



, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing  Yards of Cotten and Linnen Cheque, value 4 l. in Dwelling-House of
            

                     Thomas 
                     Fletcher 
                  




                
            , on the 21st of the Instant May
. It appear'd that Palmer was the Prosecutor's Servant
, and that Watson workt in his House that whilst the Prosecutor and his Wife were gone out Palmer took the Goods, which she confest, saying that Watson persuaded her to take them. Her Confession before the Justice was read in Court. But the Evidence not being sufficient against Watson, the Jury 

               Acquitted
             him, and found Mary Palmer 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s
            . 


               Transportation
            .
