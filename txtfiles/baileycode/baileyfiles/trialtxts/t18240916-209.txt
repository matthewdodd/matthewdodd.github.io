






1449. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     KENNEDY
                  




               
             was again indicted for a 


               like offence
            .


                     JOHN THOMAS 
                     HATHAWAY
                  




               
            . The prisoner never accounted for 25 l. 4 s. which he received from Mr. Mariner, on the 25th of February last.


                  CHRISTOPHER 
                  MARRINER
               



            . On the 25th of February last, I paid the prisoner 25 l. 4 s., partly in sovereigns - here is his bill and receipt, he wrote it and delivered it with his own hands.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
