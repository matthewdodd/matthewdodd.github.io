





604. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  McCORMICK
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of February
, 1 handkerchief, value 1s., the goods of a 
                     man unknown


                  , from his person
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  CORNISH
               



            . I am a baker, and live in Fenchurch-street


. On the 21st of February, I saw the prisoner pick a gentleman's pocket, opposite my house, of his handkerchief, which he put into his breast - I crossed over, and took him, and took the handkerchief from him - this is it - the gentleman went on, and I could not find him.
Prisoner's Defence. I saw a boy pick a gentleman's pocket; he threw the handkerchief down, and I took it up to give to the gentleman.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
