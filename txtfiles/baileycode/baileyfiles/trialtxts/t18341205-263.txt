



263. 




RICHARD COFIELD
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 3d of December, 1 handkerchief, value 1s. 6d., the goods, of 



Thomas Clements, from his person.






THOMAS CLEMENTS
. I live in Little Chapel-street, Wardour-streer. On the 3rd of December, I was in Ratcliffe-highway with a friend, in the middle of the street—the prisoner stepped on my heel—he begged my pardon, and said he was in a hurry—I let him pass, and when I got to the top of the street, some one touched me, and I missed my handkerchief—the prisoner was then at the top of the street—I said, "That man has robbed me"—I collared him, and he had it in his hand—he said he had picked it up.





CHARLES TICKLE
. I am an officer. I was called, and took the prisoner and the handkerchief.




GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Sixth Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
