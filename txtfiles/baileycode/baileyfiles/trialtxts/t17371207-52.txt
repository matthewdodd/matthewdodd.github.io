            , Nov. 29
.
Mortimer. I live in New-street, Old-Soho


, on the 22d of last Month I lost 4 l. out of a Drawer in my Shop, between 9 and 10 at Night.



                  Thomas 
                  Meighan 
               



            . The 2 Prisoners, and I with my Brother and 
               
                  Edward 
                  Johnson 
               



             committed this Robbery. I open'd the half Hatch and went in, and took out the Drawer, in which was between 3 and 4 l. then we went to an Ale-house in the Hay-Market, and parted the Money. Both Guilty,
