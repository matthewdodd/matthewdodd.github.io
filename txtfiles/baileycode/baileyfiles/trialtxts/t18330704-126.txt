






OLD COURT. Tuesday, July 8th.
First London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
1148. 
               
                  
                     BENJAMIN 
                     BAYLY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of May

, 60 handkerchiefs, value 6l. 10s.; 9 yards of lawn, value 20s.; 10 yards of Gingham, value 6s., and 25 yards of linen cloth, value 4l. 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Morley
                        



                     
                   and another, his masters
            ; 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     KENT
                  



               
            , (now wife
 to the said 
               
                  Benjamin 
                  Bayly
               



            ,) was indicted for 


               feloniously receiving the said goods, well knowing the same to be stolen
            , against the statute, &c.
The prisoner Bayly 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
No 

               evidence was offered against Kent
            . See page 623.
