



2625. 




RICHARD ADAMS
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
29th of May, 1 bed, value 3l., the goods of 



Joseph Skinner.






JOSEPH SKINNER
. I am an upholsterer, and live in Whitechapel-road The prisoner was frequently in my employ—I employed him to take a bed, on the 29th of May, to Mr. Gilmore, in the Tower, and one was to henturned, but he never brought it to me—the one he was to have brought was mine, and was worth 3l.—he was not found till the 11th of September, when a person met him in Clerkenwell, and gave him in charge.





SUSANNAH GILMORE
. I live with my husband in the Tower. On the 29th of May the prisoner brought a bed, and took another back instead of it to Mr. Skinner.




GUILTY* Aged 28.—Recommended to mercy,
—


Transported for Seven Years.
