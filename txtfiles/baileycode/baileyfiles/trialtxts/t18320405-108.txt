





OLD COURT. SATURDAY, APRIL 7.
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
904. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WELDON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of March
, 1 tub, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Potter
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  POTTER
               



            . I am the son of 
               
                  Edward 
                  Potter
               



            , a cooper
, of Ratcliff-highway


. On the 20th of March, about seven o'clock in the evening, I saw the prisoner run away with a tub; I told my father, who followed him, and took hold of the tub - the prisoner let go and ran off: I am sure of his person.


                  JOHN 
                  POTTER
               



            . My son called me; I followed, and overtook the prisoner, with the tub on his shoulder - he let go of it, and ran over the way; the Policeman took him in my sight; it was inside the shop.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
