





132. (M. 1st.) 
               
                  
                     Mary
                      Rawlinson
                  , otherwise 
Jones




            , was indicted for 


               stealing a stuff gown, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Jonathan
                      Cox
                  




               
            , December 6
. +


                  Hannah
                   Cox
               



            . I am wife to 
               
                  Jonathan
                   Cox
               



            . I lost my stuff gown on the 6th of December, and found it again at Mr. Wallis's, a pawnbroker in Houndsditch, the day after I lost it.


                  Esther
                   Wallis
               



            . I lent the prisoner four shillings upon this gown, the 6th of December. ( Produced and deposed to.)
Prisoner's Defence.
I met a woman that desired me to go and pawn this gown for her. She said, she was afraid if her husband saw her, he would use her ill. I pawned it for her.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
