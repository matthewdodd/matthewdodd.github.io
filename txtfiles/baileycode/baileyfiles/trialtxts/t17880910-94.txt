





593. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     OSBORNE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th day of July
 last, a man's hat, value 5 s. two linen aprons, value 1 s. the property of 
                     
                        Robert
                         Bullock
                     



                  , and another apron, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Bullock
                  




               
            .

               GUILTY
