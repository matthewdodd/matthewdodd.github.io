





67. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MARTIN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of November
 last, a live cock, value 18 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Jane 
                           Cox
                        




                     
                  ; and on the 28th of November, two live hens, value 2 s. her property; and on the 1st of December, a mahogany tea-chest, value 18 d. her property
            .




               GUILTY 10 d
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
