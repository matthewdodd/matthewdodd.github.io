





253. (M.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Stone 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a truss of hay, value 16 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Goodwin 
                  




                
            , March 23
. *


                  Thomas 
                  Goodwin 
               



            . The prisoner was my servant
; I am a carman
.


                  Samuel 
                  Bowyer 
               



            . I am a carman; on the 13th of March, between four and five in the morning, I came into the yard, and saw the prisoner come out of Mr. Goodwin's stables with a truss of hay; he carried it to Mr. Dix's Stable: he is a carman likewise; (he is run away).


                  Charles 
                  Haswell 
               



            . Mr. Goodwin gives me leave to lie in his hay-loft: I saw the prisoner carry a truss of hay to Dix's Stable in the morning; I don't know the time; I saw him do it two or three mornings.
Prisoner's Defence.
The man came to me about ten at night, and told me my master had given leave to send him a truss of hay.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
