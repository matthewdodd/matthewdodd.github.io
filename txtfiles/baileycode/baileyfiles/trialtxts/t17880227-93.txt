





233. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     ORMAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of February
, an iron grate, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Holmes
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY
                   BEACH
               



             sworn.
On Monday the 25th of February, I saw the prisoner take a grate from the prosecutor's door in Duck-lane, Soho


, he took it under his arm, and carried it into Berwick-street; I informed the prosecutor, and he followed him and took him.


                  THOMAS
                   HOLMES
               



             sworn.
I followed the prisoner down Berwick-street, and took him with the grate upon him.
Was it your grate? - Yes, I had repaired it myself.
(The prisoner did not say any thing in his defence.)



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
