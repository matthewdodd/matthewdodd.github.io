





800. (2d. M.) 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM
                      SPIERS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing four silver table spoons, value 30 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William
                      Francis
                  




               
            , Oct. 19
. +


                  William
                   Francis
               



             deposed, that the prisoner worked for him; that his house was broke open on the 19th of October, and the spoons mentioned in the indictment were stolen.
- Clark deposed, that when the prisoner was taken, be confessed that he had stole the spoons, and said his mother should restore them, which she did.
The prisoner said nothing in his defence.



               Guilty
            .
