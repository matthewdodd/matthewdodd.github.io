





642. 
               
                  MICHAL 
                  DONNELLY
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of October
, twenty-three pounds weight of lead, value 4 s. belonging to his Royal Highness


                        George Augustus Frederick 
                        Prince of Wales





                  , and affixed to his dwelling-house
            .
The prisoner was seen beating up the lead, and taken with it upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
