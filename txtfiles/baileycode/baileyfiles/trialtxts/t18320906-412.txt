





2143. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     SWEENY
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of August
, 1 gown, value 8s.; 1 cap, value 1s. 6d., and 1 handkerchief, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John Augustus Andrus 
                     Hoepfner
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZABETH 
                  HOEPFNER
               



            . I am the wife of John Augustus Andrus Hoepfner - he keeps the house No.35, Frith-street


; the prisoner was servant
 to my lodger. I lost my gown on the 2nd of August, and the cap about a week after.


                  JAMES 
                  WARD
               



            . I am a Police-constable. I found this gown on the prisoner on the 30th of August.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 26. - 


               Transported for Seven Years longer
            .
There was another indictment against the prisoner.
