





503. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PRITCHARD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of January
, 1 shirt, value 5s.; 1 coat, value 6s., and 15d. in copper monies
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Patrick 
                     Darragh
                  




               
            .


                  PATRICK 
                  DARRAGH
               



            . I live in Vere-street


, and am a labourer
. The prisoner was apprentice
 to Samuel Harrison, who lives in the same house - I missed a coat on the 30th of December last, and then I missed a shirt and 1s. 3d. - the prisoner was not lodging in the house at the time, but he was taken by Baker.


                  JOSEPH 
                  BAKER
               



            . I am a lock-smith, and lodge where the prosecutor does. I stopped the prisoner at the corner of Vere-street on the 30th of December - his hat was taken off, and this shirt was found in it; I gave it to the officer.


                  SAMUEL 
                  HARRISON
               



            . The prisoner is my apprentice. -On the 30th of December he was brought into my house; I took his hat off, and found the shirt.


                  JOHN 
                  STEWARD
               



            . I am a Policeman. I received the prisoner and this shirt; I found 8d. in copper on him.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
