





1662. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     DRAIN
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of August
, 2 gowns, value 23s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Collins
               




            .


                  CATHERINE 
                  COLLINS
               



            . I am the wife of George Collins. I lost these two gowns out of the same box in the prisoner's mother's room; I put them in on the 15th.


                  EBENEZER 
                  HODGES
               



            . I live at a pawnbroker's, in Aldgate. The prisoner pawned these two gowns with me, in the name of 
               
                  Ann 
                  Jones
               



            .
Prisoner. There was a woman with me. Witness. There might be, but I gave you the money.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner. Gleeson's wife was with me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
