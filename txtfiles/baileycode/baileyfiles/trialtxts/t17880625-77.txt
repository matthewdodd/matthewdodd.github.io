





479. 
               
                  
                     MICHAEL 
                     MICHAELS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of May
, one hundred pounds weight of lead, value 15 s. the property of 
                     
                        Caesar 
                        Andrews
                     




                  , affixed to a certain building of his, against the statute
            .
The prisoner was taken with the lead upon him by 
               
                  Thomas
                   Vigor
               



            .
(The lead produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
