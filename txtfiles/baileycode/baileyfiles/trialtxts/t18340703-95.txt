





986. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  CLARKE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 1s. 6d., the goods of 
                     
                        Henry 
                        Dennett
                     




                  , from his person
            .
HENRY DENNETT. On the 1st of July I was on Ludgate-hill


, about three o'clock in the afternoon - a young man tapped me on the shoulder, and said the prisoner had my handkerchief - I cried "Stop thief," the prisoner ran, and was turning into Belle Sauvage yard when the officer took him - this is my handkerchief.


                  EDWIN 
                  BLUNDEN
               



            . I heard the cry of "Stop thief" - I saw the prisoner jump off the pavement and run towards the Inn yard - I saw him drop this handkerchief - I took it up and took him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
