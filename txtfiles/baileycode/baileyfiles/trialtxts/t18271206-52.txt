





52. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  CRAMER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of October
, 4 pieces of flannel, containing 500 yards, value 48l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Jones
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 59.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
