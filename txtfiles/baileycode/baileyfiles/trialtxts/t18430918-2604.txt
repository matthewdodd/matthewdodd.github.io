



2604. 



THEODORE GRUMBRECHT
 was indicted

 for embezzling 485l. 10s., the monies of 



Frederick Huth and another, his masters; to which he pleaded



NOT GUILTY.




GUILTY. Aged 27.—Strongly recommended to mercy
—
Transported for





Seven Years.



Third Jury, before Edward Bullock, Esq.
