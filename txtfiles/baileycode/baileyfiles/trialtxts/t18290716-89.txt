





1463. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SAUNDERS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of July
, 22 yards of cotton, value 16s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Clark
               




            .


                  RICHARD 
                  THOMAS JONES
               



            . I am shopman to George Clark, a linen-draper
, of Oxford-street


. I saw the prisoner run from our door with the print in his apron; heran into several streets, and I took him in High-street - he had not got it then; it was delivered to me.


                  MICHAEL 
                  HOLDEN
               



            . I am a constable. I received the prisoner, and have had this print ever since; he had a whitish brown apron on.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was going down Marylebone-lane; a gentleman came and said I was the boy who took the print; I have no parents, but live with my grandmother.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
