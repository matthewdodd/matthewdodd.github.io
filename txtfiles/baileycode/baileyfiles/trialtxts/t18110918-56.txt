





677. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     DAVIS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that she on the 15th of May
, feloniously, knowingly, wittingly, and without lawful excuse, had in her custody and possession, a certain forged bank note, for the payment of 5 l. she well knowing it to be forged and counterfeited
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
