





28. 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Sears 
                  



               
            , of St. Botolph Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Woman's Cotton Gown, value 6s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Smallman 
                  




                
            , December 18
 last.
John Smallman, December 18. at Night, sitting in my Kitchen, I saw somebody reach over the Compter, and take Gown; he ran away with it, but was pursued and brought back: the Gown was found at the Door, and he confessed he took it.


                  Thomas 
                  Taylor 
               



            . I took the Prisoner in a necessary House, where he had endeavoured to conceal himself, he was brought back and confessed the Fact.


                  Zaccheus 
                  Bourn 
               



            . I am Constable, and was charged with him; he confessed that Night, but denied it next Morning.
The Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 4s. 10d
            .
