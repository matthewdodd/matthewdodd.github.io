





1542. 
               
                  
                     HANNAH 
                     FRENCH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of July
, 2 shifts, value 4r.; 2 petticoats, value 4s.; 4 gowns, value 8s.; 1 bonnet, value 6s.; 1 veil, value 9s.; 1 cap, value 3s.; 1 tippet, value 16s.; 1 neckince, value 18s.; 2 handkerchiefs, value 4s., and 2 bed-gowns, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Daniel Newton 
                           Crouch
                        




                     
                  , her master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
