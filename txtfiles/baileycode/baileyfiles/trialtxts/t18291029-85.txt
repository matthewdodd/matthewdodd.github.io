





1926. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     ROWEN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of October
, 6lbs. weight of pork, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Hannah 
                     Elsworth
                  




               
            .


                  ROBERT 
                  WHITLOCK
               



            . I live with Hannah Elsworth, a cheesemonger
, of Drury-lane


. On the 17th of October, I saw the prisoner come to the window, and take this piece of pork - she walked two or three yards with it; I pursued, and took her with it - she said her sister bought it, and gave it her to carry.
Prisoner's Defence. A woman was in the shop, buying butter - she gave me this, and said she bought it; I do not know whether she had paid for it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 46.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
