





760. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of April
, one snuff-box, value 10s., and six watch-cases, value 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Carolan
                        




                     
                  , privately in his shop
            .
JAMES CAROLAN. I am a watchmaker
, and live in Red Lion-street, Holborn


. On the 11th of April, about seven o'clock in the morning, I found the prisoner in custody in the shop, and the property in a chair.


                  JOHN 
                  ARTHUR
               



            . I am an upholsterer, and live two doors from the prosecutor. I heard the cry of Stop thief! stopped the prisoner, and took him to the shop. He sat in a chair, and shoved the watch-cases into it. I saw him throw the snuff-box into the fire-place; he then said,"Now you may search me."
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  SARAH 
                  GARDMAN
               



            . I live opposite the prosecutor. His servant was opening the shutters - A boy was looking through the windows, while the prisoner went in, opened the drawer, and took out the watch-cases. I gave the alarm, and he was stopped.
GUILTY. Aged 15.




               Of stealing, but not privately
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Bayley.
