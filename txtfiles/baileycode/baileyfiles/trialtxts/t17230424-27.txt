Burkit




            , of St. James Westminster

             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Tho.

 last. His Confession before the Justice was read in Court, wherein he confess'd the Fact, that he broke open the Trunk and took the Money. However, he deny'd all at the Bar, but the Fact being plainly proved the Jury found him
