





1403. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of October
, 29 pieces of calico, containing 450 yards, value 27 l., and one wrapper, value 1 s. 10 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Peter Halliday 
                  Turner
               



             and 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Johnson
                  




The prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
