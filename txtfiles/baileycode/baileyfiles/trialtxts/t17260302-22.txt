







                     William

            , (a Boy
,) was indicted for 


               privately stealing the Money of 
                     


                                    William 
                                    Godfrey 
                                 







                        , from the Person of Mary
                         his 
                        Wife





. It appear'd that as Mrs. Godfrey was standing at her own Door (the Prince's Head in Tyburn-Road


) at the Time when Sells and Mattocks were going by to be executed. The Prisoner stood close before her, and putting his Hand behind him, he cut her Pocket, and took out the Money; she happen'd to look downwards, and saw him in the Fact.
