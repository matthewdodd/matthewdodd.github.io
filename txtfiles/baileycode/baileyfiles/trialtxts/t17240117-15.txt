







                     Elizabeth 
                     Roberts 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Clement Danes


, was indicted 


               for feloniously taking the Money of 
                     
                        
                           Nathaniel 
                           Curtis 
                        




, the 17th of December
 last. It appear'd that the Prisoner being Servant
 to the Prosecutor, while her Master and Mistress were out, took the Money and went away. The Money was found in her Stockings the next Morning. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
