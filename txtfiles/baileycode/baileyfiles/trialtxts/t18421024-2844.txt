




2844. 




JOHN OSBORN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
19th of September, 30 bottles, value 7s.; 6 gallons of wine, value 24l. 10s.; 1 gallon of brandy, value 29s.; 8 knives, value 5s.; 2 forks, value 1s.; and 1 spittoon, value 6d.;—also, 

on the 
6th of October, 6 bottles, value 1s. 6d. and 6 quarts of wine, value 1l. 2s.; the goods of 



Aaron George Jones, his master; to both which he pleaded 



GUILTY
. Aged 23.—


Transported for Seven Years.
