





155. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BURKE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of November
, two eod-fish, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Appleton
                  




               
            .


                  ROBERT 
                  APPLETON
               



            . I am a fishmonger
, and live at Shadwell


. I missed two fish-the officer brought them to my house, with the prisoner. I knew one of them by a bruize on it.


                  MICHAEL 
                  MORRIS
               



            . I am an officer - I stopped the prisoner with the fish at Shadwell, about six o'clock in the evening.
Prisoner's Defence. A man gave them to me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 36.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
