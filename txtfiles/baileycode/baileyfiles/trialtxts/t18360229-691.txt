



691. 




MARY KELLY
 was again indicted

for stealing, on the 
27th of February, 1 dead fowl, value 2s.6d., the property of 



Ebenezer Howard.






JOHN JACOBS
. I am foreman to Mr. Ebenezer Howard; be keeps a fowl-stall in Leadenhall-market, next to Mr. Henry Howard—I went up while he was searching the prisoner's basket, and found my master's fowl in the

basket—we had missed it three or four minutes before—I have no recollection of seeing the prisoner before.




GUILTY
. Aged 39.—


Transported for Seven Years.
