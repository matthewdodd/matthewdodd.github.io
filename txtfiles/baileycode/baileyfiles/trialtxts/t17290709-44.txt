











                     Joseph 
                     Saunderson 
                  



               
            , of St. Ann's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously taking a Box of Mathematical Instruments, value 2 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Hennessy 
                  



                
            , the 25th of June




 last.
He was likewise indicted a 2d time for 


               stealing a Sheet, value 2 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Darney 
                  



                
            , the 25th of June




.
He was also indicted a 3d time for 


               stealing a Silver Spoon
            , the Goods of 
               
                  Samnel 
                  Scot 
               



             , the 8th of February




 last.
He was indicted a 4th time for 


               stealing a Gold Ring
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Clark 
                  



                
            , the 29th of June




.
He was a 5th time indicted for 


               stealing a Pair of Buckles
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Middleton 
                  



                
            , the 31st of March




 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner was Servant
 in the House where the Prosecutors lodg'd, and took the Goods. All which Facts being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d. upon each Indictment
            .
