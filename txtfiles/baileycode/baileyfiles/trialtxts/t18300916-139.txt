





London Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1604. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     CLEARY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of January
, 1 watch, value 3l. 10s.; 1watch-chain, value 6d., and 1 key, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Newsham
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
