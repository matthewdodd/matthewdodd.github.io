





2151. 




JOSEPH BEAUMONT SHIERS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
15th of May, 1 coat, value 3l., the goods of 



Samuel Stiff:—

also for stealing, on the 
4th of August, 2 watches, value 6l.; 1 ring, value 6s.; and 1 handkerchief, value 6d.; the goods of 



James Francis:—

also, on the 
31st of July, 1 watch, value 4l. 16s.; 2 seals, value 2s.; 1 watch-chain, value 6d.; 2 watch-keys, value 6d.; and 1 watch-guard, value 1s.; the goods of 



Richard Hemmings; from the person of 



Richard Hemmings the younger; to all which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 22.—


Transported for Seven Years.
