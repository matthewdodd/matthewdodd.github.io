





132. (L) 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM
                      HARTMAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value ten-pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Nathaniel
                      Thorley
                  




               
            , January 13th
. ++.


                  Nathaniel
                   Thorley
               



            . Last night about six o'clock, going along Bishopsgate-street


, I felt my handkerchief go out of my pocket, I turned round and saw the prisoner with it in his hand. I had it about five minutes before. I laid hold of him and he threw the handkerchief away. A gentleman that was with me picked it up.
Morton. I am constable of Bishopgate Ward, I was sent for to a public house there, the prosecutor gave me charge of the prisoner, for picking a handkerchief out of his pocket.
Prisoner's Defence.
I was coming along Bishopsgate-street, this gentleman laid hold of me, and said I had picked his pocket, there was a great many people, and the handkerchief lying on the ground. I never had the handkerchief, nor my hand near his pocket: I have no witnesses.



               Guilty
            .
