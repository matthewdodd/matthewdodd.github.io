



315. 




JAMES LYNCH
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
28th of December, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the property of 



Harry Harris, from his person.






HARRY HARRIS
. I am salesman to Gilman and Lucas, of Newgate-street. On the 28th of December, about twenty minutes to eleven o'clock at night, I was in Farringdon-street, crossing from Holborn-hill—I felt my pocket lighten—I turned round, and saw the prisoner going from me—I followed him, and took him with my handkerchief in his hand, and gave him in charge.





JOHN SMITH
. I was the officer of the night—I received him in charge.
(Property produced and sworn to.)




GUILTY
. * Aged 9.—


Transported Seven Years.
