



1579. 




WILLIAM RAVENSHAW
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
21st of April, 1 coat, value 2s., the goods of 



Edward Hall.






EDWARD HALL
. I am gardener to Mr. Pitt, of Kensington. I left my coat in the tool-house on the morning of the 21st of April, and it was gone—this now produced is it.





JOHN GROVES
. I am in Mr. Pitt's employ. Between three and four o'clock I was riding in the lane adjoining my master's house—I saw the prisoner come over the garden paling, with this coat under his arm—I secured him.




GUILTY
.* Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
