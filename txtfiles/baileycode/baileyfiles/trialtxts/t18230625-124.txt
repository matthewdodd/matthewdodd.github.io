





977. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SEYMOUR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of May
, a handkerchief, value 6 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Hannah 
                     Bowles
                  




               
            , spinster
.


                  HANNAH 
                  BOWLES
               



            . I lodge with the prisoner's mother in Ogle-street


; he does not live there. I went out on Saturday morning, between eleven and twelve o'clock, and left a handkerchief on the table, returned in the afternoon, and it was gone. I saw him next day, and charged him with taking it; he denied it.


                  JOHN 
                  SYMONDS
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Monmouth-street.On the 24th of May the prisoner pawned a handkerchief in the name of Graham.


                  JOHN 
                  WRIGHT
               



            . I apprehended him on the 29th; he said he knew what I wanted him for - that he had pawned the handkerchief, and lost the ticket.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
