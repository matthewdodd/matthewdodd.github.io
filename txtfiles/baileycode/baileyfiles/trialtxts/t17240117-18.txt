







                     Henry 
                     Atkins 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Brass Mortar
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Hugh 
                     Dart 
                  




                
            , the 26th of December
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner being observ'd by some Persons passing along to peep in at the Prosecutor's Window, and afterwards to go in and take something off from the Shelf, no Person but a Child being in the Room, they suspecting him, and seeing him coming out of the Door with the Mortar, stopt him. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
