






Fifth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
486. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     JONES
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PEDDRICK
                  




               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of December
, 1 shirt, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Timothy 
                     White
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZABETH 
                  WHITE
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  Timothy 
                  White
               



            , and live in York-street, Kingsland-road


. On the 14th of December I hung some things out to dry, and among them some shirts; I missed one of them.


                  CHARLES 
                  BENFIELD
               



            . I keep cows, and live three doors from White's. I had seen the two prisoners within four or five yards of her garden; they were both together, and I told Waller of them.


                  CHARLES 
                  WALLER
               



            . I am a Policeman. Benfield gave me information, and I took the prisoners; I found this shirt in Peddrick's jacket - he said he had picked it up.
Jones' Defence. Peddrick told me he had picked up a shirt, and the officer took us into custody.
Peddrick's Defence. I was going along, and picked it up.
JONES - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
PEDDRICK - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Confined Three Months
            .
