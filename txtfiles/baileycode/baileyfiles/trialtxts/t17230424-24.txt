








                     Joseph 
                     Wright 
                  



                
            , and 
               
                  Lewis 
                  Hudson 
               



              of St. Paul Shadwel



, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing eight Bushels of Wheat, value 28 s. and two Sacks, value 5 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  Earfard 
                  Martin 
               




             . Wright confes'd the Fact before the Justice, and Hudson several other Felonies, and their Confessions were read in Court. However, the Prisoners deny'd the Fact, and said they were in Liquor, and did not know what they did when they sign'd their Confession before the Justice. The Jury 

               acquitted
             Hudson, and found Wright 

               guilty
            .
