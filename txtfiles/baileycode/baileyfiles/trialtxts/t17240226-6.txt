







                     Henry 
                     Thorp 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Botolph Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Handkerchief, from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Marriot 
                        




, the 23d of February
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, he lost his Handkerchief. And 
               
                  Jonathan 
                  Vicount 
               



             depos'd, He saw him take the Handkerchief out of the Prosecutor's Pocket. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10d.
            



               Transportation
