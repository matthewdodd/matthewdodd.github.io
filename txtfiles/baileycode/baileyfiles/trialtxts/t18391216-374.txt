



374. 




ANN FULLER
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
14th of December, 1 veil, value 15s.; 1 pair of ear-drops, value 2s.; 3 rings, value 4s. 1 heart, value 6d.; 1 gown, value 8s.; 1 writing-desk, value 1l.; 1 tea-caddy, value 3s.; 1 shawl, value 1l.; 1 boa. value 1l.; 1 pair of boots, value 2s. 6d.; 1 penny, and 6 half-pence, the goods of 



Samuel Allen, to which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Penitentiary.
