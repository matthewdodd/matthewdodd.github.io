





375. (L) 
               
                  
                     JOHN
                      HARRIS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value ten-pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Pickering
                  




               
            , March 22
. *
Mr. 
               
                  William
                   Hurford
               



            . In passing from the custom-house to the Coal Exchange, I saw the prisoner pick a gentleman's pocket, and run down to the keys. I pursued him, he threw the handkerchief away, but was stopped before he was out of my sight. The captain, whose pocket was picked, is a quaker, and don't care to appear, his name is 
               
                  John
                   Pickering
               



            .
The prisoner said nothing in his defence.



               Guilty
            , 


               T
            .
