





11. 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Coles 
                  



               
            , a little Boy
, was indicted for 


               stealing 2 silver Spoons, and a Brass Candlestick
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Webster 
                  




                
            , May 13
.
The Prisoner was an Errand-boy
 to the Prosecutor. The Spoons being mist he was examin'd, and confest he had taken them, and pawn'd them, one for a Shilling, and the other for 8 s. at Wybourn's, the 5 Roses on Saffron-hill. And the Court order'd 
               
                  William 
                  Kay 
               



             (the Pawnbroker's Man) who receive the Spoons of the Boy, to be taken into Custody.
