





1495. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  FRANCIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of August
, 1 carpenter's plane, value 15s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Henry 
                  Hatchard
               




            .
HENRY HATCHARD. I am a carpenter
. This plane is mine - I was working with it. On the 21st of August I went to dinner at one o'clock, and returned at two, and missed it - the prisoner was then in custody.


                  RICHARD 
                  WHITE
               



            . I was at work with Hatchard, and saw the prisoner take this plane - I followed him, and took it from him.


                  GEORGE 
                  BRIDGES
               



            . I am the officer. I took the prisoner, and bring the plane.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
