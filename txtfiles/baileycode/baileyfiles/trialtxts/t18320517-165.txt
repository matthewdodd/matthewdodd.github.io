





1291. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of May
, 3 chisels, value 1s.; 1 pair of compasses, value 6d.; 1 gouge, value 6d.; 2 gimblets, value 6d., and 3 files, value 6d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Bull
                        



                     
                  ; 4 chisels, value 2s., and 2 gimblets, value 8d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Syrett
                  



               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  WILSON
               



             (Police-constable T 59). On the night of the 1st of May I was on duty at Hyde-park-terrace


, about eight o'clock; I went to the back of the buildings; I then went by a ladder into an attic of an unfurnished house, and found the prisoner there, with these articles on him - I asked how he came there; he made no answer.


                  HENRY 
                  BULL
               



            . I am a carpenter
, and was at work at these houses. On the morning of the 2nd of May, when I went to work, I missed some tools, which I had left there; some of these are mine, and some belong to 
               
                  John 
                  Syrett
               



            .



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
