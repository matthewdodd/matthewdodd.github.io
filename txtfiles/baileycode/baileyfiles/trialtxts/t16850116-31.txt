







                  Richard

            , Indicted for 


               stealing a Tankard, from 
                     
                        John 
                        Mason
                     




                   in St. Laurence-lane


, valued at 6l.
             it was proved against him, that he coming into the house of the Prosecutor, called for a Tankard of drink, and watching his opportunity, threw down the drink, and ran away with the Tankard, but was soon after apprehended, and giving no further account of his reputation upon Tryal, than that he was a Weaver
 in Spittle-fields, the Jury found him 

               Guilty
