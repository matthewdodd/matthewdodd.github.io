





1540. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  DOBSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 27th of August
, an umbrella, value 3 s.; six pairs of clogs, value 12 s., and a pistol, value 12 s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Madden
                     




                  , his master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
