






287. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     ROW
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 19th day of February
, twenty-eight pounds weight of leaden pipe, value 3 s. 6 d. belonging to 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Charles
                        




                     
                  , and then and there fixed to his dwelling-house against the statute
            .
The prisoner was taken with the pipe upon him.



               GUILTY
            .
To be 


               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
