







                     Sarah 
                     Rayner 
                  



               
            , of Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Petticoat val. 20 s. a Silver Spoon val. 10 s. and a Cup val. 12 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  Israel 
                  Crow 
               




             , on the 27th of Oct.
 last. 
               
                  Israel 
                  Crow 
               



             depos'd, that the Prisoner (who had formerly lodg'd with her) came to visit her; and after she was gone, the Goods were missing. She charged the Prisoner with the Fact, and she confess'd that she had pawn'd them at - Corket's, the Two Golden Balls in Hounsditch, at which Places they were found. 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
            .
