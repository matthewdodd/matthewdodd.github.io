







                     Edward

               privately stealing out of the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Jackson 
                        




                      
                  , a wooden Drawer, value 2 d. and 19 s and three Half pence in Money
            , the Goods and Money of 
               
                  John 
                   Jackson


             on the 2d November
 last. 
               
                  Elizabeth 
                  Jackson 
               



             depos'd that as the was sitting in a back Room, about 7 at Night, the Prisoner came into the Shop, and took out the Drawer, which the hearing, ran out and stopt the Prisoner in the Shop, with the Goods upon him, till some others came to her Assistance. The Prisoner laid in his Defence, that he was Drunk, and knew not what he did. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 4 s. and 10 l.
