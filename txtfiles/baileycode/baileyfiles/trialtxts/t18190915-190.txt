





1263. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     STANBURY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               embezzling 9 s. in monies numbered, and fourteen 1 l. bank notes, which he had received on account of his master

, 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Barker
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  BARKER
               



            . I live in King-street, Holborn


, and am a grocer
; the prisoner was two years in my service, and collected bills two days in every week. 
               
                  John 
                  Thurgood
               



             owed me 14 l. 9 s. 3 d., the prisoner never accounted to me for it.


                  JOHN 
                  THURGOOD
               



            . I keep a chandler's shop in Titchfield-street, Oxford-market, and deal with the prosecutor. On the 17th of August I paid the prisoner 14 l. 9 s. 3 d. on account of his master - I paid him in 1 l. notes, and produce his receipt.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
