





103. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     HAYDON
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of November
, 1 shirt, value 2s.; 1 frock, value 1s.; 1 night-gown, value 1s., and 2 pinafores, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Brown
               




            .


                  SUSAN 
                  BROWN
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Brown
               



             - we live at Chelsea


. On the 11th of November I missed these things, which had been hanging in the passage; I went up the street next day, and heard of them.


                  GEORGE 
                  WYATT
               



            . I took the prisoner on the other case, and found on her this basket, which contains these articles; she said they were her own.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was going to have half a pint to beer; a woman told me to mind these things for her - she went away, and I waited some time; she did not return.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years for each offence
            .
