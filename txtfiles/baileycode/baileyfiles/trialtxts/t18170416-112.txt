





650. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     YOUNG
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of March
, 96lbs of lead, value 14s. belonging to 
                     
                        
                           Sarah 
                           Coley
                        



                     
                   and 
                     
                        
                           Robert Jerrad 
                           Coley
                        



                     
                  , and fixed to their dwelling-house
            .
SECOND COUNT, stating it to be fixed to a certain building.
ROBERT JERRAD COLEY. We lost some more lead on the 3d of March.


                  DAVID 
                  YATES
               



            . On the 3d of March, between two and three o'clock, I and the prisoner took 108lbs. of lead from the pent-house, and sold it to Mrs. Watkins for 9s. 7d.


                  WILLIAM 
                  GOODENOUGH
               



            . I saw the prisoner and Yates, at the corner of Peter-street, about half-past two o'clock; I followed them and secured the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
