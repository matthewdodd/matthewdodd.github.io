





446. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  KEMBLE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of December
, 1 book, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Henson
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  HEMMINGS
               



            . I live with Mr. 
               
                  Charles 
                  Henson
               



            , a bookseller
, of Rathbone-place


. At half-past two o'clock on the 14th of December, the prisoner came and took a book off the shelf in the window; he walked away, and I took him about twelve yards off, with it under his coat.


                  JAMES 
                  THOMPSON
               



            . I am a Police-officer, and took the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I bought it of a man in the street- this young man saw me make the bargain, and said the book belonged to him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
