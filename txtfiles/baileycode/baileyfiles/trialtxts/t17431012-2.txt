





463. 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Boyce 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing seven Yards of striped Cotton, value 9 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Cole 
                  




                
            , October 1
.


                  Charles 
                  Cole 
               



            . I lost this Piece of striped Cotton out of my Shop the first of October.
- Cole. I was sitting in the Parlour backward, and saw the Prisoner look into the Shop, and take this Piece of striped Cotton out of the Window.


                  Peter 
                  Orton 
               



            . I was behind the Counter, putting some Goods up, and saw a Piece of Goods fall out of the Window; my Mistress called to me, and told me, that a Woman had taken a Piece of striped Cotton out of the Window; I pursued the Prisoner, and took her; and took this from under her Cloak.
Prisoner. Mr Cole said before the Alderman that he saw me take it?


                  Charles 
                  Cole 
               



            . I did not say so.
Prisoner. My Hands were very dirty, and the Alderman said, I looked like a hard working Girl. 

               Guilty
            .
When the Prisoner was going out of the Court, she said O - d - you all.
