





236. 
               
                  LEWIS 
                  BENNETTT
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of January
, 7 lbs. of mohair, value 5 l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Hearn
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  COKER
               



            . I am porter to 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Hearn
               



            , who keeps the King's Arms, Snowhill
. On Monday morning, about half-past seven o'clock, I stood in the cart in Fenchurch-street


, and saw the prisoner get on the step, and take this parcel out; I pursued him, and he dropped it at the corner of Gracechurch-street. I still pursued - he was secured just by Leadenhall-market. I never lost sight of him.


                  JOHN 
                  JUDD
               



            . I am a constable. About half-past seven o'clock in the morning I heard the alarm, and picked up the parcel - in a few minutes after the prisoner was brought to me.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. It was dark. I heard the alarm, ran after the man, and they stopped me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
