



638. 




PETER CHARLES KITCHNER
 was indicted

 for stealing 12 shawls, value 10l. 15s.; 52 yards of silk, 7l.; 2 scarfs, 1l.; 1 pair of cuffs, 3s.; 2 handkerchiefs, 1s. 6d.; 2 1/2 yards of cambric, 1l.; and 6 pairs of stockings, 18s.; the goods of 



Thomas Brooks, his master; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 30.
(John Adey, a dissenting minister, and Bartholomew Calway, a draper, in Tooley-street, gave the prisoner a good character.)




Transported for Seven Years.
