





1514. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PROVINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of June
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas Henry 
                           Platt
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  HENRY PLATT
               



            . I live in York-place, New-road. On the 24th of June, about nine o'clock at night, I was walking, and felt my handkerchief taken from my pocket; I turned, and saw the prisoner close to me with it - I did not see any one else there.


                  JACOB 
                  MILLER
               



             (Police-serjeant E 3). I saw the prosecutor running after the prisoner with this handkerchief in his hand.
Prisoner's Defence. I had nothing to do with it - there were three or four boys there.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
