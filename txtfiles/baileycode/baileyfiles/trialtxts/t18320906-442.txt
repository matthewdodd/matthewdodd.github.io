





2173. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     LYE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of August
, 2 pairs of shoes, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Corss
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  CORSS
               



            . I am a shoemaker
, and live in Shoreditch


. I was not at home when this happened.


                  JOHN 
                  HARPER
               



            . I am a Policeman. On the 21st of August I saw the prisoner about two hundred yards from Corss' shop, and suspecting him I took him - I found these shoes on him.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. * - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
