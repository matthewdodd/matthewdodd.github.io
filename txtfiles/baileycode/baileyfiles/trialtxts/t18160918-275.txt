





1094. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     WESTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of August
, a pair of sheets, value 25s. and a towel, value 1s. the property of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Boyce
                     




                  , in a lodging room
            .


                  ANN 
                  MORRIS
               



            , The prisoner had a bed in this house on the night in the indictment. In consequence of suspicion we had the watchman in, and he apprehended the prisoner in the Gallery, and took the sheets from him.



               GUILTY
            , aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
