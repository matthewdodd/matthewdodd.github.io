





1662. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  MALONEY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of September
, 1 shirt, value 2s.; 1 hat, value 3s.; 1 handkerchief, value 1s. 6d.; 1 shawl, value 2s.; 1 sheet, value 3s., and 1 pair of trousers, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Cornelius 
                  Gore
               




            .
CORNELIUS GORE. I live in Cato-street


. I missed this property on Monday week last; and on the following morning, at nine o'clock, I met the prisoner in Langham-place, and found it all in his possession - I asked how he came by them; he made no answer; I took him to the watch-house.


                  ELEANOR 
                  GORE
               



            . I am the prosecutor's wife - the prisoner slept at our house on this night; the things hung on a line in his room, at six o'clock that evening - the hat hung in the same room - at seven o'clock in the morning he was gone, and I missed them.


                  ROBERT 
                  WILLIAMS
               



            . I am an officer. I took him into custody at a cook's-shop.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
