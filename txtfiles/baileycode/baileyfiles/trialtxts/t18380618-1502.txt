



1502. 




JAMES BRIGGS
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
19th of June, I handkerchief value 3s., the goods of 



William Viner, from his person.






WILLIAM VINER
. On the 19th of June, a little after two o'clock in the afternoon, I was turning down the Minories—I felt a pull at my coat pocket—I turned round, and saw two boys running away—a person called out that my pocket was picked—I followed the prisoner, and called at to a person to stop him, which be did, and the prisoner threw down my handkerchief—(looking at it)—it is mine—I have the fellow to it in my pocket—the other boy ran in a different direction.

Prisoner's Defence. The other boy gave it to me, I did not steal it.




GUILTY
. Aged 14.—


Transported for Ten Years.
