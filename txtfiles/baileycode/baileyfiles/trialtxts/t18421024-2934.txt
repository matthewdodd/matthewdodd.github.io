





2934. 



WILLIAM EDWARDS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
6th of September, 2 rings, value 9l. 10s.; 2 breast-pins and chain, value 3l. 10s.; I other breast-pin, value 1l.; 1 watch-guard, value 1l.; 1 watch-key; value 2s.; 1 purse, value 3s. 6d.; 1 knife, value 5s.; and one watch-case, value 2s. 6d.; the goods of 



Frederick Daniel Basire, in the dwelling. house of 



Thomas Grace Phillips:—also, 

on the 
6th of October, 1 case of surgical instruments, value 3l. 7s. 6d., the goods of 



William Burrows—also, 

on the 
6th of October, 5 spoons, value 2l. 10s., the goods of 



George Wilson Pretty; to all which he pleaded






GUILTY
.—


Transported for Ten Years.
