





345. 
               
                  
                     Sophia 
                     Cuddy 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of July, one gold lac'd Hat value 2 s. 6 d. the Goods of 
                     
                        Jaceline 
                        Shawford 
                     



                   ; and one quilted Petticoat
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Smith 
                  



                
            .


                  Mary 
                  Smith 
               



            . I am Servant
 to Mr. Shawford. I saw the Prisoner go out of Mr Shawford's House about ten o'Clock in the Morning. I found my own Coat upon the Prisoner, and the Hat pinn'd under her Petticoat.
The Prisoner acknowledged she went into the House upon some Business, but deny'd the taking the Things.



               Guilty
            .
