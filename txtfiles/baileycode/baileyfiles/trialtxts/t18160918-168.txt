





986. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  GAMBLE
               




             was indicted for 


               steal- on the 8th of July
, eighteen live tame fowls, value 30s. and two pewter measures, value 1s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Matthews
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  GRENVILLE
               



            . I am a watchman; I stopped the prisoner in Bunhill Row, on the morning of the 11th of July, about four o'clock with this property on him.



               GUILTY
            , aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
