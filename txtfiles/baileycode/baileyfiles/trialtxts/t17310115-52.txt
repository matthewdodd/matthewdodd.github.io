







                     Edward 
                     Welsh 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted, for that 


               he together with 
                     
                        James 
                        Dickson 
                     



                  , did on the 24th of October
 last, feloniously steal a Tankard, in the House of 
                     
                        
                           Alice 
                           Kellum 
                        




.


                  Francis 
                  Kellum 
               



             depos'd, That three Men came in to drink, and after having drank several Tankards of drink, in the Space of an Hour, and he going down to draw some drink , they all run away without paying the Reckoning; that he immediately pursued them, and they running divers ways, he could pursue but one of them, which was 
               
                  Christopher 
                  Cornick 
               



            , who was convicted for it the last Sessions. That he afterwards being informed by Thomas Hall, that the Prisoner was in Newgate, he went thither, and immediately knew the Prisoner to be one of the three in Company that stole the Tankard. The Fact being fully proved, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 4s. 10d
            .
