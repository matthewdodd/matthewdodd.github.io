







                     Elizabeth 
                     Waybank 
                  



               
             , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               stealing a half-pint Mug
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Anthony 
                     Dalbys 
                  




                
            , the 7th of August
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner being a Basket-Woman
, the Prosecutor (an Apothecary
) had sent her Home with a Load of Herbs, and the Mug standing in the Shop-Window, while the Maid was taking the Herbs out of the Basket , she took the Mug and went away with it, leaving her Basket. The Fact being prov'd upon the Prisoner, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
