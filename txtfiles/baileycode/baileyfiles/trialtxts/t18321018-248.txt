





2457. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     KERRY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of October
, 2 handkerchiefs, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Taylor
                  




               
            .


                  JANE 
                  TAYLOR
               



            . I am the wife of George Taylor; I am a laundress; I lost these two handkerchiefs from a line in Lucas-street, Salisbury-street


, where they hung to dry.


                  WILLIAM 
                  WILLSHER
               



            . The prisoner was given into my charge by the prosecutrix's son; I took him, and found this one handkerchief on him.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
