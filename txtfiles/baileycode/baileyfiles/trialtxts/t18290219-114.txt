





639. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     LLOYD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of February
, 5 yards of cloth, value 4l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Hopkins
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  WALLINGTON
               



            . I am in the employ of Mr. 
               
                  John 
                  Hopkins
               



            , a draper
. This cloth was near his shop door about six o'clock in the evening of the 4th of February; the prisoner put in his arm, and reached it out - I went and took him with it on his arm, about twenty yards off; he said a man gave him 6d. to carry it.


                  BARNARD 
                  JEFFRIES
               



            . I am an inspector of the watch. Here is the property.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. The place he says it was is seven feet high - is it possible I could get it down? my wife and child were starving; the parish would not take us in.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
