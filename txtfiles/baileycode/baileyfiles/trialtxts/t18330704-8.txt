





1032. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     MAYNARD
                  




               
             was indicted was 


               stealing, on the 21st of June
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s. of the property of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Brymer
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  BRYMER
               



            . I was formerly in the army, but have retired
. On the 21st of June, about one in the morning, I was in the Circus, Piccadilly


; I felt a pull at my pocket; I turned and saw the prisoner drop my handkerchief; I laid hold of him, and with the assistance of two gentlemen who were with me, I held him till the officer came up; this is my handkerchief.
Cross-examined by MR. BARRY. Q. Did he not appear in liquor? A. No.
Prisoner's Defence. I know nothing of it; I was in liquor.



               GUILTY
             - Aged 18. 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
