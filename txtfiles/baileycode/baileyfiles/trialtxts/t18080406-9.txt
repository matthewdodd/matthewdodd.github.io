





275. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     VINING
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he being servant
 to 
                     
                        
                           Philip 
                           Rundell
                        



                     
                  , 
                     
                        John 
                        Bridge
                     



                  , and 
                     
                        
                           Edmund Waller 
                           Rundell
                        



                     
                  , was entrusted and employed by them to receive money and valuable securities for them, that he being such servant and so employed, on the 29th of January
 did receive for and on account of his said masters the sum of twelvepounds seven shillings and nine pence; and that he afterwards did embezzle, secrete, and steal the same
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
