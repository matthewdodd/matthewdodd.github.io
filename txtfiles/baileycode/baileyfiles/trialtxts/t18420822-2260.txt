



2260. 




JULIA MURPHY
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
11th of June, 8 handkerchiefs, value 12s., the goods of 



Thomas Price.






WILLIAM MEEKING
. I am assistant to Thomas Price, a linen draper, in Tothill-street, Westminster—the prisoner was in his shop on the 11th of June—I afterwards missed the handkerchiefs produced, which are my master's.





WILLIAM PLATT
 (police-constable B 55.) I took these handkerchiefs from the prisoner, in her apron, in Mr. Parton's shop.

Prisoner's Defence. They were put in my apron, but I never stole them.




GUILTY
. Aged 30.—


Transported for Ten Years.
