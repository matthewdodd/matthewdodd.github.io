





2089. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  POWELL
               




             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of October
, 1 saw, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Usher
               




            .
GEORGE USHER. I am a carpenter
. I left this saw in the house, in Montpelier-square


, between twelve and one o'clock, on the 2d of October; when I returned from dinner it was gone; the prisoner is a bricklayer
, and was working at the sewer there.


                  HENRY 
                  DUPLEX
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. On the 2d of October the prisoner pawned this saw - I am sure he is the man.


                  RICHARD 
                  MAYBANK
               



            . I am an officer, and took the prisoner; he at first denied it, but afterwards told where he had pawned the saw.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner. I am sorry for it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
