





2086. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  HARTLETT
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of October
, 3 lbs. of bacon, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Richard 
                  Castle
               




            .
RICHARD CASTLE. I am a cheesemonger
, and live in Broad-court, Long-acre


. On the 3d of October, about nine o'clock at night, the window of my shop was lifted up, and this bacon taken out; I heard an alarm; the watchman brought the prisoner in; Vaughan said he had stolen the bacon - he did not deny it.


                  THOMAS 
                  VAUGHAN
               



            . I saw the prisoner go and take the bacon from the window; an alarm was given, and he was taken immediately; a boy name Howe tried to take it first; the prisoner then went, and did take it, and threw it to Howe, who let it fall at his feet, and I took it up.


                  ROBERT 
                  CLEGG
               



            . I am a watchman, and took the prisoner as he came out of the court.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I stood in the court, but saw nothing about it; the gentleman took me, and was going to let me go - I did not take it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
