





523. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     ROBERTS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of February
, 1 bed, value 2l.; 1 bolster, value 5s.; 1 pillow, value 2s.; 1 sheet, value 2s., and 1 counterpane, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Stock
               




            ; to which he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
