




1077. 




GEORGE AUSTIN PARKER
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
9th of March, 1 box, value 6d.; 14 Jews' harps, value 4s.; 1 nail-brush, value 1s. 6d.; 1 coat, value 3l.; 1 waistcoat, value 16s.; and one pair of trowsers, value 6s., the goods of 



George Eastwood; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 13.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Isle of Wight.
