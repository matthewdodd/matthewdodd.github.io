




2231. 




THOMAS SULLIVAN
 and 




CHARLES SMITH
 were indicted for

 burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 



Alfred Brown, about the hour of five in the night of the 
30th of Aug., and stealing therein
1 watch, value 1l. 10s.; 2 spoons, 5s.; 1 pair of sugar-tongs, 6d.; 2 handkerchiefs, 4s.; 1 hat, 12s.; 523 pence and 420 halfpence, his property; and 1 pair of spectacles, value 4s.; the goods of 



Elizabeth Brown; they having been before convicted of felony: to which


SULLIVAN pleaded



GUILTY.
Aged 21.


SMITH pleaded 



GUILTY.
 Aged 22.





Transported for Seven Years.
