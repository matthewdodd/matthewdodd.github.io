







                     Edward 
                     Mullinor 
                  



               
             , of St. Giles in the Fields


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2s. 7d. Half-penny
             from 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Bannister 
                  




                
             , the Second of February
 last . The Prisoner came to the Prosecutor's House about two a Clock in the Morning to help him Bake, and was sent up Stairs for a Candle, but staying longer than ordinary, he went up after him, found the Drawer open, the Prisoner standing by, and the Money gone. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
