





1103. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     EVANS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of July
, one handkerchief, value 2 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Stephens
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  STEPHENS
               



            . On the 3d of July I was again coming up the Strand


, and near Bedford-street, felt a touch at my pocket; turned round, saw four boys behind me, and saw the prisoner, who was one of them, drop my handkerchief. I secured him, and the others dispersed.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years - 



                     Penitentiary
                  
.
Third Middlesex Jury, before H. R. Reynolds, Esq.
