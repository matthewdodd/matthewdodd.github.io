            . April 17
 ++ .


                  Samuel 
                  Bartlet 
               



            . Coming along Cheapside


 on the 17th of April, I catched the prisoner's hand in my pocket, he had my handkerchief in his hand and dropped it on the ground. [Produced in court and deposed to.]
Prisoner's Defence.
I was near King-street in Cheapside, the gentleman accused me with picking his pocket of a handkerchief. I never saw it till he picked it up.



               Guilty
            .
