





1018. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  JOHNSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of March
, 4 combs, value 7s.; 6 pencil-cases, value 8s., and 1 cigar tube, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Conden
               




            .


                  MARY 
                  CONDEN
               



            . I am the wife of John Conden, a perfumer
. On the 20th of March, I saw the prisoner in the shop; he was a stranger - I went and asked what he wanted; he said he came for relief - I saw the glass-case in confusion, and detained him; he then took three combs from his pocket - the officer came and took him.


                  THOMAS 
                  SMITH
               



            . I saw the prisoner in the shop; he gave Mrs. Conden three combs from his pocket; she left him in my care, while she fetched an officer - I asked if he had anything more; he gave me these pencil-cases and a cigar tube from his jacket pocket.


                  SAMUEL 
                  TAYLOR
               



            . I am an officer, and took him in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
