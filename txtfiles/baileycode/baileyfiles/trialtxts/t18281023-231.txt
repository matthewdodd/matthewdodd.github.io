






2164. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  EVANS
               




             and 
               
                  JAMES 
                  DEANE
               




             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of September
, 1 pair of shoes, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Howard
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  BELSHAM
               



            . I am foreman to Mr. John Howard, a shoemaker
, St. Martin's-court


. On the 16th of September, the prisoner Evans was brought in with a pair of shoes - I took them from him; I have seen him about once or twice, but I did not see Deane that morning.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BALCH
               



            . I am a hatter, and live in St. Martin's-court. On the 16th of September, between ten and eleven o'clockin the morning, I saw Deane take the shoes from Mr. Howard's door, and give them to Evans - I had frequently seen them lurking about the shop.


                  JOHN 
                  GROOM
               



            . I am an officer. I took Deane about four hours after the other; I knew they were in the habit of going about together.(Property produced and sworn to.)
EVANS - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.
DEANE - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.





               Transported for Seven Years
            .
