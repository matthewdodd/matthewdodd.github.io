





347. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     LYONS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing on the 16th day of April
, one hand saw, value 2 s. one tenant saw, value 1 s. and one pair of pincers, value 1 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Barrow
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken coming out of an unfinished house with the things upon him.
Prisoner. I found the things; I am just come from sea.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
