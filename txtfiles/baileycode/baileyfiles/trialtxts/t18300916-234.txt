





1698. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HESTER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of July
, 1 watch, value 1l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Frances 
                     Hudson
                  




               
            .


                  FRANCES 
                  HUDSON
               



            . I am a widow
. On the 29th of July I lost a watch from my mantel-shelf at ten o'clock at night - I had seen it not two minutes before; the prisoner was in the room - he has slept there two nights with my son-in-law; I called my son - he went and took it from the prisoner; this is it.


                  JOHN 
                  REDMAN
               



            . I am the prosecutrix's son-in-law. On the night of the 29th of July she said she had lost the watch - I went after the prisoner, and caught him about fifteen yards from the door; he gave the watch up to me.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 28. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
