





106. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     GRAY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of October
, 1 cheese, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Bush
                  




               
            .
JOHN BUSH. I keep a cheesemonger's-shop
, in Kentish-town


. I missed some cheese on the 18th of October - I did not see it taken; I know this to be mine.


                  THOMAS 
                  WAGSTAFF
               



            . This is part of a cheese which I left at the prosecutor's.


                  THOMAS 
                  SOPER
               



            . I am a Police-constable. On Wednesday, the 19th of October, I went to the prisoner's lodgings in search of some wet linen; I found this cheese in the cupboard - he said he had bought and paid for it, but would not tell me where; I heard that the prosecutor had lost one.
Prisoner's Defence. I bought it, and paid for it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 23. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
