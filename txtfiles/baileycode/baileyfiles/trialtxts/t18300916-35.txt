





1500. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     STUBBS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of August
, 1 counterpane, value 2s.; 1 sheet, value 1s.; 1 bolster, value 1s., and 2 pillows, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Teevan
                  




               
            .


                  MARY 
                  TEEVAN
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  John 
                  Teevan
               



            . On the 17th of August I lost these articles; the prisoner was a lodger, but had left about a fortnight before.


                  JOAM 
                  GIDEON
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a bolster, two pillows, and a sheet pawned at different times by the prisoner - on the 5th of June, and two other dates, in the name of 
               
                  Henry 
                  Williams
               



            .


                  THOMAS 
                  BULLIVANT
               



            . I took up the prisoner, and found the duplicates of this property on him.


                  JAMES 
                  SAYER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a counterpane pawned with a person who preceeded me.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was in distress, and pawned the things in the neighbourhood; I did not make away with the duplicates - I intended to pay her, and restore them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 25. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
