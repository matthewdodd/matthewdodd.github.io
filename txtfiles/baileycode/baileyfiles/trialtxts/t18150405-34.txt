





457. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     WILLIAMS
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 7th of February, feloniously and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession, a forged bank note, for the payment of one-pound, he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
