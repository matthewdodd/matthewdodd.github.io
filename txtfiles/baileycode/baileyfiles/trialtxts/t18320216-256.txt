





764. 
               
                  
                     FREDERICK 
                     BRIDGE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of February
, 1 trunk, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Illman
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  STANDOVER
               



            . I live opposite Mr. Illman's, in Dean-street, Soho


. On the 16th of February I saw the prisoner and some others near the house - I saw the prisoner take a trunk - he put it under his arm, and ran away; Mr. Wheeler followed him - I know he is the boy.


                  JAMES 
                  WHEELER
               



            . I was going out at the door, and Mr. Standover said the lad had run away with a trunk from Mr. Illman's - I went and took him with it.


                  JAMES 
                  LUFF
               



            . I took the prisoner, and have the trunk.


                  WILLIAM 
                  ILLMAN
               



            . This is my property, and was in one of my windows.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
