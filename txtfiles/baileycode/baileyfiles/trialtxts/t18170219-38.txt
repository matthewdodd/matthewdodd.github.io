





400. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     FIELD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of  January
, one handkerchief, value 4s. 6d. the property of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Reilly
                     




                  , from his person
            .
THOMAS REILLY. I am an agent
, and live in Leadenhall-street. On the 22nd of January, about one o'clock in the afternoon, as I was going along Cheapside, I thought I felt somebody at my pocket; I put my hand to it, and found my handkerchief safe. When I got to the middle of the Poultry


 I found the prisoner in the act of taking it out, and seized him with it in his hand - He made a great resistance.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 25.




               Transported for Life
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
