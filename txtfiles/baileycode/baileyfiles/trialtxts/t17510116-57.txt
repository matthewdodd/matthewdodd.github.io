               stealing one pounds and three quarters of tobacco, value 10 d.

            , December 11
.* 


                  James 
                  Emerson 
               



            . I took the prisoner with this tobacco upon him, on the 11th of December. I saw him take it at several times out of a hogshead; there was no other hogsheads near, but those of John and Caple Hambury's. I know the prisoner to be a common pilferer on the keys.



               Guilty
