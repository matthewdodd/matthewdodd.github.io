





1439. 
               
                  MARY 
                  ELLIOTT
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of June
, 7 yards of printed cotton, value 7s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Kentish
               




            .
JOHN KENTISH. I am a linen-draper
, and live in Long-acre


. On the 6th of June, in the evening, I came home to my shop and saw several people there; the prisoner was there. I saw her take this print, put it under her shawl, and walk out with it. I went out and overtook her with it, about one hundred yards off.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  MATTHEW 
                  PATISON
               



            . I am a patrol, and took her with the print.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 49.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
