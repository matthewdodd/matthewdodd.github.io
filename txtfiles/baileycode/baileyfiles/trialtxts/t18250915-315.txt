





London Cases - Third Jury.
1539. 
               
                  MARY 
                  JOHNSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of September
, a tea caddy, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Jeffreys
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 26.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
