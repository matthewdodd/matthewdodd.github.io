



1249. 




DAVID DALEY
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
16th of March, 1 coat, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 



John Neale.






SUSANNAH CHARGE
. I live in Pimlico. On the 16th of March, I hung a coat on a fence in the garden, about ten o'clock, and about twelve o'clock I missed it—this is it—I have the pieces here which I cut off to make it smaller, for John Neale, a nephew of mine—it is his coat.





WILLIAM ALDER
 (police-constable B 72.) On the 16th of March I received information, and took the prisoner at Thames Bank with this coat on his back.




GUILTY
. * Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
