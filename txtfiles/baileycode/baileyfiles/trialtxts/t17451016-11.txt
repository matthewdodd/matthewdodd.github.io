





374. 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Bruin 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary Lestrand


, was indicted for 


               stealing a cossoy cushion, value 5 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Lloyd 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Hayward 
                  



                
            , Sept. 14
.


                  Thomas 
                  Lloyd 
               



            . Between ten and eleven at night last Saturday was a month, I lost this cushion out of my chair.


                  Andrew 
                  Baker 
               



            . As I was crossing the way I saw the Prisoner between the poles of the chair, and saw him take this cushion out of the chair. I let him go to the channel, then I catched him by the right arm, and took it from under his left.
Prisoner. Do you think if I had been guilty of a robbery, I would have staid there an hour and a quarter, when they had no proper authority to keep me, but they pulled me about like a parcel of mad dogs; and when he took me, I went cordially along with him. 


               Guilty 10 d.
