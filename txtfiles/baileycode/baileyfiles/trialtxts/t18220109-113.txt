





291. 
               
                  
                     EPHRAIM 
                     DIGBY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of December
, four pounds of beef, value 1 s. 6 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Treadaway
                  




               
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  TREADAWAY
               



            . I am a butcher
, and live in Stafford-street


. On the night of the 22d of December I was serving in the shop, a little girl said a boy had stolen a piece of beef from the stall-board, I ran out and took the prisoner, with the beef under his arm; he said he found it.


                  SAMUEL 
                  PRENDERGRASS
               



            . I took him in charge.
Prisoner's Defence. I found it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
