



2037. 




MARY ROBERTS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
13th of August, 2 gowns, value 5s., the goods of 



John O'Brien.






HONORA O'BRIEN
. I am the wife of John O'Brien—we live in Monmouth-court, Monmouth-street, and deal in second-hand clothes. On the 13th of August I purchased a gown of the prisoner, between four and five o'clock, and I had two other gowns—I afterward missed the one I bought of her, and another—these are them—(looking at them.)





DAVID JORDAN
. I am fourteen years old—I live with my mother—on the 13th of August I saw the prisoner take two gowns from the prosecustor's shop—she put them on her, and walked out.





WILLIAM CARLE
 (police-constable F 77.) The prisoner was brought from the prosecutor's shop to the station-house, and she had the two gowns on her.

Prisoner. I went to the shop to ask her to exchange one gown for another—she was not there—I put one gown on—the black gown was my own, and I walked out to see for her.




GUILTY
.* Aged 42.—


Transported for Seven Years.
