





NEW COURT. - Saturday, May 17th.
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
828. 
               
                  
                     SARAH ANN 
                     TRAILL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of May
, 1 cloak, value 5s.; 1 frock, value 18d.; and 2 irons, value 1s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Traill
                  




               
            , to which she pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
