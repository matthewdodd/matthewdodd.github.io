







                     William

            , of Billingsgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing in the Ware-House of 
                     
                        Joseph 
                        Ashton 
                     



                  , 119 l. of Sugar, value 20 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  Will.
                   Tryon
               




            . Dec. 2
.
It appeared that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Wharfinger, at Lyon's-Key


; and was entrusted to take care of the Ware-Houses, in one of which he stole the Sugar out of a Hogshead, belonging to Mr. Tryon, and was taken with it in a Bag in the Passage on the Stair-Head.
