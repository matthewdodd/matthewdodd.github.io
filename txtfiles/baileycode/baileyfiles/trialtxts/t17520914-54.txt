





464. (M) 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Osbourne 
                  



               
            , spinster
, was indicted for 


               stealing one linen shirt val. 1 s. 6 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Roberts 
                        



                      
                  , one linen shirt the roperty of 
                     
                        William 
                        Waters 
                     



                   , and 6 linen shirts and one waistcoat
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Morris 
                  



                
            , July 3
.
The goods mention'd were missing from out of the house of Roberts in Denmark-street


, the prisoner had carried the things to Mrs. Hugoe's, in Johnson's-change, Rosemary-lane to sell. She suspected they were not honestly come by, so stop'd them and had them advertis'd. The prosecutors own'd them. Produced in court and deposed to.
The prisoner in her defence said, she found them in Burlington street in a bundle.




               Guilty 10 d
