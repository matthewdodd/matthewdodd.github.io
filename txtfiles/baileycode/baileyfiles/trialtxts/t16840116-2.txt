







                  John

            , Indicted for 


               Stealing a Bag of Mony out of 
                     
Mr.
 
                        Mincham
                     



                   a Goldsmith
's Shop in Fleet street


, of about Thirty Pounds
            , on the 24th of December
 last. The Goldsmiths Servant Swore it positively, That he being telling of Mony, and that Bag standing by him, Valor came running into the Shop, snatch't it up, and run away; but the said Servant pursuing him speedily, overtook and seized him; he was therefore found
