





317. 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Hendrick 
                  



               
            , of St George, Botolph-Lane


, was indicted for 


               stealing 3 lb. of Tobacco, val. 18 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Cary 
                  




                
            , June 1
.


                  Jeremiah 
                  Mascal 
               



            . The Prisoner did belong to some Ship; but he has left his Ship ever since Christmas; and now he follows Thieving upon the Keys: I caught him once before, but then I pitied him, and licked him a little, and bid him go about his Business; this Time he broke open the Head of a Hogshead, and took out about 3 lb. of Tobacco, which he had stuffed into his Coat, Waistcoat, and Breeches. 

               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
