





687 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     CURRAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 18th of July
, ten promissory notes, value 10 l.
             each, the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Lyne
                  



               
            .
SECOND COUNT for like offence the property of 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Ipwell
               



            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Justice Bailey.
