



2845. 




WILLIAM EVERILL
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
18th of August, 1 basket, value 2s. 6d.; a stock, and various other tools; the goods of 



Joshua Deane.






JOSHUA DEANE
. I am a journeyman carpenter, and live at Hammersmith. I missed my basket of tools on the 18th of Aug.—several of them are here.





JOHN GEORGE POOLE
. I am a journeyman pawnbroker—these tools were pawned by the prisoner on the 18th of Aug., with some more.




GUILTY
. Aged 25.—


Transported for Seven Years.
