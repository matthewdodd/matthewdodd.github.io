







                     Eleanor 
                     Stockdale 
                  



               
            , of St. James at Clerkenwell


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 1 Shirt and 1 Shift, the Goods of 
                     
                        Mary 
                        Benn 
                     



                   ; and a Handerchief
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Francis 
                     Holman 
                  



                
            , on the 30th of November
 last. It appeared that the Goods hung on the Lines to dry in Mrs Benn's Kitchen, and that the Prisoner (who had no manner of Business there) came in and took them, but was stopt in the Entry by one who accidentally came for an empty Cask, and the Goods found upon her. The Prisoner had nothing to say in her Defence, nor any to her Reputation. The Jury found her 

               Guilty
            .  


               Transportation
            .
