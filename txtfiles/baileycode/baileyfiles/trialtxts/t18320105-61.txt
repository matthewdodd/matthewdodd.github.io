





354. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     REYNOLDS
                  




               
             was 


               indicted for embezzlement
            .


                     JOHN 
                     COOPER
                  




               
            . I am a hay and straw salesman
 at Smithfield


; the prisoner was in my employ for two years, off and on; he often received the money for hay and straw, and accounted to me for it. On the 1st of October I sent him with the waggon and four loads of straw, worth 6l. 14s.; he was to see that they were delivered right, to receive the money, and give it to me - I never saw him again till the 10th of December.


                  WALTER 
                  PEACOCK
               



            . I received the four loads of straw on the 1st of October, and paid the prisoner 6l. 14s., for which he produced the receipt.


                  HENRY 
                  MATKIN
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
