






421. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     HUGHES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of September
, a wooden firkin, value 10 d. and fifty-six pound of salted butter, value 36 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  James and 
                        
                           
                              Joseph 
                              Mitchell
                           







            .


                  WILLIAM
                   JOSELIN
               



             sworn.
I was coming up Holborn; I saw a person drawing a truck, he went into a public house, and left the truck in the street; the prisoner and two others were pushing behind; when they came to Brook-street, the prisoner took this firkin, and put it on his shoulder, and I went up and took him.


                  JOSEPH
                   MITCHEL
               



             sworn.
I am a cheesemonger

, in New Compton-street; the butter and firkin is the property of me and my brother; we are partners.



               GUILTY
            . (Aged 19)




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. COMMON SERJEANT.
