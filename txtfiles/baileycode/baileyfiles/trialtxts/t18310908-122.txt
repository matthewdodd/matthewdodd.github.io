





1649. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     DAYMENT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 
                     
                        James 
                        Warren
                     




                  , from his person
            .
JAMES WARREN. I am a butcher
, and live in Francis-place, Lambeth. On the night of the 18th of August I was in the Strand - the prisoner and two others passed me several times before I got to Charing-cross, and opposite Northumberland-house


 I felt something go out of my pocket; I turned, and caught the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand - the others escaped.


                  JOHN 
                  COLLIS
               



            . I am an officer. I received him in charge, with the handkerchief.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
