



239. 




DAVID WHITE

was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
18th of October, 1 handkerchief, value 1s., the goods of 




George Alderson, from his person.







GEORGE ALDERSON
. I am an engineer in a steam-boat. On the 18th of October I was at Charlton fair, in one of the booths—I had a handkerchief—I missed it—I had seen it five minutes before—this is my handkerchief, it has my initials on it—(looking at it.)





WILLIAM PHIPPS
. I am a police inspector. I took the prisoner at Chalton Fair, and found four handkerchiefs on him—this was one, also a purse, a flash note for 5l., a pair of scissors, a knife, tobacco-box, and key.




GUILTY
.* Aged 28.—


Transported for Ten Years.
