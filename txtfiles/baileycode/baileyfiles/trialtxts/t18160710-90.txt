





766. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     STORY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of June
, four pounds weight of indigo, value 1l. 1s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Jones
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  JONES
               



             I am a silk dyer
; I live at No. 37, Booth-street, Spital-fields


. The prisoner was my apprentice
. On the evening of the day in the indictment, one of my men shewed me some indigo, and gave me some information.


                  WILLIAM 
                  NORTON
               



            . I am in the employ of the prosecutor. On going into my master's loft, over the dye-house, on the day in the indictment, I found a handkerchief, containing the indigo in question. The prisoner observing that I had discovered it, immediately came up, and took it away, and threw it into the stoke-hole. On getting it back. I found it contained this indigo, which is my master's property.



               GUILTY
            , aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
