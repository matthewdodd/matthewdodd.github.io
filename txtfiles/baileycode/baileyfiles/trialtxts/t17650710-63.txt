            , was indicted for 


               obtaining 14 pounds weight of white copperas, value 7 s. the property of 
                     
Messrs.
 Streadfield and Colwell


                  , by false pretences
            , June 26
. ++


                  John 
                  Salmon 
               



            , servant to the prosecutors, deposed, that they lived in Budge-row


; the prisoner came for 14 pounds weight of white copperas, for Mr. Nixon, senior, which he delivered to him. Mr. Nixon deposed he never sent him with such order. 
               
                  Hugh 
                  Corkhill 
               



            , a painter in Shadwell, deposed the prisoner came and told him he had a brother a colour-man, that had failed, and he had this quantity of copperas to dispose of, which he bought of the prisoner.
