





102. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     STICHBURY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of December
, 3 lbs. of soap, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Ansted
                  




               
            .



                  THOMAS 
                  ANSTED
               



            . I am a grocer
, and live in Union-street, Spitalfields


. On the 4th of December, I heard the cry of Stop thief, ran to the door, and saw the prisoner drop a cake of soap, about ten yards from the door; the officer brought him back.
WILLIAM SEEAR. I am an officer. I saw the prisoner drop the soap.


                  ANDREW 
                  JOHNSON
               



            . I saw the prisoner run from Ansted's door with the soap, and secured him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Garrow
               



            .
