





284. 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Morrison 
                  



               
            , of St Leonard Shoreditch


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Silver Clasp, value 2 s. and two Silver Spoons, value 20 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Warren 
                  




                
            . June 10
.


                  William 
                  Warren 
               



            . I am a Watch and Clock-Maker
; the Prisoner has been my Apprentice
 almost five Years, he went away one Morning about five o'Clock; when I came down I missed the Clasp, and the Spoons: He was taken 4 Days afterwards, and brought to my House in a Coach: he confessed before the Justice that he told the Clasp for 2 s - He behaved but very indifferently before this; this is the fourth Time he ran away from me.
Prisoner. I ran away for Fear, because I had lost some other Things: I was suddled when I was before the Justice, and did not know what I said.


                  William 
                  Warren 
               



            . I could not perceive that he was suddled when he was before the Justice.
- Painter. The Prisoner confessed the taking the Clasp, I cannot tell whether he said, he or some body else sold it, but he or his Confederate sold it for 2 s. 

               Guilty
            .
