




907. 




JOHN MURPHY
 and 




PETER CONLEY
 were indicted for

stealing, on the 
14th of March, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 



Thomas John Boulton, from his master.






THOMAS JOHN BOULTON
. I am a silk-dresser, and live in Wilson-street. On the 14th of March, I was at the corner of Lawrence-lane, Cheapside, at twenty minutes after two o'clock—I felt somebody togging at my pocket—I instantly turned round and found my handkerchief in the hands of Conley, he instantly passed it to Murphy, who Attempted to turn it round his wrist—I seized his wrist and got it from him—one of them attempted to escape, and ran down the lane—they were both immediately secured.





WILLIAM COURTENAY
. I am an officer. The prisoners were delivered into my custody.
(Property produced and sworn to.)

MURPHY—


GUILTY
. Aged 13.

CONLEY—


GUILTY
. Aged 15.




Transported for Seven Years.
