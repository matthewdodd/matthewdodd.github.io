





1255. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MASHFORD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of June
, 1 basket, value 2s., and 9 loaves of bread, value 6s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Blake
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
