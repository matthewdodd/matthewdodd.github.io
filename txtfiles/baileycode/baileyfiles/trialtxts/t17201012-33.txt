             of Finchley

               feloniously stealing 2 Holland Shifts value 2 s. 2 Suits of Headclothes 3 s. a Callicoe Gown and Petticoat 1 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Jacob 
                     Rust 
                  




                
            , on the 30th of August
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's hired Servant
, that she came on Thursday noon, and went away the Monday Morning following, and the Goods were mist: that about a Fortnight, or three Weeks after, she was found at Enfield with the Gown upon her Back. The Jury found her
