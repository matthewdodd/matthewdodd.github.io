





OLD COURT, Thursday, April 10th, 1834.
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
532. 
               
                  ELIZABETH 
                  EVERY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of March
, at St. Martin-in-the-Fields


, 1 watch, value 5l.; and 1 watch-chain, value 3l., the goods of 
                     
                        Abraham Harrison 
                        Dry
                     




                  , her master, in his dwelling-house
            ; to which she 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 48. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
