






43. (L.) 
               
                  James 
                  Wright 
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing one iron bar, val. 18 d. and one iron padlock, val. 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Woodroff 
                  




                
            , Nov. 20.
 ++
The prisoner was stopped about 4 in the morning, on the 20th of Nov. with the bar and lock upon him, in Sun-court, Cornhill, by 
               
                  William 
                  Badow 
               



            , a watchman, which was proved to be the property of Mr. Woodroff, and was taken from his celler window that night.



               Guilty
            .
