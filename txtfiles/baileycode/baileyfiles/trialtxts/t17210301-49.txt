            , of St. Katharine by the Tower


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a piece of Silver value 4 s. and 23 s. 6 d. in Money from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Elizabeth 
                           Cofferon 
                        




, on the 24th of February
 last: It appeared that the Prosecutor being in a Fit the Prisoner came in to help her, took the Money out of her Pocket, and pretending to go out to fetch her something to smell to, laid a Guinea of it over her own Door, where it was afterwards found by her Direction. The Prisoner in her Defence said she found it on the Ground. The Jury considering the matter, found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
