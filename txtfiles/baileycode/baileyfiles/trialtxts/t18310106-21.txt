





293. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JOHNSON
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of December
. 1 jacket, value 7s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Mitchell
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  MITCHELL
               



            . I am mate of the brig
. She was in the dock, and the prisoner was at work on board of her- I missed this jacket from the companion; I know it is mine.


                  RICHARD 
                  CROSS
               



            . The prisoner pointed out Benjamin's shop, and said he had sold the jacket there.


                  THOMAS 
                  SPALL
               



            . I received this jacket from Benjamin.


                  MOSES 
                  BENJAMIN
               



            . This jacket I bought of the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner. I am guilty of this crime.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
