





628. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  POOLE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of February
, 1 cloak, value 8s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Francis 
                  Cotton
               




            .
FRANCIS COTTON. I am a pawnbroker
, and live in Shoreditch


. I saw the prisoner in the road, running from my house, with this cloak, which had been hanging at least five yards within the shop - he dropped it, and I took him.


                  JOHN 
                  VANN
               



            . I am an officer, and received him in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner. I know nothing about it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
