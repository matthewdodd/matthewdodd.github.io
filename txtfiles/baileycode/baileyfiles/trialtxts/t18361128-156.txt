



156. 




MICHAEL DOYLE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
1st of December, 1 handkerchief value 4s., the goods of 



William Hodson, from his person.







WILLIAM HODSON
. I live in Panton-street, and am a surveyor, On the morning of the 1st of December I was at the corner of Holborn, in Drury-lane—l felt a tug, and missed my handkerchief—I saw prisoner running, and followed him—he threw down my handkerchief—I took it up, and then caught him.





GEORGE PALMER
 (policeman-sergeant E 17.) I took the prisoner, and have the handkerchief.




GUILTY
. *Aged 15—


Transported for Seven Years.
