





983. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  CLARK
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of May
, 1 jacket, value 20s.; 1 pair of trousers, value 8s.; and 1 shirt, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Richard 
                  Clinton
               




            .


                  CHARLOTTE 
                  HEWSTON
               



            . I lodge in the same house with Richard Clinton, in James-street, Wellington-square


. The prisoner lodged there a little while - the bundle which contained the articles stated, was in my care; the prisoner came to me last Wednesday week, and said Richard Clinton had sent him for it, and I gave it him.


                  CHARLES 
                  ESMOND
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a jacket and trousers pawned by the prisoner on the 9th of May.


                  RICHARD 
                  CLINTON
               



            . These are my property, I left them in the bundle - I did not send the prisoner for them.
Prisoner's Defence. He sent me for them. Witness. No, I did not.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
