







                     Christian 
                     Clark 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Shoes, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Samuel 
                           Davis 
                        




, the 30th of July
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner took the Shoes off the Spikes, at the Shop Window and ran away, but being pursu'd was apprehended. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10d.
            



               Transportation
            .
