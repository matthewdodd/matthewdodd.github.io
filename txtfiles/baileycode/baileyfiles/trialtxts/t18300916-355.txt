





1837. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of August
, 1 looking-glass, value 8s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William Henry 
                     Colton
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  HENRY COLTON
               



            . I am a carver and gilder
, and live at Islington


. On the 6th of August I was returning to my shop, about twelve o'clock - I saw a man standing one door from my shop, and as I got to my door I met the prisoner coming out with this glass; I went and asked my wife if she had sold it - she said No; I followed the prisoner and the other man - he threw the glass against a fence; I pursued, and took him.
Prisoner. The looking-glass was standing at the door. Witness. No, it was inside, on a wash-hand stand.


                  CHARLES 
                  WRIGHT
               



            . I am a Police-constable. I received charge of the prisoner - he told me a man had employed him to carry it, and he was to receive 1s.; the prosecutor desired me to keep him while he pursued the other, but he got away.


                  THOMAS 
                  MOULTON
               



            . I saw the prisoner throw away the glass - I took it up.
Prisoner. I did it through distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20 - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
