







362. 
               
                  MARY 
                  SMITH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of March

, a watch, value 4l. and a seal, value 6d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Trappitt
                  



               
            , AND 
               
                  ELIZABETH 
                  MORLEY
               



            , was indicted for 


               receiving the same, knowing it to have been feloniously stolen
            .
THE circumstances of this case, clearly brought home the guilt against 
               
                  Mary 
                  Smith
               



            ; but are too indelicate a nature to be inserted here.
THE evidence against Morley was not equally conclusive of her guilt.
SMITH, 

               GUILTY
            , aged 23.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
MORLEY, 

               NOT GUILTY
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
