





803. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DIXON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 12th day of July
 last, one silk handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Thomberg
                  




               
            .
The Prosecutor caught the prisoner in Grace-church-street


, with the handkerchief in his hand.




               GUILTY, 10 d.
            





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   HOTHAM
               



            .
