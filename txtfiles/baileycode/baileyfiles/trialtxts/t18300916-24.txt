





1490. 
               
                  
                     ALEXANDER 
                     BARBER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of July
, 5 pairs of shoes, value 7s., and 4 pairs of lasts, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Patterson
                        




                     
                  , his master
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 26. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
