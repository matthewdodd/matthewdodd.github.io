





1235. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     THOMPSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of August
, a handkerchief, value 2 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Brown
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  BROWN
               



            . I am a half-pay captain
. On the 20th of August, about a quarter before nine o'clock, I was walking in Gower-street, and turning into Bedford-square


, I felt somebody at my pocket - turned round, and found the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand. I took him across the square, and met Leonard, who took him - he had got it out of my pocket.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
