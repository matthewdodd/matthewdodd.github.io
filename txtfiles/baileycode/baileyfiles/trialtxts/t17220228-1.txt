







                     Thomas 
                     Pitman 
                  



               
            , of St. Michael Crooked lane


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing out of the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Coleman 
                        




                      
                  , 69 pound of Iron Wire, value 48 s.
             and 2 pound of Brass Wire, value 4 s. the Goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Coleman 
               



            , on the 8th of February
 last. It appear'd that the prisoner was stopt offering Wire to sale, and being examin'd, confest that he us'd to come to the Prosecutor's Shop to buy small quantities of Wire, and convey'd away the Goods in the Indictment. His Confession before Sir 
               
                  Francis 
                  Forbes 
               



             was read in Court. 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. and 10 d.
