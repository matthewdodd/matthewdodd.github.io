





611. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     TAYLOR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of February
, 24 yards of gingham, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  James 
                  Brown
               




            .


                  JAMES 
                  BROWN
               



            . I am a linen-draper
, and live in High-street, Shadwell


. On the 9th of February, I saw the prisoner take the gingham from within my door - he tucked it under his arm, and I took him with it, twenty or thirty yards from my house.(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner put in a written defence, pleading poverty



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
