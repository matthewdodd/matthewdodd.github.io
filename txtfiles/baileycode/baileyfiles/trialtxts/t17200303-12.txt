







                     Thomas 
                     Kingham 
                  



               
             of St. Sepulcheres


, was indicted for 


               stealing 12 pair of Yarn Stockings, out of the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Low 
                        




 on the: 6th of January
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner and another sat on a Bench  under the Prosecutor's Shop
 Window, till he went out, then the Prisoner got upon the Bench, reacht over the Grate, took the Goods and gave them to the other Person. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
