





1159. 
               
                  
                     CATHARINE 
                     ARMSTRONG
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of July
, one gown, value 7 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Hulme
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  PERRY
               



            . I am apprentice to Mr. 
               
                  James 
                  Hulme
               



            , a pawnbroker
, who lives in Museum-street, Bloomsbury


. On the 26th of July the prisoner came to the shop to pledge two articles, and as she went out she took this gown down, put it under her shawl and went out quickly. I sent the boy after her and followed myself; I saw him stop her and take it from under her shawl.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before J. Vaillant, Esq.
