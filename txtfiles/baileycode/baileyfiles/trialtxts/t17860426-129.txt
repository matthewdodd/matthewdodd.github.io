





450. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SMITH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 19th day of April
, one linen sheet, value 18 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           West
                        




                     
                  , being in his lodging-room
            .


                  Elizabeth
                   Evans
               



             took the sheet from the prisoner, wrapped round his waist.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
