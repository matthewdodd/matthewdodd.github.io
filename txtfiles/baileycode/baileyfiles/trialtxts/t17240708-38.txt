







                     Edward 
                     Stutton 
                  



               
            , of St. James, Duke's Place


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing half an Ounce of Human Hair
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Bates 
                  




                
            , the 4th of this present July
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and took the Goods. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
