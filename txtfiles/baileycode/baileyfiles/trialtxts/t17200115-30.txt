







                     Elizabeth 
                     Rigby 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles without Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Stuff Gown value 8s. a Petticoat value 5 s. and a pair of Bodice value 4 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Hayes 
                  




                
            , on the 29th of December
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that the Prisoner, who lodged in the same Room with her, took her Key, open'd her Trunk, and took the Goods mentioned in the Indictment; and that she confest the same before the Justice. Mr. Stretch deposed, that the Prisoner pawned the Petticoat to him; and that he was at the Charge of the Prosecution. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
