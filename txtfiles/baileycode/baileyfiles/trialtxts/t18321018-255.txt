





2464. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     PULLMAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of October
, 1 counterpane, value 2s.; 1 sheet, value 1s. 6d.; 1 tablecloth, value 6d.; 1 pillow-case, value 1d.; 1 hearth-rug, value 1s., and 1 blanket, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John Richard 
                     Short
                  




               
            .


                  ISABELLA 
                  SHORT
               



            . I am the wife of John Richard Short. The prisoner lodged ten days in our house; she took the lodging weekly - she went away without notice, and left the key in the door, where we found it the next morning - her husband is a compositor
, and did not live with her; she came to me as single; we missed the property stated on the Tuesday morning, and on the Friday she was taken; she owed me one week's rent.


                  JOHN 
                  OWEN
               



             ELDRED. I am a pawnbroker. I have a counterpane, pawned for 2s., a sheet 1s. 6d., and a table-cloth and pillow-case for 1s.; I cannot say who by.
MRS. SHORT. This is my property; I lost this and a great deal more, which we have not found; it was all lent to the prisoner.
Prisoner. I know I pawned them, but with the intention of recovering them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 45. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
