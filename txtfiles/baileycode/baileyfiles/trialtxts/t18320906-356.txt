





Fifth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
2087. 
               
                  
                     DAVID 
                     WATERS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of August
, 1 pair of shoes, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Brown
               




            .


                  THOMAS 
                  BROWN
               



            . I am a shoemaker
. On the 16th of August the prisoner came, took these boots, and walked out of my shop with them; he got about twenty yards - I took him with them; I did not know him before.
Prisoner's Defence. I was coming through Somers'-town with a young man; we were both drunk, and I did not know where I was.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18 - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
