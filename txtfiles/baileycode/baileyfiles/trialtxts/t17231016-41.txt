








                     Thomas 
                     Hare 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Buttolph's Bishopsgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 Coach Glasses, value 5 l.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Nathan 
                     Watts 
                  



                
            , the 24th of December

 last. He was also indicted a second Time for 


               feloniously stealing, 1 Coach Glass, value 16 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Tailor 
                  



                
            , the 24th of December

 last. The Fact was plainly prov'd, both by the Evidence, and the Prisoner's own Confession. The Jury found him 

               guilty of both Indictments
            , and of Watts's 


               to the Value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
