





2029. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  JOHNSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of October
, 4 pairs of clogs, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Howard
               




            .


                  JAMES 
                  WILLIAM BURRIER
               



            . I am foreman to John Howard, a bootmaker
 of St. Martin's-court. On the 25th of October the officer brought the prisoner in with these clogs, which are my master's.


                  JOHN 
                  GROOM
               



            . I am an officer. On the 25th of October, about ten minutes past seven o'clock, I saw the prisoner take up these clogs from the door - I watched him, knowing him, and stopped him - he threw them down.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported Seven Years
            .
