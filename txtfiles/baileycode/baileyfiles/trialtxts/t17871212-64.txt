






69. 
               

                              LEWIS 
                              SANDERS
                           



                        
                      and JANE
                   his wife
, alias 
                        
JANE NORRIS




            , wereindicted, for that 


               they, on the 20th of April
, did take away, with intent to steal, imbezzle and purloin, three check curtain, value 6 s. a bolster, value 5 s. a looking-glass, value 2 s. a tea kettle, value 1 s. a candlestick, value 6 d. the property of 
                     
                        Magdalen 
                        Hunter
                     




                  , in a lodging room
            .
The prisoner Jane pawned the things.


                  LEWIS
                   SAUNDERS
               



            , 

               NOT GUILTY
            .


                  JANE
                   SAUNDERS
               



            , 

               GUILTY
            .





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice HEATH.
