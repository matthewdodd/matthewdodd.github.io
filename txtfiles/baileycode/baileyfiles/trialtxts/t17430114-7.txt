





71. 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Miller 
                  



               
            , of St James, Westminster, was indicted for 


               stealing three Sheets and two Table-Cloths, the Goods of the Right Honourable the 
                        
                           Earl
                            of Harrington
                        




; one Sheet, the Property of 
                     
                        Lorano 
                        Clutterbuck 
                     



                   ; two Table-Cloths, the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Lewis 
                        



                      
                  , Gent
. and one Table-Cloth
            , the Property of 
               
                  Rose 
                  Eustace 
               



             , December the 11th
.
Rose Eustace. I am a Washer-woman
: On the 11th of December I missed this Linnen, and charged the Prisoner with them, and he confessed he took them.


                  Margaret 
                  Turner 
               



            . I heard Mrs Eustace charge him with the taking them, and he confessed it: I saw his Mother bring them Home.


                  Catherine 
                  Cathery 
               



            . I saw his Mother deliver the Linnen to the Prosecutrix; and the Prisoner owned before Justice Deveil, that he took them. 


               Guilty 10 d.
