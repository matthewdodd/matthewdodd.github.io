





1284. 
               
                  CRISTOPHER 
                  HARRIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        John 
                        Bayless
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  HICKON WING
               



            . I am a glazier, and live in Newport-street, Long-acre


. On the 28th of April, about four o'clock in the afternoon, I was at my door, and saw the prisoner take a handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket; I went out and told him, then followed, and took it from the prisoner's jacket.


                  JOHN 
                  BAYLESS
               



            . On the 28th of April I was in Newport-street, and had my handkerchief taken - I ran and took the prisoner with it.


                  JOHN 
                  GROOM
               



            . I am an officer and took the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was going along; the gentleman took the handkerchief from me; I said it was not his.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
