





First London Jury - before Mr. Recorder.
1365. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  KNOTT
               




             was indicted for 


               feloniously sending a letter to 
                     
                        William 
                        Knott
                     




                  , Esq.
, threatening to accuse him of a crime punishable with death, with intent to extort money from him
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 60.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
