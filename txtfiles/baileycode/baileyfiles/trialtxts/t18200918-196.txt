





1095. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BRIANT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of August
, 18 yards of printed calico, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas James 
                     Love
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  JAMES LOVE
               



            . I am a linen-draper
, and live at Shadwell


. On the 19th of August this calico hung at the door inside the shop. Shade alarmed me, I ran out, and saw the prisoner thirty yards off, with another man. He called out to the other man
"Go it, Jack!" I took him in charge.


                  HENRY 
                  SHADE
               



            . I am a cabinet-maker, and live at Ratcliff-highway. I was coming from a public-house, about eleven o'clock at night, nearly opposite Love's, and saw the prisoner and another man standing on his steps. I saw the prisoner cut something and put it on his arm, it appeared to be dark cloth - I informed Love, and we pursued them. The prisoner cried out to his companion,
"Go it, Jack!" I supposed he had the calico.
Prisoner's Defence. The gentleman said he would take me whether I was the man or not.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
