





2108. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  LARKINS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of September
, two 10l., and one 5l. Bank notes, the property of 
                     
                        Samuel 
                        Hinder
                     




                  , his master
            .
SAMUEL HINDER. I keep the Manchester Arms public-house, Adam-street East, Manchester-square




. The prisoner lived with me twice, but only two months the last time. On the 1st of September I gave him two 10l. and one 5l. notes, to go to Messrs. Combe and Delafield's, to get silver, where he had been several times before; I never saw him again till last Saturday. but I was on the look out. I have not recovered a farthing of the property.


                  PHILIP 
                  WEBSTER
               



            . I apprehended the prisoner, at the Griffin public-house, and told him the charge - he said,"I have spent it."



               GUILTY
            . Aged 27.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
