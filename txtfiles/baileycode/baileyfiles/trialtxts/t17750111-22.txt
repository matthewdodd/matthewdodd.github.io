





123. (L.) 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM
                      BUTTS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value ten-pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     William
                      Cotton
                  




               
            , January 5
. ++


                  William
                   Cotton
               



            . As I was coming over London Bridge


 I went to button my right hand pocket; I found a hand in my other pocket, I turned round, I saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand; he begged forgivness, I told him I had lost a great many handkerchiefs, and I charged a constable with him.
The constable deposed, that he heard the prisoner acknowledge the charge, and say that he did it through necessity.
The handkerchief was produced in court and deposed to by the prosecutor.
Prisoner's Defence.
I found the handkerchief on the ground.



               Guilty
            .
