





25. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CORNELIUS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of December
, one cloth coat, value 3 s. two cloth jackets, 12 d. one man's hat, value 12 d. two waistcoats, value 12 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Hugh 
                     M'Coy
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the things upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
