







                     Ann 
                     Leadbeater 
                  



               
            , of St. Bartholomew, near the Exchange


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a quilted Petticoat, Value 20 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Susan 
                     Staples 
                  




                
            , the 27th of January
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and took the Petticoat and sold it to 
               
                  Susan 
                  Elliot 
               



            ; her Confession of the Fact was read in Court, but she deny'd it at the Bar. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
