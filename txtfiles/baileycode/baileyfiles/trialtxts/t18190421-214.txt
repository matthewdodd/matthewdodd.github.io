





714. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     BROWN
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of April
, one coach-glass, value 5 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Jenkins
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  STROUD
               



            . I had the care of George Jenkins's coach. His glass was taken out of the same mews.


                  RICHARD 
                  YOUNGMAN
               



            . I saw the prisoner lay the glasses down at the same time with the others.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
