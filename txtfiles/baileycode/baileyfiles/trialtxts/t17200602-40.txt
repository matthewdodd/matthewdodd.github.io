







                     Gilbert 
                     Beard 
                  



               
            , of St. John at Hackney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Linnen Shirt value 4 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Daniel 
                     Fowler 
                  




                
             on the 14th of May
 last. The prosecutor deposed that he came into his House and called for a Pint of Drink, went into the next Room and took the Shirt and went away with it; that he followed him and found it in his Bosom. The Jury considering the matter found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
