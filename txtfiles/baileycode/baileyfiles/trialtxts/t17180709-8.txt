







                     William 
                     Ward 
                  



               
            , of Barnet


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Ram, value 40 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Charleton 
                  




                
            , the 15th of June
 last. The Prosecutor deposed his Ram was lost out of his Ground, and the Head and Skin found buried in the Prisoner's Ground, and a Quarter of the Mutton hid between the Tyling. The Fact being plain, the Jury found him 

               Guilty
            . 


               To be Transported
            .
