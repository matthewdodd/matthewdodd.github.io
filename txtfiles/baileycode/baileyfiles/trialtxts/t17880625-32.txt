







434. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     MATTHEWS
                  



               
            , 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WRIGHT
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     REYNOLDS
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of June
, six frocks, value 2 s. two pair of stays, value 2 s. two shawls, value 2 s. two aprons, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Hurst
                  




               
            .
The prisoner Wright was found with the things, and the other two prisoners were in company.
ALL THREE 

               GUILTY
            .






               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
