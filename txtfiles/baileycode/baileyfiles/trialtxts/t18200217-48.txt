





397. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     GARDNER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of February
, one handkerchief, value 2 s., the property of 
                     
                        
                           Peter 
                           Adams
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  PETER 
                  ADAMS
               



            . I am a solicitor
. On Saturday, the 19th of February, about twelve o'clock at noon, I was in Newgate-street


, and felt my handkerchief go from pocket - I turned round, and discovered the prisoner close behind me with it in his hand; I secured and took him to the Compter. He pleaded distress - 6 s. was found on him. I had seen him in Holborn, talking to other boys.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was walking with two lads before me, one of them threw the handkerchief down, and I picked it up.



               GUILTY
             Aged 16.




               Transported for Life
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
