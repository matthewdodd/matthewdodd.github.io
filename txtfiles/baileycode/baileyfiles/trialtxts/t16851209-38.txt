








                     Andrew 
                     Stapely
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     Daniel 
                     Taylor
                  



               
            , were Indicted for 


               stealing two Hoggs, in the Parish of St Martins in the Fields


, value 3 l.
             On the 17th of October
 last, from 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Page
                  




               
            : the former of them 


               pleaded Guilty
             upon his Arraignment, to that and all other Felonys within the benefit of his Clergy; but the latter pleading not Guilty, upon his Tryal it appeared that he had sold them to one Blisset of Islington; to which he pleaded that Stapely was his Master and imployed him to sell them; but that not being made out, he was found 


               Guilty, though favoured with the penalty of Perry Larceny only
            .

[Taylor: Whipping. See summary.]
