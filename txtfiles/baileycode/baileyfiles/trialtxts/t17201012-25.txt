







                     Elizabeth 
                     Firth 
                  



               
            , of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Poplin Gown and Petticoat value 8 s. a Camblet Gown and Petticoat value 6 s. A pair of Stayes 2 s. a Silk Hood 1 s. and 5 s. in Money
            , the Goods and Money of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Leadbeater 
                  




                
            , on the 12th of September
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's Lodger
, took the Goods and went away with them; that she was taken 4 Days afterwards, and the Goods found upon her, which were produced in Court, and owned by the Prosecutor. The Jury found her 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
            .
