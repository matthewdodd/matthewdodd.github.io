





17. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     HATFIELD
                  




               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 14th of November
, feloniously and without lawfull excuse, had in his custody and possession two forged 1 l. notes each
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            , aged 24.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
