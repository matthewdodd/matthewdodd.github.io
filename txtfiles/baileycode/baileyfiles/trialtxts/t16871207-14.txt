







                     Thomas 
                     Ward
                  



               
            , was Indicted for 


               Stealing from behind a Stage Coach on the first of November
 last, in St. Andrews Holbourn


, one pair of Holland Sheets, value 10 s. twelve Flaxen Table cloths, value 6 s. twenty one Cambrick Handkerchiefs, value 10 s. one Strip'd Mantua, eight Hollan Handkerchiefs, twenty Bisket Cakes value 20 d. being in a Box, and the goods of 
                     
Squire
 
                        Whitlock
                     



; it appearing he had cut the string with which the Box was fastned behind the Coach, and was carrying away the Box when the Coachman's Man overtook him and found the goods upon him, which being plain Evidence, he was brought in 

               guilty
             of the Felony.
