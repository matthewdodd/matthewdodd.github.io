



549. 




JANE THOMPSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
10th of December, 132 yards of ribbon, value 3l.; 56 yards of lace, value 3l.; 2 pairs of gloves, value 4s.; 1 pair of mitts, value 6d.; and 2 scarfs, value 6s.; the goods of Sarah Goodwin; to which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 22.—


Transported for Seven Years.—



Recommended to the Penitentiary for One Year.
