







                     Thomas 
                     Purcel 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Looking-Glass, in the Shop
             of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Wreckstraw 
                  




                
             , the 11th of this Instant February
.
Mrs. Wreckstraw depos'd, That as she was sitting in her Shop, she heard a Noise, and saw a Man take a Glass, from the Stall, and put it under his Cloak, and walk off; that she presently ran out crying stop Thief , and the Prisoner being pursued, was taken, but had first put the Glass into a Neighbour's Window.


                  Elizabeth 
                  Wood 
               



             depos'd, That she saw the Prisoner take the Glass from the Prosecutor's Shop Window.
The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
