





1187. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     BABER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of May
, 2 shawls, value 8s.; 2 shifts, value 4s.; 2 gowns, value 9s.; 1 pair of stays, value 3s.; 2 night gowns, value 4s.; 1 shirt, value 1s., and 1 frock, value 1s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Frances 
                     Wood
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  TURNER
               



            . I am a watchman. I met the prisoner on the 28th of May, at half-past four o'clock in the morning, with a bundle of women's clothes, in Great Ormond-street, Queen-square - I stopped him and asked how he came by them; he said he brought them from his mother's in Monmouth-street, and was going to take them to Finsbury-square.


                  FRANCES 
                  WOOD
               



            . My mother is a widow, and lives in Monmouth-street


. I left my situation on the 1st of May, and lived with my mother; the prisoner is my brother - he came there on the 28th of May, and my mother made him a convenience to sleep under the same roof; he went away without giving us notice - I missed the articles stated; I am a widow.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 35.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
