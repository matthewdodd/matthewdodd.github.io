



2943. 




JOSEPH WORHALL
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
7th of October, 1 copying-machine, value 2l. 10s., the goods of 



George Watson.






MARY ANN WATSON
. I am the wife of George Watson, of Portugal-street, Lincoln's-inn Fields. On the 7th of October, after six o'clock in the evening, I saw the prisoner, or some person very much like him, come into the shop and take the copying-machine now produced away.





FRANCIS SNELLING
. I am ostler at the Grange public-house. On the evening of the 7th of October I met the prisoner running down Carey. street—he dropped this machine.





WILLIAM DAVIS
 (police-sergeant S 15.) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read) the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
