339. 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Paxton 
                  



               
             , was indicted for 


               stealing 2 pair of leather breeches, val. 9 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Usher 
                  




                
            , May 2
.


                  George 
                  Usher 
               



            . I am a breeches maker
; the prisoner has worked with me this three years: I have miss'd breeches several times, when I taxed him he confessed he had stole three pair more than is in the indictment. I always took him for an honest man before.


                  Edward 
                  Timbril 
               



            . I was present when the two pair of breeches were found, and heard him confess, as before mentioned, stealing three pair more.




               Guilty 10 d
            .
