



2376. 




MARY ANN PIPE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
4th of september, 1 snuff-box, value 1s.; 6 half-crowns, and 4 shillings; the property of 



Allan Pipe; to which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 16.—Recommended to mercy.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Recommended to be 



Confined Nine Months in the Penitentiary.
