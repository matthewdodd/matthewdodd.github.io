






OLD COURT. THURSDAY, JULY 13.
Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
2028. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     NEWMAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of August

, 1 pelerine, value 2s.; 2 night-caps, value 3s.; 4 pairs of drawers, value 10s.; 5 pairs of stockings, value 5s.; 5 shifts, value 5s.; 2 slips, value 5s.; 1 apron, value 6d.; 6 napkins, value 7s., and 2 handkerchiefs,value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Ramsay
                  



               
            ; and 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     LANGLEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously receiving the same, well knowing them to have been stolen
            . - To which they both pleaded




               GUILTY
            . - 



               Transported for Seven Years
