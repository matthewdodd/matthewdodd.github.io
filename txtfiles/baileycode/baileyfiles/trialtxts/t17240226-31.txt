







                     Margaret 
                     Jackson 
                  



               
            , of St. Andrews Holborn


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Stew-Pan
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Robinson 
                  




                
            , the 3d of February
 last. It appeared by the Evidence that the Prisoner was seen to take the Stew-Pan out of the Prosecutor's Yard, and was apprehended immediately with the Pan. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
