







                     John 
                     Mann 
                  



               
            , of Billingsgate


, was indicted for 


               stealing three Jackets val 12 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Loan 
                  




                
            , Oct. 12. It appear'd, that as the Prosecutor's Ship lay in Billingsgate Dock


, the Prisoner, who was a Waterman
, got into the Cabbin about Midnight, and took away the Goods. He was perceived just as he went off; and being pursued, he dropt the Jackets, and was taken in Thames-Street. 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
            .
