






896. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CASS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 26th of July
 last, one linen handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     White
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was detected in picking the prosecutor's pocket by 
               
                  Gabriel
                   Johnson
               



            .



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
