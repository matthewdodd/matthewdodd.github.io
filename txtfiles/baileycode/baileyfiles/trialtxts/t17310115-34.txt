







                     Thomas 
                     Wright 
                  



               
            , of Enfield


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing an Iron Lock and Bolt of a Sluce
             belonging to the 
               
Governour and Company
 of the New River Water


            , the 9th of May
 last.
The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, and pleaded, That he seeing two Men have them, took the Things from them, and was going to deliver them to the Walk's-Man, when the Surveyor of the Works met him, and charg'd him with having taken them from the Sluce . He like wise call'd some Persons to his Reputation, but the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10d
            .
