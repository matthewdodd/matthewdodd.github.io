





737. 
               
                  JULIET 
                  BROOKS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of March
, two quart pots, value 2s., and two pint-pots, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Richard Arnold 
                  Hullah
               




            .


                  RICHARD 
                  ARNOLD HULLAH
               



            . I am a publican
, and live at Pentonville


; I lost the pots.


                  ANN 
                  ARCHER
               



            . I was looking out of my window, and saw the prisoner go into the garden, take the pots, and put them under her gown. I told a man, who stopped her.


                  JOHN 
                  STOCKDALE
               



            . I went after the prisoner, and secured her; she pulled four pots from under her gown, and gave them to me; I gave her in charge.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was in distress. I beg for mercy.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 42.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
