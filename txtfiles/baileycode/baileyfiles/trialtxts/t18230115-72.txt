





243. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of January
, five brushes, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Chater
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  CHATER
               



            . I am an oilman
, and live in Wellclose-square


. I saw these brushes hanging on the door-post, about five minutes before they were stolen; the prisoner was brought back with them.


                  JOSEPH 
                  KAY
               



            . I live opposite Chater, and saw the prisoner take the brushes from the door-post, and go into the square. I followed, and secured him with them, and gave him in charge.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
