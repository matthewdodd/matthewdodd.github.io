







                     George 
                     Boughton 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Weston


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, a Cloth Coal, value 9 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  Solomon 
                  Flexly 
               




             , the 10th of this present October
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Coat was stolen out of Esquire Bulstrode's Coach House, and was immediately found upon the Prisoner. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10d.
            



               Transportation
            .
