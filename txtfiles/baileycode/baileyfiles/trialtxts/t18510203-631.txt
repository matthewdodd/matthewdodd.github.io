



631. 



EDWARD HINCE



; burglary, in the dwelling-house of 



George Goff, and stealing 1 coat, and other articles, value 2l.; his goods: and 1 waistcoat, 1s.; the goods of 



James Goff:—also, burglary in the dwelling-house of 



Elizabeth Johnson, and stealing 1 pair of slippers, 4d.; her goods:—also, unlawfully breaking the dwelling-house of 



Walter Henry Biddle with intent to enter the same, and the goods therein to steal; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
.—


Transported for Ten Years.
