





688. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     NOWLAND
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 4th of September
, six pound fifteen ounces weight of quicksilver, value 1 l. 10 s. and a bottle,value 2 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Davey
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     Josiah 
                     Roberts
                  



               
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
