







                     Henry 
                     Brewer 
                  



               
            , late of the Parish of St. Mary Woolnoth


, was indicted for 


               feloniously and privately stealing from 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Goddard 
                        




                      
                  , a Handkerchief, value 3 s.
             on the 13th of May
.
It appeared that the Prosecutor mist his Handkerchief in the Post-Office-Yard


, and seeing the Prisoner, and another Boy sneaking away, he follow'd them, and found it upon the Prisoner.
