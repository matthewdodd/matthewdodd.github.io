





511. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     ALLEN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               unlawfully, knowingly, and designingly obtaining from the servant of 
                     
                        
                           Lewis 
                           Johance
                        




                     
                  , a piece of blue Persian silk, under pretence that he was servant to 
                     
                        William
                         Tiffin
                     



                  , and sent by him for the same
            .



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first London Jury, before Mr. RECORDER.
