







                     Elizabeth 
                     Polson 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing wearing Apparel to the Value of 4 l. in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Maggot 
                        




, the 20th of March
 last. The Prosecutor's Wife deposed the Prisoner came into her House, went up Stairs, took the Goods, and she apprehended her with the Goods upon her. The Prisoner pleaded that the Prosecutor gave her the Goods, bidding her go in and take them, but the Jury seeing no Reason to believe her, found her 


               guilty to the value of 4 s.10 d
            .(


               Transportation
            .)
