





889. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     PRICE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of March
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Whitehead
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
THOMAS WHITEHEAD. I am a servant
. I was in Piccadilly


 on the 28th of February, in the evening, and had a pocket-handkerchief in my right-hand pocket; a person asked if I was aware I had lost my handkerchief, and pointed out the prisoner - I ran and took him, and found my handkerchief concealed under his waistcoat.


                  THOMAS 
                  HEAD
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the handkerchief, and told the prosecutor of it, who took him.


                  THOMAS 
                  TULL
               



            . I am an officer, and took him.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I did it from distress - the prosecutor had the handkerchief half out of his pocket; I said, "I shall have something to eat to night" - I thought I should go out of the country, and I wish to go; I told the prosecutor so.


                  THOMAS 
                  TULL
               



            . He said he had had nothing to eat for twenty-four hours.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 23. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
