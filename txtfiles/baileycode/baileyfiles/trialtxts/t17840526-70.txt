





588. 
               
                  HUMPHRY 
                  DAVIS
               



             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 22d day of May
, one deal box, value 6 d. two cloth coats, value 10 s. two cloth waistcoats, value 5 s. two pair of breeches, value 5 s. two pair of stockings, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Stuart
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was employed to carry a box of cloaths, and he went off with it, and was taken soon after with some of the things.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
