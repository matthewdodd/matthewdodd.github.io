





1953. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     CAPSEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           George John 
                           Parry
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  FREDERICK 
                  MILES
               



             (Police-serjeant G 14). On the 3rd of August I was on duty in Goswell-street; I saw the prisoner and two others following the prosecutor and a lady - I followed them, and saw the prisoner take up the prosecutor's coat, and take the handkerchief; I took him - this is the handkerchief.


                  GEORGE 
                  JOHN PARRY
               



            . I am a solicitor
. This is my handkerchief - I lost it from my pocket about half-past seven o'clock, on the 3rd of August, in Goswell-street


, on my way to Pentonville; I was walking with a lady, and did not miss it till the officer told me of it.
The prisoner received a good character.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
