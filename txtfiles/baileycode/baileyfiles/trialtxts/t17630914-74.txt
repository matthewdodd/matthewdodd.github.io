





446. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Wallis 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a silver watch, val. 20 s. a pair of pumps, val. 1 s. a silk handkerchief, and a linnen stock
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Pavis 
                  




                
            , Aug. 7
. +
The prosecutor lives at Hornchurch, in Essex


, the prisoner worked with him at labouring work, and lodged in his house, he turned back when he got in the field near his work; the prosecutor, when he came home at night, missed the things mentioned, he was pursued and taken at Mile-end with the things upon him.



               Guilty
            . 


               T.
