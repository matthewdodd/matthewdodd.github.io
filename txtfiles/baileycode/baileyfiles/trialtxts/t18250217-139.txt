





523. 
               
                  HENRY 
                  CHARNICK
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of February
, two coats, value 20 s.
            , the goods of Sir


                  William 
                  Young
               




            , Bart
.


                  ROBERT 
                  MINISTER
               



            . I am in the service of Sir William Young, of Wimpole Street


. I missed two coats of his from the servants' hall, about ten o'clock in the morning of the 17th of February. I had seen them safe about seven.


                  SOPHIA 
                  WARD
               



            . I am in the service of Lady Young. On the morning of the 17th of February, I was coming out of the housekeeper's room, and in passing the door of the servants' hall, I saw the prisoner come from behind the door, with two coats on his arm, which I knew to be the footman's; - he went to the ball door, and I followed him; he ran up the area steps, and was stopped at the corner of Devonshire Street. I did not lose sight of him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  MICHAEL 
                  MORRIS
               



            . I am a constable, and took the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence. I know nothing at all about it, nor was I down the area; I was going along the street, and a man stopped me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
