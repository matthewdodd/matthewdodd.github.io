







                  Mary 
                  Shepherd
               



             was Indicted for 


               stealing a Flanders Head-dress, val. 3. l. 10 s. a Pair of Sleeves, 10 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Adams
               




            . There was no one in the House when the Goods were miss'd, but the Prisoner, which was September the 23th
 she came occasionally to fetch a Hood she had sent to Mrs. Adams to be wash'd, and whilst her back was turn'd she took the Goods. She was found 

               Guilty
