







                     Sarah 
                     Roberts 
                  



               
            , of St. Paul's Covent-Garden


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Spoon value 8 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Amos 
                     Fern 
                  




                
            , on the 28th of June
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that he lost his Spoon from the Rose Tavern, Covent-Garden


. Mr. Rawlins deposed that the Prisoner brought the Spoon to him to pawn, but there being the Prosecutor's Name and Place of Abode ingraved thereon, he carry'd the Prisoner and Spoon to the Prosecutor, who owned it. The Spoon was produced in Court. The Prisoner pleaded that one Smith brought the Spoon to her to pawn for him, but could not prove it: She also called several to her Reputation. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
