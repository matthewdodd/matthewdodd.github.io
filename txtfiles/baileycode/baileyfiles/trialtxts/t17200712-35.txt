







                     Edward 
                     Husler 
                  



               
            , of St. James's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing two Cloth Seats of a Coach value 20 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Edwin 
                  




                
             Esq. on the 5th of July
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner being seen to draw something out of the Coach, was pursued and taken within 30 Yards of the Place, with one of the Seats upon him. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
