





103. 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Monroe 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing 8 yards and a half of woollen cloth, val. 33 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Francis 
                           Hutchens 
                        




                      
                  , in the shop of the said Francis
            , Dec. 21
. +


                  Francis 
                  Hutchens 
               



            . I know this piece of cloth to be my property, but I did not see the prisoner take it.


                  Ann 
                  Gunthorp 
               



            . On Friday the 21st of Dec. about a quarter before 4 o'clock in the afternoon, I saw the prisoner stand some time looking to the prosecutor's window, and I suspected him; then I saw him take out that piece of woollen cloth and put it under his great coat, and ran away.


                  George 
                  Sparrow 
               



            . I took him in Mincing-lane, about 5 minutes after he had took it, and took it from under his great coat.
Prisoner's Defence.
I don't know what to say. 


               Guilty of stealing, but not out of the shop
