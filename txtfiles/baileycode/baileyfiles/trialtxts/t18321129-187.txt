





188. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BUCKMORE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of October
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s. 6d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Attenborough
                        




                     
                   and another; and that he had been before convicted of felony
            .


                  HENRY 
                  CHIDGEY
               



            . I was near the shop of Mr. Attenborough, in Crown-street, Finsbury


, on the 25th of October; I saw the prisoner walk by, and take up the handkerchief, and look at it - I watched him; there was another with him; they went to an oil-shop, and then came back: the prisoner took the handkerchief, and I gave information.


                  JOHN 
                  BAWLEY
               



            . I live with the prosecutors. We had information, and I ran out; a man stopped the prisoner with the handkerchief in his hand - I took it from him; this is it - it has our mark on it.


                  GEORGE 
                  HANLEY
               



            . I am an officer. The prisoner was given into my custody.


                  RICHARD 
                  ATTENBOROUGH
               



            . This handkerchief belongs to me and my uncle.


                  WILLIAM 
                  ATTFIELD
               



            . I am an officer of Worship-street. I have a certificate (which I got at Mr. Clark's office) of the former conviction of the prisoner - I apprehended him, and know he is the man.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for 14 Years
            .
