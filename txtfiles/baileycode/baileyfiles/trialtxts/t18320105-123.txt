





416. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of December
, 4 pint pots, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Philip 
                     Pearce
                  




               
            .
GEORGE BIDDELL. I live with Mr. 
               
                  Philip 
                  Pearce
               



            , he keeps the Duke of York public-house, Gloucester-street, Clerkenwell
. I saw the prisoner take these pots off the ground at a baker's shop, at the corner of Meredith-street


; I took him into my master's - I then went out again, brought in his hat, and there was another pot in it, belonging to another person - these three pots are my master's.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BAKER ASHTON
               



            . I am a Policeman. I took the prisoner, and have the pots.
Prisoner's Defence. I was going to take them to his master's.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
