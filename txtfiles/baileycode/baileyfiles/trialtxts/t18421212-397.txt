



397. 




WILLIAM AYLEWOOD, Jun., was indicted

for stealing, on the 
23rd of Nov., at 
St. Saviour's, Southwark, 13 sovereigns, 16 half-crowns, and 110l note, the property of 



William Aylewood, in his dwelling-house; to which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 13.—


Transported for Ten Years.—Parkhurst.


Before Mr. Baron Gurney.
