







                     Ann 
                     Squire 
                  



               
             , of St. Buttolph's Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               stealing Linnen to the Value of 4 l. in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Summer 
                        




, the 7th of March
 last. The Prosecutor deposed the Prisoner was his Servant
, and stole the Goods out of a Chest of Drawers. The Prisoner upon her Apprehension confes'd  the Fact before the Justice, and it being plainly proved, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 4 s.10 d
            .(


               Transportation
            .)
