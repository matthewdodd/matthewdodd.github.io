







598. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     GOLDSMITH
                  



               
            , 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     CLARKE
                  



               
            , 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CARROLL
                  



               
            , were indicted for 


               stealing on the 29th of June
 last, one piece of linen handkerchief, value 3 l. the property of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Bishop
                        




                     
                  , privily in his shop
            .
The prosecutor's wife saw the prisoner Goldsmith in the shop, with the handkerchiefs under his coat, the other boys were not in the shop.


                  JOHN
                   GOLDSMITH
               



            , 


               GUILTY. 4 s. 10 d.
            





               Transported for seven years
            .


                  GEORGE
                   CLARK JOHN CARROLL
               







               NOT GUILTY
            

Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
