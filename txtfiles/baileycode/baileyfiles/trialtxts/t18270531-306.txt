





1319. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  BENTLEY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of June
, 1 snuff-box, value 20s., the goods of 
                     
                        Radcliffe 
                        Pond
                     




                  , from his person
            .
RADCLIFFE POND. I live at Chelsea. I was in Pallmall


 on the 4th of June in the evening, and felt a trifling touch at my coat pocket; immediately after the prisoner endeavoured to rush by me; I caught him with my left hand, felt in my pocket, and missed my box, and found him putting it up his sleeve.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  EDWARD 
                  BOOTHMAN
               



            . I took him in charge.
Prisoner's Defence. I saw a boy drop it, and took it up; it was done to lead me into a snare.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
