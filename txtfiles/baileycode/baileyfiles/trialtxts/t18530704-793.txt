





793. 




CHARLES CUNNINGHAM,


 JAMES THOMPSON CURRIE
, and 



GEORGE THOMAS



, feloniously using a certain instrument upon the person of 



Eliza Mardon, with intent to procure her miscarriage.


MESSRS. PARRY
and
SLEIGH
conducted the Prosecution.

(MR. SERJEANT MILLER
appeared on behalf of the prisoner Cunningham, MR. DUNCAN
for Currie, and
MR. ROBINSON
for Thomas.)
(The particulars of the case were of a nature totally unfit for publication.)

CUNNINGHAM—


GUILTY
. Aged 48.—


Transported for Fifteen Years.


CURRIE and THOMAS—


NOT GUILTY
.
