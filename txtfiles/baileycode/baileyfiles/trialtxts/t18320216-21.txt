





527. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     BARKER
                  




               
             was indicted 


               for stealing, on the 13th of January
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas Bennett 
                           Humphrys
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  BENNETT HUMPHRYS
               



            . I am apprentice to a druggist
, in Tower-street. On the 13th of January, about four o'clock in the afternoon, I was on Holborn-hill


, and felt a pull at my pocket - I turned round, and saw my pocket handkerchief in the prisoner's left hand; he threw it over his shoulder - I immediately collared him, took it up, and took him to the watch-house.


                  BENJAMIN 
                  CATMULL
               



            . I received him in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. The gentleman accused me - I gave him leave to search me, which he did, and about twelve yards from where I stood he took up the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for 14 Years
            .
