





471. (M.) 
               
                  
                     JAMES
                      MARTIN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               ripping, cutting, and stealing 60 lb. of lead, the said lead being affixed to a stable
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert
                      Adams
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     James
                      Adams
                  



               
            , March 22d
. +


                  Robert
                   Lorrimore
               



            . I am a stone-mason: I was employed by Mr. Adams in a building in Mansfield-street


; there was 60 lb. of lead stole from the stables.


                  James
                   Darnford
               



            . I was employed to watch the buildings: I heard a cutting, and saw the prisoner; as soon as he perceived me he endeavoured to make his escape; I pursued him and took him; I found that lead cut and rolled up.
Prisoner's Defence.
I went into the building to ease myself.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
