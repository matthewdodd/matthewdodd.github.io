



1618. 




HOWARD AUGUSTINE STYLES



, feloniously forging and uttering an acceptance to a bill of exchange for the payment of 300l., with intent to defraud 



Henry Luard and others; having been before convicted.—Other Counts, varying the manner of stating the charge.


The prisoner pleaded



GUILTY
to the uttering, and
MR. HUDDLESTON
offered no evidence on the count for forgery.
  Aged 56.—


Transported for Life.
