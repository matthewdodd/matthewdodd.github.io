





1744. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BRALEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of September
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Martindale
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  MARTINDALE
               



            . On the 1st of September I was in Southampton-street, in the Strand


, between eleven and twelve o'clock at night; a person stopped me in the street, and asked if I had lost any thing. I then missed my handkerchief - I did not see the prisoner till he was in custody.


                  WILLIAM 
                  WHITTINGHAM
               



            . I saw the prisoner following Mr. Martindale, who had two ladies on his arm. I saw the prisoner make several attempts to get the handkerchief, and, at the top of Southampton-street, he got it out - I rushed forward, and he threw it behind him - I seized him, and another person picked it up for me. I got a person to overtake Mr. Martindale, and told him of it.
Prisoner's Defence. This officer seized me, and said,"D-n you, I have got you at last." The gentlemannever gave charge of me - I was standing at the top of the street when the gentleman and ladies came by - I moved to let them pass, but had not touched the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
