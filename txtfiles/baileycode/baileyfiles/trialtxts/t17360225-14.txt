





18. 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Pennelow 
                  



               
            , of St. Ann Westminster


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Cloth Drab Coat, value 10 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Norman 
                  




                
            , the 23d of February
.
The Prosecutor was my Lord Cardgan's Servant
; the Coat was left upon the Coach-Box, at the Sign of the Coach and Horses, at St. Ann's


; the Prisoner stole it from thence, and was taken with it upon him, about an Hundred Yards from the Place. 


               Guilty 10d.
