







                     James 
                     Vaughan 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Goods, in the Dwelling-House of 
                     
                        
                           David 
                           Davis 
                        




. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner took a Lodging in the Prosecutor's House, and took the Opportunity one Morning to get into the Landlady's Room, open a Drawer, and take out two Silver Spoons, &c. and afterwards went away from his Lodging, nor return'd any more, but was at last apprehended. There were several Circumstances prov'd, which render'd the Matter to plain to the Jury, that they found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
