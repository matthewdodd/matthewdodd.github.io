









                     Gilbert 
                     Wilson 
                  



                
            , of St. Dunstan's in the East



, was indicted for 


               stealing 30 Fathom of Tow-Line, value 3 l.
             the 9th of February

 last. The Prisoner came aboard the Hoy and took the Goods; and perceiving himself discover'd would have thrown it overboar'd, but it hetcht in the Boat and encangled it so that he could not get away, so he was taken about 7 or 8 Rods from the Hoy with the Goods: He had nothing to say for himself, and had confest it before the Justice, whereupon the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
