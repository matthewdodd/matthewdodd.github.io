







                     Elizabeth 
                     Walker 
                  



               
             , of the Parish of St.Ethelburg, in the Ward of Bishopsgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Gown, value 7 s. and divers Linen Cloths, value 30 s. in the Dwelling House
             of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Smith 
                  




                
            , the 9th of December
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and stole the Goods, and went away before the Family was up, was afterwards apprehended with some of the Goods upon her. The Fact being plainly prov'd the Jury found her 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
