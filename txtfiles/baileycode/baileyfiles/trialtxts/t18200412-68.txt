





418. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     READING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of August
, one sheet, value 3 s., and one shift, value 6 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Blain
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  BLAIN
               



            . I live in Cursitor-street


. The prisoner came to live with me as servant
, in August, and left me about a month ago. I charged her with robbing me - she was taken up, and gave me fifteen duplicates.


                  JAMES 
                  HOPPER
               



            . I am apprentice to Mr. Gardeen, who is a pawnbroker. and lives in Stanhope-street. On the 31st of August a shawl and shift were pledged with me - I do not know who by.


                  GEORGE 
                  WORRALL
               



            . I apprehended the prisoner - she said she pledged them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 47.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
