



1458. 




MARY KENNY
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
25th of May, 2 petticoats, value 2s.; and 2 frocks, value 2s.; the goods of 



Patrick Hoggerty.








CECILIA HOOOERTY
. I am the wife of Patrick Hoggerty. I lost two children's petticoats and two frocks on the 25th of May—these are them I—I knew the prisoner—two or three days before the 25th of May, she came to me, and said her father had turned her out—I took her in for two or three days.

Prisoner. Her husband sent me with them to pawn, which was proved at Bow-street.





RICHARD HENRY JOSELYN
. These articles were pledged on the 25th of May, by the prisoner.





WILLIAM SMITH
. I found these things at the pawnbroker's—there was a flannel shirt, which her husband sent her with in the morning, but these things were taken afterwards.





BRIDGET MURPHY
. I remember the prisoner coming to the house on the 22nd of May, between ten and eleven—she went away—the prosecutor's husband was out at that time.




GUILTY
. * Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Sixth Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
