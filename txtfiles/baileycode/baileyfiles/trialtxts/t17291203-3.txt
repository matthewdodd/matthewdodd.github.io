, was indicted for 


               stealing a Cheshire-Cheese, value 5 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Collyer 
                  




                
            , Nov. 24
.


                  William 
                  Hopkins 
               



             depos'd, That as he was shutting up Shop, he saw the Prisoner go in, and take the Cheese, that upon pursuing him, he saw him drop it; the Cheese was produced in Court, and the Prisoner had little to say in his Defence, but that a Boy came by and dropt the Cheese: But the Evidence being positive that he saw the Prisoner both take it, and drop it, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
