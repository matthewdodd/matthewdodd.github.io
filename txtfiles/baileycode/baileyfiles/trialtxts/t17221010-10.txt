







                     Susan 
                     Ware 
                  



               
            , of St. Bartholomew the Great


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing out of the House of 
                     
                        
                           Robert 
                           Swail 
                        




                      
                  , 3 Gowns, value 15 s. 3 Petticoats, 7 s. 3 Smocks, 2 Aprons, and a suit of Head cloths, and a Riding-hood, 15 s.
             on the 26th of September
last. It appear'd that the prisoner was Servant
to Mr. Swail, and about 5 in the Morning took the Goods, went out at the back door, and left it open; she was taken with some of the Goods, and being carried before Justice Hewit, confest. 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. and 10 d.
