



1369. 




JOHN MILLER
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
21st of April, 12 handkerchiefs, value 3l., the goods of 



Lea Wilson and another—2nd COUNT, calling the goods 11 yards of silk, and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.
