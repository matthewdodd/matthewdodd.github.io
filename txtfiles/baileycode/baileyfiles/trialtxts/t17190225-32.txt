







                     Ann 
                     Foster 
                  



               
            , of St. Margarets Westminster


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Riding-coat, a Riding-hood, Perriwig, &c. to the value of 3 l.
             It appeared the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor when she stole the Goods, and confest it before the Justice, which Confession was read in Court. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
