





791. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     MARSTEN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of April
, one watch, value 30s.; one chain, value 10s.; one seal, value 1l., and one key, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        Joseph 
                        Trith
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  TRITH
               



            . I am a butcher
, and live in Spitalfields. On the 28th of April, I met the prisoner in Fleur-de-lis-street


, he jostled against me as if he had been intoxicated, drew my watch from me at the same moment, and ran off; I pursued him, calling stop thief! and secured him - I never lost sight of him. I found my watchcase within three yards of the place where I took him.
Cross-examined by MR. PLATT. There is a gas-light in the street.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Garrow
               



            .
