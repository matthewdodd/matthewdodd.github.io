







                     John 
                     Easton 
                  



               
             a little Boy
, of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               stealing three Iron Bars and two Utensils belonging to a Glass-House
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Bowles 
                  




                
            . It appeared that the Goods were, stole out of the Glass-House by the Prisoner and that he sold them a great deal under-rate to one 
               
                  Mary 
                  Picket 
               



             ,(who sold them again the same Day, and who is now, bound over for buying the same, knowing them to be stollen but being out upon Bail could not be found) The Prisoner confest he took the Goods and where he sold them, which were produced in Court and Swore to. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
