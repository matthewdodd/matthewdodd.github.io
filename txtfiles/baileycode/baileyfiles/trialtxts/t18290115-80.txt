





346. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     TOPPING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of January
, 1 pair of boots, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Holsey 
                  Janson
               




            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  TURNER
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Holsey Janson, a gentleman
, who lives at Stamford-hill


. On the 10th of January I saw the prisoner going out of the front gate, about twelve o'clock in the day; I stopped him, and he gave me the boots, which he had taken from the washhouse - he had no business there.


                  JAMES 
                  GRIFFITHS
               



            . I am an officer, and took the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I begged his pardon, and he let me go about my business, but I was taken again.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 57.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
There was another indictment against the prisoner.
