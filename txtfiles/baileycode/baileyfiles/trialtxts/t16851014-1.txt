







                     Edward 
                     Lawson
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Dunstans in the West


, Indicted for 


               stealing fifty five Yards of Cloth, value 70 l.
             of the Goods of the Lady


                  Frances 
                  Turconel
               




            . It was fully proved against the Prisoner, that he had the Cloth, and was seen to go into the House, and bring the Cloth thence, which after he called a Coach, and carried the Cloth into Fetter-Lane. The Prisoner appeared very Impudent in Court, giving a very slender account of himself; and that which was given being look'd upon as very Immaterial, he was brought in 

               Guilty
            .
