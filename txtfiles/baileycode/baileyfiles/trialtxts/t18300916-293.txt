





1756. 
               
                  
                     FLORENCE 
                     CRAWLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Stephen 
                           Hart
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
STEPHEN HART. I am out of business. On the 28th of August I was in Wilson-street, Finsbury


, between four and five o'clock in the afternoon; I felt my handkerchief drawing from my pocket; I turned, and saw the prisoner running away - I pursued; a constable stopped him, andtook this handkerchief from him, which is mine; I did not see who took it from me.


                  THOMAS 
                  SAUNDERS
               



            . I was coming from Finsbury-square, and heard Stop thief! called I saw the prisoner running, and putting something under his coat - I pursued him; he ran up the City stone-yard - I ran up, and took him: I looked under his waistcoat, and found this handkerchief.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I had been some time out of employ; I saw this handkerchief down by the gentleman's heels - I put it into my bosom, and the gentleman turned and cried Stop thief! I ran, and was taken.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
