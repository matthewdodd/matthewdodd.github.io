                           Joseph 
                           Twinbrough

            , July 9
.
The Prisoner went unseen into the House, while the Prosecutor's Wife was talking next Door to a Neighbour, and brought out the Goods in her Apron: She was seen by an opposite Neighbour, was pursu'd and taken with the Goods upon her.
