                  William

,

            , the Property of 
               
                  Christopher 
                  Tilson 
               



            , Esq;


                  John 
                  Nicholson 
               



             depos'd, That himself, the Prisoner, and 
               
                  William 
                  Field 
               



             being drinking at Teddington


, they went about 11 o'Clock at Night, and stole the Fowls, and carried them to Brentford, and put them into a Stable, and they got one 
               
                  Guy 
                  Moore 
               



             to sell them.
