





101. 
               
                  
                     JONATHAN 
                     THOMPSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of October
, 1 coat, value 30s.; 1 waistcoat, value 14s.; 1 shirt front, value 4s.; 1 handkerchief, value 1s. 6d., and 1 box of water-colours, value 1l., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Pryce 
                           Jones
                        



                     
                   - also for stealing, on the 28th of October, 2 sheets, value 6s., 2 pillows, value 12s., and 2 books, value 6s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Samuel 
                           Harris
                        



. - To which he pleaded



               GUILTY
            . Aged 63. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
