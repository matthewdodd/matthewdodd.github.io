





456. 
               
                  
                     JUDITH 
                     KELLY
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               she on the 29th of July
 had in her custody and possession a certain forged note, purporting to be a bank note for the payment of 1 l. she knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
