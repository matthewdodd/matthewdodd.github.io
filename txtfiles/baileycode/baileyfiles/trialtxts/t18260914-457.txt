






1769. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  WILLIAMS
               



             and 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  JONES
               




             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of July
, 1 teaspoon, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Collender
               




            .
JOHN COLLENDER. I keep a sale-shop
. On the 12th of July the prisoners came into my shop, to look at clothes, but bought none; they were there half an hour - this spoon was on the counter, by the window, as we had drank tea there; I missed it immediately they went out; I pursued, and found it on Jones.


                  JOSEPH 
                  STONE
               



            . I took them in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)
JONES - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
WILLIAMS - 

               NOT GUILTY
            .
