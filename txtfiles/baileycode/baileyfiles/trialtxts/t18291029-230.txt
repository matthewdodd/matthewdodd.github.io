





NEW COURT. Fifth Day.
Third Middlesex Jury. - Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
2073. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     PILBEAM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing,on the 17th of September
, 1 pair of sheets, value 20s.; 2 pillow-cases, value 4s., and 1 medal, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Ross
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
