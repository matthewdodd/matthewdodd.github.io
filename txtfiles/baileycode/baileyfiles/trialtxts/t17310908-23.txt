







                     John 
                     Dowle 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary-Hill


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Petticoat, value 4 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Burn 
                  




                
            , the 24th of July
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Petticoat was laid in the window for a Decoy, (the Prosecutor having been robb'd several Times before) and a Watch set, who seeing the Prisoner put up the Sash and take the Coat, apprehended him with it. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               guilty
            .
