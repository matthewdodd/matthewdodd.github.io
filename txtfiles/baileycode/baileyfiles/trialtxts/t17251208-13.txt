               receiving the same, knowing them to be stoln
            . September 8

.
It appear'd that Dent offering to sell some of the Prosecutors Goods, was taken upon Suspicion of having stoln them at the Fire on London-Bridge, but upon a strict Examination, he confest that Carr Halbert (who was Apprentice
 to the Prosecutor's Brother-in-law) got in at the Garret Window, took the Goods and gave them him to dispose of. Halbert confest the same, but said that Dent perswaded him to it. Both 

               Guilty
            .
