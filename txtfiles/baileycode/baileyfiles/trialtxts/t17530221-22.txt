            , was indicted for 


               stealing one spade, value 4 s.
             the property of

            , February 20
. ++


                  Thomas 
                  Arnold 
               



             . I live at the next house, and am a gardener
. I got up the 20th of this month, and was gone about two hundred yards to call my son, returning home, I met the prisoner at the bar with this spade. (Produced in court and deposed to) it was taken from out of a shed where I had put it; it cost 4 s. 6 d.
Prisoner's Defence.
The prosecutor left me in custody at his brother-in-law's house, while he went for the spade, where he had it I know not.



               Guilty
