





1556. 
               
                  HENRY 
                  STANTON
               



             was indicted for 


               obtaining, on the 20th of May
, by false pretences, 1 pair of trousers, value 25s., the goods of 
                     
                        Edward 
                        Smith
                     




                  ; and on the 12th of June
, a coat and waistcoat, value 3l. 4s.
            , his goods.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
