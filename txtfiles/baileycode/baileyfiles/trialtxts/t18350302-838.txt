



838. 




FREDERICK ARNOLD
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
5th of March, 1 handkerchief, value 10s., the goods of 



George Watson.






WILLIAM DUNBAR
. On the 5th of March, I was in Islington-fields, about four o'clock in the afternoon—I felt something at my pocket, I turned and saw three boys, of whom the prisoner was one, making off in different directions—I missed my handkerchief, and pursued the prisoner, who happened to be the right boy—I found my handkerchief concealed under his jacket—this is it.

Prisoner's Defence. I was with two or three more boys—one of them took this gentleman's handkerchief—he gave it to another, who put it under my jacket.




GUILTY
. Aged 14.—


Transported for Seven Years.
