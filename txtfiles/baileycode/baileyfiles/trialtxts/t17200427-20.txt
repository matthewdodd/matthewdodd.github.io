







                     William 
                     Winn 
                  



               
             of St. Olave's Hart-Street


, was 


               indicted for privately stealing 3 Yards and half of Broad Cloth, out of the House of 
                     
                        
                           Nicholas 
                           French 
                        




, on the 17th of April
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that the Prisoner lodged in his House stole the Goods out of his Cutting-Room, and told him where he had sold them, which he found accordingly. Another confirm'd the same. The Cloth was produc'd in Court and swore to by the Prosecutor; and Mr. Baker deposed that he bought it of the Prisoner. The Prisoner in his Defence said, that the Prosecutor owed him Money, and therefore he took the Cloth. But that Excuse not being sufficient, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
