




6. 




CHARLES BLOWER
 and 



GEORGE HUNT were indicted

for stealing, on the 
29th of Oct., 70lbs. weight of butter, value 3l. 10s., and 1 firkin, 6d.; the goods of 



John Green and another: to which


BLOWER pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 24.—


Transported for Seven years.


HUNT pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 22.—



Confined Six Months.
