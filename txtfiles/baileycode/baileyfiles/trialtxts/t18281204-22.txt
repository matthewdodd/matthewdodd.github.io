





22. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of November
, 1 boot, value 6s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Ralph 
                     Wilcoxon
                  




               
            .


                  RALPH 
                  WILCOXON
               



            . I am a shoemaker
, and live in Oxford-street. I saw this boot in my shop on the 3d of November, and on the 5th a person came for a pair of boots, and I missed this; I know nothing of the prisoner, but the boot was taken to another shop of mine by the officer, and I heard of it - the boot had been near my door.


                  WILLIAM 
                  ORGER
               



            . I am an officer. The prisoner was brought to the watch-house on another charge about ten o'clock at night on the 4th of November, and had this boot in his hand.
Prisoner's Defence. I was intoxicated.


                  WILLIAM 
                  ORGER
               



            . He was sober.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
