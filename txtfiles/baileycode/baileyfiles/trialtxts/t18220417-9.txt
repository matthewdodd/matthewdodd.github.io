





543. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     WINCH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of February
, two pair of shoes, value 11 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Lewis 
                           Hunt
                        




                     
                  . Also stealing, on the 17th of March, fifty-seven pair of shoes, 15 l. 13 s. 6 d.; fourteen pair of half boots, value 2 l. 16 s., and ten pair of other shoes, value 25 s.
            , his property.
To both of which indictments the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.
The prosecutor stated his loss to be 170 pairs of shoes.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
