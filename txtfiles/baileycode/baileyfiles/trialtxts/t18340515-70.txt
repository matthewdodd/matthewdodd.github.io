






808. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     LAVENDER
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     M'INTOSH
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of April
, 1dressing gown, value 30s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Tomlinson
                        




                     
                  , and others
            .


                  JOHN 
                  PRICE
               



            . I live next door to Mr. Tomlinson, in New Bond-street


. On the 22d of April, about eight o'clock in the evening, I was bringing out the shutters, and saw Lavender looking in at Mr. Tomlinson's window - I went into out shop again, and when I came back I saw him pulling out the dressing gown, and rolling it up - he ran round the corner, and I gave information.


                  JOHN 
                  LITTLEWOOD
               



            . I am in the service of Mr. 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Tomlinson
               



            , and two others. I received information of this from Price - I ran and overtook the prisoners - M'Intosh had the dressing gown, and was folding it up - they were then about one hundred yards off.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HORSFORD
               



             (police-serjeant D 6). I took the prisoners.
LAVENDER - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
M'INTOSH - 

               NOT GUILTY
            .
