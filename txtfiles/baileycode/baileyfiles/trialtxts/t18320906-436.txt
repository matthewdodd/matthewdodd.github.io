





2167. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted 


               for stealing, on the 20th of August
, 1 coat, value 10s.; 2 pairs of trousers, value 8s.; 2 waistcoats, value 7s.; 1 pair of braces, value 3d., and 1 half handkerchief, value 2d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Humphrey
                  




               
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  GALLOWAY
               



            . I am an officer. On the 20th of August, about four o'clock in the morning, I heard a cry of Police! and apprehended the prisoner in a boat alongside a ship, in Ratcliff-cross tier


 - I found this property on him.
JOSEPH HUMPHREY. I am master of the vessel
. I lost my coat out of the cabin, and my pantaloons and two waistcoat from the state-room, where I sleep - the prisoner was taken in my ship's-boat, which was moored to the vessel; I know nothing of him.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
