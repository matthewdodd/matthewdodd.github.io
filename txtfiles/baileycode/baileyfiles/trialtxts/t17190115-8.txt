







                     Christopher 
                     Hutton 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 Ounces of Silver and Gold Waste, value 40 s.
             the property of 
               
                  Will 
                  Basson 
               




             , the 5th of December
 last. It appeared by the Evidence, He was Journeyman
 to the Prosecutor, who was a Silver Spinner
, and took the Goods, which being discovered were found in his Lodging; the Prisoner having nothing to say in his defence, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
