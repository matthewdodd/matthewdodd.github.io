



2359. 




GEORGE DARBY
the younger
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
19th of July, 1 half-crown, and 20 shillings; the monies of 



George Darby the elder
; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 11.—


Transported for Seven Years—Convict Ship.
