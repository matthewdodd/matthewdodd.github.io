



1102. 




JOSEPH EDWARDS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
12th of March, 1 coat, value 60s.; 1 silver bird, value 5s.; 2 inches of chain, value 2s. 2d.; and 2 sovereigns, the property of 



John Joseph Janson: 4 spoons, value 32s. 6d.; 1 pair of ear-rings, value 40s.; 1 necklace, value 12s.; 1 buckle, value 10s.; 1 pair of bracelets, value 7s. 6d.; 1 ring, value 12s.; 2 knives, value 2s.; 1 sovereign, 8 shillings, and 7 groats, the property of 



Charles Allen Wilday, in his dwelling-house; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
.† Aged 24.—


Transported for Ten Years.
