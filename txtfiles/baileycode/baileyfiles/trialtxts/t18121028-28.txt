





870. 
               
                  
                     CATHERINE 
                     WATSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously having in her custody and possession, a forged bank-note, for the payment of 2 l. she knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
