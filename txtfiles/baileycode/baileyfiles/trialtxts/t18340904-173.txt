





OLD COURT. - Tuesday, September 9th, 1834.
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1390. 
               
                  GEORGE 
                  WHITCOMB
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of August
, 1 pair of breeches, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        Thomas Farquhar 
                        Chilver
                     



                  ; and 1 pair of trowsers, value 18d.; and 1 shirt, value 1s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Halliday
               



            ; to which indictment he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 38. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
