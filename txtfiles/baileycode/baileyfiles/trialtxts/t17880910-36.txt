





533. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     ALLEN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th day of July
 last, an iron bar, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Edgar
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the bar in his hands.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
