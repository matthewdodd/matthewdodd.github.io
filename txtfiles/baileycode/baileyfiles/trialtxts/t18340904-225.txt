





1445. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  BROWN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        Edward 
                        Thompson
                     




                  , from his person
            .
EDWARD THOMPSON. I am a clerk in a solicitor's office
, in Essex-street. On the 15th August, I was in Southampton-street, Strand


, between three and four o'clock - I felt my handkerchief taken from my pocket - I turned round directly, saw it in the prisoner's hand, and secured him.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I had been to Hungerford-market - I was walking up the Strand, looking in at the picture-shops - the gentleman turned round and accused me of stealing his handkerchief, which I am innocent of.



               GUILTY
            .* Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
