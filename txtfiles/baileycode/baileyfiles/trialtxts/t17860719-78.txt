





640. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     ABBOTT
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 17th day of July
, eight live hens, price 4 s. eighteen live chickens, value 18 d. and five live cocks, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Kemp
                  




               
            .


                  William
                   Brown
               



            , a private watchman in Whitechapel-road stopped the prisoner with the fowls upon him; he said, he brought them from Burntwood, and dealt in fowls. (The fowls produced and deposed to.) One hen had had a cropper crown, that was pulled off.
Prisoner. I bought them.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
