




1977. 




CATHERINE BRYAN
 and 




ELIZA BRYAN
 were indicted for

 stealing, on the 
12th of August, 1 pewter-pot, value 1s., the goods of 



John Edmund Briggs: 1 pewter-pot, value 11d., the goods of 



Thomas Thornton: and 1 pewter-pot, value 10d.; the goods of 



Ambrose Everard; to which


CATHERINE BRYAN pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 50.—



Confined Three Months.


ELIZA BRYAN pleaded 


GUILTY
.* Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
