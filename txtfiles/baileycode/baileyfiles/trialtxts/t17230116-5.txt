







                     Samuel 
                     Cole 
                  



               
            , of St. Lawrence Jury


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing in Hat, value 4 s. the Property of 
                     
                        
                           Benjamin 
                           Smith 
                        



                      
                  ; and another Hat, value 4 s. the Property of 
                     
                        
                           Jonathan 
                           Bushnel 
                        



                      
                  , in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        William 
                        Fisher 
                     



 the 15th of December
 last. Mr. Fisher depos'd. That being in his Bar, at the Crown Tavern in Kingstreet


, he saw the Prisoner take the Hats off the Rails of the Stair-Case and run away; that he follow'd him took one of the Hats from under his coat, and the other was taken off the Ground. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, but it being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               Guilty
             of the Indictment.
