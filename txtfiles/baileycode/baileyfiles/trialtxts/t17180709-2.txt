







                     Thomas 
                     Harris 
                  



               
            , of St. Swithin's


 , was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Perriwig value 50 s. in the dwelling House
             of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Pearse 
                  




                
            ; the 13th of June
 last. The Evidence deposed, that they saw the Prisoner put up a Sash Window and steal the Wig, and go off with it, but they pursuing him immediately took him. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, but it being plainly prov'd the Jury found him 

               Guilty
             of the Indictment
