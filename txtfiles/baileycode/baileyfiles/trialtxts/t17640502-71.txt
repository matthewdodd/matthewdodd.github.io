





305. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Benjamin 
                     Paul 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one fustian frock, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Rutlidge 
                  




                
            , April 25
. ++


                  James 
                  Rutlidge 
               



            . I was at work at the company's wharf, at Puddle-dock


, and had laid my frock down on the Capshead; and when I came to put it on, it was missing; the next morning, I was informed the prisoner was seen to go over the pales; I took him up, and he confessed he had taken it, and sold it in Field-lane, where I found it.


                  Richard 
                  Blower 
               



            , the constable, deposed to that of the prisoner's confession, and finding the frock accordingly.

Prisoner's Defence.
I was pretty much in liquor.




               Guilty 10 d.
