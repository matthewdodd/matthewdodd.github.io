





68. (M.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     West 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one cloth coat, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Cornelius 
                     Neal 
                  




                
            , Sept. 30
. ++


                  Cornelius 
                  Neal 
               



            . The prisoner lodged in the same house with me. The day my coat was missing, he went off, and 10 days after he was taken, and confessed he took it.


                  Samuel 
                  Hager 
               



            . I took in a coat of the prisoner, I am a pawnbroker. I lent him 4 s. on it. (Produced in court and deposed to.)
Prisoner's Defence.
I was going out in the morning and picked up the coat in the passage.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
