






NEW COURT. (1st DAY.)
Third Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1878. 
               
                  ROBERT 
                  SMITH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of July
, 7 shillings, the monies of James Milbourne, his master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
