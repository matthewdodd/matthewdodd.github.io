







                     Mary 
                     Smith 
                  



               
             of St. Mary White-Chapel


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2 Yards of Broad-Cloth value 12 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Hadley 
                  




                
            , on the 30th of March
 last. It appeared that she took the Goods off the Tenters in a Tenter-Ground in Goodmans-Fields


, but being seen by two Persons, was taken. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
