




1158. 



WILLIAM DAVIS
 was indicted

 for embezzling, on the 
27th of February, a Bill of Exchange for £7 7s., which he had received on account of 




Henry Rowles, Esq., and others, his masters: also, 

for embezzling, on the 
30th of January, another Bill of Exchange for £7 7s.; to both of which he pleaded





GUILTY
.—


Transported for Seven Years.
