





Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
928. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     POPE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of February
, 1 counterpane, value 12s.; 1 pair of blankets, value 20s.; 1 sheet, value 6s.; 1 pillow, value 7s., and 1 bolster, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Leach
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZABETH 
                  LEACH
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  William 
                  Leach
               



            ; we live in Zion-terrace


, and let a furnished lodging to the prisoner on the 18th of February by the week; all the articles stated were in the room, as part of the furniture - he went away on the 20th without giving notice, and these articles were missing; we have not found any of them - he was taken in about a month.


                  THOMAS 
                  BINFORD KERSWELL
               



            . I am an officer. Leach gave me a description of the prisoner, and I took him in St. James'-park.

Prisoner. I throw myself on the mercy of the Court.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
