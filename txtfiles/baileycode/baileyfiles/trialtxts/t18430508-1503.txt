



1503. 




PHILIP M'BRIDE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
3rd of May, 1 coat, value 1l. 10s., the goods of 



John Fisher Gould: and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.


NEW COURT.—Wednesday, May 10th, 1843.

Fifth Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
