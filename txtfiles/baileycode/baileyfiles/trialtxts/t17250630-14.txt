








                     Edward 
                     Dickenson 
                  



               
            , of S. Margaret's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Handkerchief val. 1 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     King 
                  



                
            , on the 10th of June

.
He was a 2d time indicted for 


               privately stealing a Handkerchief val. 1 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Masters 
                  



                
            , on the 10th of June

 last.


                  Robert 
                  King 
               



             thus deposed: That he lost his Handkerchief in Westminster Hall



; which the Constable found upon the Prisoner. 
               
                  Giles 
                  Bennet 
               



            , the Constable, deposed, That having Notice given him that the Prisoner was a Pick-pocket, he watch'd him, and took him in the Fact; he found one Handkerchief in his Pocket, and twenty three in his Codpiece; all which were produced in Court. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
