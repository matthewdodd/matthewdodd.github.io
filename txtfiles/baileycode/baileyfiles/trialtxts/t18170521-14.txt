





788. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     ROBINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of May
, one handkerchief, value 3s., the property of 
                     
                        Richard 
                        Buxton
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  BUXTON
               



            . On the 19th of May, I was opposite Southampton-street, in Holborn


, about twenty minutes before two o'clock in the day, I felt a hand in my pocket, I turned round and caught hold of the prisoner - He had my handkerchief in his left hand; he said he had not got it. I took it from him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Bayley.
