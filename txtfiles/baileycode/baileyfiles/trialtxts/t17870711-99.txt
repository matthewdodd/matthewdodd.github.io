





656. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     SMITH
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               stealing, on the 16th of June
 last, one deal box, value 2 s. one copper tea urn, value 20 s. one red japanned knife tray, value 1 s. one wicker hamper, value 6 d. and twenty pounds weight of mutton, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Thatcher
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor caught the prisoner removing the things from his cart on his return from an errand.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
