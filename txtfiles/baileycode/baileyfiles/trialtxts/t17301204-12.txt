







                     John 
                     Gladmore 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary le Bow


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Carcass of a Sheep, value 9 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Street 
               




             , the 28th of November
 last.


                  John 
                  Kellet 
               



             depos'd, That he being hired to carry five Sheep to Newgate-Market for the Prosecutor, having taken three of the Sheep out of the Basket, and left two, when he came again found but one, and pursuing the Prisoner, found him with the Sheep upon him.
The Prisoner did not deny the Fact, but said, he seeing a Man carrying away Sheep, thought he might carry away a Sheep as well as another Man; the Jury found him 

               Guilty
             of the Indictment.
