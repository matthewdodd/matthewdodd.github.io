





351. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     KEMP
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th day of February
 last, three sheets, value 5 s. and one napkin, value 3 d. and one brass candlestick, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Ward
                  




               
            .
The prisoner lived servant
 with the prosecutor, and went away, taking the things, which were found upon her, and she confessed stealing them.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
