




3049. 




HENRY CURRALL
 and 




GEORGE PRICE
 were indicted for

 stealing, on the 
14th of Oct., 1 bottle, value 2d.; and 1 pint of pickled onions, 1s.; the goods of 



Hannah Swan.


GEORGE KEMP (police-constable N 82.) I was on duty on the 14th of Oct.—I saw the prisoners with four other persons walking three by three—I knew them, and watched them—I saw Price go into the prosecutor's shop—Currall put his arm into the shop, and he came away with this bottle in his arms—Price said to one of the others, "I have got it"—I secured Currall in the act of giving the bottle to Price.





JOHN RICHARD RICHARDS
. This bottle of pickles is the property of my sister Hannah Swan—I live with her.

CURRALL—


GUILTY
. Aged 13.—


Transported for Seven Years.


PRICE—


GUILTY
. Aged 20.—



Confined Nine Months.


Seventh Jury, before Edward Bullock, Esq.
