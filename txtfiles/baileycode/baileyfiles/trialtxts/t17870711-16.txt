






571. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HINDLEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of April

 last, one pair of silver buckles, value 13 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Read
                  





               
            .



               NOT GUILTY
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
572. The said 
               
                  JOHN
                   HINDLEY
               



             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of June

 last, six brass powder flask moulds, value 6 s. and three blankets, value 12 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Edwards
                  





               
            .
The things were found at Mr. Lloyd's the pawnbroker's, pledged by the prisoner.




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
