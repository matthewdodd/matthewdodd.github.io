





1578. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     CARDY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of June
, 23 yards of printed cotton, value 9s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Marfleet
                  




               
            . - To which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
