





1491. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     GODFREY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of August
, 1 pair of boots, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Gardner
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 50. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
