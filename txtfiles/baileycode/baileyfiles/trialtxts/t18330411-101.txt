





772. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HARDING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of March
, 1 handkerchief, value 1s., the goods of 
                     
                        Philip 
                        Costin
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  PHILIP 
                  COSTIN
               



            . On the 3rd of March, between three and four o'clock, I was near the corner of Mitre-street, Aldgate


; Wardley told me my pocket was picked - I saw my handkerchief in the prisoner's possession, he was about forty yards off; he threw it from him - he was running away.


                  GEORGE 
                  JOHN WEST
               



             (Police-constable C 53.) I took the prisoner, and have the handkerchief.


                  ALFRED 
                  WARDLEY
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket, and put it up his breast; I told the prosecutor, and I saw the prisoner throw the handkerchief down.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
