







                     William 
                     Rouse 
                  



               
             of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted 


               stealing 6 Yards of Silver Fringe value 12 l. and 2 Yards of Silver Lace value 4 s. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Pinnock 
                        




, on the 12th of November
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that he having a rich Chariot for my Lord Craven, the Prisoner was hired to set up to watch it, which he did about 10 Nights, then cut out the Fringe and Lace, and went away. The Prisoner confest the Fact when taken, and also upon his Trail; but it not appearing to be a Shop, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
