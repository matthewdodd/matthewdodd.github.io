





OLD COURT.
FIFTH DAY. TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 3.
First London Jury - Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
2068. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     BENNETT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of October
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        Robert 
                        Roberts
                     




                  , from his person
            .
ROBERT ROBERTS. I am a wine-merchant
. On the 14th of October, about half-past six o'clock, I was in St. Paul's church-yard


; I felt a tug at my pocket, and a hand in it; I turned round, and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand; I saw him give it to a person, who ran away with it - I seized the prisoner, and gave him in charge; he resisted, and tried to get away.
Prisoner. Q.Did you not say a young woman told you I had the handkerchief? A. One came up, and said she saw you take it, but I saw it in your hand; he struggled very much, and I lost sight of the woman.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
