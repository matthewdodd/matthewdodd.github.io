





331. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     YARDLEY
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of December
, 1 pair of trousers, value 1l., and 1 waistcoat, value 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        Samuel 
                        Jones
                     



                  ; and 2 sheets, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Heard
                  



               
            .


                  SAMUEL 
                  JONES
               



            . I live at Hampstead; I am out of a situation, and was lodging at Mr. Heard's, at the Duke of Hamilton Inn, Hampstead


. On the 8th of December my property was safe in my own bed-room, at the top of the house; I know nothing of the prisoner.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HEARD
               



            . I keep the Duke of Hamilton
. I lost two sheets from the bed that Jones slept in; I saw a lad about the prisoner's size there, but I cannot say it was him.


                  JAMES 
                  MASTERS
               



             HOWIE. I am a pawnbroker. I have two sheets pawned by the prisoner on the 8th of December.


                  EDWARD 
                  FITZHUGH
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, in Church-row. I have a waistcoat pawned by the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years. - To commence at the expiration of his former sentence
            .
