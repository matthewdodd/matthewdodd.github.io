





552. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     WALKER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that he, on the 26th of December
, had in his custody and possession, a forged 2 l. bank-note, he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
