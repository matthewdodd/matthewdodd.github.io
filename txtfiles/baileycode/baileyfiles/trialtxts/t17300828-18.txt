







                     Mary 
                     Bates 
                  



               
            , of St. Peter's Cornhill


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Linen Shirt, and divers others Linen
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Evans 
                  




                
            , the 10th of August
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, The Linen was lost wet.
John Brown depos'd, He seeing the Prisoner laying them on a Butcher's-Stall in Leaden-Hall-Market to dry, suspecting the Prisoner, apprehended her, and gave Notice to the Prosecutor; the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 

               Guilty
             of the Indictment.
