






346. (2d L.) 
               
                  
                     ELIJAH
                      CLARKE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Tagg
                  




               
            , March 15th
. ~


                  John
                   Tagg
               



            . On the 15th of March in Lower Thames-street


, the prisoner passed me and gave me a shove; I missed my handkerchief; I pursued him and found my handkerchief upon him.
The prisoner in his defence denied the fact, but called no witnesses.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
