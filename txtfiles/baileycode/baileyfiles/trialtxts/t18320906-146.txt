





1871. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES BOYD 
                     DALY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of August
, 1 shawl, value 9s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Daly
                  




               
            .
MARY DALY. I lodge at the Rose and Crown, Newwharf, Whitefriars


 - the prisoner is my grandson. On the 29th of August I went out - I left the prisoner in my room, and missed my shawl next morning from my box.


                  THEODORE 
                  JAMES YATES
               



            . I am a constable. I was sent for on the morning of the 30th of August to take the prisoner - I met with him in Fleet-street, and found this duplicate in his hat - it led me to the pawnbroker's, where I found the shawl.


                  CHARLES 
                  MASON
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. Gray, a pawnbroker, in Fleet-street. I produce a shawl, pawned by a female - this is the duplicate given for it.
Prisoner's Defence. The duplicate was given to me by a young man.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
