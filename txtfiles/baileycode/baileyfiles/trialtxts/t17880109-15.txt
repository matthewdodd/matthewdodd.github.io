





112. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     IPEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of January
, a man's hat, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Frederick 
                     Richmond
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the hat instantly.



               GUILTY
            .
(Aged fourteen.)




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
