







                     John 
                     Danford 
                  



               
            , of St. Helens


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief, value 2 s. 6 d from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Lloyd 
                        




 , the 7th of April
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, he perceived the Prisoner take his Handkerchief out of his Pocket, followed him and took him immediately, and found it upon him. The Prisoner pleaded he found it in the Street. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
