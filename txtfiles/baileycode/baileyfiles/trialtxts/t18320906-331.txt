





2061. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of August
, 1 trowel, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Battershill
                  




               
            .


                  SAMUEL 
                  BATTERSHILL
               



            . I am a plasterer
, and live in Stamford-street, Blackfriars - I do not know the prisoner I had left my trowel safe in Gray's Inn-lane


, where I had been at work on the 15th, and when I came to work at half-past six o'clock the next morning it was gone - I went to buy another, and saw the prisoner with my trowel and another in his hand, in Drury-lane.
Prisoner. He stopped me, and said it was his - I said if it was he must have it; I had it of a man who had it to dispose of, and I would have taken him to the man in St. Giles', but he would not go. Witness. He was remanded from Friday till Monday, and I believe the Policeman went and made inquiries.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 38*. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
