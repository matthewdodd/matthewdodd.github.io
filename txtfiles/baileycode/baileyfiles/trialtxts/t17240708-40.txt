







                     John 
                     Ellix 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Ann Aldersgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing Iron, &c. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Olive 
                        




, the 29th Day of June
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and took the Goods and offered them to sale. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
