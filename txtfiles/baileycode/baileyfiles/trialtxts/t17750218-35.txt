






218. (M.) 
               
                  
                     ALICE
                      WHITWORTH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a child's camblet gown, value four shillings, and a child's cotton gown, value one shilling
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Patrick
                      Jacob
                  




               
            , February 11th
 ||.


                  Ann
                   Jacob
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  Patrick
                   Jacob
               



            ; the prisoner lodged in my house. On the 11th of February I missed the child's gown I suspected her, and knowing she used to pawn things at one Allen's, I went there and found them pawned in the name of 
               
                  Ann
                   Holt
               



            , I had her taken before justice Welsh, who committed her. 


                  Edmund
                   Bannister
               



            . I am apprentice to Mr. Allen, the pawnbroker, the prisoner brought these two gowns to me to pawn, (producing them) they were pawned in the name of 
               
                  Ann
                   Holt
               



            .
Prosecutor. They are my property.
Prisoner. I leave myself to the mercy of the court.



               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
