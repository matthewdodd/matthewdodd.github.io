





1240. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     PRIDHAM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of May
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        Farindon 
                        Lane
                     




                  , from his person
            ; and that he had before been convicted of felony.


                  JOHN 
                  CHALLICE
               



            . I am assistant to Mr. Coates, of Hart-street, Bloomsbury


, a surgeon. On the 14th of May, in the afternoon, I was in his shop, and saw Mr. Lane passing on the opposite side of the way; I saw the prisoner take a handkerchief from his pocket - he put it under his coat; I ran and secured him - we took him to the station-house.
FARINDON LANE. On the 14th of May I lost the handkerchief from my pocket - Mr. Challice found it on the prisoner, and gave it me; we took the prisoner to the station, and I left my handkerchief with the officer - this is it.


                  WILLIAM 
                  PRICHARD
               



            . I produce a certificate of the prisoner's conviction, which I got from the clerk's office(read) - I attended the trial, and swear the prisoner is the person.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16 - 


               Transported for Life
            .
