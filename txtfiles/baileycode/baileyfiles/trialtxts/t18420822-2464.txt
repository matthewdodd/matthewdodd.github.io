



2464. 




GEORGE TOWNLEY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
10th of August, 2 gowns, value 1l. 1s; 15 yards of calico, value 10s.; and 1 Spencer, value 9s; the goods of 



Catherine Brockly.






CATHERINE BROCKLY
. I am a dress-maker, living in Dean-street, Soho; the prisoner came to visit my servant occasionally. On the 16th of August I missed two gowns and the other things stated.





JOHN KENDALL
. I am shopman to a pawnbroker. I produce this calico, which was pawned by the prisoner.
