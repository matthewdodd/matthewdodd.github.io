






407. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     JONES
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     JANE 
                     SMITH
                  



               
             were indicted, for 


               that they, on the 25th of February
, had in their custody and possession, a certain bank note for the payment of 1 l. they knowing it to be forged
            .

To this indictment the prisoners pleaded




               GUILTY
            .





               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
