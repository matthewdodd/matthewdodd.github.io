





958. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     BULL
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Crampton
                  




               
            .
WILLIAM CRAMPTON. I am a servant
, and was in Wych-street


 on the 4th of April, at four o'clock in the afternoon; I had a handkerchief in my right-hand coat pocket - I felt a pull, looked over my shoulder, and saw the prisoner with it - he was taken into custody directly with it.


                  EVAN 
                  OWEN
               



            . I saw the prisoner and the prosecutor inWych-street; I did not see the prisoner do anything; but the prosecutor called me, and gave him into my charge with this handkerchief.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
