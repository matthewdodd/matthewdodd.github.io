





455. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JACKSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession, three forged bank notes, for the payment of one-pound, he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
