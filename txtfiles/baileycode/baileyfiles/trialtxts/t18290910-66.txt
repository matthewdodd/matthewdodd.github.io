





1598. 
               
                  
                     JEREMIAH 
                     HEMS
                  




               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 21st of July
, at St. Mary, Whitechapel


, feloniously did break and enter the warehouse of 
                     
                        
                           John, Blackburn
                        




                     
                   and another, and steal therein, 1 quadrangle, value 10s., and 1 weight-scale, value 2s., the goods of the said John Blackburn
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Life
            .
