RICHARD ESMOND
 was indicted

 for stealing a post-letter containing 1 locket, value 2l.; 1 gold case, 2l.; 1 miniature, 2l.; and 1 painting, 2l.; the property of the Postmaster-General.—Other COUNTS, varying the manner of laying the charge; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 22.—


Transported for Ten Years.

(The Rev. George Robert Glegg, Chaplain of Her Majesty's forces, deposed to the prisoner's good character.)
