





2129. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     TURNER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of September
, 1 blanket, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Walton
               




            .


                  ELEANOR 
                  WALTON
               



            . I am the wife of William Walton- we live in Spring-place


. On the night of the 21st of September, I lost a blanket from my yard - I gave information.


                  JOHN 
                  FITZPATRICK
               



            . I am an officer. I received information, and suspecting the prisoner I spoke to him, and asked if he knew that a blanket had been stolen from Spring-place, Kentish-town - he said he did, because he took it himself; he showed it me under a hedge - this is it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
