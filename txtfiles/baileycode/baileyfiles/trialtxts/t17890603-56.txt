





461. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     THOMPSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of April
 last, a linen shirt, value 18 d. a pair of worsted stockings, value 6 d. a muslin handkerchief, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Cosby
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the things upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
