






539. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS RICKETS 
                     LYON
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he on the 22nd of March last did take to wife one 
                     
                        
                           Martha 
                           Wilson
                        



                     
                  , spinster
, and that he afterwards on the 30th of May
, did take to wife one 
                     
                        
                           Charlotte 
                           Johnson
                        



                     
                  , spinster, his former wife
 being then living
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
