






724. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     PEACHEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 2d day of August
, one silk handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Jones
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken instantly with the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
