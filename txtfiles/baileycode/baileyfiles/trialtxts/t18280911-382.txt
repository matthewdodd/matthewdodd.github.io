





1934. 
               
                  ANDREW 
                  READING
               




             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of July
, 2 pairs of trousers, value 2s., and 1 handkerchief, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Alfred 
                  Stibbins
               




            .
ALFRED STIBBINS. I belong to the ship Amey
; on the 6th of July, she was at the North-quay, London-docks


. I missed two pairs of trousers and a handkerchief out of my chest; I saw my handkerchief on the prisoner's neck- when he was taken, he said it was his own; my chest was locked on Saturday night - I found it broken open on the Sunday morning.
Prisoner. Q. Did you see me on board? A. No.


                  ARCHIBALD 
                  McDONALD
               



            . The prisoner was in custody on another charge - the prosecutor came to the watch-house, and claimed the handkerchief on his neck.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
