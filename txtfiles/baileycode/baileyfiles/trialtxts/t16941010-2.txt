







                  Elinor 
                  Pelsome
               



             was Tried for 


               stealing 12 Pair of Stockins, val 9 s. on the 18th. of September
 last, the Goods of 
                     
                        Jane 
                        Wells
                     




                  , Wid
 She came to the shop to cheapen some Worsted, and got the Stockins under her Coat
            , which were found about her before she got away, with a Flesh hook also to hook any thing off a shelf, or the like: Being asked what she did with the Hook, she answered, That she found it lying on the ground near a Butcher's-stall, but she had no Witnesses; so she was found 

               Guilty
             of Felony.
