





141. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE
                     SMITH
                  , alias 
                     
THOMAS TYLER




            , was indicted for that 


               he, on the 15th of March
, feloniously and unlawfully had in his custody and possession a forged bank note for the payment of 5 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .




               To this indictment the prisoner pleaded
            

GUILTY.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
