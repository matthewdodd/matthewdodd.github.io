





1353. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BROWN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of May
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the property of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Slater
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  SLATER
               



            . I am a cabinet-maker
. I was at the corner of Great Queen-street


, at half-past eight o'clock, on the 6th of May - I did not feel my handkerchief taken, but the prisoner passed me - I saw my handkerchief in his hand; I pursued, and took him with it - this is it.


                  DANIEL 
                  HONE
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence. I was going down Queen-street, behind this gentleman; his handkerchief fell down - he came and took me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
