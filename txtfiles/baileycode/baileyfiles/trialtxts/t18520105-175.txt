




175. 




WILLIAM NORTH
 and 




JAMES FITZGERALD



, stealing 50 1bs. of printers' rule, value 5l.; the goods of 



Andrew Spottiswoode and another: to which


NORTH pleaded 



GUILTY
. Aged 13.
Recommended to mercy by the Prosecutor.—


Judgment Respited.



FITZGERALD pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
