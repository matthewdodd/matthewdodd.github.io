





NEW COURT, FRIDAY, DECEMBER 4.
Fifth Middlesex Jury, - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
77. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     TAYLOR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of November
, 1 looking-glass, value 2l.; and 300 leaves of gold, value 18s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Sweet
                  




               
             and another; to which he pleaded.




               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
