





114. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CANEW
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of November
, 2 boots, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Dupin
                  




               
            .
HENRY JENKINS. I am in the employ of Mr. George Dupin, shoemaker
, Shadwell High-street


. On the 21st of November, a woman came and said a boy had taken a pair of shoes - I went out, and saw the prisoner running; I took him about fifty yards from the shop, and took the boots from him - these are them; I had seen them safe an hour before.
WILLIAM BURN. I am an officer, and took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
