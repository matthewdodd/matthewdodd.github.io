







                     Mary 
                     Higgons 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               privately stealing a silver Watch
             from the Person of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Cheek 
                  




                
            , on the 5th of this Instant, in the Parish of St. Andrew's Holbourn. The Prosecutor depos'd, That missing his Way, ask'd Directions of the Prisoner, who went with him to King's-Head-Court, Shoe-Lane


, where she ask'd him for a Pint of Drink, which he readily gave her, that he pull'd out his Watch, and she snatch'd it out of his Hand, the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
