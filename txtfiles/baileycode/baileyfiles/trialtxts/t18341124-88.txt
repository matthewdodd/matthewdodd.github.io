



88. 




JAMES LINDSAY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
24th of November, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 



Richard Samuel Hadwen Ward, from his person.






RICHARD SAMUEL HADWEN WARD
. I live in Queen-street. I was coming up Fleet-street, on the 24th of November, and felt my handkerchief drawn out—I turned, and saw the prisoner with it—he dropped it, and his own handkerchief too—I took it up—this is it—he was secured.

Prisoner. I saw two young fellows behind him who took the handkechief and dropped it on the ground—I was going to tell the gentleman. Witness. There were no persons near.




GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.
