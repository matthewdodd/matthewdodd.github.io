







                     John 
                     Deal 
                  



               
             of the Parish of St. Gile's


, Labourer
, was indicted for 


               stealing 12 Pounds of Beef
            , the 7th of Feb.
 last, the Property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Cowly 
                  




                
            .
The Prosecutor depos'd. That he saw the Prisoner take up an Each-bone of Beef and run away with it, but being stopt, he confess 'd he stole it to sell, wanting Money to carry him into the Country: He made but a poor Defence, alledging that he was drunk, &c. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
