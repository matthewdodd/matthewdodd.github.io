





537. (L) 
               
                  
                     JOHN
                      DOWDY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value ten-pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Smith
                  




               
            , July 11th
. ++


                  John
                   Smith
               



            . As I was going through Token-house-yard


 yesterday, at about twelve o'clock, a gentleman came after me, and told me I was robbed of my handkerchief: I had it just before, (the handkerchief produced.) I cannot positively swear to it; there are the initials of my name upon it, I believe it is mine.


                  William
                   Norman
               



            . About twelve o'clock yesterday noon, near Token-house-alley. I saw the prisoner take the handkerchief out of Mr. Smith's pocket, and put it under his coat; I secured him, and found the handkerchief upon him, and then went after Mr. Smith, and informed him of it.
Prisoner's defence.
I found the handkerchief in Token house-Yard.



               Guilty
            .
