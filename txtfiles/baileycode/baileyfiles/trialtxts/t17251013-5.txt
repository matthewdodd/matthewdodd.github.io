







                     Jane 
                     Pattison 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing 18 Yards of Holland, value 3 l. 10 s. and 10 Yards of Sattin value 30 s. in the House of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Martell 
                        




, Sept. 10
. It appeared that the Prisoner was a Chairwoman
, and being trusted in the House, she broke open a Chest, and took away the Goods. They were soon miss'd; she was charged with the Felony, and confess'd it. Some of the Goods were found upon her, and the rest at Places where she had pawn'd them. 


               Guilty 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
