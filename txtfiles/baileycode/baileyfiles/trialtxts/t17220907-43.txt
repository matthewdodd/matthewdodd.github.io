 (Mires) inticed him to it; and that he sold him 11 pair of Silk Gloves, and 6 pair of Silk Stockings for a Guinea, of which he received but 14 s. 2 odd Silk Stockings and 1 pair of Worked, he sold to 
               
                  Ann 
                  Manson 
               



             an Apple Woman, and the rest to 
               
                  Joan 
                  Humphris 
               



             a Curd and Whey Woman, both in Stocks Market. 
               
                  Ann 
                  Manson 
               



             in her defence said, that Pitway told her he found the Stockings, and produced several persons of Credit who gave her a good Character. Mires said, that Pitway told him his Master was strainen'd for Money, and brought him the Stockings to pawn; Pitway was found
