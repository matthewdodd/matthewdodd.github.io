



772. 




DANIEL JONES
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
4th of February, 1 waistcoat, value 9s., the goods of 



John Giles.






ROBERT BUSHELL
. I know Mr. John Giles—he keeps a clothes-shop in Shoreditch. About a quarter-past four o'clock, on the 4th of February, I saw the prisoner at my window, which is next door—I saw him take the waistcoat—I went after him and brought him ack—he had got to the next door to me on the other side—I found the waistcoat at his feet.





WILLIAM COOK
. I am a policeman. The prisoner was given to me—I have the waistcoat.





JOHN GILES
. This is my property.

Prisoner. The gentleman came and accused me of stealing it—I am quite innocent—I never had it my hand.




GUILTY
.* Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.
