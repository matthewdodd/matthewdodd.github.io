





615. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CARTER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th day of June
 last, four silver tea spoons, value 6 s.
             the property of 
               
                  Edward 
                  Osman
               




            .
The prisoner was taken with the spoons upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
