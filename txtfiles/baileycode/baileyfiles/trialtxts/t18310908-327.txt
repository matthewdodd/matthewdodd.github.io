





1854. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     LANGHORN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of September
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Charles 
                           Peake
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
CHARLES PEAKE. I live at Kennington-common. I was going through Hatton-garden


 last Monday, and felt a motion at my pocket; I turned round instantly, and seized the prisoner, who had two others with him - he produced my handkerchief to me immediately; it was safe just before.


                  PATRICK 
                  WALLACE
               



            . I am a Policeman. I took the prisoner in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. Two boys picked the gentleman's pocket, and threw the handkerchief at me; he turned round and seized me as I was going to deliver it to him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
