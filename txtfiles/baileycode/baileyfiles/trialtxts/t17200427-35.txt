







                     Winifred 
                     Smith , alias 
Ophelia Smith




               
            , of St. Clements Danes


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing two pound of Thread value 3 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Darby 
                  




                
            , on the 19th of March last. Mrs. Darby deposed that the Prisoner came into their Shop and Cheapen'd Thread, Tape, &c. and while she was busy, took the Thread mention'd in the Indictment, but being charg'd closely with it, though she denied it at first, yet owned it afterwards, and gave it to her from under her Petticoat. A Neighbour deposed that Mrs. Darby sent for him into her Shop; and that he saw the Prisner after sometime, deliver the Goods to her. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
