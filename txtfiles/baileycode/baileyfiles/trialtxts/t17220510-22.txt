







                     Hannah 
                     Blake 
                  



               
             of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 3 Holland Smocks, value 15 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Alcock 
                  




                
            , on the 9th of April
 last; 
               
                  Ann 
                  Burt 
               



             (Washerwoman) depos'd, that going out and laying the Goods and her Children at home, when she came back the Goods were gone, and the Children told her, the prisoner (whom she knew before) had been there. 
               
                  Ann 
                  Hudson 
               



             deposed, that the prisoner brought the Smocks to her to pawn, but they being wet, she suspected them to be stoln: had them cry'd and the Prosecutor own'd them. The prisoner in her defence said. Burt lent her the Goods to Pawn: but not proving it, the Jury found her 

               guilty
             of Felony.
