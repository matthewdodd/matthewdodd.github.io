





343. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     ANSTISS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of December
, 1 plane, value 2s.6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Benjamin 
                     Jenery
                  




               
            .


                  BENJAMIN 
                  JENERY
               



            . I am a carpenter
, and live in Britannia-row, Islington. On the 20th of December I left my work at Mr. Young's shop, to go to dinner; the prisoner came in there, but did not say on what business - when I returned from dinner I missed my plane, which had been on the floor; I found where the prisoner lived, and had him taken up.


                  HENRY 
                  NEWMAN
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. Goodburn, a pawnbroker, at Islington, about five minutes' walk from Britannia-row. I have a plane, pawned by the prisoner, about half-past twelve o'clock on the 20th of December, for 1s.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  THOMAS 
                  OLDFIELD
               



            . I took the prisoner, and found this plane - he did not tell me any thing about it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
