            . I live near the Fleet Market


 On Thursday, Nov. 1. I lost a piece of pnted linen out of my father's shop, about two o'clock in the afternoon. I was informed by our porter, that she had taken a piece, and I found it upon her in her apron.


                  Daniel 
                  Gibson 
               



            . I was coming out of my master's kitchen, and saw the prisoner looking at this piece of printed linen, which lay on the shop window; she drew two pieces to her, went away, and returned and took the uppermost piece, and put it into her apron.
