





658. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     NEWETT
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of May
 last, one cotton gown, value 5 s. a check apron, value 1 s. and a cotton handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Jane 
                     Christall
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken, getting out of the prosecutrix's window, with the property upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
