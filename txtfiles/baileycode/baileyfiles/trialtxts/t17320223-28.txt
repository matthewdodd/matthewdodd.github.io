





33. 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Wright 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary le Bone


, was indicted for 


               stealing 5 Silver Spoons, value 30 s. and a Gold Ring, value 20 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Wright 
                        




                      
                  , in his House
            , Feb. 25.


The Prisoner lodg'd in the Prosecutor's House, and upon Examination confess'd he had taken them, and where they were pawn'd, and they were found accordingly. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 39 s.
