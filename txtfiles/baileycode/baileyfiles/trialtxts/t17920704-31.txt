





341. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JONES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of June
 last, fourteen yards of black silk lace, value 30 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Parker
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZABETH
                   PARKER
               



             sworn.
I live at No. 3, Crown-court, Little Poultney-street


; I keep a haberdasher's and milliner's shop
; on the 18th of June, I lost fourteen yards and 3 quarters of black lace; I was in the parlour; the lace was at the shop window; the shop door was shut; I saw the prisoner and another man come with him, and he reached and took the card of lace; first put it into his pocket, and then gave it to the other man, and I caught hold of him and held him till the constable came; the other man ran away; I never got my lace.


                  RICHARD
                   CATTLE
               



             sworn.
I am constable; I took the prisoner.
Prisoner. Two other men took it.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice BULLER.
