





229. 
               
                  
                     MOSES 
                     COSTA
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th day of February
, thirty-six pounds weight of lead, value 6 s. belonging to 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Baker
                        




                     
                  , fixed to a certain building of his, against the statute
            .
The prisoner was taken coming down from the building, with the lead on his head.
PRISONER.
I am guilty, I can say no more.




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
