





Fourth Middlesex Jury - Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1750. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SPONG
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of July
, 1 pair of boots, value 20s.; 1 pair of boot-legs, value 7s.; 1 pair of shoes, value 3s.; 3 sets of two shillings; 1 gown, value 6s.; 4 shirts, value 30s.; 1 shift, value 1s.; 1 table-cloth, value 4s., and 1 handkerchief, value 6d.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Dawe
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
