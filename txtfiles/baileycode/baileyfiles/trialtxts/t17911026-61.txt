





441. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SMITH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of October
, a leather portmanteau, six pair of silk stockings, ten pair of thread stockings, and five pair of cotton stockings, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Lellis
                  



               
            , 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Ashton
                  



               
            , and others.
The prisoner was seen with the portmanteau on his shoulders, and immediately taken into custody.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. COMMON SERJEANT.
