                           Katherine

                  , an Infant
 of 5 Years of Age, putting her in fear, and taking from her a Pair of Stays, and a Woollen Petticoat
            , the 4th of this Instant October
. It appear'd by the Evidence that the Prisoner being seen with the Child at the Land of Promise, near Hoxton


, and that she being seen to come back without the Child, and to have some Things, and the Child crying, the Prisoner was pursued, and taken at Norton Falgate, having the Stays in her Apron, but having sold the Petticoat. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
