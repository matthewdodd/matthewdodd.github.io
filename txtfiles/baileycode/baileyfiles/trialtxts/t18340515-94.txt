





834. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     DILLON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of May
, 1 handkerchief, value 1s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           James Watts 
                           Diamond
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JAMES 
                  WATTS DIAMOND
               



            . I am in the employ of a wholesale house, in St. Paul's Church-yard. On Saturday evening last, I was near Holborn


 - I felt a tug at my pocket - I turned, and saw the prisoner, who was endeavouring to get my handkerchief into his left hand trousers pocket - he ran off - I pursued him, and about the centre of Middle-row he threw down this handkerchief - I took it up, and still pursued - I did not lose sight of him till he was secured - this is my handkerchief.
Prisoner's Defence. I was running along, and kicked the handkerchief before me - I took it up, and put it into my pocket.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
