






473. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  REID
               




             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of February
, eight yards of printed cloth, value 10 s.; three yards of linen cloth, value 4 s. 6 d.; a shawl, value 2 s. 6 d.; and a snuff-box, value 3 d.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  Elizabeth Christopher 
                  Davis
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 28.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
