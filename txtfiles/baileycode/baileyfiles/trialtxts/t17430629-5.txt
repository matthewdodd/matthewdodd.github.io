                  , her Property
            , January 1
.


                  Richard 
                  Dawson 
               



            . There was about 200 Weight of Lead taken off the Closet of a House in King-Street, Covent-Garden


, belonging to Mrs Williams.


                  William 
                  Bishop 
               



            . Hamilton and I went into a House in King's-Street, Covent-Garden, about 12 at Night, in at the back Part, and took as much Lead as we sold to a Widow-Woman in Parker's-Lane for 15 s. - 'Tis a Sort of a Plumber's Shop.


                  Thomas 
                  Cutler 
               



            . When I had the Prisoner before Colonel 
               
                  Deveil
               


             the first Time, he said I will give you no farther Trouble, I own the Fact.
