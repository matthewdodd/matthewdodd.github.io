





266. 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Cooper 
                  



               
            , of St. Sepulchre's


, was indicted for 


               stealing a shift, value 12 d. a yard and a quarter of striped linen, value 12 d. a shirt, value 2 s. and a body of a child's shift, value 6 d.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Smith 
                  




                
            , May 5
.


                  Mary 
                  Smith 
               



            . On Saturday night, between six and seven o'clock, the Prisoner got into my room and stole these things; she was seen coming down stairs with them.


                  Sarah 
                  Barnes 
               



            . I saw the Prisoner come out of Mrs. Smith's apartment; she had a considerable bundle upon her.


                  Mary 
                  Smith 
               



            . I having a suspicion of her, search for her, and found her at one Goodman's in Cat Alley in Long Lane, with my shift upon her back. 


               Guilty 10 d.
