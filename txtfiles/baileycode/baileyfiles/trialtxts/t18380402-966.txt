



966. 




JEREMIAH PANNING
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
12th of March, 1 chisel, value 6d., the goods of 



James East.






JAMES EAST
. I am a carpenter, and live in Little London-street, Paddington, near to the last prosecutor. I lost a chisel on the 12th of March—this is it—(looking at it)—I have known the prisoner for some years—he is a bricklayer's labourer, and was working near my premises.





HENRY WILLIAM TURPIN
. I found the chisel in the waistband of the prisoner's at the time I found the pots.

Prisoner. I put it there to give it to the owner in the morning; I found it among some rubbish.




GUILTY
. Aged 47—


Transported for Seven Years.
