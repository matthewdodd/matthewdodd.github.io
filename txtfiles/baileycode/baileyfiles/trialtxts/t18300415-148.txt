





315 
               
                  
                     AGNES 
                     CONNELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of March
, 1 pelisse, value 5s., and 1 cloak, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Daniels
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  LAW
               



            . I am shopman to William Daniels, a pawnbroker
. On the 5th of March the prisoner came to the shop; I had received information, and suspectedher - I saw her take down a blue pelisse; she waited about the shop a few minutes, then came to the counter, and offered it me to pawn; I asked where she got it - she said she bought it at a neighbouring pawnbrokers; I asked what she wanted for it; she said 2s. - I said if that was the case, I would lend it her; I went down to that pawnbroker to ask if she had bought any thing that day, and she had not; I took her back to our shop, and lent her the 2s. - she stood about till the shop was pretty full of people, then walked round, and snatched down a cloak; I followed, and gave charge of her.
Prisoner. I did no such thing.


                  WILLIAM 
                  DEARING
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner put in a written Defence, stating that she had gone to the shop to purchase a pelisse, and thought she had paid for it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 39. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
