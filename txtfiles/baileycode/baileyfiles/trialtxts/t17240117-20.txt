







                     Sarah 
                     Chapman 
                  



               
            , of Allhallow's Lombardstreet


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Wake 
                        




, the 23th of December
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner came to the Prosecutor's and call'd for a Glass of Brandy; and some Gentlemen who had been drinking out of the Pot, going away, and leaving it on the Table, the Prisoner carried away the same, and pawn'd it. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s
            . 


               Transportation
            .
