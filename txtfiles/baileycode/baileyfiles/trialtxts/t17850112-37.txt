





251 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WILD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 8th of January
, one bar of iron, weight fifty-eight pounds, value 8 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Crawsley
                  



               
            , 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Davenport
                  



               
            , 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Crawsley
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Thompson
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken as the last.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
