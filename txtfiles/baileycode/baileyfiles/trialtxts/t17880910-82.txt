





581. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     MORGAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of August
, a silk hankerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Lyme
                  




               
            .


                  Edward
                   Ryland
               



             and 
               
                  James
                   Wilson
               



             saw the prisoner take the prosecutor's handkerchief from his left hand pocket.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
