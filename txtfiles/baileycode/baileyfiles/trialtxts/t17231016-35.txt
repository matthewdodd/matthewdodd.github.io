







                     Elizabeth 
                     Phillips 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Linnen, value 9 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Bollard 
                  




                
            , the 14th of September
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was a Chair Woman
 in the Prosecutor's House, and stole the Goods. The Prisoner had nothing to say in her Defence. The Jury found her 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
