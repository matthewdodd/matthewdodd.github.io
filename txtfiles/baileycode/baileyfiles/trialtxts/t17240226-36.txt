







                     Christian 
                     Busbey 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchres


, was indicted for 


               privately taking a Hat from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Harris 
                        




, the 2d of March
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, the Prisoner with a Person she called Husband came out of a House in Fleet-Lane


, and the Prisoner snatch'd his Hat off from his Head, and both ran away; but he pursued, and caught her, but the Man made off, but the Hat he did not find upon her. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
