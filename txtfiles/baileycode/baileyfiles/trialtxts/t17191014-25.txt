







                  Thomas 
                  Roberts 
               



            , of St. John at Wapping


, was indicted for 


               stealing 3 pair of Stockings and a Silk Handkerchief value 10 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        John 
                        Sherwill 
                     



                   ; and a Shift and 3 Aprons value 2 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  Hannah 
                  Cousins 
               



             , on the 17th of September
 last. It appeared that had washt the Goods and hung them up in Yard to dry and that the Constable going to Church late about a 11 at Night to search for  met the Prisoner with the Goods wet upon him, which he order'd to be cry'd the next Day, and the came and owned them; was there with the Prisioner, but he got away. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
