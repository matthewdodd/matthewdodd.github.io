





513. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PADMORE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th day of May
, one blunderbuss, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Lane
                  




               
            .


                  Thomas
                   Gillard
               



             saw the prisoner take the blunderbuss from the prosecutor's door; he was taken directly.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
