



2179. 




WILLIAM MASON
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
20th of September, one milk-jug, value, 1l. 10s., the goods of 



Henry Mills.






HENRY MILLS
. I live in Oxford-street, and keep a silversmith's shop. About ten o'clock in the evening, on the 10th of September, the prisoner—I followed, and gave him in charge.

Prisoner. Q. Can you swear I am the person? A. Yes; I never lost sight of you.





JOHN WHALL
 (police-constable C 63.) I heard an alarm in Duke-street—I hastened to the spot, and found the prisoner—I searched him, and found the milk-pot concealed under his coat.
(Property produced and sworn to.)

Prisoner's Defence. I was passing along, and picked up the milk-pot, and this gentleman came and took me.




GUILTY
.* Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.
