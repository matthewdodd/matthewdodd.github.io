





1763. 
               
                  
                     MARIA 
                     HEMLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of September
, 1 shawl, value 8s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Grace 
                  Roberts
               




            .
GRACE ROBERTS. I keep a coffee-shop
, in Widegate-street, Bishopsgate


. On the 1st of September the prisoner came and I trusted her with bread, butter, and coffee, which came to 6d.; she came again on the 2nd, and asked me to trust her again which I did - and when she was gone I missed my shawl; she was taken at twelve o'clock the same night.
CHARLES STILL. I am in the employ of Mr. Wood, a pawnbroker, in Union-street East, Spitalfields. I produce a shawl, which I took in of the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I had been drinking and did not know what I was doing; I pawned the shawl to get me a lodging, thinking it was my own.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 55. 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
