





918. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     BUMPSTEAD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of February
, 4 pewter pot, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Butt
                        




                     
                  , and 1 pewter pot, value 1s. 6d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Scull
                        




.
EDMUND BUTT. I keep the Black Cap public-house, Camden-town



. Four of these pots are mine; I know nothing about the prisoner.


                  JAMES 
                  SCULL
               



            . I am a publican. I keep the Wheat Sheaf public-house



. This pot is mine.
WILLIAM JAMIESON. I am an officer. On Wednesday, the 24th of February, I saw the prisoner in Camden-town; I followed her across the fields - I came up and stopped her; I found these five pots in a stuff petticoat under her apron - she begged me to let her go.
The prisoner made a long Defence, stating she was a widow with six children, and had not the means of providing for them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 37. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
