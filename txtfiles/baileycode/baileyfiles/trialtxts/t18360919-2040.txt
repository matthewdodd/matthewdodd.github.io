



2040. 




JOHN FULFORD
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
14th of September, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 



Joseph Morris, from his person.






JOSEPH MORRIS
. I live on Ludgate-bill. On the 14th of September I was on Ludgate-hill—I was not conscions of having lost any thing till my handkerchief was given to me by Mr. Cox—this is it.





WILLIAM COX
. I live in St. Paul's church yard, About half-past ten o'clock that night I saw the prisoner take a handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket—I instantly collared him—I went to take it from him, but it dropped down—I secured him till I gave him in charge—it was an inside pocket—he look the coat-flap up, and took it out.

Prisoner. I did not know what he took me for.




GUILTY
.* Aged 20—


Transported for Fourteen Years.
