





45. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     ROBERTS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th day of December
, two pair of leather shoes, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Thorne
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the shoes upon him.
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
I found the things.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
