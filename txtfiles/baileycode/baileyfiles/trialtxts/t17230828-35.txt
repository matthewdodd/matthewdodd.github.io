









                  Thomas 
                  Golding 
               



             , 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Moreton 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Edgington 
                  



                
            , of St. Giles's in the Fields




, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 7 Bermudas Hats, value 36 s. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           White 
                        




, the 25th of July
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Hats were stole out of the Prosecutor's Shop-Window, one first, and afterwards six: That the first Hat was sold, and found where sold by Thomas Golding. It appear'd that the two others had been seen in the Company of Golding: But there being no positive Evidence against Moreton and Edgington, they were 

               acquitted
            , and Thomas Golding found 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
