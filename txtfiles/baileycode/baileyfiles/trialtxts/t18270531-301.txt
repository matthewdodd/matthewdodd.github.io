





1314. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  PAFFEY
               




             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of April
, 1 jacket, value 18s., and 1 pair of trousers, value 12s
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Sharp
               




            .


                  EDWARD 
                  GINGER
               



            . I am servant to Thomas Sharp. I was informed the prisoner had stolen a jacket and trousers - I ran out, and caught him with them; he fell down with them on his arms.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was crossing Whitechapel; these things laid under the shop-window - I picked them up; a man said Stop thief! I stopped directly.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 26.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
