



2021. 




HENRY AMBRIDGE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
25th of July, 16 yards of silk, value 2l. 18s.; 1 shawl, value 6s.; 3 handkerchiefs, value 15s.; 1 pair of stockings, value 5s.; 2 yards l-
8th of woollen cloth, value 1l. 11s.; and 2 1/4 yards of kerseymere, value 15s. 9d.; the goods of 




John Cuthbertson, his master.






JOHN CUTHBERTSON
. I am a linen-draper, and live in the Borough road, Southwark. The prisoner was in my service—I missed this silk, and shawl, and other things—I believe these to be mine—(looking at them)—the prisoner had an opportunity of taking them.






SARAH KNIGHT
. I live at No. 21, John-street, Waterloo-road, and before that in William-street. The prisoner used to come and see me there—these sixteen yards of silk, and shawl, and stockings, were found at my lodgings—the prisoner made me a present of them.




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
