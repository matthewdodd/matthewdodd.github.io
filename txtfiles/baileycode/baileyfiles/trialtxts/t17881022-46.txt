





652. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     FREE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of October
, one cotton handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Rushart
               




            .
The prisoner was taken immediately with the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
