







                     Edward 
                     Grace 
                  



               
            , of St. Margaret Westminster


, was indicted for 


               stealing 2 Cheeses, value 4 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Bampton 
                  




                
            , on the 1st of January
.
It appeared that while the Prosecutor was in a back Parlour, the Prisoner crept in on his Hands and Knees and took away 2 Cheeses. But being seen in the Action by a Neighbours they stopt him. He said in his Defence that he was Drunk and so reel'd into the Shop against the Cheeses, and threw some of 'em down, and was only stooping to take 'em up again. 


               Guilty Value 10 d.
