





872. 
               
                  
                     JOHN GEORGE 
                     EYRESON
                  



               
             was indicted, for that 


               he, on the 28th of September
, had in his custody and possession, two forged bank-notes, for the payment of 1 l. each, he knowing them to be forged
            .
SECOND COUNT, that he, on the same day, had in his custody and possession, another forged banknote.
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
