







                     James 
                     Wilson 
                  



               
             of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Looking Glass
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Stephen 
                     Brabant 
                  




                
             , on the 19th of October
 last. It appeared that the Glass was hung up at the Shop Window for Sale, and the Prisoner came by, took it down and carried it away, but being seen, was follow'd and taken with it upon him. The Prisoner in his Defence said, that a Man gave it him desiring him to hold it; and that he was very drunk. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
