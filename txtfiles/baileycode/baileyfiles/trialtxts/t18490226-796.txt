JOSEPH BURROWS
 and 



THOMAS SMITH
 were again indicted for

 stealing 15 lbs. weight of lead, value 2s. 6d.; the property of the Presi dent, vice-president, and others, of the 

Philanthropic Society; to which


BURROWS pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.






CHARLES ANDREWS
