



164. 




ROBERT ENDSOR
 was indicted

 for stealing 1 work-box, value 3s.; the goods of 



Everard Banks.






EVERARD BANKS
. I keep a shop in Nelson-street, Greenwich. On the 12th of Nov. I had a work-box—I saw it safe between three and seven o'clock—I heard some one cry out, and I missed the box—I cannot swear to this box, but I believe it to be mine, and I had one exactly like it on the top of the pile.






CHARLES SMITH
 (police-constable R 71.) I saw the prisoner go to the prosecutor's door, and take this box—I took him and the box.




GUILTY
.** Aged 22.—


Transported for Seven Years
.
