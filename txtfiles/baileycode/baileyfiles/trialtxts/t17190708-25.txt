







                     John 
                     Frost 
                  



               
            , of St. Andrews Holbourn


 , was indicted for 


               stealing a pair of Cloth Coach Seats, value 20 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Jones 
                  




                
            , on the 16th of May
 last. It appeared that a Hackney Coachman being drunk fell off his Box, and one of the Evidence getting up to drive the Coach home, the Prisoner said he liv'd just by where the Coach was to go; whereupon he let him into the Coach, and was sure the Seats were there then; but just before he came to the Yard where the Coach stood, the Prisoner got out and left the Door open; that he drove into the Yard directly, and mist the Seats; that the Prisoner was taken a little after (about one a Clock) by the Watch with the Seats upon him. The Prisoner pleaded he was in drink, and that another desired him to hold the Seats for him. The Jury found him 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
