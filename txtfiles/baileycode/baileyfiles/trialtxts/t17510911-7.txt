





476. (M.) 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Snellock 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing two pair of worsted stockings, value two shillings and six pence
            ; the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Morgan 
                  




July 31
.* 
Henry Morgan. I live in Whitecross-street


. On the 31 of July the prisoner came into my shop, under pretence to buy a pair of stockings, but bought none; I mist them as soon as he was gone out, I call'd to people to stop him; he was stopt before he got out of fight, and the 2 pair of stockings found upon him in the street. Produced in Court and depos'd to.


                  John
                   Cornlius Merryweather
               



             confirm'd that of finding the Stockings under the prisoner's coat.



               Guilty
            .
See No. 275 in this mayoralty.
