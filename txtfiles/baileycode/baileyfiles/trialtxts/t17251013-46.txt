







                     Robert 
                     Page 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Pair of Stays val. 25 s. and a Pair of Jumps val. 15 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Anne 
                           Smith 
                        



                      
                  , from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Mary 
                           Rowny 
                        



, Sept. 30
. It appear'd, that at Night, as Mary Rowny was going along One Swan Yard in Bishopsgate-Street


, the Prisoner pulled the Goods from under her Arm, and ran away; but upon her crying, stop Thief, he was taken with the Goods upon him. 

               Guilty
