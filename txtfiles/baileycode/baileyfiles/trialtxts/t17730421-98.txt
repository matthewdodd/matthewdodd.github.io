





482. (L.) 
               
                  PARTRICK
                   DUNN
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing a copper tea kettle, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Edward
                      Speary
                  




               
            , March 2d
. ++


                  Edward
                   Speary
               



            . I keep a poulterer's shop
 the corner of Fleet-market


; on the 2d of March, about ten at night, I lost a tea kettle.


                  John
                   Plush
               



            . I am apprentice to Mr. Speary; I was shutting up the shop, and saw the prisoner open the hatch, and take the tea-kettle out of the pail; I catched him with it in his hand; he put it down behind him by the shutter: I gave charge of him to the watchman directly.
Prisoner's Defence.
I never had the kettle.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
