





968. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     DACEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of March
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Stevens
                     




                  , from his person; and that he had been previously convicted of felony
            .
WILLIAM STEVENS. I am in the service of Mrs. Hitchins, Beaufort-buildings, Strand. I had a handkerchief in my pocket; a man ran against me and said I had been robbed - I felt and my handkerchief was gone; in about half a minute or a minute a young man collared the prisoner with my handkerchief.


                  JOHN 
                  BRUNDLEY
               



            . The prisoner was given into my custody by Mr. Murray; this is the handkerchief.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  JOHN 
                  MURRAY
               



            . I saw the prisoner and another person with him; the prisoner took the handkerchief from the pocket - I sent a young man to tell the prosecutor; I took the prisoner who ran into the passage of a coffee shop.
GUILTY.


                  ABRAHAM 
                  HAINES
               



            . I have a certificate of the conviction of the prisoner on the 11th of June, in the 10th year of his present Majesty's reign; I was a witness on the trial - he is the man.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
