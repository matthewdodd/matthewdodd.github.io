



1095. 




WILLIAM SMITH
 was indicted

 for stealing on the 
21st of Jan, 1 shawl, value 10s.; 1 apron, 1s.; 1 handkerchief, 1s.; 1 rule, 1s.; 1 shoemaker's tool, called a frewet-wheel, 2s.; 1 shoemaker's tool, called a finishing-iron, 1s. 6d.; the goods of 



George Robert Marsh, his master; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Convict Ship.


Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
