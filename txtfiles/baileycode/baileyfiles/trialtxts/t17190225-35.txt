







                     Mary 
                     Kirby 
                  



               
             , of Ealing


 in the County of Middlesex, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Bodice, 4 Gold Rings, a Scant Tool and Scraper
            , from 
               
                  
                     Eliz.
                      Price
                  




               
             on the 8th of February
 last. The Prisoner came to the prosecutor to ask for work, and was employ'd by her, and took an Opportunity to take the Goods and go away with them: She was found at Gravesend , with part of the Goods upon her: when she was brought to Town she confest the Fact before a Justice, and where she had sold three of the Rings. The Jury found her 


               Guilty/ to the Value of 4s. 10.
            



               Transportation
             .
