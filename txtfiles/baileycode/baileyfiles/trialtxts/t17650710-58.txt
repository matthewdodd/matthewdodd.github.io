





408. (M.) 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Smith 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a pannel saw, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Southern 
                  




                
            , July 10
. ++


                  David 
                  Southern 
               



            . I am a carpenter
, and was at work by Rathbone-place


; last Wednesday between twelve and one, I left my saw with other tools on the bench, and went to dinner; and when I returned to my work, my saw was gone.


                  John 
                  Wharton 
               



            . I was at work along with the prosecutor; he went to dinner; I was sitting on a chest, and eat mine in the buildings; after which I went to gather up the chips. I met the prisoner about twenty-eight yards from the place where the tools lay, with the prosecutor's saw under his coat: I took him by the collar, and took it from him: he fell a crying, and beggd I would not hurt him. (The saw produced and deposed to.)
Prisoner's Defence.
I went there to ask for one Will. Conn, who had worked with me at the Duke of Bedford's.
