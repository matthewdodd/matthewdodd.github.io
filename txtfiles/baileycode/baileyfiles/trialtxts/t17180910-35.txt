







                     William 
                     White 
                  



               
            , of St. Stephen Coleman Street


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silver Snuff Box value 30 s.
             from the Person of 
               
                  
                     Andrew 
                     Fretchurch 
                  




                
             , the 28th of August
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that as he was standing to see the Train'd Bands pass by, he had his Snuff Box taken out of his Pocket; another Evidence deposed that being Drinking in an Ale House and looking out at the Window, one by him told him there were two Pick Pockets standing by the Prosecutor; he then went to him, and ask'd him it he had lost any thing; he feeling found his Snuff Box was gone; so following the Prisoner, took him, and he gave him the Snuff Box out of his Pocket. The Jury thereupon found him 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s.10 d.
            



               transportation
            .
