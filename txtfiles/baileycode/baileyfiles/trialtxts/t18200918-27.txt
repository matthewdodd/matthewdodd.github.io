





924. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     CHISSELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of July
, one coat, value 30 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William Henry 
                     Poland
                  




               
            .
MR. ARABIN conducted the prosecution.


                  JOHN 
                  READ
               



            . On the 3d of July I was at work near Mr. Poland's house, saw the prisoner get half in at the window, and drag the coat out with a stick with a pin at the end of it - I followed and took him about twenty yards off with it.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HENRY POLAND
               



            . I live at Islington


. The coat is mine.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
