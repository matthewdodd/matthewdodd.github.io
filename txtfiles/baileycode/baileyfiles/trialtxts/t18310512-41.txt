





1028. 
               
                  
                     MICHAEL 
                     BAGLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 5th of May
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas Francis 
                           Crew
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  FRANCIS CREW
               



            . I am an attorney
. On the 5th of May, about eleven o'clock in the morning, I was in Lincoln's-Inn-fields


 - I felt something touch my pocket; I turned, and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand - I took him into a butter-shop, and the officer came and took him.
Prisoner. Q. Were there not two more young men between you and me? A. No; there was another lad by the side of him.
Prisoner's Defence. I am without father or mother.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for 14 Years
            .
