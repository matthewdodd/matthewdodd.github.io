







                  Nathaniel 
                  Webster 
               



            , of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 4 Books, called Marshal's Epigrams, in Quires, three Quire of Paper, and 76 Pound of Printed Paper
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Redmayne 
                  




                
            , the 15th of June
 last. It appeared by the Evidence, That the Prosecutor lived in the Prisoner's House, and Mr. Redmayne lost the Goods, and the Books were taken upon the Prisoner. The Prisoner had confessed the taking of the Books before Sir 
               
                  George 
                  Merttins 
               



            . The Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
