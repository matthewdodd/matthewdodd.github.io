





267. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BEVERLEY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of November
, 1 pair of goloshes, value 11s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Clarke
               




            .
THOMAS CLARKE. I am a boot-maker
, and live in Fleet-street


. About eight o'clock in the evening of the 25th of November, my attention was drawn from the end of the shop, by the head of a person near my window; the prisoner came into the shop, and took this pair of goloshes; I called to my young man - we followed, and took him a few yards off, with them in his hand.


                  JOSEPH 
                  ALVIN
               



            . I am apprentice to Mr. Clarke. He called me; I saw the prisoner going out with the goloshes; we followed, and took him with them. He is a stranger.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 33.




               Transported Seven Years
            .
