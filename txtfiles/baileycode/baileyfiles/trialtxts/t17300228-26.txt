







                     Henry 
                     Whitham 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Linen Shirt, value 3 s. 6 d. in the Shop
             of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Temporal 
                  




                
            , on the 5th of January
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner came to his Shop pretending to buy a Shirt, and that when he was gone, he missing a Shirt, follow'd him, and apprehending him, found the Shirt under his Coat. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10d.
