







                     John 
                     Morgan 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 s. in the Dwelling-house
             of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Palmer 
                  




                
            , the 23d of June
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, That he lost 5 s. out of his Draw in the Bar, and that the Prisoner was employ'd to do some Service for him, he keeping a Publick-house. Another Evidence depos'd she saw the Prisoner go to the Draw, and take out Mony, and put it in his Pocket, but what Sum she knew not. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
