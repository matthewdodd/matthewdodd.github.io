





2434. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WALLACE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of September
, 1 coat, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles Thomas 
                     Charlwood
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  THOMAS CHARLWOOD
               



            . On the 29th of September I left my great coat in a cart, in Tavistock-place, Covent-garden


; I lost it, and afterwards saw it in Norton's hands - this is it.


                  JOSEPH 
                  NORTON
               



             (Police-constable F 44). On the 29th of September, about half-past five o'clock in the morning, I stopped the prisoner in Little Russell-street, with this coat under his arm - he said it was his own.
Prisoner's Defence. I was in Covent-garden, and saw the coat on the ground, at the horse's heels.


                  CHARLES 
                  THOMAS
               



             CHARLWOOD. It could not have fallen at the horse's heels; it was in the body of a large cart.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
