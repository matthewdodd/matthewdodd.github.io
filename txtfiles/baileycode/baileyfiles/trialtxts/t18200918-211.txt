





1110. 
               
                  
                     ELIZA 
                     MOSS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of July
, one trunk, value 6 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Wyer
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  WELLS
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  James 
                  Wyer
               



            , a trunk-maker
, who lives in Oxford-street


. On the 24th of July, about half-past seven o'clock in the evening, the trunk was outside of the door. I heard it move; ran to the door; a woman met me, and said the thief had ran down Berwick-street. I followed, and overtook the prisoner at the corner of Noel-street.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. A woman asked me to carry it to Compton-street.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 48.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
