








                  Isabella 
                  Walpole , alias 
Crick




            , of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing divers Goods, in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Wilkins 
                        



, the 15th of March

 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was a Neighbour, who us'd to be frequent at the House, and was seen about the Prosecutor's Door, while they were abroad. But there not being sufficient Evidence against the Prisoner, and the calling divers to her Reputation, she was 

               acquitted
            .
She was likewise indicted a second Time for 


               feloniously stealing two Silver Tea-Spoons, &c
            . the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Dikson 
                  



                
            , the 2d of August

 last; which Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 4s. 10d.
