





117. (M.) 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Fenton 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing one silver spoon value 4 s. 2 cloth waistcoats, value 5 s. one pair of worsted stockings, one piece of linen cloth, two skins
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Hurley 
                  




                
            , Dec. 13
. ++
The prisoner is a taylor
, and was on the 13th of December at work in the prosecutor's house, who is a distiller
, making new work and mending old, &c. he took an opportunity to convey the goods mentioned away; he was taken up, some of the goods were found upon him, and by his directions the rest were found, and produced in court. The prisoner acknowledged the whole in court.



               Guilty
            .
