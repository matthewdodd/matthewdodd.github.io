





143. 
               
                  
                     JAMES
                     MORDANT
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 2nd of December
, feloniously and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession a forged bank note for the payment of 2 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .




               To this indictment the prisoner pleaded
            

GUILTY.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
