







                     John 
                     Nicholas 
                  



               
            , of St. Brides


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing out of the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Daniel 
                           Wisher 
                        




                      
                  , 2 Jacobus's, value 50 s. 2 half Carolus's value 23 s. and other Things
            , on the 25th of July
last. Daniel Wisher depos'd, that the prisoner had been his Servant
, and the Goods being mist, and he examin'd, some of the Goods were found upon him, and he confest the Fact. In his Defence he said, he was drawn into the These by two young Men of his Acquaintance. 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. and 10 d.
