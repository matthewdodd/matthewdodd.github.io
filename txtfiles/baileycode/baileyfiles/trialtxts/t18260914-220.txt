





FOURTH DAY. MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 18.
Fourth Middlesex Jury - Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1532. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  WATSON
               



             was indicted for

                  John 
                  Mitchell
               




            .
