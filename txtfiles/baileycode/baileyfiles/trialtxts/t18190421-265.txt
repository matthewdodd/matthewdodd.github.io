





765. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HURLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of April
, one stewpan, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Peter 
                     Markland
                  




               
            .


                  PETER 
                  MARKLAND
               



            . I live in Green-street, St. George's in the East


; the prisoner lived next door to me. On the 24th of April, I lost this stewpan out of an outhouse. On the Monday he was taken, and it was produced to me.


                  SAMUEL 
                  WILLIAMS
               



            . I am a seaman. On the 24th of April, I saw the prisoner in the prosecutor's yard, about seven o'clock in the morning; he took the stewpan, and put it over into his own yard - I live opposite, and am certain he is the man.


                  JOHN 
                  SMITHERS
               



            . I am a carman. I bought the pan of a boy for 4 s.
JOHN BROWN. I apprehended the prisoner, concealed between his bed and mattress.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
