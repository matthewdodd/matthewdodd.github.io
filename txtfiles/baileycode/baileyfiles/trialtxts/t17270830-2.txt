







                     Jacob 
                     Joseph 
                  



               
            , of St. Mildred Poultry


, was indicted for 


               privately picking the Pocket of 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Probyn 
                        




                      
                  , on the 20th of July
 last; and taking thence a Silk Handkerchief value 2 s. 6 d.
            

The Prosecutor depos'd, That he felt the Prisoner jottle him, and at the same Time the Handkerchief slipt out of his Pocket, but pursuing him and searching he could not find it; upon which he let him go, but recollecting that he might possibly have did it under his Hat, he took him again and found it there; which being plainly proved upon him, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
