





1089. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     KNIGHT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Henley
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  HENLEY
               



            . I am a porter
. I was in Milton-street


 on the 24th of April - a female passed, and said a boy was picking my pocket; I turned, and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand - he was six or eight yards off; I pursued, and when I got within five or six yards of him, he threw down my handkerchief - I pursued, and took him; a female took up the handkerchief, and delivered it to the officer - I had it safe ten minutes before.


                  JOSEPH 
                  HORTON
               



            . I am an officer. The prisoner was given to me by the prosecutor, and this handkerchief by the female, who said she saw the prisoner rob the gentleman- in going to the Compter the prisoner said somebody gave it to him.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was going up the street, and the prosecutor struck me and knocked me down.
MR. HENLEY. I believe, by my running, I shoved him down.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for 14 Years
            .
