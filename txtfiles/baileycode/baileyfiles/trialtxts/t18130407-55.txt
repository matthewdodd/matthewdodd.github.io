





399. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     ADCOCK
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               that he, on the 4th of February
, without lawful excuse had in his custody and possession, a certain forged bank note for the payment of 5 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
