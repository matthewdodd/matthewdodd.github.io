





609. 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Needham 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one silk handkerchief, val. 2 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Isaac 
                           Gervace 
                        




                      
                  , privately from his person
            .

Oct. 3
.



                  Isaac 
                  Gervace 
               



            . Passing through Cheapside


, Oct. the 3d about eleven in the forenoon, I found something press against me, I turn'd about, and found my handkerchief in the hands of the prisoner; I charging him with it, he drop'd it down and ran across Cheap side, I pursu'd him; he then cross'd again, and I took him in Wood-street.




               Guilty 10 d
            .
