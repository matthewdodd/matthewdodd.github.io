







                     Richard 
                     Hedgly 
                  



               
            , of St. Margaret New Fish-street


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing out of the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Smalwood 
                        




                      
                  , 5 pair of worsted Stockings, val. 17 s. 
            on the 25th of March
last. John Smalwood depos'd, that about 9 at Night he saw the prisoner put his Hand in at the Shop Window, and take the Goods, and stopt him with them. The prisoner own'd the Fact at the Bar, but said he did it out of meer necessity. 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. and 10 d.
