






SIXTH DAY. WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 1.
First London Jury - Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
2045. 
               
                  DAVID 
                  THOMAS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of September

, 10 reams of whited-brown paper, value 2l. 10s. the goods of 
                     
                        John 
                        Phipps
                     



                   and others, his partners, his masters
            ; and 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  BROOM
               




             was indicted for 


               feloniously receiving, the same, well-knowing them to have been stolen
            .
Thomas 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 44.
Broom 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 47.
Both 



               Transported for Seven Years
            .
