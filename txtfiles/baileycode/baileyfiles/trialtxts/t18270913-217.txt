





1804. 
               
                  FREDERICK 
                  GLOVER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of September
, 16lbs. of lead, value 20s., the goods of 
                     
                        Richard 
                        Smart
                     




                  , and fixed to a certain building of his; and a brass cock, value 2s.
            , his property.
RICHARD SMART. I have a stable in Barnsbury-mews, lslington


. On the 5th of September, I went there, and missed this pipe and cock, which served the stable with water.


                  JOHN 
                  ELEMENT
               



            . I am a watchman of lslington. I was in Barnsbury mews. Lane called me, and said the prisoner had got a parcel of lead - I took him to the watch-house with it.


                  SAMUEL 
                  LANE
               



            . I stopped the prisoner on the 5th of September, with a basket of lead in his hand - he put it down by his side - another person was with him; the prisoner denied having it, but I saw it in his hand - he was within six yards of the stable.


                  THOMAS 
                  GRAFTON
               



            . I am constable of the night; here is the basket and lead. I compared it with what is left in the stable - it matches exactly.
Prisoner's Defence. I was going to see a young man. I had not touched the basket.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
