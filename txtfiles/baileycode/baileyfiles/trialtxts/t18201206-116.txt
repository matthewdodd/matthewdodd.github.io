





117. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     WILLIS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of October
, 28 yards of stuff, value 20 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Lionel 
                     Mayhew
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  KENTISH
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  Lionel 
                  Mayhew
               



            , a linen-draper
, who lives at Hackney


. This stuff hung by the door. On the 28th of October in the evening, I saw it moving, jumped over the counter, and saw the prisoner with it under his arm; I followed, and he dropped it; I secured and brought him back. He said he would fall on his knees, and beg my pardon.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Garrow
               



            .
