





1481. 
               
                  MARY 
                  COLLER
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of May
, 3 sovereigns, the moneis of 
                     
                        John William 
                        Cupit
                     




                  , from his person
            ; to which she pleaded



               GUILTY
            . 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
