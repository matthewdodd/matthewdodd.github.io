





Before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Garrow
               



            ,
56. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     GYNAR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               bigamy
            .


                  SARAH 
                  SCOTCHMAN
               



            . I live in Berkley-square, and was present at Mary-le-bone church, in May 1814, when the prisoner married my sister Lydia, who was single; she went out as nurse, and he went to service. She had four children by him. I don't know when he left her; she is now in Court.


                  PHOEBE 
                  LANNING
               




            . On the 16th of October, 1818
, I was married to the prisoner at St James's Westminster


; he lived with me ten or fourteen months.


                  MICHAEL 
                  MORRIS
               



            . I am an officer. The prisoner was brought to me on the 23d of November. I have a copy of the register of the marriage at St. James's; I compared it with the original (read). I have the other certificate, but did not compare that.
Prisoner. I throw myself upon your Lordship's mercy.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
