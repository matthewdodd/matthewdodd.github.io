





NEW COURT. - Saturday, September 6th, 1834.
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1322. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  WOOD
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of July
, 5lbs. of sugar, value 3s.; and 12 oz. of pepper, value 2s.; the goods of the 
                     St. Katherine Dock Company


                  , his masters
            ; to which he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 49. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
