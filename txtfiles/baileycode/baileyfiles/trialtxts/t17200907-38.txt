







                     Sarah 
                     Miller 
                  



               
             of St. Giles Cripplegate


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Watch value 3 l. 3 Half Guineas and 11 s. 6 d. in Money, in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Huggins 
                        




, on the 17th of August
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's Servant
, and took the Goods and Money out of his Box; that 2 Half Guineas were found in her Shoes, the rest upon her. The Jury considering the matter, found her 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s
            . 


               Transportation
            .
