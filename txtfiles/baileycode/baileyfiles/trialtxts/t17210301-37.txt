            , of St. Giles in the Fields

               feloniously stealing a pair of Shoes, value 1 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Nathaniel 
                     Pulps

 last. It appeared that the Prosecutor lost the Shoes off his Shop-board, which were found upon the Prisoner, who said she bought them in Holbourn, but could not prove it. The Jury found her
