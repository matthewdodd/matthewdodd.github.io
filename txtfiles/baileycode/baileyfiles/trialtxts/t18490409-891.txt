




891. 




EDWARD LINTON
 and 




JOHN EALES



, stealing 1 handkerchief, value 1s.; the goods of 



William McDonald, from his person; Eales having been previously convicted: to which


LINTON pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 23.—



Confined One Year.


EALES pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 25.—


Transported for Ten Years.
