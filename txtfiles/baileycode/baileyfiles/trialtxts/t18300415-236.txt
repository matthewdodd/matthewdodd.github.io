





902. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     RAYNER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of February
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Evers
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  HENRY 
                  EVERS
               



            . I am a cabinet-maker
. I was in Piccadilly


 on the evening of the 25th of February - I felt a tug at my pocket, turned, and saw the prisoner near me; my handkerchief was then gone; it was found in his breeches pocket - this is it.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BARNETT
               



            . I am an officer. I saw the prisoner try several gentlemen's pockets, and at last the prosecutor came up - part of his handkerchief was out of his pocket; the prisoner fixed his eyes upon him, and took it out of his pocket - he was in company with another boy. who ran away; I took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
