



2135. 




JOHN SAWYER
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
20th of Sept., 1 pair of boots, value 3s. 6d.;—on the 
2nd of Sept., 3 pairs of boots, 9s.; and on the 
12th of Dec, 10 pairs of boots, 1l. 10s., the goods of 



James Pavey, his master; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to all which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
