





211. (L.) 
               
                  Barbary
                   Clark, spinster
, otherwise 
Barbary Hamilton




            , spinster, was indicted for 


               stealing three linen shirts, value 10 s. a silk cloak, value 10 s. two bed-gowns, and three aprons
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Leicester 
                  




                
            , February 13
. ++


                  William 
                  Leicester 
               



            . I am a warehouse-keeper at the East India-house; the things are the property of my father, in Rose-court, Aldermanbury


: the prisoner was his servant
: I heard her confess before the Alderman she sold the shirts for twelve shillings.


                  Anne 
                  Hunt 
               



            . I am the prosecutor's daughter; I know the things are my father's and his children's property: the prisoner went out on the 11th of February, and the things were missing. 


                  Robert 
                  Role 
               



             (produced the silk cloak, two bed gowns, and three aprons;) these I took out of a bundle of the prisoner's, in the Swan and Two Necks yard; I took her in the yard, on the 19th of February; she had been advertised.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
