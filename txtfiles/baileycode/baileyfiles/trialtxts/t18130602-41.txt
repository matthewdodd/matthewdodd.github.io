





556. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     LONG
                  



               
             was indicted for that he, on the 24th of February
, 


               had in his custody and possession, a forged 1 l. bank-note, he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
