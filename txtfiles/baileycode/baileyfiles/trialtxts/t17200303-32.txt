







                     Elizabeth 
                     Fann 
                  



               
             of St. James Dukes Place


, was indicted for 


               Stealing two Callicoe Gowns and Petticoats, two Suits of Muslin Head Clothes, a pair of Shoes, and a pair of Stockings, the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Hester 
                           Lee 
                        




                      
                  , in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Lee 
                     



, on the 5th of January
 last. Hester Lee deposed the Prisoner was her Mother's Maid
 and went away with the Goods; that part of them were found on her Back and Head when apprehended, and the rest in her Lodgings. Thomas Lee confirmed the same, and the Prisoner also owned it on her Tryal. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
