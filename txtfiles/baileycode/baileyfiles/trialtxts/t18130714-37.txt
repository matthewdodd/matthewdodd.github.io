





724. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HOPGOOD
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 19th of June
, feloniously and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession, two forged bank notes, value 1 l. each, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
