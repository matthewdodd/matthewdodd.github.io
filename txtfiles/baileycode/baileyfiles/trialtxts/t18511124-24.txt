





24. 




GEORGE WILLIAM WARMAN



, stealing whilst employed in the Post-Office a post letter, containing a half-sovereign and an envelope;



also, a letter, containing a sovereign, 1s. and 4 postage stamps; 



also, a letter, contraining 1 tassel, and 3 orders for the delivery of goods; the property of 

Her Majesty's Postmaster-General: to all which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 27. (He received a good character.) 


Transported for Seven Years.
