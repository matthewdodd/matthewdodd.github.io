



923. 




WILLIAM FROOME
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
15th of February, the carcase of a pig, value 30s., the goods of 



Thomas Hollis.—2nd COUNT, stating it to be 56lbs. weight of pork; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
