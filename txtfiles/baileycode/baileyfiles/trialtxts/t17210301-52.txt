







                     Laurence 
                     Waldren 
                  



               
            , of St. Dunstan at Stepney


, was indicted for 


               breaking open the Dwelling-House of 
                     
                        
                           Peter 
                           Humphreys 
                        




                      
                   on the 2d of February
 last in the Night-time, and taking thence 2 pair of Holland Sheets, a Drugget Coat and Wastcoat, a pair of Bodice, a pair of Shoes and Stockings, &c.
             the Goods of the said Peter Humphreys. The Prisoner pleaded Guilty to the Felony; and no Evidence appearing to prove the Burglary, the Jury 


               Acquitted him of that; and found him Guilty of Felony only
            . 


               Transportation
            .
