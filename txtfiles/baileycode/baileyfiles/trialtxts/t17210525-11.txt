







                     William 
                     Carl 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary le-Bow


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Worsted Stockings
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Marriot 
                  




                
            , on the 17 th of this Instant May
. 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Burton 
               



            , deposed, that the Prisoner came into the Shop to cheapen Stockings; but he perceiving something under his Coat when he went out, followed and brought him back; when the Prisoner took them from under his Coat, put them on the Compter, and begg'd Pardon. 
               
                  Stephen 
                  Gervis 
               



             confirmed the same. The Prisoner in his Defence said, that he did not intend to steal them. But the Jury not taking his Word for it, found him 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
