





179. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  JOHNSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 27th of November
, 1 wooden-bowl, value 2d.; 1 half-crown; 3 shillings, and 1 sixpence
            , the property of 
               
                  Michael 
                  Peruzzi
               




            .


                  GEORGE 
                  WILLOUGHBY PERUZZI
               



            . I am the son of Michael Peruzzi; he keeps an eating-house
 near Golden-square


 - we have a till in the shop, and a bowl in it; I was at the back of the shop on the 27th of November, and heard a noise; I went forward, and saw the prisoner retiring from the house - I followed him, and told a Police-officer, who took him; we found on him a half-crown piece, three shillings, and one sixpence - my sister picked up the bowl next door, in the way the prisoner ran.


                  JANE 
                  PERUZZI
               



            . I was at home - I picked up the bowl.
WILLIAM FLATTELY. I am a Policeman. I took the prisoner, and found this money on him.(Bowl produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
