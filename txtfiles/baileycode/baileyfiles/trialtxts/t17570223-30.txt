





125. (L.) 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Warner 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one pair of womemens leather shoes, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Hart 
                  




                
            , Jan. 21
. ||


                  John 
                  Hart 
               



            . I live on Fish-street-Hill


. I missed a pair of shoes out of my shop window. I went out and took the prisoner with them upon him, on the other side of the way.
Prisoner's Defence.
A man ran by me and drap'd them, so I took them up, and delivered them to the prosecutor when he asked me for them.
Prosecutor. He denied he had any shoes, till I insisted upon seeing what he had got under his coat.



               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
