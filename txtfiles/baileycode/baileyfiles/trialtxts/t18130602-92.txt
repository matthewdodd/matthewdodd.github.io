





607. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DAVISON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 15th of April
, a handkerchief, value 1 s. 6 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Robins
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  ROBINS
               



            . On the 15th of April I was walking in St. John-street


; the prisoner came up to me and put his hand into my pocket, and took my handkerchief out. I turned myself round, and took the handkerchief out of his hand. This is the handkerchief; it is mine.
Prisoner's Defence. I picked the handkerchief up in the street.



               GUILTY
            , aged 33.




               Transported for Life
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
