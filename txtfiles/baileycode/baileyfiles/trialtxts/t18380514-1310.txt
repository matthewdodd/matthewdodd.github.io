



1310. 




JOHN JONES
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
24th of April, 1 stove, value 4s., the goods of 



John Smith, and fixed to a building.






RICHARD COCK
 (police-constable D 145.) On the 24th of April I was on duty in the neighbourhood of Portman-square, and saw the prisoner carrying this stove on his back—I followed and took him with it.





JOHN SMITH
. I live in William-street, Portman-town. I had a stove fixed there—I missed it on the 24th of April, about ten minutes after it was taken from the house—this is it.




GUILTY
. Aged 34.—


Transported for Seven Years.
