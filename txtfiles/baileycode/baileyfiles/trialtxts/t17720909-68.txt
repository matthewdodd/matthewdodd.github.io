





696. (L.) 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM
                      RISDELL
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a pewter quart pot, value 10 d. and 2 s. 2 1/2 d. in money, numbered
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Edmund
                      Cork
                  




               
            , Sept. 10
. *
The prosecutor keeps the Three Pigeons, in Butcherhall-lane




; the prisoner came to his house and ordered a pot of beer, and change for half a crown, to be sent to a house in the neighbourhood; the prosecutor suspecting him, secreted himself near the house, and when the boy had delivered the beer and the change to the prisoner, he sent the boy back for more, and as he was making off with it, the prosecutor secured him.



               Guilty
            .
