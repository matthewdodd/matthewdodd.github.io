





318. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     MOFFATT
                  , alias 
SQUIRE MASON




            , was indicted for that 


               he, on the 5th of March, had in his custody and possession, a forged bank note for payment of 2 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
