







                     John 
                     Greenland 
                  



               
            , of St. Andrew Holbourn


, was indicted for 


               breaking the House of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Partington 
                        




                      
                  , in the Night time, and taking feloniously a Sheet, value 18 d. and other Things
            . Thomas Partington depos'd, that between 11 and 12 at Night, his Wife came to call him from a Neighbour's House; that he went immediately with her, found the Door open, and the prisoner by the Bed side. He seiz'd the prisoner, who pretended to be drunk, and let fall the Goods. Mrs. Partington depos'd, she was sure she latch'd the Door when she went out, and staid but 3 or 4 Minutes before she return'd with her Husband. 


               Guilty of Felony to the value of 10 d
            .
