





Second London Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
2293. 
               
                  
                     MARGARET 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 10th of October
, 2 pairs of shoes, value 8s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Pendock 
                     Lewis
                  




               
            . - To which she pleaded.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 33. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
