



2086. 




JOHN WILLIAMS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
27th of August 1 handkerchief value 3s., the goods of 



William Lemmer.






WILLIAM LEMMER
. On the evening of the 27th of August I was in Oxford-street and felt a tug a my coat-pocket—I turned and saw the prisoner about a yard from me—I collard him, upon which he pulled my handkerchief form under his waistcoat, and threw it as far as he could for him—I went to pick it up—he bolted—I pursued, calling "Stop thief"—he ran down several streets to Marylebone-lane, into mews—I pursued, and the officer had got him.





JAMES PAGET
. I am a policeman I heard the cry of "Stop thief," and saw the prisoner running in James-street—I took him.




GUILTY
.* Aged 22.—


Transported for Seven Years.
