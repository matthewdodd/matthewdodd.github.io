



793. 




MARTHA BISHOP
 was again indicted

for stealing, on the 
28th of December, 1 gown, value 10s.; 1 shawl, value 18s.; 1 shirt, value 3s.; 2 aprons, value 1s.; and 1 pair of boots, value 7s.; he goods of 



Mary Turner.






MARY TURNER
. I live in Holiday-yard, Creed-lane, Ludgate-hill. I employed the prisoner to nurse my son in my house in December last—she remained with me till the 28th—she then left without notice, and the tame evening I missed a gown and these other things—these are them.





EBENEZER HANBURY
. I am shopman to Mr. Bromley, of Broadway. I produce one shirt pledged by the prisoner on the 24th of December, two aprons on the 27th, and a gown and shawl on the 28th.




GUILTY
. Aged 42.—


Transported for Seven Years.
