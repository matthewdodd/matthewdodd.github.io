





178. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  SMITH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23rd of December
, two coats, value 6l.10s., and three pair of pantaloons, value 3l.10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles Stokes 
                  Edsall
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 49.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
