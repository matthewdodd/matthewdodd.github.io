





733. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     HOBBS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of March
, 3 silver spoons, value 30s., and 2 brushes, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Adam 
                     Hill
                  




               
            .


                  ADAM 
                  HILL
               



            . I am a publican
, and live in Great Chapel-street, Westminster


; the prisoner was in my employ, to clean knives, forks, and shoes. On the 8th ofMarch, when he was gone, I missed three table-spoons from the parlour cupboard - they might have been taken from the kitchen; the parlour cupboard was their place; he had been in the habit of coming from day to day.


                  CHARLES 
                  JOHNSON
               



            . I am shopman to a pawnbroker. I have a table-spoon, pawned by the prisoner, on the 25th of February.


                  LEONARD 
                  GEORGE NEEDES
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a spoon, pawned by the prisoner, on the 7th of March.


                  JOHN 
                  JAMES LLOVD
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Strutton-ground, Westminster, I have a spoon, pawned on the 5th of March by the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 35.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
