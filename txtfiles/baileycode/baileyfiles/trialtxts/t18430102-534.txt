



534. 




EDITH RUGG
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
15th Dec., 1 petticoat, value 3s.; 3 aprons, 1s. 6d.; 3 wrappers, 4s.; 1 blanket, 3s.; 1 sheet, 2s.; 1 wine-glass, 6d.; 1 flat-iron, 1s. 6d.; 1 spoon, 6d.; 1 habit-shirt, 2s.; 2 napkins, 8d.; 1 scarf, 1s.; and 1 shift, 8s. 6d.; the goods of 



John William Fegar:—also, on the 
19th of Dec., 1 nipple-glass, 2s.; 1 sheet, 2s.; and 2 bedgowns, 2s.; the goods of 



John William Feger; and that she had been before convicted of felony; to which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 49.—


Transported for Seven Years.

(There was another indictment against the prisoner.)
