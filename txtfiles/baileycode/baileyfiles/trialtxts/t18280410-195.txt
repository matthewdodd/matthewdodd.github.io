





987. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  WARREN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of March
, 1 bolster, value 7s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Buckel
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  GROVES
               



            . I am a watchman. I stopped the prisoner in Bartholomew-square at eight o'clock in the evening of the 18th of March, with this bolster under his arm; he said he had bought it in Finsbury-market.
JOHN BUCKEL. I am a broker
, and live in Old-street


. This is my bolster, and was taken from a chair at my door- I never saw the prisoner.
The prisoner put in a written defence, stating that he was intoxicated, and met a person in Finsbury-market, who sold him the bolster.


                  JOHN 
                  GROVES
               



            . He was sober, and was running as fast as possible. 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
