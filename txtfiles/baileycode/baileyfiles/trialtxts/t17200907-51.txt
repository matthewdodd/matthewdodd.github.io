







                     Sarah 
                     Davis 
                  



               
             of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Cloth Coat. Wastcoat and Breeches, the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Simon 
                           Obrion 
                        




                      
                  , in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Fenwick 
                     



 on the 7th of this Instant September
. It appeared that the Prosecutor was gone to Bed in a lower Room in the Vine Tavern in Wildstreet


, and the Prisoner went in and took his Clothes, but being seen was followed and taken with them upon her. The Jury Found her 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s
            . 


               Transportation
