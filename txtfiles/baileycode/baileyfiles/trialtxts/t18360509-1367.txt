



1367. 




JOHN WILLIAMS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
20th of April, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 



John Bond Ratcliffe, from his person.






JOHN BOND RATCLIFFE
. I was going through Union-street, South-wark, on the 20th of April, about half-past four o'clock, and stopped to allow some carriages to pass—I felt my handkerchief drawn from me—I turned and saw the prisoner crossing the road with it under his arm—I took it from him and gave him into custody.





JOHN FEAR
 (police-constable M 42.) I was called and received the prisoner and the handkerchief.

Prisoner. There were a mob—a young man and woman were passing—I saw this handkerchief drop—I picked it up, and was putting it into my pocket, and the prosecutor seized me.





JOHN BOND RATCLIFFE
. There was no one nearer me than him—I know it was him took it from me, and he owned to it afterwards.




GUILTY
.* Aged 25.—


Transported for Seven Years.
