





2045. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     COLLINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of October
, 1 ham, value 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        Anthony 
                        Medcalf
                     




                  ; and that, at the General Session for thedelivery of the Gaol of Newgate, held on Thursday, the 31st of May, in the 8th year of his present Majesty's Reign, he was convicted of felony, by the name of 
                     
                        William 
                        Davies
                     



.


                  WILLIAM 
                  WEIBLE
               



            . I am a clothes-dealer - I know Mr. Medcalf, he lives in Gray's Inn-lane


. On the 8th of October I saw the prisoner take this ham from his door - he carried it two doors down the lane, and left it at a private door; I told Mr. Medcalf, and the prisoner was taken.


                  ANTHONY 
                  MEDCALF
               



            . This is my ham here.
THOMAS DELANY. I am a Police-constable. I took the prisoner and brought the ham.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  THOMAS 
                  COLLINS
               



            . I am an officer of Walbrook. I got this certificate from the clerk of the arraigns (read); I was sent at the conviction of the prisoner for stealing a silver spoon, and he was sentenced to one year's imprisonment in the House of Correction; I know he is the person.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 62.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
