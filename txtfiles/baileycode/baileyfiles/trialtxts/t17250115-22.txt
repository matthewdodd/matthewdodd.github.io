







                     John 
                     Short 
                  



               
            , of Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Silver Tankard value 8 l.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Jones 
                  




                
            , on the 9th of January
 last. It appear'd that the Prosecutor keeps an Alehouse
 at the Fiery Beacon in Duke's-Place


, and on Saturday Night the Prisoner (who had formerly been his Servant
) came in and called for a Pint of Beer, and while the Maid was gone backward, took away the Tankard, and went off, without either drinking his Beer or paying for it. He confess'd the Fact, and that the Tankard was sold by two Women to 
               
                  William 
                  Field 
               



             in Southwark for 56 s. 


               Guilty to the Value of 39 s
            . 


               Transportation
            .
