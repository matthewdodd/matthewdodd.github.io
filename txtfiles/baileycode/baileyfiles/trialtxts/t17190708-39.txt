            , the 12th of June
 last, William James, deposed he lost his Mare from Pitchcot near Ailesbury in Buckinghamshire


. 
               
                  Matthew 
                  James 
               



             his Brother deposed, that he living in Town Advertised the Mare in Print, whereupon Mr. Stevenson, who keeps an Inn in Hart street, Covent Garden, came and said he had it, and that he bought it of the Prisoner; the Prosecutor owned it and had it again; and it appeared to be stole on Saturday Night, and sold again on Sunday. The Prisoner said he bought it on the Road, but could nor prove it. The Jury found him
