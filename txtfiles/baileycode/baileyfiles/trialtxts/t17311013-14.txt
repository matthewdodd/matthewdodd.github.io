







                     Margaret 
                     Thomas
                  



               
            , of St. Botolph's Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing five dozen of Pewter-Spoons
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Jonathan 
                     Broadhurst
                  




               
             , the 1st of August 
last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That he had lost 12 dozen of Spoons, but had found but 5 dozen, which he found at Mrs. Rich's.
Mrs. Rich depos'd, That the Spoons were brought to her to pawn, by 
               
                  Margaret 
                  Bedford
               



             the Prisoner, who goes also by the Name of 
               
                  Margaret 
                  Thomas
               



            , and also Hardis. The Prisoner pretended she had the Spoons to pawn of a Man that deals in such Ware, and keeps Fairs; but not being able to produce any such Person, the Jury found her 

               Guilty
             of the Indictment.
