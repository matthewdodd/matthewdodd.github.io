





554. 
               
                  
                     JOHN DAVIS 
                     SAINTSBURY
                  



               
             was indicted, for that 


               he, on the 30th of May
, feloniously, and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession two forged bank-notes, for the payment of 10 l. each, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
