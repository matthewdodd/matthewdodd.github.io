                  Henry
                   Eastlake
               



             . Lee was in my house; I used to give him three shillings a week in order to prevent his getting into this gang of boys. I intended to take him as an apprentice.
- Eastlake. I am the wife of the last witness: I sent the boy up for some eggs; I saw him take the spoon and run away with it.


                  Charles
                   Handler
               



            . I bought the spoon of a woman; I cannot be certain as to her person; it was such a woman as the prisoner.
Lee, Mouls and Robinson, in their defence, said the woman encouraged them to steal things and bring them to her.
