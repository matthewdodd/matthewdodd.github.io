







                     Thomas 
                     Addington 
                  



               
            , of St. Andrew's Holborn


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 19 s. in Money, in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           Patrick 
                           Robinson 
                        




, the 11th of August
. The Prosecutor depos'd, The Prisoner was his Apprentice
, and he had miss'd Money several Times, and suspecting the Prisoner, counted his Money, watch'd him, and saw him take our some Money; and that he having Tenderness for him did not prosecute him, tho' he had behav'd very ill, often collaring him and beating of him; but being irreclaimable, he at last did. The Fact was plainly prov'd, and the Jury found him 


               guilty in the Value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
