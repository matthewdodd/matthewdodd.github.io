







                     William 
                     Field 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary Whitechappel


, was indicted for 


               stealing 3 Jackets in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Possonby 
                        




, the 26th of March
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner came to the Prosecutor's Shop under Pretence to buy a Pair of Breeches, and took an Opportunity to run away with the Goods; but being discover d, was presently pusued, and taken with the Goods upon him. The Prisoner having nothing but what was trifling to say in his Defence, and the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d
            .
