





1456. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     ENGLISH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of June
, 1 necklace, value 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        Caleb 
                        Cusack
                     



                  , from the person of 
                     
                        Susan 
                        Cusack the younger



.


                  SUSAN 
                  CUSACK
               



            . I am the wife of Caleb Cusack; I have an infant, eighteen months old, named Susan. On the 19th of June I was in Princes-court, Drury-lane


, with her in my arms - she had a necklace on; the child became restless, I turned, and saw the prisoner with a handkerchief in his hand, to which the child's necklace had been fastened; he had broken the string - I took hold of him, and gave him into custody with the necklace.


                  JOHN 
                  LEFT
               



            . I am a patrol. I took the prisoner and the necklace.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
