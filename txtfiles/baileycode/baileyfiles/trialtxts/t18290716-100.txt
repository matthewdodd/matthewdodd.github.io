





1474. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     HINTON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of July
, 1 coat, value 3s.; 1 jacket, value 1s. 6d.; 2 aprons, value 6d., and 48 farthings
            , the property of 
               
                  William 
                  Robinson
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
