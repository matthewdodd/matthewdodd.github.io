



502. 



THOMAS PRICE
 was indicted

, for that he being employed in the Post-office, did feloniously steal one letter, containing 1 half-sovereign and 1 6d., of Her Majesty's Postmaster-General:—other COUNTS, varying the manner of laying the charge; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
.—


Transported for Ten Years.

(Mr. Cuthfield, a type-founder, and Richard Brown, lapidary, of St. John's square, Clerkenwell, gave the prisoner a good character.)
