





1576. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     KEMBLE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of August
, 1 cheese, value 14s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Allwright
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
