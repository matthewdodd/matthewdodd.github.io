







                     Mary 
                     Bisset 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Gown, value 15 s. and other Goods, in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Godfrey 
                        




, the 8th of February
. The Evidence depos'd, That the Goods were taken away; and, that the Prisoner did own, that she had pawn'd the Gown for 9 s. but she deny'd it at the Bar. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
