





61. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     JOSEPH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of November
 last, a pair of linen sheets, value 3 s. a pillow-bier, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Hyams
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the sheet and pillow-case under his arm.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
