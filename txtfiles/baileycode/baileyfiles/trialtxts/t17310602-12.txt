







                     Thomas 
                     Been 
                  



               
             , of St. Magnus the Martyr


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk-Handkerchief, value 2 s. 6 d.
             from the Person of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Reynolds 
                  




                
             , the 3d of May
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That he lost his Handkerchief out of his Pocket, and that it was found upon the Prisoner.
Shaley Shadwell depos'd, That he saw the Prisoner take the Handkerchief out of the Prosecutor's Pocket; the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
