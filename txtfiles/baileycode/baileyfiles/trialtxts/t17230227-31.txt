







                     Mary 
                     Keatly 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St.Bernard Pauls Wharf


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Tankard, value 7 l. a House-man's Coat, value 5 s. and other Goods in the Dwelling house of 
                     
                        Richard 
                        How 
                     



, the 27th of January
 last. Her Confession of the Fact before Sir 
               
                  John 
                  Fryer 
               



             was read in Court, but however, she deny'd it at the Bar; saying, They were given her by a Man to pawn, but not being able to prove it, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
