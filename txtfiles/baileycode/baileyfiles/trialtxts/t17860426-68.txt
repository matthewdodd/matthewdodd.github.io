





386. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     ECCLES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 10th day of April
, twelve pounds weight of iron, value 15 d.
             the property of 
               
                  William 
                  Hill
               



             and 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Taylor
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the property upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
