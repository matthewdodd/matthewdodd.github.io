




2548. 



JAMES WATSON
 and 



ANN WATSON
 were indicted for

 stealing, on the 
12th of September, 2 pewter pots, value 3s., the goods of 



George Augustus White; and 1 pewter pot, value 1s., the goods of 



William Evans; to which


J. WATSON pleaded 


GUILTY
.—


Transported for Seven Years.

A. WATSON pleaded 


GUILTY
.—



Confined Ten Days.
