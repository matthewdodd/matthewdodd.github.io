





755. 
               
                  
                     JANE 
                     WOODEN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of March
, two gowns, value 12 s.; one sheet, value 5 s.; one shift, value 5 s., and one sheet, value 6 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Simon 
                     Levy
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZA 
                  LEVY
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  Simon 
                  Levy
               



            , we live in Three Tun-alley, Whitechapel


. On the 16th of March I hired the prisoner as my servant
 - on that very morning I missed these things, with others. I lost four sheets, but only found one. I only left her in the house an hour, and when I returned she had absconded with the property, and left my child crying in the house alone.


                  JAMES 
                  WALTER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a sheet, a bed-gown, and a shift, which the prisoner pledged with me on the 16th of March.


                  JOHN 
                  JOHNSON
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. On the 16th of March the prisoner pledged a shirt with me.

FRANCIS FREEMAN. On the 16th of March I found a gown on the prisoner.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. Distress drove me to it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 27.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
