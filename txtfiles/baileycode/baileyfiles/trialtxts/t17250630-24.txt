







                     Martha 
                     Mead 
                  



               
            , (a Girl
) of Wapping


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Coat and Wastecoat val. 4 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Dobbins 
                  




                
            , on the 31st of May
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner got into the Prosecutor's House at a Trap-Door on the House Top; that she took the Cloaths out of a Chest, and hid herself and them under a Bed, where she was discovered by a Chairwoman. 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
