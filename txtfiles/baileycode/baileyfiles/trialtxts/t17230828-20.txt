







                  William 
                  Lane 
               



            , of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Pair of Iron Grates, value 4 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Cook 
                  




                
            , the 10th of August
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner, who was a Boy
, took the Grates as they stood at the Prosecutor's Door, who was a Broker
, and putting them on his Shoulder, was carrying them away, when he (the Prosecutor) pursu'd and took him. The Boy had nothing to plead, but that he wanted Victuals. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
