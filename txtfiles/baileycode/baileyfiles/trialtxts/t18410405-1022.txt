



1022. 




RICHARD WINTON
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
17th of March, 12 pairs of stockings, value 11s.; the goods of 



John Wills and an-other; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






HENRY WITHERS
. I am in partnership with John Wills, as hosiers, in the Poultry. On the forenoon of the 17th of March I was behind the counter—I saw the prisoner snatch these goods, and run away with them—I followed him about thirty yards, and took him with them in his hand.





WILLIAM MARTIN
 (City police-constable.) I was on duty in Poultry—the prisoner was delivered into my custody, with the property.





EDWARD EXTON
 (City police-constable.) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—I was present at the trial—he is the person described in the certificate—(read.)




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Ten Years.
