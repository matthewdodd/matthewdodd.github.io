





941. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JOHNSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of March
, 1 cheese, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Bridger
                  




               
            .
WILLIAM BRIDGER. I live in Speldhurst-street


, and am a cheesemonger
. On the evening of the 19th of March I was in the parlour; I saw the prisoner take this cheese from my window, and run out - I followed, and caught him with it; he tried to throw it into an area.


                  JAMES 
                  FULLER
               



            , (Police-constable E 118.) I took the prisoner, and have the cheese; the prisoner had 4 1/2d. on him - he said he did it for want.
Prisoner I have no father or mother, and leave it to your mercy.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
