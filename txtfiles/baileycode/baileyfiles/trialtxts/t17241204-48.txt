







                     Stephen 
                     Dyer 
                  



               
            , of S. Martin's Ludgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Firkin and 43lb. of Soap, Value 15s. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Tims 
                        




, on the 21st of Oct
. last. 
               
                  Rich 
                  Boomhall 
               



             Jun depos'd, that at Night, as he was easing himself in Prujan-Court in the Old-Baily, he saw the Prisoner go into the Shop and came out with the Goods; and suspecting them to be stolen, he called to the Woman in the Shop, and then ran after the Prisoner and step him in Black and White-Court. 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
