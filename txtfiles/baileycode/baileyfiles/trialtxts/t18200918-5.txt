





903. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     SMYRKE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of July
, one silk handkerchief, value 4 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William Robert Henry 
                           Brown
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Life
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
