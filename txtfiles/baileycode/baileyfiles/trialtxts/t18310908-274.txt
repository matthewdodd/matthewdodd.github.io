





1801. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  BLACKFORD
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of August
, 60 yards of calico, value 25s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Robert 
                  Philps
               




             and another; and that he had been before convicted of felony.


                  ROBERT 
                  PHILPS
               



            . I live in High-street, Poplar


, and have one partner; we are linen-drapers
. On the 7th of August I lost this calico from my shop, but I knew nothing of it till the officer came - it had been safe at nine o'clock that evening, and the officer came at eleven.


                  AARON 
                  GERMAIN
               



            . I am an officer. I came down my heat, and stood against the toll-bar; a man crossed into the road; he saw me, and turned round - I followed him, and saw the prisoner with this parcel in his apron; I took him, he said he had it from a pawnbroker.(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner put in a written Defence, stating that the goods were given to him to carry by a man named Scott.


                  WILLIAM 
                  MATTHEW
               



            . I am a Police-officer. I have a certificate of the former conviction of the prisoner; I know he is the person - (read.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
