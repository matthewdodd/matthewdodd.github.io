







                     Elizabeth 
                     Banks 
                  



               
            , of St. James Duke's Place


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Linnen and wearing Apparel, value 4 l. in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Wood 
                        




, the 15th Day of this present October
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner had been Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and stole the Goods. Her Examination was read in Court, wherein she confess'd the Fact, and that she went into Kent, and left the Goods in a Wood. She having nothing to say in her Defence. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
