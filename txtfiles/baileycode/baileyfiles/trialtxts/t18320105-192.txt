






485. 
               
                  
                     HANNAH 
                     GADSBY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of January
, 2 sheets, value 4s, and 1 counterpane, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Cowell
                  




               
            .
ANN COWELL. On Monday, the 2nd of January, the prisoner hired a furnished room of me; a soldier took the room with her for a week, and they said they wanted clean sheets - I put them clean sheets; the soldier gave me 1s. earnest, and said the prisoner was his wife - the next morning they went out, and never returned; they left the door wide open - I fetched a Policeman, and missed two sheets, and the counterpane, which were let to them; I had the soldier apprehended the next morning.


                  HENRY 
                  KEAN EDWARD NEWSTEAD
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Tothill-street. I took in a sheet and a quilt from a woman, on the 3rd of January.


                  JAMES 
                  STANTON
               



            , I live at Mr. Morritt's - I have a sheet, pawned by the prisoner, on the 3rd of January.


                  GEORGE 
                  PECK
               



            . I am a Policeman. I took the prisoner; she gave me these duplicates of the sheet and counterpane.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I slept there the next night.


                  ANN 
                  COWELL
               



            . She never came back to my knowledge.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 28. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
