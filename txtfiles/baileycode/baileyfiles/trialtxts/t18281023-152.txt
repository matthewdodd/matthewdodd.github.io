





2095. 
               
                  GEORGE 
                  HARNETT
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of October
, 5 lbs. weight of bacon, value 1s. 8d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Byers
               




            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  LACY
               



            . I live in Earl-street, Lisson-grove


, opposite to Mr. Thomas Byers, who is a cheesemonger
. On the 4th of October I saw the prisoner come and takea piece of bacon from his shop-window - he did not go into the shop, but went on the steps of a private-door, and took it round some casks; he put it under his coat - I ran and took him; he dropped it at my feet - I chased him to No. 2, Little Exeter-street; he ran into a house, where the door was open and shut the door in my face - I then returned and went to my work.


                  SARAH 
                  LACY
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the bacon from Mr. Byers' board - I am sure he is the person.


                  JOHN 
                  BOYD
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner at No. 1, Little Exeter-street; he appeared to be asleep - I said "You appear very fast asleep?" he opened his eyes, and said "I did not take the bacon. it was another boy."
Prisoner's Defence. I said I knew nothing about it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
