





564. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     ROBERTS
                  



               
             was indicted for that she, on the 13th of March
, 


               feloniously had in her custody and possession, two forged 1 l. bank-notes, she knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
