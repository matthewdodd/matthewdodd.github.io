





263. 




ROBERT HOULDER
 was indicted

for feloniously and knowingly uttering, on the 
31st of Oct., a forged request for the delivery of 6 pairs of Blucher boots;—


also, on same day, a forged request for the delivery of 2 pairs of Clarence boots, with intent to defraud 



Henry Bonham;—


also, on the 
19th of Nov., a forged request for the delivery of 4 pairs of boots, with intent to defraud 



William Ager;to all of which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 36.—


Transported for Ten Years.


Before Mr. Justice Coltman.
