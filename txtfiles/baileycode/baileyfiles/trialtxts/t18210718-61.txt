






964. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     STALKER
                  



               
            , was indicted, for 


               having feloniously in her custody and possession, one 1 l. forged Bank note, knowing it to be forged
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.

               disposing, and putting away, a certain forged Bank note, with intent to defraud the 
                     Governor and Company of the Bank of England



.
No evidence.




               NOT GUILTY
            .
