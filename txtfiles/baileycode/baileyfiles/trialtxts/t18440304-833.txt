



833. 




ESTHER BORASTON
 was indicted

 for stealing, at St. Giles's-in-the-fields, 54 yards of linen cloth, value 3l. 10s.; 1 table-cloth, 10s.; and 1 quilt, 14s.; the goods of 



William Thomas Hester:—1 gown, value 1l.; 1 yard of cashmere, 1s.; 4 yards of orleans, 8s.; 1 shawl, 2s.; 5 yards of calico, 2s.; 2 pairs of stockings, 4s.; 1 pair of stays, 2s.; 1 pair of boots, 3s.; and 1 pair of shoes, 3s.; the goods of 



Eliza Hester, in the dwelling-house of 



Henry Manning:—also, for stealing 3 yards of calico, value 2s.; the goods of 



William Attwood; to which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 24.—


Transported for Ten Years.
