



1586. 




BENJAMIN BIRDSEYE REEVE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
30th of March, 72 cambric handkerchiefs, value 20l.; 23 silk handkerchiefs, 5l.; and 33 scarfs, 45l.:—also, on the 
1st of July, 22 cambric handkerchiefs, 6l.; and 21 scarfs, 30l.; the goods of 



James Beale, his master: to both which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 39.—


Transported for Seven Years.
