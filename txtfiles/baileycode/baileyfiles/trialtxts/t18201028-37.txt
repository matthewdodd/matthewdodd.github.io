





1264. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     REES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of October
, one waistcoat, value 4 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Sadler
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  RICHMOND
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Sadler
               



            , a pawnbroker
, who lives in Aldersgate-street


. On the 6th of October between four and six o'clock, the prisoner was lurking about the door. The boy missed a waistcoat, and pointed the prisoner out; I followed, and stopped him in Barbican, and found it in his hat.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
