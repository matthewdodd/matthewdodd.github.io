



438. 




JANE SKUSE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
20th of December, 1 gown, value 6s., the goods of 



Henry William Morrison.






SARAH MORRISON
. I am the wife of Henry William Morrison. We live near the Regent's Park—the prisoner has been with me occasionally—she came on the 20th of December—I was ill in bed—she wanted me to lend her 5s.—I said I could not then, but would on Monday—on the 25th I missed the property—I told my husband—he went with the officer to her lodging, but I did not go—this is my gown.





JOHN ROSE
. I am shopman to a pawnbroker. I took in this gown of the prisoner.




GUILTY
. Aged 36.—


Transported for Seven Years.
