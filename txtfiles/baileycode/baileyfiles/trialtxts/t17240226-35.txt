







                  John 
                  Brown 
               



            , of the Parish of St. Ann's Aldersgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Pair of Leather Breeches, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Turner 
                        




, the 20th of March
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner came into the Prosecutor's Shop, and took the Breeches, and went away, but was pursued and taken, and drop'd the Breeches upon his being apprehended. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
