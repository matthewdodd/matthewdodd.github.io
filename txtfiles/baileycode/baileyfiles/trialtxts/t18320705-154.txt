





1577. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     HUGHES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of June
, 1 jacket, value 1l.; 1 pair of trousers, value 16s., and 1 waistcoat, value 12s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Saunders
                        




                     
                  ; and that she had been before convicted
            . -To which she pleaded.




               GUILTY
             - Aged 28. 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
