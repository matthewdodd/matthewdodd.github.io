



2353. 




CECILIA INMAN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
23rd of June, 7 table-cloths, value 3l. 14s.; 48 napkins, value 3l. 1s.; 2 rings, value 1l. 2s.; 1 gun and case, value 1l.; 4 sheets, value 10s. 6d.; 2l. d'orleys, value 4s.; 1 curtain, value 1s. 6d.; 1 bed-tick, value 1s.; and 1 reticule, value 2s.; the goods of 



Lucy Eliza Mackay: 3 yards of silk, value 5s.; and 1 fan, value 2s.; the goods of 



Emma Florentine Mackay: and 1 fan value 2s., the goods of 



Charlotte Mackay, in the dwelling-house of 



Lucy Eliza Mackay:—also, on the 
19th of July, 2 wine-coolers, value 1l., the goods of Lucy Eliza Mackay; to all of which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 29.—


Transported for Ten Years.
