





1647. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     GREEN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23rd of July
, 1 watch, value 2l.; 1 key, value 2s.; 4 crowns, and 4 shillings
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Northage
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
