



2220. 




JOHN PERRING
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
15th of August, 1 coat, value 5s.; and 1 waistcoat, value 2s.; the goods of 



Stephen Nelson.—2nd COUNT, stating them to belong to 



Sarah Perring: to which he pleaded




GUILTY
.** Aged 12.—


Transported for Seven Years—Convict Ship.
