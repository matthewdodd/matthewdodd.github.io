The prosecutor is a shoemaker

The pumps were at his window hanging upon two pins at nine o'clock in the morning on the 19th of August: they were missed about two; the prisoner was seen to take them by a neighbour , who informed the prosecutor of it. The prisoner was taken up, and confessed she had taken them, and had pawned them, but said she was drunk, and did not know where.
