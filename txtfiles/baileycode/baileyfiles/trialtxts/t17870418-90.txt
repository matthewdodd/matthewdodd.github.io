







420. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  ATKINSON
               



             and 
               
                  BENJAMIN 
                  FURBER
               



             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th day of April
, five linen table-cloths, value 30 s. one pillow-case, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Harrison
                  




               
            .


                  John
                   Graver
               



             saw the prisoner Atkinson drop the linen; and 
               
                  John
                   Webb
               



             took the prisoner Furber in company with Atkinson.
BOTH 

               GUILTY
            .





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   HOTHAM
               



            .
