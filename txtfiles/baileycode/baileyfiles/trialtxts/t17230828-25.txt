







                     Susannah 
                     Newport 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Brides


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Tankard, value 5 l. 15 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Lily 
                  




                
            , the 19th of August
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner being there drinking, was seen to go into the Bar and take something, and to stoop down; and coming out was siez'd, and the Tankard was dropp'd from under her Coats. The Fact being plainly prov'd the Jury found her 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
