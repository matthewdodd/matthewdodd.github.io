



1384. 




JOSEPH WHEELER
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
19th of April, 1 coat, value 10s.; the goods of 



John Davis.






JOHN DAVIS
. I am a servant at a public-house at Greenwich. I lost my coat from an outbuilding in the yard, where we clean the boots and knives, on Friday the 19th of April—this is it.





JOSEPH ABATHY
. I am a pawnbroker at Blackheath-hill. I took in this coat of the prisoner on the 19th of April, in the name of Joseph Wheeler, for 5s.

Prisoner's Defence. I bought the coat in the fair for 1s. 6d.




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
(There was another indictment against the prisoner.)
