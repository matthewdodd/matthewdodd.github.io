





225. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     ROBINSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of January
, four plate glasses, belonging to a post chaise, value 30 s. and one wooden frame, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  West 
                  Hyde
               




            , Esq
.
The prisoner was taken with the glasses upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
