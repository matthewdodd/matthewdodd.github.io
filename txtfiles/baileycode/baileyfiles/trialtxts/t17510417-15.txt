





276. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Peacock 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing one quilted petticoat, value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Stone 
                  




                
            , April 9
. +


                  John 
                  Stone 
               



            . I live in Hatton-garden


, the prisoner is a soldier
 and was quarter'd in my house; last Monday sen'night my wife gave him a candle at about 12 at night to go to bed, he went up stairs, but went out and left my doors open all night; the next day we found a trunk broke open, and a white quilted petticoat of my wife's was missing.


                  Mary 
                  Stone 
               



            , Wife to the prosecutor, confirmed the above.


                  John 
                  Bivon 
               



            . I am a watchman in St. Martin's, I and another watchman took the prisoner out of a night cellar with this petticoat under his arm; he had told us different stories about it, where he had it and whose it was.


                  George 
                  Graunt 
               



            , another watchman, confirmed the testimony of Bivon.


                  Peter 
                  Nichols 
               



             the constable produced the petticoat, the prosecutor and his wife deposed to it.
Prisoner's defence.  I never saw the petticoat in my life before, 

               Guilty
            .
