





2178. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  JONES
               




             was indicted for indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of October
, 2 table-cloths, value 8s.; 1 pair of salt-holders, value 5s., and 6 glass castors, value 9s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Catlin
               




            .


                  SEBIA 
                  CATLIN
               



            . I am the wife of George Catlin, who keeps an eating-house
 in Aylesbury-street, Clerkenwell


. On the 16th of October the prisoner came there for his dinner; he paid 7d. for it, and then went out - I thought all was not right; my husband went and missed the property off the table; he fetched the prisoner back with it; he produced it from his person.


                  CHARLES 
                  FREEMAN
               



            . I was at Mr. Catlin's on the 16th; I saw the prisoner come to the house and go up stairs; I afterwards saw the prosecutor bring him back - he pulled the table-cloth from his hat, and the other things from his pocket.


                  SAMUEL 
                  MOUNTSTEVENS
               



            . I am an officer. I received him in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
