



107. 




DENNIS FITZGERALD,alias
 Kennelley




, breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 



Jane Smith, on 
14th Nov., at 

St. Pancras,✗ and stealing therein 11 spoons, value 2l. 4s. 2 pair sugar-tongs, 16s. 2 half-crowns; and 5s. her property; having been before convicted; to which he pleaded 


GUILTY. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
