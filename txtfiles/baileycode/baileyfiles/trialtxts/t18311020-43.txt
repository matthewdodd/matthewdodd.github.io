





1998. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  BISHOP
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of September
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Nicholas 
                     Morritt
                  




               
            .


                  FREDERICK 
                  HINGSTON
               



            . I live with Mr. 
               
                  Nicholas 
                  Morritt
               



            , a pawnbroker
, in King-street, Westminster


. On the forenoon of the 19th of September, I went out, and saw the prisoner in the street - he was running; I stopped him, and found on him this handkerchief, which is my employer's; I had seen it safe half an hour before when I hung it in the shop.


                  JOHN 
                  BARKER
               



            . I am a Police-officer. On the forenoon of the 19th of September I heard an alarm in York-street; I saw the prisoner pass me running - I followed, and stopped him in the presence of the shopman; I took this handkerchief from him.


                  ELIZABETH 
                  YATES
               



            . I am the wife of Christopher Yates. On the 19th of September I was at our window, and saw several handkerchiefs hanging at his door - among the rest was a black one; the prisoner came up, looked at them, and put the black handkerchief behind a quilt which was there; he then pulled it down, took it from under the quilt, and put it under his jacket - I gave information at the shop.
Prisoner's Defence. I picked it up in the street, outside the door.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
