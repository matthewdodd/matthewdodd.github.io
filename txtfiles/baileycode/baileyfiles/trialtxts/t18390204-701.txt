



701. 




GEORGE JAY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
19th of January, I tea-kettle, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 



Thomas Joyce and another.






WILLIAM WOOD
. I am in the service of Thomas Joyce and another, ironmongers, in Whitechapel. On the 19th of January I was coming out of the shop, I saw the prisoner and another boy—I saw one take a teakettle, and give it to the prisoner, who was about half a yard from the door—I ran past the one that took the kettle, and took the prisoner with it under his coat, and brought him back.





WILLIAM HUTCHINGS
 (police-constable H 128.) I took the prisoner.

Prisoner. I was looking in at the shop window—a boy took it, and as soon as he took it he dropped it at my feet—I took it up.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.
