



1401. 




WILLIAM WILDING
 was indicted

 for feloniously forging, on the 
27th of February, a request for the delivery of 6 skins of leather, with intent to defraud 



Alfred Rymer.—2nd COUNT, for uttering the same, with the like intent; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years.
