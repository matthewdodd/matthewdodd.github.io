





1089. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of July
, one handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Barrowcliffe
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  BARROWCLIFFE
               



            . On 12th of July, about eleven o'clock in the morning, I was in Charles-street


, looking at the fire, a boy came up, and told me my pocket had been picked - I missed my handkerchief - He pointed the prisoner out to me, who was running; I pursued and took him; my handkerchief was found in his bosom.
EDWARD STEPHENSON. I saw the prisoner take the handkerchief out of the prosecutor's pocket, and told him; he immediately took the prisoner, and found the handkerchief in his bosom.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30.




               Transported for Life
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Graham
               



            .
