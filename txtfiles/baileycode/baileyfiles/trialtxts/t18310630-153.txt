





1341. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     RICHARDSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               embezzling the sum of 755l. 2s. 3d.
            , which he had received on account of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Phillips

               GUILTY
