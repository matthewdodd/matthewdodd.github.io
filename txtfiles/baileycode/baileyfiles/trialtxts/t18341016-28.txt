





1502. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     CLARKE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing,on the 20th of September
, 2 boots, value 8s.; and 1 coat, value 2s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  
                     Jessop 
                     Edwards
                  




               
            .
JESSOP EDWARDS. I am 
servant


 to Mr. Davison, and live in John-street, Portland Town


. I was ill, and was away for a fortnight - the prisoner took my place - these are my coat and boots - I had left the boots behind the corn-bin, and the coat in the loft.


                  ROBERT 
                  AUSTIN PYALL
               



            . I took the prisoner - I found this property and some other articles by the prisoner's side - he said he had found them all.
Prisoner's Defence. I picked them up as I was going over to Kensal-green, in the turnpike-road, between the Running Horse and the Red Lion - I went and had a pint of beer, and offered these things to some young man - he offered me a shilling for them - I said they were not worth picking up for 1s. - I would take 3s. for them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
