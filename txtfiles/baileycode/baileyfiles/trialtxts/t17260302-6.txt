







                     Elizabeth 
                     Ayres 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a Velvet Cushion, value 40 s. 1 green, and 2 blue Squabs, value 28 s.
             the Goods of Persons unknown, February 13
. It appear'd the Goods were taken out of Mr. Nesbits Meeting-House, in Hare Court, in Aldersgate-street


, and that about Mid-night, the Prisoner was stopt by the Watchman, with those Goods upon her.
