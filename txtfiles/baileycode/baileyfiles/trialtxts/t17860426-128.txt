






449. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     GREEN
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     FLETCHER
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 7th day of March
 last, seven pounds weight of coffee, value 1 l. 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Gladwin
                  




               
            .


                  Joseph
                   Hillett
               



             saw the prisoners, one take out the parcel and give to the other, and immediately stopped them with the parcel.
BOTH 

               GUILTY
            .
Each 



               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
