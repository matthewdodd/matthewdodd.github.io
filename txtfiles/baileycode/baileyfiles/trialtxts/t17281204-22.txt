







                     Edward 
                     Williams 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Plow-Coulter
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Rooks 
                  




                
            , on the 12th of November
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That he being a Farmer
, keeps a Boy to scare away the Crows, and the Boy inform'd him that the Prisoner had taken the Plow-Coulter, by which Information he pursu'd and took the Prisoner with the Coulter upon him.


                  George 
                  Fisher 
               



             the Scare Crow depos'd, That he saw the Prisoner take the Plow-Coulter from the Plow, and go away with it, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
