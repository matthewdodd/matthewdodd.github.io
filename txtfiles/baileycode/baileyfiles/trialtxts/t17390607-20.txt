Alderton. The Beginning of April I lost two Cocks, and two Hens, out of my Celler under my Shop in Covent-Garden


: I don't know who stole them.


                  John 
                  Lewis 
               



            . Wright broke open the Cellar-Door, and Jenkinson went down and kill'd two Cocks and two Hens; they three took one a-piece for their Share, and I carry'd one home to my Lodging.
Jenkinson. I can't say any Thing to it.
Glass. Did any body come by while this was doing?
Lewis. Yes; and then we shut down the Cellar-Door.
Wright. I have nothing to say about it. All
