





495. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     DYE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously having in his custody and possession a forged promissory note, made to the likeness and similitude of the notes of the Governor and Company of the Bank of England, he well knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            , aged 33.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
