





434. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     STEWART
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing on the 15th day of February
 last, one silk gown, value 15 s. one bombazeen sack, value 10 s. and various other things
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Hatfield
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was employed as a porter
 to carry these things in a sea chest, and went off with them.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   EYRE
               



            .
