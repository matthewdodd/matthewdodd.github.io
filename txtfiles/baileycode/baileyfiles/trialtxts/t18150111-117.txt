





239. 
               
                  
                     MARY
                     RICHARDSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 14th of December
, a gown, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  Elizabeth
                  Larst
               




            , widow
.


                  ELIZABETH
                  LARST
               



            . I am a widow, I live at 24, West-street, Soho


. I lost my gown on the 14th of December, I had just hanged it out in the yard to dry; I hung it up over two lines to dry in my own yard; I ran into the parlour to fetch another, and before I returned, the prisoner had stolen this.


                  RICHARD
                  HATHAWAY
               



            . I lodge in this house. On the 14th of January, I saw the prisoner, come into the yard, and take the gown off the line, she rolled it up, and made off with it.
Prosecutrix. It is my gown.



               GUILTY
            , aged 42.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
