





869. 
               
                  
                     ISAAC 
                     WISE
                  



               
             was indicted, for that 


               he, on the 30th of September
, had in his custody and possession, two forged bank-notes for the payment of 2 l. each, and a certain other forged bank-note, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
