







                     Diana 
                     Ferry  alias 
Cole




               
            , of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 10 Yards of Edging, value 40 s.
             the 28th of March
 last. The Fact was fully prov'd upon her, and her Examination before the Justice, where in she confess'd she took it out of the Prosecutors Shop, was read in Court. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s.10 d.
