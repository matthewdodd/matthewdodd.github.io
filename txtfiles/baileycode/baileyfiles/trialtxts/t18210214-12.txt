





349. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     COTTER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of January
, three pictures in metal frames, value 6 d., and one looking-glass, value 2 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas Batts 
                     Hodge
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  BATTS HODGE
               



            . I am a pewterer
, and live in Long-lane


. On the 10th of January, about seven o'clock in the evening, the prisoner and two others came in, pretendingto buy some things. I sent my children to fetch something, while their backs were turned the prisoner took these things; my boy said the prisoner had got something; I saw him drop them.


                  JOSEPH 
                  BATT
               



            . I brought the prisoner back, and saw the property found on him.
GEO. STANLEY. I am an officer. I was fetched, and found the property on him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 11.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
