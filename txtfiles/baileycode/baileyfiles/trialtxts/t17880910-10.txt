





506. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     GOSS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of July
, a cambrick handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Owen 
                     Wynne
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was seen dropping the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
