               privately stealing 23 lb. of Sugar, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Hammers 
                        




. - Vivian depos'd, that he being at the farther-end of the Shop, the Prisoner came in and took the Loaf of Sugar, and went away with it under his Arm; that he follow'd him, apprehended him, and saw him drop the Sugar. The Prisoner's taking and dropping the Sugar was confirm'd by another Evidence. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
