







                     Leonard 
                     Burton 
                  



               
             , of St. Clement's Danes


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Leather-Boots, Value 3 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Timothy 
                     Farmer 
                  




                
            , the 1st of this Instant September
.
It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Boots were hung at a Cobler's-Stall, and that the Prisoner took them, and went away with them; but being pursu'd was taken with them. The Jury found him 

               guilty
