



2238. 




FRANCES ADAMS
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
28th of September, 1 shawl, value 12s., the goods of 



Charles Mirfin and another.






CHARLES DAVIES
. I am in the employ of Mr. Charles Mirfin and another, linen-drapers, in Tottenham-court-road. This shawl was taken off a stand two or three feet inside the door, on the 28th of September—I was informed the prisoner had taken it—I went out, saw her, and took her—she had got the shawl and other articles on her.





JOHN BRIDGLAND
. I took the prisoner, and found the shawl on her.




GUILTY
. Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
