





218. 
               
                  
                     JONATHAN 
                     FORBES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously, knowingly, and without lawful excuse, having in his custody and possession, a forged and counterfeited bank note for the payment of two pounds, he knowing it to be forged and counterfeited
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded -




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr Justice Le Blanc.
