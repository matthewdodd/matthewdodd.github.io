





320. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  DANIELS
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st day of February
, two linen shirts, value 2 s. two linen waistcoats, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Thompson
                  




               
            .


                  Mary
                   Keen
               



             saw the prisoner take a shirt off the line in the prosecutor's garden, and put it in his bosom, and he was taken with it upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
