             the 3rd of July
. The Prosecutor deposed he being at St. Anns Church, laid his Hat down in the Paw , and that the Prisoner took it, got it on his Head, thrusting his own into his Bosom, and went out of the Church. The Prisoner pleaded he found the Hat on the Church-Steps. But this did not avail him, the Jury found him
