





152. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DRURY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 10th day of January
, one linen shirt, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Bullpeth
                  




               
            .


                  DAVID
                   BULLPETH
               



             sworn.
I lost my shirt at the bottom of Holborn-hill


, out of my pocket, I found the prisoner's hand in my pocket, and I run after him and caught him, and I saw him put the shirt in another man's hand.
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
I was coming down Holborn-hill a gentleman took me, I had no shirt about me.



               GUILTY
            .
To be 


               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice HEATH.
