



89. 




FREDERICK TRAVERS



, robbery on 



Robert John Moore, at 
Lambeth, and stealing from his person 1 watch, 1 chain, and 1 key, value 1l. 8s. his property: to which he





PLEADED GUILTY
. Aged 28.**
(MR. M'OUBREY, for the Prosecution, stated that this was a garotte robbery, with three others.) 


Transported for Fourteen Years.
