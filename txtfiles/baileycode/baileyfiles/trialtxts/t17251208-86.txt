







                     Edward 
                     Dorrington 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing the Second Volume of Caryl upon Job Folio; value 8 s. and the First Volume of Rushworth's Collections, Folio; value 2 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Green 
               




             . October 25
. Thomas Green thus depos'd, As I was in my Shop, and my Servant shutting it up, a Gentlewoman told me, that a Fellow had taken two Books out of my Window, and run away with them. I pursued and took him with the Books upon him. 
               
                  Edward 
                  Baxter 
               



             depos'd, that he saw the Prisoner take the Books. 

               Guilty
