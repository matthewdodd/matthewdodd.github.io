





1136. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JAMES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously having in his possession a forged Bank of England note, for the payment of 1l., knowing it to be forged
            .
To which indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 23.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
