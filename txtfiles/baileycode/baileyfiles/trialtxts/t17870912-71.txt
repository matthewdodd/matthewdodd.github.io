





741. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     QUIN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 27th of August
 last, a metal watch, value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  Henry 
                  M'Guire
               




            .
The prisoner pawned the watch soon after the prosecutor missed it.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
