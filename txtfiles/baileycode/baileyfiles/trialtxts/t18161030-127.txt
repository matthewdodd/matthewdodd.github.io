





1225. 
               
                  
                     PETER 
                     JOHNSON
                  




               
             was indicted 


               for stealing, on the 26th of September
, a shirt, value 5s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Christal
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  CHRISTAL
               



            . The prisoner was in my tap-room on the day in the indictment for about a quarter of an hour. When he left the tap-room, he stole a shirt out of the parlour.


                  FRANCIS 
                  COBLER
               



            . The prisoner sold this shirt to me.



               GUILTY
            , aged 29.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
