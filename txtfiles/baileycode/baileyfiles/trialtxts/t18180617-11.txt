






890. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BLACKMORE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of June
, one handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John William 
                           Rhodes
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  WILLIAM RHODES
               



            . On Saturday, between twelve and one o'clock, I was in Guildhall-yard


; I put my hand into my pocket, and missed my handkerchief. I turned round, and found it on the prisoner - it was tucked into the waistband of his breeches. I seized him, and took it from him.


                  CHARLES 
                  STAPLE
               



            . I am an officer. I was on duty, heard the cry, and took the prisoner in charge.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. The handkerchief laid between me and another man, on the ground.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 44.




               Transported for Life
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
