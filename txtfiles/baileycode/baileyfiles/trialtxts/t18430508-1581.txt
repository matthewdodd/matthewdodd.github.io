






1581. 




JOSEPH TUCKER
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
1st of April, 1 pair of shoes, value 7s., the goods of 



Anthony Leechley:—also, 

on the 
6th of April, 1 pair of boots, value 6s., the goods of 



John Chapman:—also, 

on the 
12th of April, 1 pair of boots, value 11s., and 1 pair of shoes, 7s.; the goods of 



Charles Heather:—also, 

on the 
15th of April, 1 pair of boots, value 9s.; 1 pair of shoes, 6s.; and 1 bag, 2s.; the goods of 



William Jones; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to all which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.
