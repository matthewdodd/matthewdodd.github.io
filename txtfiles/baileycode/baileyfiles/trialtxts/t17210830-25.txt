







                     Owen 
                     Pritchard 
                  



               
            , of Paddington


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 11 s. 2 d. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Heath 
                        




, on the 18th of July
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that the Prisoner was his Yearly Servant
, and that he missing his Money several times, at last had some markt, and put it into his Till; that missing of it, he searcht the Prisoner, and found 4 s. 6 d. markt upon him. 
               
                  William 
                  Barrow 
               



             deposed, that he markt the Money for the Prosecutor, and was present at searching of the Prisoner and saw it taken from him. The Prisoner owned the taking the Money, but said he intended to return it again. The Jury considering the matter, found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            .
