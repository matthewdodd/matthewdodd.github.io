







                     Robert

            , of St. James's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing of Knives, Scizors, Seals, and other Cutlery Ware
            , the Property of 
               
                  Conquest 
                  Jones 
               




             , the 17th of January
 last.
Conquest Jones depos'd, The Prisoner was his Servant
, and he missing Goods at several times, suspected the Prisoner, and thereupon searching his Lodgings, found several of his Goods. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
