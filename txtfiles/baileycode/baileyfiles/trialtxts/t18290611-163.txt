





First London Jury - Before Mr. Recorder.
1162. 
               
                  
                     PHILIP 
                     DIGNUM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               embezzling the sum of 13l. 9s. 6d., also for stealing goods, value 23l. 7s. 3d., 8l., and 18l. 13s. 9d.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Cook
                  




               
            , and another, his masters.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 32.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
There were three other indictments against the prisoner.
