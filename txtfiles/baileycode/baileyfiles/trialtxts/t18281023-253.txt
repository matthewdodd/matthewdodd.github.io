





2186. 
               
                  JOSEPH 
                  HILLIER
               




             was again indicted for 


               staling, on the 26th of September
, 1 watch, value 30s.; 2 waistcoats, value 10s.; 1 shirt, value 5s.; 5 handkerchiefs, value 4s.; 1 neckcloth, value 1s.; 2 pairs of stockings, value 2s.; and 1 pair of boots, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Robert 
                  Pleasant
               




            .
ROBERT PLEASANT. I am coachman
 to a gentleman at Brighton


. I lost these articles from my room at the mews the same morning; the prisoner assisted now and then at the stable; I came to town with the constable, and went to Wardour-street, where I found my property - he had my boots on; a watch and two handkerchiefs were found.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. He left them in my room.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40.




               Transported for Seven Years, from the expiration of his former sentence
            .
