





214. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  HARRIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of December
, 2 pairs of shoes, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  James 
                  Blake
               




            .


                  JAMES 
                  BLAKE
               



            . On the 3rd of December I was in my parlour in Southampton-place, Camden-town


; my wife said, "There is a man taking some shoes;" I went out, and saw the prisoner running - he dropped the shoes, and I took them up; these are them - I took him, and he said it was distress made him do it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
