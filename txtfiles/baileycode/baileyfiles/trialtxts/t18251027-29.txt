





1635. 
               
                  EDWARD 
                  KEENE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of September
, a stone jar, value 2 s., and eight quarts of wine, value 6 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Priscilla 
                  Edwards
               




            , widow
.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HEALEY
               



            . I am footman to 
               
                  Lady 
                  Knightly
               



            . On the morning of the 20th of September, she changed her residence, and the prisoner assisted in removing her goods - he was discharged in the afternoon, being drunk. I went the same evening to his lodgings with Fish, the officer, where we found this cask of gooseberry wine, which had been made by Priscilla Edwards, our housekeeper
, for her own use - we found his wife in the room.


                  JOHN 
                  FISH
               



            . I went with Healey, and found this wine.
Prisoner's Defence. I was intoxicated, and knew nothing of it till I awoke and saw it on the table.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
