




2061. 




JOSEPH KEDGE
 was indicted

 for stealing 1 railway truck-sheet, value 4l., the goods of 



Benjamin Worthy Horne and another:—

also, 1 sack, 6d.; and 171bs. weight of rope, 5s.; the goods of 



Benjamin Hicklin and another: and that he had been before convicted of felony: to all which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 28.—


Transported for Seven Years.
