







                     Mary 
                     Hawley 
                  



               
            , of St. Thomas Apostles


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 3 Carolus broad Pieces of Gold, and 5 s. in Money
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Booth 
                  




                
             the 22d of November
 last: It appear'd that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutors Landlord, and had taken the opportunity of getting into the Prosecutors Room and stole the Money, and immediately run away; She confess'd the fact before the Justice, but deny'd it at her Tryal, the Jury however found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
