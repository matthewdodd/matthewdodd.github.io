



82. 




DAVID JONES
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
9th of November, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 



Richard Thompson, from his person.






GEORGE DORRELL
. I live at Chelsea. I was in Fleet-street on the 9th of November, at half-past twelve o'clock—I saw the prisoner with another person—the prisoner had his hand in the prosecutor's pocket, and took out his handkerchief—I took hold of him.





RICHARD THOMPSON
. I live at Clapham. I was in Fleet-street, and lost this handkerchief—it is mine.





THOMAS HERDSFIELD
. I am an officer, and took the prisoner.

Prisoner's Defence. I was looking in at a picture-shop, and the gentleman came and said I had taken the handkerchief—I was carrying some things for my mistress.




GUILTY
. Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
