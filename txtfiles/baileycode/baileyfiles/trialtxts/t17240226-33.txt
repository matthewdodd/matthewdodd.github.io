







                     Martha 
                     Mountier 
                  



               
            , of St. Martins Ludgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Suit of Headclothes, &c. in the House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Beckington 
                        




, the 7th of March
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and after she had liv'd with him three Days stole the Goods and went away; the Goods were found where she had pawn'd them. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
