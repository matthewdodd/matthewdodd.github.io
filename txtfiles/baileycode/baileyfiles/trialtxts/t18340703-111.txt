






NEW COURT. Saturday, July 5, 1834.
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1003. 
               
                  ROBERT 
                  LAST
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of May

, 7 silver forks, value 4l., and 3 silver spoons, value 20s., the goods of 
                     
                        Richard 
                        Mayne
                     



                  , his master
             - 


               also on the 16th of May

, 1 silver fork, value 10s.; and 1 silver spoon, value 4s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  Dawson 
                  Mayne
               



            , to which he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 27. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
