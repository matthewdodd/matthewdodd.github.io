





283. 
               
                  
                     STEPHEN 
                     REYNOLDS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of December
, two metal weights, value 15 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Paine
                  




               
            .
MR. ALLEY conducted the prosecution.


                  WILLIAM 
                  PAINE
               



            . I am a butcher
, and live at Hounslow


. On the 5th of December, I lost two weights, and found them at Kilsby's. I took the prisoner myself, and he confessed.


                  WILLIAM 
                  KILSBY
               



            . I live at Brentford, I bought the weights of the prisoner.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 48.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
