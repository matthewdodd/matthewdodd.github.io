





569. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PHILLIPS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 30th of May
, two cushions, value 2 l. 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Crenidge
                  




               
            .




               To this indictment the prisoner pleaded
            

GUILTY, aged 40.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
