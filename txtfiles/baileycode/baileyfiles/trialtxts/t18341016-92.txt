





Third London Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1568. 
               
                  
                     JONATHAN 
                     DAKIN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of August
, 1 piece of handkerchiefs, value 24s.; 21 handkerchiefs, value 3l. 10s.; 2 shawls, value 4l. 4s.;and 26 yards of linen cloth, value 2l.; the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Waithman
                        




                     
                  , his master
            , to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 25. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
