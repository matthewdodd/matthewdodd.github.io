





368. (M.) 
               
                  Eleanor, wife
 of 
                     
                        Dennis
                         O'Brian
                     







            , was indicted for 


               stealing one copper saucepan, value 2 s. one brass saucepan, value 18 d.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Leybourne 
                  




                
            , Sept. 27
. +



                  George 
                  Leybourne 
               



            . I lost these two saucepans out of my shop. (Producing them.) I found them in the custody of 
               
                  William 
                  Urwin 
               



            .


                  William 
                  Urwin 
               



            . The prisoner brought these two saucepans to my house.
Q. Did you know her before.
Urwin. I did.
Q. What are you?
Urwin. I am a pawnbroker, she pawned them with me.
Prisoner's defence.
I was in liquor, and went to the pawnbroker's house the next day, to get the saucepans again to carry them to Mr. Leybourne's house again, but his wife was not at home, and I could not have them.



               Guilty
            .
