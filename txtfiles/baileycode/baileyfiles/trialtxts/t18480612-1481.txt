





1481. 




EDWARD BENJAMIN VIDLER



, feloniously forging an order for payment of 360l., with intent to defraud 



John Taylor and others:



alsoembezzling—44l. 18l. 1d.; the moneys of 



Edwin John Hall and another, his master; to which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 27.—


Transported for Ten Years
.
