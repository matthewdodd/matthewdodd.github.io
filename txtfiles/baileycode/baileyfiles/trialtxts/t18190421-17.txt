





517. 
               
                  
                     JANE 
                     GRIFFIN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of March
, one watch, value 30 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Fosbrook
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  FOSBROOK
               



            . I am a hostler
, and live in Prince's-court, Moor-lane


. The prisoner was employed to assist my wife to wash. I lost my watch out of my drawer, and found it in pledge at Walter's.


                  JOHN 
                  WALTER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Goswell-street. The prisoner pledged the watch with me.



                  JOHN 
                  WALTER
               



            . I am a constable. I apprehended the prisoner; she said as she had made the bed she must lay on it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was intoxicated.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 43.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
