





145. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Orton 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               ripping and stealing 45 lb. weight of lead, value 4 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           John Rogers 
                           Norton 
                        




                     
                  , Esq
; fixt to a messuage belonging to the said John
            , April 5
. +


                  Jonathan David 
                  Carter 
               



            , a watchman, stopt the prisoner in the street between 12 and 1 at night, the 5th of April, with the lead, and secured him. The lead produced.


                  Thomas 
                  King 
               



             and 
               
                  Henry 
                  Parr 
               



             deposed, they were at work on repairing two houses belonging to Esquire Norton; that the lead was a gutter to one of them.
The prisoner in his defence said, he was full of liquor, and was rambling about to get more; that three sailors met him in Bell-yard, and swore be should carry that lead for them cross Whitechapel, and the watchman came and took him.



               Guilty
            .
