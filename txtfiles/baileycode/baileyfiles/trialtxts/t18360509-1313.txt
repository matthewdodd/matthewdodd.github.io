



1313. 




FREDERICK BETHELL
 was indicted

 for stealing on the 
12th of May. 1 pair of trowsers, value 18s., the goods of 



Edward Phillips Doudney and another.






EDWARD DAVIES
. I am shopman to Edward Phillips Doudney and Son, who live in Lombard-street, On the 12th of May a gentleman came into the shop and said that the prisoner had taken these trowsers—I ran and took him with them not twenty yards off—these are the trowsers—they were hanging at the door—the prisoner has been begging about as some time past—I watched him not a week ago.




GUILTY
.* Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years.
