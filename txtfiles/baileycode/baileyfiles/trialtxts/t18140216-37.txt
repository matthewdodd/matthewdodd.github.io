





223. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     ENGLAND
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that she, on the 22nd of September, without lawful excuse had in her custody and possession two forged 1 l. bank notes, she knowing them to be forged
            ; and
ANOTHER COUNT, that she had one other 1 l. bank note, she knowing it to be forged.
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
