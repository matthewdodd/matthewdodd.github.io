                     Edward

            , of St. Ann's Westminster





, were indicted 


               for feloniously stealing divers wearing Apparel, in the Dwelling-House of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Williams 
                        




, the 2d of this Instant Jan.




                  Katharine 
                  Williams 
               



             depos'd, That the Prisoner Gill had brought her a Letter two Days before, for her to go to a certain Place to fetch some Materials for Apparel, that were to be made in haste (she being a Mantua-maker) but she not knowing the Person, did not go, and that after he was apprehended, he did own, that he had carried her a Letter, and that if she had gone out, the Robbery was to be committed at that time by the rest of the Prisoners.


                  Elizabeth 
                  Savil 
               



             depos'd, That she had taken notice of all the Prisoners lurking about near the Prosecutor's for three or four Days before the Robbery.


                  Ann 
                  Goddard 
               



             depos'd, That she lodging in the same House with the Prosecutor, saw Carrol coming down Stairs with the Prosecutor's Goods, and stopping him, caus'd him to be apprehended.


                  John 
                  Savil 
               



             depos'd, That the other three Prisoners were apprehended, by means of the Impeachment of Carrol, upon which Carrol pleaded, he was by the Justice admitted as an Evidence, which appear'd to be so; but at finding the Bill, Carrol being put in the Indictment, was oblig'd to stand the Trial, and the Fact being plainly prov'd upon him, by his own Confession, as well as the Evidence of 
               
                  Anne 
                  Goddard 
               



             , the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 39 s.
             but there not being sufficient Evidence against any of the other three, they were 

               acquitted
            .
