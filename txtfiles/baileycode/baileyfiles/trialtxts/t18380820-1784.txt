



1784. 




SAMUEL HORNER
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
12th of July, 1 coat, value 3l., the goods of 



Samuel Grindsell.-2nd COUNT, stating it to be the goods of 



Richard Laws.






RICHARD LAWS
. I am a coachman to Mr. Samuel Grindsell. About nine or ten o'clock on the 12th of July, I was returning with carriage to the stable in Dunning-alley, Bishopsgate-street—I had a coat of my master's on the box—I went to the stable for two or three minutes, and when I came out the coat was gone—this is it—(looking at it.)





HENRY ROUND
. I was in Dunning-alley about nine or ten o'clock that evening—I saw the prisoner take the coat off the box, and ran away with it—I stopped him, and took hold of him till the witness came and took him.





PETER CHISELL
. I was called and saw the prisoner with the coat, in possession of Round.

Prisoner. I was coming through the alley—I saw the cost by the side of the chaise, and took it up.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.
