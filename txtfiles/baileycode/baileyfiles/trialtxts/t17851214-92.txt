





92. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     FOXHALL
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 21st day of October
 last, fourteen china plates, value 7 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Dixon
                  




               
            .


                  John
                   Wyatt
               



             saw another boy hand them to the prisoner, and he was taken with them upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
