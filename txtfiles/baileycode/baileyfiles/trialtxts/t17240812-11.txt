







                     Elizabeth 
                     Candy 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Goods, in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Higginson 
                        




, the 29th of June
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor. She confessed the Fact before the Justice, which Confession was read in Court. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
