





320. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Michael 
                     Curry 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing forty pound weight of cherries, value 18 s. and one wicker sieve
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Garret 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Isaac 
                     Goodman 
                  



                
            , July 18
. ++


                  John 
                  Garret 
               



            . The cherries belong to a gentleman in Kent, but were left in the care of me and 
               
                  Isaac 
                  Goodman 
               



            , who are watchmen in the Fleet-Market

; and what is lost we are obliged to answer for. I saw the prisoner take the cherries mention'd in the indictment, and when he found I saw him he drop'd them down.


                  Isaac 
                  Goodman 
               



            . I went to assist Garret, and we secured the prisoner.
Q. Did you see the prisoner take the cherries?
Goodman. No, I did not. I saw them after he had thrown them down.
Prisoner's Defence.
I know nothing of the cherries. I was carrying a load from the water side, there was a great crowd of us all together; this man has ow'd me a spight these three years, and this is all spight.
He call'd 
               
                  Bridget 
                  Rion 
               



            , who had known him ever since he was born; 
               
                  Nicholas 
                  Branagan 
               



            , a year; and 
               
                  John 
                  Fitzpatrick 
               



            , above half a year; who all gave him a good character.



               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
