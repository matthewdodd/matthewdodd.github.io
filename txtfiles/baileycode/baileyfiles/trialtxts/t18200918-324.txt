





1124. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     WELCH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of September
, one rug, value 5 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Davis
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  DAVIS
               



            . I am pass-master for the County of Middlesex
, and live in Vine-street, Liquorpond-street. In the morning, as the waggon was loading to go to Bristol, my man insisted on searching the prisoner's bag, we were taking him to Bristol. The rug was found in it.


                  GEORGE 
                  BROWN
               



            . I suspected the paupers. I missed a rug, and found it in the prisoner's bag.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. A countryman told me to take it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 39.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before J. Vaillant, Esq.
