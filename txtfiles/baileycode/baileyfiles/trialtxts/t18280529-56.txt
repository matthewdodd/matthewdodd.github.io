






NEW COURT, (1st DAY.)
Fourth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1132. 
               
                  ANN 
                  SHUTTLEWORTH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of April
, 3 sheets, value 15s.; 1 table-cloth, value 5s.; 3 gowns, value 9s.; 10 napkins, value 5s.; 3 shifts, value 9s.; 1 shirt, value 3s., and 1 book, value 1s., the goods of 
                     
                        James 
                        Stallard
                     




                  , her master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
