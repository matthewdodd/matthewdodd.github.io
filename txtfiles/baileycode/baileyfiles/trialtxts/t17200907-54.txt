







                     John 
                     Fuller 
                  



               
            , of Stanmore


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Knife and Fork value 8 d. and 22 s. in Money
            , the Goods and Money of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Smith 
                  




                
            , on the first of October
 last. It appeared that the Prosecutor and the Prisoner lay together, and that the Prisoner took his Breeches from under his Head, and the Goods and Money out of his Pocket about three in the Morning and went away, leaving the Breeches only at the Feet of the Bed. The Jury found him 

               Guilty
