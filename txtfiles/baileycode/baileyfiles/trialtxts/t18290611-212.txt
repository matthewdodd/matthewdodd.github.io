





NEW COURT, Fifth Day.
First Middlesex Jury - Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1211. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PAGE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of June
, 61 handkerchiefs, value 8l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Venables
                  



               
             and 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Venables
               



            , his masters.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
