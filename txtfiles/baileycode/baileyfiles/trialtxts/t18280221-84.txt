






566. 
               
                  DANIEL 
                  NOWLAND
               




             and 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  BATES
               




             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of February
, 1 pair of boots, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Sadd
               




            .
NOWLAND 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 10.





               Whipped
             and Discharged.
BATES 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
