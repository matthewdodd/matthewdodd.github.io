







                     Samuel 
                     Martin 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Clement Danes


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Cloth Coat, val. 20 s. a Wastcoat, val. 16 s. a pair of Breeches, val. 10 s. a Drugget Coat val. 4 s. and a Shirt, value 6 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Rug 
                        



                      
                  ; and a Coat, value 5 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Huddlestone 
                        



                      
                  ; in the House of 
                     
                        Sarah 
                        Roche 
                     



, Widow.


                  John 
                  Rug 
               



             thus depos'd. The Prisoner was a Lodger with me, in Mrs. Roche's House about a Week. He went away early next Morning, and soon after I found that the Cloths were taken out of my Trunk. I took him the same Day, he confest that he had pawn'd them to 
               
                  Tho 
                  Rudge 
               



             in Houns-ditch


, and there I found them. 


               Guilty to the Value of 39 s.
