





470. 
               
                  HENRY 
                  BROWN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of December
, 1 great coat, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Hall
               




            .
THOMAS HALL. On the morning of the 24th of December, about ten minutes to seven o'clock, I took my luggage to the door of the Flower Pot, Bishopsgate-street


 - I stopped to speak to the guard of the Telegraph coach, and my coat was gone.


                  WILLIAM 
                  LARKE
               



            . I was with Mr. Hall, assisting him with his luggage; his great coat was by the door - I saw the prisoner come and take it up; he and another, who was with him, walked on - I followed them, and heard his companion say, "Drop it! Drop it!" he did so - I took it up, and secured him.


                  WILLIAM 
                  RIXLEY
               



            . I am an officer, and took him with the coat.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
