





648. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     COLLEGE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 30th day of June
 last, one cornelian seal set in gold, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Worboys
                  




               
            .


                  Robert
                   Sanders
               



             saw the prisoner come out of the prosecutor's shop, and put something on the groove, which another man came and took away; and the prosecutor missed a gold seal which he gave the prisoner to look at.
The prisoner called five witnesses to his character.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
