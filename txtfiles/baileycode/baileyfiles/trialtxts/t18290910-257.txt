





1788. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     BROWNE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of July
, 1 trunk, value 12s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Adam 
                     Margatroyd
                  




               
            .


                  ADAM 
                  MARGATROYD
               



            . I am a trunk-maker
, and live at Knightsbridge


. I lost a trunk from my door on the 28th of July; I followed the prisoner, and took her with it, about two hundred yards off.


                  CHARLES 
                  THOMPSON
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the trunk from the door, and gave information - she was taken.
The prisoner pleaded poverty.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 35.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
