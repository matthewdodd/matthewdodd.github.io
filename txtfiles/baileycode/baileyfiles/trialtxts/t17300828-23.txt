







                     Francis 
                     Dean 
                  



               
            , of St. Paul's Covent-Garden


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing seven Guineas, and three half Guineas, one Quarter Moidore, and other Money, of 
                     
                        Joseph 
                        Hoar 
                     



                  , in the Dwelling-House
             of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Keilly 
                  




                
             , the 29th of June
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, The Prisoner was employ'd as a Servant, he keeping a Night-Cellar, and that he stole the Money out of a Cupboard, and went away; that he pursuing him, found him at a Place call'd Morgan in Kent, where he found most of the Money upon him. The Prisoner having nothing to say in his Defence, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 39 s.
