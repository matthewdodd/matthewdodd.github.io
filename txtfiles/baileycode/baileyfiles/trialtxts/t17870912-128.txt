





798. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     NUGENT
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th day of September
, two shillings and six-pence, and one hundred and eight copper halfpence, value 4 s. 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Barham
                  




               
            .


                  William
                   Rogers
               



             saw the prisoner coming out of the shop with the money and the till.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
