








                     Elizabeth 
                     Francis 
                  



                
            , and 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Clark 
                  



                
            , of the Parish of St. James Westminster



, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing leather Shoes
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Frazier 
                  




                
            , the 12th of July
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Shoes were taken out of the Prosecutor's Stall, and the Prisoners were apprehended between 2 and 3 a Clock in the Morning by the Watchmen. The Prisoners pleaded, that they were sent for a Midwife, about 3 or 4 a Clock in the Morning, and found the Shoes in a Sack. The Jury found them both 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            




               Transportation
            .
