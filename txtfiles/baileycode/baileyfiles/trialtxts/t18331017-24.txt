





1428. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     KENNEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 5th of September
, 1 coat, value 1l.; 1 waistcoat, value 7s.; and 1 pair of trousers, value 1l.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  
                     Catherine 
                     Foxwell
                  




               
            , to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 21 - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
