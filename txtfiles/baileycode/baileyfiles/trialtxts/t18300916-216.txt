





1680. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     BETTS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of August
, 1 gown, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Burgess
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  BURGESS
               



            . I am a pawnbroker
, and live in Old-street-road


. I lost a gown on the 25th of August - I had seen it about two hours before I missed it, which was about two o'clock; in the course of the afternoon a pawnbroker, who lives near, came and asked if I had lost a gown - this is it.


                  HENRY 
                  BARRON
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. The prisoner came on the 25th of August, and offered this gown in pawn; I gave her into custody, and took it to Burgess.


                  JOHN 
                  McWILLIAMS
               



            . I took the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence. It laid at the door, and I took it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
