







                     John 
                     Anderson 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 29 Deal Boards, value 25 s. and 6000 Nails, value 4 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Bond 
                  




                
            , the 15th of September
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Journeyman
 to the Prosecutor, and the Boards which the latter swore to be his, were found in the Prisoner's Possession.
The Prisoner call'd several Persons who had known him for 12 or 14 Years, who gave him a very honest Character; but the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               Guilty
             of the Indictment.
