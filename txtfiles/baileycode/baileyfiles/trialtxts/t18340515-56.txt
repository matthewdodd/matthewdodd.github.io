





794. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     HUBBACK
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of April
, 1 saw, value 4s.; the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Edmund 
                           Bailey
                        



                     
                  ; 1 hammer, value 1s.; and 1 trowel, value 1s. 6d.; the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Robert 
                           Morris
                        



                     
                  ; 1 trowel, value 1s. 6d.; the goods of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Harris
                        



                     
                  ; and 1 trowel, value 1s. 6d.; the goods of 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           Martin
                        



.


                  JAMES 
                  COOK
               



            . I am a Thames police-constable. On the 26th of April, I took the prisoner - I found on him five duplicates, which led me to some pawnbroker's, where I found these articles.


                  THOMAS 
                  BOWSTED GAMSON
               



            . I am a pawnbroker at Bow. The prisoner pawned this saw at our shop.


                  RICHARD 
                  CRAWLEY
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a hammer and trowel, which were pawned by the prisoner.


                  ROBERT 
                  MORRIS
               



            . I am a plasterer
. This hammer and trowel are mine - I lost them from the East India Dock Tavern


, where I was at work on the 25th of April - I do not know the prisoner.


                  EDMUND 
                  BAILEY
               



            . I am a carpenter
. This saw is mine - I lost it on the 24th of April, from the East India Dock Tavern, while I went to breakfast.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
