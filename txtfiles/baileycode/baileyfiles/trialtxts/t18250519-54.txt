





911. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  DUNWORTH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of April
, forty-two yards of printed cotton, value 42 s. 6 d., the goods of 
                     
                        Samuel William 
                        Cousens
                     




                  , in his dwelling-house
            .
SAMUEL WILLIAM COUSENS. I am a linen-draper
, and live in Norton-falgate


. On the 20th of April, about half-past seven o'clock in the evening, some ladies in the shop gave me information - I ran out, and when I got a few yards from the shop I saw the prisoner nearly one hundred yards off, going from the house; I hallooed Stop thief! a man collared him, and he immediately threw down these prints, which had been on a pile within the door - they were picked up.
(Property produced and sworn to.)




               GUILTY. Aged 17. Of stealing to the value of 39 s. only
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
