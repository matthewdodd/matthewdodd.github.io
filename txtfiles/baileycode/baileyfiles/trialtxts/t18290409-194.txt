





873. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MCNAMARA
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of March
, I shovel, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Ivers
                        



                     
                  ; I shovel, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Ivers
                        



                     
                  ; and 1 jacket, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Southcott
                        



.


                  JOHN 
                  IVERS
               



            . I am a labourer
. I lost a shovel on the 5th of March, from Battle-bridge


, where I was at work for Mr. Southcott - this is it.


                  GEORGE 
                  WADDINGTON
               



            . I am an officer. On the 5th of March I was in the New-road, and saw the prisoner with these two shovels and this jacket; I took him to the office - he asked to go backwards, and put this jacket down the privy.


                  THOMAS 
                  SOUTHCOTT
               



            . This jacket is mine; I lost it from Mr. Jackson's yard, on the 5th of March - I never saw the prisoner.
The prisoner pleaded poverty.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 27.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
