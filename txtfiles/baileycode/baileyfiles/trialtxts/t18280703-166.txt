





1514. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  PALMER
               



             was indicted 


               for stealing, on the 9th of March
, 1 live pig, price 30s., the property of 
                     
                        Daniel 
                        Winch
                     



                  ; also, for stealing, on the 9th of March, 2 live pigs, price 5l.
            , the property of 
               
                  Eleanor 
                  Cock
               



            .
To which indictments the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years upon each indictment
            .
