





NEW COURT. Tuesday, September 10, 1833.
Fourt Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1369. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JUDE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of July
, 1 cart, value 3l. the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Dickenson
                        




                     
                  ; and that he had been before convicted of felony
             - To which he pleaded.




               GUILTY
            . 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
