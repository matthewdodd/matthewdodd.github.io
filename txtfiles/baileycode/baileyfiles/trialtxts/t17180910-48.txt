







                     Thomas 
                     Saul 
                  



               
             , of St. Buttolphs Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 8 Bushels of Meal, value 30 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Elisha 
                     Collet 
                  




                
            , the 12th of August
 last. The Evidence deposed that the Goods being Landed on the Wharf of 
               
                  Elizabeth 
                  Fletcher 
               



            , the Prisoner came about One a Clock at Night and was carrying them off, to put into his Boat but being discovered was pursued and taken. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, but it being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Felony. 


               Transportation
            .
