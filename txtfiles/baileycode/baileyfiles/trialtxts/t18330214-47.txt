





444. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     SIMPSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of January
, 1 ham, value 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Somers
                        




                     
                  ; and that he had been before convicted
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  ELSAM
               



            . I am in the employ of Mr. 
               
                  James 
                  Somers
               



            : he lives at the corner of North Audley-street


 - Ihad hung this ham at his door on the 16th of July; I heard an alarm, went to the door, and took the prisoner with it.


                  JOHN 
                  CRAWLEY LITTLE
               



             (Police-constable D 74). I took the prisoner with this ham.


                  GEORGE 
                  ELLIS
               



             (Police-constable C 66). I produce a certificate from Mr. Clark's office, of the conviction of the prisoner - he was whipped and discharged; I know he is the same person.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
