





77. 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Robinson 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing some thin Cheese
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Isaac 
                     Ogden 
                  




                
             of Whitechapel, on the 16th of December
.


                  Isaac 
                  Ogden 
               



            . I missing my Cheese and hearing some Noise in the Street, went out and took the Cheeses upon the Prisoner. He confess'd the Fact. I carried him before Justice Quarril. He confess'd he took the Cheese from the Bulk, and handed it down to the other Boy.
The Prisoner had nothing to say for himself, neither had he any Witnesses. 


               Guilty 10 d.
