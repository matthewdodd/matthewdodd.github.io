





491. 
               
                  
                     LUCY 
                     COOPER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 15th of February
, a bed, value 1 l. a bolster, value 6 s. a pair of sheets, value 5 s. a blanket, value 1 s. a tea-kettle, value 1 s. a looking-glass, value 1 s. and a flat iron, value 9 d.
             the property of 
               
                  Sarah 
                  Goodman
               




            .


                  SARAH 
                  GOODMAN
               



            . I live in Hatfield-street


. I let lodgings
. On the 13th of February, I let the prisoner a one pair of stairs back room, furnished; she was to pay me five shillings a week. She came into it on the 13th, and on the 15th she went away. After she was gone, I went into her room, and missed all the things contained in the indictment. She contrived to put the things out of the window.
- PRINCE. I am an officer. I took the prisoner in custody for another robbery. I found Mrs. Goodman's bed at Mr. Tuck's, in Whitecross-street.
MR. TUCK. I am a broker. My wife bought the bed. This is the bed.
Prosecutrix. It is mine.



               GUILTY
            , aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
