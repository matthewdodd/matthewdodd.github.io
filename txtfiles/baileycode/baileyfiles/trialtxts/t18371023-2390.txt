



2390. 




CHARLES MITTON
 was indicted

 for embezzlement.






JOHN ALDRIDGE
. I live in Union-yard, Shoreditch, and am a turner. The prisoner was in my service as errand-boy—on the 16th of October I sent him to Hoxton to receive 5s. of Mr. Harbridge—I had before sent him for money—it was his duty to pay me directly—he has never paid me—he left, and came back no more.





HENRY HARTRIDGE
. I live in Gloucester-street, and am a cabinet-maker. I paid the prisoner 5s. on the 16th of October, for his master.




GUILTY
. † Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.
