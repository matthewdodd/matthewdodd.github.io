



1810. 




EDWARD ATKINSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
3d of August, 1 handkerchief, value 1s., the goods of 



William Daniel Turner, from his person.






WILLIAM DANIEL TURNER
. I live in Devonshire-court, Temple. About eight o'clock on the evening of the 3d of August, I was looking into a bookseller's shop; I felt a slight touch; I turned, and my handkerchief was gone—I saw the prisoner near me—I took him, and he dropped it from his bosom—it is mine.

Prisoner. I picked it up. Witness. No; he could not have done it.




GUILTY
. Aged 14.—


Transported for Seven Years.
