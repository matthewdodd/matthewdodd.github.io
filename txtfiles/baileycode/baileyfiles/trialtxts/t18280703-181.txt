





1529. 
               
                  JOSEPH 
                  WEAVER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of June
, 18ozs. of silver, value 4l. 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Augustus 
                  Brown
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
