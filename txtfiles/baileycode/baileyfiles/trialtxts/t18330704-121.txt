





1143. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MARNEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of May
, 1 pair of boots, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Morley
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  MORLEY
               



            . I live in Bishopsgate-street


. On the 22nd of May, about nine o'clock, I was in my shop walking towards the back of it, I fancied I heard something taken from the door; I came to the door, and saw the prisoner going away; I followed him, and caught him three doors off with these boots on him; I brought him back, and gave him into custody - these are my boots.
Prisoner's Defence. I was walking along, and at the end of his window I saw them against the door, I took them up, and was looking at them; I walked slowly.



               GUILTY
            .* Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
