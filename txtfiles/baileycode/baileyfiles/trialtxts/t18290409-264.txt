





943. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     BURN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Parton
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  PARTON
               



            . I am an attorney
. I was in Charlotte-street, Fitzroy-square


, on the 3d of April, and was informed the prisoner had picked my pocket; I pursued him to a court in Goodge-street, and found my handkerchief on him - I took him to the office, and he was committed.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
