






85. 
               
                  
                     BENJAMIN 
                     INGRAHAM
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 8th day of November
 last, one linen handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Timothy 
                     Brown
                  




               
            .
The Prosecutor saw the prisoner take his handkerchief, and seized him directly.




               GUILTY, 10 d.
            





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. Justice WILLES.
