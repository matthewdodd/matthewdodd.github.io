







                     John 
                     Jackson
                  



               
             a poor Fellow, was indicted for 


               Robbing a certain Ship called the Diligence, Riding at Anchor in the River of Thames


, on the 8th. of April last, 1688. taking therefrom Ninety yards of Serge, value 12 l. and Two hundred thousand of Pins, value 3 l.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Wake
                  




               
            . The Evidence against the Prisoner deposed, that he got into the Ship by the help of some Ropes about her, and carried away the Goods. The Prisoner said that he knew nothing of it, and had no Witnesses on his side, so he was found 

               guilty
             of Felony.
