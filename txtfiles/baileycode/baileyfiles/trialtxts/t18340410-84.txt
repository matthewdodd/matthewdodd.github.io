





607. 
               
                  ALFRED 
                  CONNEW
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of March
, 1 pair of shoes, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Penn
               




             and another.


                  WILLIAM 
                  COOK
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. John Penn and another, of Holborn-bars


. They sell shoes
. On the 26th of March, I heard some person had taken a pair of shoes - I ran out, and saw the prisoner - I asked where the shoes were - he said he knew nothing of them - I laid hold of him - he had these shoes in his apron - they are my master's.
Prisoner's Defence. I was going down Brook-street - two boys met me, and told me to put these shoes into my pocket, and go with them to Smithfield.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
