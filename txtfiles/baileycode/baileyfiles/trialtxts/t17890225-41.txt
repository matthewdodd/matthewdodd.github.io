






222. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JONES
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     LAWRENCE 
                     WYNN
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st day of January
, a loaf of sugar, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Catherine 
                     Hewer
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Floodgate
                  



               
            .


                  James
                   Hicks
               



             saw the prisoner Wynn take the loaf of sugar and give it to Jones.
BOTH 

               GUILTY
            .





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice GROSE.
