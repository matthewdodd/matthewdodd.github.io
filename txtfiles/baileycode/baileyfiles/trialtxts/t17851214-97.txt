





97. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     ATKINSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 31st of October
 last, one japanned iron tray, value 2 s. 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Powis
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the tray upon him, by 
               
                  Daniel
                   Wilmot
               



             who saw him take the tray.



               GUILTY
            .
To be 


               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
