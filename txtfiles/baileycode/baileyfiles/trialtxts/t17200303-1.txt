







                     William 
                     Witherel 
                  



               
            , of St. Michael's Cornhill


, was indicted for 


               Privately stealing a Cambrick Handker-chief from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Holloway 
                        




, on the 19th of February
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that about 8 a Clock the Night aforesaid the Prisoner pickt his Pocket of his Handkerchief near Finch-Lane


, that he followed and took him as he was crossing the way, and the Handkerchief by him; that no Body else was near him. The Prisoner denyed the Fact, but called none to his Reputation. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
