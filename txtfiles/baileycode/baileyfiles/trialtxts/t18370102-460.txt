



460. 




MICHAEL ROACH
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
21st of December, 1 pair of boots, value 4s. 6d. the goods of 



Robert Summers.






CHARLES SMITH
. I am apprentice to Mr. Robert Summers, a pawn—I broker, of Bath-street, City-road. On the 21st of December, about four o'clock, I saw the prisoner cut something down from the door—I followed tad took him, I found a boot in his possession, and a knife in his hand—he was walking away—I gave him to a policeman who was passing"—a pair of boots had been cut from the door.

JURY. Q. Was the pair hanging there? A. Yes, on one string—I found only this one on him—I thought he had given the other to his companions.




GUILTY
—Aged 13.—


Transported for Seven Years.
