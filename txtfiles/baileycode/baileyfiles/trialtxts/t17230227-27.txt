







                     Roger 
                     Stephens 
                  



               
            , of St. Martins in the Field


, was indicted for 


               stealing two Coach Glasses, value 40 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Drelincourt 
                  




                
            , the 18th of October
 last. The Evidence depos'd, That the Glasses were stolen, and taken upon the Prisoner. The Prisoner pleaded the Glasses were given him by 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Pierce 
               



            , the Lady 
               
                  Philippa 
                  Walton 
               



            's Coachman, to sell. The Coachman deny'd it upon Oath, and he having been found in several Stories, the Jury found him
