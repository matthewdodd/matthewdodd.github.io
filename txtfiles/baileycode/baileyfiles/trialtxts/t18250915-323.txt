





1548. 
               
                  BENJAMIN 
                  HUGHES
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of August
, a handkerchief, value 5 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Ann 
                  Garton
               



             and 
               
                  Eliza 
                  Garton
               



            .


                  JOHN 
                  FAREY
               



            . I am in the employ of Ann and Eliza Garton - they are hosiers

, and live in Cheapside


; this handkerchief is their property. On the 10th of August, it laid on the right hand counter, where we expose goods for sale. I did not miss it, till the officer brought in the prisoner about twelve o'clock.


                  DANIEL 
                  FORRESTER
               



            . On Wednesday, the 10th of August, I was going past the shop with a prisoner, and saw this lad come from the door with something yellow under his jacket; I went into the shop and said to the young man, "Have you sold a yellow handkerchief;" he said, No; I said, "If you will take care of my prisoner, I will go and see if I can get it;" I went to Bow-lane, and took the prisoner with this handkerchief under his coat.
Prisoner's Defence. I was coming from Mitcham, and saw the handkerchief lying on the ground; I picked it up - the constable came and took me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
