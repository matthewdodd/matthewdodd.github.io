







                     Samuel 
                     Hobson 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Martin Ludgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing half an Ounce of Human Hair, value 10s. and half an Ounce of Horse Hair, value 2 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Launder 
                  




                
            , the 6th of April
. The Evidence depos'd, that the Prisoner was Journey-Man
 to the Prosecutor, and took the Hair and Sold it. His Confession before Sir 
               
                  John 
                  Fryer 
               



             was read in Court, wherein he had own'd the Fact. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
