







                  Elisabeth 
                  Lee
               



             was Tryed for 


               Robbing one 
                     
                        John 
                        Veale
                     




                   in the King's High-way, a Child
 about the Age of five Years, in Stepney Parish


, on the 13th of May
 of a Rasey Stuff Coat, value 5 s.
             The Child happening to straggle abroad alone into the Fields, the Prisoner met him, and stript him; and one who went to seek the Child, met the Prisoner, and saw the Child's Coat under her Apron, which she knew very well; The Fact was plainly proved, so she was found 

               Guilty
            .
