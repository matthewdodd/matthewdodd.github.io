





1261. 
               
                  ELIZABETH 
                  DISNEY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of April
, 28 yards of cotton, value 28s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Isaac Thomas 
                  Welchman
               




            .


                  URIAH 
                  JOYCE
               



            . I am in the employ of Isaac Thomas Welchman, a linen-draper
, of Battle-bridge


. On the 23d of April a person came in and said a woman had taken a piece of print from the door - I followed the prisoner some distance, and brought her back with it under her cloak.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  WILLIAM 
                  COLTON
               



            . I am an officer, and took her.
The prisoner pleaded distress, and stated that she was intoxicated at the time the offence was committed.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 37.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
