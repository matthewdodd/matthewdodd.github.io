







                     Mary 
                     Clark 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silk Gown and Petticoat, value 15 s. and other wearing Apparel
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Griffith 
                  




                
            , the 27th of May
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner was with a Person that lodg'd in the same House with her; that she went out about Two o'Clock in the Afternoon, and coming Home about Eight at Night, she found her Door open, and her Cloaths to have been stolen, and that she found the Goods where the Prisoner had pawn'd them; the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
