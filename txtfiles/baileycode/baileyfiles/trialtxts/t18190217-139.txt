





452. 
               
                  
                     PATRICK 
                     DABBS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of January
, two handkerchiefs, value 3 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Wilson
                  




               
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  WILSON
               



            . I am the son of 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Wilson
               



            , who is a hosier
, and lives in Shoreditch. On the 18th of January, about a quarter past ten o'clock at night, the prisoner came to sell some printing letters, I refused to buy any. I turned round, and saw him drawing his hand off the counter, where the handkerchiefs lay. Jones stopped him, and took them out of his bosom.


                  EVAN 
                  JONES
               



            . I am in the prosecutor's service. The prisoner came in to sell some letters as I was locking up. As he went out I stopped him, and found two handkerchiefs in his bosom.
(Property produced and sworn to. )
Prisoner's Defence. I took them up to look at. He took them out of my hand.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 54.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
