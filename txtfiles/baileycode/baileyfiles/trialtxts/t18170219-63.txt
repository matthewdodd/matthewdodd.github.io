





425. 
               
                  CHARLOTTE 
                  HARRISON
               




             was indicted for 


               having in her possession a forged Bank of England note, she well knowing it to be forged
            .
To which indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Burrough.
