





257. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Brown 
                  



               
            , spinster
, was indicted for 


               stealing a stuff petticoat, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Hanson 
                  




                
            , March 29
. +


                  Charles 
                  Hanson 
               



            . I am a stay-maker
, the prisoner was at work for me; we missed my wife's petticoat out of a box; on the 29th of March the prisoner absconded, we did not find her till last Friday; she confessed she had taken it, and sold it to 
               
                  Elizabeth 
                  Hopcraft 
               



            , where we found it, (produced in court.)


                  Elizabeth 
                  Hopcraft 
               



            . I keep a clothes-shop; the prisoner brought the petticoat to me on the 4th of April; I bought it of her for 12 s.


                  Elizabeth 
                  Hanson 
               



            . I am wife to the prosecutor; this is my petticoat; the prisoner absconded at the time it was lost; I heard her own she took it.
Prisoner's defence.
I was ill was the reason I staid away; I was obliged to make away with some of my own things.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
