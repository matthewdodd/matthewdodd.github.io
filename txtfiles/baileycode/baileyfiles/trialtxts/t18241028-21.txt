





1274. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     HAMILTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of October
, a watch, value 2 l., and two seals,value 50 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joshua 
                     West
                  




               
             and others, his partners.
The prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.
