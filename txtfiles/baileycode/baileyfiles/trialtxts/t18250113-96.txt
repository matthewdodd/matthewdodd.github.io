





London Cases, Second Jury, Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
277. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  BOOTH
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of January
, 4 1/2 lbs. of opium, value 5 l. 12 s. 6 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Butler
               




            , his master.
The prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
