





496. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of January
, 1 pair of trousers, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     King
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  KING
               



            . I keep a sale-shop
, in St. Marylebone-street


. On the night of the 5th of January I was in the back of my shop, and saw a person's hand inside my shop door; it had hold of one of the legs of a pair of trousers, which were hanging inside my shop - I saw them pulled down; I went to the door, and saw no one near but the prisoner; I followed, and took hold of him - I found under his arm the trousers; he said, "I am very glad you have taken me; I am very much distressed, and wished to be transported."


                  JOHN 
                  HARDY
               



            . I am a Policeman. I took the prisoner- he said he was glad he was taken, and wanted to be transported.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
