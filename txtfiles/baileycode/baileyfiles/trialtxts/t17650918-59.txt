





439. (M.) 
               
                  Ann, wife
 of 
                     
                        James 
                        Swift 
                     



                   otherwise 
Ann May




            , spinster
, was indicted for 


               stealing two cotton bed curtains, value 2 l. 10 s. and three crimson moreen bed curtains
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Leonard 
                     Jennings 
                  




                
            , August 29
. *


                  Leonard 
                  Jennings 
               



            . I am an upholsterer
: the prisoner was employed by me to make up furniture; I missed two cotton bed curtains, and three crimson moreen curtains; I charged the prisoner, she confessed she had taken them, and directed me to the pawnbroker's where she had pawned them. (Produced in court and deposed to).


                  Thomas 
                  Jones 
               



            , servant to Mr. Fell, a pawnbroker, deposed, the prisoner pledged the two cotton curtains, and two of the red ones to him. And 
               
                  Thomas
                   Nash


            , servant to 
               
                  Richard 
                  Monk 
               



            , a pawnbroker, deposed the prisoner pledged one red curtain with him.
Prisoner's Defence.
I was in hopes to have got money to have fetched them again.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
