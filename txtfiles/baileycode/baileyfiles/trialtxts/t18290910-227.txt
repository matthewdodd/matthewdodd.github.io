





1758. 
               
                  
                     ALEXANDER 
                     GUYON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of July
, 11 handkerchiefs, value 27s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Plant
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
