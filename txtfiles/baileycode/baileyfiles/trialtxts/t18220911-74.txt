





1218. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     NEWBY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously exposing for sale certain silver plate, the same having forged and counterfeit stamps thereon, he well knowing the same to be forged
            , with intent to defraud the worshipful 
               
Company of Goldsmiths




            .
The prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Before Mr. Justice Best.
