





1231. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     GUEST
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of July
, one basket, value 6 d., and twelve ounces of butter, value 6 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Dougan
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  BAYLESS
               



            . I am a watchman, at Islington. On the 21st of July, at twenty minutes past four o'clock in the morning, I was on duty in the back road; a boy gave me information, I went down Dougan's area, and found the prisoner with this basket, containing butter and candles - I secured him.


                  MARY 
                  RUSSELL
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  John 
                  Dougan
               



            , who lives at No. 6, Barnsby-place, Islington


. The basket, butter, and candles, are his, and were safe on a shelf in the area when I went to bed.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before W. Arabin, Esq.
