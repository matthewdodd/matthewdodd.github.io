





574. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WATKINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of February
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Abraham 
                           Duffell
                        




                     
                  , from his person; and that he had been before convicted of felony
            .


                  ABRAHAM 
                  DUFFELL
               



            . I am a cheesemonger
, and live at No. 128, Edgware-road. Some time after nine o'clock in the evening of the 3rd of February, I was at the bottom of Holborn-hill


 - I felt a snatch at my pocket; I turned, and missed my handkerchief - I saw the prisoner, searched him, and found my handkerchief on his person; it has my mark on it.


                  THOMAS 
                  LONG
               



            . I am an officer. I received this handkerchief from the prosecutor - I saw him take it from the prisoner; I found another handkerchief and a knife on him.


                  DAVID 
                  LAING
               



             (Police-constable E 58.) I have a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office - I was present, and know he is the person.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
