





880. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DUNCOMBE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of September
 last, two hundred and ten yards of everlasting, value 10 l.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Archibald 
                     Bryson
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken by the watchman, who, on suspecting him, confessed the robbery; no promises were made to him.




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
