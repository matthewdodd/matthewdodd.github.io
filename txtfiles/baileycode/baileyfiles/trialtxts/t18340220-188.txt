





503. 
               
                  
                     ELIZA 
                     WILSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of February
, 1 shawl, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Gudge
                  




               
            .


                  REBECCA 
                  GUDGE
               



            . I am the wife of James Gudge - we keep a potatoe shop
, No. 4, White Horse-place, Mile End Old Town


 - my shawl was hanging over the potatoe bin on the 15th of February, the prisoner came in to offer a picture for sale - I refused to buy it - she went away - in about half an hour I went to take my milk pails, and missed my shawl - this is it.


                  JAMES 
                  RICHMAN FOLKARD
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I tookin this shawl on the 15th of February, from the prisoner - I knew her before.



               GUILTY
            .* Aged 23. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
