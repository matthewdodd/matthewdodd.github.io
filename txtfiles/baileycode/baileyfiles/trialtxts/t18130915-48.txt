





872. 
               
                  
                     HENRIETTA 
                     GREGORY
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               she, on the 28th of August
, had in her custody and possession, divers forged bank notes for the payment of 1 l.
            

To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
