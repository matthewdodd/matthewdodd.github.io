





227. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SMITH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of December
 last, fifty-six pounds weight of lead, value 5 s. belonging to 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Hall
                        



                     
                   and 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Wallis
                        



                     
                  , affixed to a certain building of theirs
            , against the statute.
The prisoner was found in the prosecutor's, which was not inhabited; he was up the first floor chimney, and the lead was cut off and rolled up.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
