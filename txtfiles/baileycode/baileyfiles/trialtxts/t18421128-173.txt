



173. 




JOSEPH BROWN
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
17th of Nov., 1 metal cock, value 1s. 6d., and 11 inches of pipe, 6d.; the goods of 



Ann Heading, and fixed to a building.—2nd COUNT, not stating it to be fixed.






HARRIETT HEADING
. On the 17th of Nov. the prisoner came to our house for a pail of water—then he came again for a pitcher of water—my mother told him to go down to the cock—he was down about five minutes—after he was gone, we missed the cock, and pipe—the cock now produced it like it, but I could not swear to it—my mother's name is Ann Heading—the prisoner said he stole the cock.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years—Convict Ship.
