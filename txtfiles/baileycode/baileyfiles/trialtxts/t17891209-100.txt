





101. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     WELCH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of November
 last, one pair of cotton stockings, value 2 s. one pair of nankeen breeches, value 1 s 6 d. a silk handkerchief, value 2 s. 6 d. and other things
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Owen
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the property on her.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
