







                     William 
                     Winnard 
                  



               
            , of the Precinct of St. Catharines


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Cock, value 4 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Francis 
                     Day 
                  




                
            , the 15th of this Instant January
.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That his Bulk was broke open, and the Fowl was stolen.
Bennet Hern depos'd, That he saw the Prisoner breaking the Prosecutor's Bulk, and come out with the Cock, that he cry'd out, stop Thief, pursu'd him, and took him, he having thrown the Cock away in the Street; the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10d
            .
