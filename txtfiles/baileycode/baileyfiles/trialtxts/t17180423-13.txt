







                     Edward 
                     Warburton 
                  



                
             , of the Parish of St. Brides


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Can, value 3 l. in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Cort 
                        




, the 4th of April
 last. The Evidence deposed, the Prisoner having a Pint of Drink in the Tankard went away with the Can. The Fact being plainly proved upon the Prisoner. She was found 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s
            .(


               Transportation
            .)
