




2186. 



THOMAS WILLIAMS, alias
 Robert Thomas,

 was indicted

 for marrying, on the 
30th of June, 1841, 



Mary Sarah Canty, his former wife being alive:—

also for marrying, on the 
1st of December, 1840, 



Elizabeth White, his former wife being alive; to both of which he pleaded




GUILTY
.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Fifth Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
