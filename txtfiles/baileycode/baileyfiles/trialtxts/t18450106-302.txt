JOHN BUXTON
 and 




GEORGE BROWN
 were indicted for

 stealing, on the 
26th of Dec., 1 coat, value 3l.; 4s.; and 1 pair of trowsers, 1l.; the goods of 



John Stewart; to which


BUXTON pleaded 


GUILTY.* Aged 21.

BROWN pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 25.
