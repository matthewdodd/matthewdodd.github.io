





405. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     ABEL
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               that he, on the 20th of February
, had in his custody and possession, a bank note for the payment of 5 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
