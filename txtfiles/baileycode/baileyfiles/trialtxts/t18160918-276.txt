






1095. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     LONG
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     COULING
                  




               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of August
, a tea-urn, value 4l.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Walker
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  WHITLEY
               



            . On the 28th of August, the prisoner 
               
                  Daniel 
                  Long
               



            , walked up to the shop door and took a tea-urn from off a stove that was there. I pursued them both, and brought them back with tea-urn in their possession.
LONG, 

               GUILTY
            , aged 21.
COULING, 

               GUILTY
            , aged 18.





               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
