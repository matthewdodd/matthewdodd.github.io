





55. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DOVDY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of October
, forty pounds weight of leaden pipe, value 5 s. belonging to 
                     
                        Walter 
                        strictland
                     




                  , affixed to a stable of his, against the statute
            .
The prisoner was taken by the watchman, with the pipe, and carried to the watch-house.
(The lead produced and deposed to.)



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
