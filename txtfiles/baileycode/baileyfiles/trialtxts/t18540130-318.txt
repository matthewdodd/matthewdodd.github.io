



318. 



THOMAS BROWN
alias
BELL
, was indicted for

a rape upon 



Elizabeth Marsh, on 
28th December.


MESSRS. PARRY
and
DEARSLEY
conducted the Prosecution.

(The prisoner requested the Court to assign him Counsel, and
MR. LAURENCE
undertook the Defence.)




GUILTY
.
(The prisoner was further charged and proved to have been previously convicted at. this Court, in July, 1852, of larceny, and imprisoned three months.)




Transported for Eighteen Years.


Before Mr. Recorder.
