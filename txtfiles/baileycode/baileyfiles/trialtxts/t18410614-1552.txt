



1552. 




JOHN THOMAS EARL
 was indicted

 for forging and uttering, on the 
19th of May, a certain power of attorney, for the sale of 255l. 15s. 6d., with intent to defraud the 

Governor and Company of the Bank of England.—Six other COUNTS, varying the manner of laying the charge: to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 44.—Recommended to mercy,—


Transported for Seven Years.
