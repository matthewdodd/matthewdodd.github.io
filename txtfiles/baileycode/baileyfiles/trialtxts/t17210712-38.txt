







                     Mary 
                     Johnson 
                  



               
            , of St. James at Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Boots, 7 pair of Shoes and a pair of Slippers
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Siggins 
                  




                
            , on the 11th of this Instant July
. It appeared that the Prisoner being seen with the Goods in a Handbasket, and askt how she came by them, said she bought them of a Man at Islington: but as she was carrying to Newgate confest where she had them and went along with Mr. King to the Prosecutor, who owned them. The Jury considering the matter, found her 


               Guilt to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
