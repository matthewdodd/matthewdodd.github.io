







                  Winifred 
                  Haynes 
               



             of St. Martins in the Fields


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 8 Shirts,13 Turnovers and 3 long Neckcloaths
            , from 
               
                  Isabella 
                  Sutherland 
               




              the 4th of March
 last. It appeared that, the Prisoner had been the Prosecutor's Weekly Servant
 3 or 4 Years, and that she took the Goods and pawned them, and owning where she had pawned them her Mistress had them again; that she confest it before the Justice. The Prisoner in her defence said that she did not design to steal them, but intended to redeem them and bring them again. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s.10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
