





467. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  WILSON
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of January
, three shirts, value 12 s.; five handkerchiefs, value 8 s. 6 d., and two half-handkerchiefs, value 2 s., the goods of 
                     
                        Henry 
                        Blundell
                     



                  ; - seven yards of stuff, value 6 s., and five yards and half of linen cloth, value 5 s. 6 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Richard 
                  Robins
               



            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
