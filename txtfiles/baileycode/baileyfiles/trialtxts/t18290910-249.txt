





1780. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted 


               stealing, on the 11th of September
, 1 writing-desk, value 18s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Bowden
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  BOWDEN
               



            . I am a broker
. On the 11th of September I missed this desk, about five minutes after I placed it on the table - the officer brought the prisoner back with it.
Prisoner. Q. Did not you say you did not know it? A. I said I would look and see, and then I missed it - I know the desk now.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HOLT
               



            . I was standing fronting the Sessions' House, about ten o'clock in the morning, and saw theprisoner running by with this desk; I took him, and asked the prosecutor if he had lost any thing; he said No; then he looked, and said, "I lost it from this table;" I took the prisoner to Hatton-garden.
Prisoner's Defence. A gentleman came up, and asked if I was willing to earn a shilling; he said, "Take this box to Islington," and told me to go on - I went about two hundred yards, and then this gentleman came and asked where I got it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
