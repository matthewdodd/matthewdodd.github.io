







                     Mary 
                     Mackdonnel 
                  



               
            , of St. James's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Moidore, and 21 Guineas, in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           Patience 
                           Page 
                        




, the 28th of May
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, The Prisoner was her Servant
, and having liv'd with her but four Days, stole the Money out of a Drawer, and went away; but she finding her upon Enquiry, she return'd her eleven Guineas of the Money, having bought Clothes with some of the rest. The Prisoner did not deny the Fact, but only said she took but 15 Guineas. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s.
