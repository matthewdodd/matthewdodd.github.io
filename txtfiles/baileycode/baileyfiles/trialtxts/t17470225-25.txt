                     Timothy 
                     Aldridge 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Aldridge 
                  



                
             were indicted for 


               stealing one Copper Porridge pot, value 7 s one Brass Ladle, value 2 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Roberts 
                  




                
            .


                  Sarah 
                  Roberts 
               



            . the principle Witness, who deposed (she keeps the George and Bull in Orchard street) that the Prisoners came into her House, and called for a Pint of Beer; after they had been in the House some Time, they went backward into the Yard, then came again into the Room; afterwards two of them went out into the Street; about a quarter of an Hour after she missed her Pot; there were three of the Boys, but one was run away. 
               
                  Timothy 
                  Aldridge 
               



             was in the House all the Time; and 
               
                  Robert 
                  Aldridge 
               



             confessed, when he was taken, that he had sold the Pot, but it was stole by the other Boy, that was run away.


                  Timothy 
                  Aldridge 
               





               acquitted
            ;

                  Aldridge
