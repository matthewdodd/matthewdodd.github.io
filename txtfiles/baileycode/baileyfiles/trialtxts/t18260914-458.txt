





1770. 
               
                  HENRY 
                  CARPENTER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of September
, 1 saw, value 2s.; 1 smoothing plane, value 1s. 6d.; 1 mallet, value 1s., and 1 basket, value 10d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Hugh 
                  Fletcher
               




            .
HUGH FLETCHER. I am a carpenter
. On the 9th of September I was at work at Mr. Lee's, in Bread-street


, and left a basket, containing these tools, there, while I was gone to breakfast opposite; I saw the prisoner open the street door; I ran out, and secured him with the property.


                  JOHN 
                  WILLIS
               



            . I received him in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 38.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
