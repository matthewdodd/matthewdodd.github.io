







                     Lawrence 
                     Johnson 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a Watch value 4 l.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Oliver 
                  




                
            , Oct. 2.


Robert Oliver thus deposed: I lost this Watch one Night, about five Months ago, from the Side of my Hammock, while I was at Sea near Jamaica


. The Prisoner was then on board the same Ship; but I never mistrusted him, till t'other day I heard that he had pawn'd such a Watch for 40 s.


                  Mary 
                  Dawson 
               



             thus deposed: The Prisoner brought this Watch to me at my Lodging in King Edward Street in Wapping, and desired me to pawn it for 30 or 40 s. I got 40 s. upon it, and brought him the Money.
The Prisoner in his Defence said, that the Watch was pawn'd to him by a Woman in Jamaica. 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
            .
