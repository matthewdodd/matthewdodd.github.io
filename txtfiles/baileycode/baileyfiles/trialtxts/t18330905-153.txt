





1356. 
               
                  
                     DAVID 
                     EVANS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of September
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        Henry Hiblin


                     
                  ; from his person
            .
HENRY HIBLIN. I was at Bartholomew fair


 on the 3rd of September; an officer came and asked, if I had lost anything; I felt and missed my handkerchief - the officer had the prisoner, and took my handkerchief from him.


                  PHILLIP 
                  PARISH
               



             (City police-constable No. 45). I was at the fair, and saw the prisoner and another lad following the prosecutor; I followed them, and saw something peep under the prisoner's coat, I laid hold of him, and called to the prosecutor - I took this handkerchief from under his coat.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I picked it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
