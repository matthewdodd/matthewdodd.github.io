







                     Sarah 
                     Laurence 
                  



               
             , of St. Giles's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Guinea, and 18 s. in Money
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Fleetwood 
                  




                
            , the 6th of this Instant April
.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Money was in a Drawer, and the Prisoner was washing for her; and that she missing the Money, the Prisoner owned the taking of it, and that she had 13 s. 6 d. of the Money again; the Jury found her 

               Guilty
