





410. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     OVERALL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of December
, 1 pair of trousers, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Henry Flower 
                           Fenner
                        




                     
                  ; and that he had been before convicted of felony
            .


                  HENRY 
                  FLOWER FENNER
               



            . I keep a clothes-shop
 in Somers'-town


. On the 5th of December, at half-past five o'clock in the evening, the prisoner came and took a pair of trousers from my door; I pursued, and took him in a dark court contiguous to my house - he was then taking the mark off the trousers; he said, "Pray don't, I am very sorry."


                  RICHARD 
                  HOLLAND
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  ABRABAM 
                  HAM
               



            . I am street-keeper of Coleman-street ward. I have a certificate of the prisoner's conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office - I was a witness on his trial, and know he is the man - (read).



               GUILTY
            . Aged 45. - 


               Transported for 14 Years
            .
