





1284. 
               
                  
                     FRANCIS 
                     SWINTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of July
, one handkerchief, value 7 s., the property of 
                     
                        
                           Michael 
                           Chick
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  MICHAEL 
                  CHICK
               



            . I live in Watling-street. On the31st of July, about half-past eight o'clock in the evening, I was in Coppice-row


, walking arm-in-arm with a friend, and felt a pull at my pocket, turned round, and saw the prisoner running. I pursued, and overtook him without losing sight of him, and found my handkerchief on him.


                  EDWARD 
                  READ
               



            . I am a constable. I heard the cry of Stop thief! and saw the prisoner running. I took him in charge.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. It laid on the ground, I picked it up, and ran away with it. The gentleman was in liquor.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Life
            

Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
