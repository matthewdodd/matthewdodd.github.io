







                     Elizabeth 
                     Jones 
                  



               
            , of St. Botolph without Bishopsgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 Linnen Clouts value 5 d. a Linnen Tablecloth, 2 d. a Towel, 2 d. 1 Linnen Handkerchiet 1 d. 1 Silk Handkerchief and two pieces of sheet
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Freeman 
                  




                
            , on the 5th of March
 last. It appeared that the Prosecutor's Wife going down Stairs, found the Prisoner with the Goods bundled up and going away with them. The Prisoner in her Defence said, that she was going to carry some things to pawn for another Woman; that she was in Drink and did indeed go in to make Water. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
