





266. 
               
                  
                     BRIAN 
                     GAHAGAN
                  , alias 
CARGAN




            , was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of March
 last, a plank of mahogany, containing twelve feet, value 26 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Sherwood
                  




               
            .
(The prisoner offered the plank for sale, and was stopped.)
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
I know nothing of the wood.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for fourteen years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. COMMON SERJEANT.
