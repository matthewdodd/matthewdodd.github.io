





1843. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BROTHERTON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of August
, 3 brass locks, value 1s.; 8 brass knobs, value 8d., and 7 brass plates, value 4d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Ames
               




            .

SAMUEL AMES. I am a furniture broker
, and live at Islington


. On the 2d of August I lost the property stated, which I had seen safe about two o'clock that afternoon - they were taken from a place in my warehouse; the prisoner was in my service as porter for a fortnight - these are my articles; here are locks, knobs and plates - they had been in my tool box.


                  JANE 
                  GORDON
               



            . I am servant to the prosecutor: he told me to watch the prisoner; I saw him take these things from a place where they were concealed, and put them into his trousers pocket.


                  THOMAS 
                  COOPER
               



            . I am an officer. I was sent for, and took the prisoner; I found these articles in his trousers pocket.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 31.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
