





370. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SELBY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 11th day of March
 last, two glass decanters, value 10 d. one pint of brandy, value 12 d. and one pint of shrub, value 12 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Benjamin 
                     Painter
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor saw the prisoner take the decanters off the counter.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Baron HOTHAM.
