





1873. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     WILLETT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of October
, 3 silver spoons, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Frances 
                     Reyner
                  




               
            .



                  FRANCES 
                  REYNER
               



            . I am single
. I lost this property from the closet in the kitchen of my house at Stoke Newington


; the prisoner had come into my service on the Wednesday - I discharged her on the Saturday, and found the spoons in her box.


                  JOSIAS 
                  HOLMES
               



            . I am an officer. I was sent for - the prisoner denied having the spoons, but I found them in her box.


                  CATHERINE 
                  DAVISON
               



            . I was present when they were found.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 33.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
