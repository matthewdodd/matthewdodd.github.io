



3052. 




SOPHIA DOBSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
15th of October, 1 jug, value 1s.; and 1 pair of scissors, value 1d.; the goods of 



James Bourne; and that she had been before convicted of felony.







SARAH BOURNE
. I am the wife of James Bourne, and live in Brown-street, Greenwich. On the 14th of October I placed a jug and a pair of scissors in my window—I missed them, and asked the prisoner about them—she said she had not got them—these are my scissors.





SARAH RATE
. I saw the prisoner take this jug and scissors, and put them under her cloak—I told Mrs. Bourne, and she was taken.

Prisoner's Defence. I only had some chips, which I picked up, and a bit of old oil-cloth.





JOSEPH BUCKMAN
. I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
.* Aged 58.—


Transported for Seven Years.
