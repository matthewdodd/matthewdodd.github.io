



1077. 



JOHN SMITH
 was indicted

 for forging and uttering a bill of exchange for 5l., with intent to defraud 



James Hore:—also for stealing a coat, value 5s., the goods of 



Lionel Williams Dyer 1 watch, value 15s.; and 1 time-piece, 4l.; the goods of 



Solomon Zempelburgh.





GUILTY
.—


Transported for Seven Years
,
