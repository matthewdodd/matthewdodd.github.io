



1136. 




WILLIAM HALFORD CLARKE
, was indicted

 for feloniously forging and uttering, on the 
30th of Jan., an order for the payment of 24l. 11s. 2d., with intent to defraud 



Abel Smith and others;—also, for forging and uttering, on the 
27th of Feb., another order for the payment of 84l. 17s. 2d., with intent to defraud 



William Masterman and others; to both of which he pleaded.


(Mr. 



John Jerram, wholesale tea-dealer, Fenchurch-street, deposed to the prisoner's previous good character.)




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Twenty Years.
