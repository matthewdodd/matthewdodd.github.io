





693. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     GRANTHAM
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of December
, 2 saws, value 5s.; 4 squares, value 5s.; 8 planes, value 13s., and 2 rules, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joel 
                     Grantham
                  




               
            .
JOEL GRANTHAM. I am the prisoner's father. I lost these tools, which were pawned against my consent.


                  JOHN 
                  MILTON
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I produce the articles, pawned, to the best of my belief, by the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 28.




               Transported for Seven Years more
            .
There was another indictment against the prisoner.
