






1241. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     BROWN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of August
, three pewter pots, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Clark
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  CLARK
               



            . I keep the Camden's Head, public-house, Camden-town




. On the 16th of August, I was within twenty yards of my door, and saw the prisoner with three pots. I sent my man after him, he brought him to me with them. I gave him in charge.


                  JOHN 
                  SEAL
               



            . I am a baker. I was in Baham-street, Camden-town, and saw the prisoner take two pots off the rails of No. 32.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 31.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before W. Arabin, Esq.
