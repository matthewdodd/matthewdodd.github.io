





1800. 
               
                  
                     JABEZ 
                     HUNT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of July
, 1 pair of boots, value 9s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Candy
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  CANDY
               



            . I live in King-street, Hammersmith


, and am a boot-maker
. I missed a pair of boots from my door on the 7th of July, between eight and nine o'clock in the evening; I had tried them on a customer within half an hour before.


                  ARTHUR 
                  MILLS
               



             (Police-constable T 5). On Saturday, the 7th of July, I took the prisoner into custody; I searched him, and found a boot in each of his pockets - I took him on another charge at Kensington, near two miles from the prosecutor's.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was coming out of the country, and bought them of a man I do not know.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
