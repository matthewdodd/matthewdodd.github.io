





615. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     THOMPSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of May
, one shift, value 2 s. and one table cloth, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Kelly
                  




               
            .


                  John
                   Orange
               



             saw the prisoner running with some things, near the prosecutor's house, and stopped him; and Mr. Kelly deposed to the things.
Prisoner. I bought the things in Back-lane of a person who is not here.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
