





1146. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  HARRIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of May
, 1 coat, value 12s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Gillis
               




            .
SAMUEL GILLIS. I live in Fox and Goose-street, Shadwell. On the 7th of May I was at work at St. Catherine's-dock


, and put my coat on a wall, about seven o'clock in the morning - I heard a cry of Stop thief! looked round, and missed it - I saw the prisoner with it, and a man secured him.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was looking for employment there, and protest I never saw the coat until it was shewn to me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 28.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
