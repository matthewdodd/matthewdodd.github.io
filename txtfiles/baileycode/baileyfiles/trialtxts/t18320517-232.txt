






1359. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     THOMAS
                  




               
             and 
               
                  CHARLES 
                  WINTER
               



             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of May
, 2 knives, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Wright
               




            ; to which Thomas 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
The prosecutor's name being 
               
                  Charles 
                  David Wright
               



            , WINTER was 


               ACQUITTED
            .
