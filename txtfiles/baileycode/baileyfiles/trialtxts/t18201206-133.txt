





135. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     DREW
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of November
, 60 lbs. of mutton, value 20 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Williams
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  WILLIAMS
               



            . I am a butcher
, and live in Grub-street


. On the 14th of November, I had a carcase of mutton stolen from the door, between one and two o'clock.



                  JAMES 
                  ALLEN
               



            . I live in Grub-street. I was standing at my door and saw the prisoner take the carcase from the close - he was brought back with it.


                  GEORGE 
                  BINN
               



            . I saw the prisoner with it - I thought by his manner he was not a regular butcher - I stopped him with it on his shoulder.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. In the morning a man asked me to fetch a load, he took me into Grub-street, and laid it on my shoulder.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 23.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
