





2067. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     KIRBY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of August
, 1 bed, value 1l.; 1 pillow, value 2s.; 1 pair of sheets, value 8s.; 1 pair of blankets, value 8s., and 1 quilt, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Perren
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZABETH 
                  MATILDA PERREN
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  William 
                  Perren
               



             - we live in Featherstone-buildings, City-road


. On the 16th of August the prisoner was at our house, and asked for a lodging; I had a bed, a pair of sheets, and blankets there; he came about nine o'clock at night, and I missed the articles stated at six in the morning.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BRUFF
               



             (Police-constable G 162.) In Cousequence of the witness' description I was passing Drurylane, and saw the prisoner - I apprehended him; he had the bed and things on the ground near him; I asked him what made him take them, and he said he wanted to go into the country.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
