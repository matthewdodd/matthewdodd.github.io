



288. 




ROBERT EARL
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
10th of December, 1 brass stall-board plate, value 30s., the goods of 



Michael Hart.






MICHAEL HART
. I keep a coffee-house, at No. 159, Aldersgate-street This stall-board plate is mine—it fits on under the window—I lost it on Monday last, the 10th of December—it was not a fixture—it takes off at night—this is it—(looking at it)—I have often seen the prisoner round the neighbourhood.





JAMES KENDALL
. I was coming from my tea on Monday evening, and saw the prisoner take this plate off the window, and walk away—I brought him back to the coffee-shop—he had got half away across the road with it.

Prisoner's Defence. It was lying on the pavement, and I took it up.




GUILTY
.* Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years.
