







                     Ann 
                     Simpson 
                  



               
            , of Tottenham


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2 Holland Shirts value 4 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Ralph 
                           Skinson 
                        



                      
                  , and 2 Butten Cloths value 1st the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Susannah 
                           Calvert 
                        



 on the 16th of this Instant July
. It appeared that the Goods hung on a Hedge to dry with other Linnen, and the Prisoner being seen to come out of the Ditch with the Goods upon her, was secured. The Prisoner in her Defence said that a Gentleman gave them to her: but could not prove it, nor call any to her Reputation. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
