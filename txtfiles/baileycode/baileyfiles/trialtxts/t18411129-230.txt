



230. 




CHARLES DARBY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
30th of November, 1 cloak, value 15s., the goods of 



Gregory Dumanski.






GREGORY DUMANSKI
. I was at the Palace Arms public-house, Pimlico, on the evening of the 30th of November, and lost my cloak—this now produced is it.





JOHN PENROSE
. I was at the Palace Arms, on the evening of the 30th, of November, about half-past nine o'clock—I saw the prosecutor and prisoner there.





JOHN CURRY
. I am a corporal in the First Battalion of Coldstream Guards. I met the prisoner at a quarter-past ten o'clock that night—he had a bundle with him which contained the cloak—I took him into custody.

Prisoners Defence. I went into the Horse Guards and had a pint of beer; a gentleman asked me to drink with him, which I did; I then went out, and a man gave me the cloak to pawn.




GUILTY
.* Aged 22.—


Transported for Seven Years.
