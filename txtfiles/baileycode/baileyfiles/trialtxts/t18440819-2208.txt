





2208. 



RICHARD GAINEY
 and 



EDWARD SMITH
 were again indicted for

 stealing 6 sacks, value 6s., and 24 bushels. of oats, 3l.; the goods of 



William Roffey and another; and 





JOHN HARVEY



, for feloniously receiving the same, well knowing them to have been stolen, &c.

(Gainey and Smith received good characters.)

GAINEY and SMITH pleaded 



GUILTY
.—




Confined Six Months.


HARVEY pleaded 



GUILTY
. Aged 60.—


Transported for Fourteen Years.


Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
