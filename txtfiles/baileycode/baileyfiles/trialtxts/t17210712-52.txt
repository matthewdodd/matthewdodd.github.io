







                     John 
                     Unwin 
                  



               
            , of St. James in Westminster


, was indicted for 


               Assaulting 
                     
                        
                           Ann 
                           Shaw 
                        




                      
                   on the High Way, on the 19th of March last, putting her in Fear, and taking from her a Pocket, a Silk Handkerchief, 2 Keys and 2 s. 6 d. in Money
            . 


               The taking of the Goods was fuily proved upon him, but it not appearing that he put her in Fear, the Jury found him Guilty of Felony only
            .
