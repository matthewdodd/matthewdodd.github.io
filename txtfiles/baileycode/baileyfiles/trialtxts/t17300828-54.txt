







                     William 
                     Baker 
                  



               
            , of Christ Church, Spittle-Fields


, was indicted for 


               stealing 300 Yards of Ferret, value 35 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Wilcox 
                  




                
            , the 8th of August
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner went to the Prosecutor's, under pretence of Buying a Parcel of Ferrets, and took the Opportunity to steal 10 Pieces, which were found in his Pockets; he being immediately pursu'd, and apprehended, and the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               Guilty
             of the Indictment.
