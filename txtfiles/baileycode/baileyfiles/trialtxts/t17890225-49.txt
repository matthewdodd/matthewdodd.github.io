





230. 
               
                  
                     CATHERINE 
                     WOOD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of January
, three pair of cloth breeches, value 6 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Jane 
                     Fosgate
                  




               
            , widow
.
The prosecutrix saw the prisoner take down the breeches.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
