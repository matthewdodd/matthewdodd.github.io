                     William

            , Nov. 5
.


                  William 
                  Clark 
               



            . As my Lord Mayor was going to St. Paul's, I lost a handkerchief out of my pocket.


                  William 
                  Broomer 
               



            . I saw the Prisoner take a handkerchief out of the Prosecutor's pocket, and the handkerchief was taken from the Prisoner: he owned it, and said it was the first fact, and begged for mercy.
