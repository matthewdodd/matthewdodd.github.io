





344. 
               
                  
                     ANDREW 
                     DICKSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 8th of April
 last, two French horns, value 10 l. and sixteen brass trumpet mouth-pieces, value 40 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Woolfe
                  




               
            .



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. Justice NARES.
