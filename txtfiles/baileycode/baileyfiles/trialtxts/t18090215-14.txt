





188. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MILLIGAN
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he on the 2nd of December
, was clerk
 to 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Lee
                        



                     
                  , 
                     
                        
                           John Scott 
                           Martineau
                        



                     
                   and 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Wilkinson
                        



                     
                  , and was employed and entrusted by them to receive valuable securities for and on their account, and being such servant so employed and entrusted, did receive and take into his possession, a banker's draft, value 72 l. their property, and that he on the same day, feloniously did embezzle, secrete, and steal the said draft
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
