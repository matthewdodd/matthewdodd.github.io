





255. (M.) 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Udith 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a cloth clock, value 8 s.
             the property of 
               
                  William 
                  Johnson 
               




             , March 12
. +


                  William 
                  Johnson 
               



            . About five weeks ago I lost a cloth coat which I had to dye; I was in company with the prisoner at a house in Long lane


; it was taken from off a line in the yard; there were several people saw the prisoner go into the yard; I took him up, and before Justice Girdler he owned he took it, and had sold it in Rag fair for 3 s. 6 d.


                  William 
                  Eaton 
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the cloak from off a line, and sold it up, and put it under his left arm, and go off with it.
Q. Why did you not stop him?
Eaton. The prisoner is a dyer as well as the prosecutor, and we did not know but that it belonged to him.
Prisoner's defence.
I did not take it from the line.



               Guilty
            .
