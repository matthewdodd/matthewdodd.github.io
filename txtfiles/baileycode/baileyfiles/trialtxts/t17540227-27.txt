






181. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Luke 
                     Jeffries 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing two silver spoons, value 8 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Taylor 
                  




                
            , January 17
. ++
The prosecutor keeps a publick-house
 in Marybone-street


, the prisoner was his servant
, the two spoons were missing in the morning of the 17th of January; the prisoner having eat last with one of them, and the other being at that time lying by him, he was suspected, and charged with taking them. He at first denied it, but after that owned before three witnesses he had taken them both, and given them to a brother-in-law of his about nine o'clock over night, who was going to France, in order to help to bear his charges.



               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
