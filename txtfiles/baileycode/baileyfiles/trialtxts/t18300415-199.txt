





866. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     HENDERSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of April
, 3 loaves of bread, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Alexander 
                     Robertson
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  COCKRANE
               



            . I am a baker in the employ of Mr. 
               
                  Alexander 
                  Robertson
               



            , of Oxford-street, On the 8th of April I lost three loaves from my basket at the corner of Wild-street


, while I was round the corner serving a customer - I had not been away ten minutes; I saw the bread at the office.


                  JOHN 
                  McGREGOR
               



            . I was in Harley-street on the 8th of April, and saw the prisoner with two 4lb. loaves and one 2lb. loaf - I followed him into the New-road and stopped him; I asked where he bought it - he said he did not buy it at all; I asked who gave it him - he said, "No one, I took it out of a baker's basket, take the bread and let me go;" I said, "You shall go back with me, you have been long enough at this business;" I took him back and found the witness.


                  JOSEPH 
                  CLARK
               



            . I took the prisoner - he said he had not bought it, nor had it given him.
Prisoner's Defence. I have been out of work and have a large family.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 46. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
