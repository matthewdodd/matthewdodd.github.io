





2138. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  VERNON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of September
, 1 glass goblet, value 6s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Robert 
                  Johnstone
               




            .


                  ROBERT 
                  EDWARD POWELL SEALEY
               



            . I am assistant to Mr. Robert Johnstone, chemist
, of Greek-street, Soho


. On the 28th of September I saw the prisoner leave the shop hastily - I followed him into the street, and brought him back; he produced this goblet, and said he was starving, but was no thief.


                  CHARLES 
                  BOWLER
               



            . I took him in charge; he seemed much distressed.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 56.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
