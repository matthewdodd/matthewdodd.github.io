







                  Elizabeth 
                  Wife
 of 
                     
                        John 
                        Stibs 
                     







            , of the Parish of St. Clement Danes


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Brass Paper Box, and some other Goods of small Value
            , the 28th of September
 last. The prosecutor depos'd, The Prisoner was a Chair-Woman
 in the House, and he having miss'd Things, Suspected the Prisoner, and searching her House, found the Pepper-Box and other Things in the Prisoner's Room. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact. call'd several Persons to her Reputation, her none appear'd. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
