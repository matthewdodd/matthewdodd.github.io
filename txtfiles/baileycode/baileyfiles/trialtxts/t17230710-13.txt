







                     William 
                     Mac Guinis 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 7 Silver handled Knives, value 4 l. in the Dwelling-house
             of 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Fenwick 
                  




                
            , the 8th of June
 last. It appeared by the Evidence, that the Boy was a Servant
, and took the Opportunity to steal the Knives. He confess'd the Fact before the Justice, which Confession was read in Court. He had nothing to say in his own Defence, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
