






494. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     KING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of May
, a tea-caddy, value 2l. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Sophia 
                           Damond
                        




                     
                  , in her dwelling-house
            .


                  SOPHIA 
                  DAMOND
               



            . I know nothing of this robbery of my own knowledge. The tea-caddy was replaced before I heard of the circumstance, and I never missed it.


                  SARAH 
                  LOVING
               



            . I live directly opposite to the prosecutrix. I saw the prisoner take this caddy from Mrs. Damond's shop, and when he perceived I was going over to tell her, he came back, and replaced it.
GUILTY, aged 20,




               Of stealing to the value of 39s, only
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Park.
