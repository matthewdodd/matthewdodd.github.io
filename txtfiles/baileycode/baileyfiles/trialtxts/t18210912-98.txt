






1145. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     MOORE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of August
, one quart pot, value 10 d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Hewett
                        



                     
                  ; one quart pot, value 10 d., the goods of 
                     
                        Isaac 
                        Haynes
                     



                  , and one quart pot, value 10 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Kentish
                  



               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  HEWETT
               



            . I keep the City of Hereford, public-house, Charles-street, Portman-square




. I have lost three or four dozen of pots. On the 22d of August, Fellows brought one to me which I knew.


                  THOMAS 
                  MAYO
               



            . I am cellarman to 
               
                  John 
                  Kentish
               



            , who keeps the Apollo, public-house in Paddington-street




. Fellows brought me a pint pot, which I knew to be my master's.


                  ISAAC 
                  HAYNES
               



            . I keep the Dover Castle, public-house, Portland-place




. Fellows brought me a pot which I knew.


                  JOSEPH 
                  FELLOWS
               



            . I am a constable. On the 22d of August, about five o'clock in the afternoon, I took the prisoner in Great James-street, Lisson Green. I found two pint pots buttoned inside his breeches, and two quart pots in his coat pockets. I asked where he got them, he gave no account - the prosecutors' names were on them.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was coming down Montague-street, and picked up a bag with the pots, and put them in my pocket to take them to where they belonged, as I cannot read.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 31.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before W. Arabin, Esq.
