






1376. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     BERRY
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CHICKS
                  




               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of October
, 2 lbs. of bacon, value 18 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Tandy
                  




               
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  TANDY
               



            . I am a cheesemonger
, and live in Beach-street, Barbican


. On the 11th of October, about seven o'clock at night, while I was busy, two officers brought the prisoners in with this bacon, which had been stolen from the window.


                  JOHN 
                  TWEEDY
               



            . I am a constable. I saw Bury put his hand upon the bacon, and walk away; he turned back, walked by the shop several times, and at last came up with Chicks, who knocked it off the window and Bury caught it, put it under his coat, and we secured them.
CHICKS'S Defence. I was going home.


                  RICHARD 
                  BURY
               



             - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.


                  JOHN 
                  CHICKS
               



             - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.





               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
