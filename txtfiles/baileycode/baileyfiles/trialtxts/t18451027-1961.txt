



1961. 




JOHN WARD
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
4th of Oct, at Wilsden, 1 watch, value 3l. 10s.; 1 seal, 1l.; 1 key, 15s.; 1 coat, 30s.; 1 pair of trowsers, 15s.; 1 waistcoat, 15s.; 2 handkerchiefs, 5s.; 1 pair of boots, 10s.; 1 sheet, 1s.; and 2 razors, 2s. 6d.; the goods of 



James Mamham, his master, in the dwelling-house of 



Stephen George Bowson; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 42. 


Transported for Ten Years.
