





694. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  BROWN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of March
, 2lbs. of butter, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Morton
                  




               
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  MORTON
               



            . I am a cheesemonger
, and live at Ratcliff-highway


. On the 31st of March I heard the alarm, and saw the prisoner stopped with the butter.


                  JAMES 
                  LONG
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Morton. I saw the prisoner lurking about the shop for about ten minutes; he then took the butter, and ran off with it; I pursued, overtook him, and brought him back.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
