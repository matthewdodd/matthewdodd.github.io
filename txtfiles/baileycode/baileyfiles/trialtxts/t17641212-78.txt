






85. (M.) 
               
                  Elizabeth, wife
 of 
                     
                        Hopkin 
                        Roberts 
                     







            , was indicted for 


               stealing a linen gown, value 3 s. two linen shifts, and two linen aprons
            , the property of 
               
                  James 
                  Lane 
               




             , November 23
. +
The prosecutor keeps a chandler's shop
 in Shadwell: the things mentioned in the indictment were missing: the prisoner was suspected, and when taken up, she had one of the aprons and gown on; she confessed where she had pledged the other things, and they were found accordingly.



               Guilty
