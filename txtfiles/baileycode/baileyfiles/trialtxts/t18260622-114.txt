





1191. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  THOMPSON
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of June
, 1 reticule, value 10s.; 1 handkerchief, value 1s.; 1 purse, value 6d.; 6 shillings, and 2 sixpences
            , the property of 
               
                  Isaac 
                  Darby
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
