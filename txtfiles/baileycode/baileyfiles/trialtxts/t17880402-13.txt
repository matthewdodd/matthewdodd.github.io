





265. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     BAYLEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of March
 last, a leaden glue-pot, value 2 s. a pound weight of lead, value 2 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Chesterman
                        



                     
                  ; a glue-pot, value 2 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Hitchcock
                        



                     
                  , and a glue-pot, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Wilson
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken by the patrol with the glue-pots upon him, which were deposed to.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice ASHHURST.
