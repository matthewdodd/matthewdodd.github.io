





1639. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     LAYCOCK
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of August
, 1 shilling, 1 sixpence, and 18d. in copper monies
            , the monies of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Bennett
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded.




               GUILTY
            . - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
