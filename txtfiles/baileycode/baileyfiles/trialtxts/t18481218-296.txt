



296. 




JAMES CATLIN



, stealing 1 bullock's heart, value 2s.; the goods of 



William Brett; having been before convicted.






MICHAEL JAMES COX
. I am shopman to William Brett. On 9th Dec. I missed a bullock's heart—I saw the prisoner running away with it under his arm—it was brought back in half-an-hour with the pipe on it, which matches with this pipe (produced).

Prisoner. I was at home. Witness. I am sure of you—I knew you by sight.





HENRY OSBORNE
. I produce a certificate of the prisoner's conviction (read—Convicted Nov. 1845, confined four months)—I was present; he is the person.




GUILTY
. ** Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
