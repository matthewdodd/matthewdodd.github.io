





572. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Jonas 
                     Abrahams 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a woollen cloth coat, value 20s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Andrew 
                     Grant 
                  




                
            , Esq
; October 8
 ++


                  John 
                  Grace 
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  Andrew 
                  Grant 
               



            . On the 9th of this instant, I saw the prisoner in the King's-arms yard. Coleman street


: I saw him take the coat from the box of the chariot, and carry it towards the gate, and was putting it into his bag: we went and seized him with it.


                  John 
                  Ward 
               



             and 
               
                  James 
                  Hunt 
               



            , two fellow-servants, deposed they saw the prisoner through the window take the coat, as the former witness had said before.
The prisoner said nothing in his defence.



               Guilty
            .
