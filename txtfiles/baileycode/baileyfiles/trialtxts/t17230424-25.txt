







                     Richard 
                     Swinburn 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Hamstead


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing three Silver Spoons, value 27 s. and other Goods
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Shipway 
                  




                
            , the 26th of April
. It appeared by the Evidence, that the Spoons were stolen from the Prosecutor, and were stopped at London when offered to Sale. The Fact being plainly prov'd the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
