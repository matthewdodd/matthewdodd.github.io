





1271. 
               
                  
                     HARRIET 
                     WISE
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of August
, two shawls, value 4 s.; one quilt, value 2 s.; one table cloth, value 1 s.; and one tea spoon, value 3 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Dawson
               




            .


                  MARY 
                  DAWSON
               



            . The prisoner lodged with me. I missed the articles stated in the indictment, and found them in pawn My husband's name is Thomas.


                  WILLIAM 
                  MASTERS
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Turner, a pawnbroker. The prisoner pawned a quilt and shawl on the 19th of July, at different times.


                  JOHN 
                  WENBORNE
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Cotterell, pawn broker. I have a table cloth and tea spoon pawned I believe by the prisoner.
(Property produced and sworn to.)




               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Best.
