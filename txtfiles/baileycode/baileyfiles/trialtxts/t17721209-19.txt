





33. (2d. M.) 
               
                  
                     BENJAMIN
                      DUNN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen shirt, value 2 s. a child's shirt, value 10 d. and a check apron, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Matthew
                      Sage
                  




               
            , Nov. 24
. +


                  Matthew
                   Sage
               



            . I lost the things mentioned out of my garden.


                  John
                   Cockran
               



            . I am a labourer to Mr. Sage: I saw the prisoner take the things mentioned in the indictment, from a line as they were drying in the garden, and he put them in his pocket, and immediately went away: I followed him, and secured him. The constable took the things out of his pocket at the Justice's.
The clothes mentioned in the indictment were produced by the constable and deposed to by the prosecutor.
Prisoner's Defence.
I went to gather some sloes, and saw the things lie by the hedge.




               Guilty 10 d.
            



               T
            .
