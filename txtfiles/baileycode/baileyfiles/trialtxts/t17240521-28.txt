







                     Thomas 
                     Todd 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing silver Lace, and silver Thread
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Tailor 
                  




                
            , the 8th of April
 last.
It appeared by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was a Lace Weaver
, and work'd as a Journey-Man
 with the Prosecutor, and cut the Lace off from his Piece in the Loom, and sold it. The Fact was plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment. 


               Transportation
