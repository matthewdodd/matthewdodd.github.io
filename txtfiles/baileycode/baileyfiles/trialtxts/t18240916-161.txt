





1403. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     QUIN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of August
, a watch, value 5 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Beetham
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  BEETHAM
               



            . I live in Austin's-buildings, Hackney


. On the 17th of August, between nine and ten o'clock in the morning, I left my watch in my bedroom, over the mantle-piece - the prisoner was there at the time; he came to buy a pair of shoes. I returned about twelve at night, but did not miss it till next morning.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  WILLIAM 
                  CARTWRIGHT
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live at Stoke Newington. On the 17th of August, about eleven or twelve or twelve o'clock in the morning, the prisoner brought this watch to my shop.


                  THOMAS 
                  PECK
               



            . I apprehended the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 31.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
