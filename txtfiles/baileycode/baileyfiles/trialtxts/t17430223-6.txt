





141. 
               
                  Dickin 
                  Cartwright 
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing four Deal Boards, val. 4 s. three Mahogany Boards, val. 12s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Alexander 
                     Black 
                  




                
            , Feb. 4
.


                  Alexander 
                  Black 
               



            . The Prisoner stole three Mahogany Boards at several Times, and confessed the taking two Pieces of Mahogany, and three or four Deals. I lost these out of my Yard, and found them and several more at one Mr Barrow's; the Prisoner said, he sold them to him; I know them to be my Boards, because I had more of the same Wood, and there happened to be a particular Mark on one of them.
Hawkins. I saw him take two Fineres out of the the Yard, and carry them off.
Prisoner. There was a couple of Fineres, which the Boy saw me take away; I was going to shew them to a Customer, but the Boy brought me back again: I know nothing of the others. 


               Guilty, 10d.
