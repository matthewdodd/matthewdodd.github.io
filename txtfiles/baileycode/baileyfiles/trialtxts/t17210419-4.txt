







                     Jane 
                     Short 
                  



               
             of St. Vedast alias Foster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 yards of Ribbon, value 2 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Hutton 
                  




                
            . on the 1st of this Instant April
. 
               
                  Mary 
                  Hutton 
               



             deposed, that the Prisoner came into her Mother's Shop under pretence of buying Ribbon; that she reach'd her down 7 pieces, and she (the Prisoner) made bold with one of them; which this Evidence perceiving, let her go out of the Shop about 3 Doors, then had her brought back, and took the Ribbon out of her Breast. The Prisoner denied the Fact, but had none to her Reputation. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
