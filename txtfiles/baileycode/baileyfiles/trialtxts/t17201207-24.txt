







                     John 
                     Underhill 
                  



               
            , of St. Dunstan at Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2 Gold Rings, a Silver Snuff Box, a Shirt, Turnovers, Ec.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Homston 
                  




                
            , on the 14th of November
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that the Prisoner was his hired Servant
, and took the Goods; that he found the shirt upon him, and the other Goods where he had sold them. The Goods were produced in Court; and 
               
                  Edward 
                  Long 
               



             deposed, that they were the same which the Prisoner  owned he had stole from his Master. The Prisoner had nothing to say in his Defence. The Jury found him 

               Guilty
            .
