





16. 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Starkey 
                  



               
            , of St. Bartholomew, near the Royal-Exchange


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Pewter Gallon Pot, value 4 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Rutty 
                  




                
            , the 5th of this Instant February
.
As the Prosecutor's Servant was sitting on the Stairs Head, he saw the Prisoner as he was going through the Tavern (it being a thorow Fare) take the Pot which stood on a Table; he follow'd and seiz'd him. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
