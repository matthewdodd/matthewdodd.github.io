







                     Thomas 
                     Smith
                  



               
            , Indicted for 


               stealing a silver Tankard, valued at 9 l. 17 s. 6 d.
             he could produce no Witnesses to prove his Civility, but had a debauched Character; and endeavouring to prove himself (at that time the Tankard was stole) to be at a Tavern in the Strand, he called several persons to vouch it, but they not agreeing in what they said, were disbelieved, and he found 

               Guilty
             of Felony.
