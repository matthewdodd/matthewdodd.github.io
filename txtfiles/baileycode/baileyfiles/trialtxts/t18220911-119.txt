





1262. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     RUSHTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of August
, one teapot, value 7 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Russ
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  RUSS
               



            . I live in Oxford-street. On the 28th of August, my wife put a teapot out for sale; it was stolen.


                  WILLIAM 
                  SELLERS
               



            . I am a constable. I stopped the prisoner in Oxford-street, on the 28th of August, about two hundred yards from Russell's house, and found the teapot in her bag, she said she bought it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I bought it of a ragman for 6 s.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 36.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Best.
