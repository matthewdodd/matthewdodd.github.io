





2168. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of August
, 1 watch, value 3l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Stock
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  GALLOWAY
               



            . I found this watch on the prisoner.
JOHN STOCK. I belong to the Lord Teignmouth


, which was about four ships from Humphrey's; I lost this watch, which hung over my head in the bed-cabin of the Helen.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for 7 Years longer
            .
