





1800. 
               
                  ELIZABETH 
                  SMITH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of July
, 1 sheet, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Mary 
                  Burke
               




            .
MARY BURKE. I am a widow
. On the 16th of July I missed a sheet off a bed, which I let to the prisoner with a lodging, and found it in pawn.


                  THOMAS 
                  WOLSTENHOLME
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. On the 16th of July the prisoner pledged this sheet.(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
