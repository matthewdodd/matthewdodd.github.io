







                     William 
                     Mekins 
                  



               
             of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing from 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           Criton 
                        




                      
                  , a Cambric Handkerchief, value 18 d.
             on the 6th of March
last. George Criton depos'd. that he and -- Thorp, were walking near St. Giles's Church


, and the prisoner passing close by him, he felt something go from him; whereupon he run after the prisoner, and brought him back to the place where he mist his Handkerchief, and there found it lying on the Ground. 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
