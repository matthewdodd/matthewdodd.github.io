





284. 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Ball 
                  



               
             was indicted for stealing 


               two ruffled Shirts , one Shift, two Cambrick Aprons, two check'd Aprons, one white Handkerchief , two Pair of Stockings, ten Silver Buttons, and one Silver Spoon, the Value of the whole 38 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Alexander 
                     Holland 
                  




                
            , June the 18th.




                  Eleanor 
                  Holland 
               



            . I left the Prisoner the 18th of June to wash some Cloaths; about eleven o'Clock she went away.
Q. What have you got of your Things again?
Holland . I have got four Buttons ; I gave 5 s. for them.
Q. to the Prisoner . Would you ask this Witness any Questions?
Prisoner. No.
Q. What have you to say in your Defence ?
Prisoner . I was in Liquor, or I should not have done it.




               Guilty 4 s. 10 d.
