





335. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     SHARP
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 27th day of January
 last, two iron bolts, value 3 s. eight pounds weight of tire nails, value 2 s. twenty pounds weight of iron, value 20 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Hannah 
                           Uze
                        



                     
                  ; and two flannel jackets, value 1 s. and one iron crow, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Walker
                  



               
            .


                  John
                   Shaw
               



             a watchman, took the prisoner with the property on him, which the shop-man saw in the shop the night before.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
