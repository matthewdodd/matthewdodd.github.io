




1536. 




JAMES MITCHELL
 and 




WILLIAM WEAVER



, stealing 5 pewter pots, value 8.;s.; the goods of 



Elizabeth Randall: also, 1 metal tap, 5s.; the goods of 



Joseph Poole, fixed to a building: also, 1 metal tap, 5s the goods of 



James Cole, fixed to a building: also, 2 pewter pots, 2s. 6d.; the goods of 



John Taylor: and 1 pewter pot, 1s. 3d.; the goods of 



Thomas Reynolds: Weaver having been before convicted: to all of which


WEAVER pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 23.—


Transported for Seven Years
.

MITCHELL pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 23.—



Confined Three Months
.
