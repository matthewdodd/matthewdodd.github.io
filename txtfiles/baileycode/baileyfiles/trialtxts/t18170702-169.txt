





1098. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     PARKIN
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of July, one jacket, value 5s., and one pair of trowsers, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        Robert 
                        Varney
                     



                  ; and one other jacket, value 4s.; one pair of trowsers, value 3s.; and one knife, value 3d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Henderson
                  




               
            .
ROBERT VARNEY. I was on board the Clinker, in the same tier of ships, and lost my things.
JAMES WHITE. I took the prisoner with the property.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
