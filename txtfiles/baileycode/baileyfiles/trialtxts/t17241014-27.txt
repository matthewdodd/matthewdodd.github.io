








                     Elizabeth 
                     Cordwell 
                  



               
            , of St. James's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Sauce Pan, value 16 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  Jane 
                  Burnham 
               





             , the 10th of August

 last. The Fact not being plainly prov'd, the Jury 

               acquitted
             her.
She was also indicted a second Time, for 


               feloniously stealing a Coverlet and Sheet
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Holms 
                  





                
            , the 18th of August

 last. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
