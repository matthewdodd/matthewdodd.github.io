




1766. 




JOHN MORRIS
 and 




GEORGE JONES



, burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 



William Giblee Habershon, and stealing 1 pencil-case, and other goods, value 14l.; his property: 1 pencil-case, and other goods, 5l.; the goods of 



Edward Habershon: Jones having been before convicted: to which


MORRIS


pleaded GUILTY
. Aged 16. JONES


pleaded GULITY
. Aged 16.





Transported for seven years.
.
