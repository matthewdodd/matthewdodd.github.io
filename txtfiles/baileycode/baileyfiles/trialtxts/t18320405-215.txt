





Before Mr. Recorder.
1018. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     ROBERTS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of February
, 1 pair of boots, value 6s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Woolston
                  




               
            .
SAMUEL WOOLSTON. I am a shoemaker
, and live in St. Giles'


. On the 20th of February I was going up the shop, and caught a glance of the prisoner going out of the shop; I went out, and found him within twenty yards of the door, with these boots, which hung by the side of the counter.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. Two boys who were running threw the boots down; I took them up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for seven Years
            .
