





861. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     LAWSON
                  , alias 
JOHNSON




            , was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing on the 7th of October
, four silver forks, value 40 s. five silver table spoons, value 50 s. and four silver salt spoons, value 8 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Jourdan
                  




               
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
