







                     Francis 
                     Rouse 
                  



               
            , of St. Margaret's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 3 Gold Rings, value 40 s and other Goods
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Jane 
                     Johnson 
                  




                
            , the 10th of March
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, she employed the Prisoner to look after her Child and she took the Opportunity of stealing the Goods. The Prisoner pleaded she found them as she was throwing out Dirt. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s
             (


               Transportation
            .)
