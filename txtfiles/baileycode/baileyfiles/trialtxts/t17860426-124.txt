





445. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     BRACE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 28th day of February
, one piece of leaden pipe, value 2 s. belonging to 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Slade
                        




                     
                  ; and fixed to his dwelling-house
            .


                  Samuel
                   Wilson
               



            , the watchman; took the prisoner with the property.



               GUILTY
            





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
