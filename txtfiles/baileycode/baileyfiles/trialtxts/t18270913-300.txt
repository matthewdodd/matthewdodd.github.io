





1886. 
               
                  ELIZABETH 
                  BROADWAY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of September
, 1 blanket, value 4s.
            , the goods of the 
               
Overseers for the time being, of the parish of St. Mary, Islington




            .


                  THOMAS 
                  WESTON
               



            . I am master of Islington work-house


. The prisoner was a pauper
 there - I missed a blanket off her bed last Saturday.


                  MARY 
                  HATTON
               



            . I was at Islington work-house last Saturday, and saw the prisoner with this blanket pinned round her.


                  THOMAS 
                  COPE
               



            . Hatton gave me information - I took the blanket from the prisoner.


                  JOSEPH 
                  BIRCH
               



            . I saw the blanket found on her.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 38.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
