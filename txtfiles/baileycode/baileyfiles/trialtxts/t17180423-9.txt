







                     Arthur 
                     Herbert 
                  



               
            , of St. James's Clerkenwell


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Bridle and Saddle, value 14s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Dent 
                  




                
            , the 14th of Feb
 last. The Evidence deposed, that going into the Stable he took the Prisoner putting them into a Sack, in order to carry them away. The Prisoner having little to say in his Defence, the Jury found-him 


               Guilty to the value of 10d
            . (


               Transportation
            .)
