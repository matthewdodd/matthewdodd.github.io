







                     Richard 
                     Freeman 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing 3 Books, value 6 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Reason 
                  




                
            , on the 5th of November
. It appeared that Mr. Reason had lost several Books, and missing Dr. Taylor's Living and Dying from his Window, when the Prisoner was loitering thereabouts, he took him, and found it upon him. That the Prisoner confest that he had before took 2 other Books, entitled Antiochus and Stratonice, and an Apology, for 
               
                  Antonia 
                  Bourignon 
               



            ; all which was produced in Court, and sworn to by the Prosecutor. 


               Guilty 10 d.
