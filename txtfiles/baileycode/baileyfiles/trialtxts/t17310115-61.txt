







                     William 
                     Lancaster 
                  



               
            , of Allhallows Lombard-street


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Cloth Riding-Coat, value 1 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Thompson 
                  




                
            , the 16th of this Instant January
.
It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prosecutor was going with Coneys in two Hampers on a Horse, and leading his Horse, the Coat lay between the Hampers, and that the Prisoner snatch'd off the Coat, and was running away with it, but was stopped. The Fact being clearly proved, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10d
            .
