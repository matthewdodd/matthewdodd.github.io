





281. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Benjamin 
                     Morris 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing eighteen yards of rope, with a grappling-iron, value 12 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Smith 
                  




                
            , March 22
. ++


                  William 
                  Smith 
               



            . I am a waterman and lighter-man
: I was at Gravesend when the things were lost, on the 21st of March: I only know the things to be my property; they are now at the Queen's-head here.


                  John 
                  Boulter 
               



            . I am a waterman and lighter-man: on the 21st of March, between one and two in the morning, I took up the prisoner and another man in a boat, between Billinsgate and the Custom-house, on the water; they had this grapling-iron and rope in their boat, they were rowing off with them: we took them on shore and before my Lord Mayor: the other man made his escape out of the Mansion-house.
Prisoner's Defence.
The boat was adrift, and I went to bring her in; the rope was in her; there were two other lads with me; the other rowed our boat on shore: one was named 
               
                  John 
                  Hinkins 
               



            , and the other 
               
                  John 
                  Pearcy 
               



            : I sent to them; but they don't come near me.
To his Character.


                  John 
                  Walker 
               



            . I live at the corner of Kennington-lane, in the parish of Lambeth: the prisoner is a waterman: I never heard any ill of him before this, and I have known him from a child.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
