                     Nicholas 
                     Ellsey 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing one crane rope, value 6 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Whetstone 
                  




                
            , March 14
. ++
The rope was missing from out of the prosecutor's crane-room at Limehouse


, a knife was found in the room, by shewing it about, it appear'd to be the prisoner's knife, which he upon it's being shew'd him own'd; he was taken up, and confessed where he had sold it, and the rope, was found accordingly.



               Guilty
            .
