







                     William 
                     Talmarsh 
                  



               
            , of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing from 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Hill 
                        




Gent.
 a Cambrick Hankerchief, value 1 s. 6 d.
             On the 7th of May
 last; The Prosecutor deposed, that on Monday Morning being in the Strand


, he felt the prisoners hand in his Pocket, saw him pull out the Handkerchief, and throw it behind him. In his Defence he said, he found the Handkerchief on the Ground, near where the Prosecutor stood. 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            .
