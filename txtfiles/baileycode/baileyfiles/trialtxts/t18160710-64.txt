





740. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  COLLINS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of July
, a quilt, value 5s.
             the property of 
               
                  James 
                  Chitty
               




            .


                  MARY 
                  ANN CHITTY
               



            . The prisoner came into my back room, and took the quilt of my bed; I heard a noise in the next room; I opened the door and saw the prisoner, between the bed and the drawers, with the quilt in his apron; I asked him who he wanted? and he made a blundering kind of answer, and tried to get away, but I held him fast on the stairs, untilthe man of the house came to my assistance.
(Property produced, and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            ,aged 54.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
