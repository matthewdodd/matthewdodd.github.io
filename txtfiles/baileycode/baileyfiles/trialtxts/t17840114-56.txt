





202. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     GRAINGER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 5th of January
, one linen handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Hemming
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN
                   HEMMING
               



             sworn.
On Monday the 5th of this month, near six o'clock in the evening, going down Ludgate Hill


, I was called to by two gentlemen, who told me the prisoner had picked my pocket; I immediately turned back, they had then hold of the prisoner, and I saw them take from him my pocket handkerchief, the constable has the handkerchief.
(The handkerchief produced.)
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
I was going to Ludgate Hill, and I saw this handkerchief lay on the ground, I picked it up, and was running towards this gentleman.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
