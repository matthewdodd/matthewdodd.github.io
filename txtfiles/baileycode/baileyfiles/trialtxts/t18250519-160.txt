





1015. 
               
                  ABRAHAM 
                  BOLTON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of May
, two bells, value 5 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Mills
               




            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  THURLOW
               



            . On the 19th of May I was near Mr.
 Charles Mills's house, and met the prisoner carrying two bells; I stopped him, and informed Mr. Mills.


                  THOMAS 
                  RICHARDS
               



            . I am in the employ of Mr. Mills; he is a Director of the East India House




. There were four bells on a form in the back kitchen; they were all safe at eight o'clock in the morning. I was at dinner in the kitchen, and heard the alarm; I pursued the prisoner, and found him with them.


                  JOHN 
                  MARTIN
               



            . I am a constable. I took the prisoner, and found the two bells, one in his hand, and the other in his hat.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
