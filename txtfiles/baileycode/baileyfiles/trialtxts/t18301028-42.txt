





1882. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     CLEMENTS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of October
, 13 sovereigns and 4 half-sovereigns, the monies of 
                     
                        
                           Francis 
                           Harrison
                        




                     
                  , his master
            .



                  FRANCIS 
                  HARRISON
               



            . I am a silversmith
, and live at Nos. 185 and 186, Tottenham-court-road


; the prisoner was in my employ. On the 2nd of October, about five o'clock, I gave him 15l. to take up an acceptance at Mr. Campbell's - he took it, and never came back; on the Thursday week he wrote me a letter from Rochester - he had been two months in my employ.


                  JAMES 
                  CAMPBELL
               



            . I called on the prosecutor for the payment of the bill; he said he would send it before five o'clock, by his boy - I went home about four o'clock, and staid till six; I never heard any more of it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
