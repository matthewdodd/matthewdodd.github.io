








                     Joseph 
                     Sheppard 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 1 cock and 6 Hens, value 6 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Matth.ew 
                     Cook 
                  



                
            , the 23d of August

 last. And also for 


               feloniously stealing 1 Cock and 6 Hens, value 6 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Storey 
                  



                
            , at the same Time. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Place where the Fowls roosted had been broken open, and the Fowls carried and laid under a Bank, in a Field hand by, and the Prisoner was apprehended near them, at two a Clock in the Morning. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d. upon each Indictment
            .
