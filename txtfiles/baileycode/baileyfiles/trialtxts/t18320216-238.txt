





745. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BRINKLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of January
, 1 shirt, value 3s.; 1 waistcoat, value 2s.; 2 shifts, unmade, value 5s.; 1 petticoat, value 18d.; 1 gown, value 1s., and 1 pair of stockings, value 18d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Poole
                        




                     
                  ; and that he had been before convicted of felony
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 27. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
