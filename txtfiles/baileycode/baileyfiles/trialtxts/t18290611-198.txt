





1197. 
               
                  RICHARD 
                  ROBINSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of May
, 1 pair of shoes, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Aaron
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  CAPPS
               



            . I am in the employ of John Aaron, a pawnbroker
. On the 22d of May I was in the shop, and saw the prisoner take the shoes; I went out and secured him; they had been hanging at the door - he dropped them; some person took them up and brought them in -I never lost sight of him.
FRANCIS KEYS. I took the prisoner in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
