





1646. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     READING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of August
, 2 gowns, value 5s.; 2 pairs of trousers, value 2s.; 3 waistcoats, value 1s. 6d.; I quilt, value 2s.; and 1 petticoat, value 1s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Benjamin 
                           Loring
                        



                     
                  ; and 2 shifts, value 3s.; 1 gown, value 1s., and 2 window-curtains, value 1s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Elizabeth 
                           Loring
                        



.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 34.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
