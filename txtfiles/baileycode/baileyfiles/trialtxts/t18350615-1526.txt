



1526. 




MARY BIRDSILL
 was again indicted

for stealing, on the 
21st of May, 1 shawl, value 3s., the goods of 



Louisa Sims and another.






LOUISA SIMS
. I live with my father—the prisoner went away without notice, on the 21st of May—I then missed a shawl—this is it.





BENJAMIN MEEHAM
. I am in the service of a pawnbroker—this shawl was pawned with me—I believe, by the prisoner, in the name of Jackson—she has had an affidavit for it since.

Prisoner. It was her sister's shawl, and was lent me three days before.





LOUISA SIMS
. I never lent it her, she had nothing to do with the rooms, where my sister and I lived,




GUILTY
. Aged 18.—


Transported For Seven Years.
