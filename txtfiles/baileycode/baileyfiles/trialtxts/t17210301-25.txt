







                     David 
                     Forster 
                  



               
            , of Norton Falgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 32 Yards of Garlick Holland value 24 s. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Patterson 
                        




, on the 5th of February
 last. It appeared that the Prosecutor being in his Compting-house, and his Servant behind a Pile of Goods marking some of them; that the Prisoner supposing no body had been there, came in and took the piece mentioned in the Indictment from off the said Pile, and was followed and taken. The Prisoner called two Masters he had workt with to his Reputation. The Jury considering the whole matter, found him 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
