





430. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 20th of June
, a muslin shawl, value 3s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Hilton
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM
                   HILTON
               



             sworn.
I am a linen-draper
, in Oxford-street


: On Monday last the 20th of June, between three and four o'clock, I saw the prisoner tear off a muslin shawl from an iron that was made use of to hang them upon; she wrapped it in her cloak, and went away with it, I went out, and brought her back to the shop, through the passage, and took it from under her cloak; I sent for an officer, and she was taken to Marlborough-street.


                  DAVID
                   COLE
               



             sworn.
I had this shawl from the last witness, I have had it ever since, (produces it).

Prosecutor. This is my shawl, I saw her take it.
Prisoner's defence. I was going along Oxford-road, and a woman asked me to carry it for her, I did not know there was any thing in it.



               GUILTY
            . (Aged 23.)




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. RECORDER.
