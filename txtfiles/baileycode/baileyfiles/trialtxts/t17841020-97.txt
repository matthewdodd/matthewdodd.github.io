





1027. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     TREES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of October
, four live pigs, value 8 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Emmett
                  




               
            .


                  Samuel
                   Wheatley
               



            , a watchman, met the prisoner at half past four, with a knapsack on his back, with four dead pigs, in it which were the prosecutor's.


                  William
                   Thomlinson
               



            , the constable, confirmed the above, and said the prisoner gave different accounts of them.
(The pigs deposed to by the Prosecutor.)
Prisoner. I bought them coming to London; I am a disabled old man.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
