





141. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     CHANDLER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of October
, 2 saws, value 5s.
            , the goods of the 
               
overseer of the poor of the parish of St. Martin in the Fields




            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  WEST
               



            . I am one of the masters of St. Martin's workhouse. On the 26th of October the prisoner came for relief - he was quite drunk, and conducted himself in a very disorderly manner; it took four men to convey him to the watch-house.


                  GEORGE 
                  WADDINGTON
               



            . I am an officer. I took this duplicate for two saws from the prisoner in the watch-house - he said he had stolen them from the carpenter's shop at the workhouse.


                  THOMAS 
                  STRUTT
               



            . I am a carpenter at St. Martin's workhouse


. These saws are the property of the parish, and were kept in the workshop there.
Prisoner. Q.Were they locked up? A. Yes, the shop was locked.


                  JEREMIAH 
                  BEATON SMITH
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I took in these saws from the prisoner, and gave him this duplicate.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven years
            .
