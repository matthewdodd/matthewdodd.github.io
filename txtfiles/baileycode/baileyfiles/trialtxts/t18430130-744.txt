



744. 




JAMES LICKFOLD
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
23rd of Jan. 3/4 lb. weight of Indian rubber, value 2s., the goods of 



John Faulkner.






SARAH LEFACE
. I live with my uncle, John Faulkner, a stationer, in Queen-street, Tower-hill. About half-past nine o'clock, on the 23rd of Jan., I was in the kitchen, and heard a click of a lock, and saw the prisoner running out from behind the counter—I ran, and caught him three doors off—he dropped this piece of Indian rubber, which is my uncle's.





WILLIAM LOOKER
. I saw the prisoner drop the Indian rubber.




GUILTY
. * Aged 14.—


Transported for Seven Years.
