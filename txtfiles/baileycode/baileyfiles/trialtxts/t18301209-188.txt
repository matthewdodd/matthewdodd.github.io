





188. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     LANDIGAN. alias 
HURLEY





               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of November
, I coat, value 10s., and I cloak, value 6s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Davis
                  




               
            .


                  MARY 
                  DAVIS
               



            . I am the wife of Joseph Davis; we live in Chapel-street, Soho


. On Friday, the 5th of November. I left my apartment to go in the kitchen, leaving my little girl there - she left to go to school, and left the door locked; when I returned the articles stated were gone - the officer brought them the next morning.


                  RICHARD 
                  JAMES JEFFERY
               



            . I am apprentice to a pawnbroker, in Drury-lane - the prisoner brought these things on the 5th of November, between three and half-past three o'clock - she said she brought them from a person named Morgan, a person we knew, and with whom the prisoner had lived - I had received previous information, and gave her in charge.
MARY DAVIS. These are my property - the person who took them might have gone in at the street door, and unlocked my door, which is up two pair of stairs; when I returned from the kitchen the drawers were opened, and I missed these articles.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 11. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
There were two other indictments against the prisoner.
