








                     Jonathan 
                     Becket
                  



               
            , Indicted for 


               stealing a Velvet Coat, value 5 l.
             from 
               
                  
                     Michael 
                     Dickenson
                  




Esq
. The Evidence thus: A Woman meeting the said Becket with the Coat in his hand, and Mr. Dickenson charging him with the Felony, he the said Becket pray'd he would forbear to send him to Newgate, and he would pay him the price of the Coat in money. All the Prisoner could say in his own defence, was but little; upon which he was found 

               Guilty
            .
