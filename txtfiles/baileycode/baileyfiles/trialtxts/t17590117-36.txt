





104. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Celia 
                     Scott 
                  



               
            , spinster
, was indicted for 


               stealing one pewter chamber-pot, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Girdler 
                  




                
            , Jan. 8
. ++


                  Martha 
                  Girdler 
               



            . I am wife to 
               
                  John 
                  Girdler 
               



            , we live near the Seven Dials


; I missed a pewter-chamber-pot from off the stairs; the prisoner worked for a lodger of mine, and had come down the stairs a little before we missed it; we took her up and charged her with taking it; she confessed she had taken it.


                  Thomas 
                  Woodard 
               



            . I am constable: I had the prisoner in charge; she confessed she had stole the chamber-pot, and took me to her lodgings in Holbourn, where the pot was in a closet.
Prisoner's defence.
I worked in the prosecutrix's house with a lodger there; I was coming down stairs and wanted to carry a little water home, so took that pot; but did design to bring it again.



               Guilty
            .
