



71. 




THOMAS GREENWOOD
 was indicted

 for stealing on the 
19th of November 5 pair of shoes, value 20s. the goods of 




Henry Bonham.






JOSEPH KIENEY
. I am shopman to Mr. Henry Bonham shoe maker, on the 19th of November and when I got next door but one to my master's shop, I saw the prisoner with these shoes—I said "Halloo there"—he therw them down and ran awn—I saw him stopped in Greek street—these are the shoes—they had hung at the door by a string, which out once before—he had a handkerchief thrown over the shoes.




GUILTY
 Aged 19—


Transported for seven Years.
