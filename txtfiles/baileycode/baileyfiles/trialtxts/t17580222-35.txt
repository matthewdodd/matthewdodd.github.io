





142. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Godfry 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing two aprons, one silver tea spoon, one silver salt, one copper lamp, one linen handkerchief, three linen shifts, one pair of bellows, and one tin kettle
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Jesset 
                  




                
            , Jan. 11
. ++
The prosecutor lives in Kingsland Road


, at the Gentleman and Porter, whence the goods mentioned were missing. The prisoner being suspected was taken up, and confessed she took them, and where she had pawn'd them.
The prisoner had nothing to say in her defence.




               Guilty, 10 d.
