





365. (2d L.) 
               
                  
                     PETER
                      BARRETT
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, a linen handkerchief, value ten pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Dalphan Micah 
                     Andre
                  




               
            , February 27th
 ++.
Dalphan Micah Andre. Going down Thames Street


, I stopped at a gate way to let a cart go past and the prisoner came and took my handkerchief out of my pocket, I turned round and laid hold of him by the collar and found my handkerchief in his hand.
Prisoner's Defence.
The gentleman droped the handkerchief, I took it up and was going to give it him.



               Guilty
            .
