



1807. 




JOHN CAFFERY



, stealing 1 handkerchief, value 3s. 6d.; the goods of 



John Nightingale Hotson, from his person.






JOHN NIGHTINGALE HOTSON
. On 12th July 1 was going by Victoria-street, Holbom-bridge—I saw by the shadow a hand pass my pocket—I wened immediately, and saw the primmer with his right hand behind him—

he dropped this handkerchief (produced) from his hand—it is mine—there was no one near me who could have taken it but him.

Prisoner's Defence. I was turning the corner; several persons were between us; he turned and said I had picked his pocket.




GUILTY
.* Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years.
