





1278. 
               
                  BENJAMIN 
                  RAY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of May
, 3 pewter pots, value 2s. 8d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Meecham
               




            .
THOMAS MEECHAM. I keep the Duke of Cumberland public-house, Red Lion-street, Spitalfields
; the prisoner was brought in on the 10th of May, with a pint pot under his coat; I fetched a constable, who found two quart pots on him.


                  JOHN 
                  FITZGERALD
               



            . On the 10th of May I was at my house, No. 3, Red Lion-street; I saw the prisoner go up the steps of No. 2, and take some pots from the window cill; he had sweep's clothes on; I secured him, and took him to Meecham's; he said he was going to take them to the public-house, but he was going the wrong way - he dropped one.


                  THOMAS 
                  HART
               



            . I am a constable, and took him in charge.


                  THOMAS 
                  GOODLAD
               



            . I am a baker. I saw the prisoner and witness going along; I saw the prisoner take one pot from under his clothes, and put it on a potatoe sack; I took it to the watch-house.


                  ELIZABETH 
                  PRATT
               



            . I live in Red Lion-street. On the 10th of May I put some pots out of the window.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
