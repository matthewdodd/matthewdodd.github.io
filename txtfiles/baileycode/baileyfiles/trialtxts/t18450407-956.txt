



956. 




MICHAEL HEAVY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
20th of Jan., 1 cash-box, value 6s.; 40 sovereigns; 16 half-sovereigns; 2 £10 and 1 £5 Bank-notes, the goods and monies of 



Edmond Godfrey, his master; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 11.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Recommended to Pentonville Prison.
