





43. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     MILLS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of October
, 1 carpet bag, value 8s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Lowley
                  




               
            .
WILLIAM LOWLEY. I live at No. 6, Bear-street, Leicester-Square


. On the 26th of October, about a quarter before six o'clock in the evening; I was told something; I ran out, and saw the prisoner running off with this bag, which had been taken from my shop; I followed him; he threw it down; I took it up and still pursued him; I took him in three minutes without losing sight of him.


                  JOSEPH 
                  OSTELL
               



            . I am an officer; I took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
