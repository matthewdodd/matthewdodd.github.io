





25. 
               
                  EDWARD 
                  PERKINS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of November
, two sheets, value 10s.; one gown, value 3s.; three pair of stockings, value 3s.; one waistcoat, value 5s.; and one handkerchief, value 2d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Ann 
                  King
               




            .
ANN KING. I am a laundress
. On the 28th of November, about five o'clock in the afternoon, I missed the things off my lines, and found the prisoner lying down in the wash-house with them - I laid hold of him - He got away, but was taken immediately.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
THOMAS KING. I found the articles stated in the indictment under his clothes.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 32.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Bayley.
