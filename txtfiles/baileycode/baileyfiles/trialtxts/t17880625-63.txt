





465. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     GYLES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 26th of June
, ten pieces of cotton bed-furniture, value 5 s. and a cotton window curtain, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Philip 
                     Bowyer
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the things upon her.
Prisoner. I picked them up in the street.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. RECORDER.
