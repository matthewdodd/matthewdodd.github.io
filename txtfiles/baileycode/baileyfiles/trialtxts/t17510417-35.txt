                           James

                   did make an assault, putting him in corporal fear and danger of his life, one hat, value 2 s. from his person did steal, take, and carry away
            , April 10
.


                  James 
                  Strickland 
               



            . On the 10th of April, going through Red-lion-street, Goodman's-fields


, about half an hour after ten o'clock, the prisoner came behind me and snatched my hat-off and ran away.
Q. Did he say any thing to you, or strike you before he took it?
Strickland. No, he did not, my lord, he ran down into Quiet-row, there he was taken it being no thorough-fare, and the hat found lying within a yard and a half of his feet ; he was got into the mud.




               Guilty of felony only
            .
