





67. (L.) 
               
                  
                     JOHN
                      HODGES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value ten pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Nathaniel
                      Thawley
                  




               
            , December 7th
. ++


                  Nathaniel
                   Thawley
               



            . Last night, between six and seven o'clock, a I was going up Lombard-street, I felt my handkerchief go out of my pocket: I laid hold of the prisoner, and found my handkerchief in his hand.
The handkerchief was produced in court, and deposed to by the prosecutor.
Thomas Lane. I am a constable: I had charge of the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence.
I never had the handkerchief,



               Guilty
            



               T
            .
