





29. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     WRIGHT
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of October
, £30, in monies numbered, one bank-note for the payment of and value £15, four other bank-notes for the payment of £2 each, value £8, and seven other bank-notes for the payment of £1, value £7
            , the monies and property of 
               
                  
                     Mathias 
                     Prime
                  




               
            , Lucas, and others.
The prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
