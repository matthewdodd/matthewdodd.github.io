





1313. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  CONNER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Legg
                     




                  , from his person
            .
THOMAS LEGG. I am a hosier
, and was in Smithfield


 on the 20th of July, at twenty minutes past four o'clock - I felt a hand in my pocket - I turned and found my handkerchief on the ground - the prisoner was close to me - there were a great number of people, as two persons were preaching - this is my handkerchief.


                  FRANCIS 
                  FIELDING COLEMAN
               



            . I was there, and saw the prisoner and two others - the prisoner took this handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket - the prosecutor turned round, and the prisoner dropped the handkerchief by my side.
Prisoner's Defence. I was going through the street, and picked it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
