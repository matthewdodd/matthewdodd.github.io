







                     John 
                     Wilson , alias 
Smith




               
            , of St. Mary Wallbrook


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Padlock, Value 2 s.
             on the 5th of this Instant May
, the Goods of 
               

                        Edward 
                        Niel 
                     




                   and Comp







            .
- Savil a Watchman depos'd, That he saw the Prisoner and another Person, whom he took on Suspicion of having taken the Padlock from off the Prosecutor's Warehouse-Door; and the other Person getting away, he, with another Watchman searched the Prisoner, and found 8 picklock Keys in his Coat Pocket, but the Padlock he dropt in the Channel, which they afterwards found. This Evidence was corroborated by the Deposition of Ball, the other Watchman; who assisted in taking the Prisoner: The picklock Keys and the Padlock was produc'd in Court, and it appearing by several plain Circumstances that he took the Padlock with a design to rob the Warehouse, the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of single Felony.
