







                     Sarah 
                     Starling 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Peter le Poor


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Spoon
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Ambrose 
                     Harvey 
                  




                
            , the 11th of this Instant May
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was employ'd by the Prosecutor as a Washer-woman
; and the Spoon was missing the next Day, after she had been there; and the Prisoner was apprehended offering, the Spoon to Sale, being suspected not to have come honestly by it. The Prisoner pleaded, The Spoon was given to her by the Maid, with broken Victuals. But this thing contradicted by the Maid, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
