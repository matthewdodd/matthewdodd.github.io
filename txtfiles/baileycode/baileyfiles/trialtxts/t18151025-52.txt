





1031. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     STAGG
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 27th of September
, a shift, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Eglar
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZABETH 
                  EGLAR
               



            . I lost my shift on the 27th of September; the prisoner came in, and took it; I saw him go out with it; I did not see him come in; I only saw him going out with it; I cried stop thief; he threw them away, and he was stopped; I picked them up.


                  JAMES 
                  VALLENTINE
               



            . I heard a cry of stop thief, and stopped the prisoner.
Prosecutrix. That is my shift.



               GUILTY
            , aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
