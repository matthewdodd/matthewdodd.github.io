



547. 




JOHN BROWN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
22nd of January, 2 gowns, value 17s., and 2 aprons, value 1s.; the goods of 



Mary Ann Sutton; and I gown, value 5s., the goods of Corbet Cook.






MARY ANN SUTTON
. I live in Hanover-place, Old Kent-road, in the service of Mr. Corbet Cook—I hung these things out to dry, on the 22nd of January, in the middle of the day—the yard is inclosed by a wall and paling—I missed them about six o'clock in the evening—the policeman afterwards gave me information—these are the things—(looking at them)—





JAMES HARRIS
. (police-constable C 73.) About twenty minutes past seven o'clock in the evening, I met the prisoner in Gracechurch-street, with a bundle—I stopped him, and found these things on him—he was remanded, and next day I found the owner.

Prisoner's Defence. I found the things tied up in a handkerchief.




GUILTY
.* Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
