







                     Thomas 
                     Bradbourn 
                  



               
            , of Aldermanbury


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Camblet
            , the Goods of 
               

                        John 
                        Snee 
                     




                   and Company




            , the 31st of August
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner was Footman
 to the Prosecutors, and took the Opportunity to steal the Goods, and had sent them down by the Carrier to a Brother of his at Newport in Shropshire , which Goods were taken upon the Carrier at St. Alban's, directed by the Prisoner's own Hand.
The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s.
