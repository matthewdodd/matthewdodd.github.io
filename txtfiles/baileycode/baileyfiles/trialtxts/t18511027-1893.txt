




1893. 




WILLIAM CHERRY
 and 




WILLIAM KINSBURY



, stealing 1 mare, value 30s.; the property of 



James Bartlett: Kinsbury having been before convicted: to which


CHERRY pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 19.—



Confined Twelve Months.


KINSBURY pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
