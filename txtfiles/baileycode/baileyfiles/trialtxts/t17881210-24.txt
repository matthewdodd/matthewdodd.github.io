





24. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     FONSECA
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of November
 last, one wooden washing tub, value 2 s. eight clouts, value 8 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Christian 
                           Dover
                        



                     
                  , widow
; and one wooden pail, value 14 d.
             the property of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Johnson
               



            .
The things were found at a Mrs. Taylor's, which the prisoner confessed taking.




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
