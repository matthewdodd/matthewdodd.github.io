




1697. 




LYDIA FORD
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
30th of May, 1 pair of boots, value 2s. 6d.; 1 petticoat, value 1s. 1 pair of stockings, value 6d.; and 1 bag, value 6d.; the goods of 



Thomas Pankhurst.






ANN PANKHURST
. I am the wife of Thomas Pankhurst; we live in Banner-street, St. Luke's; the prisoner lived servant with the landlord of the house—I went out on Friday, the 28th of May, and remained away till the following Thursday—I had left the key of our apartment with the landlord, to give to my husband—when I came home I found the prisoner was in custody for robbing her master, and on going to my room I missed the articles stated—they were afterwards found upon the prisoner—they are mine.





HARRIET HATWARD
. I searched the prisoner, and took these articles off her.




GUILTY
. Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Recommended to the Penitentiary.
