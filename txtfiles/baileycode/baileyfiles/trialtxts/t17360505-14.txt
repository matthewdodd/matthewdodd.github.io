





16. 
               
                  
                     Martin 
                     Wright 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               privately stealing a silk Handkerchief value 12 d. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Robert 
                           Dennet 
                        




, Esq
; in the Parish of St. Michael Cornhill



February 3d
.


                  Robert 
                  Dennet 
               



            . The 3d of February, going down the Poultrey


, the Prisoner and another rushed by me, one of each side: I immediately missed my Handkerchief, and saw one of them go up towards Cheapside, the other towards the Exchange. I followed the Prisoner, took hold of him, and told him he had pick'd my Pocket: I saw him drop this Handkerchief, and it has my Name upon it.
- Saunders. I saw him drop the Handkerchief exactly against Stocks Market, from his left Side. 


               Guilty 10 d.
