





1031. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     POWELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of February
, 3 table-cloths, value 9s.; 2 petticoats, value 3s.; 3 shirts, value 9s.; 3 shifts, value 9s.; 4 handkerchiefs, value 7s., and 1 apron, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Matthew 
                     Fletcher
                  




               
            .


                  SARAH 
                  FLETCHER
               



            . I am the wife of Matthew Fletcher- he lives in John's-place, Islington-green


; I take in washing - the prisoner was in my service; I gave her 7s. a week, and her board, but she did not sleep there. I missed some articles on the 25th of February. and asked her about them - she denied it; I sent her home with a basket of linen, and that she did not carry home.


                  GEORGE 
                  ELLIS
               



            . I am a Police-constable. On the 27th of February the prisoner was given into my custody- I searched her, and saw her throw away the duplicates of this property.


                  THOMAS 
                  HARVEY
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have a number of articles pawned by the prisoner.


                  GEORGE 
                  BARBER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have two shifts and three handkerchiefs pawned by the prisoner -I gave her these duplicates.(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner put in a written Defence, stating that she had pledged the articles by the prosecutrix's desire.
SARAH FLETCHER. I never sent her to pawn any thing.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 47. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
