







                     Tho.
                      Bedder
                  



               
            , of St. James's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Boots, value 15 s and a pair of Shoes value 4 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Tho.
                      Unwin
                  




               
            , the 16th of June
 last. The Evidence deposed that the Boots hung up in the Stoke-hole of a Brewhouse, and that the Prisoner coming here with a Cart to fetch away the Grains stole them, and they were found lock'd up in his Box at home. However the Prisner deny'd the Fact, and said they were given him to lay up by his Fellow-servant. But this not appearing, the Jury found him 

               guilty
            . 


               To be Transported
            .
