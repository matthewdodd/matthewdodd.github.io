



2037. 




JOSEPH GIBBS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
16th of September, 1 basket, value 1s.; 1 butter cloth, value 6d.; and 25lbs. weight of butter, value 1l. 9s.; the goods of 



John Worters.—2nd COUNT, stating them to be the goods of 



Samuel Steer.—3rd COUNT, stating them to be the goods of 



Thomas Acock; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 32.—


Transported for Seven Years.
