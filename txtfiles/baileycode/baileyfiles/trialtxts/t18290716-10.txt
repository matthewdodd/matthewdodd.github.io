





1385. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     BENTHAM
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of April
, 9 yards of woollen cloth, value 3l.; 1 waistcoat, value 3s.; 1 pair of gaiters, value 2s.; 1 razor, value 2s., and 1 razor-case, value 3d., the goods of 
                     
                        John 
                        Atkins
                     




                  , his master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
