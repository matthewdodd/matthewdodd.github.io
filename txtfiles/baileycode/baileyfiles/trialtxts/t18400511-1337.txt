



1337. 




GEORGE COYLE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
5th of May, 1 half-crown, 9 shillings, 21 sixpences, and 8 groats, the monies of 



Henry Borton; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
.* Aged 9.—


Transported for Seven Years—Isle of Wight.
