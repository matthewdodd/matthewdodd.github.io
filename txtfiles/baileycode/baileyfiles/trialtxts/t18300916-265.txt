





1729. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     THOROGOOD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of August
, 1 tub, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Beal
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  BEAL
               



            . On the 30th of August, about ten o'clock in the morning, I missed a tub from my shop; I had seen it a quarter of an hour before - a person came and told me of it; I went up the street, as I was directed, and found the prisoner fifty yards off with it.


                  RICHARD 
                  MILLER
               



            . I took charge of the prisoner; the prosecutor took the tub back - the prisoner said he had no tub; at that time the man had it again.
Prisoner's Defence. It was out at the door, and distress drove me to do it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 35. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
