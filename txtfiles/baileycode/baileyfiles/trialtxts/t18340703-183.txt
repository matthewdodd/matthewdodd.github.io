





1075. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  JONES
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of June
, 1 jacket, value 5s.; 1 hat, value 3s.; and 1 handkerchief, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Herbert 
                  Rawlins
               




            .
HERBERT RAWLINS. I live with my parents; my father is in the 2nd Life Guards. On the 29th of June, I was at my uncle's premises, at Kilburn


, hay-making
, and leftthe articles stated, in the field - I missed them, and a servant told me that a man had gone off with a bag - I pursued and found the prisoner three quarters of a mile off, with my hat on his head, and my jacket in his bag - I called to him - he ran off, and was taken, when I called, "Stop thief" - these are my things.


                  RICHARD 
                  CHARD
               



            . I am a cow keeper and farmer - I saw this boy run after the prisoner, and he was taken.
Prisoner's Defence. A man said I might have them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 38. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
