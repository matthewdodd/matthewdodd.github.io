



2333. 




JOHN SIMS
 was again indicted

for stealing, on the 
28th of September, 1 printed book, value 7s., the goods of 



Christopher Mudie and another.






PETER MUDIE
. I am partner with Christopher Mudie, of St. Martin's-court. I lost a book on the 28th of September—it was safe at half-past four o'clock in the afternoon—I went out, and returned about five o'clock, and it was then gone—I know nothing of the prisoner—this is our book.





JOHN EDWARD NEEDES
. The prisoner brought this book to our shop on the 28th of September, and we took it in—he was not detained then.




GUILTY
. Aged 31.—


Transported for Seven Years.
