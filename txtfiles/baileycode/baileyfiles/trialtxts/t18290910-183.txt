





1713. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     FISHER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of September
, 2 sow pigs, value 3l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Heard
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 55.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
