



1483. 




ANN SULLIVAN
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
2nd of June, 4 handkerchiefs, value 14s., the goods of 



Robert Everett Linsey and another.






ROBERT EVERETT LINSEY
. I am a linen-draper—I have a partner—we live in Great Titchfield-street. I saw the prisoner in my shop on the 2nd of June, and saw her extract a piece of silk handkerchief from the window—I followed her, and accused her of it—she denied having them—I took these four handkerchiefs from under her shawl—she would not come back with me, and I gave her in charge—they are ours.





JOHN LEGGATT
 (police-serjeant E 4.) I took the prisoner into custody.

Prisoner. They were given to me.




GUILTY
.* Aged 19—


Transported for Seven Years.
