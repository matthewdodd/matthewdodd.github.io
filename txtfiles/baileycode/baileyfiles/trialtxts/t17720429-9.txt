





326. (2d. M.) 
               
                  
                     MARY
                      WEBSTER
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a linen gown value 14 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Sarah
                      Chance
                  




               
            , April 18th
. +


                  Sarah
                   Chance
               



            . I keep a shop
 in Spittal-fields


; the gown mentioned in the indictment I had pinned up at my door for sale on the 18th of April. About ten in the morning 
               
                  Sarah
                   Bignell
               



             informed me that a woman had taken this gown; I followed the prisoner and overtook her about 30 yards from my house as she was turning the corner; she had my gown in her apron, (produced and deposed to.)


                  Sarah
                   Bignell
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the gown as it hung at Mrs. Chance's door; I went over the way and informed her of it, and she pursued and took the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence.
I did not take the gown down, I saw it on the ground and picked it up.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
