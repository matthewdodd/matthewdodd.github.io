





162. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     DOWNING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of October
, 51 shirts, value 4l. 18s. 3d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Robinson
                  



               
            .
SECOND COUNT, stating them to be the goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Mott
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     Bartholomew Peter 
                     Drowett
                  



               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  HUTCHINSON
               



            . I am assistant to Messrs. Mott and Drowett, contractors for Lambeth workhouse

; 
               
                  John 
                  Robinson
               



             supplies them with articles, which we employ the poor to work up - the prisoner was a pauper
. On the 10th of October a bundle, containing these shirts was delivered to him to take to Mr. Robinson, in the Commercial-road; they belong to Mr. Robinson; we have never seen them since.


                  THOMAS 
                  LAWRENCE
               



            . I put the fifty-one shirts into a bag, and gave them to the prisoner to take to Robinson's; he went as far as London-bridge


 with me - I told him and another man to go on while I went to Houndsditch; I did not see him again till the 19th of November, when he was in custody.


                  WILLIAM 
                  WHITE
               



            . I live with Mr. John Robinson. -These shirts were never brought there.
Prisoner's Defence. I put them down in a public-house while I got half a pint of beer, and they were gone.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 26. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
