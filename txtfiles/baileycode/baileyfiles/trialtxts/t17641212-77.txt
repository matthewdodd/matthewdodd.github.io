





84. (M.)

                     Doe 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a cloth coat, value 3 s.

            , December 14

                  Thomas 
                  Hanks 
               



            . I keep the Angel in St. Giles's




. The prisoner came to my house, and had a pennyworth of purl: when he was gone, I missed a great coat; I pursued him, and catched him in Monmouth-street, with the coat upon him.
