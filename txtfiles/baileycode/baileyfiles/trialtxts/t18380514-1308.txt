



1308. 




GEORGE WATKINS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
1st of May, 1 pewter pot, value 3s., the goods of 



Jacob Greave.






JACOB GREAVE
. I keep the Manchester Arms, in Adam-street, East. This pot (looking at one.) is my property.





WILLIAM LASCO
. I live in Berkeley-mews, Marylebone. On the evening of the 1st of May I missed a pot, which was standing outside the door—I saw the prisoner go past, I pursued him, and found this pot on him—I had not left above a minute—I just went inside the stable.




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.
