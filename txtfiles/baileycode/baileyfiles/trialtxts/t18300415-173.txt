





841. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     ELLIS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of February
, 1 silver mug, value 50s.; 1 glass decanter, value 7s., and 1 glass tumbler, value 18d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Alexander 
                     Smyth
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded.




               GUILTY
            . Aged 38. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
