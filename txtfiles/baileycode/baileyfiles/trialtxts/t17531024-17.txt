





458. (L.) 
               
                  Sabati 
                  Israel 
               



            , was indicted for 


               stealing one cotton handkerchief , val. 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  Jeremiah 
                  Freeman 
               




             , Sept. 13
. 


                  Jeremiah 
                  Freeman 
               



            , Returning from 'Change about a Quarter after two o'clock, on the 13th of September, one Capt. Castleton told me, he beliv'd I had lost my handkerchief, shewing me one; I said it was mine. He then shewed me the prisoner, and said, he took it out of the prisoner's hand, he is not here, being then going abroad ; so I seized the prisoner, and took him before my Lord-Mayor.
Dineley Wardman. I was upon 'Change and saw the prisoner pick Mr. Freeman's pocket of this handkerchief, and Capt. Castleton took it out of his hand and delivered it to Mr. Freeman again.
The prisoner had nothing to say.



               Guilty
            .
