





1650. 
               
                  JANE 
                  HYDE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of the July
, 14 plates, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Pawley
               




            .
JOHN PAWLEY. I live in St. John-street


. On the28th of July, I received information and missed these plates, from outside my door; I followed and came up with the prisoner, about three hundred yards off, with these plates under her arm: she said a woman had told her to take them - she had not bought them.


                  JOHN 
                  ROBINSON
               



            . I am a street-keeper. Mr. Pawley gave the prisoner into my charge.
Prisoner's Defence. I was overtaken by a respectable looking woman, who asked if I knew a woman to clean a house down for her; I said I should be glad of the job - she said "Go home with me and bring these plates with you, they are paid for;" and I took them up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 53.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
