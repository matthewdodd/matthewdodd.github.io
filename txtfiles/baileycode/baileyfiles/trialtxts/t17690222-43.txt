





192. (M.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Barfoot 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a coachman's great coat, value 30 s. and a pair of leather breeches, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Daniel 
                     Green 
                  




                
            , Jan. 1
. ++


                  Daniel 
                  Green 
               



            . I lost a great coat and a pair of breeches out of Hemings's yard, about a month or five weeks ago. The prisoner had worked in the yard some time before.


                  James 
                  Chandler 
               



            . I live in James-street, Grovesnor-square. On New Year's day the prisoner came with this great coat on his back, and offered it to me sale. I bought it of him. ( Produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)


                  Mary 
                  Canning 
               



            . I keep a clothes shop. I bought these breeches of the prisoner the Thursday after Christmas day. (Produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)
Prisoner's Defence.
A man that worked with me got me to sell them, and I gave him the money.
Prosecutor. The prisoner had lived five years in the yard, but I never heard any ill of him before.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
