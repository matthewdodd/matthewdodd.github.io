





(L) 637. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD
                      LYNCH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value ten-pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Michael
                      Warren
                  




               
            , August 6th
. ++


                  Michael
                   Warren
               



            . Upon the 6th of August, coming up St. Dunstan's Hill


, I felt a twitch at my pocket. I turned round, and seized the prisoner. He got from me. I pursued him. I saw him drop the handkerchief. A gentleman took it up, and delivered it to me.
"The handkerchief was produced in court,
"and deposed to by the prosecutor."
Prisoner's Defence.
I never saw the handkerchief.



               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
