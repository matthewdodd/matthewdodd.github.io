




165. 




RICHARD IVE



, burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 



Robert Baker, on 
7th Nov., at 
Lambeth, and stealing 1 seal, 1 coat, 2 frocks, and other articles, value 3l. 10s., his property:—also, 

for stealing in the dwelling-house of 



Robert Baker, on 
14th Sept., 1 coat and other articles, value 3l. 17s., 4 shillings, and 1 sixpence, his property; and afterwards burglariously breaking out of the said dwelling-house: to which be pleaded 



GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Ten Years.
