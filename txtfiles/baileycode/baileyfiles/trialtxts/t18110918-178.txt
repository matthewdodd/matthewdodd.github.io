





799. 
               
                  
                     ROWLAND 
                     MATTHEWS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that he on the 9th of September
, unlawfully, knowingly, and designedly did falsely pretend to 
                     
                        Carolina 
                        Heaton
                     



                  , spinster, that he came from

                        Thomas 
                        Roberts
                     



                  , for a dozen of plated spoons, and a dozen of plated tea-spoons to match the table-spoons, and by means of which said false pretence he did obtain from Carolina Heaton, one dozen of table-spoons, value 12 s. and one dozen of tea-spoons, value 6 s. with intention to defraud 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Heaton
                        




.
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
