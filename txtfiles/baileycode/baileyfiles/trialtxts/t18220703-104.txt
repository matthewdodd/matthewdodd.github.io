





1093. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     WESTLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of July
, three pounds of soap, value 18 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Burnett
                  




               
            .


                  FLOCKTON 
                  BUTLER
               



            . I am apprenticed to Mr. Leach, a grocer, of Union-street, Bunhill-row. On the 2d of July, I saw the prisoner walking by our window, in Steward-street; it is a corner shop - I watched him into 
               
                  Henry 
                  Burnett
               



            's shop, ran across the road, and saw him take a cake of soap off the counter - I went in, laid hold of him, and he dropped it. I secured him, and picked it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
