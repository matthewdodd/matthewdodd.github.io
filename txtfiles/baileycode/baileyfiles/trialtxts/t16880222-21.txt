







                     James 
                     Jordan
                  



               
             of St. Matin's in the Fields


, was Indicted for 


               Breaking open the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           Matthew 
                           Stanno
                        




                     
                  , on the 17th. of January
 last, at Two in the Morning, and with other Persons stealing thence two Beaver Hats, value 3 l. two Caster Hats, value 20 s. Eleven dozen of Cordubeck Hats, value 44 l. and 20 yards of Ribbon Galloone
            . It appeared, that he had confessed the Fact before the Justice, upon which his Examination was read, to which he had set his hand, and that he had his share of the said goods; upon which he was brought in 


               guilty of Felony
            .
