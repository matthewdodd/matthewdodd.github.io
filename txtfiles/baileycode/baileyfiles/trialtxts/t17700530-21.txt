





365. (M.) 
               
                  
                     John
                      Knight
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a copper tea-kettle, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Cameron
                  




               
            , May 7
. ++


                  Sarah
                   Cameron
               



            . I am wife to 
               
                  John
                   Cameron
               



            . I live in New-court, facing St. Clement's church


, the and keep a public house
; the prisoner lodged with us about two months. I missed tea-kettle. I took him up the 5th of May. I found it again at Mr. Rotchford's, pawned in the prisoner's name. (Produced and deposed to).
Mr. Ratchford. The prisoner pledged this with me.
Prisoner's Defence.
The maid lent me the tea-kettle full of water; I did pawn it. I am a sawyer
 by trade. I had a little boy dead in the hospital of the smallpox. My wife was ill of a fever, and I was out of work and owned a quarter's rent. I did intend to have put it in its place again.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
