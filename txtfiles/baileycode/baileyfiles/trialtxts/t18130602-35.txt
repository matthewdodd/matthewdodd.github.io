





550. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     THOMPSON
                  



               
             was indicted for that he, on the 20th of May, 


               feloniously had in his custody and possession, four 1 l. forged bank-notes, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            

First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
