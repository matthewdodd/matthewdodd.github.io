







                     John 
                     Burgess 
                  



               
            , of St. Katherine Creed Church


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 7 s. in Money, a Penknife, a Pair of Silver Buttons, &c. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Abraham 
                           Mortier 
                        




, the 20th of February
 last. The Fact being fully prov'd, and the Prisoner making but a trifling Defence, tho' he called some to his Reputation, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
