






427. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DAVY
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SIMPSON
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of September
, 16 s. in monies
            , numbered the monies of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Dibble
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM
                   DIBBLE
               



             sworn.
I keep a publick-house
, the Hoop and Bunch of Grapes in Cable-street


; I lost 16 s. I received an order for a pot of beer, and having no servant, I went with it myself; I suspected, and I shut the back-door, and I heard it rattle, and I saw Davy with it; I know nothing of the other prisoner.
Both 

               GUILTY
            .





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. 
               
                  BARON
                   HOTHAM
               



            .
