







                     Robert 
                     Heath 
                  



               
            , of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Broad-Piece val. 23 s.
             the Money of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Drury 
                  




                
            , on the 26th of June
. It appear'd that the Prosecutor keeps an Alehouse
 at Mile-End


, and the Prisoner was his Lodger, that about Ten at Night, the Prosecutor and his Wife fell asleep together in the Chimney Corner, and did not awake till after Eleven, when feeling in his Pocket, he miss'd a Broad-Piece, which was there when he went to sleep. The Prisoner being suspected, was examined; he confess'd he took it; but said he found it lying upon the Dresser. 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
            .
