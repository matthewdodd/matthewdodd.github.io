





1712. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     LEGG
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of July
, 1 jacket, value 4s.; 1 pair of breeches, value 20s,; 2 shirts, value 10s.; 1 pair of drawers, value 1s.; 6 cravats, value 6s., and 2 sovereigns
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Loader
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
