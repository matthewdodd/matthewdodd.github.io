







                     John 
                     Baugh 
                  



               
            , of St. Andrew's Holbourn


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Copper Pot-Lid, a Copper Sauce-Pan, a Pair of Bellows, and several Books
            , on the 5th of August
 last, the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Pocklington 
                  




                
            . It appear'd, That the Prisoner was very officious at the Fire, at Hatton-Garden, and pretended to help off with the Goods of the affrightned Sufferers, but one Mr. Lloyd seeing him loaded with Goods, and remembering he had seen him in Newgate, suspected he had stolen them, and apprehended him on Suspicion, when the Goods with which he was taken, appeared to be the Goods of the Prosecutor, the Fact appearing plain, the Jury found him 

               Guilty
             of single Felony.
