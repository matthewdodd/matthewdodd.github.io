





1656. 
               
                  EDWARD 
                  EVANS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of October
, three umbrellas, value 12 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  James 
                  Wood
               




            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  KNOWLES
               



            . I am in the employ of James Wood, pawnbroker
, of Islington


. On the 10th of October he had six umbrellas stolen. I received information and went into the street - I ran down Pied-bull-yard, and saw the prisoner with three umbrellas under his arm, wrapped up in the cover of another umbrella - he turned the corner, and put them down - I collared him, and brought him back - he was delivered to the officer.


                  DANIEL 
                  WARREN
               



            . I am headborough. I took the prisoner and have the property.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  ANN 
                  RAVER
               



            . I was coming past the shop and my child's bonnet blew off - I turned to pick it up and saw the prisoner take the umbrellas from the door - I gave information.
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 50.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
