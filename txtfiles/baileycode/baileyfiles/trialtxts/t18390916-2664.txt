



2664. 




THOMAS LOW
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
21st of August, 1 pair of shoes, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 



Thomas Purvis; and that be bad been before convicted of felony.






WILLIAM BUCKWELL
. I am shopman to Thomas Purvis, of High-street, Deptford. On the 21st of August I saw the prisoner reach his hand in, take a pair of shoes, and run away with them—I am sure he is the person—they were not outside the door—I ran after him, crying, "Stop thief"—he was taken with them—these are the shoes—they are my master's.





BENJAMIN LOVELL
. (police-sergeant R 15.) The prisoner was given to me, with the shoes.





WILLIAM DYKE
. I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—he is the person.




GUILTY
 Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years.
