





NEW COURT, First Day.
Third Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1860. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     BAKER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of August
, 12 pairs of boots, value 5l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Reeve
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 38.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
