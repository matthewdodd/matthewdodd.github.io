





1550. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  WORCESTER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of June
, 20 yards of printed cotton, value 14s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Scott
               




            .
JOHN SCOTT. I am a linen-draper
, and live in High-street, Shadwell


. I received information on the 26th of June, between six and eight o'clock in the evening; I sent my young man out, and he brought back the prisoner, with this print in his apron.


                  WILLIAM 
                  PITT
               



            . I followed the prisoner, and found a piece of print on him, which had hung on a rail outside the shop. I told him he had taken a piece of print - he said he had not; I asked what was in his apron - he said Nothing - I found the print in it.


                  EDWARD 
                  STEARN
               



            . On the 26th of June, between seven and eight o'clock, I saw three men skulking about my window, at No. 226, High-street; I went out and saw the prisoner go to Mr. Scott's, and take the print; I could not leave my door, but I went to my next neighbours, and told them of it; the prisoner was brought back in about five minutes.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I saw this piece of print on the pavement beyond Mr. Scott's door.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
