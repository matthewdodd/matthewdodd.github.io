





2079. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     ROBINSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of October
, 3 shillings, the monies of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Marshall
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  MARSHALL
               



            . I am a sailor
. I was in Ratcliff-highway


 on the evening of the 28th of October - I was sober; the prisoner and another girl
 came and asked me to go home with them - I said No; they took hold of me. and the prisoner put her hand into my breeches pocket and went away; I put my hand down and missed my money, which was 3s. - I called out, and the prisoner was taken; I had not lost sight of her.
Prisoner. Q. Can you say I put my hand into your pocket? A. Yes.


                  SAMUEL 
                  DYER
               



            . I am a Police-officer. I was on duty - the prosecutor called out, and the prisoner ran; I caught her - the prosecutor appeared sober, and charged her with robbing him of 3s.; two other girls came up, and I heard the money rattling from the prisoner to another - I caught her hand, and found there 4s. 4 1/2d.
Prisoner's Defence. I saw a crowd, and this man was calling the Police - there were three or four girls; I heard money chinking - the Policeman caught me, andsaid he thought I was the girl; he took the money out of another girl's hand - the prosecutor said he did not think I was the girl.
JOHN MARSHALL. I did not say so - I swear she is the girl: it was between eleven and twelve o'clock.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
