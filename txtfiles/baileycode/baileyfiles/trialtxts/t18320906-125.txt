





1850. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     ROWLAND
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Field
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
JOHN FIELD. I have a situation in the Ordinance-office
; I was in Wilderness-row


, on the 21st of July, between seven and eight o'clock in the evening; I felt a twich at my pocket - I turned and saw the prisoner and another boy
, I pursued the prisoner, and he threw my handkerchief at my feet; this is it.
Prisoner's Defence. I had it given me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
