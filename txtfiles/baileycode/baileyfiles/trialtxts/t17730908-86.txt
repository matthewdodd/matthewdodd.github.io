





616. (2d. M.) 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH
                      HYATT
                  



               
            , spinster, was indicted for 


               stealing a child's linen frock, value 3 s. and a child's stuff skirt, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Matthews
                  




               
            , July 7th
. ++


                  Mary
                   Matthews
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  John
                   Matthews
               



            , the mother of the child who had these things: on the 7th of July, about three or four o'clock, she went out with these things upon her; we found her missing, as we supposed taken from the door; about seven she was returned stripped; I enquired after the things; I traced them into the custody of a pawnbroker.


                  John
                   Brooks
               



            . On the 7th of July in the afternoon, these goods were brought to my shop, and pawned by the prisoner.

Prisoner's Defence.
I know nothing about taking the things.
She called three witnesses to her character.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
There was another indictment against her for a crime of the same kind.
