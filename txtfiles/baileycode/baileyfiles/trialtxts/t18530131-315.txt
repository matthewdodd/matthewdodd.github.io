




315. 




JOHN FAHAY
 and 




THOMAS FINEY,



 burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling house of 



Thomas Billings, and stealing 4 lbs. weight of tea, and other articles, value 305., his goods: to which


FAHAY pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 14.—



Confined Twelve Months.


FINEY pleaded 


GUILTY
.** Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years.
