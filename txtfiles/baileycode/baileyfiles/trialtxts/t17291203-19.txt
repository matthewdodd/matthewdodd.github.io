







                     James 
                     Manly 
                  



               
            , of St. John's, Wapping


, was indicted for 


               stealing 2 Handkerchiefs, value 4 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Hashfield 
                  




                
            , November the 11th


The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner and one more, came with a pretence to buy Handkerchiefs, and had not been gone out of his Shop long, before he miss'd six Handkerchiefs, he went out and took the Prisoner, and found but two upon him.
The Prisoner in his Defence said, he was drinking at an Alehouse, and his Partner wanting some Handkerchiefs, he went with him to the Prosecutors Shop, and suppos'd that he (the Partner) put them into his Pocket: But that not being believ'd, and no body appearing to his Reputation, the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of Felony.
