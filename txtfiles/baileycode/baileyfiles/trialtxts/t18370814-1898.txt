



1898. 




JOHN SHIRTCLIFFE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
31st of January, 2 pairs of candlesticks, value 4l. 16s.; 1 other candlestick, value 1l. 4s.; 1 snuffers-stand, value 1l.; 1 bottle-frame, value 6l. 6s.; 1 waiter, value 2l. 14s.;—and 1 tea-pot, value 4l. 16s.; the goods of 



Thomas Bradbury and another, his masters: and also for embezzlement; to which indictments he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 28.—


Transported for Seven Years.
