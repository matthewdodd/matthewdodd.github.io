 last) fourscore Iron Bullets val. 40s. and 18 Iron Crows value 30s. the Goods of Captain

                        Applebey
                     




. The Evidence deposed, That How was found aboard the Ship, and he confest that he and Hunt had oftentimes stole great quantities of Iron-work from off several Ships, and that Hunt was with him at the Robbery; but his saying so did not affect Hunt; and there was no other Evidence against him; So he was 

               acquitted
            : But How was found 

               Guilty
