





498. 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Clarke 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary Whitechapel


, was indicted 


               for stealing a linnen waistcoat, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Alexander 
                     Bagnall 
                  




                
            , Sept 17
.


                  Alexander 
                  Bagnall 
               



            . I live in Rosemary Lane


, on the 17th of September, I lost a linnen waistcoat out of my window; it was pinned to some other things, and the prisoner came to the window to peep in, to see if he could see any thing; I saw him unpin the handkerchief, I took him and carried him to an officer's house, and there the waistcoat was taken from him.


                  Christopher 
                  Fisher 
               



            . I live in Rosemary Lane, over-against Mr. Bagnall.
Q. Was any thing found upon the prisoner?
Fisher. He had a waistcoat.
Q. Was it concealed?
Fisher. Yes, it was, there was nothing of it to be seen.
Q. To Bagnall. Is that your waistcoat?
Bagnall. Yes.



               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
