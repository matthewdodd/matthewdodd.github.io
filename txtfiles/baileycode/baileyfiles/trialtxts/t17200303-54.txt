







                     Elizabeth 
                     Upcott 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles in the Fields


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a black Callimancoe Coat, a Riding Hood, &c. in the House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Jones 
                        




, on the 23d o January
last. It appear'd that the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's Servant
, and took the Goods. She had nothing to say for her self, nor any to her Reputation. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s.
