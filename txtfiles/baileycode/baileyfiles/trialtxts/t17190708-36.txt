







                     John 
                     Birch 
                  



               
            , of St. Clements Danes


 , was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief value 2 s.6 d. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Sackvil 
                           Graves 
                        




, on the 26th of June
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that going by Temple-Bar he mist his Handkerchief, and no body being near him but the Prisoner, he search'd him and found it in his Pocket. The Prisoner pleaded he found it on the Ground; but the Prosecutor deposing it was very wet Weather, and no Dirt on the Handkerchief, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            , 


               Transportation
            .
