





1132. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     CLUTTERBUCK
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of August
, one suit of boy's clothes, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Morley
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  HUNT
               



            . I am servant to 
               
                  George 
                  Morley
               



            , who is a clothes-salesman
, and lives in Whitechapel


. On the 18th of August, these clothes hung on the door-post, pinned to a cloth. I heard an alarm, ran out, and a gentleman stopped the prisoner. He immediately dropped the clothes. I saw him running with them.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 26.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before J. Vaillant, Esq.
