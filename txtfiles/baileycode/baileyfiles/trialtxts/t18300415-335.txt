





001. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HAYS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of March
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Hugh 
                           Leslie
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  HERDSFIELD
               



            . On the 29th of March about one o'clock, I followed the prisoner from the Mansion-house, and saw him put his hand into the pocket of a gentleman who turned into the Bank


; the prosecutor then came by, and he took his handkerchief - an apple-woman called out; he then threw it behind him - I laid hold of him, and called the prosecutor.


                  HUGH 
                  LESLIE
               



            . I was near the Bank; I felt nothing at my pocket - an apple-woman called me; I missed my handkerchief - I found it on the pavement; I gave the prisoner into custody.
Prisoner's Defence. Two boy
s were walking behind the gentleman; one of them took the handkerchief and threw it behind - I caught it in my hand, and threw it on the ground; another lad was taken and was in Newgate, his friends have been drinking with the officer till two o'clock in the morning, to make it up.


                  CHARLES 
                  HERDSFIELD
               



            . I saw him take the handkerchief; another boy was taken with him, but the bill was thrown out.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
