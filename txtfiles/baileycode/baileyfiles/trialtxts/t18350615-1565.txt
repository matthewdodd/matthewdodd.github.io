



1565. 




GEORGE REED
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
10th of June, I tobacco-box, value 2d.; the goods of 



William Brown, from his person.






WILLIAM BROWN
. I am a Greenwich pensioner. On the 10th of June, I went to Greenwich fair—I had a tobacco-box, and the prisoner took it from my pocket—this is it.





WILLIAM YEOMAN
 (police-constable R 161.) I was on duty, and saw the prisoner—I watched him half an hour—I saw him follow a gentleman, and take his handkerchief three parts of the way out, and the gentleman stopped it—he then went to the prosecutor, and took out his tobacco-box—I took him with it.




GUILTY
. Aged 14.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Second Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
