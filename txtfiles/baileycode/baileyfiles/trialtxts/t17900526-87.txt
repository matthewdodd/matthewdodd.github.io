





525. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     BACCHUS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of April
 last, a cotton handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Nowell
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was seen taking the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
