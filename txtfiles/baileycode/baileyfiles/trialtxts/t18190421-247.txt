





747. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     THOMAS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of April
, 60 lbs. of lead, value 20 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Marshall
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  MARSHALL
               



            . I am a plumber
. On the 15th of April this lead stood at my door. I went to dinner about one o'clock, returned at two, and saw the prisoner walking by the door with the lead on his shoulder. I followed him, he threw it down, and I secured him without losing sight of him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  WILLIAM 
                  WIBER
               



            . I am a constable. I assisted in securing the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
