





215. 




THOMAS WATTS
 and 




THOMAS HART
 were indicted for

 stealing 5 waistcoats, value 15s.; and 1 pair of breeches, 9s., the goods of 



Edwin Richard Summerfield, and that they had been before convicted of felony; to which

WATTS pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 17.

HART pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 19.





Transported for Seven Years.
