







                     Richard 
                     Warren 
                  



               
            , of Tottenham


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a She-Ass, value 8 l.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Jennings 
                  




                
             , the 15th of May
 last.


                  John 
                  Eland 
               



             depos'd, It was Mr. Jennings's Ass, and taken from off a Common where it was feeding at Tottenham High-Cross


 .



                  John 
                  Hat 
               



             depos'd, That the Prisoner sold the Ass to him the 15th of May; and he suspecting he had stolen it, went to his Serjeant to inquire concerning him, and he being afterwards examined, confess'd that he found the Ass Grasing in a Lane, and drove it away. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
