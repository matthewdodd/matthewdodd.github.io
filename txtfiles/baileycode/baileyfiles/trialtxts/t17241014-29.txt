







                     David 
                     Kennier 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 3 Hundred Weight of Logwood
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Brailsford 
                  




                
            , the 4th of September
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Logwood was taken out of a Lighter and put into a Boat in the River of Thames. The Fact was plainly prov'd, and also by his own Confession. the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
