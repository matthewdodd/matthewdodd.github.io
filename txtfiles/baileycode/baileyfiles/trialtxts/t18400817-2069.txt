



2069. 




JOHN EDWARDS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
13th of August, 1 coat, value 20s. the goods of 



Allan Belling.






THOMS TIBBETT
. I am in the employ of Allan Belling, Esq., of Camden-road. On the 13th of August, about two o'clock, I brought my matter to the Strand in the carriage—I stood at the horses' heads, and saw the prisoner take the coat from the inside of the carriage—I made my way round, stopped him, and brought him to the shop where my master was—the prisoner had the coat on when I took him.





THOMAS BUTLER
 (police-constable D 166.) I took the prisoner, and have the coat.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.
