





155. (L.) 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM
                      BROWNE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Dowley
                  




               
            , Dec. 20
. ~


                  John
                   Dowley
               



            . About six in the evening of the 20th of December, going by St. Magnes church


, I found something at my pocket; I turned about and saw the prisoner with his hand in my pocket; I followed him, and when he had got about forty yards, he was secured by another person, and the handkerchief found close by his feet.

Prisoner's Defence.
I met the gentleman; I did not steal the handkerchief.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
