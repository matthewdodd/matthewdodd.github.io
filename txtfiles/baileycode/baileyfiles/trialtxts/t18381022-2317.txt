





2317. 




ELIZABETH WESTBROOK
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
19th of September, 1 coat, value 16s., the goods of 

Edward Magin;—

also, on the 
22nd of September, 1 shawl, value 18s.; 2 pairs of trowsers, value 7s.; 1 gown, value 4s. 6d.; and 1 handkerchief, value 6d.; the goods of 



Benjamin Thomas: to both of which she pleaded




GUILTY
.* Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years.
