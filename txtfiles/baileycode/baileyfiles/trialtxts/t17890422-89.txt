





374. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     ATKINS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 6th of January
, twenty pounds weight of leaden pipe, value 3 s. the property of 
                     
                        John 
                        Lisle
                     




                  , affixed to his dwelling-house
            , against the statue.


                  JOHN
                   LISLE
               



             sworn.
I am a house-keeper
, the lead was stole from the house in which I live, I lost it on the 6th of January, I don't recollect the day of the week, it was about eight o'clock in the morning; I saw it on the day before when the water came in, I knew it to be my property; one of the tenants gave me information of it's being stolen.
- WRIGHT sworn.
I am a watchman, I took the prisoner, he had a sack on his back; I took him with the property; I knew him before.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
