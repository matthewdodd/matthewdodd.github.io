





1624. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     HOOKER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of July
, 1 bird-cage, value 18d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Lydia 
                     Watts
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 32. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
