





2281. 




SAMUEL DICKINSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
23rd of July, 15 yards of satin, value 3l.; 34 yards of silk, value 4l. 19s.; 10 yards of linen-cloth, value 1l. 2s.; half a yard of lawn, value 1s. 6d.; 4 pairs of stockings, value 19s.; 1 shawl, value 2l. 10s.; 1 scarf, value 10s.; 1 purse, value 1s.; 1 pair of gloves, value 2s.; and 3 handkerchiefs, value 9s.;—also, 

on the 
1st of July, 5 handkerchiefs, value 15s.; and 1 pair of gloves, value 1s. 6d.:—also, 

on the 
25th of July, 2 handkerchiefs, value 8s., the goods of 



Griffith Thomas, his master: to both of which he pleaded 



GUILTY
. Aged 27.—


Transported for Seven Years.
