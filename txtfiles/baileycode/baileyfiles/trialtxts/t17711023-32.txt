





721. (L.) 
               
                  
                     George
                      Howell
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one linen handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert
                      Vale
                  




               
            , Oct. the 2 d
. ++


                  Robert
                   Vale
               



            . I lost my handkerchief in Guildhall


 on the 2d of October. I turned round and missed my handkerchief; the prisoner, who stood near me, was secured, and my handkerchief was taken upon him.


                  William
                   Payne
               



            . I saw the prisoner and another in Guildhall on the 2d of October; there was a gentleman lost his handkerchief. I seized the other man, the prisoner got out of the hall; I followed him and secured him, and this handkerchief was found upon him; he said it was his own, (produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)



               Guilty
            , 


               T
            .
