





225. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Francis 
                     Tipping 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing two calicoe shirts, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Adams 
                  




                
            , April 2
. ++


                  Richard 
                  Adams 
               



            . I am a shoe-maker
, and live in Kingsland-road; I lost two calicoe shirts out of a yard where they were drying, at Ironmongers-alms-houses


.


                  James 
                  Kendrick 
               



            . I found these shirts upon the prisoner by the side of London field; I pursued and took him there, (produced and deposed to) he kept running till I took him.
Prisoner's defence.
I was going by the alms-houses, and I picked up these shirts by the side of a wall; I saw two people follow me, and I stopped till they came up.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
