





825. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     EVENDEN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously having in his custody and possession, a forged bank note, well knowing it to be forged
            .
To which indictment, the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 36.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
