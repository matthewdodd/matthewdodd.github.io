






1281. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     GRAY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of September

, three books, value 7 l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Keys
                  



               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .
Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1282. 
               
                  JOSEPH 
                  GRAY
               



             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of October

, two pair of boots, value 3 l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Hedges
                  



               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 42.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
