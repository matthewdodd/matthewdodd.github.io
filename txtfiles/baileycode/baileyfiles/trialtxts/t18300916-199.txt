





1663. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     LEACH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of August
, 2 spoons, value 6d.; 1 salt-holder, value 6d.; 2 brushes, value 1s., and three knives, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Harding
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
