



1626. 




WILLIAM CORBY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
3rd of July, 1 handkerchief, value 2s. 6d. the goods of 



Charles Frederick Edgar, from his person.






CHARLES FREDRICK EDGAR
. On the 3rd of July I was in Clerkenwell—I had this handkerchief in my pocket, and lost it—I do not know who took it.





THOMAS HEADWORTH
. I live in St. John-street. About three o'clock, I saw the prosecutor walking in that street, and the prisoner was close behind him, touching his pocket—I went to the door, and saw his hand in the pocket—he drew the handkerchief out, and passed it form his right hand to his left—I immediately ran and laid hold of him—he threw it down—I told the gentleman, who took it up.

Prisoner. Q. Did you not say a man in a velvet coat threw the handkerchief down, and it went on my breast. A. I did not.




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
