





1271. 
               
                  
                     JONATHAN 
                     ELSOM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of June
, one pocket handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Lewis
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  LEWIS
               



            . I live in Greek-street, Golden-square. On the 20th of June, about five o'clock in the afternoon, I was in Brydges-street, Covent-garden


, and felt something at my pocket; I turned round, and saw the prisoner putting my handkerchief under his coat. There were two other men with him, who went away immediately. I seized the prisoner - He made a great resistance. I endeavoured to take him to Bow-street. - an officer came and took him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I found it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Life
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
