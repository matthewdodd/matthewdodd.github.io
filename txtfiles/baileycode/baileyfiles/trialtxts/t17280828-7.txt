








                     Ann 
                     Wiechard 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


 was indicted for 


               assaulting 
                     
                        Katharine 
                        Brandal 
                     




                   , a Child
 of six Years of Age on the Highway, and stripping her of a Linnen Frock and a Cap
            , on the 23d of Aug.
 last.


                  Henry 
                  Brandal 
               



             depos'd, That he sent the Child to School with the Cap and Frock on, and afterwards hearing she had been stripp'd he went and found her without both, but he could not say the Prisoner was the Person who took them.


                  Matthew 
                  Edwards 
               



             depos'd, That he seeing a Crowd about the Prisoner as a suspicious Person, went up to her and charg'd her with stripping the Child, and going to search her she pull d out the Frock, and the Cap they found upon her Head, she obstinately denied the Fact, but the Goods being found upon her, the Jury found 


               her guilty of Felony, but acquitted her of the Assault
            .
