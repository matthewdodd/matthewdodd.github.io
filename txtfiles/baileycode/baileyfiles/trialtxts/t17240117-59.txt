







                  William 
                  Welsh 
               



            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchres


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Ann 
                           Hopkins 
                        



                      
                  , and a Riding Hood, value 10 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Justin 
                           Cooper 
                        



, the 17th of January
. The Prosecutor depos'd, The Prisoner lodg'd with her, for some Time, and she took him for a very honest Man, and after he went away, a Woman came and ask'd her if she had lost any Goods, and carried her to a Pawn-Brokers, Mrs. Wallis, where she found her Goods. Mrs. Wallis depos'd, The Prisoner came and pawn'd the Goods to her. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, said they were pawn'd by another Person in his Name, and Mrs. Wallis was an ill Woman, and kept an ill House. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
