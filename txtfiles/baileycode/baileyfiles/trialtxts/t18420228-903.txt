



903. 




SAMUEL LEWIS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
5th of February, 1 half-crown, the monies of 



George Leicester Hillas, his master; also, on the 
12th of February, 3 shillings, the monies of George Leicester Hillas, his master; to both of which he pleaded





GUILTY.** Aged 12.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Convict Ship.
