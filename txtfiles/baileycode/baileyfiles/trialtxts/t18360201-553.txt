



553. 




WILLIAM CALVERT
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
27th of January, 4 handkerchiefs, value 16s., the goods of 



Mary Redit and another, and that he been before convicted of felony.






HENRY PARNELL
. I am assistant to Mary Redit and Emma Redit, Sidney-alley, Leicester-square, they are hosiers. On the evening of the 27th of January, the prisoner came into the shop—he asked if I had a handkerchief to match one which he brought in his hand—I showed him the nearest we had—he purchased one at 6s. 6d., and as was going out, I lifted up a little apron he had on, and found these four handkerchief of ours—I took him into custody—he said he was very sorry but he admired the patterns—they are worth 16s.,





RICHARD DUDLEY
 (Police-constable C 56) I took the prisoner in charge and the handkerchiefs.





WILLIAM PRAT
 (police-constable C 130) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction (read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 12—


Transported for Seven Years.
