             . I am a servant to Mr. Flower. On the 27th of January last two persons came into our shop, and asked for a pennyworth of cheese; my master is a cheesemonger
 . While I was serving them, a third person slipp'd in, and stole half a firkin of butter ; he was got about eight doors from the shop door; I ran after him, and took him with it upon his head.
