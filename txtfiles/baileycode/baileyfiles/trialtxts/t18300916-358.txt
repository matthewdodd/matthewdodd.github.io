





1840. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     VILLIERS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas Perronet 
                           Thompson
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            ; to which he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 38. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
