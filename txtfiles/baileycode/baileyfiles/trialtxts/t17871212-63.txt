





68. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     MORRIS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th day of November
 last, two damask table cloths, value 36 s. two lawn handkerchiefs, value 5 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Portal
                        



                     
                  ; one leather snuff box, covered with skin, value 6 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Samuel 
                           Colton
                        



                     
                  , Esq
; in the dwelling house of the said Joseph Portal
            .
The things were found in the prisoner's box.




               GUILTY, 39 s.
            





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. Justice HEATH.
