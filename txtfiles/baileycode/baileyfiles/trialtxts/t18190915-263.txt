





1358. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     BEWS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of August
, one coat, value 12 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Dawe
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  DAWE
               



            . I live in Montague-square


; the coat was on the rails, in the area, before the housekeeper's window - the gate was shut. Soon after, I was informed that it was gone, ran out, and secured the prisoner with it under his coat.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  ANN 
                  WHEELER
               



            . I was in the housekeeper's-room, and saw the prisoner hook the coat up with a hook.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
