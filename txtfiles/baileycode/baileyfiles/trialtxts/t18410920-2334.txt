



2334. 




JOHN ASHMAN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
11th of September, 10lbs. weight of horsehair, value 10s.; 1 pattern-case, value 1s.; 4 yards of damask, value 6s.; 2 yards of printed moreen, value 5s.; 3/4 of a yard of linen tick, value 1s.; 20 yards of gimp, value 7s.; 7 tassels, value 6s.; 3 yards of carpet, value 9s.; 119 yards of horsehair seating, value 15l.; 11 yards of canvas, value 5s.; and 6 yards of holland, value 4s.;—also, on the 
27th of August, 1 1/4 lb. of horsehair, value 1s. 3d.; the goods of 



William Thurnell; to both of which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 58.—


Transported for Seven Years.
