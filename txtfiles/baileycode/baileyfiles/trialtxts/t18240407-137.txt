





745. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of March
, a looking-glass, value 38 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Daniel Fenwick 
                     Elms
                  




               
            .


                  LYDIA 
                  WILLIAMS
               



            . I am servant to 
               
                  Daniel 
                  Fenwick Elms
               



            , who keeps a broker's shop
 in Grafton-street, Soho


. On the 13th of March, in the afternoon, I was in the parlour, behind the shop, and saw the prisoner walk out with a looking-glass, which had stood four feet inside the shop; I ran after him, took it from him, and held him till assistance came.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
             Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
