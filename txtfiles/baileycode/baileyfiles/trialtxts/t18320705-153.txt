





1576. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     LAWRENCE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of June
, 2 lbs. 10 ozs. of mutton, value 1s. 10d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Johnson
                  




               
            . - To which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 57. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
