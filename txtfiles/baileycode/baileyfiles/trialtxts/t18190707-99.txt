





1004. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DOWLING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of June
, one brass die, value 5 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Michael 
                     Rook
                  




               
            .


                  MICHAEL 
                  ROOK
               



            . I am a silversmith
, and live in Burleigh-court, Burleigh-street, Strand; the prisoner was my apprentice
; I found this die in his pocket. I had not given him leave to put it there.


                  JOHN 
                  MOUNT
               



            . I work for Mr. Rook. The prisoner told me that he took the die, and intended to steal it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
