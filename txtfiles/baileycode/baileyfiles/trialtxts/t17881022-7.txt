





613. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     JOHNSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of September
, one silver table-spoon, value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Rowley
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Leach
                  



               
            .


                  JOHN
                   LEACH
               



             sworn.
I only can prove the property.


                  GEORGE
                   CLARKE
               



             sworn.
I am a waiter at the London coffee-house


, which is kept by Mr. Rowley and Mr. Leach; on the 25th of September, the prisoner and another man came in, and called for two basons of soup when they went out, I missed a silver spoon; I followed the prisoner, and took the spoon out of his pocket, and dragged him back into the coffee-room.
(The spoon produced and deposed to.)
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
The waiter followed me, and put the spoon in my pocket, and took me back, and then took it out of my pocket; I knew nothing of it.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
