





1934. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     MATHER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of August
, 18 1/2 yards of printed cotton, value 9s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Matthews
                  




               
            .
JOHN MALLET. I am shopman to 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Matthews
               



            , a linen-draper
, of Aldgate


. This cotton hung about four feet within the door - I saw the prisoner come into the shop, and take it: I pursued, and took him about fifteen yards off, with it on his arm.


                  JOHN 
                  FORRESTER
               



            . I am a constable. I received him in charge; he said he did not care any thing about it.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I never had it in my possession.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 27. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
