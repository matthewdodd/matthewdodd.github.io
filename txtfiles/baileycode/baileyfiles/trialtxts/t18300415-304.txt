





969. 
               
                  
                     ALEXANDER 
                     FOX
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of March
, 4lbs. weight of beef, value 1s. 8d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Frederick William 
                           Hilder
                        




                     
                  ; and that he had been before convicted of felony
            .


                  FREDERICK 
                  WILLIAM HILDER
               



            . I am a butcher
. On the evening of the 6th of March, about half-past eleven o'clock at night, I was just going into the shop hanging up the meat; the prisoner walked by and took a piece of beef off the board - he laid a handkerchief on it and took it; I followed him about twenty yards, and gave charge of him.


                  JOHN 
                  DANE
               



            . I took the prisoner into custody.
Prisoner's Defence. A man said "I want you," took me to the shop, and said I had stolen a piece of beef; I did not know what he wanted me for. GUILTY.


                  RICHARD 
                  MILLIN
               



            . I have a certificate of the conviction of the prisoner on the 15th of January, in the ninth year of his present Majesty's reign; I gave evidence - he is the person.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
