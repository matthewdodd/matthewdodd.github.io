





673. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Samuel
                      Barns
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen bag, value 3 d. a woolen cloth waistcoat, value 2 s. and a man's hat. value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Summers
                  




               
            , August 28
.
"John Summers deposed that the cloths were stolen from his son, who is about fourteen years of age."
"
               
                  William
                   Summers
               



             deposed that his master's brother gave him a suit of clothes, which he was carrying him to his father, wrapped up in a bundle; that he met the prisoner, who desired him to buy him a pennyworth dock, and offered to hold his clothes; that the witness gave the bundle to another boy to hold; that he heard an outcry, and saw the prisoner running away with the bundle; that he was pursued and taken immediately.
"The prisoner in his defence called several people, who gave him a good character.



               Guilty
