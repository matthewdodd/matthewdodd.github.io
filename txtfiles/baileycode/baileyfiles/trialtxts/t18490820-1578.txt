




1578. 




JOHN PARKER
 and 




SAMUEL WEBB



, breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 



John Hartley, and another, and stealing 38lbs. weight of tobacco, and 3lbs. weight of cheroots, value 6l.; 840 pence, and 960 halfpence; their property: Parker having been before convicted: to which


PARKER pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 26.—


Transported for Seven Year.


WEBB pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 47.—



Confined One Year.
