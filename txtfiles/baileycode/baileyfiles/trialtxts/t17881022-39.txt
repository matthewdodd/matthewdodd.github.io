





645. 
               
                  
                     AMELIA 
                     HARDING
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of October
, one cotton gown, value 2 s. two linen aprons, value 12 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Sarah 
                           Moysam
                        



                     
                  ; and two shifts, value 2 s. one apron, value 6 d. and a frock, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Barber
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken coming down stairs with the property in a bundle.
(Deposed to.)



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
