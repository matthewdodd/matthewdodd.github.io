





461. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     LEVY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of January
, 1 coat, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     East
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  WILLIAM COGGINS
               



            . I am groom to Mr. Burrows, of Bishopsgate-street. On the 3rd of January I saw 
               
                  William 
                  East
               



             go into a stationer's shop in Threadneedle-street


, to deliver some wine, leaving his cart at the door; I saw the prisoner cross over from the other side of the way, and take the coat from the cart; I ran and collared him with it on his arm.
WILLIAM EAST. I am carman
 to Mr. Jarkson, a wine-merchant. I had two dozen of wine to deliver at Mr. Rudyman's; I delivered one, and when I came out for the other my coat was gone - Coggins had the prisoner in custody.


                  WILLIAM 
                  DRINKWATER
               



            . I received the prisoner in charge with the coat.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was walking along; a young man ran by my side, and threw a bundle at the side of my feet - this young man came and took me in charge.



               GUILTY
            . - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
