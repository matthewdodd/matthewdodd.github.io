





670. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     HENDON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing , on the 6th of February
 , 1 handkerchief , value 3s ., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Benjamin 
                           Dickison
                        




                     
                   , from his person
             .


                  BENJAMIN 
                  DICKISON
               



            . I was in the New-road


 on the 6th of February , between eleven and twelve o'clock in the day - I did not feel my handkerchief taken, but a man apprised me of it; I turned, and saw it in the middle of the road - the prisoner was pointed out to me , and I seized him.


                  JAMES 
                  PARTRIDGE
               



             . I was going from Somer's-town , on the 6th of February , about twelve o'clock in the day ; I saw the prisoner and two others take hold of this gentleman's pocket; one of them took the handkerchief and gave it to the prisoner ; I seized him - he dropped it, and I took him to the office , he said he did not take it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18 - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
             .
