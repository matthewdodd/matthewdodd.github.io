





1217. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  MUDGETT
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of June
, a handkerchief, value 5 s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Harrison
                     




                  , from his person
            .
MR. WILLIAM HARRISON. I lost my handkerchief while I was entering my house in Chancery-lane


, on the 7th of June - I turned round, and saw the prisoner with it in his hand; he gave it to another boy, who returned it to him. The prisoner ran down Chancery-lane; I pursued, and he was taken by the officer.


                  WILLIAM 
                  SEARLE
               



            . As I was going along Chancery-lane the prisoner ran by me - I stopped him.


                  THOMAS 
                  KERRY
               



            . I am an officer, and have the handkerchief.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
