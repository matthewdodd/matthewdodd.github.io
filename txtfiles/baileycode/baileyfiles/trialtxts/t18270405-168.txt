





THIRD DAY. SATURDAY, APRIL 7.
Third Middlesex Jury - Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
921. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  TATE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of March
, 4 saws, value 15s.; 7 planes, value 15s.; 1 stock and 3 bits, value 2s.; 3 screw-drivers, value 1s.; 2 squares, value 1s.; 5 chisels, value 2s.; 2 bevils, value 6d.; 6 guages, value 1s.; 2 axes, value 2s.; 2 baskets, value 2s., and 1 oil-stone, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Dove
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 42.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
