







                     William 
                     Winn 
                  



               
            , of St. John Wapping


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Shoes
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Wigg 
                  




                
            , the 24th of December
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner was his Journeyman
, and came to him to borrow some Money; that he being an idle drunken Fellow, refus'd to lend him any; that thereupon he going away, put his Hand behind him, and took the Shoes, and went out with them; the Prisoner being immediately followed, the Shoes were found upon him.
The Prisoner pleaded, That he took down the Shoes to look upon them, which is an usual thing, and being in Drink, happen'd to reel out of the Shop, and they came and charg'd him with stealing them; but the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d
            .
