





638. (L.) 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Polin 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a silk handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Gist 
                  




               
            , Sept. 12
. +


                  Samuel 
                  Gist 
               



            . On Sunday the 12th of September, about four in the afternoon, going along Leaden-hall-street


, the prisoner shoved against me; I turned round and saw he had got my handkerchief; I took him in custody, a constable happened to be by; he had put the handkerchief in his breeches, part of it hung out (produced and deposed to.)
The prisoner said nothing in his defence.



               Guilty
            .
