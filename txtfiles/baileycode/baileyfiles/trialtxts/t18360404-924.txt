



924. 



ANN JONES
 was indicted

for feloniously breaking and entering the dwellling-house of 



Ann Mears, on the 
15th of March, at 
St. Matthew, Bethnal green, and stealing 4 shirts, value 20s. 10 table cloths value 20s. her goods and that she had been before convicted of felony; to which she pleaded





GUILTY



Transported for Seven Years.
