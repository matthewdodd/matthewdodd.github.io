





588. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     BURTON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th day of May
 last, fifty-six pounds weight of lead, value 5 s. belonging to 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Elgar
                        




                     
                  , and fastened to a house of his
            .
(The prisoner was seen ripping the lead from the gutter, and taken immediately.)



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
