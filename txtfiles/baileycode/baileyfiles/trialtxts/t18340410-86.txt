







609. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  SWINDLER
               




             and 
               
                  FREDERICK 
                  JONAS
               




             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 6s., the goods of 
                     
                        Henry 
                        Brewer
                     




                  , from his person
            .
HENRY BREWER. On Sunday last, I was on Holborn-hill


 - I put my hand to my pocket, and missed my handkerchief - I turned, and saw Swindler taking it from my pocket, and giving it to Jones - I took Swindler by the collar - he dropped my handkerchief on the pavement, and said he had not taken it - this is it.


                  BENJAMIN 
                  CATMULL
               



            . I took the prisoner Swindler into custody.
Swindler's Defence. I went to church at half-past six o'clock, and as I was going home, I saw a mob - I went to the spot, and the gentleman took me.
SWINDLER - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.
JONAS - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.





               Transported for Seven Years
            .
