





709. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BOND
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d day of August
 last, one muslin frock, value 5 s. a waistcoat, value 4 s. a pair of breeches, value 2 s. the property of 
                     
                        John 
                        Green
                     



                  , Esq
; one silk cloak, value 8 s. one striped cotton petticoat, value 6 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Umbrall
                  



               
            , spinster
.
The prisoner was taken with one leg in the parlour window with the things.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
