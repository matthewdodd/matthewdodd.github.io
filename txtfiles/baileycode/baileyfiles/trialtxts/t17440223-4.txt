





143. 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Smith 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary Colechurch


, was indicted for 


               stealing a cotton handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Rammell 
                  




                
            , Jan. 19
.


                  Thomas 
                  Rammell 
               



            . Between eleven and twelve at noon, in Cheapside


, as I was going towards the Exchange, the Prisoner put his hand in my pocket, and took out my handkerchief - I felt his hand in my pocket, saw his hand in my pocket, and saw him drop the handkerchief upon his shoes.

Q. How far was he from you before you took him?
Rammell. Not an inch; I turned about, and took him directly. 

               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
