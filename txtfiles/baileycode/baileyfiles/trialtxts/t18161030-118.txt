





1216. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BROWN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of October
, a handkerchief, value 2s. the property of a certain person unknown
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  THOMPSON
               



            . On the day in the indictment, I saw the prisoner following a gentleman. I saw him take the handkerchief from the gentleman's pocket, and I took him, and found the handkerchief in his bosom.



               GUILTY
            , aged 29.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
