




875. 




WILLIAM GRIFFIN
 was indicted

 for stealing 1 tea kettle, value 5s; the goods of 



Ann Green:—


also 1 shirt, 4d; 1 shift, 6d; and 1 cloak, 3d; the goods of 



James Bunce; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to both of which he pleaded




GUILTY. Aged 41.—


Transported for Seven Years.
