





292. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     MALONY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of December
, one gown, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Smith
               




            .
WILLIAM SMITH. I live in Mercer-street, Long-acre


. I went over the way, a young man said there was a man in my house, I turned round and saw the prisoner come out with the gown under his arm. I secured him. He threw the gown away, and I picked it up.
THOMAS JONES. I searched the prisoner, and found six new combs in his hat.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
