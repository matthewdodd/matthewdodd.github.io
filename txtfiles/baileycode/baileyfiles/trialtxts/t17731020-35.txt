                     THOMAS
                      LEWIS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               breaking and entering the dwelling house of 
                     
                        
                           Richard
                            Muchlow
                        





                     
                  , on the 10th of October

, about the hour of eight in the night, and stealing two loaves of bread, value 4 d. and a hempen sack, value 1 s. the property of the said Richard in his dwelling house
            . ~



               Acquitted
            .
(2d M.) 
               
                  THOMAS
                   LEWIS
               



             was a second time indicted for 


               stealing one copper saucepan, value 15 d.
             the property of 
               
                  Richard
                   Muchlow
               



            , Oct. 10th. ~


                  Richard
                   Muchlow
               



            . I left a saucepan in my yard; when I went out I bolted the middle door; when I came back again the middle door was forced and the saucepan gone out of the yard. The prisoner being taken up for other offences, he acknowledged that the saucepan which was found in the brick kiln where he was, was my property, he had taken it out of the yard. (It is produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)


                  Alexander
                   Peckham
               



            . I was present when he made the above confession.
Prisoner's Defence.
I was in liquor; I know nothing about it.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
