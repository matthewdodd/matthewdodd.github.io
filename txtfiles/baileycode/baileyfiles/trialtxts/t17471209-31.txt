





37. 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Clark 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a silk handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Turnpenny 
                  




                
            , November the 23 d
.
Turnpenny. As I was walking on the Exchange


, I thought the boy
 had got my handkerchief; the Prisoner when he was taken and brought to the coffee-house, was known to be an old offender; when he was pursued he dropt the handkerchief in the street.




               Guilty 10 d.
