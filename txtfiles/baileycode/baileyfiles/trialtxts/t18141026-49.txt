





916. 
               
                  
                     JACOB 
                     ISAACS
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 30th of June
, feloniously and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession, a certain forged bank note, for the payment of 500 l. he knowning it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
