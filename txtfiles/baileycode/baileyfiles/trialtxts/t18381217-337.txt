



337. 




JOHN MURRAY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
2nd of December, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 



George Taylor, from his person; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






GEORGE TAYLOR
. I am an upholsterer. About half-past eight o'clock on the evening of the 2nd of December, I was walking in Biabopsgate-street—I felt a tug, turned and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his right-hand—I collared and held him until I saw the officer—this it my handkerchief—(looking at it)





DANIEL PAMPLETT
. I took the prisoner into custody.

Prisoner's Defence. I picked it up.





ROBERT TYRRELL
. I am an officer. I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former, conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 14.—


Transported for Ten Years.
