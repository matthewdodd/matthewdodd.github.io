





565. 
               
                  
                     CHRISTOPHER 
                     EAVES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of August
, one muslin handkerchief, value 4 s. three pair of muslin ruffles, value 1 s. 6 d. a pair of laced, ruffles, value 6 d. and two napkins, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Cotton
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor's wife met the prisoner on the stairs with the property.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
