




1031. 




GEORGE LANGLEY
 was again indicted with 




JAMES SMITH



, for burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling house of 



William Frederick Reeve, and stealing 2 jackets, and other articles, value 1l.; the goods of 



William Bruton Alleyne; 1 gown and 1 knife, value 32s., of 



Henry Daniel Morden Garnham; and 1 shawl, value 18s., of 



William Frederick Reeve: to which


LANGLEY pleaded 


GUILTY
.* Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.


SMITH pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 23.—



Confined Twelve Months.
