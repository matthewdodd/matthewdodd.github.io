



879. 




JOHN HASKEW
 was indicted

 for feloniously forging and uttering a certain transfer of a share and interest of 110l. in the Long Annuities, standing in the names of 


Robert Bamford, senior, and 


James Berriman Tibbetts, since deceased, with intent to defraud the 

Governor and Company of the Bank of England.—Other COUNTS, varying the manner of stating the charge: to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 46.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Before Mr. Justice Coltman.
