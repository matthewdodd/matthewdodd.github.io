





722. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     TODD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of July
, three live geese, value 6 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Hankin
                        



                     
                  ; and two live geese, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Barrow
                  



               
            .
The prosecutor took the prisoner with the geese.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice GROSE.
