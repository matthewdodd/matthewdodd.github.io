






NEW COURT. (4th DAY.)
Middlesex Cases, Fourth Jury, Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1376. 
               
                  MARY ANN 
                  WILLIAMS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of August

, a shift, value 1 s.; three caps, value 3 s., and a table-cloth, value 1 s., the goods of 
                     
                        Sarah 
                        Martin
                     



                  ; two shirts, value 12 s., and a night-cap, value 6 d., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Martin
                     



                  ; two shirts, value 12 s., and two handkerchiefs, value 1 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Martin
               



            . - Also for 


               stealing, on the 27th of August

, a nightgown, value 6 d.; a night-cap, value 6 d.; a gown, value 4 s.; a petticoat, value 1 s.; a handkerchief, value 3 s.; a sheet, value 6 s., and two caps, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Cooke
               



            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 26.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
