



1415. 




THOMAS DITCHFIELD
 was indicted

 for feloniously stealing, embezzling, and secreting, on the 
14th of April, a letter containing 2 sovereigns, 1 40l., 1 5l., part of a 50l., and part of a 100l. Bank-notes, whilst employed in the 

General Post Office.—also, for stealing, embezzling, and secreting, on the 
2nd of March, a letter containing 4 sovereigns, 1 shilling, and 3 10l. Bank-notes, the monies of 



Thomas William Earl of Litchfield, whilst employed as aforesaid; to both of which indictments he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 22.—


Transported for Fourteen Years.
