                  Jeremiah
                   Hart
               



            . I was attending on a trial at Guildhall


 on the 12th of December. While I was standing in the crowd, I felt my handkerchief going out of my pocket. I turned round short, and caught the handkerchief in her hand.
- Pain. I was going that way with some persons. I saw the prisoner with the prosecutor's handkerchief in her hand.
Prisoner's Defence.
I picked it up under my feet.



               Guilty
