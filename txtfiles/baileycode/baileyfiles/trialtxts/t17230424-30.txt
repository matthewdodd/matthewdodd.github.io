








                  Humphry 
                  Jones 
               



            , of St. Andrew Holburn


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Curtains, Vallance, and other Goods
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Haydon 
                  



                
            , the 21st of February

 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner took Lodgings in the Prosecutor's House, and having lain there one Night, went away with the Goods the next Day. The Fact being plainly prov'd the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s
            . 


               Transportation
            .
He was also indicted a second Time for the like Fact, in 


               stealing the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Rutkins 
                        



. But the Evidence not being so full to this, as to the former Indictment, he was 

               acquitted
            .
