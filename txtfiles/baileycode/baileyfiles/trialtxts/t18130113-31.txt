





159. 
               
                  
                     ELEANOR 
                     TOMLINSON
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               that she, on the 21st of November
, feloniously, and without lawful excuse, had in her custody and possession divers forged bank-notes, that is to say, two forged Bank notes of 2 l. each, she knowing them to be false and forged
            .
SECOND COUNT, that she had in her possession a certain other forged note, for the payment of 2 l. she knowing it to be forged.
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
