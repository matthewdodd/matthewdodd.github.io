





254. (L.) 
               
                  
                     EDWARD
                      WELCH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a cotton handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of a person unknown, January 14
. ++


                  Percival
                   Phillips
               



            . I saw the prisoner take a handkerchief out of the pocket of a person passing by; I called to the person, but I could not make him hear. I secured the prisoner, and took the handkerchief out of his hand.
The prisoner said nothing in his defence.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
