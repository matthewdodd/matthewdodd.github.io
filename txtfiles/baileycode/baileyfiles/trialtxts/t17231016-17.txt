







                     Hannah 
                     Highfield 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchres


, was 


               indicted for breaking the House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Dayton 
                        




                      
                  , and stealing a Pair of Grates, and other Goods, value 9 s.
             the 30th of August
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, That she being abroad, when she came home about 10 a-Clock, her House was broke open, and her Goods stolen, and the Grates were taken upon the Prisoner; and that the Prisoner had lodg'd with her a Night or two. Another Evidence depos'd, That he took the Prisoner with the Grates, about 11 a-Clock at Night, the Night the Robbery was committed. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
