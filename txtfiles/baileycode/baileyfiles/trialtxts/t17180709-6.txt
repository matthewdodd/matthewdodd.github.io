







                     John 
                     Mills 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               privately stealing a pair of Stockings value 1 s. in the Shop
             of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Rushatch 
                  




                
             the 6th of June
 last. The Prosecutor's Servant deposed the Prisoner came to his Masters Shop to buy a pair of Stockings and stole a pair, putting them in his Pocket, which upon taxing him with he pull'd out, and threw down upon the Counter. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, saying the Stockings were fallen down. which he stooping to take up the Lad mistook, supposing him to take them out of his Pocket. However the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               to be Transported
            .
