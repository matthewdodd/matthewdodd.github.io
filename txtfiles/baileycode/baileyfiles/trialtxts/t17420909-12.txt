





66. 
               
                  
                     Caleb 
                     Walker 
                  



               
             , of St Clement Danes


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Pair of Sheets, val. 1 s. a Blanket, val. 1 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Axe 
               




              , May 28
.
Mrs Axe. I live in New-Inn Passage, without Temple-Bar


. The Prisoner lodged at my House last May, he took the Lodging by the Name of Smith The second of August last, my Daughter met him, and charged him with taking these things; he told Alderman Hankey he had sold them in White-Chapel.
Prisoner I never did lodge at her House.
Mary Axe. I remember that Man's lodging at my Father's. He told me he had pawned them in White-Chapel , but would not tell me the Street. He would have given me 3 s. for them.
Prisoner. I Am please you, my Lord, she said she only wanted the Money for the Goods.
Axe. I never said any such thing. 

               Guilty
             .
