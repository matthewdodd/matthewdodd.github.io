





189. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PEAKE
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he on the 4th of February
, had in his custody and possession, a forged and counterfeited note, purporting to be a promissory note of the governor and company of the Bank of England, for the payment of one pound, and that he at the same time had in his custody and possession one other like forged and counterfeited note, for the payment of one pound, he well knowing them to be false and forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
