





28. * 
               
                  Benjamin 
                  Dixon 
               



            , was indicted, for that he on the 14th of Oct
. about the hour of three in the night, on the same day, 


               the dwelling house of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Buckingham 
                        




                      
                  , did break and enter, and stole from thence 56 lb. wt. of flax, value 50 s. one linnen bag, val. 2 d.
             the goods of the said 
               
                  Edward 
                  Buckingham 
               



            .
 This is the same Person tried by the Name of 
               
                  John 
                  Thomas 
               



            , last Sessions, No. 602.


                  Edward 
                  Buckingham 
               



            . I live in Broad St. Giles's


, on the 15th of November, between 5 and 6 o'clock, I was called up by a neighbour, who told me my seller was broke open : I got up and found the lock was wrench'd off, and the door broke, there was taken out a linnen bag, with upwards of half a hundred of flax.


                  Richard 
                  Osman 
               



            . I am a watchman, I took the Prisoner with the goods upon him in Holborn, about half an hour after three o'clock the same night: they were produced in Court.




               Guilty, Felony only
            .
