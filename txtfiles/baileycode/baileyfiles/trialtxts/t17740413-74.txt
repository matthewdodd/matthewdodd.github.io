





312. (L.) 
               
                  
                     MARY
                      MAGDALEN EDGLEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a silver table spoon, value 6 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Edward
                      Williams
                  




               
            , March 13th
. ++


                  Edward
                   Williams
               



            . The prisoner was my servant
; her mistress sent her out of an errand; she was brought back about nine at night with the spoon.
- Essex. The prisoner brought a spoon to me to pawn on the 16th of March, between the hours of seven and eight in the evening; (the spoon produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)
The prisoner said nothing in her defence.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
