







                     James 
                     Walnen 
                  



               
             , of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Wearing and Linnen, value 4 l. in the dwelling House
             of 
               
                  
                     Philip 
                     Brown 
                  




                
            , the 30th of August
 It appeared that the Prisoner lodged with the Prosecutor and carryed away the Goods. The Prisoner pleaded he did pawn them, being necessitated but design'd to redeem them, but was discover'd before he could do it. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
