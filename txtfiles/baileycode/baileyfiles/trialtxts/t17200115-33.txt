







                     Margaret 
                     Wilson 
                  



               
             of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2 Silk Handkerchiefs, a Muslin Apron, a Shirt, a pair of Flaxen Sheets, &c. 
            the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Smith 
                  




                
            , on the 9th of this Instant January
. It appeared that the Prosecutor's Wife saw the Prisoner go out of her House, and missing the Goods, sent her Husband after her, who overtook her with them upon her. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
