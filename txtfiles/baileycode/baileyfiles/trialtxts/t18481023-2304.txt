








2304. 




JAMES HARE
 was again indicted for

stealing 4 capes, value 9l. 8s.; the goods of 



Stafford Northcote and another:



also, 2 shawls, 3l. 2s.; the goods of 



William Edgar and others:



also, 2 shawls, and other goods, 5l. 6s.; the goods of 



John Mair, and others:



also, 15 1/4 yards of satinette, 3l. 1s. 8d.; the goods of 



Robert Turner; and 



CHARLES REHDEN



, feloniously receiving the same,


and harbouring Hare; to all of which

HARE pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 37.—


Transported for Fourteen Years
.

MR. CLARKSON
offered no evidence against


REHDEN—


NOT GUILTY.
