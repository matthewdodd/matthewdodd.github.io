







                     Edward 
                     Doe 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Mary Hill


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Money of 
                     
                        
                           Samuel 
                           Breeton 
                        




, the 9th of August
. The Prosecutor depos'd, The Prisoner was his Servant
, and he keeping an Eating-house, he had often taken his Money, and defrauded him of it. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
