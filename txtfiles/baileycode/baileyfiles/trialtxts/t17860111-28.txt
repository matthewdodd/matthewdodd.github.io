





149. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     INGRAM
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 23d day of December
 last, two sheets, value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Laidley
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the sheets upon him.



               GUILTY
            .
To be 


               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice HEATH.
