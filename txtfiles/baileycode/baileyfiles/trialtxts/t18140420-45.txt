





330. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     RAMPLING
                  




               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 23rd of February
, without lawful cause had in his custody and possession, a certain bank note for the payment of 1 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            , aged 28.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
