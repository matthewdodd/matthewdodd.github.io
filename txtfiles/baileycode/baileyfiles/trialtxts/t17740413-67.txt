





303. ( 2d L.) 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM
                      MOORE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing 21 yards of printed linen cloth, value 40 s. and 27 yards of linen cloth for handkerchiefs, value 50 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph
                      Barlow
                  




               
            , January 10th
. ++


                  Joseph
                   Barlow
               



            . When the prisoner was taken he confessed stealing the cloth, and said they were sold to one Bugden; and were in the cellar under the stairs.
- Jones. The goods were in a warehouse done up in a truss; I never saw the truss opened.
The prisoner said nothing in his defence.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
