





931. 
               
                  JOSHUA 
                  JESSOP
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of May
, a handkerchief, value 5 s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Mynn
                     




                  , from his person
            .
WILLIAM MYNN. I am a hop-factor
, and live in York-street, Borough. On the 12th of May, at noon, I was in Fenchurch-street


, and felt a twitch at my coat pocket, turned round, and seized the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand - nobody else was within six yards of him - he begged to be let go.


                  JOHN 
                  BURNLEY
               



            . I am an officer. I took him in charge with the handkerchief.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Life
            .
