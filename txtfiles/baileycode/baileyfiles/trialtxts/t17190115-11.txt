








                     Amy 
                     Furnace 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Harding 
                  



                
            , of St. Buttolph Aldgate



 were indicted 


               privately stealing printed Linnen value 48 s. in the Shop
             of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Lawrence 
                  




                
             the 5th of this Instant January
. It appeared by the Evidence the Prisoners came to the Prosecutors Shop under pretence of buying a Shift, and then wanted to see Linnen, and were perceived one slily to draw away the Linnen from off the Counter, and let it fall, and the other to take it up and conceal of The fact being plainly prov'd upon them, the Jury found them each 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            




               Transportation
            .
