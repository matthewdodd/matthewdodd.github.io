







                     John 
                     Flint 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Hornsey


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Linnen Cloaths, to the Value of 10 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Daniel 
                     Bun 
                  




                
            , the 11th of October
 last. The Evidence depos'd, That the Cloaths hanging on an Hedge to dry, the Prisoner was seen to take them off, and carry them away; that thereupon being pursued, he flung the Linnen over a Gate, ran away, and hid himself from the Pursuers under a Hedge. The Prisoner deny'd the having the Linnen, and pleaded that he was apprehensive of being arrested, and supposing the Pursuers to be Bailiffs ran away. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
