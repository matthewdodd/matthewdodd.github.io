





651. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     THOMPSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of May
, a pair of trowsers, value 2s. 6d. and four pair of stockings, value 2s. 6d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Baker
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  BAKER
               



            . I am a seaman
. I lost my trowsers and stockings, on the night of the 15th of May; they were in my chest locked up.


                  JOHN 
                  WILLIAMS
               



            . I am a constable of the London Docks. I apprehended the prisoner coming out of the Docks, and there was information lodged at the watchhouse of several other ships having been robbed, and I found the property on him.
(Property produced, and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            , aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
