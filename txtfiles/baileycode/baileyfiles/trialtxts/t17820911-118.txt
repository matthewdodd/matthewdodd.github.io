






602. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BEAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 22d of July
 one cotton handkerchief, value 4 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Walter 
                     Cope
                  




               
            .


                  WALTER
                   COPE
               



             sworn.
I saw the prisoner take my handkerchief on Snow-hill


 the 22d of July, between nine and ten in the evening; I laid hold of him, and he threw it down; I took him; he was never out of my sight; it was an India one, value 4 s.
Prisoner. I did not take it; I have been troubled with fits.



               GUILTY
            .
(To be 


               transported to America for seven years
            .)
Tried by the London Jury, before Mr. RECORDER.
