





550. (L.) 
               
                  
                     JAMES
                      BROWN, otherwise 
MACDONALD




               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a tackle rope, value 15 s
            . the 
               
property of the company of vintners




            , July 11th
. *



                  John
                   Hood
               



            . I am servant to the Vintners Company. I saw the prisoner with a rope last Saturday, about ten o'clock of the night; I know the rope to be the property of the Vintners Company; He had worked for the Company about three years ago. (The rope produced and deposed to.)


                  John
                   Lee
               



            . I am one of the Master Porters to the Vintners Company. I took the prisoner on Sunday; he struck me several blows, and got from me several times.


                  William
                   White
               



            . I am one of the Master Porters. I know the rope to be the property of the Vintners Company.
Prisoner. I only designed to borrow the rope.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
