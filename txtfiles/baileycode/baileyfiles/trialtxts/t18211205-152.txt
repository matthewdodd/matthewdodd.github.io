





157. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     HOLMES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of November
, nine pounds of bacon, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Richard 
                  Shail
               




            .


                  RICHARD 
                  SHAIL
               



            . I keep a chandler's shop
 in Arthur-street, St. George's in the East


. On the 30th of November I was in my back parlour, the shop door was open; I heard a person come in, looked through the glass door and saw the prisoner take a piece of bacon out of the window and go out. He heard me following him, and dropped it on the pavement. I secured him on the other side of the way.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
             - Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
