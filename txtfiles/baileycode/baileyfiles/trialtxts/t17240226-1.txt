







                     John 
                     Vantier 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Ann Aldersgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Hat, from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Stephens 
                        




, the 3d of February
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, That as he was going Home from a Burying in St. Ann's Lane


, his Hat was snatch'd off from his Head, and a Person who was with him, pursu'd the Prisoner, and took him, with the Hat. This was confirm'd by 
               
                  Richard 
                  Clarke 
               



            , the Person who was with him. The Prisoner had no Defence to make, but a bare Denial of the Fact, which being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
