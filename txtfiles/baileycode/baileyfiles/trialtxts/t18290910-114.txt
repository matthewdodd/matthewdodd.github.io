





1645. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SHORE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of August
, 2 shirts, value 6s., and 5 shillings
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Dennis 
                     Collins
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 23.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
