



1541. 




GEORGE COOK



, stealing 1 sweeping-machine, 1 shovel, 1 cloth, and 1 sack, value 40s.; the goods of 



John Francis: having been before convicted.






JOHN FRANCIS
. On 26th July I missed my chimney sweeping-machine—this is it (produced)—my place was broken into—the prisoner knew my place.





SAMUEL PROCTER
. On 26th July the prisoner rung my bell; he had a machine on the steps—I followed him, and found him at Brentford with it.

Prisoner. I bought it.





EDWARD BARKER
 (policeman, E 124). I produce a certificate of the prisoner's conviction—(read—Convicted Dec, 1840, having been before convicted; transported for seven years)—he is the man.




GUILTY
. Aged 33.—


Transported for Ten Years.
