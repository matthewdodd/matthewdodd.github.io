





1517. 
               
                  
                     HENRY VENDWORTH 
                     DUNOIR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of June
, 1 pair of boots, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Bohag
               




            .


                  SUSANNAH 
                  BOHAG
               



            . I am the wife of Charles Bohag, a shoemaker
 - we live in Tothill-street


. On the 29th of June, about ten minutes to nine o'clock at night, I saw the prisoner put his foot into the shop, reach up, break the string, and take the shoes off a nail; I followed, took him about five yards from the house, and brought him back -I did not know him before.
Prisoner. Q. Did you not offer to set me at liberty if I paid 2s. for them? A. Yes, I thought it would save me the trouble of coming here.
DANIEL LYONS. I am a Policeman, and received the prisoner in charge.(Property produced and sworn to)
The prisoner put in a written Defence, stating that he was intoxicated.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34. - 


               Confined Fourteen Days
            .
Before Mr. Recorder.
