



440. 




GEORGE SIMMONS
 was indicted

 for stealing 1 handkerchief, value 4s.; the goods of 




Joseph Smith, from his person.






JOSEPH SMITH
. I live in Union-place, and am a shoemaker. On the I afternoon of the 10th of Dec. I was at the corner of Finch-lane, Cornhill, and felt a pull at my pocket—I turned, and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand—this is it.

Prisoner. It was not in my hand, it was on the ground. Witness. I saw it in his hand, and he threw it down.

Prisoner. I was passing, it laid on the pavement, he turned and collared me.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported for Ten Years.
