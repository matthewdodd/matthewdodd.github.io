






NEW COURT. - Thursday, April 10th.
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
541. 
               
                  NICHOLAS AUGUSTE LOUIS 
                  LEMOYNE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of December

, 12 yards of velvet, value 12l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Antoine 
                  Leroux
               



             - and for 


               stealing, on the 13th of March

, 1 silver spoon, value 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Bird
               



            , to which indictments he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 35. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
