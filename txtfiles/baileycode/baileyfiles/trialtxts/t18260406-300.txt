





873. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  SAVAGE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 23d of February
, 1 cask, value 8s., and 8 gallons of Cloves cordial, value 3l. 16s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John Gillyatt 
                  Booth
               




            , and another, his partner.


                  JOSEPH 
                  SMITH
               



            . I am servant to Messrs. John Gillyatt Booth, and his partner - they live in Cow-cross-street. On the 23d of February, I was in White-cross-street


 - I went up a yard, and left my waggon with some casks in it; I came back, and saw a man in the waggon, who gave the prisoner a cask out, and ran away; the prisoner went down Whitecross-street, with the cask on his shoulder - he walked as fast as he could, and I stopped him - he brought the cask back - it contained eight gallons of cloves cordial.


                  JACOB 
                  COHEN
               



            . I saw the prisoner with the cask - he said he had to carry it to Smithfield for sixpence.


                  JOSEPH 
                  HORTON
               



            . I am a constable. I was coming down Whitecross-street and saw a number of people - the witness had hold of the prisoner by the collar - I took charge of him.
Prisoner's Defence. I was employed to take it to Smithfield.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
