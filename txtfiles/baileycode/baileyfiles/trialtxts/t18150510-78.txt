





669. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     PARKER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 16th of April
, two traces, value 2 s. and one brush, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Taylor
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  TAYLOR
               



            . I am a pasteboard-maker
; I live No. 70, Old-street


. On the 16th of April, I saw the prisoner come out of my stables, with the traces; I caught him; he threw them down; I gave him in charge to a officer.
Prisoner's Defence. I saw the property lay by the stable door, and picked them up.



               GUILTY
            , aged 25.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
