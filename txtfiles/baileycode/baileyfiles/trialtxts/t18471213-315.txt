




315. 




JOHN LOVELL
 and 




JOHN WILLIAMS



, stealing 1 coat, value 15s.; and 1 snuff-box, 2s.; the goods of 



Charles Freeman; Williams having been before convicted; to which


LOVELL pleaded 


GUILTY. Aged 18.—



Confined Four Months



WILLIAMS pleaded 


GUILTY. Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years
