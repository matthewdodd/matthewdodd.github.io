





441. (M.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Simpson 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a coach-seat, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Barley 
                  




                
            , August 20
. ++


                  Henry 
                  Barley 
               



            . I had set down a fair in the Almonry


; when I went to drive on, I saw the door of my coach open on that side which the fair did not come out of; I got down to shut it; the prisoner was stopt, offering my my seat to sale in New-palace-yard, by 
               
                  Francis 
                  Atkins 
               



            , a coachman. 


                  Francis 
                  Atkins 
               



             The prisoner is a soldier
, he offered the seat to me and other coachmen; I stopt that and him, and found out the prosecutor; I called upon the prisoner's serjeant, he said he would not give a bad halfpenny to save him from transportation. 
Prisoner's defence. 
I found it in the Broad way, Peter's-street, Westminster.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
