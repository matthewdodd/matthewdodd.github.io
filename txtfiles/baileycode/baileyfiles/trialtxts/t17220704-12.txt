







                     Peter 
                     Mills 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Spoon, value 16 s.
             the Goods of the Right Honourable; the 
               
Earl
 of Essex


            . It appear'd that the prisoner belong'd to a Sand Cart, and carrying Sand into the Kitchen, he took an opportunity of hiding the Spoon, and left his Sand-bag behind him, that he might have an excuse to come again; but being suspected, and threatned to be carried before a Justice, he confest where he had hid it. 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            .
