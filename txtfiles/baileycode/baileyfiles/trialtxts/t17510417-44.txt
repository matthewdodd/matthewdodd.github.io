





310. (M.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Coats 
                  



               
            . was indicted for 


               stealing one linen gown, value 5 s.
             the property of a certain person unknown, March 13
. *
The prosecutrix is a washer-woman, and lives at Hackney


; she had washed this gown for a person whose name she did not know, and hung it on a line to dry. The prisoner was seen by her looking for rags, &c. on a dung-hill, where he went under the line to get to it; the gown was miss'd, and search was made for the prisoner, he was found, and the gown in his bag; it was produced in court, and depos'd to.



               Guilty
            .
