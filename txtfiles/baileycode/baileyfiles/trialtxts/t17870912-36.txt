





706. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     BUTTS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 26th of August
 last, one silk handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Pitcher
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor took the prisoner with the handkerchief upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   THOMPSON
               



            .
