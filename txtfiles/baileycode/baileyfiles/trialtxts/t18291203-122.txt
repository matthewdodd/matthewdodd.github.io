





121. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BRENAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of November
, one 1000l. Bank note
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Bowman
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Winter
                  



               
            , his masters.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 27.
Strongly Recommended to Mercy.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
