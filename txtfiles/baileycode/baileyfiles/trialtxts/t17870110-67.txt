





226. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     BECKFORD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of October
 last, twelve pounds weight of Gloucester cheese, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Austen
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken instantly with the cheese.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
