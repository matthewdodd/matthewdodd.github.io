



2265. 




GEORGE BARRON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
20th of July, 1 pair of boots, value 9s., the goods of 



Jane Jones; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






JAMES WOLSTONCROPT
. I am shopman to Jane Jones, of High Holborn. On the 20th of July the prisoner came to our shop—I saw him take a pair of boots—he walked away—I followed, and took him three doors off, with these boots in his hand.

Prisoner's Defence. I pointed to the man who gave them me; he said, "I don't care a d—, I shall take you."





THOMAS BLUNDELL
 (police-constable L 93.) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Ten Years.
