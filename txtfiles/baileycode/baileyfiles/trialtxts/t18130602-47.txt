





562. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     JONES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that he, on the 26th of March
, feloniously had in his custody and possession, three 1 l. forged bank-notes, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
