





339. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HUNTLEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               burglariously and feloniously breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Child
                        



                     
                  , about the hour of twelve in the night, on the 24th day of March
 last, and burglariously stealing therein two live pigs, value 40 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Carr
                  



               
            .


                  Sarah
                   Horton
               



             bought the two pigs of the prisoner, which were deposed to by the prosecutor.
Prisoner. My brother deals in pigs, and he gave them to me to sell.
The Prisoner called five witnesses to his character.




               GUILTY, But not of the burglary
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   EYRE
               



            .
