





529. (2d M.) 
               
                  
                     SARAH
                      BATEMAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a cotton gown, value 2 s. a linen shift, value 1 s. a cheque linen apron, value 1 s. a muslin apron, value 1 s. a quarter of a yard of linen cloth, value 1 s. and a silk handkerchief, value 12 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Francis
                      Hazard
                  




               
            , May 31st
. +


                  Francis
                   Hazard
               



            . The prisoner was my servant
: she lived with me about eighteen days. On the 31st of May, about ten at night, she went away; I missed the things mentioned in the indictment (repeating them); she was taken on the 2d of June in St. James's Park, with my gown, apron, and cap on.


                  William
                   Hunter
               



            . I am a pawnbroker: I have a cardinal and shirt I received of one Beamen; I don't know the prisoner; (they are produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)
Prisoner's Defence.
I was going to see an acquaintance at Brentford; I put them on and intended to return them.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
