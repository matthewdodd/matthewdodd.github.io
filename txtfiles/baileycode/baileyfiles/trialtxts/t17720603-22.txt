





441. (L.) 
               
                  
                     DAVID
                      ALLEN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a silk handkerchief, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Atkinson
                  




               
            , May 28th
. ||


                  John
                   Atkinson
               



            . I missed my handkerchief in the street, the 28th of May; the prisoner was near me; I suspected him and searched immediately, and found my handkerchief upon him; I felt a hand in my pocket at the time I missed it.
Prisoner's Defence.
The gentleman came up to me and struck me. I know nothing of the handkerchief.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
