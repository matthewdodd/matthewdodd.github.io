





THIRD DAY. SATURDAY, MAY 31.
Fourth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1226. 
               
                  SAMUEL 
                  MORRIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of April
, 1 watch, value 30s.; 1 watch-chain, value 5s.; 2 seals, value 2s.; 2 watch-keys, value 2s.; 2 spoons, value 7s., and 1 sixpence
            , the property of 
               
                  William 
                  Fry
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
