





1614. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PURCHASE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of July
, 1 pair of boots, value 9s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Pike
                  




               
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  PIKE
               



            . I am a boot and shoemaker
, and live in Ludgate-street


. On the 10th of July I saw the prisoner stoop down, take these boots off the floor inside the door, and run across the road; I followed him to Broadway, Blackfriars - I took him, and pulled them from under his coat.


                  THOMAS 
                  WILLMOT
               



            . I took the prisoner in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 25. 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
