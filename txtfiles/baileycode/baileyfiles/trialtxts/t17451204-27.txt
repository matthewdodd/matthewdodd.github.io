





27. 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Abey 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a Cloth Coat and two Shirts
            .
- . He stole a Livery Coat out of a Stable in Saville-Row


, on a Tuesday: The Man catched him with it coming out of the Stable.
Q. Was that the Coat you used to wear?
- . It is my Livery Coat; I have had it better than half a Year.
- . I am a Coachman; I was going to water my Horses; I went into the Back Stable, and I said to him, What, are you a Smuggler? I saw him with the very Coat. 

               Guilty
            .
