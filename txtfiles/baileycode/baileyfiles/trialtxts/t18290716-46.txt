





1419. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     BRIGHT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of June
, 5 ozs. of tobacco, value 18d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Edward 
                  Toplis
               




             and others.


                  EDWARD 
                  GEORGE BUTLER TOPLIS
               



            . I am in partnership with my father, 
               
                  Edward 
                  Toplis
               



            , and my brother - the prisoner was employed in our factory for about three years. On the 26th of June this tobacco was found on his person, it was not completely manufactured.


                  WILLIAM 
                  COX
               



            . I am an officer. I was sent for and searched the prisoner; I found this tobacco in his shoes - there are between five and six ounces of it.


                  JOHN 
                  TOPLIS
               



            . I was present when the prisoner was stopped; on leaving our factory he was searched, and this found on him; he said it was the first time he had ever done so.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
