





199. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     DUFF
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of December
, 4 half-crowns and 5 shillings
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     James Joseph 
                     Olivine
                  




               
            , to which indictment he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
