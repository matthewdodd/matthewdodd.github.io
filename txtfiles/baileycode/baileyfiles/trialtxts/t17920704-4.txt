







314. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     DYER
                  



               
            , 
               
                  BISHOP 
                  UNDERWOOD
               



            , and 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     HARRIS
                  



               
            , were indicted for 


               stealing on the 20th of June
 last, a silk and muslin waistcoat, value 3 s. 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Enoch
                  




               
            .
The prisoners were taken immediately with the property.
BISHOP UNDERWOOD, 
               
                  WILLIAM
                   DYER
               



            ,



               GUILTY
            .





               Transported for seven years
            .


                  THOMAS
                   HARRIS
               



            , 

               NOT GUILTY
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Baron BULLER.
