







                     Samuel 
                     Clay 
                  



               
            , of St. Dunstan at Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Pint Pot out of the House of 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Cock 
                        




, on the 26th of April
. The Prosecutor deposed that he came into his House and ask'd for a Gentleman; that he drew him a Pint of Drink in a Silver Mug, but missing of it as soon as he was gone, pursued and took him with the Mug under his Coat. The Prisoner owned he took it, but not with a Design to steal it, but to bring it back again. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
