







                     James 
                     Washford 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Giles Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 28 Yards of Strip'd Silk Cotton, value 5 l. in the shop of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Finch 
                        




, the 16th of May
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence the Prisoner stole the Goods out of the Prosecutor's Shop, no Body being therein. The Fact being plainly prov'd the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
