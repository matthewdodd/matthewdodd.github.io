



374. 




BENJAMIN BAILEY
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
27th of April, 1 table-cloth, value 3s.; 2 sheets, 5s.; 2 blankets, 8s.; 1 other blanket, 4s.; 1 counterpane, 4s.; 1 carpet-cover, 8s.; 20 yards of carpet 1l., and 2 hearth-rugs, 5l. 10s.; the goods of 



Ann Brandram.







AMELIA SWEETMAN
. I am the wife of Robert Sweetman. I am in the service of Mrs. Ann Brandram—she lives in Gower-street—she employed the prisoner and his wife to take care of the house when she went to France—I have since missed the articles produced—I have examined them—they are her property.





JAMBS SWAIN
. I am a pawnbroker—I have two hearth-rugs and some other articles pawned by the prisoner.





WILLIAM STARTUP
. I am a pawnbroker—I produce a table-cloth, carpet, and blanket, pawned by the prisoner on the 27th of April and 16th of Aug.

Prisoner. I did not do it with any intention of guilt.
(The prisoner received a good character.)




GUILTY
. Aged 30.—


Transported for Seven Years.
