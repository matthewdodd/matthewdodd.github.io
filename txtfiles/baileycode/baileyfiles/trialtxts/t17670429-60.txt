





261. (L.) 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Clifford 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a pickax, value 2 s. and an iron crow, value 12 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Stockley 
                  




                
            , March 7
. ++


                  William 
                  Stockley 
               



            . I am a bricklayer
, and live in Crutched-friars


; on the 7th of March I was standing at my door, I saw the prisoner come from my shed, with a pickax and iron crow, my property; I went and took him with them upon him.




               Guilty 10 d.
