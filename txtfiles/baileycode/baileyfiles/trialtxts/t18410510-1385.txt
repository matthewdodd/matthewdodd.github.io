



1385. 




WILLIAM YOUNG
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
27th of April, 1 coat, value 1l. 10s., the goods of 



Samuel Gibson.






SAMUEL GIBSON
. I live in Garnault-place, Clerkenwell. On the 27th of April my nephew brought roe a note—I went down into the passage, and missed my coat—this now produced is it.





JOHN EDWARD CLINE
. I live with my uncle. On the 27th of April a knock came to the door—I went, and the prisoner was there—he gave me a note, which I took to my uncle—when I returned the prisoner and the coat were gone—the note was fictitious.





WILLIAM MARTIN
. I am in the service of Mr. Boyce, a pawnbroker. I produce the coat, which I took in of the prisoner, on the 27th of April, about nine o'clock in the evening.




GUILTY
. Aged 23.—


Transported for Seven Years.
