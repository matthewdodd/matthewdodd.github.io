




1401. 




WILLIAM COOK
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the of April, 1 watch, value 3l.; 1 watch guard, value 15s.; 3 half-crown 2 shillings, and 1 sixpence; the goods and monies of 



James Hayhoe.






JAMES HAYHOE
. I am a carpenter, and live in Robert-street, Brixton The prisoner came to me on the 8th of April—I left him in my room for about five minutes—when I returned he was gone, and I misted my watch, which I had left there—this is it—(looking at it.)





THOMAS MUNTON
. I am a pawnbroker in the Westminster-road, on the morning of the 9th of April the prisoner pawned this watch at on house for 25s.





JOHN HUGH SWEETING
. I am a police-sergeant. I found the duplicate of this watch on him, on board the steam-boat.




GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Before Mr. Recorder.
