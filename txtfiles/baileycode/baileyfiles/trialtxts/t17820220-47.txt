





203. 
               
                  
                     FRANCIS
                     NICHOLLS
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               stealing upon the 9th of February
, a flannel waistcoat faced with sattin, value 5 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Andrew
                     Stephenson
                  




               
            .


                  Andrew
                   Stephenson
               



             deposed, the prisoner and another man not yet taken, came under pretence of buying a waistcoat, the other man stole several and ran off, the prisoner ran likewise, but was taken and brought back, and then he offered to pay for two, one for himself and another for the other man.



               GUILTY
            .




               To be transported to the East-Indies for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury, before Mr. Justice BULLER.
