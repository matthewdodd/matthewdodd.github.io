




2373. 



CHARLES COOK
 and 



RALPH COOK
 were again indicted for

 stealing, on the 
17th of October, 1 handkerchief, value 6d., the goods of 



Leitch Ritchie, from his person.






LEITCH RITCHIE
. I was at the sale. I did not feel my pocket picked—I recollect having my handkerchief in my pocket, and a few minutes after I missed it—I had used it shortly before—I turned round and observed the prisoner Ralph at some distance from me, going away—I did not see Charles at all.





HENRY LLOYD
. I am a porter. I saw Ralph searched—I took two handkerchiefs from his hat, and one was found on his person.
(Property produced, and sworn to.)

RALPH COOK—


GUILTY
. Aged.—


Transported for Ten Years. CHARLES COOK—


NOT GUILTY
.
