







                     Mary 
                     Smith 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary White Chappel


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Brass Porridge-pot, Sauce-pan, Candlestick, a Pewter Plate, a Sheet, Pillowbear,&c
            . the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Giles 
                     Alexander 
                  




                
            , on the 2d of June
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner was the Prosecutors Lodger, and took the Goods out of her Lodging and sold them. The Prisoner pleaded she sold them by the Prosecutors Consent; but did not call any to prove it, nor to her Reputation. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
