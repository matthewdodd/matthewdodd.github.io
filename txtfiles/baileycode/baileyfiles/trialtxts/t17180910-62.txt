








                     Jane 
                     Watkins 
                  



               
             , of St. Clement-Danes


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Spoon, value 11 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Orway 
                  



                
             , the 1st of August

 last: It appeared by the Evidence that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor stole the Spoon: the Jury thereupon found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
She was likewise indicted a 2nd time, for 


               stealing a Silver Spoon, value 8 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Watkins 
                  



                
            , the 2d of September

. It appear'd that the Prisoner was also at that time Servant to the Prosecutor, and stole the Spoon; this Fact being plain, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
