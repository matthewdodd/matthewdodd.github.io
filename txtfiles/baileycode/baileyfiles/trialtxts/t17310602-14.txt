







                     Elizabeth 
                     Woodward , alias 
Franklin




               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing divers Goods, in the Dwelling-House
             of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Bradley 
                  




                
            , the 23d of April
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner had lodg'd in the Prosecutor's House a Night or two, and took an opportunity of carrying away the Key of the Door, by which means she got into the House, and stole the Goods, which Goods were found where the Prisoner had sold them; and the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
