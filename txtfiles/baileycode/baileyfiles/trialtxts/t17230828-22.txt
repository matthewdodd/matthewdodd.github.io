







                     John 
                     Baracle 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Tobacco-Box, value 10 s. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Waldoe 
                        




, the 2d of August
. The Prosecutor depos'd, That as he was standing near his Door, about 11 a-Clock at Night, making Water, the Prisoner came behind him, and took it out of his Pocket. Another Evidence depos'd, He saw the Prisoner take something, as the thought, from the Prosecutor; and he attempting to run away he pursu'd him, and apprehended the Prisoner, who stooped down, and gave the Box a shove under some Rails. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, but it being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
