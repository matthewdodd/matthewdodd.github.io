







                     Michael 
                     Canon 
                  



               
            , of St. Magnus the Martyr


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           West 
                        




, the 19th of May
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor. And 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Gascoign 
               



             depos'd, That he saw the Prisoner sell 3 Parcels of Hemp of a Quarter of a Hundred each Parcel to one Mrs. Dier's Son, and converted the Money to his own Use, and used to give him now and then a Penny to keep his Secret. The Prisoner's Confession was read in Court, wherein he confess'd the whole Fact. However the Prisoner deny'd it at the Bar. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
