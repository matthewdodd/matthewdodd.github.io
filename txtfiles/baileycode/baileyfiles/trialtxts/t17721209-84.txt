





114. (L.) 
               
                  
                     ABIJAH
                      MANSER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a pair of mens leather shoes, value 6 d. and three mens leather shoes, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Benjamin
                      Williams
                  




               
            . +



                  Benjamin
                   Williams
               



             deposed, that the prisoner came to the shop where he was at work, and asked to buy some shoes; that he snatched five shoes out of the window.


                  Mary
                   Bishop
               



            , the daughter of 
               
                  Benjamin
                   Williams
               



            , deposed, that she saw the prisoner snatch five shoes out of the window; that she caught hold of him, and called to her father, who secured him.
Prisoner's Defence.
I heard say that this man would take away 40 lives for 40 shoes.



               Guilty
            .
