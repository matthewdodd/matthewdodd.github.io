





433. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     BAKER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of April
, three ounces weight of tea, called hyson tea, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Turner
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was seen taking the tea.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
