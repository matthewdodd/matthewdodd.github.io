





934. 
               
                  MARY 
                  PORTER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of April
, 60 yards of calico, value 30s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Hughes
                  




               
            .
JOHN HUGHES. I am a linen-draper
, and live in Chiswell-street


. On the 12th of April the prisoner took this calico, which was laying inside my door; I followed her, brought her back, and took it from her.


                  JOHN 
                  KELLY
               



            . I took the prisoner, and have the property.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 39. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
