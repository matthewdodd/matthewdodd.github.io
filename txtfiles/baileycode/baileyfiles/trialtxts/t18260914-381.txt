





1693. 
               
                  HENRY 
                  LONGLEY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of August
, 1 watch, value 18s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Berman
               




            .


                  CAROLINE 
                  BERMAN
               



            . I am the wife of John Berman, a watch-maker
, of Park-terrace, Lambeth


. On the 10th of August I saw the prisoner come to the shop, reach his hand in, and take a watch from the window - he went out with it; I went out and overtook him, when a man had stopped him; I had not lost sight of him; he had thrown it away.


                  MARIA 
                  WEBB
               



            . On the 10th of August I saw the prisoner throw this watch over the rails; I saw a man pick it up.


                  WILLIAM 
                  SELLERS
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner and the watch.(Property produced and sworn to).



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
