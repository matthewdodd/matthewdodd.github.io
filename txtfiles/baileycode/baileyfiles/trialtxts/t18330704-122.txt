





1144. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     MANNING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of June
, 1 hat, value 7s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Clamp
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  CLAMP
               



            . I live in Aldersgate-street


. I was at home on the 26th of June, when Price brought the prisoner back with this hat on his head.


                  CHARLES 
                  PRICE
               



            . I saw the prisoner go by the prosecutor's shop, take the hat, and run off with it; I ran after him, and took him to the shop - Mr. Clamp owned the hat, and sent for the officer.(Property produced and sworn to).
Prisoner's Defence. I was walking along, and had nothing to eat; I took the hat, as I thought I should get something provided for me, if I had that.



               GUILTY
            .* Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
