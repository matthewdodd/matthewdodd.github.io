





198. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     BRAHAM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of January
, a pair of stockings, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Clarke
               




            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  ANSLEY
               



            . I am an apprentice to Thomas Clarke, who is a hosier
, and lives in Barbican


. On the 3d of January, about six o'clock, I was behind the counter, and saw the line at the door jerk, where the stockings hung; I run to the door, and missed a pair, and saw the prisoner run across the road - I pursued and took him; he threw the stockings away, I picked them up.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
