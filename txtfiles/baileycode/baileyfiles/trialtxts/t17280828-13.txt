







                     Thomas 
                     Wagstaff 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's without Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief, from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Bartholomew 
                           Toon 
                        




, on the 13th of August
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That he being in the Artillery Ground


 on the 13th of Aug. seeing the Train'd Bands Exercise, miss'd his Handkerchief, and a Person telling him the Prisoner had just before pick'd his Pocket, he seiz d him, and found his own Handkerchief and four others in his Pocket.


                  Thomas 
                  Joyns 
               



             depos'd, That he saw the Prisoner pick the Handkerchief out of the Prosecutor s Pocket, and he, with the Prosecutor searching the Prisoner, found it upon him; this being likewise confirm'd by the Deposition of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Denby 
               



            , the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
            .
