





269. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WILSON
                  



               
             was indicted, for that 


               he, on the 11th of January
, feloniously, and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession a forged bank note for the payment of 1 l.
            

To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            





               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
