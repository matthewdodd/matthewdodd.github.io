





284. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     KING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of January
, ten quires of waste paper, value 3s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Cadell
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Davis
                  



               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  MITCHELL
               



            . I am a warehouse-man to Messrs. Cadell and Davis, who are book-seller

s, and have a warehouse in Drury-lane


. Whilst I was up stairs at that warehouse, some body came and took this paper off, the window being open; it was imperfect copies of Burne's Justice.


                  BENJAMIN 
                  JOHNSON
               



            . On the evening of the 19th of January, at about seven o'clock, I was in Drury-lane, near the Wheatsheaf, and I observed the prisoner coming from the Wheatsheaf. with a parcel under his arm; I stopped him, and he had this paper. The Wheatsheaf is a notorious house that thieves use.
(Property produced, and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            , aged 21.




               Transported for Seven years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
