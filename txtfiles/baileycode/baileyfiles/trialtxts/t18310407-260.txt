





955. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     TRINDELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of March
, 1 jacket, value 2s., and 1 pair of trousers, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Sedwell
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  SEDWELL
               



            . I am a gunner
 in the Royal Artillery; I occupy the adjoining room to Doubleday (see the next case.)I left it between two and three o'clock, and my box was safe - I returned at eight in the evening, and missed  my stable-dress, a pair of trousers, a jacket, and waistcoat.


                  DANIEL 
                  RIERDON
               



            . I am a Police-constable. I found the jacket and trousers on the prisoner at the New - way watch-house on the 31st of March.(Property produced and sworn 10.)
Prisoner's Defence. I bought these things of a man for 3s, as I was going to Greenwich, at half-past seven or near eight o'clock on Sunday night.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven years
            .
