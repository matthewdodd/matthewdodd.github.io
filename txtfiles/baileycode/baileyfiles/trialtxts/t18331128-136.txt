





NEW COURT. Tuesday, December 3, 1833.
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
141. 
               
                  
                     CHRISTIAN 
                     DALLINGER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of November
, 1 pair of shoes, value 11s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Gilley
                  




               
            , to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
