



3048. 




HENRY FREEMAN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
11th of Oct., 5 yards of doe-skin, value 2l.; the goods of 



Peter Shea.






GEORGE KEMP
 (police-constable N 82.) On the 11th of Oct. I saw the prisoner and a companion go to the prosecutor's shop—his companion took this doe-skin off the counter, and gave it to the prisoner who put it into his lap, and I took him with it.





WILLIAM BROCKLESS
. This is the property of Mr. Peter Shea—the officer brought the prisoner and his companion to the door with it—I did not authorize them to take it.




GUILTY
.* Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years.
