





1493. 
               
                  
                     JEREMIAH 
                     DONOVAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of May
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Taylor
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JAMES 
                  TAYLOR
               



            . I am a servant out of place
. On the 28th of May, about half-past eight o'clock in the evening, I was in St. James'-street


, and felt my handkerchief taken out of my pocket; I turned, and seized the prisoner with it in his hand - I gave him in charge.

Prisoner. Q. Can you swear I took it from you. A. I swear you had it in your hand.


                  JOHN 
                  BARRATT
               



            . I am a labourer in the London-docks. I saw the prisoner take the handkerchief from the prosecutor, who turned, and took it from him.
Prisoner. Q. Can you positively swear I took it? A. Yes; I was close to him - there were no persons between us.


                  JAMES 
                  KENNERLEY
               



             (Police-constable C 30). I took the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was going down Piccadilly on the 28th of May, which was the illumination night; I turned down St. James'-street, as there was some disturbance with a drunken man, and I saw this handkerchief on the ground - I took it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
