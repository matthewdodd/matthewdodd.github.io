



1633. 




JACK ROBERTSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
24th of June, 1 handkerchief, value 2s6d. the goods of 



Samuel Brown, from his person.







SAMUEL BROWN
. About half-past eleven o'clock, on the morning of the 24th of June, I was coming up Cheapside, and felt a tug at my pocket—I turned round and saw the prisoner at my elbow—I caught him by the are, and said "You have my handkerchief"—he said "No I have not;" and on looking round I saw it in his left hand—he dropped it on the grant—this is it—I had it in my pocket just before.

Prisoner. I had not had it in my hand. Witness. I swear I saw it in his hand.





PHILIP PARISH
. I was called, and took the prisoner and the handkerchief.

Prisoner. I did not take the gentleman's handkerchief.




GUILTY
. Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years.
