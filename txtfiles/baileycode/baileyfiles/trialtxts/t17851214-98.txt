





98. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     GLADMAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 9th day of November
 last, one silk handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Haylett
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor took the prisoner with the handkerchief upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
