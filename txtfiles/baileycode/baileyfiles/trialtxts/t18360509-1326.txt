



1326. 




JAMES BECKLEY
 was indicted

 for stealing on the 
11th of May. 1 handkerchief value 5s. the goods of 




James  Gosling, from his person






JAMES GOSLING
. I am butler to Dr. Russell, of Devonshire-square On the 11th of May, about eleven o'clock, I was walking in Newgate-street, and two men came behind me—I had just taken my handkerchief from my pocket and put it in again—the two men then crossed—I missed it, and went after them, and accused them of it—the other man then gave it to the prisoner—he dropped it and a carman picked is up—the prisoner than went off—I pursued him into Warwick-square—he could not get out, and the watchman took him.





JOHN SMITH
. I was at the watch-house—I received the prisoner, and produce the handkerchief.
Prisoner, I was not with any other man at all.





JAMES GOSLING
. Yes, he was—the other handed the handkerchief to him—he dropped it, and a carman took it up and gave it to me.




GUILTY
. Aged 18—


Transported for Seven Years.
