







                     Sarah 
                     Hughs 
                  



               
            , of S. Sepulchre's


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing six Pewter Plates, value 6s. and other Things
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Medley 
                  




                
            . It appeared that the Prisoner was a Chair-woman
 to the Prosecutor, and made use of an Opportunity of carrying away the Plates; but offering them to Sale, she was stopt, and upon Examination confest the Fact before the Justice. 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
