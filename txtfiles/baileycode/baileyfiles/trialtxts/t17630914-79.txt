                     Andrew

Mrs. Fitzgerald deposed, that she lodged in the Back lane, where 
               
                  Andrew 
                  Ruff 
               



             had a room, she hearing a noise at Ruff's door went, and there saw the prisoner sitting on the floor at Ruff's door; she called 
               
                  John 
                  Turner 
               



            .


                  John 
                  Turner 
               



             deposed, that he found a pannel of Ruff's room door broke, and the prisoner in the room; that the prisoner said the things were his own, that he had the things mentioned in a bundle.
