





444. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JEFFKINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of February
, a fender, value 30 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Evans
                  




               
            .


                  DAVID 
                  EVANS
               



            . I am a stove-great-maker
, and live in Crutched-friars. I know this fender to be mine, by the ticket on it.


                  JOHN 
                  HEMMINGS
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Evans. I saw the prisoner take this fender from my master's warehouse in Mansion House-street


. I followed him to Grocer's Hall-court, and asked where he was going with it; he said nothing. I took him back with it on his shoulder.


                  WILLIAM 
                  ODELL
               



            . I met the prisoner with the fender on his shoulder, and saw my fellow-servant stop him with it.
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 53.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
