







                     John 
                     Pope 
                  



               
            , (a little Boy
) of S. Andrew's Holborn


, was indicted for 


               stealing in the Shop of 
                     
                        William 
                        Cook 
                     




                   , a Wooden Drawer value 1d. and a Guinea and 47s. in Silver
            , on the 24th of Feb
. It appear'd that the Prisoner seeing no body in the Shop, crept in, took out the Drawer, and ran a way with it; but being observed by a Neighbour, he was quickly stopt. The Jury found him 

               guilty
             of Felony. 


               Transportation
