





2054. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     JAMESON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of August
, 6 books, value 24s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Peter 
                           Wright
                        




                     
                  ; and that he had been before convicted of felony
            .


                  JOHN 
                  POYNTON
               



            . I was employed in making a catalogue of books for 
               
                  Peter 
                  Wright
               



            , of High-street, St. Giles'


. On the 22nd of August, about half-past five o'clock in the evening, I received information, and the prisoner was pointed out to me - I pursued, and took him about five hundred yards from Mr. Wright's; he thrust some books into my hand, and started off again - they were six volumes of Moore's Life of Byron; he was secured without getting out of my sight; I gave him and the books to the officer.


                  ROBERT 
                  KNIGHT
               



            . I am a Policeman. I received him in charge with the books.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  WILLIAM 
                  PICKARD
               



            . I have a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I received from Mr. Clark's office - (read) - I am sure he is the man.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 26. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
The prisoner had been twice convicted before.
