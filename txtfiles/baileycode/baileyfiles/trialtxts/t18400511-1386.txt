





1386. 



HENRY GARDENER,


 MARK BARKER
, and 



JAMES SMITH
, were indicted for

 stealing, on the 
1st of May, 3 spoons, value 1l. 5s.; and 1 fork, value 5s.; the goods of 



Charles William Elwood; to which they severally pleaded





GUILTY
.—




Transported for Seven Years,—Isle of Wight.
