





439. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WILSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 20th day of March
 last, forty four pounds weight of lead, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Thompson
                  




               
             and others.


                  Edward
                   Ryland
               



             stopped the prisoner with the lead, and he confessed stealing it from the prosecutor.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   EYRE
               



            .
