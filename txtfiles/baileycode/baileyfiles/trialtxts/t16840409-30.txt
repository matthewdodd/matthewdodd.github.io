







                  John 
                  Francis
               



             Indicted for 


               stealing a piece of Dowlas, containing many Ells, out of a 
                     
Linnen Draper




                  's Shop in New Fish street



: the Servant of the Draper seeing him snatch it out, and running away, made such quick Pursuit, that he dropt the Dowlas at the Door; and before he got out of the Street was taken. The Prisoner pretended he found it; but that appearing a common Excuse, he was found 

               Guilty
            .
