





760. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  M'GEE
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d day of August
 last, one wicker basket, called a flat, value 6 d. and fifty-one pounds weight of butter, value 30 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Markham
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the flat and butter upon him.
Prisoner. A man hired me to carry it.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   THOMPSON
               



            .
