







                     Richard 
                     Kilburn 
                  



               
            , of St. Paul's Shadwell


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Ann 
                           Fletcher 
                        




                      
                   a Parcel of Silk Handkerchiefs
            , the 3d of this Instant October
. It appear'd by the Evidence that the Prisoner was seen by the Prosecutor to come into her Shop, and she immediately coming into the Shop and seizing him, he threw down the Handkerchiefs into a Chair, he having gotten them under a Rug Coat. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d.
