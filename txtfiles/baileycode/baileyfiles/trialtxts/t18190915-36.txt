





1118. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PERKINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of August
, one handkerchief, value 10 d., the property of 
                     
                        
                           Samuel 
                           Dunstone
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  SAMUEL 
                  DUNSTONE
               



            . On Sunday the 29th of August, about nine o'clock at night, I was in Fleet-street


, with afriend; I felt somebody touch my pocket, turned round, and missed my handkerchief. I saw the prisoner run from me, pursued him, and saw him throw it down. I never lost sight of him, till he was secured.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Life
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
