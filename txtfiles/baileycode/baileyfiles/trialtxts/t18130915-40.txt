





865. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     PHILLIPS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 10th of May
, a handkerchief, value 3 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Upton
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  UPTON
               



            . I am a tailor
. On Monday, the 10th of May, I was in Oxford-street, coming past Cumberland-gate


. I felt something at my pockets; I turned round and my handkerchief was in the prisoner's hand; the officer, Bacon, was by and took him in custody.
- BACON. I am an officer. On coming through Cumberland-gate, I saw the prisoner draw from Mr. Upton's pocket this handkerchief.
Prisoner's Defence. I know nothing at all about it; I work hard for my living.
The prisoner called two witnesses, who gave him a good character.



               GUILTY
            , aged 24.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Justice Heath.
