



1399. 




JAMES HAYES
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
10th of June, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 



Samuel Jordan.






SAMUEL JORDAN
. I live in Gracechurch-street. At half-past three o'clock, on the of June, I was looking behind over London Bridge, and fast a twitch at my pocket—I turned my hand behind me, and caught the prisoner's hand, and with my left hand I drew my handkerchief from him—I then turned round, and it was the prisoner I had hold of—I collared him and kept him.

Prisoner. The gentleman turned and took hold of me—it was not my hand he had hold of. Witness. I did not leave go of his hand till I turned and took hold of his collar—he asked me particularly to let him go this once.





JAMES HARRIS
. (City police-constable, No. 73.) I was on duty, and was called to take the prisoner.


Prisoner. I never saw the handkerchief till think very moment—there was a great crowd behind me.




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.
