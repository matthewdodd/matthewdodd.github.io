





289. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of January
, a pair of reins, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Bannister
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  BANNISTER
               



            . I am a carman
, and live in Castle-court, Oxford-market. On the 8th of January, about five o'clock in the evening, I was taking a pianoforte into a public-house in Long-lane


, my caravan was outside. I came down in ten minutes, and two officers brought the prisoner into the tap-room, with these reins, which are mine.


                  JOHN 
                  CARLILE
               



            . I am an officer. I watched the prisoner from Cloth-fair into Long-lane, saw him take these reins off the horse, and secured him.


                  JOSEPH 
                  MATTHEWS
               



            . I was with Carlile; he appeared distressed.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
