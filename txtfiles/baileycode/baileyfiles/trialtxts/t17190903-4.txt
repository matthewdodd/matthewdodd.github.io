







                     Mary 
                     Wood 
                  



               
            , of Alhallows the Less


 , was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing half a Guinea and 2 Holland Necks
             from 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Tennant 
                  




                
             on the 18th of July
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that he was a Ticket Porter
, and having been at work on the Keys, went to an Alehouse in Bear Lane


 by the Custom-house to drink, and sat in the Publick Room; that the Prisoner sat down by him, and he pulling out his Money (half a Guinea and a Sixpence) to pay his Reckoning she clapt hold of the half Guinea, put it into her Mouth and sollowed it; and pickt the two Necks out of his Pocket. The Prisoner in her Defence said, that the Prosecutor ask'd her to whip him, and brought Rods; that she did whip him behind, and he not being contented with that, gave her half a Guinea to whip him before. But the Jury not believing her, and the two Necks being found upon her, found her 

               Guilty
            .
