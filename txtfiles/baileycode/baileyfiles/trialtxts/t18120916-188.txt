





811. 
               
                  
                     MARY 
                     SQIRES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 30th of August
, a pewter pint pot, value 14 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     West
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  WEST
               



            . I live at the 
               
                  Montague 
                  Arms
               



            , Manchester-square. I can only say it is my pot.
- . I am a pot-boy. I saw the prisoner take the pot from 16, in Montague-place


. I asked her, what she was going to do with the pot. She flung it down. I went and told my master. She was taken. She had two pint pots on her then, one my master's, and one the prosecutor's. This is my master's pot.
Prisoner's Defence. I lost my way. I do not know any thing about it.



               GUILTY
            , aged 40.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
