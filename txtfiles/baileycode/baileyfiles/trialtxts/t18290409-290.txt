





NEW COURT, Seventh Day.
Fourth Middlesex Jury - Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
969. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     TAYLOR
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of April
, 1 gown, value 4s.; 1 shawl, value 3s.; 1 petticoat, value 1s., and 1 yard of black velvet, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Harriet 
                     Digby
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
