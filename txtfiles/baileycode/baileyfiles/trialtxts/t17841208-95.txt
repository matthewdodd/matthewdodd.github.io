






103. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     BRETT
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 26th day of November
 last, one linen handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  Emmerson 
                  Tidsly
               




            .
The Prosecutor caught the prisoner in the fact, with his handkerchief in his hand.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. Justice NARES.
