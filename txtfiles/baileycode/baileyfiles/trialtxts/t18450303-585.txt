



585. 




CHARLES SMITH
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
8th of Nov., at 
St. Botolph Without, Bishopsgate, 2 bags, value 1s.; 174 sovereigns, 2 half-sovereigns, 34 half-crowns, 219 shillings, 203 sixpences, 2 groats, 2 £20, 16 £10, and 18 £5 Bank-notes; the property of 



Frederick De Lisle, in his dwelling-house; to which he pleaded.





GUILTY
. Aged 23.—


Transported for Ten Years.
