





685. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  SUTTE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of March
, two shirts, value 3s.; two shifts, value 2s.; two petticoats, value 4s.; two caps, value 2s.; one handkerchief, value 6d., and one pillow-case, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Clara 
                     Brown
                  




               
            .


                  CLARA 
                  BROWN
               



            . I live in Weston-street, Somers' Town


. On the 6th of March, between seven and eight o'clock in the evening, I missed the things from my door.


                  WILLIAM 
                  READ
               



            . I am an officer. I apprehended the prisoner on the 10th of March, at Somers' Town; I found a duplicate of the shirt and shifts on him, and took a shirt off his back.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
