







                     Henry 
                     Cranfield 
                  



               
            , of St. Botolph without Aldersgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a silver Watch, Case and Chain, value 35 s.
             on the 10th of October
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that the Prisoner coming to see him the Day aforesaid, he bid him set down and warm himself, that he took the Watch out of the Kitchen and sold it; which he confest before the Justice. His Confession was read in Court: and the Jury found him 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
