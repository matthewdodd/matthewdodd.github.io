



1263. 



CATHERINE STOKES
 was again indicted

for stealing, on the 
18th of April, 1 shawl, 1 value 6d.; 3 yards of edging, value 2d.; 3 yards of carpet, value 1s.; 1 coat, value 2s.; and 1 frock, value 6d.; the goods of 



John Henry Cherry.






WILLIAM SHAW
. I am a policeman. I apprehended the prisoner on the 19th of April, and found this shawl on her under the other—I found nothing else on her.





SARAH CHERRY
. I am the wife of John Henry Cherry, a weaver, in Manchester-street, Bethnal-green. The prisoner lives in our neighbourhood—on the 18th of April I had to go to the hospital, and left her in care of my place—when I came back, she was gone, and I missed these things—this is my shawl—I have never seen the other things since.




GUILTY
.—


Transported for Seven Years.Recommended to the Penitentiary
