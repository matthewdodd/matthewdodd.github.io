






FOURTH DAY. MONDAY, JUNE 26.
Middlesex Cases, Fourth Jury,
Before Mr. Common Serjeant.
1270. 
               
                  JOSEPH 
                  PARSONS
               



             and 
               
                  GEORGE 
                  HILSLEY
               



             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of June
, 1 basket, value 2s., and 30 carpenter's tools, value 2l. 14s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Kippen
               




            .
The prisoners 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .





               Transported for Seven Years
            .
