





2072. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BARRETT
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of September
, 1 tea-chest, value 10s., and 1 sugar-basin, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Richard 
                  Gould
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
