





SECOND DAY. FRIDAY, JULY 4.
Fifth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1434. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  DONALD
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of June
, 1 fork, value 5s., and 1 spoon, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William Henry 
                  Trant
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
