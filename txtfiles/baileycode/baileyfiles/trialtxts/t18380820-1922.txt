



1922. 



EDWARD WADHAM LAWSON
 was indicted

for forging, on the 
26th of July, at 
St. Swithin, London-stone, a certain order for the payment of £25, with intent to defraud



 James Thomas De La Salie.-2nd COUNT, for uttering the same; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
.—


Transported for Seven Years.
