





626. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BROWN
               



             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 1st day of July
, one linen handkerchief, value 9 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Forrester
                  




               
            .
(
               
                  William
                   Forrester
               



             called on his recognizance, and did not appear.)


                  WILLIAM
                   TABERNACLE
               



             sworn.
I followed the prisoner, and I saw him pick the prosecutor's pocket of a handkerchief; I stopped him, and took the handkerchief from him and gave it to the constable.


                  WILLIAM
                   WARREN
               



             sworn.
I produce the handkerchief marked W. F.
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
I was coming up Holborn, I had been drinking.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
