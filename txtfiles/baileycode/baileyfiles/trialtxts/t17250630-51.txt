







                  Martha 
                  Blaithwaite , alias 
Braithwait




            , of Wapping


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing two Gowns val. 27 s. a Petticoat val. 6 s. 6 d. three Scarves val. 15 s. three Hoods val. 12 s. thirty Yards of Lace val. 10 s. and other Things, the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Elizabeth 
                           Mitchell 
                        




                      
                  , in the Shop of Eliz. Mitchell
            , on the 18th of January
 last. It appear'd that the Prisoner took a Shop (or a Stall) on the Backside of the Prosecutor's Shop in Rag-Fair


, there being only a thin Deal Partition betwixt them, Part of which was taken down in the Night, and the Goods removed into the Prisoner's Shop. 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
