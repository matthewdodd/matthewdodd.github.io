







                     Thomas 
                     Lazenby 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Shoes and a Slipper, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Croucher 
                        




, the 24th of December
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Shoes were stolen out of the Prosecutor's Shop-Window, and there being an Outcry made, the Prisoner was stopp'd as he was running away, having a Slipper, and some of the Shoes, he having dropp'd the rest, which were taken up. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
