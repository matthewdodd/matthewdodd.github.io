



2726. 




WILLIAM JOHNSON
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
12th of October, 1 pewter pot, value 1s., the goods of 



James Perring.






JAMES PERRING
. I am a publican, and live in King David-street, Berkeley-square. On Saturday, the 12th of October, I lost a pot out of my yard.





THOMAS WALTON
. I am a coachman. I saw the prisoner on the prosecutor's premises—I suspected him, took., him into custody, and found this pint pot in his hat.





DAVIS LEWIS
. I am a policeman. I searched him at the station-house, and found part of another pot in his trowsers pocket.
(Property produced and sworn to.)




GUILTY
. Aged 34.—


Transported for Seven Years.
