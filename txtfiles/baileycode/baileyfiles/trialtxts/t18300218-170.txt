





Fourth Middlesex Jury. - Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
571. 
               
                  JOSEPH 
                  SPENCER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of January
, 1 watch, value 6s.; 3 pencil-cases, value 3s.; 9 pieces of silver, value 5s.; 1 tooth-pick, value 6d., and 1 crown
            , the property of 
               
                  John 
                  Spencer
               




            ; to which the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
