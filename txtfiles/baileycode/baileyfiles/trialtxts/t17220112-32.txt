







                     Francis 
                     Wing
                  



               
            , of Wapping


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 20 Oak knees, val. 15 s. and 6 Wainscot Boards, 6 s.
             the Good of 
               
                  
                     Francis 
                     Barrow
                  




               
            , on the 19th of December
 last. It appear'd that the prisoner took the Goods out of the Prosecutor's Yard at Execution Dock, and carried them by Water to Paul's Stairs, where being stopt, he confest the Fact. His Confession was read in Court, and he not denying it, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
