





529. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     SULLIVAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of February
, one firkin, value 1s., and 56lbs of butter, value 2l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Jones
               




            .
GRORGE JONES. I am a cheesemonger
, living in Whitecross-street


. On he 21st of February, I missed my butter from the side of my door. Some person directed me the wrong way. My boy got the firkin, and told me which way the prisoner was gone. I found him in a privy in Chequer-alley.


                  JAMES 
                  SUMMERS
               



            . I saw the prisoner run down the alley with the butter; he tried to get over a wall. I am sure the prisoner is the man.


                  JAMES 
                  NEWMAN
               



            . I was in the alley, and saw the prisoner drop the butter. I am sure the prisoner is the man.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
