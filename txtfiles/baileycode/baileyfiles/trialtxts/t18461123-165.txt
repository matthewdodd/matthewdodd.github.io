




165. 




JOHN SCOTT
 and 




JOHN MITCHELL
 were indicted for

 burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 



Thomas Hawley, in the night of the 
6th of Nov., at 
Christchurch, and stealing 4lbs. weight of cheese, value 2s. 6d. his goods: and 1 coat, value 4s. 6d.; 1 umbrella, 6d.; and 1 handkerchief, 2s.; the goods of 



William Emanuel Eades; to which they pleaded


SCOTT—



GUILTY
. Aged 19.

MITCHELL—



GUILTY
. Aged 22





Transported for seven Years.
