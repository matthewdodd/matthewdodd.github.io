



2283. 




CHARLES CURTIS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
24th of July, at 
St. James's, Westminster, 5 sovereigns, and 1 £5 Bank note, the monies and property of 



Edward Curtis, his master, in his dwelling-house; to which he pleaded





GUILTY. Aged 21.—Recommended to mercy.
—


Transported for Ten Years.
