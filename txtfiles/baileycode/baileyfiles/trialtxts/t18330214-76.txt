                     THOMAS EMANUEL 
                     DAY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of January
, 1 pair of trousers, value 18s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Day
                        



                     
                  ; and 1 watch, value 20s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Jane 
                           Hawkes
                        



.


                  RICHARD 
                  DAY
               



            . I am the prisoner's brother - I lodge with my mother and the prisoner; I lost my trousers, and my aunt lost a watch which was hanging in the same room; my brother left home on the 15th of January, he came back on the Saturday evening and gave himself up; he is a tailor, but has been living with my mother for the last three years.


                  GEORGE 
                  KEDGE
               



            . I am a pawnbroker; I have a watch, pawned by the prisoner on the 15th of January.


                  JOHN 
                  WILCER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker; I have a pair of trousers, pawned by the prisoner on the 15th of January.


                  JANE 
                  HAWKES
               



            . I am great aunt of the prisoner. This watch is mine, and was in the room.


                  RICHARD 
                  DAY
               



            . These are my trowsers.
Prisoner. I leave it to the mercy of the Court.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
