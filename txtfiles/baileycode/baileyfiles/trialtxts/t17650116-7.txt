            , December 13
. ||
Jerrard Bourn. I and 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Dunnavan 
               



             are chairmen

, and partners. On the 14th of December, we missed the things mentioned in the indictment, out-of our chair.


                  Henry 
                  Flood 
               



            . The prisoner came into a cook's shop, in Gray's inn-lane, with the curtains and seat; I thought he was no chairman, and stopped him, and soon found they belonged to the prosecutors. (Produced and deposed to).
Prisoner's Defence.
A man gave me six-pence to carry the things to Field-lane.
