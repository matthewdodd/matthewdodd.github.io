








                     James 
                     Harvey 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Roger 
                     King 
                  



                
             of St. Botolph without Aldgate



, were indicted for 


               breaking the House of 
                     
                        
                           Charles 
                           Hughes 
                        




                      
                   on the 28th of October
 last in the Night-time, and taking thence 7 Hats value 3 l.
             The Prosecutor deposed that he lost 7 Hats out of his Shop, between 9 and 10 a Clock the Night aforesaid, but could not swear the Door was bolted. 
               
                  Edward 
                  Lloyd 
               



             deposed that the Prisoners and himself committed the Fact, that King open'd the Door, and Harvey took the Hats; that they sold them for 12 s. and divided the Money. The Evidence not being sufficient to prove the Buglary, the Jury Acquitted them of that, and found them 


               Guilty of Felony only
            . 



               Transportation
