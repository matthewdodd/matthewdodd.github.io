                  Margaret 
                  Tucker 
               



            . Last Monday, I saw the prisoner and another man standing by a hogshead of tobacco, the prisoner was taking some out; I asked him if that tobacco belonged to him? he said yes. I said, I would call for assistance; he ran away, and was stopped with 17 lb. and an half of Tobacco, bag and all, by Mr. Sedgwick.


                  John 
                  Sedgwick 
               



            . I am clerk to Mr. Longden on Smart's key. I knowing 
               
                  Margaret 
                  Tucker 
               



            's voice, ran and seized the prisoner with the tobacco upon him, to the amount of about 17 lb. weight; the hogshead from whence he took it, belonged to 
               
                  Robert 
                  Fairbane 
               



            , and 
               
                  William 
                  Trevers 
               



            , of Hull.
Prisoner's Defence.
I was going to Billingsgate, to get some fish; a person said, if I would carry that parcel for him, he would give me something. I took the bundle, and walked away, and was seized with it directly.
