








                  Miles 
                  Allison  alias 
Coatsworth




            , and 
               
                  Mary 
                  Allison 
               



             , of St. Martin's in the Fields



, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Damask Gown, and Holland
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Seneff 
                  




                
            , the 1st of this Instant August
. It appear'd by the Evidence that the Prisoners lodg'd in the Prosecutor's House. The Goods were found where they were pawn'd, by Miles Allison. The Jury found Miles Allison 


               guilty to the value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            . And 

               acquitted
             Mary Allison.
