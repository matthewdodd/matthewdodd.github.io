







                     Zephaniah 
                     Martin 
                  



               
             of St. Botolph without Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Perriwig, value 2 l. 2s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Hewet 
                        




                      
                   in the House of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Limes 
                     



, on the 25th of April
 last. - Limes deposed that the Prisoner came into his Father's Shop, and ask'd for a Gentleman, who he said, was to meet him there; and that he took up the News Papers, walked about, and gave him his Wig to comb, which while he was doing, he observed the Prisoner to take the Wig mentioned in the Indictment, and put it up his Back under his Coat; that when he had done combing his Wig he gave him 2 d. and was going away; that he let him go to the Door, then seized him, and got the Neighbours to secure him while he call'd a Constable; that he gave him the Wig from under his Coat, and offer'd him a Ring and Money, desiring him for God's sake not to Prosecute him. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
