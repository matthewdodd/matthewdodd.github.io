





220. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     MINCE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of February
, a glass jar, value 3 s. eight pounds and three quarters of hair powder, value 7 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Salter
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Cox
                  



               
            .
Mrs. Cox saw the prisoner take the jar, and he was taken immediately.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
