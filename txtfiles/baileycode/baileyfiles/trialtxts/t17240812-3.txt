







                     John 
                     Heath 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchres


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2 Hogs, value 36 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Taylor 
                  




                
            , the 25th of July
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Hogs were lost out of the Prosecutor's Neighbour's Yard, at Westminster


, and were found upon the Prisoner, selling them at Smithfield. The Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
