





634. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     ASHTON
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               that he, on the 18th of July
, feloniously, and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession, a forged bank-note for the payment 10 l. and that he had another bank-note for the payment of 10 l.; and that he had, on the same day, another bank-note for the payment of 10 l.
            

To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
