





530. 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Spencer 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing one handkerchief, val. 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Simpson 
                     Levi 
                  




               
            .

July 17
.
The prisoner pick'd the prosecutor's pocket of the handkerchief coming through Ludgate


; he was detected in the fact; he flung down the handkerchief, and run a little way; but was taken and begg'd for mercy.




               Guilty 10 d
            .
