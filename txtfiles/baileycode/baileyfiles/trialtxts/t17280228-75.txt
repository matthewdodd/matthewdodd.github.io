







                     Hannah 
                     Cartwright 
                  



                
            , of St. Anne's, Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Holland Shirt, a Silk Petticoats, and other wearing Apparel
            , on the 30th of December
 last, the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Purdis 
                  




                
            .
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner being a miserable Object, he took her out of the Streets, and employ'd her as a Servant
, but missing his Goods, he suspected her, and accordingly charged her with the taking them. which she confess'd, and in Consequence of her Confession, the Jury found her 

               guilty
             of single Felony.
