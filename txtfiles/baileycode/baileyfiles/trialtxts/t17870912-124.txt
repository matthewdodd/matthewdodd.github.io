





794. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     HAGAR
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th day of August
 last, four shirts, value 10 s. a child's frock, value 1 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Robert 
                           Ball
                        



                     
                  ; an apron, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Ferrell
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the things upon her.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
