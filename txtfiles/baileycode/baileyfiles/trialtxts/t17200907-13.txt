







                     Thomas 
                     Grant 
                  



               
            , of St.Bennet Gracechruch


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief value 3 s. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Zachary 
                           Polock 
                        




, on the 15 th of July
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that going by Lombard-street


 he felt a Hand in his Pocket, whereupon he turn'd short, and seized the Prisoner with his Handkerchief in his Hand. The Prisoner denied the Fact but called none to his Reputation. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
