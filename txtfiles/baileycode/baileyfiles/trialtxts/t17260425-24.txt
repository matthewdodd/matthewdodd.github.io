







                     Mary 
                     Tyler 
                  



               
            , of St. Anns Westminster


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Cotton Gown, value 14 s.
             The Goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Granfield 
                  




                
            , on the 2 of March
.


                  Elizabeth 
                  Granfield 
               



             thus depos'd. I washt my Gown and hung it up to dry in my Landladys Celler, where I lodge; I went out and left none but my Landlady and the Prisoner there, and when I came back my Gown was gone. - We went to her lodgings and found her Drunk, and she confest that she had taken my Gown, and shew'd us where she had pawn'd it. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s 10.
