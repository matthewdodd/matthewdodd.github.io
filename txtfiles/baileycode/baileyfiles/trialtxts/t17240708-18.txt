







                  Ann, the Wife
 of 
                     
                        John 
                        Pugh 
                     







            , of the Parish of Whitechapel


, was indicted for 


               stealing divers Goods, value 4 l. and 4 Guineas
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Britton 
                  




                
            , the 26th of February
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was hired to carry the Trunk of Goods, &c. from Frying-Pan Alley into Whitechapel, and over against Whitechapel Church


 she gave the Prosecutor's Wife the Drop, turning up Black-Lion Yard. The Jury found her 

               guilty
            .
