





488. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  HOPKINS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of January
, 29 yards of flannel, value 1l. 18s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Castle
                  




               
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  OTTLEY
               



            . I am shopman to 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Castle
               



            , who is a hosier
, and lives in Oxford-street


. On the 30th of January, between seven and eight o'clock in the evening, I saw a young man come into the shop, take up the flannel, and giveit to the prisoner, who ran away with it. I pursued, calling out Stop thief! - He threw it down - I collared him. I am sure he is the man.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
