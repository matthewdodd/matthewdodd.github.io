







                     Ann 
                     Skerlet 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Copper Pot
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Mosely 
                  




                
            , the 4th of this Instant January
. It appeared by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's Lodger, and carried away the Goods. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
