





1770. 
               
                  
                     FREDERICK 
                     CAREY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of July
, 1 work-box, value 8s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Morgan
                  




               
            .
DAVID MORGAN. I am a broker
, and live at Kensington-terrace


. I had a work-box in my shop - on the 19thof July I missed it, and went into the street; I saw the prisoner running, two hundred yards off, with it under his arm; he threw it on the ground, and a boy stopped him in my presence - this is it.


                  RICHARD 
                  REEVES
               



            . I live at a green-grocer's. I saw the prisoner take the box.


                  WILLIAM 
                  MOORE
               



             (Police-constable T 63). I was passing by, and took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
