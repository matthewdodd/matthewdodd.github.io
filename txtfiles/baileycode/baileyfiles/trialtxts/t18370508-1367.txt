




1367. 




JOSEPH MILLER
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
6th of March, 1 coat, value 1l. 1s., the goods of 



Richard Moser, his master: 


also, on the 
15th of March, 1 coat, value 1l.; 1 towel, value 6d.; and 1 pair of boots, value 8s.; the goods of 



Richard Moser, his master; to both of which he pleaded.




GUILTY
. Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Recommended to the Penitentiary.
