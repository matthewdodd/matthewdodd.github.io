





55. 
               
                  
                     JOLEPH 
                     ANDERSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of November
, one handkerchief, value 3 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Thompson
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  THOMPSON
               



            . On the 7th of November, in the morning, I was coming out of a narrow court into Birchin-lane


, and thought somebody pressed against me. I saw my handkerchief pass from one man's hand to another. turned round, and saw the prisoner stuffing it into his breeches. I collared him, and gave him in charge.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Life
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
