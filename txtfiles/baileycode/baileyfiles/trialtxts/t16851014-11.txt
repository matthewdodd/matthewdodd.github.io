                  , of Hellingdon


, Middlesex, September the 30th
. with a Rapier value 2 s. on the left Pap. of which Wound the said Pocher Died
            . It appeared that eight Souldiers going into an House to Drink, after they had Drank some time, ran away without paying any thing for it, pursued by the Man of the House for his Reckoning: upon his demand thereof, the Souldiers Drew, and one of them Killed one Pocher: The Souldiers in their Defence say, that they were Assaulted, and Turner in his Defence had his Scabbard struck from his Sword. and then he knew not but he might prick one of them. They were brought in all 


               guilty of Man-slaughter
            .
