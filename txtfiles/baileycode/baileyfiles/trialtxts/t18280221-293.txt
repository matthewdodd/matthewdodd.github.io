





771. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  JOHNSTON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of February
, 6 lbs. weight of beef, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Smellie
               




            .
THOMAS SMELLIE. I am a butcher
, and live at Shadwell. I was in my shop at ten o'clock at night, on the 23d of February, and saw the prisoner take this beef from my window, just inside the shop; I called the watchman, and gave him in charge directly.


                  EVAN 
                  THOMAS
               



            . I am a watchman. I was called, and took the prisoner and beef.


                  THOMAS 
                  SLACK
               



            . I am headborough. The prisoner was brought to the watch-house; I found 9 1/2d on him.
Prisoner. I leave it to the mercy of the court.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 53.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
