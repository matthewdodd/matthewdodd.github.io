



1781. 




DENNIS CONNOR
. was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
8th of August, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 



George Berkeley Harrison, from his person.






GEORGE BERKELEY HARRISON
. I live at Stoke Newington. On the 8th of August, I was passing down Gracechurch-street—I felt some intimation at my pocket—I felt, and my handkerchief was safe, but the prisoner was close to me—I went on to the Flower Pot—I again felt something—I turned and saw the prisoner close to me, and missed my handkerchief—he threw it down—I took it up, and took him.

Prisoner. It was not me picked his pocket—I was going to pick it up to give it him—there were two more boys there. Witness. There was no one near me but him that could have taken it.





SALLY HALL
. I saw the prisoner have the handkerchief—he threw it down by me—I saw this gentleman take him and the handkerchief.




GUILTY
.*Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.
