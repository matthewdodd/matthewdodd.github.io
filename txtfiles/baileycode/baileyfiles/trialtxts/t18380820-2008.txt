THOMAS SHAW
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
21st of August, 4 boots, value 1l., the goods of 



George Hollingsworth.






GEORGE HOLLINGSWORTH
. I keep a shoe shop, in High-street, Camden-town. On the 21st of August, about eight o'clock in the evening, I was in my shop—I cast my eye to the window, and the prisoner was taking my boots away—they were on the pane of the window outside—he had got these four down—(looking at them)-and there were four others missing—he ran off, and—I ran and took him-two of these are odd ones—the others are a pair—I have lost four others—I was told there were two other persons with the prisoner, but I did not see them.





JAMES RICHARDS
. I am a policeman. I took the prisoner.




GUILTY
. † Aged 25.—


Transported for Seven Years.
