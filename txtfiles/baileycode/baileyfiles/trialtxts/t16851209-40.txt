







                     George 
                     Smith
                  



               
             was Tryed for 


               Stealing a Silver Tankard, value 3 l.
             from 
               
                  
                     Jane 
                     Philips
                  




               
             of the Parish of St. Andrews Holborn


 on the 20th of November
; upon his Tryal it appeared that the Prosecutor being a Servant
, was sent out for Drink in the said Tankard, and calling with it at a Tallow-Chandlers Shop, whilst She was buying Candles, the Tankard was taken from the Compter by a little Boy whom the Prisoner had set on work, and that they made their Escape; to this he pleaded ignorance, but the Evidence being positive as to his being there, and giving the Boy Mony, as it was supposed, for his better incouragement, he was found 

               Guilty
             of the Felony.
