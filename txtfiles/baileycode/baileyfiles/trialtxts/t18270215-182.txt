





675. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  SHEFFIELD
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of February
, 2 printed books, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Wilton
               



             and 
               
                  William 
                  Wilton
               



            .
JOHN WILTON. I am a bookseller

, in partnership with my son - we live in High Holborn


. I know these books, by the labels, which are partly torn off; I did not see them taken - they were just inside the window.


                  GEORGE 
                  WADDINGTON
               



            . I am an officer. On the 8th of February, about twenty minutes past seven o'clock, I was near the prosecutors', and saw the prisoner and two others walking backwards and forwards by their shop; they then crossed over to me; I secured the prisoner, and found six books in his hat - these are two of them; he said, "Waddington, you know I am a bookbinder - they are all my own;" my partner took the other two, but the bill is thrown out against them.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I am a bookbinder

; there are a thousand of these books - I sell books in the street, and bought these at the corner of Fleet-street.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 42.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
There was another Indictment against the prisoner.
