





97. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SIMCOE
                  




               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of November
, one watch, value 3l., and one seal, value 18s., the property of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Boxhall
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
RICHARD BOXHALL. I am a marine
. On the 25th of November I was at Execution Dock


, between eleven and twelve o'clock in the morning, the prisoner came up to me; I thought I felt my watch moving; the officer took my watch out of the prisoner's pocket.
WILLIAM MOODY. I am an officer. I saw the prisoner put the watch into his pocket, and I took it out.
(Produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I saw the watch laying on the ground, I picked it up, and asked if any body had lost one. I was answered, "No." I put it into my pocket, and the officer took me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 27.




               Transported for Life
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
