





657. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     BRUCE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th day of June
, three pair of mens leather shoes, value 6 s. and a pair of plated buckles, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Falkener
                  




               
            .


                  Ann
                   Gurney
               



             took the prisoner in the prosecutor's house, with the property upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
