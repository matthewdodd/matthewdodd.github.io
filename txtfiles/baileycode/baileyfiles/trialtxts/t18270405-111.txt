





864. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  HARPER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of March
, 2 cakes, value 7s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Partington
               




            .


                  HANNAH 
                  BAKER
               



            . I am servant to Thomas Partington, a confectioner
 of Oxford-street


. I saw the prisoner come, and take a melon cake out of the window on the 7th of March; he ran up Bond-street - I followed, and did not lose sight of him; he was taken, and two cakes were found on him; they were worth 7s.


                  ANDREW 
                  VALLANCE
               



            . I am the officer. I took the prisoner, and produce the cakes; he said he took them for hunger.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
