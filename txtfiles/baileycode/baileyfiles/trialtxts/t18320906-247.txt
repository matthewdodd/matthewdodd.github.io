





1976. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     CREASLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of July
, 1 pair of boots, value 13s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Mark 
                     Kerslake
                  




               
            .


                  MARK 
                  KERSLAKE
               



            . I am a boot and shoemaker
, and live in the Strand


. These are my boots.


                  CHARLES 
                  HIVOUTTERS
               



            . I am a peruke-maker. I was near the prosecutor's shop, on the 20th of July, and saw the prisoner take a pair of boots down from the window, with the ticket; I ran after him - I found I could not overtake him, and called the Policeman.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BADDLEY
               



            . On the day stated, the prisoner came and took a pair of boots off the door of the prosecutor's shop - I pursued him, and never lost sight of him till he was taken - these are the boots.
Prisoner's Defence. I am quite innocent; I did not take them - a man gave them to me to hold.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
There was another indictment against the prisoner.
