





335. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Benjamin
                      Payne
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing one silk handkerchief value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert
                      Bestland
                  




               
            , May 13
. +


                  Robert
                   Bestland
               



            . I was going down Cheapside near the end of Newgate-street


, a butcher informed me the prisoner had picked my pocket of my handkerchief. I had observed the prisoner had followed me some time; he ran, I pursued him and called out stop thief; he was taken at the north door of St. Paul's. I took him to Guildhall immediately.


                  Richard
                   Nash
               



            . I searched the prisoner at Guildhall, and found the handkerchief wraped up in a foul sheet which he had in his hat, he denied having it when he came to Guildhall.
Prisoner's Defence.
I picked the handkerchief off the ground.



               Guilty
            , 


               T
            .
