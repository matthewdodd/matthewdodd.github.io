







                     Sarah 
                     Broomhall 
                  



               
            , of St. Andrew's Holbourn


, was indicted for 


               privately taking three Guineas and a half from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Smith 
                        




, the 18th of June
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That he having been drinking, was going home, and in Fleet-street was met by the Prisoner and one 
               
                  Sarah 
                  Copington 
               



            , who carried him home to the Prisoner's Lodging, and there setting him down in a Chair, the Prisoner unbuttoned his Breeches, and took his Money; that Copington went away before him, that afterwards the Prisoner running out, and crying out, where are you? He fearing she was calling some of her Bullies, who would Murther him, got up, and went home; and coming to the Prisoner the next Morning, charg'd her with taking his Money.


                  Mary 
                  Heather 
               



             depos'd, That the Prisoner own'd to her she had got a Guinea from the Prosecutor in the Street, and two in the House, and that her Husband had met her, and got it from her, and had lost 35 s. of it the next Night in Gaming, saying, she might go a Whoring and Thieving all her Days to no purpose.


                  Mary 
                  Hussey 
               



             confirm'd the former Evidence; the Fact being plain, the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
