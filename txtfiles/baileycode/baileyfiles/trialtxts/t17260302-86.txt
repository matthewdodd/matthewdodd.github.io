It appear'd that Mary Philips was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and being left at home with only a Child. She bundled up the Goods, put them into a Coach, and she and Revel went away with them. They were taken up, and the Goods found upon. Philips was found 

               guilty
            , and Revel 

               acquitted, but she was order'd to remain till next Sessions, to be then try'd for feloniously receiving the said Goods
