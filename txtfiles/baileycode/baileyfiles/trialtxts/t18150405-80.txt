





503. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     HOOKER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 7th of March
, seven yards of ribbon, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Heslep
                  




               
            .


                  EDWARD 
                  BRAYBROOK
               



            . I am shopman to Robert Heslep. I saw the prisoner draw the ribbon through the bolt hole, he cut it off; I called out to the other shopman; he ran out, and caught him.
ROBERT BARFORD. I am shopman also to Mr. Heslep. From information of Braybrook, I ran out and caught the prisoner, and took the ribbon from him; there are seven yards, it is worth four shillings; it is Mr. Heslep's property.



               GUILTY
            , aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
