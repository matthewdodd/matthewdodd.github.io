






2467. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WILSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of October

, 1 handkerchief, value 6d., and 1 shilling
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     George William 
                     Barron
                  



               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  WILLIAM BARRON
               



            . I belong to the brig Eliza Ann
. On the 17th of October she was laying at Woolwich - I was at a lodging house that night, as I had come up with our ships paper's; the next morning, when I awoke, I missed my handkerchief and 1s. out of my pocket - the prisoner had slept there, but he was gone; I had no more money in my pocket, all I had was gone - my handkerchief was found on his neck.


                  ROBERT 
                  EDWARDS
               



             (Police-constable K 43). I took the prisoner about eleven o'clock that day; I charged him with robbing the sailor of the 1s., he told me I might go and find it - I found the handkerchief on his neck.
Prisoner. I put the handkerchief on in the dark; I am an entire stranger here.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
2468. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  WILSON
               



             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of October

, 1 pair of trousers, value 5s., and 1 sixpence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Richardson
                  



               
            .
RICHARD RICHARDSON. I am a sailor
. I slept in the same room and in the next bed to the prisoner; in the morning, after the prisoner was gone, I missed a pair of trousers and sixpence, which was in the pocket,


                  ROBERT 
                  EDWARDS
               



            . I took the prisoner with these trousers on; I believe he had thrown his own old ones away.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for 7 Years longer
            .
There were three other indictments against the prisoner.
