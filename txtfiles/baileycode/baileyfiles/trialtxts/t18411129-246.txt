



246. 




FREDERICK GOSLING
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
27th of November, 1 pair of boots, value 8s., the goods of 




Israel Foster; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






GEORGE HOMES
. I am in the service of Israel Foster, who keeps a shoe-shop in Lower-street, Islington. I was at home on the 27th of November—I missed a pair of boots—I had seen them safe a quarter of an hour before

—I ran out and saw the prisoner running away with them—I ran after him, and tried to secure him—the policeman came up—he threw the boots away—the policeman took them up, and took him.





THOMAS HOBBS KING
 (police-sergeant N 22.) I saw the prisoner throw the boots away—I took him.





HENRY BLYTH
 (police-constable K 302.) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 18.—


Transported for Seven Years.
