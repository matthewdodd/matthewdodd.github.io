



1645. 




JOSEPH MONTGOMERY BENNETT
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
29th of May, 9 Shirts value 2l.; 1 shift, value 4s.; 1 shawl, value 9s.; 3 table-cloths, value 7s.; 1 pair of stockings, value 1s.; 1 flannel shirt, value 2s.; 3 printed books, value 7s.; the goods of 



robert Robinson Alexander: and 2 shirts, value 5s.; and I waistcoat, value 3s.; the goods of 



Samuel Waller.






MARY ALEXANDER
. I am the wife of Robert Robinson Alexander; we keep the king or prussia public-house, in Wych-street. The prisoner lodged there some time—he left in May last—we then missed nine shirts, a shift, and other articles—these are the things.





WILLIAM WATSON
. I am in the employ of a pawnbroker in Green-street, Leicester-squa re. These things were all pawned by the prisoner between March and May last.




GUILTY
. Aged 27.—


Transported for Seven Years.
