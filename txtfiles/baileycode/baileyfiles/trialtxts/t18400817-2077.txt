



2077. 




MARY LYON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
3rd of August, 4 rings, value 1.; and 1 brooch, value 2l.; the goods of 



Thomas Genna, her master:—also, 2 handkerchiefs, value 5s. the goods of 



James Ludlam: and 3 handkerchiefs, value 1l.; and 24 yards of silk and cotton cloth, value 1l.; the goods of 



Jeffery Ludlam, her master; to both of which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 35.—


Transported for Seven Years.
