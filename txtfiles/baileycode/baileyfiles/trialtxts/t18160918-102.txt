





919. 
               
                  ELIZHBETH 
                  COLLIER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of August
, three table-cloths, value 1l. 7s. five handkerchiefs, value 1l. one pillowcase, value 2s. three pairs of cotton hose, value 7s. one napkin, value 2s. and one apron, value 2s.
             the property of 
               
                  Mary 
                  Cooper
               




            , widow
.


                  MARY 
                  COOPER
               



            . I lost these things at several different times, and in nearly three years. I know the prisoner.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BROCK
               



            . I have got three table-cloths, some handkerchiefs, and some stockings; I took them in pledge from the prisoner at the bar, at several different times.


                  JOSEPH 
                  GASS
               



            . I apprehended the prisoner, and found on her the duplicates relating to this property; I found four duplicates on her which led to th1s.



               GUILTY
            , aged 35.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
