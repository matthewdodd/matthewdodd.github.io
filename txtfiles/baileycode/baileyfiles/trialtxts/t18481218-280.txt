



280. 




ALFRED PERRY



, stealing 2 pairs of trowsers, value 3s.; the goods of 



Thomas Jefferys; having been before convicted.






ALFRED JEFFERYS
. I live with my father, Thomas Jefferys. On Tuesday, 5th Dec., his trowsers were safe in his bed-room at eight o'clock—I missed them about one—these are them.





GEORGE TAYLOR
. At twenty minutes before one o'clock, that day, I saw the prisoner go into Mr. Jefferys's, and come out with a bundle on his arm—I told the policeman.





THOMAS STILL
 (policeman, G 247). I went after the prisoner—I saw him drop these trousers—I found on him the handkerchief that had been about them.





JOHN SULLIVAN
 I produce a certificate of the prisoner's conviction—(read—Convieted June, 1847; confined six months)—he is the man.




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
