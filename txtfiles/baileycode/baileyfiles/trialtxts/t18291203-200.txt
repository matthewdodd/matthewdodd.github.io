





198. 
               
                  SAMUEL 
                  BRAIL
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of November
, 1 tub, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Rogers
               




            .
WILLIAM WHITE. On the 14th of November, I saw the prisoner with this tub; I asked him where he got it - he gave me no answer, and I took him into custody.
MARY ROGERS. I am the wife of 
               
                  George 
                  Rogers
               



            . This is his tub, and was in the drying ground, where we dry the clothes; I know nothing of the prisoner.
Prisoner. I did it through hunger.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 38. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
