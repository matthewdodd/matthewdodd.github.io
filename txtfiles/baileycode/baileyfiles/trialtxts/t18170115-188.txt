





336. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     HOLT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of December
, one pair of shoes, value 10s.
            , the property of 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Rivolta
               




            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  RIVOLTA
               



            . I am a shoe maker
, and live in Great Saffron-hill


. On the 30th of December, between five and six o'clock in the evening, I was cleaning my windows, and saw a hand snatch a pair of water-tight shoes from my door; I went out, and the prisoner was secured.


                  RICHARD 
                  PORRI
               



            . I live next door to the prosecutor. On the 30th of December, I was standing at my door, and saw the prisoner before the window; he had nothing in his hand, in a little time I saw him with the shoes; I took him, and he threw them down.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
HIGHAM BARNETT. I took the prisoner into custody.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 32.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
