






1460. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PRIGNELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 27th of June
, 1 saw, value 2s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Gale
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  MURPHY
               



            . I had the care of some carpenter's tools in Portman-market; Gale had left this saw there - I saw the prisoner come out with it, and I took him.
WILLIAM GALE. This is my saw.



               GUILTY
             - Aged 32. 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
