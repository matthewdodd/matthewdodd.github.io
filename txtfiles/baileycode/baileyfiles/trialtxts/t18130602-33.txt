





548. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     GAMES
                  



               
             was indicted for that he, on the 1st of May
, 


               feloniously had in his custody, two forged 1 l. bank-notes, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded.




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. Recorder.
