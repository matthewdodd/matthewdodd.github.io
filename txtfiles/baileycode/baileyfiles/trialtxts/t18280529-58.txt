





1134. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  JONES
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of May
, 1 piece of printed muslin, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Graham
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 38.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
