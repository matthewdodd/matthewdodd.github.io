



1640. 




JOHN HARRIS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
10th of June, 10 yards of wooolllen cloth, value 1l. 13s. the goods of 



Thomas Brown.






JAMES SUTTON
. I am shopman to Thomas Brown, of Leadenhall-street. On the 16th of June, about half-pst foru o'clock I was stitting behidn the shop—I saw the prisoner come to the door, and take ten yards of cloth whcih stood en a chair inisde the door and out it under it under him arm—I immedistely ran out, and brought him back with it is his possession—this is it—(looking at it)




GUILTY
.* Aged 18—


Transported for Seven Year.
