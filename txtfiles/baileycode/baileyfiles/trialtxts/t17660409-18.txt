                     Hannah 
                     Philips 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a gold ring, value 2 s. two cotton bed-curtains, a guinea and half a guinea
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Heath 
                  




                
            , widow
. ++


                  Elizabeth 
                  Heath 
               



            . I live in Orchard-street


; the prisoner was destitute of a lodging and I took her in; she had been with me five or six months; I missed the things mentioned in the indictment, I told her of it, she would not own to the taking them; I got a warrant and took her up, then she owned to the taking the bed-curtains and half a guinea; she said she had pawned the curtains to Mr. Gunston.


                  John
                   C 
               



             I live with Mr. Gunston in Germyn-street, a pawnbroker. On the 25th of January last the prisoner pledged these two bed-curtains with me in the name of 
               
                  Hannah 
                  West

Prisoner's defence.

               Guilty
