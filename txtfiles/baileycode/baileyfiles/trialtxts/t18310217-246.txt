





672. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of February
, 1 coat, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Corder
                  




               
            .


                  ROBERT 
                  RUST
               



            . On the 17th of February, between two and three o'clock in the day, I was in Seymour-street, Somers'-town


, and saw the prisoner take this coat off the box of a hackney-coach - the driver was in a house; he put it under his arm, and walked down Southampton-street - I followed, and took him; he dropped it, and I told a boy to take it up.
THOMAS CORDER. I am a hackneyman
. This is my coat - I had left it on the box.
Prisoner's Defence. I picked it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
