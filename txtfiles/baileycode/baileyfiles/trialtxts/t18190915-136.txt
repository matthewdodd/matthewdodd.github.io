





1218. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JOHNSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of July
, three coats, value 9 l.; three pair of trowsers, value 4 l., and four waistcoats, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Jenkins
                  




               
            .




               The prisoner pleaded
            

GUILTY. Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
