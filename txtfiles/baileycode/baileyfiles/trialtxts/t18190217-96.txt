





409. 
               
                  
                     MARTHA 
                     HUDSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of February
, one tub, value 3 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Tomlinson
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  TOMLINSON
               



            . I am a cooper
, and live in Charles-street, Hatton-garden


. On the 1st of February, between twelve and one o'clock, my tub stood under the window; about one o'clock I saw the prisoner take it up, and go towards Leather-lane - I followed and secured her.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I never touched it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 44.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
