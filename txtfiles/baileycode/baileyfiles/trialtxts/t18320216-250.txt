





758. 
               
                  
                     MICHAEL 
                     HOPKINS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of January
, 1 cap, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John William 
                     Willis
                  




               
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  CHIPPERFIELD
               



            . I live with Mr. 
               
                  John 
                  William Willis
               



            , a hatter
. I was in his glass-case cleaning his window on the 10th of January - I saw the prisoner step in, take a cap, and run off; I overtook him in Bowlyard, St. Giles' - he had parted with the cap in Charles-street - it has not been found.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
