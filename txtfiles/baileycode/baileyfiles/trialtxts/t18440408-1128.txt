



1128. 




SARAH SMITH
 was again indicted

 for stealing 1 pair of boots, value 8s., the goods of 



Thomas Wolstenholme and another.






JOHN KELLY
. On the 8th of March I missed a pair of boots from the same place where the shirt hung, the duplicate of them was found on the prisoner—I went to Mr. Brown's, a pawnbroker, in Fetter-lane, and there J found the boots—they are my master's, Thomas Wolstenholme and another.





CHRISTOPHER BROWN
. These boots were pawned by the prisoner in her own name.





MARY ANN REDMAN
. I searched the prisoner, and found on her two duplicates—one was for these boots pawned on the 8th of March.

Prisoner's Defence. A strange woman came and asked me to pawn them—I gave her the money, and she asked me to keep the tickets.




GUILTY
. Aged 44.—


Transported for Seven Years
