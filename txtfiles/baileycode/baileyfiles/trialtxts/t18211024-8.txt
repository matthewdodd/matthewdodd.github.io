






1280. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WALDRON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of October
, one handkerchief, value 3 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Glandfield
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Life
            .
Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
