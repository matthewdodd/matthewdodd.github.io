               stealing a Cheese, Value 3 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William

, (about 8 at Night) the Prisoner reach'd over the Shop Window, and took the Cheese off a Shelf. This was observ'd by a passenger who stop'd him.
