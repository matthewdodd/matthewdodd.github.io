







                     Mary 
                     Malshaw 
                  



               
            , of Edmonton


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing divers Goods, in the Dwelling-House of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Riggot 
                        




, the 30th of December
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner was his Servant, and getting up betimes in the Morning, went away with divers wearing Apparel , that he being inform'd by his Boy that the Maid was gone away, arose, and taking his Horse, rode towards Shoreditch and in the Road overtook her, and carrying her into a House, she delivered to him all the Things she had stolen; the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 39 s
            .
