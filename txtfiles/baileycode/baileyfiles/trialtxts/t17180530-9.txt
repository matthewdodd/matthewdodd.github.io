







                     John 
                     Ellis 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Pair of Shoes, value 4 s. in the Shop
             of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Shipley 
                  




                
            , the 8th of May
 last. It appeared by the Evidence, that the Prisoner was seen to reach his Hand over the Prosecutor's Window and take the Shoes off from a Shelf and go away with them, and being pursued was taken with the Shoes in his Bosom. Notwithstanding he denied the Fact, or that he had the Shoes at all, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation for 7 Years
            .
