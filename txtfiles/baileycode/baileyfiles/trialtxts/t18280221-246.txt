                  NATHANIEL 
                  DAVIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of January
, 1 watch, value 2l.; 1 watch-chain, value 10s., and 1 seal, value 6s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Ward
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 31.




               Transported for Seven Years
