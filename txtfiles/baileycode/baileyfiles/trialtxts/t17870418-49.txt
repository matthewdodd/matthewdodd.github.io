





378. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HEARNON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of April
, one silk handkerchief, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Lumley
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor saw the prisoner pick his pocket on Fish-street Hill


, and took him in three or four minutes; and saw him drop the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   HOTHAM
               



            .
