





Fourth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1925. 
               
                  MATTHEW 
                  DUNN
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of August
, 4 half-crowns, 15 shillings, and 10 sixpences, the monies of 
                     
                        Morris 
                        Fitzgerald
                     




                  , from his person
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
