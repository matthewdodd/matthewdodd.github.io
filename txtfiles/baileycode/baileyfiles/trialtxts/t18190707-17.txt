





922. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of May
, 37 lbs. of veal, value 6 s. 3 d.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Cobner
                  




               
            .
SARAH COBNER. My husband is a butchers' carrier
, and lives at St. Clement's. The meat was in a cart, which stood at the corner of Fetter-lane


.


                  THOMAS 
                  NIBLET
               



            . On the 29th of May I saw the prisoner jump into the prosecutor's cart, take out a hindquarter of veal, and run off with it. I afterwards heard it was stolen, and gave information.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
