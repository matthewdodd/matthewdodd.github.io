






460. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     BURN
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     HOLMES
                  




               
            , were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of February
, 11lbs of beef, value 2s. 6d.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Christopher
                  




               
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  CHRISTOPHER
               



            . I am a butcher
, and live in Cross-street, Hoxton


. On the 1st of February, I missed the beef, and in about ten minutes after, the prisoners, and beef, were brought to my house by the patrol.


                  ROBERT 
                  LOCKE
               



            . I am a patrol. On the 1st of February, I saw the two prisoners and another man, coming along with the beef; I asked them what they had got; they said it was beef, which they had bought at Hoxton. The prisoner, Holmes, had it in his hand - He ran away when I took hold of him.
(Property Produced and sworn to.)
BURN - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.
HOLMES - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.





               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
