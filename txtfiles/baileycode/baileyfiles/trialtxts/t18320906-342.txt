





2072. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     RADFORD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Richard Griffin 
                           Edwards
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  CHILDS
               



            . On the 31st of August, about noon, I was near St. Giles' church; I saw the prisoner and another lad - I cannot tell which of them took the handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket, but I saw the prisoner putting it into his apron; he was near enough to have taken it, and was in company with the other lad - I gave an alarm; the prisoner was taken, and the handkerchief found on him.
RICHARD GRIFFIN EDWARDS. This is my handkerchief - I lost it from my pocket in Broad-street, St. Giles'


, on the 31st of August.


                  GEORGE 
                  PALMER
               



             (Police-constable E 5). I took the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence. I only picked it up from the ground.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
