




839. 




OWEN SUFFOLK
 was indicted

for feloniously forging and uttering an undertaking for the payment of 19l. 1s. 6d., with intent to defraud 



Robert Wilson; 


also for unlawfully obtaining 2 sovereigns, 1 half-sovereign, 1 crown, 1 half-crown, 2 shillings, and 1 sixpence, and other monies, of 



Robert Wilson, by false pretences; to which he pleaded




GUILTY
.— Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
