




1185. 




THOMAS RICHARDS
 was indicted

for feloniously forging a request for the delivery of 4 yards of calico and 4 yards of flannel, with intent to defraud 



John Danford, against the Statute, &c.—2nd COUNT, for uttering the same with like intent.—

And also for stealing, on the 
21st of March, 1 sovereign, the money of 



Abraham Toulmin, his master;to both of which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.
