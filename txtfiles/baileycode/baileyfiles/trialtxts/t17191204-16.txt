







                     Joseph 
                     Williamson 
                  



               
             of St. Giles in the Fields


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Hafted Knife and Fork, value 5 s. out of the House of 
                     
                        
                           Cornelius a 
                           Tilburgh 
                        




, on the 13th of November
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner was in the Prosecutor's Kitchen, and took the Knife and Fork off the Dresser from among others. The Prisoner had nothing to say in his defence, but that a Man persuaded him to take them, and would not let him bring them again. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
