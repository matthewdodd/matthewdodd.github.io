






1696. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DENNY
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     DENNIS 
                     DENNIS
                  




               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        John Lee 
                        Dale
                     




                  , from his person
            .
JOHN LEE DALE. I am a horse-dealer
, and was in Sweeting's-alley


, about nine o'clock on the 31st of August - I felt something at my pocket, I turned, and saw Girton holding Denny, who had my handkerchief - I saw him drop it.


                  JOHN 
                  GIRTON
               



            . I am an officer. I saw these two lads following the prosecutor; Dennis took the handkerchief from his pocket, and gave it to Denny, who put it towards his breast - I took of it; he dropped it, and I picked it up - Dennis ran up the alley; I gave an alarm, and he was secured.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Denny received a good character.
DENNY - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Confined Three Months
            .
DENNIS - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
