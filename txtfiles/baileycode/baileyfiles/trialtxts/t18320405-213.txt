





1016. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PURFLEET
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of September
, 1 tea-kettle, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Capps
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  CAPPS
               



            . I am a pawnbroker
, and live in Old-street-road


. On the 24th of September I lost a tea-kettle, which the officer produced the same evening; I cannot say at what the officer produced the same evening; I cannot say at what time it was taken.


                  GEORGE 
                  KEMP
               



             (Police-constable, N 82.) On the 24th of September, about ten o'clock at night, I was in Boot-street, and saw the prisoner in company with severalnotorious thieves, with something under his jacket - he gave it to another boy, and made off; I stopped the boy, and took it from him - it was this kettle; I took the prisoner afterwards; they were about five minutes' walk from the prosecutor's.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
