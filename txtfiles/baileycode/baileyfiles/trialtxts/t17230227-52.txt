








                     Sarah 
                     Swan 
                  



                
            , and 
               
                  
                     Jane 
                     Taff 
                  



                
            , were indicted, the Former for 


               feloniously stealing a Wig, value 3 l. and 7 flaxen Sheets, value 3 l. in the Dwelling House of Gregory Swinney
            , the 28th of November

 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That Sarah Swan was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and stole the Goods; and, that Jane Taff 


               sold them
            ; but it did not appear, that Taff knew they had been stolen, wherefore the Jury 

               acquitted
             Jane Taff, but found Sarah Swan 


               guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
