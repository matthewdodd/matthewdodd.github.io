








                     William 
                     Davis 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Velvet Cap, value 15 s. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Charles
                            Henry Beauly
                        



, the 23d of October

 last.
He was likewise indicted a second time for 


               stealing a Hat, value 5 s.
             from 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Holden 
                  



                
             , the 23d of October

 last.
Mr. Beauly depos'd, That the Cap was stolen out of the Shop-Window.
Other Evidences depos'd, That seeing him steal some other Things in Cornhill, they apprehended him, and found the Cap and Hat, and other Goods upon him. The Facts being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d. upon each Indictment
             .
