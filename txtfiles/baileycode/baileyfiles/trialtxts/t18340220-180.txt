





495. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     EDMONDS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of February
, 1 brass fender, value 1l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John James 
                     Rippon
                  




               
            .


                  FRANCIS 
                  WILLIAM STEBBINGS
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. John James Rippon, of Castle-street, Oxford-street


 - on the 17th of February I was at home in the evening - I received information, went out, and saw the prisoner walking away with the fender on his shoulder - this is it; it is my employer's - I had seen it safe ten minutes before in the shop- I took him with it.
Prisoner. I was standing talking to a young man; and when I left him a man asked me to carry the fender - he took it from the door and gave it to me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
