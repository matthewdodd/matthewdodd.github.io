





66. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Matcham 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one cloth coat, value 3 s. and one cloth waistcoat, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Martin 
               




             , Dec. 5
. ++


                  Thomas 
                  Martin 
               



            . The prisoner lay with me at my lodgings in Witch-street


: two men of the prisoner's acquaintance called on me last Monday, to know if I had not lost something; I looked in my box, and I missed a coat and waistcoat: I took up the prisoner; he acknowledged the fact, and begged mercy; and found my cloaths in the house of Sarah Maundry.


                  Sarah 
                  Maundry 
               



            . The prisoner brought a handkerchief with the things mentioned in the indictment, to leave at my house.
M. Preston. I heard the prisoner, before the Justice, confess he took the things, and begged for mercy.
Prisoner's Defence.
I was in liquor, and took the things, believing them to be my own.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
