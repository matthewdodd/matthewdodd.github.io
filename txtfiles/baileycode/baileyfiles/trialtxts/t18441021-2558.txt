




2558. 




MARIA SMITH
 was indicted

 for stealing 3 handkerchiefs, value 9s.; the goods of 



James Baker, her master.—2nd COUNT, stating them to be the goods of 



Joseph Fenn.—

Also, 8 napkins, 7s. 6d.; 3 shirts, 2s.; 2 bed-gowns, 4s.; and 2 blankets, 6s.; the goods of 



John Benjamin Burling; and that she had been before convicted of felony; to which she pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 46.—


Transported for Seven Years.
