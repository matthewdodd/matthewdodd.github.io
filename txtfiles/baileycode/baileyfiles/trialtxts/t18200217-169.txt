





517. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WRIGHT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of February
, one pair of pantaloons, value 20 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Eddles
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  EDDLES
               



            . I am nephew to 
               
                  James 
                  Eddles
               



            , who keeps a clothes shop
 in Coventry-street


. On the 18th of February I saw the prisoner at the door, watched him, and saw him snatch the trowsers from inside the shop, and run away. I followed, secured him, and picked the trowsers up.


                  WILLIAM 
                  JONES
               



            . I saw the prisoner throw the trowsers down.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 23.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
