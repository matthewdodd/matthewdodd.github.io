





775. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     PETTIT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of February
, 44 yards of carpet, value 35s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Cooper
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Phillips
                  



               
            .
JAMES PHILLIPS. I am in partnership with Richard Cooper - we are linen and woollen-draper

s, at Charing-cross


. On the 26th of February, about twelve o'clock, I saw the prisoner go out at the door, with a carpet on his shoulder - I followed, and brought him back with it; he said distress drove him to it - this is it; there are forty-four yards of it.


                  BENJAMIN 
                  BATES
               



            . I was standing near the door, and saw the prisoner taken.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
