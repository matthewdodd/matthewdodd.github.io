



1203. 




MARY WILSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
19th of April, at Lambeth, 2 purses, value 3s.; 1 ring, 10s.; 1 brooch, 1s.; 2 card counters, 10s.; 2 Bank tokens, 10s.; 2 guineas; 46 sovereigns; 1 half-sovereign; 5 sixpences; 5 pence; 7 halfpence; and 1 £5 Bank-note, the goods and monies of 



Sarah Caroline Edmonds, her mistress, in her dwelling-house; to which she pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 14.—


Transported for Ten Years.
