





Before Mr. Baron Vaughan.
911. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of March
, 1 coat, value 7s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Cole
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  COLE
               



            . I live at Deptford
. On the 15th of March, about eleven o'clock at night, I left my coat safe on the box of my fly, at Covent-garden theatre; I missed it soon after.


                  JOSEPH 
                  NORTON
               



            . I am a Policeman. I stopped the prisoner in Tavistock-street, about a quarter-past eleven o'clock that night, with the great coat, and asked if it was his; he said it was - I said I thought it belonged to somebody about the theatre, and detained him. I found Cole.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner. I was intoxicated.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 23. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
