



2090. 




THOMAS LAKE WILLS
 was indicted

, for that he, being employed in the Post-office, did, on the 
17th of July, steal a certain post letter, containing 1 10l. promissory-note, 1 half-sovereign, and 3s.; the property of her Majesty's Postmaster-general.—5 other COUNTS, varying the manner of stating the charge; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Fifteen Years.
(There was another indictment against the prisoner.)
