





1955. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of August
, 28 yards of printed cotton, value 1ls.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Smith
                  




               
            .


                  EDWARD 
                  ABEL BARNES
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. 
               
                  William 
                  Smith
               



            , a linen-draper
, of Titchfield-street


. This print was just at the entrance of the door, with others, on the 10th of August - it was not beyond the house; it laid on a piece of flannel; the prisoner was brought in with it.


                  WILLIAM 
                  NASH
               



            . On the 10th of August, in the afternoon, I was coming along Titchfield-street - I saw the prisoner put a piece of print into his apron, close to the prosecutor's door - he ran off; I pursued, and the officer took him.


                  WILLIAM 
                  PLUME
               



             (Police-constable E 41.) I took the prisoner in Riding-house-lane - I saw him running.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
