







                     Robert 
                     Rhodes 
                  



               
             of St. Clements Danes


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing an Optick Glass, an ivory Box, 2 Silver Tea-Spoons and 3 pair of Silver Buckles
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Caton 
                  




                
             on the 27th of this Instant May
. The Prisoner was found hid under the Bed with the Goods in his Pocket; who in excuse said that he was in Liquor. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
