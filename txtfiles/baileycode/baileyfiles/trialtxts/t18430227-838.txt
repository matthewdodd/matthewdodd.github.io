



838. 




JAMES JOHNSON
 was indicted

 for embezzling and stealing, on the 
16th of Jan., 71l. 7s., which he had received on account of 



James Ivyleafe, his master.—2nd COUNT, for stealing an order for the payment of 71l. 7s. to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 33.—


Transported for Seven Years.
