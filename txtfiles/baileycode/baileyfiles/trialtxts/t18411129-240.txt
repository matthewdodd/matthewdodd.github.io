



240. 




JAMES OGLEBY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
2nd of December, 1 trowel, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 




James Lucas.






THOMAS LUCAS
. I am assistant to my father, James Lucas, a builder, in Cromer-street. About twelve o'clock, on the 2nd of December, I left my trowel inside the second-floor window of a house in Monmouth-street—I missed it—this now produced is it.





JOHN O'BRIEN
. I am a broker, and live in Monmouth-court. Between two and three o'clock, on the 2nd of December, the prisoner came to me with this trowel, and asked me to buy it—I asked where he got it—he said he had picked it up in the street—he was taken the next day—I went to the station, and took the trowel.





WILLIAM MASON
 (police-constable G 127.) I took the prisoner into custody from Mr. Lucas's description.




GUILTY
.* Aged 13.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Convict Ship.
