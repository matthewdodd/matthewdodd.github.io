



1971. 



HUGH HENRIGHT
 was indicted

 for feloniously breaking and entering the dwelling-house of 



John Jupp, on the 
21st of August, at 
St. Giles'-in—the-Fields, and stealing therein, 1 watch, value 13l. 10s.; and 1 watch-chain, value 1l. 7s.; the goods of the said 



John Jupp; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged.—


Transported for Ten Years.
