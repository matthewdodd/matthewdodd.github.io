






1880. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  ROGERS
               




             and 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  PITT
               




             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of September
, 12 chisels, value 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        Frederick 
                        Hughes
                     



                  ; 15 chisels, value 8s., the goods of 
                     
                        George 
                        Forbes
                     



                  ; 1l chisels, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        Richard 
                        Flatt
                     



                  ; and 7 chisels, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Blackwell
                     



.
FREDERICK HUGHES. I am a stone-mason
. On the 13th of September I was working in Five Fields, Chelsea


. I left my tools, on Wednesday night, in a work-shop in the fields; they were gone on Thursday morning - I lost twelve chisels.
GEORGE FORBES. I was at work at the same place, and lost eleven chisels from the mason's shop.
RICHARD FLATT, I lost my chisels from this workshop.
WILLIAM BLACKWELL. I lost my chisels from the same shop - the prisoners are strangers.


                  JOHN 
                  BOSTON
               



            . I am an officer. On Thursday morning, at half-past nine o'clock, I stopped the prisoners, and found a quantity of chisels on each of them - they were taken to Guildhall; and there, they said, they got them from Five Fields, Chelsea.
ROGERS - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.
PITT - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 23.





               Transported for Seven Years
            .
