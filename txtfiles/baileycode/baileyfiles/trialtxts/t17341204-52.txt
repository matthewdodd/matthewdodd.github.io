               stealing a Silver Spoon, value 8 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Joyce 
                     Harrison 
                  




                
            , Octob. 16
.


                  Magdalen 
                  Overy 
               



            . I put the Spoon in a Saucepan upon the Shelf in the Kitchen. The Prisoner is a Dust-man
, and came down for the Dust. I went up to my Mistress. The Spoon was afterwards mist; the Prisoner was examined; he denied it at first, but upon being charged with a Constable, he confest that he had taken it, and had hid it in a Hole in an empty old House in May-Fair, and there it was found.
Prisoner. Well, if it be so, I hope you'l give me Corporation Punishment.



               Guilty
            .
