                        Samuel 
                        Dawson 
                     



                   ; and a Petticoat, value 4 s. 3 Shirts, 6 s. and other Things

                     John 
                     Bishop 
                  



                
            , on the 16th of April
 last. It appear'd that the prisoner was a Lodger; some of the Goods were found upon her, and the rest at the Pawnbrokers. Her Confession was read; she said nothing in her Defence, and the Jury found her
