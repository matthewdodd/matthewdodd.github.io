





624. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SIMMONS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 26th of May
, a plane, value 2 s. and a handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  John 
                  Franklin
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  FRANKLIN
               



            . I am a carpenter
; I was at work at a building in Edgeware-place


. On the 26th of May, my partner took the prisoner with the planes under his arm.


                  THOMAS 
                  JONES
               



            . I met the prisoner between eight and nine o'clock in the evening; I saw the planes under his arm. These are the planes; one is mine, the other is Franklin's.
Prosecutor. That is my plane.
Prisoner's Defence. I was coming across the fields, I found the planes on the grass.



               GUILTY
            , aged 38.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
