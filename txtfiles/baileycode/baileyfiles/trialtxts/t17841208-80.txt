





87. 
               
                  
                     MICHAEL 
                     SULLIVAN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 25th of October
 last, one white cambrick handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Dilks
                  




               
            .
William Hall saw the prisoner pick the prosecutor's pocket, and took him directly.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for fourteen years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
