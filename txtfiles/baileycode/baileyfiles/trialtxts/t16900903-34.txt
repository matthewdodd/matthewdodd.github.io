







                     Thomas 
                     Allen
                  



               
             was tryed for 


               stealing a Silver Tankard, value 5 l. and a Taster 4 s.
             the 2d of this instant September
, from 
               
                  
                     Bartholomew 
                     Hunt
                  




               
            . The Prisoner and another came to drink, and took their time to defraud the People of the Goods, by running away with them: And the Evidence swore, That the Prisoner was one of them. The Prisoner said, That he was at his Father's House when the thing was done, but could not prove it, so he was found 

               Guilty
             of Felony.
