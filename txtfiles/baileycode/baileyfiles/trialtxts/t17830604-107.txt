





459. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     WILLIAMS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 12th day of May
 last, one carpet, called a Wilton carpet, value 6 l.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Fitzgerald
                  




               
            .



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
