





663. 
               
                  
                     PETER 
                     WARD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of May
 last, six silver tea spoons, value 12 s. two silver table spoons, value 20 s. one silver skewer, value 10 s. one silver marrow spoon, value 8 s. the property of 
                     
                        John 
                        Stead
                     




                  , in his dwelling house
            .
The prisoner was employed as a clerk
 to the prosecutor, and he broke the locks, and took the property out of the room, in which he wrote; he was stopped by a pawnbroker, with some silver.
Prisoner. I confessed; and the gentleman promised to forgive me.




               GUILTY, 39 s.
            





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
