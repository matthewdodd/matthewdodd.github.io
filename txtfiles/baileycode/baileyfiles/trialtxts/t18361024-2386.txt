



2386. 




JAMES KELLY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
20th of October, 1 hammer, value 1s.; and 1 trowel, value 1s.; the goods of 



John Lewis.






JOHN LEWIS
. I am a plasterer. On the 20th of October I was working at No. 20, Manchester-square—the family was out of town, and the house was under repair—I left my tools there while I went to breakfast—I was absent from eight till twenty till twenty minutes before nine—the house was shut up—I missed this hammer and trowel—I do not know the prisoner—I cannot tell how the person got in.





GEORGE SPELLER
. I am a pawnbroker. The prisoner pawned these with me on the 20th of October.

Prisoner. I was standing in Paradise-street—the officer came and took me.




GUILTY
. Aged 20—


Transported for Seven Years.
