






213. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CHRISTIAN
                  



               
             and 
               
                  CHARLES 
                  O'HARA
               



             were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 24th of December
 last, nine quarts of whale oil, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  William 
                  Cowley
               




            .
The prisoner Christian was seen taking the oil in a tin bottle, in a bag. The oil deposed to, being remarkably rank.


                  JOHN
                   CHRISTIAN
               



            , 

               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .


                  CHARLES
                   O'HARA
               



            , 

               NOT GUILTY
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
