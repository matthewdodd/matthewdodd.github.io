







                     Thomas

               stealing one pound of Thread, value 3 s. and 12 Yards of Gartering, value 6 d.
             on the 28th of this Instant May
, the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Amos 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Hickerton 
                  



                
            .


                  William 
                  Wats 
               



             depos'd, That the Prisoner was at his Master's Shop on the Day aforesaid about Business, and that after he was gone the Goods was missing; upon which he pursued and took him, and upon Search found the Gartering in his Right Coat Pocket.


                  Thomas 
                  Spicer 
               



             depos'd, He was likewise at the taking of the Prisoner, and found the Thread in his Left Coat Pocket, which he confess'd to be the Goods of the Prosecutor, and was thereupon found
