                           Philpot 
                        



                      
                  , and

                           Lee

.


                  Richard 
                  Wolley 
               



             and 
               
                  William 
                  Dunkin 
               



             proved, that the Tobacco was found upon him, some between his Shirt and his Skin, and three or four Hands were hanging out of the Cod-piece of his Breeches; he was asked his Name, and he said, he had forgot it; he is one of the Lumpers; he said, he had no Family; but it appeared afterwards, that he had a Wife and two Children.
