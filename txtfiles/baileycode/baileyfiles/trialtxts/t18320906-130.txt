





1855. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of September
, 3 pewter pots, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Lambert
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  LAMBERT
               



            . I keep the Grapes, in Little St. Martin's-lane




. The prisoner came to scour the pots on the 3rd of September, in the morning; I said there were not many to do - he staid about a quarter of an hour; I asked if he was done - he said Yes, and came up with his hat and coat in his hand; he put his coat on, and put his hat down - I took up his hat, and found a quart pot in it - I sent for an officer, who found three pint pots on his person; two of the pints, and the quart pot are mine, and the other is a neighbour's; they are all hent.
Prisoner. I have a very heavy family, and was in distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 35. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
