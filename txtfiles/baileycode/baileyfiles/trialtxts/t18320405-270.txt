





1073. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SULLIVAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of February
, 5 lbs. of beef, value 2s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Procter
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  HERCOCK
               



            . I am servant to Richard Procter, a butcher
. Between four and five o'clock in the afternoon I saw the prisoner take this beef from the board in front of the shop; I followed and stopped him, without losing sight of him - he was searched and no money found on him.


                  THOMAS 
                  RAYLE
               



            . I am a Policeman. I saw the prisoner going from the shop, and received him in charge - he had also got a loin of pork, and part of a leg of mutton; and two pieces of beef in his hat, and another in his pocket, a dried haddock and a newspaper of the day - he did not say where he got them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
