








                     Elizabeth 
                     Bromfield 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Williams 
                  



                
            , of Enfield



, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Table Cloth, and other Linnen
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Bourn 
                  




                
            , the 27th of May
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Linnen was hang'd out to dry, and was taken by both the Prisoners, they were seen committing the Fact, and were apprehended soon after. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found them 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
             each.
