







                     Deborah 
                     Ayres 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 9 s. 6 d. in Money
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Bourne 
                  




                
            , the 23d of September
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Money was taken out of his Box, in his House, and that the Prisoner, who was a Chair-Woman
, confess'd that she did open the Box, and take out the Money; the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
