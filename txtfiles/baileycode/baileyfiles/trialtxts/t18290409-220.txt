





899. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     BEDDOES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of March
, 4 caps, value 5s.; 3 petticoats, value 7s.: 1 pair of stays, value 3s.: 1 collar, value 1s., and 1 pocket, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Rogers
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZABETH 
                  ROGERS
               



            . I am the wife of John Rogers. On the 23d of March I had packed up the articles stated in a band-box, to take to the owner; I was driving them in a cart, about a quarter to nine o'clock - I have seen the articles since; these are them.


                  CHRISTIAN 
                  PODZUS
               



            . The watchman brought this property and the prisoner to the watch-house; I asked him what he had to say; he said Nothing.


                  MICHAEL 
                  LYON
               



            . I am a watchman. A gentleman brought the prisoner into Hand-court, and gave him to me, with this property; the owner was not there - I took him to the watch-house; I saw the cart, but did not see him take any thing; but that gentleman did.
Prisoner's Defence. I was crossing up Red Lion-street, and two lads were fighting in the road; one said,"I shan't carry this;" and another said, "I shan't;" I said, "What is in it;" and they said, "Will you have it;" I took it, and this gentleman took me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
