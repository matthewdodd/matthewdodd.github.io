







                     Robert 
                     Lockey 
                  



               
            , of St. Brides


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 3 dozen of Sword Blades, value 50 s. in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Baily 
                        




, on the 1st of August
 last. It appear'd that the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's Journeyman
, and took the Goods, and gave them to one Vaughan (another of his Journeymen) to sell, who confest the Matter. That when before the Justice he said that Vaughan enticed him to do it, and Vaughan said that the Prisoner enticed him. His Confession was read in Court, and he also owned it on his Tryal. The Jury considering the matter, found him 


               Guilty to the value of 4 s. 10 d
            .
