





Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1422. 
               
                  HENRY 
                  STONE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of August
, 1 truck, value 5l.; and 1 dog harness, value 1s. 6d.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Robins, the elder




            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  ROBINS
               



            , Jun. I am the son of Joseph Robins, of Green-street, Stepney


, a carpenter
. On the 7th of August, we had a truck at our shop, and the prisoner came to me, and said, "Joseph, I want your truck, if you can lend it me" - I said he could not have it for less than 3d. an hour - he wanted it for 2d. - I fetched it him - he said he should not want it more than two or three hours - he was to have brought it back, but did not - it has not been found - it was worth 5l. - I did not see him again till he was in custody - the harness was with it.
Prisoner. Q. What time was it in the morning? A. You came about half-past seven o'clock, but half an hour was allowed for the time I was talking with you, I know you.



               GUILTY
            .* Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
