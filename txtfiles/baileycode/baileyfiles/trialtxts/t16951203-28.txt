








                  Jane 
                  Jenkins alias 
Browne




            , and 
               
                  Anne 
                  Jones alias 
Povey




            , both of the Parish of St. Martins Ludgate



, were indicted for 


               Stealing 30 Yards of Shagreen Silk from 
                     
                        Henry 
                        Rapier
                     



                   and 
                     
                        William 
                        Vere
                     



                  , on the 5th of December
 Instant. It appeared that they came into the Shop and cheapned some Silk, and whilst the man's Head was turned, they had conveyed away the Silk
            : Then they went their ways, and went down Black-Fryers, and being suspected by a man to be Shoplifters he search'd them, and found the Silk between Povey's Legs. Browne declared that she knew nothing of it. And they both being known to be Persons that followed that way of Trade, the Jury found them both 

               guilty
            .
