





147. 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL 
                     BAKER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 17th day of December
 last, one iron grate, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Smith
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the grate upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice HEATH.
