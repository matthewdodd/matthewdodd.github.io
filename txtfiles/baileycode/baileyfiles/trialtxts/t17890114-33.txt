







130. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     FARRELL
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     WILLIAMS
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of December
 last, one cotton counterpane, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Allen
                  




               
            , widow
.


                  Robert
                   Hale
               



             saw the prisoner, Williams, hand the counterpane off the line to Farrel, who put it under his jacket; he pursued him, and took him with the property upon him.
(Produced, and deposed to.)
BOTH 

               GUILTY
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice BULLER.
