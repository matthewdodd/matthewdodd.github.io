





SEVENTH DAY. WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 13.
96. 
               
                  
                     EBENEZER 
                     JOHNSON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of March
, one watch, value 3 l., and one seal, value 7 s.
            , the goods of 
               

                        Peregrine 
                        Courtney
                     



                  , the elder




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
