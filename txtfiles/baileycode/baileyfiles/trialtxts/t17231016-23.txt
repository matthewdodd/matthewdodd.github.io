







                     Elizabeth 
                     Upton 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing wearing Apparel, value 10 s. in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        
                           Grace 
                           Fitzgerald 
                        




, the 2d of October
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner having lodg'd one Night in the Prosecutor's House, went away the next Morning, and carrying away the Goods, they were found upon her when taken. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
