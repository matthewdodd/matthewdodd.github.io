





Fifth Middlesex Jury - Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
78. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  FORD
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of November
, 12 pairs of stockings, value 15s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Sarah 
                  Solomon
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 29.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
