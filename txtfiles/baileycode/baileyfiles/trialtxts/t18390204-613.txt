



613. 




MARY BRIANT
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
9th of January, 3 earthenware pans, value 2s. 6d. the goods of 



Richard Huxley.






AUSTEN DAY
. I live with Richard Huxley who keeps a china and glass-shop in Tottenham-court-road. On the 9th of January I saw the prisoner take some pans from the door, and walk away with them—I followed her, and gave her in charge with the property on her.





THOMAS CROSSMAN
. I am a policeman. I met the prisoner in London-street, with the three pans in her possession.




GUILTY
. Aged 31.*—


Transported for Seven Years.
