



190. 




JAMES OSCAR ALEXANDER CAMERON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
23rd of July, 4 pairs of trowsers, value 2l.; 3 printed books, value 6s.; 3 waistcoats, value 15s.; and 1 comforter, value 1s.; the goods of 




Robert Russell.







ROBERT RUSSELL
. I am a law student, and live in London-street. I lodged at the prisoner's mother's up to the 13th of July, when I went to France—I left these boots and other things with the prisoner's mother, and some clothes were sent home—after my return from France I missed these things—these are mine—(examining them)—I received the duplicates from his mother—she asked me before giving them to me to lend her 30s. to redeem the things, and I said I would no longer pardon him, as I had done once before, but I would prosecute him.





ROBERT NOBLE
. On the 15th of July I delivered these trowsers at the prosecutor's lodgings.





THOMAS HUGHES
. I am a pawnbroker. I produce four pairs of trowten, one waistcoat, and three books, which I took in pledge of the prisoner.




GUILTY
.—Aged 24. 


Transported for Seven Years.
(There was another indictment against the prisoner.)
