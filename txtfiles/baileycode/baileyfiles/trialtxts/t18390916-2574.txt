



2574. 




FRANCIS HOPKINS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
9th of September, 1 frock, value 2s. 9d., the goods of 



Mary Ann Stainsley.






MARY ANN STAINSLEY
. I am a milliner, and live at the corner of Brewer-street. On the 9th of September I missed a frock—I saw the policeman with the prisoner, and this frock, which is mine.





GEORGE REESON
. I am a policeman. I stopped the prisoner, from information from a man—I unbuttoned his jacket, and took this frock out—he said he had found it—I took him back to the shop, and the prosecutor claimed it.




GUILTY
. * Aged 11.—


Transported for Seven Years-Convict ship.
