







                     Mary 
                     Collier 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Brides


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 s. 6 d. in Money, and a Diamond Ring, value 36 s. the 17th of July
 last, the Property of 
                     
                        
                           Roger 
                           Getting 
                        




                      
                  , in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        Mary 
                        Getting 
                     



. It appear'd, That the Prisoner was employ'd as a Chair-Woman
 in the Prosecutor's Mother's House, and the Money and Ring were taken out of the Prosecutor's Breeches, which hung up in his Chamber. The Prisoner upon her being apprehended own'd the taking of the Money and Ring, but deny'd it on her Trial. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
