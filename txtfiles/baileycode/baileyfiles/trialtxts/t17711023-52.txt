





740. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Luke
                      Anderson
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a silk handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William
                      Ellis
                  




               
            , Oct. 1st
. ++


                  William
                   Ellis
               



            . I was coming by Holborn bridge


; Mr. Gill informed me that the prisoner, who was then a little before us, had stole my handkerchief; Mr. Gill seized him; then I saw the prisoner throw my handkerchief down upon the ground; I picked it up.


                  Thomas
                   Gill
               



            . I saw the prisoner take Mr. Ellis's handkerchief out of his pocket; I laid hold of him, and then he threw the handkerchief from him, and Mr. Ellis took it up.
(The handkerchief produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)
Prisoner's Defence.
I did not take the handkerchief; I sell things about the streets.




               Guilty 10 d
            . 


               T
            .
