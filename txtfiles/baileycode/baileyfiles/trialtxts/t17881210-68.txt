






68. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     COLLINS
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     FARREL
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of December
, a looking glass, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  John 
                  Watson
               




            .
The prisoner Collins was taken with the glass directly after it was lost.


                  JOSEPH
                   COLLINS
               



            , 

               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            . 


                  EDWARD
                   FARREL
               



            , 

               NOT GUILTY
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice WILSON.
