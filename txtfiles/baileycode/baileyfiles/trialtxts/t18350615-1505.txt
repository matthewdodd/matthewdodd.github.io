



1505. 




THOMAS RILEY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
15th of June, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 



Lawrence Hutchinson, from his person.






LAWRENCE HUTCHINSON
. I live in Bread-street, Cheapside. On the 15th of June, about nine o'clock, I was near the post-office—I turned round quite promiscuously, and saw the prisoner drop my handkerchief—I took it up and took him—this is my handkerchief.
The prisoner put in a written defence, stating that he saw the handkerchief fall on the ground, and upon taking it up the prosecutor secured him.





LAWRENCE HUTCHINSON

re-examined. He had it in his hand, and dropped it when he saw me—on the way to the watchhouse he said he was sorry for what he had done.




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.
