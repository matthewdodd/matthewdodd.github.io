





62. 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Harrison 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing three Trusses of Hay
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Bridget 
                     Miller 
                  




                
             of Thames-street


, on the 20th of December
.


                  Q. 
               


            (to 
               
                  Bridget 
                  Miller 
               



            .) What have you to say to the Prisoner at the Bar?
Miller. I have lost Hay several Times, but never knew by whom till the 17th of December last, the Prisoner at the Bar came to my House and confess'd that he had that Morning taken from me three Trusses of Hay.


                  Q. 
               


            (to the Prisoner.) What have you to say for yourself?
Prisoner. My Lord, I never took any before.


                  Q. 
               


            (to Mrs. Miller.) Do you know any Thing of the Boy
?
Miller. No, my Lord. 

               Guilty
            .
