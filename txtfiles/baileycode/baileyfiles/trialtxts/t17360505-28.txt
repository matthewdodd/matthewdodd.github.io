





34. 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Hopkins 
                  



               
            , of St. Andrew's Holborn


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Silver Spoon, value 10 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Imeson 
                  




                
            , the 30th of March
.


                  Thomas 
                  Imeson 
               



            . The Prisoner came to be hired as my Cook
, and stole the Spoon before she went out of the House She confessed the Fact before the Justice, and sign'd her Confession after it had been read over to her.


                  Joseph 
                  Higginson 
               



            . The Woman offered the Spoon to me to pawn. I asked her some Questions, and she trembled and quak'd exceedingly. I believe she had been guilty of such Practices before, for she carried me herself to the Prosecutor. 


               Guilty 10 d.
