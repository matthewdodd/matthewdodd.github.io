







                     Thomas 
                     Edwards 
                  



               
             , of Marybone


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Pistols, value 50 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Knapp 
                        




                      
                   ,2 old Holsters, and a Bridle
            , in the Stable of Vallentine Judge, the 7th of August
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence that the Pistols were stollen out of the Stables and that the Prisoner was apprehended by the Watch with the Pistols; the Prisoner having nothing to say in his defence, but that he had them of a Boy, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 4 s.10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
