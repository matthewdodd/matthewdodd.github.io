



268. 




JOSEPH JACKSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
4th of December, at 
St. Botolph without Aldgate, 1 gelding, value 5l.; 1 set of harness, value 1l.; the goods of 



Thomas Kinch; and that he had been previously convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. * Aged 25. (See First Session, page 40.)



Transported for Life.
