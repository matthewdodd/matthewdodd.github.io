





2074. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  PARSONS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 28th of October
, 1 waistcoat, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Abraham 
                  Solomon
               




            .
ABRAHAM SOLOMON. I am a clothes-dealer
. On the 28th of October, while I was at a neighbour's, this waistcoat was taken from my shop; I saw it at Eagle-street watch-house, and know it - I had seen it safe half an hour before.


                  JAMES 
                  ISAAC
               



            . I saw the prisoner running up Hatton-wall - I came up to him, and he was going to throw this waistcoat down an area; he was about three hundred yards from Solomon's - I took him to the watch-house; the prosecutor came, and said if it was his there was a figure of 8 on the collar, and there is one; the prisoner said he found it in the middle of Great Saffron-hill.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was going down Saffron-hill. and saw a young man running, who I suppose dropped it; I was running away with it, and was taken.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
