





538. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     STRUTTON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th day of May
, a linen shirt, value 8 s. a cloth coat, value 27 s. a waistcoat, value 9 s. a pair of breeches, value 18 s. a pair of stockings, value 3 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Hart
                        



                     
                   the younger
; two pair of thread stockings, value 5 s. one pair of worsted ditto, the property of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Graves
                        



                     
                  , and one pair of worsted stockings, value 2 s. 6 d. the property of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Hart
                        



                     
                   the elder

.
The prisoner was taken with the property upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
