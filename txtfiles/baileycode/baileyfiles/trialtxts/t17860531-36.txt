





496. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     LLOYD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, one box-coat, value 40 s.
             the property of the Right Hon. 
               
                  
Earl
 
                     Bathurst
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the coat upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
