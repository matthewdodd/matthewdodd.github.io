





356. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     KING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of January
, 1 ham, value 6s.
            , the goods of John 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Strother
                  




               
             and another.


                  GEORGE 
                  MORRIS
               



            . On the 29th of January, I saw the prisoner at the prosecutor's shop, in Tabernacle-walk


 - a man took a ham off a nail, and gave it to the prisoner, who was at his side; the prisoner put it in his apron and went off - I ran after him, and, just as I overtook him, he dropped it - I had not lost sight of him.
JOHN JAMES STROTHER. I have one partner
 - this ham is ours; but I was out when it was taken.
Prisoner's Defence. A young man took it down, and gave it to me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
