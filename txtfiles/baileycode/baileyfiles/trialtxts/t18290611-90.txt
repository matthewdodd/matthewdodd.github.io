





1088. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     SAVAGE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of May
, 7 glass castors, value 15s., and 1 castorstand, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Wetters
                  




               
            .
HEPZIBAH GREENAWAY. I live with my uncle 
               
                  William 
                  Wetters
               



            , who keeps a broker's-shop
, near Foley-place


. On the 8th of May, about ten o'clock in the morning, I missed a set of seven cut glass castors and a plated stand - I had put them out about half an hour before.


                  HENRY 
                  STOWELL
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner with these castors in the street, about half a mile from Mr. Wetter's, between ten and eleven o'clock - he first said that he got them from his mother; he then said a man gave him a shilling to carry them from Coventry-street.
Prisoner's Defence. A gentleman gave me a shilling to carry them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
