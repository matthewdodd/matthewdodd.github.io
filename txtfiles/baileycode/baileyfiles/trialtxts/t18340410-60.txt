





585. 
               
                  MARY 
                  BATES
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of December
, 5 yards of cotton, value 3s.; 1 cloak, value 5s.; 1 bonnet, value 3s.; 1 pair of shoes, value 3s.; 1 shift, value 1s.; 1 table-cloth, value 6d.; 1 pair of stockings, value 9d.; and 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Francis 
                  Keffia
               




            , to which she 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
