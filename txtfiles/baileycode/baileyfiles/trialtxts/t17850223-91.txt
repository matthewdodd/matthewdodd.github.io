






382. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     MOSELEY
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MALY
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 13th day of January
 last, one linen handkerchief, value 6 d.
             the property of a certain person unknown.


                  WILLIAM
                   MOSELEY
               



            , 

               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .


                  JOHN
                   MALY
               



            , 

               GUILTY
            .





               Whipped
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
