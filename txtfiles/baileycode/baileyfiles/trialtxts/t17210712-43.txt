







                     Ann 
                     Richardson 
                  



               
            , of Edminton


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Porridge Pot value 5s. a Brass Kettle 5s. and a Skimmer 6d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Scroggs 
                  




                
            . The Prosecutor deposed that the Prisoner made Hay for him Seven Days, and lay in his Barn, that when she was gone he mist his Goods, followed her to London, and took her as she was selling of them. The Prisoner said it was her first Fact. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10d.
            



               Transportation
