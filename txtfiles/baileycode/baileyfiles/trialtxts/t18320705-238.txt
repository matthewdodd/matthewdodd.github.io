





Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
1664. 
               
                  
                     FREDERICK 
                     BENNETT
                  




               
             was indicted 


               for stealing, on the 7th of July
, 1 pair of of trousers, value 18s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Sharpe
                        




                     
                   and others
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  TALBOT
               



            . On the 7th of July, a little before twelve o'clock, I was in the shop of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Sharpe
               



             and others, tailor
s, Worship-street


, and saw the prisoner snatch these trousers down from about a yard within the shop - they were pinned on an iron; he ran away with them in his apron - I pursued and took him with them, without losing sight of him.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HOMES
               



            . I am a Policeman. I received him in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
