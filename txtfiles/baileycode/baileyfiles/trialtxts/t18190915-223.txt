





1297. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     RICHARDS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of August
, 14 napkins, value 6 s.; three caps, value 18 d.; one handkerchief, value 6 d.; one petticoat. value 1 s.; three stockings, value 1 s.; one purse, value 1 d., and one frill, value 6 d.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Messenger
                  




               
            .


                  MARY 
                  ANN MESSENGER
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  John 
                  Messenger
               



            , who is a publican
; the prisoner was our servant
. She only lived three days with us, and on the 10th of August she absconded with these things; I also lost 48 l., and a purse out of the same drawer. My husband apprehended her.


                  BARNARD 
                  GLEED
               



            . I am an officer. I found the purse and handkerchief in the prisoner's pocket. She dropped the napkins.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
