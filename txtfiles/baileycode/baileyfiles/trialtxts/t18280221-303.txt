





781. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  CLANCEY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of February
, 36 lbs. weight of cheese, value 30s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Surridge
               




            .
JOHN SURRIDGE. I am a chesse-monger
, and live in Fenchurch-street


. On the 4th of February the officer called, and asked if I had lost any cheese; I missed one from the door.


                  CHARLES 
                  THOROGOOD
               



            . On the 4th of February I was in the street, and saw the prisoner and another; I watched them, and saw the prisoner go and take this cheese from Mr. Surridge's door; he crossed over, and I stopped him with it - he said he had 9d. given him to carry it from London-bridge to Mile-end-road; he had called to the other to take it, but he would not - the other got away.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
