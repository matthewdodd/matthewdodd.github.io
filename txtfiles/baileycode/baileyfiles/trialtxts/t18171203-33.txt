





32. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     DAVEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously and knowingly having in his possession a forged Bank of England note
            .
To which indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
