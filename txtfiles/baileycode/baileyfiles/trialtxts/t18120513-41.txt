





469. 
               
                  
                     ISAAC 
                     NOWLAND
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 11th of January
, feloniously, knowingly, and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession two forged bank-notes for the payment of one pound each; he knowing them to be forged
            .
SECOND COUNT, for having in his custody and possession a certain other forged bank-note, he knowing that also to be forged.
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
