





1400. 




GEORGE THOMPSON
 was indicted

 for feloniously and knowingly uttering, on the 
17th of April, a certain forged warrant for the delivery of 1 milk-pot, 5 tea-spoons, and 2 table-spoons, with intent to defraud 



George Orpwood
:—Also, 

for feloniously and knowingly uttering, on the 
9th of January, a forged warrant for the delivery of 1 sugar-basin, 1 pair of tongs, 8 spoons, and 1 tea-pot, with intent to defraud 



George Harvey
:—Also, 

for obtaining money by false pretences; to all of which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 41.—


Transported for Seven Years.
