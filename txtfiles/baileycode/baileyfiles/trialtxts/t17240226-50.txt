







                     Abraham 
                     Appleby 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. John Wapping


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing Goods, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Christopher 
                           Ruscum 
                        




, the 23d of February
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Piece of Cloth was taken out of the Prosecutor's Shop Window, and was found upon the Prisoner immediately. He confess'd the Fact before the Justice, which Confession was read in Court. He called several Persons of Credit to his Reputation, who had never known any ill Action by him, and gave him a very good Character, as a sober honest Person. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
