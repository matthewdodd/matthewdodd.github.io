





455. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     PITCHER
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               she on the 22d of July
, previously, knowingly, wittingly, and without lawful excuse, had in her custody and possession, a certain forged note, purporting to be a bank note for the payment of 2 l. she knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
