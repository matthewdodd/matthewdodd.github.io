





597. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     WILSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of January
, 1 saw, value 2s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Pett
                  




               
            .
HENRY PETT. I am a carpenter
. I was at work at a new beer-shop in St. Luke's


, building a kitchen
, on the 29th of January; the prisoner came to warm himself at the fire - I went to dinner, and left my saw in the yard; I returned in about three-quarters of an hour; when I came back; I saw the prisoner going away; I missed my saw.


                  LEONARD 
                  CLARE MATTHEWS
               



            . I have the saw, which was pawned on the 26th of January by the prisoner and another lad.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
