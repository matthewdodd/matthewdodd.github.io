





515. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     LEAVER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of October
, one silk handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Giles 
                     Long
                  




               
            .


                  GILES
                   LONG
               



             sworn.
I am a sheriff's officer
, I was going along Cannon Street


 on Tuesday last, about three o'clock, I was informed my pocket was picked, and I went up to the prisoner, seized him; and took it from under his coat. (Produced, and deposed to.)

Prisoner. there was a young man in a green coat, that dropped it and I picked it up, and that man came.



               GUILTY
            . (Aged 21.)




               Transported for seven Years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
