





1376. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     TURNER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of September
, one coat, value 4 s., and one whip, value 1 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Wilson
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  WILSON
               



            . I am a coachman
. On the 18th of September, about ten o'clock, my coat and whip were on my coach-box, which stood in King-street, Covent-garden


. The waterman told me that they had been stolen; the prisoner was pointed out to me, and I secured him with them. He made a very great resistance, and said the coat was not mine.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was drinking with some of the coachmen, and they took my handkerchief. I found the coat on the ground, and ran away with it to have a game with him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 33.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
