





770. 
               
                  
                     DAVID 
                     DAVIS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of September
 last, a pair of leather shoes, value 4 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Maxfield
                        



                     
                  ; a shirt, value 5 s. three pair of stockings, value 6 s. a pair of boots, value 7 s. a sheet value 6 s. two handkerchiefs, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Rees
                  



               
            .
The two prosecutors deposed that they lodged in the same house with the prisoner; and missed the respective articles in the indictment, which were found on the prisoner, by 
               
                  Joseph
                   Pick
               



            ; and the prisoner confessed taking them, without promise or threat.
The prisoner called two witnesses, who gave him a good character.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
