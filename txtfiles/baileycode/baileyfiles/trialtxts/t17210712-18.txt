







                     Zechariah 
                     Collier 
                  



               
            . of St. Botolph at Billinsgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 23 s. 6 d.
             the Money of several Persons, on the 14th of May
 last. 
               
                  William 
                  Bedman 
               



             deposed, that the Prisoner was his Servant
; that he sent him and the Maid to Church, and his Wife and self went out and lock up the Doors, that the Prisoner return'd, got in at the Back Door, took his own things and the Money mentioned in the Indictment, and went away; that he came to him but the Monday before, that he found him at the Crown at Epsom, where by means of a Counterfeit Recommendation he was got into Service; that he owned the Fact when taken saying, that he went first to Eltham and spent 5 s. brought the rest of the Money back to his Mother to fetch his Clothes out of pawn. His Confession before the Justice was read in Court. The Jury found him 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
