







                     Joseph 
                     Vaughan 
                  



               
             , of St. Mary le Bow


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Quarter of Mutton, and 31 lb. of Beef, in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Barrow 
                        




, the 23d of March
 last.


                  John 
                  Barrow 
               



             , depos'd, That he lost 31 lb. of Beef and a Quarter of Mutton, out of his Shop; that the Prisoner having stolen and carried Home the Beef came again for the Mutton, and being seen as he was carrying it away, was apprehended with the Mutton, and own'd also the taking of the Beef, and directed them to his Lodgings, where it was found. The Fact being plainly proved, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d.
