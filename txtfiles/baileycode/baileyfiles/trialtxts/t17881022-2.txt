





608. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     MILLER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 24th day of October
, one linen handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Akerman
                  




Esq
;


                  RICHARD
                   AKERMAN
               



             Esq; sworn.
Coming up Ludgate-hill


 about ten o'clock to the Sessions-house, the prisoner picked my pocket; I did not know it till Newman came up to me and said, Mr. Akerman, here is a man has picked your pocket; Newman pulled up the man's apron and the handkerchief was taken out of his breeches.
NEWMAN sworn.
I saw the prisoner take it, and I informed Mr. Akerman, and I took the handkerchief out of the prisoner's breeches in Mr. Akerman's presence.
(Produced and deposed to.)



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
