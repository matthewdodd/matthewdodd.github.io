







                     Sarah 
                     Parsons 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Curtains, Linnen, &c. value 40 s, in the Dwelling house of 
                     
                        Edward 
                        Ogden 
                     




, the
11th of September
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence. That the Goods were taken out of the Prosecutor's House, and that they had been pawn'd by the Prisoner. While the Prosecutor's Wife was in the Country, another Woman Servant being left to take Care of the House. The Prisoner pleaded. That she did not steal the Goods, but was desir'd to pawn them by the Woman Servant, and she knew not that she had stolen them. The Prosecutor depos'd. That the Servant was run away. The Jury found her 


               guilty of the Indictment. 


                     Transportation
                  . But recommended her to the Favour of the Court
            .
