






109. 




CHARLES ARNOLD



 was separately indicted for stealing 1 watch, value 1l., the goods of 



Eliza Surry Eason:


1 spoon, 1l., the goods of 



Caroline Susanna Stott:—

5 spoons, value 1l. 5s.; I eyeglass, 10s.; and 1 brooch, 5s.; the goods of 



Ellen Paine:


and 1 coat, value 8s.; and 1 umbrella, 4s.; the goods of 



William Turner; to all which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 26.—


Transported for Seven Years
.
