







                     William 
                     Shaw 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles without Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 dozen of Oiled Sheep Skins value 4 l. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Peter 
                           Fesse 
                        




                      
                  , in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        Abraham 
                        Crowley 
                     



, on the 17th of this Instant April
. Mr. Shaw deposed that the Goods were taken out of his Kitchen about 8 a Clock on Monday Night. and found on the Prisoner the next Morning, all but 2 Skins, which he said he had sold over Night in Long Lane for the Full value. 
               
                  John 
                  Palmer 
               



             confirmed the finding the Goods upon the Prisoner as aforesaid. The Prisoner in Excuse said that he had received a Kick in his Head by a Horse, since which he oftentimes knew not what he did. The Jury considering the matter found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
