





50. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     HOWARD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th day of December
, one corded dimity petticoat, value 3 s. two muslin aprons, value 4 s. a child's laced cap, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Reader
                  




               
            .
The prisoner went to nurse the prosecutor's wife, and carried away the things the next day, and the petticoat was on her; she was taken directly.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
