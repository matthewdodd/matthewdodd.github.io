






443. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     CARTER
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     MADERICK
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 9th day of April
, eleven quarts of oil, value 11 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Morrice 
                     Thomas
                  




               
            .
The prisoners were stopped; they dropped the oil, which was in two skins.
BOTH 

               GUILTY
            .
Each 



               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
