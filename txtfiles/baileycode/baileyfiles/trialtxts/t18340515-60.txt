





798. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HATTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of April
, 10 yards of printed cotton, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Galliers
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  SMITH
               



            . On the 10th of April I was at the corner of Bedford-street


, near the prosecutor's shop - I saw the prisoner there - he passed the shop door, put in his hand, and pulled out a piece of print - I called "Stop thief" - he ran up the street, and threw the print on a baker's neck - the baker said, "I do not want it" - I then called, "Stop thief - stop him, Mr. Godfrey;" which he did.
Prisoner. Q. Did not you see the baker and another at the door? A. No.


                  MARY 
                  ANN CHILD
               



            . I was living with Mr. Galliers - I was in the parlour - I saw Mr. Galliers run out - I went out and found the print in Edward-street - I brought it back.


                  JAMES 
                  GODFREY
               



             (police-constable N 74). I took the prisoner, who ran away very fast.


                  WILLIAM 
                  GALLIERS
               



            . This is my print - I know nothing of the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence. I was returning from my uncle's - I saw the baker and another young man pulling this print round the corner - I ran after them, but they ran away and the officer took me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
