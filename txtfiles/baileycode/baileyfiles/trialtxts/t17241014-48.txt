







                  Esther 
                  Montgomery 
               



            , of the Parish of Pancrass


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing divers Linen in the House of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Montague 
                        




 the 24th of August
 last. It appeared by the Evidence, that the Prisoner, assisted the Prosecutor in washing Linen, she being a Washer-woman
, and took the Goods and pawn'd them. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
