



2205. 




WILLIAM MASON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
13th of September, 1 handkerchief, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 



John Parker, from to person.






THOMAS BLOSSETT
. I am a constable of Covent Garden-market. I know a Person of the name of John Parker—I was in Covent Garden-market on the 13th of September—I saw the prisoner following Mr. Parker down the market several times, and then as he came to the centre row, I saw the prisoner go behind him and take this handkerchief (producing it) from his left-hand pocket—I took him.

(John Parker being called on his recognizance, did not appear.)




GUILTY
.* Aged 18.—


Transported for Ten Years.
