





52. 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Brannam 
                  



               
             of St. Martin in the Fields


 was indicted for 


               stealing 22 Yards of Linnen, value 25 Shillings
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Reavis 
                  




                
            , the 6th of February
. The Prisoner and 
               
                  Ann 
                  Curson 
               



             went to the Prosecutor's Shop to cheapen some Linnen: The Prisoner had a Child in her Arms, and convey'd away the 22 Yards of Linnen under the Child's Cloaths. Curson was taken up on suspicion of being concerned in another Robbery, and before Justice Mitford she confessed being concerned with the Prisoner in this Fact. 


               Guilty, 4s. 10d.
