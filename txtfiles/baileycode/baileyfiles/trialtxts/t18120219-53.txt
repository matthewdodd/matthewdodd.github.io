





265. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     COOPER
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 10th of January
, had in his custody and possession divers forged and counterfeited bank notes, for the payment of two pound, he knowing them to be forged and counterfeited
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded.




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
