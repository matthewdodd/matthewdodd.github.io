





1025. 
               
                  
                     HANNAH 
                     COOPER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of June
, 4 lbs. of butter, value 4 s. 6 d.; 3 lbs. of pork, value 2 s. 6 d., 10 caps, value 5 s.; two pair of stockings, value 2 s.; two sheets, value 2 s.; two petticoats, value 3 s., and one pair of shoes, value 6 d.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Brewer
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  BREWER
               



            . I am a cheesemonger
, and live at Kensington


; the prisoner was my servant
. I suspected her, and requested to see her box; she said she had lost the key. I got a constable, who searched her box, and found this property, with the butter and pork.


                  GEORGE 
                  HULL
               



            . I am an officer. I searched the prisoner's box, she gave me the key. I found all the property in it, except the petticoat. She said she put the butter and pork there to eat when she was hungry. The pork was not dressed.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I intended to eat the pork and butter - I found the other things.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
