





78. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     MAPLE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of November
, one coat, value 4 l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Isaacs
                  




               
            .


                  SAMUEL 
                  ISAACS
               



            . I keep a clothes shop
 in Brick-lane


; this coat was stolen from my door about half-past three o'clock. I stopped the prisoner with it under his coat.


                  ELLEN 
                  CLARK
               



            . I live next door to Mr. Isaacs. I saw the prisoner run away with the coat. I alarmed Isaacs, who secured him - another boy was with him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Garrow
               



            .
