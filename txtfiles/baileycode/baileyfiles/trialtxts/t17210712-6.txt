







                     Samuel 
                     Annable 
                  



               
             (a Boy
) was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Cotton Handkerchief value 1 s. 6 d. from the Person of 
                     
                        Barnard 
                        Dixon 
                     



, on the 19th of June
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that as he stood to see them play at Cudgels in Smithfield


, a little Boy told him that the Prisoner had taken his Handkerchief out of his Pocket; whereupon he went to him and Charged him with it, and she gave it him but of his Breeches. The Jury considering the matter, found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
