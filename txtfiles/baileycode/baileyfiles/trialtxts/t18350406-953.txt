



953. 




JAMES NEALE
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
5th of April, 1 handkerchief, value 6d., the goods of 



Robert Corser, from his person.






ROBERT CORSER
. I live in Leadenhall-street. Last Sunday evening I was in Skinner-street, Snowhill, and lost this pocket-handkerchief from my pocket.





CHARLES THORP
. I am a patrol of St. Bride's. Last Sunday evening, at a quarter past nine o'clock, I saw the prisoner and two others—I watched them—I saw the prisoner take this handkerchief out of the prosecutor's pocket—I seized him by both arms, and took this handkerchief out of his right-hand pocket.




GUILTY
. † Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.
