







                     Peter 
                     Cartwright 
                  



               
            , of Bishopsgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing three Yards of Bays, value 7s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Warman 
                  




                
            , on the 26th of Jan
 last. It appear'd, that in the Evening the Prosecutor observing the Prisoner to pass by his Door several times, he suspected him, and went backwards to watch him. The Prisoner seeing nobody in the Shop, stept in, look'd about him, took the Goods, and went out. The Prosecutor follow'd and took them upon him 


               . Guilty Value 4s. 10 d.
