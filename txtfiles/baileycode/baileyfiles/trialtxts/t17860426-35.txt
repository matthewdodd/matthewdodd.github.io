





353. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     GREY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 1st day of April
, eleven pounds weight of beef, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Theophilus 
                     Pomroy
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor saw the prisoner take the beef, and stopped him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   EYRE
               



            .
