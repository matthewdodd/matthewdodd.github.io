







                     Mary 
                     Brown 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary White-Chappel


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Shift, value 4 s. and 4 l. in Money, in the Dwelling-House
             of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Nichols 
                  




                
            , the 6th of July
 last.
Mrs. Nichols depos'd, That she being to go to a Christening, and the Prisoner lodging in her House, she offer'd to look after her Child while she went, and that when she came home the Prisoner was gone, and Money too. The Prisoner had confess'd the taking of the Money before the Lord Mayor, and the Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 39 s.
