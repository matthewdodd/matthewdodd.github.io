





1679. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     PURTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of July, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Thompson
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JAMES 
                  THOMPSON
               



            . I hold a situation in the Bank. On Sunday, the 1st of July, I was at Holborn-bars


, at a quarter before twelve o'clock at night - I felt my handkerchief taken - I turned, and seized the prisoner with it in his hand; I gave him to the officer, but I saw him run from him, and I held him till the officer came up again and took him - I understood the officer had been knocked down and had lost the handkerchief; and while I stood there I was surrounded by thirty or forty bad characters.
Prisoner's Defence. I was ten yards from the gentleman when he took me, and I was ill-used by the Policeman.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
