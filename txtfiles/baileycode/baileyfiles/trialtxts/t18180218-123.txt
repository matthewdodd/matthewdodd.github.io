





492. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     STEPHENS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of February
, two loaves of bread, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  George 
                  Ulrich
               




            .


                  GEORGE 
                  ULRICH
               



            . I am a baker
, and live in Bowling-street, Westminster.


                  THOMAS 
                  COLLINS
               



            . I am a journeyman baker. On the 12th of February, about nine o'clock at night, I saw the prisoner come out of the prosecutor's shop with two loaves. I knew him before. I pursued, and took him at the door.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. A boy gave them to me to carry.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
