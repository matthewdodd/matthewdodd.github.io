







                     Elisabeth 
                     Tucker 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Dunstan in the West


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of Sheets, value 18 s. a Gown value 10 s. a pair of silk Stockings, value 10 s. and other Goods, in the Dwelling-House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Rimes 
                        




, the 9th of December
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, the Prisoner came into his House to drink with these Men, and there not being Room below, they carried them up Stairs, and the Prisoner went away, and left the Men there, the Goods were carried away, and the Gown was taken upon the Prisoner when she was apprehended. The Prisoner deny'd the Fact, and said the Gown was her own, and not the Prosecutor's, but this did not avail, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 4s. 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
