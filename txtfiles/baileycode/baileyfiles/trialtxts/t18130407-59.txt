





403. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     BOGG
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               that he, on the 16th of February
, had in his custody and possession, divers forged; bank notes, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
