





393. + 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Naylor 
                  



               
             , was indicted for 


               stealing a handkerchief, value 2 s. the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Day 
                        




                      
                  , privately from his person
            , July the 1st
.


                  John 
                  Day 
               



            . On the 1st of July about ten o'clock at night, I met the Prisoner, and asked her the way to the water-side; she sent me down an alley and followed me, clapped her hand upon my shoulder, and asked me where I was going? I saw a watchman, and asked him if that was the way to the water-side? He said it was not, and drove the Prisoner out before him. I put my hand into my pocket and missed my handkerchief, and found it under her arm holes in the watch-house - I am a Gardener, I live at Rotherbith .
Prisoner. He pushed me down the alley, and gave me the handkerchief to lie with me.


                  Edward 
                  Wells 
               



             . I took the handkerchief from her, she said then that he gave it her, but he denied it.
Prisoner. He said if I would return him the handkerchief he would give me a shilling.
Day. I did not say so. 


               Guilty 10 d.
