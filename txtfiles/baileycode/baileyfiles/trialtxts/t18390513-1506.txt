



1506. 




JAMES HASWELL
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
15th of April, 1 tea-chest, value 1s.; and 12lbs. weight of tea, value 3l. the goods of 



John Potts and others, and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 11.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Isle of Wight.
