





347. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     DOUTY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 13th pay of January
 last, one linen handkerchief, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Stapleton
                  




               
            .
The prosecutor was coming up Snow-hill


, and felt a hand in his pocket, and turned round, and saw the prisoner with his handkerchief in his hand, which he saw him drop.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
