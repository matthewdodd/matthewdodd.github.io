





1479. 
               
                  CATHERINE 
                  ARMSTRONG
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of August
, 4lbs. weight of bacon, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Geary
               




            .


                  HENRY 
                  POTTER
               



            . I am in the employ of William Geary, a cheesemonger
, of Broad-street, Bloomsbury


. On the evening of the 25th of August the watchman brought the prisoner to the shop with this bacon, which I had seen about two hours before, and knew it to be Mr. Geary's.


                  FRANCIS 
                  REYNOLDS
               



            . I saw the prisoner pass the shop and take the bacon out of the window - I took her with it in her apron.
Prisoner's Defence. It was the greatest distress that made me do it.



               GUILTY
             Aged 40.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
