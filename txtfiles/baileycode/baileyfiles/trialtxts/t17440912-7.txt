





358. 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Waters 
                  



               
            , of St. John Hackney


, was indicted for 


               stealing a hat, value 1 s. a perriwig, 2 s and a shirt, 2 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Isaac 
                     Alvares 
                  




                
             , July 26
.


                  John 
                  Stedder 
               



            . The Prisoner came out of a lower window of Mr. Alvares's house at Clapton


, towards the highway; I saw her shuffling something into her pocket, pursued her about 10 or 12 rod, and found her behind a door, and saw a shirt taken from her.


                  Hannah 
                  Davis 
               



            . I was shutting Mr. Franco's windows, and saw the Prisoner jump out of Mr. Alvare's window. I sent my fellow servant over to enquire whether it was the chairwoman, and then they found they were robbed.


                  Rebecca 
                  Hyans 
               



             . I took the shirt from her, and know it to be my master's shirt. The hat and wig were taken from her by another person. 


               Guilty 10 d.
