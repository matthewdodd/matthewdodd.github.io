





481. (M.) 
               
                  
                     ABRAHAM
                      MELONE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a silver watch, value 40 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Banks
                  




               
            , May 26
. +


                  John
                   Banks
               



            . I was on Tower-hill


 on the 25th or 26th of May, about half after seven o'clock; I passed through a crowd there; I missed my watch when I got out of the crowd.


                  Moses
                   Hyam
               



            . On the 27th of May the prisoner brought this watch to me; I suspected him and therefore I stopt it. (The watch produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)
Prisoner's Defence.
I found the watch on Tower-hill.
Banks. He said so at first before the Justice; afterwards he said he found it in a ditch, and not on Tower-hill.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
