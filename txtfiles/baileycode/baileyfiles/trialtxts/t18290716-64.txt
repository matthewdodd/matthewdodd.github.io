





First London Jury - before Mr. Recorder.
1438. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     STEADMAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3d of July
, 1 coat, value 2l.; 2 pairs of trousers,value 28s.; 4 waistcoats, value 28s., and 1 jacket, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        Robert 
                        Morrison
                     




                  , his master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
