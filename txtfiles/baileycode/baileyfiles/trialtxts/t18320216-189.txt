






695. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     FARRELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of February
, 1 swing looking-glass, value 18s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Sadgrove
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  MERRICK
               



            . I am porter to Mr. 
               
                  William 
                  Sadgrove
               



            , in Elder-street, Finsbury-circus


. On the morning of the 9th of February I was sweeping opposite his door, and saw the prisoner take this glass and turn into Longalley - I pursued him, crying Stop him! he put down the glass - I pursued him, and took him; this is the glass.
Prisoner. I was out of work.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
