





599. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     DAVIS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of January
, 1 weighing-machine, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Welch
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  TAPPLIN
               



            . I am servant to 
               
                  William 
                  Welch
               



            , a milkman
, of Queen-street, Golden-square


. On the 22d of January, about half-past nine o'clock at night, I met the prisoner with this weighing-machine, about half-way down the street; I went and told the watchman - I had seen it safe about a quarter of an hour before.


                  JACOB 
                  TOWNSEND
               



            . Tapplin told me, and I took the prisoner; he dropped the machine, and ran - I pursued, and took him.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. It was given me to take into Windmill-street.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
