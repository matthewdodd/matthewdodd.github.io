






397 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MACKINTOSH
                   alias 
                     JOHN 
                     
KIRBY




             was indicted, for 


               stealing on the 8th of April
, one pair of leather shoes, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Thompson
                  




               
            . The prisoner was found with the property upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
