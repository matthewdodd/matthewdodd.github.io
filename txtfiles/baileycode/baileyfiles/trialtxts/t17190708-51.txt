







                     Elizabeth 
                     Dawson 
                  



               
             of St. Paul's Covent-Garden


 was indicted for 


               stealing a Stuff Gown and Petticoat and a Silver Spoon
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Thorn 
                  




                
            , the 24th of June
 last. It appear'd the Prisoner was the Prosecutors Lodger, who missing the Goods and suspecting her, gave her Warning; that she going to sell the Spoon (the top where the Mark was being broke off) was stopt by the Goldsmith, and the Gown found upon her. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value or 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
