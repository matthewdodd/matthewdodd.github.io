







                     John 
                     Clare 
                  



               
             of St. Leonard Shoreditch


 was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief value 2 s. 6 d. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Storey 
                        




, on the 27th of March
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that being at Stepney Church


 he felt his Handkerchief sliding out of his Pocket, whereupon he caught hold of the Prisoner's Hand and found it therein. The Prisoner said another dropt it on the Ground and he took it up: but the Jury not believing him, found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
