                  John 
                  Hudson 
               



            . The prisoner pledg'd 2 handkerchiefs with me ( producing them) I asked her if they were her own, she said they were.


                  George 
                  Mure 
               



            . The prisoner pledg'd 4 handkerchiefs and a pair of thread stockings with me ( produc'd in court) as her own property.


                  Nathaniel 
                  Adams 
               



            . These 6 handkerchiefs produced here are the property of Major Johnson, and the stockings were mine.
Prisoner's defence.
There were 2 other women besides me used to wash at Major Johnson's, if they were pawned in my name I can't help it.



               Guilty
