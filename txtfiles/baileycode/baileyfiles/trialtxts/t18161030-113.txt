





1211. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JONES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23rd of September
, a shawl, value 4l.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Harriett 
                     Henley
                  




               
            .


                  HARRIETT 
                  HENLEY
               



            . The prisoner snatched my shawl, and ran down the Court. When I saw himat the watchhouse I knew him. I never got my shawl back again.


                  MARY 
                  ANN
               



             HEARNEY. I saw the prisoner snatch the shawl from the shoulders of the prosecutrix.


                  GEORGE 
                  PERRY
               



            . I took the prisoner into custody in consequence of the description I received from Harriett Henley; I had known him before.



               GUILTY
            , aged 20.




               Transported for Life
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
