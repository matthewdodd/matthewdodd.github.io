





First London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
1219. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     GOODWIN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of June
, 1 coat, value 13s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Stafford
               




            ; and that he had previously been convicted of felony; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 46. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
