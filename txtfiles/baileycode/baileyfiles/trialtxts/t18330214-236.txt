





665. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     BAKER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of February
, 1 tea-caddy, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Danieli
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  PIERCE
               



            . On the 14th of February I was in Oxford-street


, and saw the prisoner take a teacaddy from a board fixed outside a shop, and walk away; I told the prosecutor - his shopman followed her, and took her.


                  WILLIAM 
                  VAN
               



             (Police-constable D 94). The prisoner was given to me; I found no money on her, but two breasts of mutton, five duplicates, a book, and two pairs of ladies shoes, quite new, with the ticket on them,"3s. 6d. a pair."



                  JAMES 
                  DALY
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. 
               
                  Charles 
                  Danieli
               



            , a jeweller
 - he sells caddies; this is his caddy - the witness gave me information; I took the prisoner thirty yards off, with this caddy under her shawl, secreted.
Prisoner's. Defence. I was in great distress, and in an ill state of health



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34. 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
