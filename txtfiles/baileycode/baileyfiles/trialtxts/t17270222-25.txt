







                     Charles 
                     Rawlins 
                  



               
            , of St. Catherines by the Tower


, was indicted for 


               stealing 3 Ells of Holland, from the House of 
                     
                        
                           Mary 
                           Woodsalls 
                        




, on the 28th of Jan.
 last. It appear'd that the Prosecutor was a Pawn-broker
 of Sense and the Prisoner a Thief without Conduct; for after he had stole the Goods he sent a Women with them to pawn, who carried them to the Place from whence they was taken, and being secured with the Goods, she brought out the Prisoner. The Jury brought him in 


               Guilty 10 d
            .
