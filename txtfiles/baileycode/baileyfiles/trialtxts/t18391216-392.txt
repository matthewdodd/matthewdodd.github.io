



392. 




THOMAS KENNEY
 was indicted

 for forging a request for delivery of 1 saw-plate, with intent to defraud 



Richard Richards and another, on the 
24th of August.—2nd COUNT, for uttering the same: also forging a request for delivery of 1 sovereign, with intent to defraud 



Richard Richards, and another, on the 
13th of July.—2nd COUNT, for uttering the same; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 48.—


Transported for Seven Years.
(There was another indictment against the prisoner.)
