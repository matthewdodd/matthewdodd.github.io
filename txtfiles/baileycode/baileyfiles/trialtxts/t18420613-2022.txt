



2022. 




WILLIAM FAIR
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
10th of June, lib. 3oz. weight of bacon, value 10d., the goods of 



George Charles Hunt.






REBECCA HUNT
. I am the wife of George Charles Hunt, and keep a chandler's-shop in Rotherhithe. On the 10th of June I saw the prisoner come into the shop, take the bacon off the counter, and put it under his jacket—he went out, I ran after him, and he was taken by the policeman with it. 


GUILTY.** Aged 14.—



Transported for Seven Years—Convict Ship.
