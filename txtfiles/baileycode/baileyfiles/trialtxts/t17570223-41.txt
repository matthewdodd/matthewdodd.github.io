






136. (M.) 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Cook 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one cloth coat, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Furnis 
                  




                
            , Feb 19
.*
George Furnis. I lost a surtout coat from off my coach-box at the end of Cornhill


, and found it again at Mr. Hunt's, in Monmouth Street. (Produced in court, and deposed to.)
Mr. Hunt. I deal in old cloaths. I bought this coat of the prisoner at the bar last Friday was se'nnight.
Prisoner's Defence.
I found the coat in the street.




               Guilty, 10 d.
