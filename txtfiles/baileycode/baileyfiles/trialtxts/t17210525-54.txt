







                     Elizabeth 
                     Rawson 
                  



               
            , of St. Ann in Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Linnen Gown and Petticoat, a Quilted Petticoat, a Ridinghood, 2 Lace Caps, 2 Cambrick Handkerchiefs, an Apron, a Pair  of Clogs, &c. The Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Terence 
                           Redman 
                        




                      
                  , in the Dwelling-House of the said Redman
            , on the 3 d of August
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner nurst the Prosecutor's Wife in her Lying in, and when the Prosecutor was gone out the Morning aforesaid, took the Goods and went away with them. That when taken she confest where she had sold them, and the suit of Clothes were found accordingly. The Jury considering the Matter, found her 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
