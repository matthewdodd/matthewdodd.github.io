





2392. 




WILLIAM HOLLOWAY
 and 




WILLIAM HILL
 were indicted for

 stealing on the 
17th of October, 1 handkerchief value 3s., the goods of 



Jeremiah How; and that Holloway had been before convicted of felony.






THOMAS COLIFFE
. I live with Jeremiah How at Stroud-green, Islington. I saw these boys on Monday, the 17 the about one hundred yards from my master's at half-past twelve o'clock—I suspected something, because they were at the back of the field where my master's linen was hanging—I ran to them and took them—Hill dropped this handkerchief, which I took up—it is my master's—I took them to the station-house.





JEREMIAH HOW
. This is my handkerchief.
(Francis Levy and Sarah Smith gave Hill a good character.





THOMAS SOLOMON SPENCER
. (police constable N. 121.) I produce a certificate of Holloway's former conviction, which I got at Clerkenwell—(read)—he is the person.

HOLLOWAY—


GUILTY
. Aged 12.

HILL—


GUILTY
. Aged 15.




Transported for Seven Years.
