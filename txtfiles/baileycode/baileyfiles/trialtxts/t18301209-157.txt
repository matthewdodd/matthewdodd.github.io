





157. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MACDONALD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of November
, 1 pair of shoes, value 2s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Peter 
                     Paterson
                  




               
            .


                  MITCHAEL 
                  RYAN
               



            . I was at the shop of Mr. 
               
                  Peter 
                  Paterson
               



            , in Chiswell-street


, on the 25th of November, and saw the prisoner look at some shoes, and take one pair off the hook, just inside the door; he walked away with them - I told the gentleman; he went and took him with them.


                  WILLIAM 
                  WYBERT
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner, and have the shoes.


                  GEORGE 
                  TYLER
               



            . I am apprentice to Mr. 
               
                  Peter 
                  Paterson
               



            . These are his, and were hanging inside the door; I took them from the prisoner's inside pocket.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 49. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
