



2469. 




JOHN ROBINSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
3rd of August, 1 necklace, value 7s., the goods of 



Elizabeth Way, from her person.






ELIZABETH WAY
. I am fifteen years old. I was going along Sun-street, Bishopsgate-street, on the 3rd of August—I had a necklace round my neck—two boys came to me—the prisoner was one of them—he came behind me, took off my necklace, and ran away—I am sure he is the person.





WILLIAM HORLEY
 (police-constable G 172.) I took the prisoner, from the description the prosecutrix gave at the station.




GUILTY
. Aged 13.—


Transported for Ten Years.—Convict Ship.
