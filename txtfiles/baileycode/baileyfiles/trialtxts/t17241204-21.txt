







                     Mary 
                     Hawks 
                  



               
            , of S. Magnus Martyr


, was indicted for 


               stealing in the House of John Hammerton, a Gown value 12d. a Ring value 12s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Margaret 
                           Eaton 
                        



                      
                  ; a Smock value 6d. the Goods of 
                     
                        Eliz 
                        Austin 
                     



                   , and 6 Guineas the Money of 
                     
                        John 
                        Hammerton 
                     



, on the 4th of Nov
. last. It appeared that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, (at the Globe Alehouse on Fishstreet-Hill


) and went away with the Goods and Money, part of which was taken upon her, at the Bull Head in Leadonhall-Street. 


               Guilty to the value of 10d.
            



               Transportation
            .
