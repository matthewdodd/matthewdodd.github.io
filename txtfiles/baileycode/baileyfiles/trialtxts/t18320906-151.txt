





1876. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     CANNON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           Downing
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  DOWNING
               



            . I am a draper
. On the 20th of August I was in Newgate-street


, and felt something at my pocket; I turned, and saw the prisoner, whom I collared, and found my handkerchief in his pocket.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BUTLER
               



             (City Police-constable 30). I took the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was returning from Newgatemarket, and on crossing the road I found the handkerchief - the gentleman came, and said, "You have my handkerchief;" I said, "Is this yours?" and I gave it him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
