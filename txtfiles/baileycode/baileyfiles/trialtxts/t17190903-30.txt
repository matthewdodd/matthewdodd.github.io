







                     Samuel 
                     Yeo 
                  



               
             , of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a long Saw value 20 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Adams 
                  




                
            , on the 7th of August last. It appeared that the Prosecutor lost his Saw, and found it on Mr. Browne, who bought it of the Prisonor for 16 s.6 d. His Confession before the Justice was read in Court, and the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
