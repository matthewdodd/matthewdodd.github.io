





24. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  DAVIS
               



             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 27th of November
, a bag, value 6 d. and one hundred and twelve pounds weight of sugar, value 5 l.
             the property 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Toms
                  



               
            , senior
, and 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Toms
                  



               
            , junior
.




               To this indictment the prisoner pleaded
            

GUILTY.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
