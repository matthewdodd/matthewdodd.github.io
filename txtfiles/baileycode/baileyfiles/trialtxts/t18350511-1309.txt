



1309. 



WILLIAM SIBLEY
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
5th of May, 1 bag, value 1d.; 3 sovereigns; and 1 £10 Bank-note; the goods, and monies of 



Frederick Francis Gray, his master, in his dwelling-house to which he pleaded





GUILTY
.—


Transported for Life.—Recommended to mercy by the Prosecutor.
