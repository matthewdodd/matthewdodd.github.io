



1425. 




EDWARD NELSON
 was indicted

 for stealing on the 
5th of June. 1 handkerchief value 2s., the goods of 



Charles Stevens, from his person.






CHARLES STEVENS
. On the 5th of June, a little before ten o'clock in the evening, I was on Ludgate-hill—I felt a tug at my coat pocket, and

turned and saw the prisoner drop my handkerchief from his hand—I gave him in charge directly—this is it.

Prisoner. Q. You was it on the ground? A. I saw you drop it—there were two of you, one ran away.





FREDERICK LAKING
. I was walking with Mr. Stevens, he turned round—I turned and saw the prisoner drop the handkerchief, the other man ran off, and I pursued, but he got away.

Prisoner. I saw the two gentlemen walking, and a young man behind them—they turned and the handkerchief laid on the ground—the man that took it ran away—they charged me with stealing it.




GUILTY
.* Aged 18.—


Transported for Fourteen Years.
