




2843. 




GEORGE BELL CARTER
 was indicted

 for embezzling, on the 
23rd of December, 25l.; on the 
30th of June, 6l. 13s.; and on the 
12th of July, 4l. 7s. 1d.;—also, 

for embezzling, on the 
2nd of September, 18s. 6d.; which he received for his masters, 



Samuel Thompsett Newington and others; to both which he pleaded



GUILTY
. Aged 28.—


Transported for Seven Years on each Indictment.
