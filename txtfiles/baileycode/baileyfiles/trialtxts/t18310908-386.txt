





1923. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     COWLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 3rd of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        Daniel 
                        Kinsey
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  DANIEL 
                  KINSEY
               



            . I am a saddler
, and live in Regent-street. On Sunday, the 3rd of July, I was at the Parkgate, on Constitution-hill


, and felt something at my coat pocket - I saw the prisoner putting my handkerchief up under his coat behind; part of it hung down - he seemed to be alone; I collared him, and gave him in charge.


                  WILLIAM 
                  SHEPHERD
               



            . I am a Policeman. I was in the Green-park; the people were following the soldiers from the Palace - I saw the prisoner in the crowd; Kinsey gave him in charge with this handkerchief, and I found another between his skin and his shirt; he is in the Park almos every morning.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
