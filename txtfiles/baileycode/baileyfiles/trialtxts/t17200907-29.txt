







                     Edward 
                     Preston 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               privately stealing 2 Perriwigs value 4l. and 12 Ounces of Human Hair value 6 l. out of the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Daniel 
                           Lord 
                        




 on the 24th of August
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner had been the Prosecutor's Servant
 about 11 Days, and went with him to Acton-Fair, but left his Master there, went home and took the Goods out of his Shop; and was taken offering them to Sale; when taken he owned on stole them from his Master. The Goods were produced in Court and owned by the Prosecutor. The Prisoner had nothing to say in his Defence nor any to his Reputation. The Jury considering the matter found him 


               Guilty to the value of 4s. 10d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
