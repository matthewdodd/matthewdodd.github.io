





739. 
               
                  ROBERT 
                  BARTON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of March
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Felix
                     




                  , from his person
            .
WILLIAM FELIX. I am a patten-maker
, and live in Golden-lane. I was near Gray's Inn-lane, in Holborn


, on the 14th of March, just after twelve o'clock at night. I had had a handkerchief in my left-hand coat pocket - I was going to make use of it, and missed it; I looked about, and saw the prisoner with it in his right-hand - I collared him, and he dropped it; I picked it up, and gave him in charge to the watchman - he said he did it to procure him a night's lodging.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  THOMAS 
                  MAGRATH
               



            . I am a constable, and took him in charge.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Life
            .
