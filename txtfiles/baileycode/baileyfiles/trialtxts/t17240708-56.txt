







                     Martha 
                     Brown 
                  



               
             of the Parish of St. Martins in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               Breaking the House of the 
                     
Countess Dowager
 of Oxford

                  , in the Day Time, and stealing wearing Apparel, the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           James 
                        



, the 27th of June
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was seen to come out of the Stables with the Goods, and when she was apprehended, the Goods were taken upon her. The Jury 


               acquitted her of the Burglary, but found her guilty of the Felony, to the value of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
