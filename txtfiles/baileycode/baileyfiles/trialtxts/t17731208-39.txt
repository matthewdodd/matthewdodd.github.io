





49. (2d L.) 
               
                  
                     THOMAS
                      DUGGIN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               ripping, cutting, breaking, and stealing 50 lb. of lead, value 5 s. the said lead being affixed to a dwelling house the property of 
                     
                        John
                         Bell
                     



, Nov 14
. ++


                     John
                      Bell
                  




               
            . I can only say I lost such a quantity of lead.


                  Rowland
                   Williams
               



            . I met the prisoner with the lead on his back, in New-street square, which is about sixty yards from the prosecutor's house, about five o'clock in the morning of the 14th of November; I carried him to Mr. Bell's house.
Thomas Lane. I am a servant to Mr. Bell; I compared the lead with the place from whence my master lost it.
The prisoner said nothing in his defence.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
