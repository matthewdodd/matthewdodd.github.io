




1798. 



JOHN VENNELL
 and 



EDWARD WOODGATE



, stealing 5 pairs of gloves, value 6s.; the goods of 



Richard Smith; Vennell having been before convicted; to which


VENNELL pleaded 


GUILTY
.* Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years.


WOODGATE pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 11.—



Confined Ten Days.
