



2810. 




HENRY SAMUEL WILLIAMS
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
16th of Sept., 1 pair of boots, value 4s. 6d.; the goods of 



Joseph Allworth Pash, his master.






JOSEPH ALLWORTH PASH
. The prisoner was in my service—on the 16th of Sept., he left my shop, and I missed a pair of boots—I followed him, and gave him into custody—the policeman found one boot in each of his pockets—these are them—they are mine.

Prisoner. I was in want of money; I thought I would take a pair and sell them.




GUILTY
. Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
