





1384. 
               
                  CHARLES 
                  DENNIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of June
, 1 watch, value 1l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Catherine 
                  Markham
               




            , widow
.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 45.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
