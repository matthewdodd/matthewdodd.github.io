





1089. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  FORSTER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of July
, a watch, value 2l. a watch-chain, value 3l. three seals, value 7l. and a watch-key, value 10l. the property of 
                     
                        
                           Charles 
                           Simpson
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
MR. 
               
                  CHARLES 
                  SIMPSON
               



            . On the 26th of July last, about ten o'clock at night, I was going home by the gates of the Artillery Ground


, and I saw the prisoner coming towards me. On his coming up to me, he snatched my watch, and ran off; I felt it go. He ran towards Finsbury-square, and I ran after him, crying stop thief. When I had ran after him about thirty or forty yards. I was thrown down by somebody in the middle of the road; I got up again, and when I got into Finsbury-square, I found he was stopped.



               GUILTY
            , aged 25.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
