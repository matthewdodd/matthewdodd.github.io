





597. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     LYNCH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of February
, a coat, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Maria 
                     Shephard
                  




               
            , widow
.


                  MARIA 
                  SHEPHARD
               



            . I live in Bowyer's-buildings, Commercial-road. On the 14th of February, my son was at work at a coal-shed - I find him in clothes.


                  JOSEPH 
                  SHEPHARD
               



            . I live with my mother, and work for Mr. Gordon. On the 14th of February, I hung my coat up into the shed, and missed it. I met the prisoner on the Wednesday following, in the Commercial-road, with it on his back, wearing it; I claimed it; he said it was not mine, and he would knock me down - that he had bought it in Rosemary-lane, for 2 s. 6 d.


                  FRANCIS 
                  JACKSON
               



            . I took the coat off the prisoner's back - he told me he had bought it for 1 s. 8 d.
Prisoner's Defence. I bought it in Rosemary-lane.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
