






882. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     STEWART
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of September
 last, six silk handkerchiefs, value 30 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Daniel 
                     Golding
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Golding
                  



               
            .
The prosecutor took the prisoner with the handkerchiefs in his hand.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
