







                  Abraham 
                  Smith 
               



             of St. Michael's Wood-street


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Coat
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Piles 
                  




                
            , the 2d of this Instant April
. It appeared by the Evidence, that the Cooper
 having laid down his Coat in the Entry of the Three Tun-Tavern, at Wood street


, the Prisoner was seen to bring out the Coat, and give it to another Man, who carried it off. But the Prisoner being pursued, was apprehended. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
