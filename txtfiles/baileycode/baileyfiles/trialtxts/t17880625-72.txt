





474. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     JONES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 25th of June
, a silk handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Neale
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN
                   NEALE
               



             sworn.
On Wednesday the 25th of June, I was going through George lane


, which leads into Botolph-lane, I felt something at my coat-pocket, I turned round and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand, he threw it down and ran away; I took it up, and a person in the lane stopped him, I charged a constable with him, and took him before an alderman.
Was he ever out of your sight? - Never.
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
I was coming down the lane, this gentleman laid hold of me, and said I had took his handkerchief, I know nothing of it.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
