



2274. 




RICHARD MAYBURY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
20th of July, 1 shirt, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 



William Taylor; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






WILLIAM JAMES
. I am shopman to Mr. William Taylor, a hosier in Holborn. On the 20th of July, about nine o'clock in the evening, I saw the prisoner come and take this shirt from the window—he was going to walk away—I stopped him and took it from him—he had another boy with him.





JOHN BOYCE
. I am an officer. I took the prisoner and have the shirt.





BENJAMIN CADELL
. I am an officer. I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the boy.




GUILTY
. Aged 8.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Convict Ship.
