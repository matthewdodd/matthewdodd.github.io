Dean




               
             of St. Gregory by St. Paul


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Cotton Handkerchief value 6 d. from 
                     
                        
                           Henry 
                           Brookhouse 
                        




 , the 13th of March
 last. As the Prosecutor was going along St. Pauls Church Yard


 the Prisoner pickt his pocket, for was seen by another Boy, who immediately told the Prosecutor of it; whereupon he seiz'd him, and found his Hanckerchief between his Legs. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 6 d.
