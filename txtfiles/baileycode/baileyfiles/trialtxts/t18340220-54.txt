





NEW COURT. Friday, February 21, 1834.
Fifth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
373. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     CRESSWELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 27th of January
, 6 live tame rabbits, value 5s.; and 2 live tame pigeons, value 2s.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Cresswell
                  




               
            , to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
