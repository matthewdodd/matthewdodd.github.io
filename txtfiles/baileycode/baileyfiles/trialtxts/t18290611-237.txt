





1236. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     GLENNON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of May
, 1 pair of of breeches, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Martha 
                     Jones
                  




               
            , widow
.


                  MARTHA 
                  BANCE COLLETT
               



            . I went and got these breeches from the prisoner, on Thursday the 7th of May, at half-past nine o'clock in the morning: they were under a short jacket which he wore - I had heard of Mrs. Jones' loss.


                  SAMUEL 
                  CHERRY
               



            . I am in the employ of Mr. Sexton, of Brentford; we live opposite to Mrs. Jones, who keeps a clothes-shop - I saw the prisoner take these breeches off her window, put them under his jacket, and go off.


                  JOHN 
                  JONES
               



            . I am the son of 
               
                  Martha 
                  Jones
               



            ; she is a widow
. I placed these breeches on her window: they are her property.
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
