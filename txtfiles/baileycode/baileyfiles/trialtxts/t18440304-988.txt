



988. 




THOMAS MOONEY
 was indicted

 for stealing 1 carpet bag, value 8s., the goods of 



Richard Baker; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






RICHARD BAKER
. This carpet bag hung at my door at half-past five o'clock on the 29th of Feb.—I missed it—this is it—I am sure it is mine.





AMBROSE SHILVEY
 (police-constable D 214.) I Stopped the prisoner with this bag, in Gee's-court, about half-past seven o'clock, on the 29th of Feb., where he offered it for sale.





EDWARD CALLAHAN
 (police-constable D 134.) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
.* Aged 19.—


Transported for seven years.
