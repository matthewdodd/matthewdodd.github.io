







                     John 
                     Everett
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Ethelburgh


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 50 lb. of Thread, value 50 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Harrison
                  




               
            , the 6th of September
last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That the Prisoner was his Journeyman
, and being inform'd that the Prisoner had been seen to put a Parcel of Thread in his Pocket, he being search'd, the Thread was found upon him; that upon Examination, the Prisoner had own'd his taking the Thread from his Master several times, but this the Prisoner deny'd at the Bar; but the small Parcel that was found in his Pocket being a full proof of that Fact, the Jury found him 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
