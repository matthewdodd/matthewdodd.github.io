





643. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     LLOYD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of March
, one glass decanter, value 2 s. 6 d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Gibbs
                  




               
            .


                  ANN 
                  GIBBS
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  William 
                  Gibbs
               



            , who is a broker
, and lives in Bream's-buildings, Chancery-lane


. On the 6th of March, I saw the prisoner take this decanter from the shopboard; I followed him. He ran off, and set it down - he was secured.


                  PHILIP 
                  HUDULL
               



            . I am a printer, and live at Walworth. I saw the prisoner take the decanter, and followed him; he put it down, and ran into a house. I secured him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
             Aged 30.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
