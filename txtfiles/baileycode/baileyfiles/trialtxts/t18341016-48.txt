





1523. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PEACOCK
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of September
, 1 waistcoat, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Francis 
                     Cotton
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  PADDEN
               



            . I am foreman to Mr. 
               
                  Francis 
                  Cotton
               



            , a pawnbroker
, in Shoreditch


. On the 13th of September, a waistcoat was stolen - it had hung at the door- I received information, and missed it - I found it at another pawnbroker's.



                  WILLIAM 
                  CLUFF
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live at No. 6, Church-street, Bethnal-green. The prisoner pawned this waistcoat for 2s. for his brother.


                  JOHN 
                  SMITH
               



            . I was opposite the prosecutor's on the 13th of September, and saw the prisoner and another boy
 run away together - the other boy had something in his apron.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            .* Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
