





266. (M.) 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Clark 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing 30 pounds weight of pork called bacon, value 15 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Lacey 
                  




                
            , April 29
.


                  John 
                  Lacey 
               



            . I live at the corner of Wych-street


, I am a cheesemonger and chandler
; last Wednesday evening a piece of bacon of about 30 pounds weight, was taken from my shop door; I pursued the prisoner and he was taken with it under his arm, under the gateway in Lion's inn.


                  John 
                  Crab 
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the prosecutor's bacon; I acquainted him with he and I pursued, and we took him with it under Lion's-inn gateway.
Prisoner's defence.
I saw something lying in the gateway; I did not mind what it was, they said it was a piece of bacon, and they laid hold of me; I am a baker
 by trade.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
