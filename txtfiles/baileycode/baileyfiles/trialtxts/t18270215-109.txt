





602. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  BROWN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of January
, 1 saw, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Peter 
                  Pige
               




            .
PETER PIGE. I live at Bethnal-green


, and am a pawnbroker
. I saw the prisoner come and take this saw from a board in front of my window; I pursued, and took him with it, about five yards off.


                  PHILIP 
                  PARISH
               



            . I am an officer, and took him.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I kicked it before me, picked it up, and put it by the window; the gentleman came and took me.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
