





418. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Nichols 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing one silk handkerchief, val. 4 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           Dennis 
                        




                      
                  , privately from his person
            , July 4
. *.


                  George 
                  Dennis 
               



            . On the 4th of July, about two o'clock in the day-time, as I was coming by the corner of Water-lane, out of Thames street


, I perceiv'd something touch my pocket, I felt and miss'd my handkerchief, I turn'd round and took hold of the prisoner, and found my handkerchief in his bosom.
Prisoner's Defence.
As I was coming up Water-lane, I saw three boys come rusling along, going farther I found a handkerchief and picked it up, and this man came and said it was his; I said no, not as you know of.




               Guilty 10 d
            .
