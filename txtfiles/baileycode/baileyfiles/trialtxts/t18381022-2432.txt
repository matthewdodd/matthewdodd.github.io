



2432. 




CHARLES STUBBS
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
22nd of October, 2 saws, value 7s., the goods of 



John Marsh.






JOHN MARSH
. I live in Denmark-street, St. George's in the East, and am a carpenter. On the 22nd of October while I went to breakfast, I lost two saws from a house at Darling-place, Hackney, where I was at work,—these are my saws—(looking at them.)





ROBERT BUTCHER
 (police-constable N 225.) I took the prisoner in the act of pawning these saws, about nine o'clock the same morning.





WILLIAM HOWS
. I am the pawnbroker. The prisoner was is my house, and had the saws, but had not offered to pawn them.




GUILTY
. † Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
