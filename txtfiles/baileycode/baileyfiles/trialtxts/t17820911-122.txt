





606. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     SANDERS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 3d of July
, one linen shirt, value 4 s. one linen shift, value 3 s. one muslin handkerchief, value 3 d. one cloth handkerchief, value 3 d. one hat, value 6 d. one linen slip, value 1 s. one pair of stockings, value 6 d. two pair of cotton stockings, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Solomon 
                     Aaron
                  




               
            .


                  SOLOMON
                   AARON
               



             sworn.
The prisoner lived with me but one day; the things in the indictment were taken out of a trunk; we found the prisoner in Rosemary-Lane, she had some of the things on


                  ELEANOR
                   AARON
               



             sworn.
Confirmed the same.
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
They said they would not hurt me.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported to America for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury, before Mr. RECORDER.
