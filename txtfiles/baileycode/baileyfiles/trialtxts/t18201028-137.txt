






1364. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JAMES
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     JONES
                  




               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of October
, one handkerchief, value 5 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Harrold
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  HARROLD
               



            . On the 12th of October I was walking along Coventry-street


, Wright called out that my pocket had been picked, he had Jones in custody; I missed my handkerchief. A stranger brought James up with the handkerchief.


                  JOHN 
                  WRIGHT
               



            . I was in Coventry-street, and saw Jones put his hand into Harrold's pocket, take out a silk handkerchief, and pass it to James, who ran down the street. I seized Jones, and another person brought James up with it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
JAMES - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 13.
JONES - 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.





               Transported for Life
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
