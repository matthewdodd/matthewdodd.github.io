








336. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SPENCER
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of June

 last, six linen shifts bodies, value 4 s. four aprons, value 8 s. a velveret waistcoat, value 2 s. and a pair of velveret breeches, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Thomas
                  



               
            .
And 
               
                  
                     LETITIA 
                     STEVENS
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     MASON
                  




               
             were indicted for 


               receiving the said goods, knowing them to be stolen
            .


                  WILLIAM
                   THOMAS
               



             sworn.
I am a broker
 in Maiden-lane; the prisoner Spencer worked for me a month: I lost the things; they were taken from a closet.
The different pawnbrokers produced the things pawned by the other prisoners.
(The things deposed to.)


                  JOHN
                   SPENCER
               



            , 

               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .


                  LETITIA
                   STEVENS
               



            , 
               
                  SARAH
                   MASON
               



            , (Aged 16.)



               GUILTY
            .





               Transported for fourteen years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice BULLER.
