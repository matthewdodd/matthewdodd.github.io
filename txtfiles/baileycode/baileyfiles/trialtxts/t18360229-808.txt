



808. 




GEORGE MARSHALL
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
24th of February, 1 pair of half boots, value 4s.6d., the goods of 



Zaccheus Hampton.






ANN HAMPTON
. I am the daughter of Zaccheus Hampton, a shop-maker, and live in Bunbill-row. On Wednesday, the 24th of February I was in the parlour, which was a glass door—I could see into the shop—I saw the prisoner take the half boots off the nail outside the window had run away with them—I told my father, who was in the passage, and he fetched him back with them.





ZACCHEUS HAMPTON
. My daughter informed me of this—I pursed the prisoner, and caught him a court—I took him to the station-house—I saw him drop the boots when I was within two yards of him—they hung out of the reach of the prisoner—I have repeatedly sees him watching about there—I made the boots—they are mine.





JAMES HAYWARD
. I am a policeman. I took him in charge.




GUILTY
. Aged 14.—


Transported for Seven Years.
