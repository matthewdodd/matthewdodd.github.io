





495. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     SAUL
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th day of May
, fourteen pair of mens leather shoes, value 28 s. three pair of women's leather shoes, value 4 s. 6 d. seven pair of children's leather shoes, value 7 s. and part of a linen sheet, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Schewell
                  



               
            .
A second count, stating them to be the property of 
               
                  
                     Giles 
                     East
                  



               
             and others.
A third count, for stealing the goods as the property of the 
               
parish of St. Luke



            .
A fourth count, charging them to be the property of persons unknown.


                  WILLIAM
                   HEWITSON
               



             sworn.
I stopped the prisoner with the shoes upon him.
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
A man hired me to carry them.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
