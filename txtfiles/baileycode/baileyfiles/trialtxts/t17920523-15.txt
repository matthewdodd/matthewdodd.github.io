






234. 
               
                  
                     ALEXANDER 
                     MASON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d day of May
, a leather pocket book value 2 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Meene
                  




               
            , Esq
.


                  ROBERT
                   MEENE
               



             sworn.
I lost my pocket book on the 23d of May last in the King's Mews


; it may be of the value of 2 s. or less; I heard somebody call out, and I turned short round and saw the prisoner in custody, and another person gave me my pocket book.


                  JAMES
                   TRING
               



            , sworn.
I am a seaman; I was in the King's Mews and saw a lad take a pocket book out of this gentleman's coat pocket; he was taken into custody; Capt
. Meene has the pocket book.
PRISONER's DEFENCE.
I picked it up.




               GUILTY, Stealing 1 s
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
