



1198. 




MICHAEL BURKE
 was indicted

 for stealing 1 trowel, value 1s.6l., the goods of 



Charles Cummings: 1 other trowel, value 1s. 6d., the goods of 



Richard Patterson; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






CHARLES CUMMINGS
. I was employed at a house in the Vauxhall-bridge-road. I missed two trowels—this one is mine, and the other Richard Patterson's.





EDWARD HICKSTON
 (police-constable B 192.) At five o'clock in the morning of the 8th of March, I saw the prisoner come from an unfinished building, and put down these two trowels on the pavement—I took him.


Prisoner. I had not taken them out. Witness. I took him just outside the same premises.





DENNIS DEVINE
 (Police-constable B 61.) 1 produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 32.—


Transported for Seven Years.
