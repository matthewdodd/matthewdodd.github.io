



568. 




CHARLES PERRIN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
22nd of Dec., 1 saw, value 2s.; 1 microscope, 2s.; and 2 printed books, 1s.:—also, on the 
24th of Dec., 1 crow-bar, value 6d., the goods of 



Joseph Holton: to both which he pleaded






GUILTY
. Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years—Convict Ship.
