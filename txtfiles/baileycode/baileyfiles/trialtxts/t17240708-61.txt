







                     Ann 
                     Wilson 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Mary Whitechapel


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Pair of Breeches
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Hancock 
                  




                
            , the 7th of this present July
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner and a Man came to the Shop under pretence of buying Clothes, and the Breeches were stolen out of the Shop, and found upon the Prisoner. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
