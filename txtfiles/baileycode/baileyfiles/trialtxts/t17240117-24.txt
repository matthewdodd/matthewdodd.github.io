







                     Morrice 
                     Thomas 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft


, was indicted for 


               feloniously taking the Money of 
                     
                        
                           Sarah 
                           Adams 
                        




, the 15th of January
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner being a hired Servant
 to the Prosecutor, us'd at Times to take Money which had been taken in the Shop, out of the Box, and convert it to his own Life. The Fact was plainly prov'd, and the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
