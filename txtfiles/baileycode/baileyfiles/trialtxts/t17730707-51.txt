





465. (M.) 
               
                  
                     THOMAS
                      MILLER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a pair of worsted breeches, value 10 s. 6 d. and a stuff gown, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Jasper
                      Notley
                  




               
            , June 20
. ++


                  Jasper
                   Notley
               



            . I am a pawnbroker
: I found these things wanting Thursday was week out of my warehouse; how long before they had been taken I cannot say; I found them in the custody of Mr. Kello.
- Kello. I am the constable of the night: these things were brought to the watch-house by one Blower.
- Blower. I brought these things to the watch-house; I took them on the prisoner.


                  Thomas
                   Mills
               



            , another watchman, confirmed this evidence, and that they were taken upon the prisoner bundled up.
Prisoner's Defence.
I found these things in the street.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
