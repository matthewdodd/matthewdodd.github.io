





45. (L.) 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM
                      BOOTH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value 6 d. a linen shirt, value 3 s. and a muslin neckcloth, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James
                      Wickstead
                  




               
            , Nov. 24
. ++


                  James
                   Wickstead
               



            . As I was going up Holborn hill


, on the 24th of November, about seven in the evening, a gentleman informed me my pocket had been picked by the prisoner, who was then near me; I put my hand in my pocket and missed the things mentioned in the indictment: I immediately seized the prisoner, upon which he dropt my shirt.


                  Ralph
                   Clowes
               



            . I saw the prisoner take a red and white handkerchief out of the prosecutor's pocket; I informed the prosecutor of it, and secured the prisoner.
( The things mentioned in the indictment produced and deposed to by the prosecutor.)
Prisoner's Defence.
I never had the handkerchief.
He called two witnesses, who gave him a good character.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
