





OLD COURT, Friday, July 4, 1834.
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
935. 
               
                  MARY 
                  CLANCEY
               



             was indicted for 


               feloniously uttering, to 
                     
                        Samuel 
                        Towler
                     




                  , a counterfeit shilling, knowing the same to be counterfeit, she having previously been convicted as a common utterer of counterfeit coin
             - to which she 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
