






475. 
               
                  
                     ISAAC 
                     COLLINS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 27th of May
, one cloth coat, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Pearce
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the coat upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
