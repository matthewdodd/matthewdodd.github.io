





239. 
               
                  CATHERINE 
                  DOYLE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of December
, 5 lbs. weight of pork, value 2s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Edward 
                  Leman
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  JAMES
               



            . On the 8th of December, about half-past eleven o'clock at night, I saw the prisoner near the shop of Mr. Leman, a cheesemonger
, of Whitecross-street


 - I watched and saw her go up, as if to make a purchase; I saw her go away, then go up again, and take a piece of pork off the board.


                  EDWARD 
                  LEMAN
               



            . I keep this shop. James alarmed me; I sent for an officer, and the prisoner was taken with it - I have seen her about the street for some time.


                  SAMUEL 
                  YATES
               



            . I took her in charge.
The prisoner pleaded distress.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
