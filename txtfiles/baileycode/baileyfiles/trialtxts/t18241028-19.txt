





NEW COURT. (1st Day.)
Middlesex Cases, Third Jury, Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1272. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     CHAPMAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of October
, a pair of trowsers, value 25 s.; a waistcoat, value 20 s., and two handkerchiefs, value 5 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Morgan
                  




               
            .
The prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
