



1576. 




THOMAS WORKMAN
was indicted

for stealing, on the 
12th of November, 6 spoons, value 3l.:—also, on the 
17th of December, 4 forks, value 4l.; 6 spoons, value 2l.; 1 pair of breeches, value 30s.; and 1 umbrella, value 7s.:—also, on the 
11th of December, 18 spoons, value 10l.; and 6 forks, value 4l.; the goods of 



John Allnutt the younger, his master; to which be pleaded





GUILTY.Aged 26.—


Transported for Fourteen Years
