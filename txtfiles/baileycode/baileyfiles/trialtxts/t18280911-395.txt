





1947. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  BENSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of August
, 1 time-piece, value 2l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Mayo
               




            .
CHARLES MAYO. I am a watch-maker
, and live in Sidney-place, in the Commercial-road


. On the 7th of August, between seven and eight o'clock in the evening, I went out, and when I came back there was a mob of people round the door; this time-piece was produced, which I had seen that day.


                  WILLIAM 
                  THOMAS MOORE
               



            . I am an officer. I was sent for, and found the prisoner in custody; I took this time-piece.


                  THOMAS 
                  PUGH
               



            . I live next door to Mr. Mayo. On the evening of the 7th of August Mrs. Mayo came to me; I went into the street, and saw the prisoner running along with the time-piece; I went and took it from him - when he got to a corner he turned and walked deliberately down a street, with it under his left arm.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I throw myself on the mercy of the Court.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
