





875. 
               
                  
                     DAVID 
                     BASFORD
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of March
, 1lb. weight of tea, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Edward Daker 
                     Harley
                  




               
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  MICHAEL ROSE
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. E. D. Harley, who lives near Blenheim-steps, Marlborough-street


. The prisoner came and asked for an ounce of barley-sugar -I was busy; and as he was leaving the shop he was stopped with the tea, which I had put on the counter just before with some other parcels.


                  JAMES 
                  KENNERLEY
               



            . I am a music-copier. I saw the prisoner lurking about the shop - I saw him go and take the parcel off the counter; he put it between his legs and came out with it - I took him.


                  ANGELIOUS 
                  BETRAUN
               



            . I took the prisoner in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was turning to come away, and felt myself seized by the arm - this parcel fell on the floor, and the witness took it up.


                  JOHN 
                  MICHAEL ROSE
               



            . He was stopped just at the doorway.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
