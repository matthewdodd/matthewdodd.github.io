



155. 




EDWARD BEAUMONT SMITH
 was indicted

 for feloniously forging an Exchequer-bill of 1000l., with intent to defraud our 

Lady the Queen.—2nd COUNT, for uttering the same with like intent.—Other COUNTS, varing the manner of stating the charge; to which be pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 44.—


Transported for Life.
