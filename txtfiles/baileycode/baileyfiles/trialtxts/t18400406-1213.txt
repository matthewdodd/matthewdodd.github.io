



1213. 




EDWARD ROBINSON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
31st of March, 1 coat, value 1l. 5s.; 1 pair of trowsers, value 1l.; 1 waistcoat, value 10s.; and 1 handkerchief, value 1s.; the goods of 



Samuel James Radden.






SAMUEL JAMES RADDEN
. I lodge in Reform-place, White Cross-street, and am a smith. The prisoner lived in the same house with me—these articles are mine—(looking at them)—I lost them on the 31st of March.





WILLIAM WOOLLAMS SOLOMAN
. I am shopman to Mr. Brown, a pawnbroker. I produce these articles which were pawned by a boy.





JAMES BRANNAN
 (police-sergeant G 20.) I went to the prisoner, and had a conversation with him, and he took me to the pawnbroker's where I found this property.




GUILTY
.* Aged 12—


Transported for Seven Years.—Isle of Wight.
