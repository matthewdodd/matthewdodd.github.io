





1765. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  ROACH
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        John Denny 
                        Clunn
                     




                  , from his person
            .
JOHN DENNY CLUNN. I was in Duke-street, Smithfield


, on the 14th of August, and was informed I had lost a handkerchief - the prisoner was pointed out; I secured him, but have not found the handkerchief; several persons were about.


                  WILLIAM 
                  GROMET
               



            . I was in Duke-street on the 14th of August, and saw the prisoner and two others; the prisoner took up the tail of Mr. Clunn's coat, took out his handkerchief, and gave it to one who appeared like a sailor; he came through a public-house into Bartholomew-close; I pursued, but could not catch him; I was on the other side of the way.


                  THOMAS 
                  BARKER
               



            . I am an officer. I was passing, and heard a scuffle - I turned, and saw a sailor going through the public-house - he got away; I returned, and took the prisoner.
Prisoner's Defence. I was going to see my mother at the Hospital, and was stopped.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
