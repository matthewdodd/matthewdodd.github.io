







                  Mary 
                  Hill , alias 
Hilliard




            , of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Sarah 
                           Randolph 
                        




, Widow
, the 4th of February
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, she had lost various Goods, and some were found where the Prisoner had pawn'd them, in Ratcliff-High-Way; this appear'd plain by the Evidence. The Prisoner pretended she found them in her Mother's Room. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 10d.
            



               Transportation
            .
