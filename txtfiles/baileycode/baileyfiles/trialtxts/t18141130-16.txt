





16. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  HATFIELD
               




             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 22nd of October
, unlawfully had in his custody and possession, a forged bank note for the payment of 2 l. he knowing it to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            , aged 29.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
