




1879. 



EDWARD WILLIAMS
 was indicted separately 

for embezzling and stealing a letter containing half a sovereign—also for embezzling and stealing a letter containing a sovereign, which came into his possession by virtue of his employment in the General Post-office—also for stealing three sovereigns, the monies of 



Roger Kindon; to which indictments he pleaded




GUILTY
.—


Transported for Life.
