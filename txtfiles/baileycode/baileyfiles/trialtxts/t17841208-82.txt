





90. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     WALES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing on the 21st of November
 last, one live pig, value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Wood
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was stopped by the watchman, with the pig in a sack, and carried to the watch-house, and the prosecutor came and owned it.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   HOTHAM
               



            .
