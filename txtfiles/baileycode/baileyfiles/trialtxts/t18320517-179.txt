





1305. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     CROSSLEY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of April
, 1 pair of shoes, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     George Henry 
                     Hinkins
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  HENRY HINKINS
               



            . I live in Golden-lane


, and deal in new and second-hand shoes
. On the 27th of April the Policeman came and asked me if I had not lost some shoes; I then missed a pair from the step of the door - I went with him, and saw them at the pawnbroker's.


                  THOMAS 
                  KIRKWOOD
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Brick-lane, Old-street. I took in these shoes from the prisoner on the evening of the 26th of April.


                  WILLIAM 
                  PURCELL
               



             (Police-constable C 194). I took the prisoner from information, and found the shoes.
Prisoner's Defence. I did not take them - it was another boy.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
