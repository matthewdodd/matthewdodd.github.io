





2080. 
               
                  
                     ELIZA 
                     JACKSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing on the 30th of August
, 1 half-crown, the money of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Gainsby
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  EDWARD 
                  GAINSBY
               



            . I am a seaman
 belonging to the Amphitrite collier. Between eleven and twelve o'clock at night on the 30th of August, I met the prisoner near the Old Rose, in Ratcliff-highway


; she took hold of me, began fumbling about me, whipped her hand into my pocket, and took half-a-crown out - she attempted to put it into her bosom, and dropped it.


                  THOMAS 
                  BAILEY SMITH
               



            . (Police-constable K 4). On the night of the 30th of August, I was standing on Chigwell-hill; I saw the prosecutor and the prisoner standing talking - I heard him say he had a great mind to give her into custody; I went up, and he gave charge of her.
Prisoner. I was going home, and he stopped meand said, might he go with me; I said I did not mind- he said he would give me half-a-crown to sleep with me; I said I would not for that - he then felt what more money he had, and dropped the half-crown; he gave the half-crown to the officer from his pocket.
THOMAS BAILEY SMITH. He gave it me from his hand; he was sober - she said he wanted to go home with her, and he denied that.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 21.* - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
