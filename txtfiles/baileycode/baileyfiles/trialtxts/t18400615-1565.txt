



1565. 




EDWARD SMITH
was indicted

for stealing, on the 
1st of June, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 



Francis Pierson, from his person.






FRANCIS PIERSON.
I live in Pitt's-place, Southwark. About twelve o'clock, on the 1st of June, I was in Smithfield, looking at the beasts, and my silk handkerchief was drawn out of my pocket—I saw the prisoner close to me, and he had it in his hand—this is it—(looking at it.)





THOMAS ISSITT.
I am a policeman. I received the handkerchief from the prosecutor.




GUILTY.*Aged 16.—


Transported for Ten Years
