



1912. 




JAMES TURVEY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
4th of July, 1 plane, value 9d.; the goods of 



Edward Greengrass: and 1 plane, value 1s., the goods of 



Bark Quelch.






THOMAS BUCK
 (police-constable T 32.) At a quarter past two o'clock on the morning of the 4th of July, I was on the terrace at Chiswick—I saw prisoner getting over the wall of the prosecutor's premises—I took him into custody—he had this plane—he gave no account of it at all—the other plane was on the wall.





EDWARD GREENGRASS
. This smoothing-plane is mine—I left it in a new building at Turnham-green-terrace—there was a lock on the gate, and the tools were in one of the rooms.




GUILTY
.* Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.—Convict Ship.
