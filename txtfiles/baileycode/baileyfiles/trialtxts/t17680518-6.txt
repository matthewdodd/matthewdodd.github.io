





341. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Cunningham 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that he, on the 15th of April
, about the hour of two in the night, five lilach plants, value 15 d. three privet plants, value 18 d. five honeysuckle plants, five vine plants, four jasmine plants, two box plants, one sweet-briar plant, the property of 
                     
                        
                           Crispin 
                           Hardy 
                        




                     
                  , in a certain nursery-ground, did pluck up, dig up, steal, take, and carry away
            . *


                  Crispin 
                  Hardy 
               



keeps a nursery ground
 on Milbank


; the plants were missing, and the prisoner, who had some time before worked there, was suspected; he was taken in Wyld street at his lodgings the same morning about nine o'clock, with all the plants upon him, except the two honeysuckles, one vine, and sweet-briar, which he had sold to 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Powe 
               



            , a neighbour, and he had set in his ground.
The prisoner in his defence said he would give the court to trouble about it, and owned he was guilty of taking them.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
