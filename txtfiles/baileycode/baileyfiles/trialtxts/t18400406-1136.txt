



1136. 




JOHN GAVIGAN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
4th of April, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 



Henry Sylvester, from his person.






HENRY SYLVESTER
. I am a clerk in Somerset-house. On Saturday night, the 4th of April, I was in Fleet-street, about ten o'clock—I had a handkerchief in my pocket—I felt a tug—I turned, and saw the prisoner putting my handkerchief Into his breast pocket—I gave him into custody—this is my handkerchief—(looking at it.)

Prisoner's Defence. It laid down; I picked it up, and gave it to him when he turned round, and he gave me in charge.




GUILTY
.** Aged 13.—


Transported for Ten Years.—Convict Ship.
