





380. 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     BROWN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of December
, 1 tea-kettle, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Barnard 
                     Riley
                  




               
            .
CHRISTIAN MARIA SPEED. I live opposite Mr. Riley, in Orchard-street


. On the 12th of December I saw the prisoner take a kettle from his house, and gave the alarm; I am sure she is the person.


                  BARNARD 
                  CLARK
               



            . This witness gave the alarm; I followed, and took the prisoner with this kettle.
BARNARD RILEY. This is my kettle, and was in my back yard - the passage door is sometimes open; I know nothing of the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 39. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
