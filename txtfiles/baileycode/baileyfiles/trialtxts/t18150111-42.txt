





163. 
               
                  
                     HANNAH
                     JOHNSON
                  




               
             was indicted for that



               she, on the 14th of December
, feloniously and without lawful excuse, had in her custody and possession a certain forged 1 l. note
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            , aged 29.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                  Wood
