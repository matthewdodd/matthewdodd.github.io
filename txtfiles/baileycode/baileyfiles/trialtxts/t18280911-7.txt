





First London Jury - before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1565. 
               
                  RICHARD 
                  DUDLEY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 7s., the goods of 
                     
                        Edward 
                        Burmester
                     




                  , from his person
            .
MR. EDWARD BURMESTER. I live in St. Helen's-place. On the 13th of August, at four o'clock in the afternoon, I was coming from America-square


 towards Crutchedfriars


 with a gentlemen, and telling him of having lost a handkerchief a week before; I felt a touch at my pocket and said "I have lost another" - I turned round and saw the prisoner throw it from his hand - he was a very few paces off; I charged him with it instantly, and secured him; he tried to run away - I saw it fall from his hand.


                  THOMAS 
                  DEVEY
               



            . I am an officer, and took the prisoner in charge with the handkerchief.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was about one hundred yards from the gentlemen - it rained; a gentlemen took hold of me and took me into a shop; this gentleman came up and said, "My lad, you avnd another one have taken my handkerchief."



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
