





44. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WILSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of November
, 1 dead fowl, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert Benjamin 
                     Greenwood
                  




               
            ; and that he had been before convicted of felony.


                  GEORGE 
                  ROOKER
               



            . I am a butcher. On the 1st of November I saw the prisoner go to the shop of Mr. Greenwood, and come away with a fowl; he ran, and I pursued - he threw the fowl forward, as if to throw it down an area; I took him, and took the fowl back.


                  ROBERT 
                  BENJAMIN GREENWOOD
               



            . I keep a poulterer's shop
. The fowl was mine - I missed it, but know nothing of the prisoner.


                  JOHN 
                  SHEEHY
               



            . I am a Police-officer. I have a certificate of the conviction of the prisoner, by the name of 
               
                  John 
                  Blackmore
               



             - I took him, and know he is the person.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
