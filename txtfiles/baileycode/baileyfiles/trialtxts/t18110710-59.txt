





577. 
               
                  
                     DIANA 
                     CROW
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 2d of July
, a gown, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Potts
                  




               
            .


                  JANE 
                  POTTS
               



            . My husbands name is 
               
                  John 
                  Potts
               



            , I live in Union Row, Kent Row


, I hired the prisoner as a servant
, she lived with me only a week, I lent her a great many clothes that she took away, and the gown she stole out of my daughter's room.
MR. FIRTH. I am an headborough, on the 2d of July, I apprehended the prisoner in White Lion Street, Norton Falgate, I searched her, in her pocket I found this duplicate.


                  JAMES 
                  DEMER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. On the 2d of July, the prisoner pawned this gown with me.
Prosecutrix. This is my daughter's gown.
The prisoner said nothing in her defence, nor called any witnesses to character.



               GUILTY
            , aged 29.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
