





Second London Jury, before Mr. Sergeant 
               Arabin
            .
1280. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     TAYLOR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of June
, 34 yards of crape de Lyons, value 3l. 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Southgate
               




            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 42. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
