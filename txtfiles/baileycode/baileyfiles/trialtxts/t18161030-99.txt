





1198. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MORTIMER
                  




               
             was 


               indicted for embezzlement
            .


                     SAMUEL 
                     KEENE
                  




               
            . I am a butcher
, residing at Hounslow


. The prisoner was in my service for about three years, and in the month of October last.



                  SYBELLA 
                  SPARK
               



            . I am a married woman. I reside at Hounslow. I dealt with Mr. Keene, about September last I paid the prisoner on his account, two pounds five shillings and six-pence.


                  SAMUEL 
                  KEENE
               



            . The prisoner never accounted to me for the money.



               GUILTY
            , aged 34.




               Transported for seven years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
