




2613. 




RICHARD RUSSELL
 and 



MARY ANN HAWKINS
 were indicted for

 feloniously, and without lawful excuse, having in their custody and possession a mould, on which was impressed the obverse and reverse sides of a shilling; to which


RUSSELL pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 28.—


Transported for Ten Years. No evidence was offered against

HAWKINS—


NOT GUILTY
.

Before Mr. Justice Creswell.
