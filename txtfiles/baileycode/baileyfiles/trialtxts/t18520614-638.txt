




638. 




WILLIAM TOWNSEND
 and 




GEORGE SMITH



, burglariously breaking and entering the dwelling house of William Farrow, and stealing three coats, and other articles: to which


TOWNSEND pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Seven Years.


SMITH pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 22.—



Confined Twelve Months.
