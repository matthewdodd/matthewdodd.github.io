





493. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     RAY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing on the 27th of February
 last, one linen handkerchief, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Onslow
                  




               
            , Esq.





               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
