







                     William 
                     Giles 
                  



               
            , of St. James's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Shop-Book, value 2 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Ealand 
                  




                
            , on the 2d of June
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that while he was shutting up his Shop, he saw the Prisoner come in and take the Book, that there was another stood at his Door and they ran away together; that he followed them and kept sight of the Prisoner till he took him; that about 3 Days after the Book was brought again, and dropt in his Shop. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
            .
