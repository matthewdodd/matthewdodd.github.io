





539. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     BEAZER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th day of December
 last, one earthen-ware mug, called an ewer, value 12 d. an earthen-ware bason, value 12 d. a tea pot, value 12 d. a quart mug, value 12 d. a basket value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Hollings
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Sutton
                  



               
            .
The things were found in the prisoner's apartments concealed in a closet.
(The things deposed to.)

Prisoner. My wife left the door open, and somebody put the basket in.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
