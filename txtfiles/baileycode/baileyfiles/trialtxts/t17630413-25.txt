





175. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Bamham 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one cloath coat, val. 10 s. two waistcoats, one pair of shoes, two shirts, a pair of stockings, a copper quart mug, and a gold ring
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Holland 
                  




                
            , March 30
. +


                  Sarah 
                  Holland 
               



             deposed, her husband was in the Poultry-compter, that the prisoner lodged in her house, she missed the things mentioned, suspected the prisoner, she found a pair of stockings in the prisoner's box, and he owned he had given the gold ring to a person to whom he was going to be married, where she found it. (Produced and deposed to.)


                  Matthias 
                  Cane 
               



            , the constable, confirmed that of the prisoner's confession, and the finding the ring accordingly.



               Guilty
            



               T
            .
