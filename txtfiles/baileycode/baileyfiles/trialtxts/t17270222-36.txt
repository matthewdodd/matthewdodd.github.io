







                     William 
                     Minton 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Michael Cornhill


, was indicted for 


               stealing one Tortoiseshell Snuff-Box, value 10 s.
             on the 14th of January
 last, the Goods of 
               
Mr.
 Vernon


            . It appear'd that the Prisoner had lodged in the House of the Prosecutor, and living an idle Life, was suspected; and by the Information of one Hastings, the Box was found at a Place where the Prisoner had pawn'd it. Which Fact appearing plain, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
            .
