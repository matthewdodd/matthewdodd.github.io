







                  Emanuel 
                  Hamilton
               



             of St. Buttolphs Aldgate


, was Indicted for 


               stealing from 
                     
                        Ralph 
                        Hutchinson
                     




                  , on the 12 of March
, 5 Papers of Stockings, value 4 l. It appeared the Prosecutor being abroad, the Prisoner and some others took the Advantage when there was none in his Shop but a Girl, to enter it; and pretending to buy some Worsted, two of them stood before the Girl while the rest took the Stockings, and escaped
            . Having little to say in his Defence, he was found 

               Guilty
            .
