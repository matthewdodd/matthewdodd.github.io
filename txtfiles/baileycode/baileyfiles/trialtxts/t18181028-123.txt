





1514. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     DAVIES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of September
, one shawl, value 16s.
            , the property of 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Haines
               




            .
JOSEPH HAINES. I am silk-mercer
, and live at Ratcliff-highway


. On the 19th of September I saw the prisoner come into my shop and take a shawl. I called out, he ran off with it - I pursued and took him with it. I never lost sight of him.
ELIZA HART. I was in the shop, and saw the prisoner take the shawl.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
