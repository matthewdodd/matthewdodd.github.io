






1103. 




GENICEO MACHIN



, feloniously forging and uttering a request for the delivery of two printed books, with intent to defraud 



William Bovill:


—also, embezzling 11l., which he had received on account of 



Thomas Birch, his master:


—also, stealing 2 books, value 1l.; the goods of 



William Bovill:


—also, obtaining 1 sovereign; the goods of 



Thomas Robert Skidmore, by false pretences: to which he pleaded




GUILTY.* Aged 21.—


Transported for Ten Years
