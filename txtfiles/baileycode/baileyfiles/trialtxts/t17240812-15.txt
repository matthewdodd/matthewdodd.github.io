







                     Elizabeth 
                     Wakelin 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Martin in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Goods, in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           Ebenezer 
                           Mitchel 
                        




, the 5th of July
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, The Prisoner was his Servant
, and on Sunday, he being out of Town, and no Body at Home but her self, she went away, and carried away the Goods, Part of which were found upon her about a Fortnight after in Shadwell: That she own'd the Commission of the Fact, and said she was sorry for what she had done, and it was the first Fact she had been guilty of. The Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
