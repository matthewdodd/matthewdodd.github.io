





1457. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     STOKES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of May
, 1 wooden door, value 2s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Benjamin Francis 
                     Barfoot
                  




               
            .


                  JEMIMA 
                  ELLIOTT
               



            . I know Mr. Barfoot's premises in the North-road


. On the 30th of May, at nine o'clock at night, I saw the prisoner and two more boy
s with him - the prisoner went down Mr. Barfoot's yard, and brought out this door; I went and told the prosecutor, and when he came the prisoner ran off, but he was taken the next day, when he came to work.


                  BENJAMIN 
                  FRANCIS BARFOOT
               



            . I am a carpenter. This door is mine - I went out, and found it on the pavement - the prisoner was then gone - he had been three weeks in my employ, as a porter.
Prisoner's Defence. I fastened up his gate, and went home - I saw no door.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
