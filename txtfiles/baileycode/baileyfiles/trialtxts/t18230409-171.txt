





665. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     REAS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of March
, a handkerchief, value 4 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Robert 
                           Wroots
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  ROBERT 
                  WROOTS
               



            . On the 30th of March, about a quarter before eleven o'clock, I was in Regent-street


. I felt my handkerchief go from my pocket, and turning round I saw the prisoner with it in his hand. I pursued and took it from him, and gave it to the watchman.


                  JOHN 
                  FOLEY
               



            . I am the watchman. I was in Regent-street,and heard the cry of
"Watch;" I took the prisoner from the prosecutor's hands with the handkerchief.
(Property produced and sworn to).


                  ROBERT 
                  CHAPMAN
               



            . I searched him and found the handkerchief on him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
