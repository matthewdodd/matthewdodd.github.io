



976. 




JOHN TEMPLE
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
5th of April, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 



Frederick Bowman, from his person.






FREDERICK BOWMAN
. I live in Great Alie-street, Goodman's-fields. I was in Leadenhall-street, on the 5th of April—I felt a had behind me—some one tapped me on the shoulder—my handkerchief was handed to me almost immediately, and Mr. Herbert had hold of the prisoner's collar—it was the handkerchief I had in my pocket just before—this is it.





THOMAS HERBERT
. I was in Leadenhall-street, and saw the prisoner

put his hand to the prosecutor's pocket, take the handkerchief out, and put it into his coat—I seized him when he prosecutor seized him—he threw the handkerchief behind me.

Prisoner. It is false—my hand was down by my side. witness. I was in my employer's shop at the time they passed, and I saw him take it out of the gentleman's pocket—I seized him before he threw it from him—he then threw it down—my shopmate took it up, and gave it to the gentlemen.




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.
