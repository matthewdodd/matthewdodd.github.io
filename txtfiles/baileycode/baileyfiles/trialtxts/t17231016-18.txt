







                     John 
                     Jones 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Leonard Shoreditch


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Pair of Shoes, value 8 d.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Benjamin 
                     Holloway 
                  




                
            , the 20th of September
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Shoes were taken by the Prisoner out of the Stall of the Prosecutor. The Prisoner pleaded, he put the Shoes on to wear an Hour or two, with a Design to return them again, working in the Stall with the Prosecutor. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
