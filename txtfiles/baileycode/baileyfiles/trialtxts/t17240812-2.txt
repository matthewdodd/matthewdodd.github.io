







                     John 
                     Grimes 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Peter Cornhill


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Handkerchief, from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Newnham 
                        




, the 30th of July
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that as the Prosecutor was going thro' Peter's Alley in Cornhil


, there was the Prisoner and another Person, who took the Handkerchief out of his Pocket, and the Prisoner receiv'd it, and upon an outcry threw it down on the Ground, but was apprehended. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
