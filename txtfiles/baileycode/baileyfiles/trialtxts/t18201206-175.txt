





178. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     CLUTTERBUCK
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealling, on the 20th of November
, six yards of cotton, value 6 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     John Kinder 
                     Cheese
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  KINDER CHEESE
               



            . I am a pawnbroker
. On Monday evening, the 20th of November, I was in my shop, saw the prisoner come within my door, and take some cotton which hung up for sale; I followed him, overtook him six yards off with it, and gave him in charge.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
