





1378. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WILSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of July
, three sheets, value 15 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Botcherley
                  




               
            .


                  DAVID 
                  BOTCHERLEY
               



            . I keep the Sol's Arms
, Wych-street


. On the 23d of July I heard a noise up stairs, ran up, and found the prisoner concealed behind a folding skreen, with three sheets - he jumped out of the window into the street. I called for assistance, ran out, and secured him; he lodged with me. He dropped a key in the bar which would open all the drawers in my room.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 27.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
