





874. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     CLIFT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of April
, 4lbs. of bacon, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Rowe
                  




               
            .


                  DAVID 
                  LARKEY
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  William 
                  Rowe
               



            , cheesemonger
, of Chiswell-street


. On the 5th of April the prisoner took this bacon from the window, which was open - I ran after her up Bunhill-row; I took the bacon and dropped it - I asked her to come back and put it in its place and beg pardon; she screwed herself out of my hands and ran up the row, but I took her.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BAKER
               



             ASHTON. I am a Policeman. I took the prisoner, and have the bacon.


                  THOMAS 
                  ANDERSON
               



            . I am a millwright. I saw the prisoner struggling with Larkey - I saw the bacon on the ground, and took it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 25. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
