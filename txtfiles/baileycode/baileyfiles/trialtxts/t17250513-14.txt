







                  Mary 
                  Hill 
               



            , of S. Mary-le-Bone


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Watch, val. 30 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Lewis 
                     Robert 
                  




                
            , on the 23d of April
. It appeared that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor at the Salutation in Marybone, staid about a Week, took the Watch from her Master's Bed, went off at the Back-Door; and next Day offering it to Sale to 
               
                  Lewis 
                  Mattaire 
               



            , she was apprehended. The Prisoner in her Defence said, that her Master pox'd her; and because his Wife kept him bare of Money, he gave it her to sell to pay the Surgeon. 


               Guilty to the Val. of 4 s. 10 d.
            



               Transportation
