







                     William 
                     Burt 
                  



               
            , of St. Mary's Whitechapel


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silk Handkerchief, from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           How 
                        




, on the 21st of July
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That in a Place call'd Hornsey-Lane, near Stepney


, as he was going along on the 21st of July last, he felt one behind him endeavouring to kick up his Heels, and turning about, saw the Prisoner, who took to his Feet and run cross the Fields, that he felt in his Pocket, and missing his Handkerchief pursued and took him, that he see him drop something and found it to be his Handkerchief.
He had nothing to say in his Defence, but that one 
               
                  John 
                  Wells 
               



             gave it to him, which he not being able to prove, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
