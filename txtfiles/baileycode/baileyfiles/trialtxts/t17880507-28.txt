





358. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     THOMPSON
                  , alias 
GOULDING




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of April
, a silver watch, value 1 l. 11 s. 6 d. and six guineas, and 21 s. in money, the property of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Miller
                        




                     
                  , in the dwelling house of Elizabeth Goffee
            .
The prisoner was taken pawning the watch.




               GUILTY, Of stealing the watch, to the value of 1 l. 11 s. 6 d.
            





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice ASHURST.
