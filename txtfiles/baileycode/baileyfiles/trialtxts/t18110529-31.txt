





411. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     FRAZER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing from the person of 
                     
                        
                           Amos 
                           Dixon
                        




                     
                  , a pocket book, value 1 s. three ten pound bank notes, and two five pound bank notes, his property
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Life
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
