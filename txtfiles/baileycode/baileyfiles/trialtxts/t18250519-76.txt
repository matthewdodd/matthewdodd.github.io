





NEW COURT. (2d DAY.)
Middlesex Cases, Fourth Jury, Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
933. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  GAMSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of April
, two bonnets, value 8 s.; four bonnet crowns, value 20 s., and four bonnet fronts, value 12 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Jarvis
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 25.




               Transported for Seven Years
