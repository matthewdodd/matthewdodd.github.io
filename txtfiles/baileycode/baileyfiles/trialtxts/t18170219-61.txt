






423. 
               
                  
                     MARY ANN 
                     YORK
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               having in her possession a forged Bank of England note, she well knowing the same to be forged
            .
To which indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 27.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Justice Burrough.
