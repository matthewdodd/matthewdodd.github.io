            , Feb. 7

. +
Edward Tailsworth. Mary Edwards is between 13 and 14 years of age, she was servant
 to me.
Q. Where do you live?
Tailsworth. I live in Leadenhall-market



, and keep a public-house. I missed money several times out of my drawer, I taxed her with taking it, she owned she did take it; I missed between 3 and 4 l. in the whole, and she owned to the taking money at several times before my Lord-Mayor.
Q. What do you know against the mother?
Tailsworth. I know nothing more than that she used to come after the daughter, I used to bid her keep away, I know nothing more than what the girl has said.


                  William
