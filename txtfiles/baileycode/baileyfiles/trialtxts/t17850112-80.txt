






283. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     BUNDY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing on the 7th day of January
, fifty-six pound weight of molasses, value 10 s. and one flint glass bottle, covered with wicker, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Pallatt
                  



               
            , 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Silverside
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     Charles 
                     Midford
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the property upon him. (Vide 234.)



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
