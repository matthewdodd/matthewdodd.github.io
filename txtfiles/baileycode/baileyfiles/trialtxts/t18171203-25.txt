





24. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     MORTIMER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 11th of November
, one saddle, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Hughes
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  GOGGIN
               



            . I was painting 
               
                  Robert 
                  Hughes
               



            's premises, in Tyler-street


, on the 11th of November - I saw the prisoner come into the yard, and walk up to the stable; he looked round - I concealed myself - He took the key of the stable door off the nail, opened it, and went in, and came out with the saddle; I called Prist, and he dropped it - I secured him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was never in the place.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 23.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Garrow
               



            .
