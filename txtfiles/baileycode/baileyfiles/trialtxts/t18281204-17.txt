





17. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     PARSONS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of October
, 12 locks, value 24s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Edward 
                  Shearman
               



             and others.
SECOND COUNT, stating them to be the goods of 
               
                  
                     Daniel Prince 
                     Clements
                  



               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 74.
