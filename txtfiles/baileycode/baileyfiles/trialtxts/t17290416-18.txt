







                     Samuel 
                     Salmon 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               stealing 43 Washballs
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Barnard 
                  




                
            , the 29th of March
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Prisoner came to the Prosecutor's Shop about Ten a-Clock at Night, and being seen by some Neighbours to run away with the Goods, was pursued, and stopp'd, the Goods being dropp'd down by him. The Fact was plainly prov'd, and the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d
            .
