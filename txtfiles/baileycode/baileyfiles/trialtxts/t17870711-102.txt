





659. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     WITHERS
                  



               
             was indicted, for 


               stealing, on the 19th day of June
 last, 73 yards of linnen, value 3 l.
             the property of 
               
                  David 
                  Treasurer
               




             and Co.
Mary Milford saw the prisoner take the linen out of the shop.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
