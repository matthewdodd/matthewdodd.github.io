




429. 




THOMAS GRIFFITH
 was again indicted for

 forging and uttering an order for the payment of 10l., with intent to defraud 



John Barnard and others.—Two other COUNTS, stating his intent to be to defraud 



Benjamin Myers; and 




MARY WALTROP



, for inciting him to commit the said felony; to which


GRIFFITH pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 21.—


Transported for Fifteen Years.


MR. BODKIN offered no evidence against

WALTROP—


NOT GUILTY
.
