








                     John 
                     Cross 
                  



               
             of St. Anns Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously, stealing a Sheet value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Ward 
                  




                
            , the 26th of July
 last: The Prosecutor deposed the Prisoner lodg'd in his House and took away the Sheet and pawned it; the Prisoner pleaded he only did it in his necessity, designing to redeem it and return it; however the Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
