





SECOND DAY. FRIDAY, APRIL 6.
Fourth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
880. 
               
                  GEORGE 
                  SPRATT
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of March
, 1 wooden till, value 1s.; 5 shillings, 1 sixpence, 4 penny pieces, 2 halfpence, and 5 farthings
            , the property of 
               
                  Richard 
                  Shaw
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
