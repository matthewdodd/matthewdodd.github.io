





420. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of January
, one pair of shoes, value 5 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Hayhow
                  




               
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  HAYHOW
               



            . I am a shoemaker
, and live in New Road, St. George's


. On the 19th of January, about five o'clock in the afternoon, I was going home, and saw the prisoner run from my shop - I followed, and he was stopped at the corner of Prince's-square.


                  THOMAS 
                  DOUGLAS
               



            . I am shopman to Hayhow. I was standing in the shop, saw the prisoner come up to the door, take a shoe in each hand, and break it from the doorpost - they hung by a string inside the shop. I immediately pursued him down an alley adjoining the shop, and all round Prince's-square - I secured him without losing sight of him. The shoes were picked up close by him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
             Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
