



551. 




JOHN JOSEPH SAXON
 was indicted

for feloniously stealing a letter, containing a £50 bank-note, which had come into his possession by virtue of his employment as a clerk in the 

General Post-office—Othet COUNTS, varying the manner of stating the charge; to which he pleaded



GUILTY
. Aged 10.—


Transported for Life.

Before Mr. Justice Williams.
