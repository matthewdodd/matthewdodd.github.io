





208. ( M.) 
               
                  
                     Susanna 
                     Sherman 
                  , spinster, otherwise 
                     
Susanna Clayton




            , spinster
, was indicted for 


               stealing a silver watch, value 15 s. two silk gowns, value 10 s. one linen gown, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Caleb 
                     Wheeler 
                  




                
            , March 1
. ||


                  Caleb 
                  Wheeler 
               



            . I am a publican
, the prisoner was my servant
; we missed her from her service, and in about two or three hours time we missed the things laid in the indictment (mentioning them by name;) this was on the first of March, we pursued and took her the same night, with the things upon her.
The prisoner said nothing in her defence.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
