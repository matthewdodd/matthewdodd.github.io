





128. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     WARWICK
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of November
, nine books, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Howard
                  




               
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  HOWARD
               



            . I am a bookseller
, and live in Gray's Inn-lane


. On the 7th of November, about three o'clock the books were inside the door, on a stool - I saw the prisoner going out of the shop as I turned my back, I followed him, he ran but I overtook him in Baldwin's-gardens, and took the books from under his coat - he said he found them in the street.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. They laid on the curb.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 49.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
