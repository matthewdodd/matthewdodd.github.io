







                     Jane 
                     Best 
                  



               
             . of St. Dunstan at Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 2 Hats, value 7 s. out of the House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Dagg 
                        




, on the 13th of November
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner went into the Prosecutor's House about 7 a Clock in the Morning, and call'd for a Pint of Ale, which she would have had warm'd, saying she had taken Physick, but there being no Fire she drank it cold; that while the Girl went down to draw the Drink, she took the Hats, and put them under her Coats; that the Prosecutor came down about Eight, and missing his Hats, went to see after her, and found her in Rosemary-Lane with his best Hat in her Hand, crying, Who will by a New Hat? and he answer'd he would; that she confest the Fact before the Justice, which Confession was read in Court. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
