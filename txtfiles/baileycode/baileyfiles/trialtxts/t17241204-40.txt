







                     John 
                     Wood 
                  



               
            , of Chelsea


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Coat, Wastecoat, Breeches, Shirt, and Hat
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Man 
                  




                
            , on the 23rd of November
 last. It appear'd that the Prisoner was lodg'd in a Room over the Prosecutor; and when the Prosecutor was gone out, the Prisoner got into his Room, took away his Cloths, and was stopt selling them in Monmouth-Street. 


               Guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10d.
            



               Transportation
            .
