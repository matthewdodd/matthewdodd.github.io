





187. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS SHARP 
                     BARKER
                  



               
             was again indicted for 


               that he on the 29th of December, in the forty fourth year of his Majesty's reign
, was clerk
 to 
                     
                        Peter 
                        Esdaile
                     



                  , 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Esdaile
                        



                     
                  , 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Hamet
                        



                     
                  , and 
                     
                        James Esdaile 
                        Hamet
                     



                  , was employed and entrusted by them to receive money and notes for them, that he being such servant and so employed and entrusted, did receive and take into his possession, a bank note, value 500 l. for and on account of his said employers, and that he afterwards feloniously did, secrete, and steal the same
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            ,




               Transported for Seven Years
            ,
