







                     Mary 
                     Harris 
                  



               
             of St. Margaret's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           King 
                        




, the 9th of March
 last. It appeared the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and stole the Goods. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
