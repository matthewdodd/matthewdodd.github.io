





First London Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
204. 
               
                  HENRY HAMMOND 
                  FLEMING
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of October
, 1 watch, value 3l. 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Bamfield
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 45.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
