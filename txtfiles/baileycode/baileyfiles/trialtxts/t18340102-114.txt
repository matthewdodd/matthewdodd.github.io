





298. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     KERR
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of December
, 8 half-crowns, and 12 shillings
            , the property of 
               
                  Ann 
                  Kerr
               




            .


                  ANN 
                  KERR
               



            . I am a widow
, and take in washing
 - the prisoner is my son - I sent him and my daughter to receive 1l. 12s. for me - his sister received it and gave it to him - he came home, but has never given me the money.


                  JANE 
                  KERR
               



            . I went on the 31st of December, and received the 1l. 12s. - I gave it to my brother to take home to my mother.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 25. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
