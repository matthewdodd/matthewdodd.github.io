





671. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of February
 , 24 lbs. of beef , value 12s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Howes
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  HORWOOD
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  Henry 
                  Howes
               



             ; he is a butcher
 , and lives in Brook's-market


 . On the 2nd of February I was at home , but did not see this meat taken ; I was told of it, and then missed some ribs of beef; I ran into Leather-lane, and saw the prisoner with it .


                  WILLIAM 
                  RAWLINSON
               



             ( Police-constable G 54). The prisoner was brought to the station - I asked if knew any thing about the meat ; he said he did not, but it was another boy he had formerly been with .


                  HENRY 
                  HALL
               



            . I saw it in his possession - he threw it down ; I ran and took him - when I got back to our shop I found it there.
Prisoner. I saw a boy throw it down , and I took it up .



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
             .
