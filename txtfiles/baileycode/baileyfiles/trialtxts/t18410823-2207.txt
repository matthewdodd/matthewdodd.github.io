





2207. 




WILLIAM HOPKINS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
21st of August, 1 shovel, value 1s., the goods of 



Charles Woodward:—

also, on the 
20th of August, 1 hammer, value 1s., the goods of 



James Dodd;—

also, on the 
11th of August, 1 saw, value 2s. 6d., the goods of 



Hezekiah Mower; to all of which he pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years—Convict Ship.

Before Mr. Justice Erskine.
