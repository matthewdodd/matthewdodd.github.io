





650. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     MITCHELL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of January
, 1 saucepan, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Coxill
                  




               
            .
ROBERT MILLS. I live with Mr. 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Coxill
               



            , a cooper
, of Upper Seymour-street


. On the 13th of January we lost a saucepan out of the shop - the officer brought it back; this is it.


                  JOHN 
                  RADCLIFFE
               



            . I am a Policeman. On the 13th of January I saw the prisoner in Clarendon-square,with this saucepan - I asked where he got it; he would not give me an answer; there were two boys at some distance - I took the prisoner to the station, and then found the prosecutor.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
