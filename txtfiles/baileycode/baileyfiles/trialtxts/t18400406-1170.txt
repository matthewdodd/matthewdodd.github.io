



1170. 




ALFRED CRITCHELL
 was indicted

 for embezzlement.






STEPHEN HAYMAN SWEETLAND
. I am a baker, and live in Carnaby-street. The prisoner was my apprentice, and authorized to receive money from my customers, which he was to enter in the book in the evening, and pay it to me—if he received 2l. 3s. 3d. on the 2nd of March, he has not entered it or paid it to me—he absconded with it.






SUSANNAH BANEZETTI
. On the 2nd of March I paid the prisoner 2l. 3s. 3d. for bread, on account of hit master.




GUILTY
. Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
(The prosecutor stated that the prisoner had absconded twice before with cash.)
