





466. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MOORE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of January
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        Daniel 
                        Mesman
                     




                  , from his person
            .


                  DANIEL 
                  MESMAN
               



            . On the 12th of January, about half-past three o'clock, I was crossing towards the Haymarket; a man tapped me on the shoulder, said "You have lost your handkerchief, and here is the lad I saw pick your pocket;" I turned, and he had my handkerchief; I took it, and collared the prisoner - I gave him to the officer with it.


                  GEORGE 
                  WATSON
               



            . I saw the prosecutor passing Piccadilly


; the prisoner and another walked close behind him - I watched, and saw the prisoner take the handkerchief from the pocket; I told the gentleman.


                  WILLIAM 
                  LONDON
               



            . I am an officer, and took the prisoner.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
