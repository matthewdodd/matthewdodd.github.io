





48. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     SPENCER
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st day of December
, one canvas bag, value 3 d. and 117 lb. weight of iron nails, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Saunders
                  



               
            , and 
               
                  
                     Edward Grey 
                     Saunders
                  



               
            .
The prisoner was taken at his lodgings with the nails, having been previously seen carrying them from the prosecutor's shop.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. Justice HEATH.
