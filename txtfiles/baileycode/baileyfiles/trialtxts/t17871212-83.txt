





88. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     SAVAGE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of November
, one linen handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  William 
                  Fearn
               




            .
Mrs. FEARN sworn.
I was in company with my husband, and saw the prisoner at the bar take the handkerchief out of his pocket, and I laid hold of him; I am sure of him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   PERRYN
               



            .
