







                     Samuel 
                     Eslic 
                  



               
            , jun. of the Parish of St. Mary Whitechappel


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Pestle and Mortar, Linnen, and other Goods
            , the 24th of February
 last. His Father was Evidence against him, and said, That he took such ill Courses, and was so irreclaimable, he was obliged to take this Method to save him from the Gallows. The Jury considering the Matter, found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
