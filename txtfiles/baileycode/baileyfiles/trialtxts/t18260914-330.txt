





1642. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  HEAGREN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of September
, 1 handkerchief, value 1s. 6d., the goods of 
                     
                        Philip 
                        Burgess
                     




                  , from his person
            .
PHILIP BURGESS. On the 5th of September I was in Piccadilly


; my handkerchief was in my right-hand surtoutcoat pocket; I felt a pull, and turned round immediately; I found it in the prisoner's hand; I had used it a moment before, and put it safe into my pocket.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I picked it up, and gave it to the gentleman.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
