





347. 
               
                  
                     ABRAHAM 
                     HYAM
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 24th of April
 last, two cotton gowns, value 20 s. one silk gown, value 5 s. one callico gown, value 5 s. eight linen shifts, value 8 s. one cloth cloak, value 3 d.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Nathan
                  




               
            , widow
.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   EYRE
