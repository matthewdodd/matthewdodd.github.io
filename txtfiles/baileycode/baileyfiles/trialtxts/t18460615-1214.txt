




1214. 




HENRY KINTON
 and 




GEORGE STONE



were indicated for stealing 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 



William Christopherson, from his person; to which

KINTON pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 17.

STONE pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 18.





Transported for Ten Years.

(The prisoners have both been convicted of felony on several occasions.)
