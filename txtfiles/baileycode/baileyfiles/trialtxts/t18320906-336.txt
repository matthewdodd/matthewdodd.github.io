





2066 
               
                  
                     ANN 
                     WILLIAMS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of August
, 1 gown, value 4s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Nicholls
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  CHAPMAN
               



            . I am shopman to Mr. 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Nicholls
               



            , a pawnbroker
, of Gray's Inn-lane


. I had a gown hanging up for sale, and was told by a person who was passing that it was gone - I went after the prisoner, and found it in her apron; it was between four and five o'clock in the afternoon.
Prisoner. It was another man took the gown from me, and two girls had given it to me. Witness. There were two girls with her - she did not say they gave it her.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  RICHARD 
                  BAYLIS
               



             (Police-constable G 67.) I took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
