





220. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     GRUBHAM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of November
, 4 pairs of shoes, value 22s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Johnson
                  




               
            .


                  GEORGE 
                  PAINE
               



            . I was near Mr. Johnson's shop on the 30th of November; I saw the prisoner standing close to the window - I watched him; I saw him reach over and take these shoes from the shop, he ran away - I pursued and took him with them.
JOSEPH JOHNSON. I keep a shop
 in Tottenham-court-road


. These are my shoes.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
