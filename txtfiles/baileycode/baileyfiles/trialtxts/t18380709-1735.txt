



1735. 




ISABELLA ADAMS
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
9th of July 15 yards of printed cotton, value 8s., the goods of 



John Brown.






THOMAS BERRYMAN
. I am an assistant to Mr. John Brown, a linendraper, in High-street, Shadwell. I received information on the 9th of July, and ran after the prisoner—I found this piece of printed cotton on her, which is my master's.

Prisoner. I saw it lying, and picked it up.




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.

Fifth Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Ardbin.
