





217. (M). 
               
                  REBECCA, the wife
 of 
                     
                        James
                         Holmes
                     







            , was indicted for 


               stealing eight ounces of black silk, value twenty-four shillings; a check apron, value one shilling, and a flat iron value six-pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Abraham
                      Cole
                  




               
            , January 31st
 ||.


                  Ann
                   Cole
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  Abraham
                   Cole
               



            , I am an engine windster
, in Spicer-street, Spital-fields


, the prisoner worked for me at five shillings a week. On the 31st of January, I went down into the yard, when I returned the prisoner was gone, and had taken my apron, a flat iron and eight ounces of silk out of the drawer. I took her afterwards in Bishopsgate-street.
Prisoner's Defence.
I am intirely innocent.




               Guilty of stealing to the value of 10 d
