







                     John 
                     Claxton 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Botolph Bishopsgate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Shift, the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Clement 
                        




, the 18th of July
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, that the Shift was hung out in a Yard to dry, and the Prisoner came and took it off from the Line, and upon an outcry, he threw down the Shift and ran away, and was stopp'd. The Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
