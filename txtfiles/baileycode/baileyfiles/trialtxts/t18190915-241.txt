





1315 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     KEEN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of August
, one pair of shoes, value 2 s.; one pair of stockings, value 1 s.; one pair of breeches, value 14 s.; one shirt, value 3 s.; two handkerchiefs, value 6 d.; one waistcoat, value 3 s., and one shawl, value 2 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Bryan
                  




               
            .




               The prisoner pleaded
            

GUILTY. Aged 36.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
