







                  Benjamin 
                  Boswell 
               



             of the Parish of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Cloth Coat, value 2 s . 6 d.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Rogers 
                  




                
            , jun. the 1st of this present October
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Coat was laid on a Bench at St. Martin's Church


, the Owner being at Work there. The Coat was found upon the Prisoner. He had nothing to say in his own Defence. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
