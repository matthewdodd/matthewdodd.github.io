





447. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WALLINGTON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 12th of July
, two curtains, value 15 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Sandall
                        



                     
                   and 
                     
                        William 
                        Green
                     



                  , his masters

, Also for stealing, on the 8th of August
, three curtains, value 15 s., the goods of the same persons. And also for stealing, on the 13th of November
, two curtains, value 8 s.
            , the goods of the same persons.
To all three indictments the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
