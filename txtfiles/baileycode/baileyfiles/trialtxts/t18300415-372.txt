





1028. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     NORTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of March
, 1 pair of half-boots, value 8s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Zachariah 
                     Foster
                  




               
            .


                  ZACHARIAH 
                  FOSTER
               



            . I am a boot and shoemaker
, and live in Newgate-street


. I lost a pair of half-boots from about two feet within the door of my shop, on the 8th of March - I saw the prisoner take them and run away; I ran after him, and took him with them.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  JOSEPH 
                  BATES
               



            . I have the boots, and took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
