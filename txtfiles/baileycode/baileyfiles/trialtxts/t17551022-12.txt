





376. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Duncastle 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one silk handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Francis 
                     Shepherd 
                  




                
            , October 19
. ++


                  Francis 
                  Shepherd 
               



            . On Sunday last about a quarter after five in the afternoon, as I was coming under Bishopsgate


, I saw the prisoner take a handkerchief out of my pocket; he ran away, I call'd out, Stop thief; he was stopp'd; I saw him put his hand behind him and drop the handkerchief, and I took it up. I took him to the Compter, it being Sunday, and before a magistrate the next day, and he was committed.
Prisoner's defence.
I know nothing of the handkerchief.



               Guilty
            .
