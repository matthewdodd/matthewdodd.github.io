





70. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     BROWN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of November
, one handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        Barnet 
                        Cohen
                     




                  , from his person
            .
BARNET COHEN. On the 17th of November I was in Cheapside


, at three o'clock in the afternoon, with a friend. I found a person pushing against me, which excited my suspicion - I turned round and saw my handkerchief inthe prisoner's hand - He dropped it, and I picked it up. A person behind collared him. I am certain he is the man.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I did not drop it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
