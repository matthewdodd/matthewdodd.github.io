





305. 
               
                  THOMAS LONGLAND 
                  HACK
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of December
, 29 brass knobs, value 5s.; 12 door handles, value 4s.; 7 bells, value 5s.; 2 bolts, value 1s.; 2 finger-plates, value 2s.; 4 bell-pull rings, value 1s.; 1 lock, value 2s.; 14 striking-plates, value 5s.; 6 door keys, value 1s., and 40 door escutcheons, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        William 
                        Kingdon
                     




                  , and fixed to a certain building of his
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 25.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
