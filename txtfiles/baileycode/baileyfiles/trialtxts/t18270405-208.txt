





FOURTH DAY. MONDAY, APRIL 9.
Fourth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
960. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  PIKE
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of April
, 36lbs. of hemp, value 36s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Samuel 
                  Clay
               




             and others, his masters.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
