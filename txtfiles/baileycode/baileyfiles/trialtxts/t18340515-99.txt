





839. 
               
                  
                     FRANCIS 
                     MAYNE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of April
, 1 ham, value 9s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Hawksbee
                        




                     
                  , and another
            .


                  PHILIP 
                  HILLS
               



            . I am servant to William Hawksbee and another - they live in Broad-street, St. Giles's


. On the 19th of April, I was told something - I followed the prisoner across the road - he was carrying this ham which is my master's - I secured him.
Prisoner's Defence. It was distress drove me to it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
