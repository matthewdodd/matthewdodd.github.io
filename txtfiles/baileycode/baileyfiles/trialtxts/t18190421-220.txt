





720. 
               
                  
                     DENNIS 
                     CONNAUGHTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of March
, two planes, value 4 s., and three chisels, value 2 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Powell
                  




               
            .


                  RICHARD 
                  POWELL
               



            . I am a carpenter
. On the 18th of March, while I was at dinner, I lost these tools out of a house in Goswell-street-road


, where I was at work - the prisoner worked there as a bricklayer's labourer. He was discharged that day, which made me suspect him.


                  ELIZA 
                  STRINGER
               



            . I keep an old iron-shop in Turnmill-street. On the 18th of March I bought a plane and a chisel of the prisoner.


                  ANN 
                  STRINGER
               



            . I am the daughter of the last witness. I bought a plane, two chisels, and a pair of pincers of the prisoner.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I am innocent. Stringer buys stolen goods.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 30.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Fourth Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
