





1044. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     HAMMOND
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 14th of July
, one coat, value 30 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Thompson
                  




               
            .


                  ELIZA 
                  THOMPSON
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Thompson
               



            , a tailor
. I saw the prisoner come in, and take this coat and go out. I run after him; he dropped it; I took him immediately; he got off, and was stopped again immediately. I am sure he is the person.


                  PETER 
                  PATTERSON
               



            . I was standing at my door, I heard the cry, and saw Mrs. Thompson holding the prisoner; he got loose and ran off; I secured him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I never had it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
