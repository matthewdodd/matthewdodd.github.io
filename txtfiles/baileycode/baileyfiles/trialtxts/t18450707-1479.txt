



1479. 




ROBERT PERCIVAL BERKS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
24th of June, at 
St. James's, Westminster, 1 watch, value 10l., the goods of 



James Cowley, in the dwelling-house of 



Edward Cartwright:—also 1 seal, 10s.; 1 watch-key, 6d.; and 2 handkerchiefs, 5s., the goods of 



William Hoskins; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to all which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Ten Years.
