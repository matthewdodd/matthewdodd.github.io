





489. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     PRICE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of October
 last, a wooden drawer called a money till, value 6 d. an iron key, value 1 d. 73 halfpence and 59 farthings, value 3 s. 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Russell
                  




               
            .


                  ANN
                   RUSSELL
               



             sworn.
I live in South-street, Manchester-square


, I keep a chandler's shop
; I lost my money in the till, mentioned in the indictment, on the 9th of last month; at half past eight in the morning, the prisoner came in and took it; I saw him go out, nobody was in theshop; I called out stop thief! immediately, and he was stopped; I saw him set down the till at a distance from the door, that was on my calling out; he was brought back by a life-guardsman.


                  JOHN
                   COOPER
               



             sworn.
I apprehended the prisoner; there was a cry of stop thief!
Prisoner. I was not nigh the house.



               GUILTY
            , (Aged 17).
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Lord KENYON.
