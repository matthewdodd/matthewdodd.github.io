





440. 
               
                  
                     ANN
                   the wife
 of 
										
                     WILLIAM 
                     DAVIS
										 







             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 27th day of April
, eight pair of silk stockings, value 8s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Atkinson
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken close to the house with the stockings upon her.



               GUILTY
            .
To be 


               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. 
               
                  Baron
                   EYRE
               



            .
