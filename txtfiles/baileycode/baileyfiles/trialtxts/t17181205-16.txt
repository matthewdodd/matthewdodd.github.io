







                     Edward 
                     Reynolds 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchres


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 3 Hats value 20 s. in the shop of 
                     
                        
                           Walter 
                           Sutton 
                        




, the 23d of October
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence that the Prisoner came to the Prosecutors shop pretending to buy Hats, and privately convey'd 3 Hats under his Coat, but being perceiv'd was stopp'd, and being search'd the Hats were found upon him. The Prisoner in his Defence pleaded he was much in drink and knew nothing of the matter, alledg'd that he had receiv'd a Wound in his Head which had hurt his brain so that if he drank a little he was disorder'd so that he had not the use of his reason, call'd several Persons to prove it, and that he had sometimes fits, but it did not appear but he had the use of his reason and was sober when he did the Fact. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
