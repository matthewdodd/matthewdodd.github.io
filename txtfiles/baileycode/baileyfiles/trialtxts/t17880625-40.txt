





442. 
               
                  
                     LAWRENCE 
                     KELLY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of May
 last, two silver table forks, value 20 s. and one silver desert spoon, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Campbell
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the property.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
