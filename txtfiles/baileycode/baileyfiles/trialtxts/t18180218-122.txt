





491. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of December
, two sheets, value 5s.; and one child's dress, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Wardrope
                  




               
            .
LUCY WARDROPE. I live in White Lion-yard, Whitecross-street. On the 22d of December the prisoner took a lodging at my house. Next morning, I missed the sheets and child's dress. The prisoner said he had sold them.


                  THOMAS 
                  VANN
               



            . I am an officer. The prisoner said he had sold them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
