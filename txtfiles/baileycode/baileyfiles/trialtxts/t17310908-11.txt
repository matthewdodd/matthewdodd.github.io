, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Piece of Broad Lawn, in the Shop of 
                     
                        Joseph 
                        Baxter 
                     



 , the 7th of this Instant September
. The Prosecutor deposed, That the Prisoner coming to his Shop, under Pretence of buying some Cambrick, and a Piece of Lawn being miss'd, the Prisoner being suspected , and search'd, the Piece of Lawn was found upon her.


                  Matilda 
                  Conyers
