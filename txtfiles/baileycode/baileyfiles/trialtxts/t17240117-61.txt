








                     John 
                     Yarwood 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchres


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Salt, value 20 s. and a Silver handled Knife, value 5 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Lewis 
                  




                
            , the 31st of December
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was taken in as an Assistant to the Dustman, and took the Goods, as appear'd by his own Confession, which was read in Court. The Prisoner pretended he found them in the Dust Basket. The Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
