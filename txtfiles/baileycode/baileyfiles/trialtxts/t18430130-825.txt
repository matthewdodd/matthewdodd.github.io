




825. 



TIMOTHY SULLIVAN
 and 



MARY MARTIN
 were indicted for

stealing, on the 
26th of January, 86 sheets of printed paper, value 4s. 1d., and 9 pamphlets, value 1s. 1d.; the goods of 



William Mark Clark, the master of Sullivan; to which


SULLIVAN pleaded 


GUILTY
.—


Transported for Seven Years.


MARTIN pleaded 


GUILTY
.—



Confined One Year.
