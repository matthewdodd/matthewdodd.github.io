







                  Thomas 
                  Papworth alias, 
Reynolds




            , of the Parish of St. Dunstans in the West


, was Indicted for 


               Stealing from 
                     
                        
                           Richard 
                           Ellis
                        




                     
                   eight Gold Rings value 4 l. three twenty shilling pieces of Coined Gold of King James the first, one Guinea, and 3 l. in a Linen Bagg
            . The said Ellis deposed, that his Wife keeping a House of Entertainment, Lodged the said Papworth, and he taking his opportunity, carried off the aforesaid Things, whom Mr. Ellis enquiring after, found, and in his Trunk the Rings and some of the Gold. The Prisoner making a slight Defence, was found 

               guilty
             of the Felony.
