







                     Matth.ew

            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Linnen Handkerchief, value 12 d. from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Macknig 
                        




, on the 27th of Nov
. last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That he saw the Prisoner and another suspicious Person together, that the Prisoner put his Hand in his Pocket, and he caught the Handkerchief in his Hand.
He said in his Defence that he was drunk and knew nothing of either the Handkerchief or the Prosecutor; but the Jury believing otherwise, found him 


               guilty to the Value of Ten pence
