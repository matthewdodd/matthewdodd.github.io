





25. 
               
                  
                     Owen 
                     Griffith 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing two Guineas, and three half Guineas and two Shillings in Money, the Money of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Wood 
                        




                      
                  , in his Dwelling-House
            , June 12
.


                  Thomas 
                  Wood 
               



            . Between the Hours of Eleven and One at Night, June the 12th, I lost two Guineas, three half Guineas, and some Silver, out of a Chest. The Prisoner lived in my House, I trusted him for Lodging and Victuals, and in Return he robb'd me.


                  Eleanor 
                  Wood 
               



            . The Prisoner confessed to me, he took the Money, both in the Round-house and in the Gatehouse.


                  Ann 
                  Williams 
               



             He confessed the same in my Hearing. 


               Guilty, 39 s.
