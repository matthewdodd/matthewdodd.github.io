





760. 
               
                  
                     EDWARD 
                     LOWRY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 4th of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Westoby
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
JOHN WESTOBY. I am a harness-maker
. I was in New Compton-street


 on the 4th of April, at six o'clock in the evening; I had a handkerchief in my coat pocket -I felt some one drawing it; I turned, and found the prisoner with it in his hand - he dropped it; I pursued, took him, and gave him in charge, without losing sight of him.


                  JOHN 
                  SHEEN
               



            . I am an officer. I took the prisoner.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
