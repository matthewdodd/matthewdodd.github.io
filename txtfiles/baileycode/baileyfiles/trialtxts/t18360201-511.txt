



511. 




JOHN SPINKS
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
13th of January, 1 coat, value 2l., the goods of 



Robert Jeffrey, his master.






ROBERT JEFFREY
, I live in Upper North-place, Grays-inn-road. The prisoner was in my service about two for months—he used to brush my clothes—on the 13th of January, I missed a coat—this is it,





HENRY KEESING
, I am a clothes-salesman, On the evening of the 13th of January, the prisoner brought this coat on his arm, he had another person with him—I bought it of the prisoner.




GUILTY
. Aged 35.—


Transported for Seven Years.
