





1023. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     BEACH
                  




               
             was again indicted for 


               embezzling a 1 l. bank note, which he had received on account of 
                     
                        William Steadman 
                        Ritchie
                     




                  , his master

.
WILLIAM STEADMAN RITCHIE. The prisoner was my servant
. I sent him to receive a guinea of Mrs. Somerscales - he never accounted to me for it.


                  ANN 
                  SOMERSCALES
               



            . I live in Tabernaele-walk. I owed the prosecutor 1 l. 1 s., which I paid the prisoner on the 12th of June, and produce his receipt.
Prisoner's Defence. He sent me to receive the bill in part of my wages.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
