





654. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     HANNELL
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th day of June
 last, one wooden box, value 2 s. one pair of sheets, value 20 s. two diaper table cloths, value 20 s. twenty-three linen towels, value 10 s. three dimity petticoats, value 14 s. four shirts, value 20 s. one cotton gown and coat, value 40 s. and divers other things, value 2 l. 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Bailey
                  




               
            , widow
.
The prisoner was taken with the box upon him which he had taken out of a cart.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
