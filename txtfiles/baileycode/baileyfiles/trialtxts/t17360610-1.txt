





1. 
               
                  
                     Stephen 
                     Cramp 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing four Cows, value 11 l.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Heather 
                  




                
            , May 6
.
Richard Heather. I lost my Cows from Woodford in Essex


, and on the 6th of May, I saw the Prisoner with them in Smithfield; he own'd that he drove them out of my Ground at 12 o'Clock at Night, and that no one else was concerned with him.


                  Edmd 
                  Pinfold 
               



            , Constable. The Prisoner acknowledged he took the Cows; an ill Thought coming into his Head, after he had lost all his Money by Gaming.


                  John Badger 
                  Welham 
               



            , spoke to the Prisoner's Character, and said he took him to be an Honest Man, and had known him from a Child. 


               Guilty 39s.
