





73. (2d M.) 
               
                  
                     JOHN
                      DILLON
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a blunderbuss mounted with brass, value thirty shillings, the property of 
                     
                        
                           Mary 
                           Dowager Countess
 of Shelburne


                     
                  , and one man's hat trimmed with gold lace, value nine shillings
            ; the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas
                      Dunkett
                  



               
            , November 9th
.
The hat was produced by the pawnbroker, who received it of the prisoner, and the prisoner confessed in court that he committed the crime with which he stood charged.



               Guilty
            .
