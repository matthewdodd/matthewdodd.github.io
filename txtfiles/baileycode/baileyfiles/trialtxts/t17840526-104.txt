






624. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     SANDERS
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     ANDREW 
                     HACKMAN
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               stealing on the 1st of May, one copper pot, value 5 s. two wicker baskets, value 1 d. nine china plates, value 3 s. three cups, value 2 d. three saucers, value 1 d. one tea kettle, value 6 d. two tin saucepans, value 6 d. five soup plates, value 4 d. one iron frying pan, value 6 d. one clothes brush, value 6 d. and one pair of bellows, value 3 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Herring
                  




               
            .
The prisoners were stopped by the watchmen with the property on them, which was deposed to.
BOTH 

               GUILTY
            .
Each to be 



               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. RECORDER.
