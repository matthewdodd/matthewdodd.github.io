







                     John 
                     Grey 
                  



               
            , of St. James Clerkenwell


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Cup value 5 l. in the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Sharp 
                        




, on the 15th of May
 last. The Prosecutor deposed that the Prisoner came under a pretence to buy a Penny-worth of Cheese, and he coming from a Neighbour's saw the Prisoner step off the Threshold and found the Cup upon him. Another deposed he saw the Cup taken from the Prisoner. The Prisner in his Defence said that he went to buy some Cheese, and took the Cup off the Compter to draw some Water in; not with an Intent to steal it, but to bring it again. The Jury considering the matter found him 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s
            . 


               Transportation
            .
