






265. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     NICHOLLS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of January
, sixty-six pounds weight of bar iron, value 4 s. and two iron rings, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Jones
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the iron in his apron.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
