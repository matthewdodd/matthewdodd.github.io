







                     Margaret 
                     Ford 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Scarlet Riding Hood, value 10 s. and other things the Goods
             of 
               
                  
                     Christopher 
                     Moreland 
                  




                
            , on the 10th of July
 last; It appear'd that the prisoner being Servant
 to the Prosecutor, confest where she had pawn'd the Goods, and went and fetch'd them home again. In her defence she denied the Fact: and said they might be taken by any body else, for her Master kept a very disorderly House, (the Crown Tavern in Drury Lane


;) and that the worst thing she ever was guilty of there, was by serving her Master in helping Gentlemen to Whores. 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
