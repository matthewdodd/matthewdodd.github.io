





1139. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BROWN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22d of August
, one watch value 3 l.; one piece of ribbon, value 2 d., and one key, value 2 d., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Napier
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  NAPIER
               



            . I live in Grand Junction-street, Whitefriars. On the 22d of August, at half-past four o'clock, I was in St. James's-square


, and felt my watch going from my pocket, a great many people were about. I looked down and saw the prisoner pulling it from my pocket, and saw it in his hand. I seized his hand with it, he stooped down and dropped it. Butcher picked it up. I gave him in charge.


                  JOHN 
                  BUTCHER
               



            . I am a carpenter. I was in the square facing Mr. Napier, and saw him seize the prisoner round the waist. I saw the watch at his heels. I forced myself down and got it.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HURST
               



            . I live in Richards's-buildings, Shirelane. I was with Mr. Napier, he missed his watch, we both seized the prisoner. I saw him drop it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I was in the square, a parcel of people shoved me into the prosecutor's hands, and said I had robbed him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22.




               Transported for Life
            .
Third Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
