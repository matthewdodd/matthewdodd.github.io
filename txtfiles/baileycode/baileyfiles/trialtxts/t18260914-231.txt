





1543. 
               
                  ROBERT WILLIAM 
                  WHITE
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of August
, 1 sheet, value 12s.; 1 gown, value 5s.; 2 petticoats, value 5s.; 8 pairs of stockings, value 3s. 6d.; 1lb. weight of quicksilver, value 3s., and 2 pairs of shoes, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Austin
               




            .


                  HANNAH 
                  AUSTIN
               



            . I am the wife of Thomas Austin, of Radcliff-terrace, Goswell-street-road


, we keep a silversmith's shop
. On the 22d of August I left the things out to dry all night; on the 23d of August the watchman called in the morning, and found the articles; this quicksilver is my husband's.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BARKER
               



            . I am the watchman, and saw theprisoner coming out of President-street, about five o'clock in the morning - he crossed the road, I stopped him, and asked what he had got there; he said, linen, which he brought from the City-road, and was going to take to Old-street - it being damp, I left him at the watch-house, and went to the house to which he said he was going, and between 8 and 9 o'clock in the morning I found the owner.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I picked them up in the square, within the rails.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
