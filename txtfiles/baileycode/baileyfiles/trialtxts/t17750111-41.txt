





143. (L.) 
               
                  
                     THOMAS
                      DANIEL
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a cotton handkerchief, value ten-pence
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Cooper
                  




               
            , December 17th




                  John
                   Cooper
               



            . On the 17th of December, between five and six in the afternoon, going along Lombard street


, with a lady, I felt a hand between her and me, I turned and saw my handkerchief in the hand of the prisoner, I collared him, but the lady fainting away he got from me, he was stopped by a constable who saw him throw away three or four handkerchiefs, and they were run away with by the mob all but one, which is not mine, I am positive to the man, and am sure I saw my handkerchief in his hand.
Prisoner's Defence.
I was further from the gentleman than I am now, there was another person running, the gentleman called stop thief, and I ran after him.



               Guilty
            



               T
            .
