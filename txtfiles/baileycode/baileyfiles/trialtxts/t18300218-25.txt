





522. 
               
                  JOHN 
                  MILDENHALL
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of January
, 1 horse-cloth, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Scrivener
               




            : to which he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 40. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
