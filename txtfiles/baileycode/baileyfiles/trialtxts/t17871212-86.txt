





91. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     LILLEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th day of October
 last, a piece of linen containing twenty-six yards, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  Hardy 
                  Holinds
               




            .
The prisoner was taken near the prosecutor's warehouse, immediately after the robbery, with the property upon him.



               GUILTY.
            





               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
