







                     John 
                     Newport 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Brides


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 4 s.
             the Money of 
               
                  
                     George 
                     Dascomb 
                  




                
            , the 1st of this instant October
. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor, and having several Times lost Money out of his Breeches Pocket, he put in some remarkable, and it was taken upon the Prisoner the next Morning. The Jury found him 

               guilty
             of the Indictment.
