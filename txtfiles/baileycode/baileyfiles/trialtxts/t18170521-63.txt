





837. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     HYLAND
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of April
, six yards of bombazeen, value 15s., the property of 
                     
                        Thomas 
                        Saundby
                     




                  , privately in his shop
            .


                  JOHN 
                  GAY
               



            . I am a patrol. On the 23d of April I was in Holborn, and saw the prisoner, with another man, about Mr. Saundby's shop. The prisoner put his hand through a pane of glass which was broken, and took the hombazeen out; I secured him.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. I picked it up.
GUILTY. Aged 17.
Of 


               stealing, but not privately
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Garrow
               



            .
