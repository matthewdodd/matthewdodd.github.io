





835 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM SIMPSON 
                     HENNINGHAM
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               that he, on the 19th of September
, feloniously had in his custody and possession three forged Bank notes, for the payment of ten pounds each, he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
