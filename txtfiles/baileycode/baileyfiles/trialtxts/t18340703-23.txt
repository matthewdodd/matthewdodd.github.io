





914. 
               
                  MARTIN 
                  FLETCHER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 27th of May
, 1 coat, value 10s.; 2 cloaks, value 14s.; 1 umbrella, value 3s.; 1 shawl, value 2s.; and 1 napkin, value 1s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William Royston 
                  Miles
               




            , to which he 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
