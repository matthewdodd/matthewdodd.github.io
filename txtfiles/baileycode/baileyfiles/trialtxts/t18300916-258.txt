





1722. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SPACKMAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of August
, 8 half-crowns, 20 shillings, and 4 sixpences
            , the monies of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Almond
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
