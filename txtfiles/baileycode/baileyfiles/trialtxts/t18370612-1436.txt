



1436. 




ELIZA SMITH



 was again indicated for stealing, on the 
13th of May, 1 handkerchief, value 2s. 6d., the goods 



Marmaduke Robert Jeffreys, from his person.






MARMADUKE ROBERT JEFFREYS
. I was in the strand, and missed my handkerchief—I had felt it safe within an hour—this is it.

Prisoner. There was a man followed you with a stick and nightcap. Witness. That was, much earlier in the evening, we were annoyed by a man begging, but he did not touch me; but on this occasion, some woman came behind and touched me.





EDWARD OWEN SIMONS
. I am a policeman, I found this handkerchief on the prisoner with the others—she was sober, and could, walk well.




GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for seven years.
