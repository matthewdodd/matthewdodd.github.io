



1411. 




GEORGE BENNETT
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
3rd of June, 37 handkerchiefs, value 1l. 7s. 6d.; and 30 yards of gingham, value 1l. 5s. 6d.; the goods of 



James Marsh.—2nd COUNT, stating them to be the goods of 



William Leaf and others; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 54.—


Transported for Seven Years.
