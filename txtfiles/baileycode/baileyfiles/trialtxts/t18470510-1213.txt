




1213. 




JAMES LUCKSON
 was indicted

 for stealing 1 purse, value 1s.; 2 sovereigns, 2 shillings, 1 sixpence, 1 threepenny piece, 1 penny, and 1 halfpenny; the monies of 



John David Rochat,✗ from the person of 



Mary Ann Rochat; and that he had been before convicted of felony; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 22.—


Transported For Seven Years.
