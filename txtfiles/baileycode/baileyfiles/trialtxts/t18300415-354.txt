





1010. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     HANDS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of March
, 1 handkerchief, value 1s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas Moriah 
                           Otton
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  MORIAH OTTON
               



            . I lost my handkerchief at Smithfield-bars


, on the 15th of March; I was not aware of it till the officer told me of it - I then missed it; I had not seen the prisoner.


                  JOHN 
                  TREE
               



            . I was in Smithfield on Monday morning, the 15th of March; I saw the prisoner and two other boys following this gentleman - I got as close as I could, and saw the prisoner draw the handkerchief from this gentleman's pocket, and pass it to another boy
, who got away; I took the prisoner, and got him through the crowd - I told the prosecutor of it.
Prisoner's Defence. I was going through Smithfield, and this gentleman accused me of robbing a person of his handkerchief; I knew nothing of it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
