






1666. 




JOHN HAWKINS
 and 




WILLIAM FARMER



, stealing 24 plane-irons, value 5s.;



also, 26 knives and forks, 14s. 6d.; the goods of 



William Borwick; Hawkins having been before convicted: to which

HAWKINS pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years.


FARMER pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 21,—



Confined Twelve Months.
