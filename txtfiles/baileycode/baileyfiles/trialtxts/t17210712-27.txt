








                     Richard 
                     Ralph 
                  



                
             and 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Bealey 
                  



                
            , of St. Giles without Cripplegate



, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 67 Glass Bottles value 6 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Ward 
                  




                
             on the 1st of June
 last. Mr. Ward deposed, that Mr. Meers call'd him out of Bed at Midnight, telling him he was robb'd, that he went to the Watch house, found the Prisoners there, and a parcel of Bottles on the Table, but could not swear to them. Edward, Meers deposed that as he and Watchmen were going their Rounds, they saw three Boys, but one made his Escape, that they found the Prisoners putting Bottles into a Canvas Bag: that they owned they took them out of Mr. Ward's Yard, that Bailey was hoisted over the Wall and let the rest in; and that they carried and hid some of them in Bunhill-Fields. Their Confession was read in Court. The Jury considering the Matter found them both 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            




               Transportation
            .
