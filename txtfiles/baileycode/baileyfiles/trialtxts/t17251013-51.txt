







                     Hester 
                     James , alias 
Bampton




               
            , of Acton


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Table-Cloth, a Sarcenet-hood, a Pair of Silk Stockings, a Silver Spoon, and other Things
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Lock 
                  




                
            , Oct. 3
. It appear'd that the Prisoner was Servant
 to the Prosecutor; that she went away with the Goods, and they were taken upon her, at the Pack-Horse at Turnham-Green.
