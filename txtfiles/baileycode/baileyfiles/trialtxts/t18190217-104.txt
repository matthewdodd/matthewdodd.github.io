





417. 
               
                  
                     MARK 
                     FORSTER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of February
, one hat, value 4 s
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Franks
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  ELFORD
               



            . I am servant to Mr. 
               
                  Robert 
                  Franks
               



            , who is a hatter
, and lives in Barbican


. The prisoner was brought into our shop, about twenty minutes after one o'clock, with the hat on his head - I had not missed it before, but did then.


                  GEORGE 
                  THORPE
               



            . I live opposite to Mr. Franks. I saw the prisoner lurking about his door - he had no hat on. I watched, saw him take one, and run off. I pursued, and took him with it on his head.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
