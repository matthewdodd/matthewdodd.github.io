



1897. 




JOHN BARRY
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
1st of June, 1 handkerchief, value 6d., the goods of 



William Mossom, from his person.






WILLIAM MOSSOM
. I was in High-street, St. Giles's—about twelve o'clock, on the 1st of June, I received information and missed my handkerchief, which I had safe about five minutes before—this is it.





STEPHEN THORNTON
 (police-constable E 53.) I was on duty and saw the prisoner and two others attempt several gentlemen's pockets—I then saw a taller boy than the prisoner put his hand into the prosecutor's pocket—he then spoke to the prisoner, who changed sides with him, and the prisoner took this handkerchief from the prosecutor—I took him—the taller one kicked me, but I kept the prisoner.

Prisoner. I met two persons, who took me into a cook's shop and gave me some pudding—we then went out, and one of them told me to hold this handkerchief while he tied his shoe.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported Ten Years.—Convict Ship.
