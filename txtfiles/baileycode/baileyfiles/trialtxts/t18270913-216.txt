





1803. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  FULLER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of July
, 34 yards of printed cotton, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Neville
               




            .


                  ROBERT 
                  BYERS
               



            . I am servant to William Neville, who lives in Mile-end-road


. This is his cotton. I was out when it was taken - it was safe two hours before.


                  JOHN 
                  WALLIS
               



            . I am a groom. On the 20th of July I was sitting in a chaise, nearly opposite Mr. Neville's. I saw the prisoner and another take this print from the shop. I jumped out and secured the prisoner - he scuffled with me, and dropped it - the other got away - Wheatley picked it up.


                  PETER 
                  WHEATLEY
               



            . I picked up the print, and saw the prisoner scuffling with Wallis.


                  JAMES 
                  LEA
               



            . I took him in charge.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 23.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
