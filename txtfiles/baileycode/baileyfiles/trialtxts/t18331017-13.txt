





OLD COURT. Thursday, October 17, 1833.
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1416. 
               
                  
                     SARAH 
                     HOLLINGTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of September
, in St. Matthew, Bethnal-green


, 5 sovereigns and 8 half-sovereigns, the monies of 
                     
                        
                           Robert 
                           Firmin
                        




                     
                  , her master, in his dwelling-house
            , to which she pleaded
- 


               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
