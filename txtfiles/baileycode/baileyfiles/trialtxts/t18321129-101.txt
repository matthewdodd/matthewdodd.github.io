





102. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     DYE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of November
, 1 cloak, value 9s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Sharman
                  




               
            .



                  CHARLES 
                  PRETTY
               



            . I am shopman to William Sharman, linen-draper
, Chiswell-street


. On the 10th of November, about six o'clock in the evening, I was in the shop; I saw the prisoner come to the door, pull the cloak down, and drop it partly in the shop and part out; it was hanging inside the shop; I went to the door, and he dropped it -I believe he did not see me coming; he run off, but I pursued, and took him without losing sight of him.


                  JAMES 
                  CROSS
               



             (Police-constable G 172). I took the prisoner, and have the cloak



               GUILTY
            *. Aged 10. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
