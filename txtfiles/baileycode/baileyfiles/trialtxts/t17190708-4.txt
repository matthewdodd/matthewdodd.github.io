







                     John 
                     Taylor 
                  



               
            , of Alhallows the Less


 , was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing wearing Apparel to the value of 6 l. out of the Dwelling House of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Bennet 
                        




 , on the 2d of May
 last. It appeared the Prisoner was the Prosecutor's Servant
, and took the Goods and went away with them, which were afterwards found at the Brokers where he had pawn'd them. His Confession before the Justice was read in Court. The Jury considering the Master had all his Goods again, found him 


               Guilty to the value of 39 s.
