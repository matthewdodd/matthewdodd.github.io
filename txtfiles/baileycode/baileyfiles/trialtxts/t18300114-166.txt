





399. 
               
                  THOMAS 
                  GRIGG
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of December
, 4lbs. of pork, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William Robins 
                  Carden
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  BULL
               



            . I am apprentice to 
               
                  William 
                  Robins Carden
               



            , of Newgate-street


, cheesemonger and salesman
. On the 29th of December, at a quarter-past five o'clock, the prisoner came into the shop, took this pork off a bench in the shop, put it under his coat, and went out; I stopped him two or three doors off with it, and gave him in charge.


                  THOMAS 
                  PEEL
               



            . I am a patrol, and took him in charge- I found nothing on him.
Prisoner. I cannot deny taking it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
