





1339. 
               
                  
                     JANE 
                     HUGHES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of May
, 2 brooches, value 2s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Illman
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
