





48. (M.) 
               
                  
                     SAMUEL
                      RICH
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a silk cloak, value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Letitia
                      Moody
                  




               
            , Nov. 20
. *


                  Letitia
                   Moody
               



            . I had been out; when I came home this boy (the prisoner) and a man followed me in Stonecutters-alley


; the boy came up, and pulled my cloak off; then the man laid hold of me, and held me for about four minutes; the boy run away; I cried out, Stop thief! My master's apprentice, 
               
                  John
                   Hobson
               



            , saw the boy
 run; he run after him, and took him. This is the cloak, (producing it) it is my property.


                  John
                   Hobson
               



            . I was coming home; I heard a cry of Stop thief! the prisoner run post me; I pursued him; he ran into an alley; I followed him; I saw him on the ground, and the cloak lay by him.
The prisoner said nothing in his defence, nor called any witness.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
