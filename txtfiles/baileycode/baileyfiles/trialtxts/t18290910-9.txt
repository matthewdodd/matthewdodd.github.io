





NEW COURT, First Day.
Third Middlesex Jury. - Before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1540. 
               
                  
                     DANIEL 
                     SULLIVAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s. the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Simon Andrew 
                           Foster
                        




                     
                  , from his person.
            

The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Fourteen years
            .
