





400. 
               
                  ZENOPHON HEARN 
                  BASHAM
               



             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 1st of April
, feloniously had in his custody and possession, a certain forged bank note for the payment of 5 l. he knowing the same to be forged and counterfeit
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
London jury, before Mr. 
               
                  Baron 
                  Graham
               



            .
