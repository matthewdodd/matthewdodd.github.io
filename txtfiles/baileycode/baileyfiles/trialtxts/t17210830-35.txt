







                     Nathaniel

            , the Goods and Money of 
               
                  
                     Tatney 
                     Blake 
                  




                
            , on the 29th of May
 last. 
               
                  Ann 
                  Blake 
               



             deposed that the Prisoner was her Apprentice

His Confession before the Justice was read in Court. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
