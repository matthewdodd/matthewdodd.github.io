





2097. 
               
                  MARY 
                  WALKER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of October
, 2 pewter pots, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        James 
                        Mantle
                     



                  ; 2 pewter pots, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        John 
                        Hemery
                     



                  ; and 1 pewter pot, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Hiddon
               



            .


                  JAMES 
                  BARBER
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Mantle, who keeps the Berkeley Arms public-house, in John-street, Berkeley-square




. I saw the prisoner on the 18th of October, with two pots close by her; I turned my head to speak to a person, and she was gone - I ran after her and found four pots in her apron, and one in her pocket; two of these are ours.
JOHN HEMERY. I keep the Masons' Arms public-house




. Two of these pots are mine.
WILLIAM HIDDON. I am a publican
. One of these pots is mine.
Prisoner's Defence. I was in great distress and have two children.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 39.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
