






652. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     SMITH
                  , alias 
CAVE




            , and 
               
                  THOMAS
               


             (a black man
) were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 2d of June
 last, one metal watch, value 3 l. one pair of stone knee buckles, value 1 s. a silk handkerchief, value 6 d. and a crown piece, value 5 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           Jones
                        




                     
                  , in the dwelling house of 
                     
                        James
                         Webster
                     



.
BOTH 


               GUILTY, 39 s.
            

Each 



               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
