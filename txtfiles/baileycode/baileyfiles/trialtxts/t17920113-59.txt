






106. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     BRITTAIN
                  



               
             and 
               
                  JOHN 
                  BAILEY
               



             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of December
, a wooden firkin, value 3 d. and 65 lb. weight of butter
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Grover
                  




               
            .
The prisoner Brittain was taken with the property.


                  JOSEPH
                   BRITTAIN
               



            , 

               Guilty
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .


                  JOHN
                   BAILEY
               



            , 

               Not Guilty
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. JUSTICE WILSON.
