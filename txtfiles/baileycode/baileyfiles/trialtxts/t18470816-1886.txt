





1886. 



JAMES BARRYMORE
 was again indicted for

 stealing 1 coat, value 1l. 1s., the goods of 



Isaac Moses, his master; and 




MARY ANN BARRYMORE



 for receiving the same, well knowing the same to have been stolen; to which


JAMES BARRYMORE pleaded 


GUILTY.



Transported for Seven Years.

(MR. HUDDLESTON
offered no evidence against Mary Ann Barrymore.)




NOT GUILTY.
