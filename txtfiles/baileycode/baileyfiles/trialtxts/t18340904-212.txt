





1432. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  GREEN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of August
, 6 frocks, value 6s.; 1 gown, value 1s. 6d.; 1 apron, value 4d.; 1 pinafore, value 4d.; 1 handkerchief, value 1s.; and 1 tippet, value 3d.
            ; the goods of 
               
                  Robert 
                  Pepperell
               




            .


                  MARY 
                  CONNER
               



            . I am servant at the Ship and Whale, at Wapping-wall. The prisoner came the reon the 2nd of August, about seven o'clock in the morning, and left a bundle - my curiosity led me to look into it - I found in it these articles, which were all wet - he came back, and asked me for it - I refused to let him have it - he got an officer, who came and demanded the bundle, and I gave it the officer.


                  HANNAH 
                  PEPPERELL
               



            . I am the wife of Robert Pepperell - we live at Bridge-place


. I had these articles in a tub in my yard on the 1st of August - I missed them the next morning, about twenty minutes past eight o'clock - I live two miles from the public-house.


                  JOHN 
                  STILWELL
               



            . I am an officer - the prisoner came to me, and said his bundle was at the public-house - I went, and the witness gave me these things.
Prisoner. He told her to mind and swear what he toldher, as she would be well paid, and I should be convicted. Witness. I deny it all together.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
There were two other indictments against the prisoner.
