





1337. 
               
                  
                     JANE 
                     GRAHAM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of June
, 2 caps, value 4s.; 1 gown, value 2s.; 1 shift, value 1s.; 2 yards of ribbon, value 2s., and 1 basket, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Burrows
               




            .
SARAH BURROWS. I am the wife of Charles Burrows - we live in Eden-court, Regent-street


; the prisoner slept at my house for ten nights. On Sunday night I went out nursing at Chelsea - on the Tuesday I returned and missed these articles.


                  JOHN 
                  VARLEY
               



            . I am shopman to a pawnbroker in Lisson-grove. On the 9th of June the prisoner pawned this cap for 1s. 6d.


                  RICHARD 
                  BULL
               



            . I took the prisoner. She said she did not care about going to the watch-house.


                  SARAH 
                  BURROWS
               



            . This is my cap.
Prisoner's Defence. I met the prosecutor's wife, she gave me something to drink, and then persuaded me to go and live with her; a broker came to seize the things - he gave me leave to take mine; she is no more than I am myself, and used to go out with me of an evening.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
