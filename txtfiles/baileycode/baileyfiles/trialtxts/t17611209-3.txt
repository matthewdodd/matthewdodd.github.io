                     Mary 
                     Arnott 
                  



               
            , widow
, was indicted for 


               stealing one pair of leather clogs, value 3 s. 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  Robert 
                  Maris 
               




             , Dec. 2
.~


                  Robert 
                  Maris 
               



keeps a shoe-maker's shop
 in Holbourn


. The prisoner went into the shop to inquire after a person, which, she said, lived in the neighbourhood; and, at going out, was observed by a neighbour to take a pair of leather clogs that hung in the window. The neighbour informed the apprentice, who pursued and took her with the clogs upon her.




               Guilty 10 d.
