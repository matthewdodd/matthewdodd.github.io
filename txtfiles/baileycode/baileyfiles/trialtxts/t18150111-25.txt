





146. 
               
                  
                     MARY
                     WATSON
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               she, on the 20th of December
, feloniously and without lawful excuse had in her custody and possession a forged bank note for the payment of 2 l.
            

To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
