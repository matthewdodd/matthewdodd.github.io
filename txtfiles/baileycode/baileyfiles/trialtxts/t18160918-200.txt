





1020. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     HENRY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of August
, a shawl, value 2s. 6d. a counterpane, value 4s. 6d. a hat, value 7s. and a frock, value 1s.
             the property of 
               
                  James 
                  Stevens
               




            .


                  CHARLOTTE 
                  STEVENS
               



            . I lost these things between five and six weeks ago. I quitted my house for about a quarter of an hour on the day in the indictment, and on my return, my son told me a man had been in and had opened the drawer, I missedthe property in the indictment. I accused the prisoner in consequence of information which I received.


                  DINAH 
                  MOSES
               



            . I keep a slop shop. about half past nine in the evening of the day in the indictment, the prisoner came into my shop, and offered me the property in question for sale; I suspected he had not come honestly by it, and I sent for an officer.



               GUILTY
            , aged 21.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
