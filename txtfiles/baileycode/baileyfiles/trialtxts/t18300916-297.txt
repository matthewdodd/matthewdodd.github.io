





1760. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     THORNS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 22nd of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Thomas 
                           Dunne
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .
THOMAS DUNNE. I am assistant to a linen-draper
. I was in St. James-park


, about the middle of the day, on the 22nd of July; I had a handkerchief in my pocket - I did not feel it taken, but a person told me I had lost it; I turned, and searched my pocket - a person seized the prisoner, and said he was the person who took it - my handkerchief was found on him; it was given to the Policeman, who has since been discharged, and I have not heard of it since - I swore to it at Queen-square.


                  GEORGE 
                  DORRELL
               



            . I was at the review that day - I saw the prisoner pick two people's pockets, and then the prosecutor's; I endeavoured to reach each of the other gentlemen, but I could not; the prisoner was taken with the prosecutor's handkerchief, and six others.
Prisoner's Defence. When I was taken to the office, he said he never saw me near the gentleman.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
