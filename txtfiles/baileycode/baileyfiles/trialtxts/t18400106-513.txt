



513. 




ROBERT RAVENS
was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
26th of December, 1 pair of trowsers, value 8s., the goods of 



James Earl; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






JAMES EARL.
I live in Goswell-street. On the 26th of December I was behind the counter in my shop, and heard a noise outside—I ran out, and saw the prisoner on the opposite side of the road—I ran after him up Noble-street—I took him—he threw these trowsers under a cart—they are mine—they have my shop-mark on them—I had teen him carrying them.

Prisoner. Q. You did not see me with them? A. Yes, I saw you carrying them on the opposite side of the road.

Prisoner's Defence. I am innocent—the prosecutor stated twice that he did not see them on me, and at the last bearing he said to saw them on me.





JOSEPH WOLGEMUTH.
I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got at Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY.Aged 20.—


Transported for Seven Years
