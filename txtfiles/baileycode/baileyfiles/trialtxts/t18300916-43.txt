





1508. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     SHEARMAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of a certain man unknown, from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  ANDERSON
               



            . On the 21st of August, I saw the prisoner in St. James'-street, Piccadilly


; he put his hand into five different peoples' pockets - it was the evening of the King's birth-day; I then saw him take this handkerchief from the pocket of a person who I did not know - I followed him with two other officers; I had seen him put the handkerchief into his trousers - we took him, and found it on him at the watch-house; I could not find the gentleman he took it from - I asked the prisoner if there were any marks on it - he said No, but I found three large letters on it; I found two other handkerchief on him, both marked, though he said they were not.
Prisoner. I said it was not marked that I knew of.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
