







                     Margaret 
                     Hampton , alias 
Ann Thomas




               
            , of Broad-Street


, was indicted for 


               stealing 4 Pewter Plates, value 2 s. 6 d.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Pitches 
                  




                
            , December 21
.
The Witness thus depos'd. The Prisoner had been Cook Servant
 to Mr. Pitches at the Ship Tavern in Bartholomew-Lane


. Her Master had mist several Things, upon which he turn'd her away; and soon after she off'd 4 Plates to Sale to Mr. Broadburst, who stopt her, and upon enquiry found them to be the Goods of the Prosecutor. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
