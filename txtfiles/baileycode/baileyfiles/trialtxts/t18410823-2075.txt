




2075. 




SARAH JONES
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
14th of July, 1 pair of boots, value 3s. 6d., the goods of 



Owen McCarthy, from the person of 



Charles M'Carthy;—

also on the 
12th of July, 1 pair of shoes, value 1s. 6d., the goods of 



Henry Nathaniel Norval, from the person of 



Samuel Denman Arundel; to both of which she pleaded




GUILTY
. Aged 24.—


Transported for ten Years.
