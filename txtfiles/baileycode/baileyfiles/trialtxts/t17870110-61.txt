





220. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     HALEY
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of December
, an iron hammer, value 2 s. a trowell, value 6 d. and a chissel, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Hosier
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the tools upon him.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
