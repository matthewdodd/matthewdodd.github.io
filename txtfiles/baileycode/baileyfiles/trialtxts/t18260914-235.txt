





1547. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  WALKER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th of July
, 1 pocket-book, value 6d.; 1 handkerchief, value 1s., and 1 1/2d. in copper
            , the monies of 
               
                  Charles 
                  Logan
               




            .
CHARLES LOGAN. I am a baker
, and live in the Harrow-road


. On the 5th of July, my wife being ill in bed, I went up with some breakfast to her, and when I came down I found the prisoner with the pocket-book in his hand, shuffling down the lid of my desk; I took hold of him, and took him to my till, where I had left 1 1/2d. - they were then gone; the handkerchief had been in my hat in the parlour; I took him to the constable, and the 1 1/2d. were found in his pocket.
The prisoner pleaded distress.




               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.
Recommended to Mercy. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
