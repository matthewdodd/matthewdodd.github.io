



1033. 




JAMES POTTER
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
27th of January, 1 saw, value 5s. 6d. the goods of 



John Allen Burgess.






JOHN ALLEN BURGESS
. I am a sawyer, and live in the Edgware-road. On the 27th of January I lost a saw—the prisoner had worked for me, but was not working for me then—this is the saw I lost.





CHARLES CRUMP
. I am a wood-cutter, I saw the prisoner with this saw—he asked me if I knew any body who wanted such a thing—I I did not—this was one evening, in the latter part of January.






THOMAS BATNE
. (police-constable D 79.) I took the prisoner in charge, and found this saw up the chimney of the house where the prisoner lives.




GUILTY
.* Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years.—To the Prison ship.
