




1479. 




WILLIAM CANTY
 and 




JOHN TYLER



, stealing 25l.-notes, and 1 cash-box; the property of 



Henry Bosanquet and others, in the dwelling-house of 



Oliver Vile: Tyler having been before convicted: to which

CANTY pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 69.

TYLER pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 62.





Transported for Ten Years
.
(There was another indictment against Canty.)
