





First London Jury. - Before Mr. Sergeant Arabin.
1009. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     COUNTY
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of April
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William Robertson 
                           Hayward
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  ROBERTSON HAYWARD
               



            , ESQ
. I live in the Temple. On the 15th of April, about eight o'clock in the evening, I was in Bridge-street


, and felt my pocket lighten- I instantly turned round, and seized the prisoner; I saw my handkerchief in his hand - he put it behind him, dropped it, and struggled hard to get away, but I secured him, and gave him in charge.


                  RICHARD 
                  ANDREWS
               



            . I received the prisoner in charge.(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
