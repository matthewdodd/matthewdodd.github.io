






503. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     RUDD
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th day of May
, one pair of man's leather shoes, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Chapman
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was a dust-man
; the shoes were missed and found in the cart; he was taking out the dust; the maid servant went down to get a light; the shoes were then laying behind the street door, and when she returned they were gone.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
