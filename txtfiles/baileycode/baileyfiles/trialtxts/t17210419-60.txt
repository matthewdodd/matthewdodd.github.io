







                     Margaret 
                     Wilson 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing 2 Gold Rings value 5s. a piece of a Silver Buckle, and 2 s. in Money from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Bradley 
                        




, on the 7th of March
 last. The Prosecutor deposed, that he lost his Goods and Money in a House in Newtonners Lane


 with the Prisner and another Woman; that he had them the Minute before, and no Body was near him but the Prisner, who he felt take them, and saw her clap her Hand in her Bosom. The constable deposed, that he found the two Rings upon her, tho she denied she had them at first. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
