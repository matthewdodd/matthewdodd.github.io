                     Thomas 
                     Rummings 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one silk handkerchief, val. 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Waller 
                  




                
            , July 28
. *
William Waller. As I was coming down Ludgate-street


, July 28, between four and five in the afternoon I saw the boy
 take my handkerchief from my pocket, I laid hold on him immediately, and secured him.
The prisoner had nothing to say in his defence.



               Guilty
            .
