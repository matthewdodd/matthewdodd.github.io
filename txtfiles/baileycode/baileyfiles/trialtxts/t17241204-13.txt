







                     John 
                     Randal 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               privately stealing from 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Woodroff 
                        




                      
                   a Handkerchief value 10d.
             on the 24th of October
 last. It appear'd, that as the Prosecutor was coming out of the Playhouse in Drury-Lane


, the Prisoner ran against him, and took the Handkerchief out of his Pocket. Mr.Shrimpton saw him commit the Fact, seiz'd him, and he being searched, 4 other Handkerchiefs were found upon him. 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
            .
