





83. 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Crank 
                  



               
            , of St Leonard's, Shoreditch


, was indicted for 


               stealing four Silver-Buttons, Value 2 s. and 8 s. in Money
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Pendrice 
                  




                
            , Dec. 28th
.
Pendrice. She lodges with me, I found these Buttons upon her, she pulled them out of her Bosom, and owned she took them.


                  Elizabeth 
                  Eldridge 
               



            . I saw Mr Pendrice's Buttons taken from her.
Prisoner. I found them in a Piece of brown Paper upon the Stairs. 


               Guilty, 10 d.
