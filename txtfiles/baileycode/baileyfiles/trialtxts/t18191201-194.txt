





193. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     OLIVER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23d of November
, one brass mortar, value 10 s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Simmonds
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  SIMMONDS
               



            . I am a broker
, and live in Old-street


. On the 23d of November I saw two boys walking by my shop - I saw the prisoner take the mortar; I ran out, and secured him with it.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
