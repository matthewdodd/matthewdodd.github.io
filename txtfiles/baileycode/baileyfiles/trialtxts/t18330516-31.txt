





915. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     STOCKES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of May
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Francis 
                     Taylor
                  




               
            .
FRANCIS TAYLOR. I am a silk manufacturer
, and live in Brown's-lane, Spitalfields


. I lost this handkerchief on the 2d of May, but I was not at home.


                  FRANCIS 
                  TAYLOR
               



            , JUN. I was at work at the time, my sister called me, I went to the door, and saw the prisoner at the window taking a handkerchief out which hung in the window; he pulled it down - the glass had been cut previously; he ran and dropped this handkerchief, and I pursued and took him.


                  ELIZA 
                  TAYLOR
               



            . I saw the prisoner take the handkerchief - I called my brother.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 12. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
