








                     Humphry 
                     Jones 
                  



                
             and Ann his Wife
, of St. Ann in Westminster



 were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 5 Window Curtains, 4 Bed Curtains and Vallains, 2 Sheets and a pair of Blankets
            , the Goods of 
               
                  Samuel 
               



             on the 1st of February
 last. It appeared that the Prisoners lodged in the Prosecutor's House and took the Goods, which were found where they had sold them. 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Jones 
                  



                
             in her Defence said that she was obliged to carry them by her Husband. The Jury considering the matter 

               Acquitted
             her, and found Humphrey Jones 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
