







                     Mary 
                     Doe 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles in the Fields


 was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 70 yards of Ferreting out of the Shop
             of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Rowe 
                  




                
             the 10th of February
 last. The Prosecutor deposed the Prisoner came into her Shop on Shrove-tuesday last, pretending to buy 3 yards of Black Sixpenny Ribbon, and took part of three pieces of Ferret. Another Evidence deposed that she was in the Shop when the Prisoner came in, and saw her take the Goods and put them in her Pocket. When she was carried before the Justice she would not own her Name, but said she was Mrs. No Name. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
