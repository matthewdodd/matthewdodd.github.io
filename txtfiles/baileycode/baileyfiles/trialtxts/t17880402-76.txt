





328. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WEST
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2d of April
, three iron bars, value 3 s. belonging to 
                     
                        
                           James 
                           Morris
                        




                     
                  , affixed to his dwelling house
            .
The prisoner was seen taking the bars.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
