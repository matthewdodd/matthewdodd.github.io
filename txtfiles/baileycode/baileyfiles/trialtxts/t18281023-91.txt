






2044. 
               
                  GEORGE 
                  CURTIS
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of September

, 1 saddle, value 2l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Leonard 
                  Briggs
               



            : - also for 


               stealing, on the 29th of September

, 1 set of harness, value 3l. 10s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  William 
                  Gullock
               



            .
To which indictments the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 25.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
