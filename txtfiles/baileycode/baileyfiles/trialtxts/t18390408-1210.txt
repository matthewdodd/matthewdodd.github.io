



1210. 




JANE BRODERICK
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
18th of March, 3 gowns, value 17s.; 1 cloak, value 6s.; 1 shawl, value 1s. 6d.; and 1 apron, value 6d.; the goods of 



Mary Ann Hayward; and 1 pair of boots, value 4s.; 1 pair of stockings, value 3d.; and 1 apron, value 3d.; the goods of 



Abraham Hayward, her master; to' which she pleaded





GUILTY. Aged 15.—


Transported for Seven Years—Penitentiary.
