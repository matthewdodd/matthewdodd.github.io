







                     Mary 
                     Higgins 
                  



               
            , of St. Mildred in the Poultry


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silver Taster value 14s. in the shop of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Kendal 
                        




 on the 14th  of May
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner came into the Prosecutor's Shop for some Pickled Cucumbers, and took the Taster and went away with it; but being follow'd, was taken with it upon her. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            . 


               Transportation
