





SECOND DAY, FRIDAY, OCTOBER 24.
Fourth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
2071. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  NEWLAND
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of September
, 1 toast-rack, value 2s.
            , the goods of The Honourable


                  Robert Henry 
                  Clive
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 44.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
