





2271. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     INCE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th of September
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Josiah 
                           Saxon
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOSIAH 
                  SAXON
               



            . I live in Park-street, Regent's-park. On the 25th of September I was on Holborn-hill


, and felt something press against my pocket, and on turning seized the prisoner, with my handkerchief in his hand - he was alone; I saw him let it fall, and took it up - I took him to the watch-house.


                  JOHN 
                  MARCHANT
               



            . I am a constable. I received the prisoner with the handkerchief.(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  JAMES 
                  BRANNAN
               



            . I was on Holborn-hill, about three yards from the prosecutor, and saw the prisoner take the handkerchief out of his pocket; he dropped it on the pavement - the prosecutor picket it up; I assisted in taking him to the watch-house.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 22. - 


               Transported for Life
            .
