





789. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     SMITH
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 6th of July
, eleven handkerchiefs, value 11 s. the property of certain persons to the jurors unknown, from their persons
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            , aged 64.




               Transported for Life
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
