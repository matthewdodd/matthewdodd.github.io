



98. 




EDWARD OREEN
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
5th of November, 1 handkerchief, value 5s., the goods of 



Hugh William Ruel, from his person.






HUGH WILLIAM RUEL
. I am a refiner, and live in Broad-street, Bloomsbury. About six o'clock in the evening of 5th of November I was in Holborn—I had a handkerchief in my coat pocket—I felt it go from my coat pocket—J turned round, and. saw the prisoner crossing the road—I went after him, and charged him with taking my handkerchief from my pocket—he said he had not, but a boy had—I took his collar—he let the handkerchief fall—I took it up, and gave him into custody.

Prisoner's Defence. I was crossing the road, and dragged the handkerchief with my foot.




GUILTY
.* Aged 20.—


Transported for Ten Years.
