





550. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     BROWN
                  



               
             was indicted, for that 


               he, on the 9th of May
, feloniously, knowingly, wittingly, and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession, a forged bank-note, for payment of 2 l. and another for the payment of 1 l
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
