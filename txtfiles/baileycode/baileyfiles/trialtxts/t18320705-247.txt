





1673. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BOWLES
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 23rd of June
, 1 watch, value 30s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Benjamin 
                     Holley
                  




               
            .


                  BENJAMIN 
                  HOLLEY
               



            . I am a sweep
. The prisoner was employed by me on the 23rd of June; I left him in bed, and my watch was in my bed-room; when I returned it was gone - I found him on the Brentford road; he was secured.


                  JAMES 
                  ALDOUS
               



            . I am a pawnbroker, and live in Berwick-street, Soho. I received this watch from the prisoner on the 23rd of June, in the name of 
               
                  John 
                  Bowles
               



            .


                  HUGH 
                  SANDILAND
               



             (Police-constable T 80). I took the prisoner, and found the duplicate on him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
