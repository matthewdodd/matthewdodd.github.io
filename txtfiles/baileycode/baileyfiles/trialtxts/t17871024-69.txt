





881. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     PEAKE
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of September
, a stuff petticoat, value 3 s. two yards of silk ribbon, value 9 d. half a yard of linen cloth, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  Sarah 
                  Ashton
               




            .
The prisoner was taken with the things.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the first Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
