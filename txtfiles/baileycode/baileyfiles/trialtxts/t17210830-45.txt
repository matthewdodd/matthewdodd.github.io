







                     Frances 
                     Barker 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Silver Watch, value 4 l. and 11 s. in Money, from the Person of 
                     
                        
                           Edward 
                           Price 
                        




. on the 19th of July
 last. It appeared that the Prosecutor being out late, went to St. Ann's Round-House rather than disturb the Family where he lodg'd, and that the Prisoner told him she would carry him to a Place where he might lie on a Bed; that she did accordingly; but when he waked he mist his Watch and Money. That the Prisoner confest the Fact when taken; which Confession was read in Court. The Jury considering the matter, found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d
            .
