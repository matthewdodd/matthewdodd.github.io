





548. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     DARDIS
                  



               
             was indicted, for that 


               he, knowingly, wittingly, and without lawful excuse, having in his custody and possession, two forged bank-notes, and one forged Bank 2 l. note; he knowing them to be forged
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
