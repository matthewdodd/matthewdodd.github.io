                     SAMUEL 
                     SANDYS
                  



               
             and 
               
                  
                     MICHAEL 
                     BRADBURY
                  



               
             were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing on the 21st day of November
 last, one linen handkerchief, value 1 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Richard 
                     Cole
                  




               
            .


                  Gabriel
                   Johnson
               



             saw one of the prisoners pick the prosecutor's pocket, and give the handkerchief to the other.
(The handkerchief deposed to.)
Prisoners. We have nothing to say.
BOTH 

               GUILTY
            .
Each 



               transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
