








                     Katherine 
                     Perry 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 25 Yards of Stuff, and other Goods, value 36 s. in the Dwelling-house of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Baker 
                        



, the 1st of May

 last. She was also indicted a Second Time for 


               feloniously stealing a Diamond Ring, value 12 s. and 2 Gold Rings, value 11 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Partington 
                  



                
            . The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
