





847. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JOHNSON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of May
, 3 pair of trousers, value 11s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Mills
                  




               
            .


                  CHARLES 
                  MILLS
               



            . I am brother of 
               
                  John 
                  Mills
               



            , he lives in Oxford-street


. On the evening of the 9th of May, I was told by a woman that some boy
s were stealing some trousers - I ran to the door, and saw the prisoner take one pair from the door, and give them to another boy, who went off - I caught the prisoner, but could not take the other.


                  MARY 
                  CARY
               



            . I saw the prisoner and another lad, but I did not see them take any thing - I told what I suspected.


                  JOHN 
                  CRAWLEY LITTLE
               



             (police-constable D 74). I took the prisoner, and found this check shirt in his hat.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 20. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
