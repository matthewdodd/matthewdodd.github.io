





978. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HOLYLAND, alias 
NEAL





               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 18th of November
, one ring, value 2l. 6s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Mayfield
                  



               
            , sen. and 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Mayfield
                  



               
            , jun.

SECOND COUNT, the same, only stating it to be the property of Thomas Mayfield, jun.


                  THOMAS 
                  MAYFIELD
               



            , JUN. I am a jeweller

, in partnership with 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Mayfield
               



            , sen. The prisoner was apprenticed to my father; after which he worked as journeyman
. I missed several rings, which I found pledged at Fleming's-the prisoner had given me a list of places where he had pledged them.


                  ALEXANDER 
                  MILL
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Fleming, pawnbroker, of Newgate-street. On the 18th of November, the prisoner pledged a mourning-ring for 18s. with me. I have known him four years.
(Property produced and sworn to.)


                  HOWEL 
                  GODDARD
               



            . I am a constable-on the 29th of December, I took the prisoner into custody. He told Mr. Mayfield where he had pledged the property.
Prisoner. I beg for mercy.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 26.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
