





128. (L.) 
               
                  Frederick 
                  Young 
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing a woollen cloth coat, value 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Hardey 
                  




                
            , January 17
. ||


                  Samuel 
                  Hardey 
               



            . I am ostler
 at the King's-arms, in Leadenhall-street


: the prisoner was taken by 
               
                  John 
                  Alcock 
               



            , going out of the stable with my coat. He brought him and that back; it is the same I now have on.


                  John 
                  Alcock 
               



            . I seeing the prisoner come out of the stable, with Hardey's coat, pursued him; he dropped it, and ran; and I took and brought him back.
Prisoner's Defence.
I hope you will take it in consideration; it was for want that I took it.




               Guilty, 10 d.
