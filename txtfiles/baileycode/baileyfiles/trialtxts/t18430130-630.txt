




630. 




JOHN GREGORY
 was indicted for

stealing, on the 
21st of Dec, 45 knives, value 3l.; 6 forks, 2s. 6d.; and 5 pairs of scissors, 4s. 6d.; the goods of 



Richard Benbow Bradley: and 





THOMAS PEPPER



for feloniously receiving the same, well knowing them to have been stolen; against the Statute, &c.; to which


GREGORY pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 18.—



Confined One Month.


PEPPER pleaded 


GUILTY
. Aged 48.—


Transported for Seven Years.




Before Mr. Justice Williams.
