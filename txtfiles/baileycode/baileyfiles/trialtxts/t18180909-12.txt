





1073. 
               
                  
                     JOSEPH 
                     SAUNDERS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 9th of July
, two coats, value 15s.; two table-spoons, value 20s.; one desert-spoon, value 7s., and four teaspoons, value 8s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Peter 
                  Wallis
               




            .
To which indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 38.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Sergeant.
