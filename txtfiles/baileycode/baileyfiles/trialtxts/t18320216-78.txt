





583. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     HULL
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 20th of January
, 2 lbs. weight of bacon, value 1s. 2d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry Francis 
                     Piagett
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  FRANCIS PIAGETT
               



            . I am a cheesemonger
, and live in Whitecross-street


. On the evening of the 20th of January I was going towards my shop, and saw two persons near it - I had been robbed before, and I watched them; I saw one of them make a snatch at the window - they both ran; I pursued, and took the prisoner with this bacon, which is mine.
Prisoner. I was out of work.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
