





1436. 
               
                  SAMUEL 
                  MORGAN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of May
, 1 milk-kettle, value 6d.; 2 shillings. 2 sixpences, 3 pence, and 12 halfpence
            , the property of 
               
                  Ann 
                  Miller
               




            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
