



1970. 




THOMAS WATKINS
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
19th of May, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 




John Philpott, from his person.






JOHN PHILPOTT
. I am a butcher, and live at Deptford. I was at Greenwich fair on the 19th of May, and lost my handkerchief—this is it.





RICHARD COLLARD
 (police-constable B 110.) I was at the fair at a quarter-past seven o'clock in the evening of Sunday, the 19th of May—I saw the prisoner take this handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket—I took it from him.





JAMES FOWLER
 (police-constable B 84.) I was on duty with Collard—I saw the prisoner go up to the prosecutor, who was standing looking at a caravan of beasts, and take this handkerchief from him.

Prisoner's Defence. I went into the crowd, boy-like, and picked up the handkerchief, not knowing the owner.




GUILTY
. Aged 16.—


Transported for Ten Years.
