





824. 
               
                  SAMUEL 
                  HEARN
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 1st of April
, 1 pair of breeches, value 9s., and 1 hat, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  David 
                  Davis
               




            .
DAVID DAVIS. I am in the service of Mr. Sparke's, a cow-keeper. I lost a pair of breeches and a hat on the 1st of April - I had seen them safe at half-past seven o'clock the evening before.


                  WALTER 
                  OWEN HILLS
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. This pair of breeches were pawned on the 1st of April, about nine o'clock in the evening, in the name of John Bennett, by the prisoner.


                  ELIZABETH 
                  JONES
               



            . I bought this hat of the prisoner on the 1st of April, between eight and nine o'clock in the evening.


                  JAMES 
                  GRIBBLE
               



            . I apprehended the prisoner. He told me he had sold the hat and pawned the breeches - he told me the places - he is well-known in our neighbourhood.


                  WILLIAM 
                  BARNET
               



            . The prisoner asked me to go down with him and take the things - he gave them to me - I did not know they were stolen - he went and pawned the breeches and sold the hat.(Property produced and sworn to).
Prisoner. It was him who pawned the breeches - I took them and gave them to him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
