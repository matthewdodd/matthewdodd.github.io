





597. 
               
                  William 
                  Green 
               



            , was indicted for 


               stealing one piece of stuff damask, val. 40 s.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Shepherd 
                  




                
            , July the 5th
.


                  William 
                  Shephard 
               



            . I live in Spittle-fields


, and keep a mercer's shop
; I had been out, coming home about one o'clock in the day, I saw the prisoner at the bar walking about my door, looking hard into my shop; as I came near, I saw him go in; he took one piece of Saxon green damask, he put it into his apron, and ran away with it; I ran round a cart and took hold on him, I brought him back to my shop, with the goods upon him.
Another witness depos'd he saw Mr. Shephard take the piece of stuff out of the prisoner's Apron.




               Guilty 4 s. 10 d.
