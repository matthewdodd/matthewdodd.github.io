                     JAMES
                      WATSON
                  , otherwise 
DUNNY JEMMY




            , was indicted for 


               stealing four cheeses, weighing 28 lb. value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas
                      Carpenter
                  




               
            , Feb. 14
. ++
An interpreter was sworn, who interpreted by signs.


                  Ann
                   Carpenter
               



            , the wife of the prosecutor, deposed, that four cheeses were stole out of a shop at St. Mary Hill, belonging to her husband, in the night of the 14th of February.


                  George
                   Wade
               



             deposed, that he stopt the prisoner between one and two o'clock in the morning, of the 15th of February, with four cheeses. (They were produced in court and deposed to by the prosecutrix ).
The prisoner, in his defence, signifyed that the cheeses were given to him.



               Guilty
