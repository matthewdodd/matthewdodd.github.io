







                     Mary 
                     Cope , alias 
Hannah Banks, alias 
Hannah Hill




               
            , of St. Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               breaking open the House of 
                     
                        
                           Peter 
                           Wattis 
                        




                      
                   in the night time, and taking from thence a silver Cup, value 50 s. and other things
            , on the 12th of March
last. Peter Wattis depos'd, that the prisoner was hir'd as his Servant
 on Saturday, and on the Monday following went away with the Goods, and left the doors open; that she was apprehended in the Mint, when he found the Cup and a Mug upon her: which 
               
                  Edward 
                  Vaughan 
               



             confirm'd. Her Confession was then read, in which she said; that about 4 in the morning she let in 
               
                  William 
                  Banks 
               



             her Husband, and believ'd he then took the Goods; for that afterwards he gave her the Plate to sell. The Jury found her 


               Guilty of felony only
