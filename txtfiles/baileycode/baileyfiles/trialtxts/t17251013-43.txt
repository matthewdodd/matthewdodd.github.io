







                     Nicolas 
                     Webb 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing 3 l. 5 s. the Money of Joseph Blower, in the House of 
                     
                        
                           Joseph 
                           Blower 
                        




, Aug. 19
. It appear'd that the Prisoner was Apprentice
 to the Prosecutor, a Turner
, in Beach Lane


; and while his Master was taking a Nap in the Chair, he took the Key of the Chamber-Door, went up Stairs, and finding the Keys in the Chest of Drawers, he opend them, took out the Money, came down again, and went away directly; and the next News they heard of him was, that he was put into Bridewell at Ware for breeding a Riot; and there he confess'd this Fact. 


               Guilty 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
