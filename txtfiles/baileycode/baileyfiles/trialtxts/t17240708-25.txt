







                     Mary 
                     Vickery 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchres


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Mary 
                           Ball 
                        




, the 5th of this present July
. The Prosecutor depos'd, she employ'd the Prisoner as a Chair Woman
, and she put the Ring into a Mortar, and she having scoured the Brass, the Ring was taken away; that taxing the Prisoner with it, she deny'd it; but she found the Ring in the Prisoner's Pocket. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
