





341. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     BRISTOW
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of December
, I pair of shoes, value 3s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Walter 
                     Pitmann
                  




               
            .


                  THOMAS 
                  ROBERTS
               



            . I am a painter. On the 30th of December, at half-past nine o'clock, I saw the prisoner and another, near Mr. Pitmann's shop; I watched them. and saw the prisoner go into the shop, and take a pair of shoes from the right-hand side of the door - I told the shopman, who ran and took him with the shoes in his possession; he was brought back and given to the officer.
WALTER PITMANN. I keep the shop
, but was out that night - when I came home my shopman told me of the circumstance; these shoes are mine.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 14.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
