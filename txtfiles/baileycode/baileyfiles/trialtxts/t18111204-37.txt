






41. 
               
                  
                     ELIZABETH 
                     BUTCHER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 16th of November
, a pewter pint pot, value 1 s. 2 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Joseph 
                     Halson
                  




               
            .


                  JOSEPH 
                  HALSON
               



            . I am a publican
, I keep the Mason's Arms in Oxford Market


.


                  THOMAS 
                  JONES
               



            . On the 16th of November I was going along Charles Street, Oxford Market, a woman pointed out this woman, I searched her and found four pots on her, I took her to the prosecutor.
Prosecutor. That is my pot.
Prisoner's Defence. I did not steal them, I have had beer in them at different times.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
