







                  Job 
                  Medison
               



             of the Parish of St. Andrews Holbourn


, was tryed for 


               stealing a Silver Cup value 3 l.
             from 
               
                  Isaac 
                  Hall
               




             on the 11th of June
 last. It was proved against him, That he had the Cup in his Custody, being stopt by a Smith, where he went to have it broke, and the Prosecutor swore that it was his Cup. The Prisoner said that he took it from a drunken Woman at Fleet-bridge, but could not prove it: So he was immediately found 

               guilty
             of the Felony.
