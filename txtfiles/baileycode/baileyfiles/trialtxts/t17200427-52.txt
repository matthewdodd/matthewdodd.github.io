







                     Elizabeth 
                     Walters 
                  



               
             of St. Paul's Covent-Garden


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Poplin Gown and Petticoat, &c.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Curtis 
                  




                
            . It appeared that the Prisoner came to the Prosecutor, desiring that she might be there till she could provide her self a Lodging, took the Goods and went away, but was found in Holbourn with the Clothes on, which she had wore ever since, and had on at her Tryal. The Jury found her 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
