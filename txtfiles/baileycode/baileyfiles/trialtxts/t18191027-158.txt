





1534. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     FLETCHER
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 8th of October
, one coat, value 25 s., and one gaiter, value 1 s.
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Newberry
                  




               
            .


                  JOHN 
                  NEWBERRY
               



            . I am a clothes salesman
, and live in Monmouth-street


; the coat and gaiters hung in the shop; about half-past six o'clock in the evening, I saw theprisoner take the coat from the door, ran out, and secured him. He had thrown it down.
(Property produced and sworn to.)



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
