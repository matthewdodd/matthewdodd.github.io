





221. 
               
                  
                     JAMES 
                     POWELL
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 29th of December
 last, a linen check shirt, value 2 s. two pair of worsted stockings, value 3 s. a pair of shoes, value 5 s. and a handkerchief, value 1 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Davis
                  




               
            .
The prisoner was taken with the things directly after they were missed.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
