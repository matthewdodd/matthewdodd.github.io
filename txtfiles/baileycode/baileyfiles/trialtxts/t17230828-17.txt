







                     Elizabeth 
                     Drummer 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of Stepney


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Linnen, value 14 s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Sheppard 
                  




                
            , the 12th of August
. The Prosecutor depos'd, the Prisoner had been her Nurse
, and took the Goods mentioned in the Indictment. The Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
