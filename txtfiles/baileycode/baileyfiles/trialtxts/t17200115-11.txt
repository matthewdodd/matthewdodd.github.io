







                     Mary

            , of St. James's Westminster


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Silver Watch value 20 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Dunn 
                  




                
             on the 8th of December
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner was Chare-Woman
 to the Prosecutor, and lay there two Nights, took the Watch which hung at his Beds-head and pawn'd it at an Alehouse for 22 s. she confest it before the Justice, and did not deny it on her Tryal, nor say any thing for her self. The Jury found her
