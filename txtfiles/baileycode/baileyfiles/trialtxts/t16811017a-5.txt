







                     Mary 
                     Marshal
                  




               
             was tryed for 


               stealing a Curral, with a Silver Chain, valued at twenty Shillings
            ; which being proved against her, she was 

               Convicted
             of the Felony; but not being above twelve years of age, though an old Offender, having been in Newgate before, for the like Robberies, she received Sentence with the rest; but notwithstanding, was reprieved after Judgment, for 


               Transportation
            .
