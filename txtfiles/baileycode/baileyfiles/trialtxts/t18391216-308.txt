



308. 




THOMAS SUTTON
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
12th of December, 37 lbs. weight of iron, value 1s. 3d., the goods of 



James Chitty; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






JAMES MOORE
. I am in the employ of James Chitty, a wheelwright, and live at Old Brentford. We have lost some old iron, which is here—some of the pieces I can swear to—I had this piece (looking at one) in my hand a week ago—some of it had been inside and some outside our shop—there is 37 lbs. weight of it—it is worth 1s. 3d.





JOHN SAWYER
. I am assistant to Mr. Soloman, a dealer in marinestores at Brentford End. On the 12th of December the prisoner came with this iron to sell—I sent for an officer, who took him.

Prisoner's Defence. I found it on a dung heap.





EDWARD JAMES LEVY
 (police-sergeant S 18.) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction which I got from Mr. Clark's office—(read)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.
