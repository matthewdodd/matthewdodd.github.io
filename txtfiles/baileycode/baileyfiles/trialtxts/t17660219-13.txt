





125. (L.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Stafford 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a frail, value 1 d. and 50 pounds weight of raisins, value 10 s.
             the property of persons unknown, February 9
. ++


                  Stephen 
                  Goodson 
               



            . I am a constable. Last Sunday was se'nnight 
               
                  John 
                  Miller 
               



            , a watchman, brought the prisoner to me, with a frail of raisins on his head; I asked the prisoner how he came by them, he said he stole them.


                  John 
                  Miller 
               



            . I am a watchman in Tower-street; I was in my box, the prisoner came by with a frail of raisins upon his head, I jumped out, and took him by the collar; he threw the frail on the ground, I called for assistance, and 
               
                  Joseph 
                  Richardson 
               



             came: we took him and the raisins to the constable.


                  Joseph 
                  Richardson 
               



             confirmed the account the last evidence gave, and likewise that of the prisoner's confession.
The prisoner said in his defence, he found the frail in the street.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
