





1064. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     CLAPP
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 17th of August
, ten yards and a half of cotton value 15s.
             the property of 
               
                  George 
                  Abell
               




            .


                  HENRY 
                  ABELL
               



            . I was in my brother's shop, and saw the prisoner come and take this cotton from the door; it was within two feet of the door. I pursued him; he dropped it; I never lost sight of him until he was stopped.



               GUILTY
            , aged 20.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
London Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
