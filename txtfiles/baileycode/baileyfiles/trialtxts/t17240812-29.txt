







                     Philip 
                     Zeman 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Botolph, Aldgate


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing Spectacles in the Shop of 
                     
                        
                           Elizabeth 
                           Mappleston 
                        




, the 28th of July
 last. The Prosecutor's Man depos'd, That the Prisoner came to his Mistress's Shop, and bought 6 Pair of Spectacles, and put other Spectacles into his Pocket besides, and went away with them, being pursu'd, he deny'd the having them, but being carried into the Shop, threw them down on the Counter. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
