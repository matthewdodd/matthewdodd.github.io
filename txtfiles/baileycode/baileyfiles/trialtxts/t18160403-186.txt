





472. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  RODD
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 6th of April
, a coat, value 30s.
             the property of 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Cussans
               




            .


                  JOHN 
                  GALE
               



            . I am footman to Mr. Cussans, who lives at 68, Harley-street. I saw the prisoner come down the area steps, and go into the pantry, and then he went up the steps again with this coat under his arm. I pursued him, crying stop thief, and he dropped the coat, but was brought back.



               GUILTY
            , aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
