







                     William 
                     Serjeant 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a Leather-Trace of a Coach
            , the Property of her Royal Highness the 
               
Princess
 Anne


            , the 26th of February
 last.
It appear'd by the Evidence, That her Highness's Coach waiting at the Play-House in Drury-Lane


, the Prisoner took an opportunity to steal a Spare Trace that had been put in the Coach Seat; the Fact being plainly prov'd, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
