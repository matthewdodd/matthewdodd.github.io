



819. 




DENNIS DOWNING
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
27th of Jan., 1 purse, value 3d.; 2 sovereigns, 2 half-severeigns, 1 half-crown, 36 shillings, and 10 sixpences; the property of 



Harriet Pryer; to which he pleaded




GUILTY
. * Aged 13.—


Transported for Seven Years—Convict Ship.
