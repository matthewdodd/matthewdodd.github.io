





27. 
               
                  
                     CATHERINE 
                     KEENE
                  




               
             was indicted for that 


               she, on the 26th of July
, had divers forged and counterfeit notes in her custody and possession
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            , aged 26.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
First Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
