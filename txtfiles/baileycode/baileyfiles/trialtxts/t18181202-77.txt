





75. 
               
                  
                     WALTER 
                     DUGGAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of November
, four cut glass salt-cellars, value 18 s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           Franklin
                        




                     
                  , privately in his shop
            .


                  JOHN 
                  RUMFITT
               



            . I am shopman to 
               
                  George 
                  Franklin
               



            , who is a glass and chinaman
, and lives in St. Martin's-lane


. On the 26th of November, about eight o'clock I went to the back of the shop - I turned round, saw the prisoner, and asked what he wanted, he asked for Mr. James, a tailor. I said he had taken something off the place, pointing to where I missed the salt-cellars, he saidhe had taken nothing. I found the four salt-cellars, one in each pocket.
(Property produced and sworn to.)
GUILTY. Aged 17.




               Of stealing, but not privately
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
First Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
