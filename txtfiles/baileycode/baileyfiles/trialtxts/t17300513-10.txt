







                  Lydea 
                  Deane 
               



            , of St. Giles's in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               privately stealing a Cloth Coat and Wastecoat in the Shop
             of 
               
                  
                     Lionel 
                     Chancey 
                  




                
            , the 18th of April
 last.
The Prosecutor depos'd, That standing at a Neighbour's Door opposite to his own, he observ'd the Prisoner to be in his Shop, and he suspecting her, seeing her go out of the Shop pursu'd and took her, and being apprehended, she let the Coat and Wastecoat fall, and begg'd for God sake that he would let her go, and she would never do the like again; the Jury found her 


               guilty to the Value of 4 s. 10 d.
