



1344. 




JAMES CARTER



, stealing 4 chisels, 3 gauges, and 1 plane, value 10s.; the goods of 



Thomas Alfred Lister, having been before convicted.






THOMAS ALFRED LISTER
. I saw my tools safe on 22d May—I misted them—these are them (produced),





WILLIAM STEWART
. I am a pawnbroker—these tools were pledged wish me—I produce the duplicate.





JOSEPH ELLIOTT
. I am a pawnbroker—these tools were pledged with me—I lost the ticket this morning.





WILLIAM HAROLD
 (policeman). I took the prisoner, and found these three duplicates on him—one corresponds with this produced—the one for the plane is dated 23d May.





THOMAS EGERTON
. I produce a certificate of the prisoner's conviction—(read—Convicted April, 1849, and confined six months)—I was present—he is the man. 


GUILTY
. Aged 31.—


Transported for Seven Years.

(There was another indictment against the prisoner.)
