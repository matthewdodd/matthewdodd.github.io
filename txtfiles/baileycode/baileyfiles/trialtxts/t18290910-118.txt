






1649. 
               
                  
                     SOLOMON 
                     BARLIENER
                  




               
             and 
               
                  
                     PHILIP 
                     LEVY
                  




               
             were indicted for 


               stealing, on the 2nd of September
, 6 watches, value 8l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Matthias 
                     Lassar
                  




               
            .
Barliener 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 27.
Levy 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 16.
