







                     Simon 
                     Sherlock 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing 30 Pound of Lead, and 6 Iron Bars
            , on the 1st of February
 last, which belonged to the City of London, (it being taken from the Place which was Pulling down adjoining to Newgate.)
Mr. Smith depos'd, That he being Overseer of the said Work, and hearing the Prisoner was stopt, selling the Lead, he went and took the Dimensions of the Place, from whence it was taken, and the Lead found on the Prisoner agreeing with the same; besides several other Circumstances. He was found 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d
            .
