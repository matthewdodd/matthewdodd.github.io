



382. 




FRANCIS BOUCHER
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
13th of December, 1 handkerchief, value 3s. 6d., the goods of 



Thomas Price Jones; and that he had been before convicted of felony.






SAMUEL TURNER
. I live with Mr. Davies, in Tothill-street, Westminster. On the 13th of December I was at our window, and saw the prisoner at Mr. Jones's door, putting something in his pocket—I went over and took the prisoner—I told him I thought he had taken something.





PRICE JONES BULLOCK
. I am in the service of Mr. Thomas Price Jones. About half-past ten o'clock in the morning of the 13th of Decem-ber,

the prisoner was brought into our shop by this witness—I saw him drop this handkerchief—I took it up—it is my master's.





WILLIAM CLIFTON
 (police-sergeant B 2.) I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former conviction, which I got at Westminster—(read)—I know he is the person.

Prisoner's Defence. In the morning I was in bed—my sister took my handkerchief and pawned it—I got up, and went to get it out. I waited in the shop for ten minutes or a quarter of an hour, and in coming out I knocked down the handkerchief with my hand.




GUILTY
.* Aged 16.—


Transported for Seven Years.
