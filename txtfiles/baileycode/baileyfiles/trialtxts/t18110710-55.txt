





573. 
               
                  
                     HENRY 
                     ASHTON
                  



               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 20th of May
, was clerk
 to 
                     
                        
                           Francis Molynew 
                           Ommaney
                        




                     
                  , and 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Druce
                        




                     
                  , and was employed and entrusted by them to receive monies and valuable securities for them; and that he being such clerk, so employed and entrusted, did receive a banker's draft of 102 l. 16 s. for, and on account of his said masters

, and that he afterwards did embezzle, secrete, and steal the same
            .
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .


                  HENRY 
                  ASHTON
               



             again indicted for receiving 84 l. and embezzling, secreting, and stealing the same.
To this charge the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .
AGAIN indicted for receiving 208 l. 3 s. 10 d. on the 7th of June, and afterwards secreting and stealing the same.
To this indictment the prisoner pleaded




               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
