





923. 
               
                  SARAH 
                  DRISCOLL
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 31st of March
, 3 drinking-glasses, value 1s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  John 
                  Gurney
               




            .
JOHN GURNEY. I keep the Rum Puncheon, in White-cross-street




. On the 31st of March, about six o'clock in the evening, the prisoner came in, and had half a pint of porter in a glass - she stopped about an hour and a half, then went out, and we missed three glasses; I followed her to the Asylum for the Houseless, in Playhouse-yard, and found three glasses on her, which are mine - one has my name on it.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 34. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
