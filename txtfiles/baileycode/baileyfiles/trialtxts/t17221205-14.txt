







                     Stephen 
                     Walker 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Stephen Colemanstreet


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing 73 Horse-Shoes, value 20 s. and 10 Pound of Iron, value 20 d. 
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Dart 
                  




                
            , the 6th of November
. Mr. Dart depos'd, He had lost Horse-Shoes at Times, about 70, and 6 Pieces of Iron, and that he had seen them at Mr. Simpson's. Mr. Simpson depos'd, The Prisoner brought them to him to sell, saying he had them in Colemanstreet, of one 
               
                  Charles 
                  Mainard 
               



            , who owing him Money for Service, gave him them to sell, to pay himself. The Prisoner pleaded the same at the Bar, but it was deny'd by 
               
                  Charles 
                  Mainard 
               



            . The Jury found him 

               Guilty
            . 


               Transportation
            .
