





First London Jury, before Mr. Recorder.
1494. 
               
                  
                     CHARLES 
                     MASON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of September
, 3 printed books, value 7s.; and one pair of clogs, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Cooms
                  




               
            ; to which he pleaded.




               GUILTY
            . Aged 60. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
