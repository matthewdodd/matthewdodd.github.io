






655. 
               
                  SCIPIO 
                  AFRICANUS
               



             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 5th day of July
 last, one cotton gown, value 2 s. one black silk cloak, value 12 d. two aprons, value 4 s. two half handkerchiefs, value 12 d. one shift, value 1 s. and one pair of robins, value 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Sarah 
                     Lever
                  




               
            , spinster
.
The prisoner pledged part of the property, and sold the other part.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. ROSE.
