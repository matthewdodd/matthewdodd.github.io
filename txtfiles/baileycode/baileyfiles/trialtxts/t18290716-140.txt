





Second London Jury. - Before Mr. Sergeant 
               Arabin
            .
1514. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MASTERS
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of March
, 15 silver spoons, value 5l.; 13 silver forks, value 5l.; 3 shirts, value 1l.; 3 table-cloths, value 10s.; 1 pair of breeches, value 5s.; 1 pair of pantaloons, value 5s., and 1 pair of boots, value 10s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           Richard Baker 
                           Wingfield
                        




                     
                  , his master
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
