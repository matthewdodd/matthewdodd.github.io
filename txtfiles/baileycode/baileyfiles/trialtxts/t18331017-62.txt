





NEW COURT. Friday, October 18, 1833.
Second Middlesex Jury, before Mr. Common Serjeant.
1468. 
               
                  
                     ROBERT 
                     BLAKIE
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of October
, 1 watch, value 30s.; 1 seal, value 10s.; and 1 watch-key, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Ann 
                     Lamb
                  




               
            , to which he pleaded




               GUILTY
            . Aged 29. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
