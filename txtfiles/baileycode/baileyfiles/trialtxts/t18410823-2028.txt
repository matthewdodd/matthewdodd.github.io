




2028. 




JOHN SMITH
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
14th of July, 2 coats, value 12s.;1 gown, value 2s.; 2 handkerchiefs, value 1s. 6d.; 2 pinafores, value 6d.; 2 frocks, value 6d.; and 1 pair of trowsers, value 6d.; the goods of 



Thomas Walsh; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
.* Aged 16—


Transported for Seven Years.—Convict Ship.
