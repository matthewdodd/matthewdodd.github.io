





1648. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     CHARLTON
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 19th of August
, 2 shifts, value 2s.; 2 pinafores, value 1s.; 2 aprons, value 6d.; 1 sheet, value 6d.; 1 pillow-case, value 1d.; 2 towels, value 6d.; 2 bed-gowns, value 1s.; 1 table-cloth, value 1s.; 1 napkin, value 3d.; 1 shirt, value 2d., and 1 pocket, value 3d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Sharpe
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            . Aged 10.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
