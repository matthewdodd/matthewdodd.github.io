



1604. 




JOHN THOMPSON
 was again indicted

 for stealing, on the 
26th of April, 1 portmanteau, value 8s., the goods of 



John James.






GEORGE DAVIES
 (police-constable C 33.) I met the prisoner on the 26th of April carrying this portmanteau—he was turning out of Oxford-street into Berwick-street—I saw him offer it for sale for half-a-crown—he then went to a pawnbroker's, and offered it there for 6s.—I then took him.





WILLIAM STAITE BREWER
. I am shopman to John James. This portmanteau is his, and was taken from the front of his shop—I missed it about half-past four o'clock on the 26th of April.




GUILTY
. Aged 19.—


Transported for Seven Years.
