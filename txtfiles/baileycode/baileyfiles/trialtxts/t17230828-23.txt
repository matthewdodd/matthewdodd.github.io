







                     John 
                     Price 
                  



               
            , of St. Giles's Cripplegate


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Half an Ounce of Gold Waste
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Bassam 
                  




                
            , the 17th of July
 last. It appear'd by the Evidence, That the Prisoner work'd as a Journeyman
 with the Prosecutor, who was a Silver Spinner
, and was seen to take the Goods, and being afterwards apprehended, the Gold Waste was found upon him. 


               Guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
