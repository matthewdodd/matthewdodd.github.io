





324. 
               
                  
                     GEORGE 
                     NASH
                  



               
             was indicted 


               for feloniously stealing, on the 10th of April

 last, two cotton gowns, value 40 s. one box, value 2 d. and one linen wrapper, value 3 d.
             the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Lackin
                  




               
            .



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second London Jury before Mr. RECORDER.
