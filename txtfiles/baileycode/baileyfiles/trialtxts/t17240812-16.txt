







                     Francis 
                     Wetherel 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Clement Danes


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing Tea, and Chocolate
            , the Property of 
               
                  
                     David 
                     Davis 
                  




                
            , the 23d of July
 last. The Prosecutor depos'd, He lost his Tea, and 
               
                  James 
                  Spooner 
               



            , his Servant, depos'd, That he met with the Prisoner at the Castle and Falcon, and he claimed Acquaintance with him, telling him he was his Countryman, and he knew his Parents very well, and must needs be his Cousin, and he would come and see him; and he did come several Times, and took away Goods at several Times, sometimes Cloves, and sometimes other Goods, and threatned if he told of it he would murder him, and promised to help him to a good Place; and that Morning he came, and took the Tea out of the Canister with his Hand, and put it into his Pocket. The Prisoner call'd several Persons to his Reputation: The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
