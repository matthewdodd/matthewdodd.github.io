





1564. 
               
                  JAMES 
                  PARKER
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 16th of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of a 
                     man unknown


                   from his person
            .


                  ANGELIOUS 
                  BERTRAUN
               



            . I was in Bond-street


, near the corner of Brook-street, on the 16th of July, and saw the prisoner with a companion, who took this handkerchief from the pocket of a gentlemen who gave his name as"Mr. Bright, Chandos-street;" he gave the prisoner the handkerchief, and I ran and took hold of him; I went to Chandos-street, but could not find the gentleman.
Prisoner's Defence. I was returning home - a young man asked me to lend him 6d., and said he would leave this handkerchief with me till morning; the constable came up, and asked me where I got it - I told him, and he made me go to two gentlemen, whom he asked if they had lost their handkerchiefs - they said No, and then he said some one had.
ANGELIOUS BERTRAUN. This is false - the gentleman gave me his address, but I could not find him.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 16.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
