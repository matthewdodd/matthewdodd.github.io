





NEW COURT. (1st DAY.)
Third Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1412. 
               
                  WILLIAM 
                  JOHNSON
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 30th of June
, 1 shawl, value 14s.; 3 sovereigns, and 1 half-sovereign
            , the property of 
               
                  Mary 
                  Johnson
               




            , widow
.
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
             Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
