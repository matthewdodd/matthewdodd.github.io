





540. (L.) 
               
                  
                     ISAAC
                      AUSTIN
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen handkerchief, value 10 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Thomas
                      Willoughby
                  




               
            , June 11th
. ++


                  Thomas
                   Willoughby
               



            . Coming up Holborn


 on the 11th of June, I felt a hand in my pocket; I turned round and saw the prisoner give my handkerchief to another person; I immediately secured him.
Prisoner's Defence.
I know nothing of the matter.



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
