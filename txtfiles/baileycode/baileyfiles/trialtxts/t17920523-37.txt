





256. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     JONES
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 25th day of May
, one linen sheet, value 5 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Henckell
                  




               
            .


                  MARY
                   HENCKELL
               



             sworn.
I am the wife of 
               
                  John
                   Henckell
               



            . I lost a sheet the 25th of May: I lost a sheet off the bed: the prisoner lodged at my house; this was the first night; I missed it in the morning.
Mr. BRAY sworn.
I am a publican: I keep the Horse and Groom at Hampstead


: the prosecutor is my tenant. On Friday, the 25th of May, I saw the prisoner come out of Henckell's house; Mrs. Henckell came after him in about a minute, and said that man had stolen a sheet: I followed him, and found the sheet wrapped round his body.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the second Middlesex Jury before Mr. COMMON SERJEANT.
