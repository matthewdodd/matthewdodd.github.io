





17. (M.) 
               
                  Nathanael 
                  Sharrard 
               



            , was indicted for 


               stealing one Panel Saw, value 7 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Robert 
                     Abby 
                  




                
            , Nov. 16
. ++
The prosecutor was a journeyman Carpenter
 at work at the corner of Plow-court, White-chapel


; he had lost his coat Nov. the 14th, from out of the workshop, the time he was at dinner; so set a little boy to watch while they dined the 16th of Nov. the boy saw the prisoner go up and take the saw and button it under his coat, he run to the prosecutor at the next door, who took the prisoner with it upon him.



               Guilty
            .
