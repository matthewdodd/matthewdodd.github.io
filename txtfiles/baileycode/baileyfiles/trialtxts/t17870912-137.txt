





806. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     PORTER
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 26th of July
 last, two cheeses, value 11 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     James 
                     Clarke
                  




               
            .


                  James
                   Clarke
               



             saw the prisoner come out of the prosecutor's shop with two cheeses.



               GUILTY
            .




               Transported for seven years
            .
Tried by the London Jury before Mr. ROSE.
