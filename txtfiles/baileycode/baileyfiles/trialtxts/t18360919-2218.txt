




2218. 



WILLIAM BILLINGS
 was indicted

 for stealing, 4 planes, value 6s. the goods of 



James Henry Beagent;


also for stealing, on the 
13th of September, 1 flannel waistcoat, value 2s.; and 1 glass cloth, value 9d.; the goods of 



Valentine Morris; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. 


Transported for Seven Years.

Before Mr. Recorder.
