





1783. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HEWITT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 13th of August
, 1 feather bed, value 2l. 10s.; 3 blankets, value 9s.; 2 sheets, value 5s.; 1 candlestick, value 1s. 6d., and 1 flat-iron, value 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Craft
                  




               
            .


                  SARAH 
                  CRAFT
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  John 
                  Craft
               



             - we live in Gloucester-court, Whitecross-street


. I let a lodging to the prisoner's wife - he lived there with her; they came in June, and left in August - they owed one week's rent - I missed the articles stated, and have only found one pair of sheets.


                  CHRISTOPHER 
                  DEER
               



            . I am a pawnbroker. I have two sheets pawned by a female, in the name of Davies.


                  ROBERT 
                  LOCK
               



            . I am a constable. The prisoner was taken before the Alderman, but being a county charge, it was given to me - I asked where the woman's bed was, and he said, "It is where you can't get it;" I found the duplicate of these sheets at his lodging.
Prisoner's Defence. I did not have them.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 19.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
