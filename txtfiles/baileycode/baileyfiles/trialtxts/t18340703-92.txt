





983. 
               
                  ROBERT 
                  MAY
               




             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 15th of June
, 1 handkerchief, value 3s., the goods of 
                     
                        Richard 
                        Dutton
                     




                  , from his person
            .
RICHARD DUTTON. I am a wool-broker
. I was in the new opening to London bridge


, on the evening of the 15th of June - I felt a touch at my pocket - I turned and saw the prisoner - I knocked him down, with my handkerchief in his hand - I took him to the Mansion-house, and gave charge of him - this is my handkerchief.
Prisoner's Defence. I was coming over London bridge, and this gentleman knocked me down - he hit me three or four times, and kicked me - I knew nothing of the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 17. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
