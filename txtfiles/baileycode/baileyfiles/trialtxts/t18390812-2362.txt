



2362. 




SAMUEL CLARKE
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
16th of August, 1 gown, value 4s.; and 1 shirt, value 4s.; the goods of 



Richard Senior.






RECECCA SENIOR
. I am the wife of Richard Senior, and live in Prospect-place, Kentish-town. On Thursday morning I missed a gown and sheet from the garden—these are them—(looking at them.)





EDWIN DANIEL
. At half-past seven o'clock on Thursday, I was in a field at the back of the prosecutor's house with a boy named Parker, and saw the prisoner leaning against the garden fence with a basket by his side—in a short time we saw him get over the fence—Parker ran round to tell Mrs. Senior—I watched the prisoner, and saw him put the gown into his basket and unpin something else—I gave information to the officer—he heard the officer coming over the fence, and threw the basket into the ditch, and laid down by the side of it.





WILLIAM RAMSAY
. I am a policeman. I received information; and went after the prisoner—the basket was produced from the ditch with the gown and sheet in it.




GUILTY
. *—Aged 31. 


Transported for Seven Years.
