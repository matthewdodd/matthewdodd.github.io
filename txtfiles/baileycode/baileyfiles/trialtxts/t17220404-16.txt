







                     Elizabeth 
                     Fen 
                  



               
            , of St. Martin in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing out of the House of William Cowden, a Gown, value 20 s. two Table Clocks, value 6 s. a Sheet, 5 s. and other Things
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Cowden 
                  




                
            , on the 24th of March
last. It appear'd that the prisoner was a Servant
