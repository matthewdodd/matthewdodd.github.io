







                     John 
                     Harris 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing three Books, value 12s.
             the Property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Bradly 
                  




                
            .
The Prosecutor depos'd, That he keeping a Place to sell Books
 in Bow-lane, Cheapside


, the Prisoner took the Books mention'd in the Indictment from off his Stall, and ran away with them; and that he being taken, he himself found the Books upon him, which being plain Evidence, the Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
