







                     Sarah 
                     Gray 
                  



               
            , was indicted for 


               stealing a Wastecoat val. 10 s. three Pair of Stockings val. 10 s. and seven Shirts val. 50 s. the Goods of 
                     
                        
                           Robert 
                           Watford 
                        




                      
                  , in the House of 
                     
                        John 
                        Gerrard 
                     



, Sept. 1
. It appear'd that the Prisoner came to Mr. Gerrard's House in Budge-Row


, (with a Letter from a Convict in Newgate, who lay for Transportation) and found Means to go off with the Goods in the Indictment. She was pursued, and taken in Newgate-Street with the Cloaths upon her. 


               Guilty 39 s.
            



               Transportation
            .
