





196. (M. 1st.) 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth
                      Chappel
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing a linen shirt, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     David
                      Reddle
                  




               
            , Jan. 26
. ++


                  Elizabeth
                   Reddle
               



            . I am wife to 
               
                  David
                   Reddle
               



            . The prisoner was our servant
. She took this shirt backwards to wash, on the 26th of January. She put on her cloak and went out, and that was missing. I went to enquire at the pawnbroker's after it, and found it at Mr. Gordon's in Holloway-lane.
Mr. Gordon. I believe it was the prisoner pledged this shirt with me the 26th of January, for 2 s.




               Guilty 10 d.
            



               T
            .
