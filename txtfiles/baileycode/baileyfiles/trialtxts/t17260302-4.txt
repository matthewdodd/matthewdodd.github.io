            , Feb. 12
. John Temple pleaded Guilty. The Witnesses thus depos'd Mrs Hawkins going up Stairs with a lighted Candle, about 7 at Night, she met Temple with her Bed at his Back, Have a Care Mistress, says he Yes you Rogue, says she, I shall take care of you, and calling some Neighbours to her Assistance, she secur'd him. Phenix was stopt in the Street by Mr. Lake, who saw him come out of the Prosecutors House, with 2 Pillows, a Quilt, and a blanket under his Arm.
