







                     John 
                     Harris 
                  



               
            , of the Parish of St. Sepulchre


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing a pair of flaxen Sheets, value 7 s.
             the Goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Whiteside 
                  




                
            , the 22d of December
 last. It appear'd that he Prisoner came to the Prosecutor's, at the Windmill-Inn in St. John's Street


, and having lodg'd there one Night, in the Morning carried away the Sheets. The Jury found him 


               guilty to the Value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
