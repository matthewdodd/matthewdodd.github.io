






167. 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Morse 
                  



               
             , was indicted for 


               stealing seven pounds weight of beef, value 18d. and seventeen pounds weight of beef, value 3 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Banbrow 
                  



                
            , Jan. 22

.
It appeared that the Prisoner had broke open several shops in Clare-Market



 Market-House, but being discovered by the watch he threw down his basket of meat and run for it, and he was pursued and taken.



               Guilty
            .
He was also indicted for 


               stealing an iron cleaver, value 12 d. and twelve pounds weight of beef, value 2 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Edward 
                     Parker 
                  



                
            , but he was not tried on this indictment.
