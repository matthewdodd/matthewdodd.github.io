





2082. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     WRIGHT
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of August
, 1 handkerchief, value 2s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           William 
                           Cumming
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  WILLIAM 
                  CUMMING
               



            . I am a carpet-manufacturer
. On the 20th of August, after twelve o'clock at night, I was in Picket-street


; I felt a pull at my pocket; I turned, and saw the prisoner throw my handkerchief to another young man - I seized it, and secured the prisoner.


                  WILLIAM 
                  SMITH
               



             (Police-constable F 129). I took the prisoner.
Cross-examined by MR. DONNE. Q. What did he say? A. He said he had done it for a meal.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner's Defence. There were several persons before and behind me; this gentleman said I had picked his pocket, but I had not done it. 

               GUILTY
            . Aged 18. - 


               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
