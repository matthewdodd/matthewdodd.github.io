





42. (L.) 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Wells 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing one silk handkerchief, val. 6 d.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Edw 
                     Upton 
                  




                
            , Nov. 14
. ++


                  Edward 
                  Upton 
               



            . Nov. 14, I was in Cheapside


. I felt somebody at my pocket. I missed my handkerchief; the prisoner crossed the way; I followed him into Bow-lane; there he flung my handkerchief at me. (Produced in court, and deposed to.)



               Guilty
