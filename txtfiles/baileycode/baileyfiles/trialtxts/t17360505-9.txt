





10. 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Field 
                  



               
            , of St. Botolph Aldgate


 was indicted for 


               stealing twenty Yards of Silk Ferret
            , value 2s. the Goods of 
               
                  
                     William 
                     Richardson 
                  




                
            , March 29
.
The Prisoner came into the Prosecutors's Shop to buy Ferret; she took the Goods mentioned, and went out of the Shop; but being observed, she was followed and taken with the Goods upon her. 


               Guilty 10d.
