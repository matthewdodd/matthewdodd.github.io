



1325. 




ROBERT SPENCER
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
22nd of April, 1 handkerchief, value 1s., the goods of 



Alfred Henry Maynard, from his person






ALFRED HENRY MAYNARD
. I am a butcher. I was at Greenwich fair on the 20th of April—about seven o'clock in the evening there was a great crowd—a policeman said I had my pocket picked, and I found my handkerchief was gone—he pointed the prisoner out, and produced my handkerchief—the prisoner begged for mercy.





DANIEL REARDON
. I am a policeman. I was at the fair—I saw the prisoner draw this handkerchief from the prosecutor's pocket—he was in company with two boys of his own age—he was putting the handkerchief into his own pocket—I took him with it in his hand.




GUILTY
. Aged 13—


Transported for Seven Years.
