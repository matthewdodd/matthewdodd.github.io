





390. (M.) 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth
                      Gargle
                  



               
            , spinster
, (a black) was indicted for 


               stealing one muslin apron, value 10 s. one laced muslin handkerchief, value 2 s. and one pair of laced muslin double ruffles, value 20 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Jane
                      Walker
                  




               
            , spinster
, January 27
. ++


                  Jane
                   Walker
               



            . I lodge at Mrs. Askews; the prisoner was her servant
; I brought the things mentioned in the indictment and left them in the shop; Mrs. Askew and the prisoner were in the shop. I never saw them again till the third of May.


                  Martha
                   Hagston
               



            . The prisoner brought the ruffles to me and desired me to take care of them; she said they were her own, and she was afraid her mistress would wear them.
(The Ruffles produced and deposed to by the prosecutrix.)


                  Mary
                   Bensent
               



             and 
               
                  Mary
                   Randall
               



             deposed to their being the property of the Prosecutrix.
Prisoner's Defence.
I put them in Mrs. Hagston's possession to take care of for me; they are my own property.



               Guilty
            , 


               T
            .
