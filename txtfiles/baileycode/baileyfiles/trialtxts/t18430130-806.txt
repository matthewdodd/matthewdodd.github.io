



806. 




WILLIAM SMART
 was again indicted

for stealing, on the 
22nd of Jan., 56lbs. weight of hay, value 2s. 6d., the good of 



George Hall Graham.






JAMES MOULTON
. On the 22nd of Jan. I saw the prisoner take this truus of hay away, and throw it over the fence—he then took it into his stable—I got an officer, and took the truss of hay down, and found in it these two tickets, which I had put there.





JOHN WADE
. I am a hay-binder. I found this truss—I believe it to be Mr. Graham's property.




GUILTY
. Aged 63.—


Transported for Seven Years.
