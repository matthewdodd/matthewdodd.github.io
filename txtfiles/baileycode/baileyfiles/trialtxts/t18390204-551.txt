



551. 




CHARLES SANDYS PACKER
 was indicted

 for feloniously forging and uttering a bill of exchange for £800, at 
St. Marylebone, with intent to defraud 



George Stone and others.—Other COUNTS, stating his intention to be to defraud



 Henry Fowler Broadwood and others; to which he pleaded





GUILTY
. Aged 29.—


Transported for Life.
(Mr. Henry Fowler Broadwood, pianoforte-maker; James Martii, hosier and outfitter, Oxford-street; Erskine Perry, Esq., barrister at law; and Mr. Sergeant Talfourd, M. P., deposed to the prisoner's good character.)
