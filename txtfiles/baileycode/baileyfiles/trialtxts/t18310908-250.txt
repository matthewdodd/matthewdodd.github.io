





1777. 
               
                  
                     TIMOTHY 
                     DONOVAN
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 24th of August
, 2 pictures and frames, value 20s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  Wallis 
                  Drew
               




            .
WALLIS DREW. I keep the White Lion public-house, in White's-alley, Chancery-lane




. In August I had two pictures hanging in my back parlour; on the 23rd I missed one, and on the 24th the other - I have since found them; the glass of one has been taken out.


                  JULIA 
                  MATTHEWS
               



            . I am servant to Mr. Drew. I saw the prisoner in the tap-room on the 24th of August, in the afternoon; master and mistress went out that evening, and about eight o'clock, I saw a man go out at the back door with a picture: I could not see him clearly, and do not know who he was - I went into the back parlour, and missed a picture.


                  THOMAS 
                  LAW
               



            . I bought a picture of the prisoner on a Monday, about the middle of August, for 1s.
Witness. I gave him one shilling.
Prisoner. You only gave me, 10d.


                  SARAH 
                  HAND
               



            . On the 24th of October I bought a picture of the prisoner for 9d.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner. When I took the large one there was no glass to it; I was in distress, and had warning to quit my lodgings.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 29. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
