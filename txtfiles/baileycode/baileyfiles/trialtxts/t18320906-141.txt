





1866. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     MAGENNIS
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of July
, 1 handkerchief, value 4s., the goods of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Kymer
                        




                     
                  , from his person
            .


                  JOHN 
                  KYMER
               



            . On the 17th of July, about twelve o'clock, I was passing Whitechapel


 - I felt something at my pocket, turned, and saw the prisoner with my handkerchief in his hand; I followed, and took him.(Property produced and sworn to.)
Prisoner. I went on an errand; I saw two boys going into a pawnbroker's, and I saw this handkerchief on the ground; I did not have it in my hand. Witness. Yes, he had it in his hand, and threw it down.


                  GEORGE 
                  FURLONG
               



             (Police-constable K 1.) I took the prisoner, and have the handkerchief.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 13. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
