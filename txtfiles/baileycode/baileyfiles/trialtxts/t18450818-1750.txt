



1750. 




CATHERINE KELL
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
7th of August, 11 yards of mouselinde-laine, value 12s. 6d.; the goods of 



Thomas Francis Giles; and that she had been before convicted of felony.






THOMAS HODDER
. I live with Thomas Francis Giles, of Powis-street, Woolwich. On the 7th of Aug., a little after nine o'clock, I saw this dress safe on the counter—I missed it before eleven—the prisoner was in the shop between nine and eleven, I am certain—I have seen it since, and know it to be my master's.





GEORGE STEVENS
. I am a pawnbroker. The prisoner brought this dress to pawn, between ten and eleven o'clock on Friday the 8th.

Prisoner's Defence. A woman sent me with it, and said she would give me something for me trouble.





HENRY WEBB
. I produce a certificate of the prisoner's former convic-tion—(read—Convicted 27th Feb., 1843, of larceny, and imprisoned six months)—the prisoner is the person.




GUILTY
. Aged 52.—


Transported for Seven Years.



Before Mr. Justice Wightman.
