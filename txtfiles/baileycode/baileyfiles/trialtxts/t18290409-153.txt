





832. 
               
                  
                     THOMAS 
                     HARDING
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 21st of March
, 1 half peck measure, value 1s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Thomas 
                     Cropley
                  




               
            .


                  JAMES 
                  TERRY
               



            . I am an officer. I saw the prisoner on the 21st of March, coming from Mr. Cropley's door - he went down Sharp's-alley with something under a white great coat; I went and asked what he had got there - he said a coal measure; I took him to the watch-house.


                  THOMAS 
                  CROPLEY
               



            . I keep a coal-warehouse
 in Cow-cross-street


; the officer brought back the prisoner with this measure, which I had been using a quarter of an hour before.
Prisoner's Defence. I told him I picked it up.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 18.




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
