





741. (L.) 
               
                  
                     James
                      Mitchell
                  



               
             (a black
) was indicted for 


               stealing a wooden-cask, value 2 d. and twenty-eight pounds of butter, value 10 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     John
                      Fry
                  




               
            , October the 2d
. ||


                  John
                   Davis
               



            . The prisoner on the 2d of this month took a firkin of butter from the Custom-house Key


, which belonged to Mr. Fry of Whitechapel; he had got about sixty yards when I was informed of it; I ran after him; the prisoner seeing the follow him threw down the firkin upon the stones.
Prisoner's Defence.
I was carrying it for a man who was to pay me for it.



               Guilty
            , 


               T
            .
