





473. 
               
                  
                     JOHN 
                     HICKEY
                  




               
             was indicted for that 


               he, on the 2nd of May
, feloniously and without lawful excuse, had in his custody and possession, a forged bank note, for the payment of 5 l
            .
To this indictment the prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            , aged 17.




               Transported for Fourteen Years
            .
Second Middlesex jury, before Mr. Recorder.
