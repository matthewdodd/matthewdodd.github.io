






1164. 
               
                  
                     RICHARD 
                     WILSON
                  



               
            , and SARAH his wife
, were indicted for 


               feloniously stealing, on the 10th of June
, at St. Pancras


, 20 sovereigns the monies of 
                     
                        
                           George 
                           Bathurst
                        




                     
                  , in the dwelling-house of 
                     
                        William 
                        Lyon
                     



. - To which indictment the said Richard Wilson pleaded.




               GUILTY
             - 


               Transported for Life
            .




               No evidence was offered against
            

                     Sarah 
                     Wilson
                  



               
            .
