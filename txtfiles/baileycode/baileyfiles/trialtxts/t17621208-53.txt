





55. (L.) 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Sykes 
                  



               
            , spinster
, was indicted for 


               stealing one gallon pewter pot, val. 4 s.
             the property of 
               
                  
                     Hugh 
                     Evans 
                  




                
            , Nov. 28
. ~
The prosecutor keeps the White Swan and Sugar-loaf alehouse
 in Fetter-lane


. Deposed to his gallon pot, which was produced in court.


                  Thomas 
                  Atkinson 
               



            , who keeps another public house in the neighbourhood, deposed he met with the prisoner about 7 at night, on the 28th of Nov. with the gallon pot, she dropt it down, and ran away, he followed and secured her.



               Guilty
