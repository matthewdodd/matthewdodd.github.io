





232. (L) 
               
                  Mary, wife
 of William Squires


            , was indicted for 


               stealing one pair of linnen sheets, value 5 s. the property of 
                     
                        
                           John 
                           Tate 
                        




                      
                  , the same being in a certain lodging-room, let by contract
            , &c. Aug. 29
. ++


                  Elizabeth 
                  Tate 
               



            . My husband's name is John, I let the prisoner at the bar a lodging about five weeks ago; it was ready furnished; a pair of sheets were missing about the latter end of August, and she had left her lodging, and absconded.


                  William 
                  Masters 
               



            . The prisoner pawned this pair of sheets with me, August 29, for 5 s. [Produced in court, and deposed to.]
The prisoner said nothing in her defence.



               Guilty
            .




[Transportation. See summary.]
