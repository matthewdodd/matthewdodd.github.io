





354. 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Woodward 
                  



               
            , of St Giles in the Fields


, was indicted for 


               stealing one Camlet Gown, a Shift, four Caps, a Cambrick Hood, two Cambrick Handkerchiefs, a Linnen Apron, a Silk Knot, two Pair of Shoes, and one Pair of Cloggs
            , the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Elizabeth 
                     Aldridge 
                  




                
            , June 7
.


                  Elizabeth 
                  Aldridge 
               



            . I took the Prisoner in Covent-Garden, with my Gown, Shift, Apron, Cap, and Knot on.


                  Joseph 
                  Murphy 
               



            , the Constable: I saw them upon her Back; the Prisoner was stript; and I have kept them ever since.
The Prisoner said, the Prosecutrix lent them to her to go to see a Friend; but this she denied. 

               Guilty
            .
