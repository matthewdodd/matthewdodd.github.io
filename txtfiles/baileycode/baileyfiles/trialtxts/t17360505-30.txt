





36. 
               


             of St. Andrew's Holborn


, was indicted for 


               stealing a Feather Bolster, a Linnen Sheet, and two Pillow-beers, out of her Lodging
            ; the Goods of 
               
                  Humphry 
                  Bushton 
               




             , March 24
.
Humphry Bushton. The Prisoner and her Husband took the Lodging. Having missed the Goods, and he being missing, I took her up with a Warrant, and she own'd she had pawn'd the Goods at the two Places where I found them.


                  John 
                  Dorman 
               



            . A Sheet and two Pillow-beers I took in from the Prisoner: I ask'd her no Questions when she brought them, because she had used our Shop many Years.
Q. The People of your Trade are not apt to ask Questions.



                  Ann 
                  Gilner 
               



            . I lent a Shilling upon the Bolster. 


               Guilty 10 d.
