



2250. 




JOHN ROBERTS
 was indicted

for stealing, on the 
9th of October. 1 handkerchief value 3s., the goods of 



Reynolds Goddard, from his person.






REYNOLDS GODDARD
. I am a warehouseman, and live in Edmond's-place Aldersgate-street. On the 9th of October, between seven and eight o'clock, I was on Holborn-bridge, and saw the prisoner running across the road—I followed him into Farringdon-street—he shortly after returned, and stood against a post—I had lost my handkerchief just before, and seized him—it was found on him.





THOMAS BONCHER
. I am an officer. I took charge of the prisoner, and found this handkerchief on him, concealed in the flap of his trowsers.

Guilty

(Property produced and sworn to.)
The indictment also charged a pervious conviction.





HENRY LAKE
. I was present on the 4th of January, when the prisoner was tried and convicted—he is the person to whom this certificate, which I produce, refers—(read)




GUILTY
.* Aged 16—


Transported for Seven Years.
