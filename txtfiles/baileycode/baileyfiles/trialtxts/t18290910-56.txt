





NEW COURT, Second Day.
Fifth Middlesex Jury - before Mr. Common Sergeant.
1588. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     CHAPPELL
                  



               
             was indicted 


               for stealing, on the 1st of August
, 1 watch, value 3l.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Mary 
                     Hughes
                  




               
            .
The prisoner 


               pleaded GUILTY
            .




               Transported for Seven Years
            .
