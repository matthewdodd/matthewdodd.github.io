                  , belonging to a certain building, with an intent to steal it,
            
March 20

                  Thomas 
                  Truelove 
               



             and 
               
                  Thomas 
                  Holloway 
               



            , two watchmen deposed, that they stopped the prisoner on Palm-sunday, about four o'clock in the morning, with lead on his shoulder, produced in court.


                  Matthew 
                  Jenkinson 
               



             deposed, he saw the lead fitted to a building in Scotland-yard, belonging to Mr. Devale, and the nails and nail holes fitted exactly, where some had been taken away, that it weighed forty seven pounds, and he believed it to be his property.
The prisoner in his defence said, a man bargained to give him three-pence to carry it for him from Whitehall to Covent-garden.



               Guilty
