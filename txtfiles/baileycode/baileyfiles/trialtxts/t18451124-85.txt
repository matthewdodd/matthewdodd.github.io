



85. 




MARY COTTERELL
 was indicted

 for stealing, on the 
17th of Oct.,3 shawls, value 3l.; and 1 gown, 16s.; the goods of 



Charles Andrews, her master:—also on the 
14th of Sept., 2 petticoats, value 3s.; 2 night-gowns, 4s.; 3 caps, 1s.; 3 shirts, 3s.; and 1 frock, 3s.; the goods of 



Frederick Crouch, her master; to both which she pleaded





GUILTY
.† Aged 17.—


Transported for Seven Years.
