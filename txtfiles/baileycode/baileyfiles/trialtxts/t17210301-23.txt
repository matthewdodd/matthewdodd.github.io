







                     Richard 
                     Queen 
                  



               
            , of St. Jame's at Clerkenwell


, was indicted for 


               feloniously stealing an Iron Chain value 6 d. a Streak of a Coach Wheel value 1 s. and a Wire Male value 5 s
            . the Goods of 
               
                  
                     Samuel 
                     Roberts 
                  




                
            , on the 17th of February
 last. It appeared that the Prisoner went to the Prosecutor's Brew-house for Grains and took the Goods; and that his Son seeing the Chain hang out at a Pattin-maker's, he askt him who he had it of, and he told him, the Prisoner. The Prisoner in his Defence said that he was fuddled, and the Goods lying without the Gate he took them. The Jury found him 


               Guilty to the value of 10 d.
            



               Transportation
            .
