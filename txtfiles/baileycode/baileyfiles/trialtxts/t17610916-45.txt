                  Johanna 
                  Sykes 
               



            . I keep a milk-cellar
, and live in Prince's street


, the prisoner was my weekly servant
, she lay with me, she went to bed when I did. On the fifth of August she saw where I laid my pocket; I awaked between four and five in the morning, and missed her and the things mentioned in the indictment. I apprehended her on the 27th, and before the justice she confessed to the taking the money and things.
