





203. (M.) 
               
                  
                     William James 
                     Preston 
                  



               
             was indicted for 


               stealing two aprons, value 2 s. two shifts, a bed-gown, a shirt, and a handkerchief
            , the property of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Parker 
                  




                
            , January 26
. ++


                  John 
                  Parker 
               



            . I live in Rose-lane, Spital-fields


. I took the prisoner in, out of charity: he took an opportunity to rob me of the things mentioned. I took him up; he had my shirt on his back, and owned where he had pawned the other things, where I went and found them all.


                  Robert 
                  Ayre 
               



            , the pawnbroker, produced the other things, which he deposed the prisoner brought and pledged. (Deposed to by prosecutor.)



               Guilty
            . 


               T
            .
