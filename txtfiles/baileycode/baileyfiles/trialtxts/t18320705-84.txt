






1506. 
               
                  
                     MARGARET 
                     QUIN
                  





               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of June

, 1 pillow, value 1s. 6d.; 1 pillow-case, value 6d.; 1 sheet, value 2s. 6d.; 1 table-cloth, value 1s.; 1 gown, value 2s.; 1 pelisse, value 1s. 6d.; 1 frock, value 2s.; 1 petticoat, value 1s.; 1 bed-gown, value 1s. 6d., and 2 yards of fringe, value 2s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Smith
                  



               
            .


                  MARTHA 
                  SMITH
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  John 
                  Smith
               



            ; my little girl had had the meazles, and I put these things out at the door to clear my room; I missed them about nine o'clock - the prisoner had lodged in the front kitchen for one week; the landlady had suspicion of her, and we went after her, but could not overtake her.


                  DUNCAN 
                  McLEAN
               



             (Police-constable D 100). I found the prisoner drunk in the street, about eleven o'clock the same night; she had the bundles tied in a sheet.
Prisoner. I was very much in liquor - I never took any thing before; I have a child ten years old.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24.
1507. 
               
                  MARGARET 
                  QUIN
               



             was again indicted for 


               stealing, on the 7th of June

, 1 sheet, value 1s. 6d.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Keys
                  





               
            .


                  SARAH 
                  KEYS
               



            . I am the wife of 
               
                  John 
                  Keys
               



             - I live in the same house with Smith; I have the back parlour. I missed a sheet from the back yard; I gave information, and it was found with the bundle.


                  MARY 
                  ANN
               



             CARD. I live in the same house. I saw the sheet hanging on the line; the prisoner took it down a little after nine o'clock at night - she then went up the steps, but I do not know where.


                  DUNCAN 
                  McLEAN
               



            . This sheet was with the other things.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 24. - 


               Transported for 14 Years
            .
