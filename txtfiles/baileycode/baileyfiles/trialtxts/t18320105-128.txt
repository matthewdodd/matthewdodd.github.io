





421. 
               
                  
                     WILLIAM 
                     MARKHAM
                  




               
             was indicted for 


               stealing, on the 10th of December
, 6 lbs. of pork, value 5s.
            , the goods of 
               
                  
                     Henry 
                     Parks
                  




               
            .


                  HENRY 
                  PARKS
               



            . I live in High-street, Kingsland


, and am a cheesemonger
 - I lost a hand and spring of pork on the 10th of December.


                  MARY 
                  ANN FIELD
               



            . I know the prosecutor's shop. On the 10th of December I saw the prisoner take the pork, and go away with it, between one and two o'clock in the day; I sent my boy to give the prosecutor information.


                  THOMAS 
                  HUTCHINS
               



            . I live with the prosecutor. I received information - I chased the prisoner, and found the pork in his apron.


                  WILLIAM 
                  HARTNETT
               



            . I am a Policeman, and took the prisoner.



               GUILTY
            . Aged 15. - 


               Transported for Seven Years
            .
